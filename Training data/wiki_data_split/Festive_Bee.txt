        Like all other Event bees, this bee does not have a favorite treat, and the only way to make
        it gifted is by feeding it a [[Star Treat|Star Treat]] or [[Gingerbread Bear]]s.

        It likes the [[Mountain Top Field]], [[Mushroom Field]], and the [[Pine Tree Forest]]. It
        dislikes the [[Blue Flower Field]].

        === Abilities ===

        [[Treats]], honey, and \"[[Ability Tokens#Festive Blessing|Festive Blessing]]\" to all players

        * '''[[Ability Tokens#Bomb|[Red Bomb+]]]''': Collects 10 pollen from 29 surrounding red
        [[Flowers]] (+10% pollen per level). You can combo with other bombs to increase power.
        * '''[[Ability Tokens|[Festive Mark]]]''': Marks a very large random area on the field for
        12 seconds (+0.5s per level) that grants +10% [[Honey Per Pollen|Honey per Pollen]], x1.4

        all players who stand in it. While active, it summons Red Boosts and random gifts within its
        radius. Rarer gifts grow more likely with bee level, and certain gifts are only possible if
        the bee is Gifted. This mark can link with marks from all players. '''This ability token can
        only be obtained with the [[Festive Wreath]] [[Beequip]] when it’s equipped to Festive
        Bee.''' The beequip could’ve been obtained by completing all of [[Bee Bear]]'s quests during
        the 2020 [[:Category:Beesmas|Beesmas]] event or by opening the [[Gift Boxes|Festive Gift
        Box]] during the Beesmas 2021 and Beesmas 2022 events.

        == Trivia ==
        * This was the third Event bee to have ability tokens that can be summoned by non-Event bees
        (Honey Mark and Red Bomb+), the first being [[Photon Bee]] ([[Ability Token#Haste|Haste]])
        and the second being [[Vicious Bee]] (Blue Bomb+).
        * This bee likes the [[Pine Tree Forest]]. This is a reference due to most Christmas trees
        being pine trees.
        ** This also makes it the only red bee to like this field.
        * This bee, [[Buoyant Bee]], and [[Digital Bee]] are the only bees that can apply buffs to
        the entire server. (More specifically, balloons, festive gifts, and Field Corruption.)
        * This bee, [[Rage Bee]] and [[Spicy Bee]] are the only red bees to produce tokens that
        don't give primary benefit to only red (Festive Bee yields Honey Mark tokens and both Rage
        Bee and Spicy Bee yield [[Ability Tokens#Rage|Rage]] tokens).
        * This was the second bee available to be bought for robux, the first being [[Bear Bee]].
        ** However, this was the first bee to be obtainable through both [[Quests]] and robux; and
        the second bee to be obtained through quests overall, the first being Gummy Bee.
        * When Festive Gift activates, it plays the first few notes from the classic Christmas song
        \"[[Wikipedia:Deck the Halls|Deck the Halls]]\". When collecting the Festive Blessing and
        Beesmas Cheer tokens, the players can hear the next few notes.
        * A [[First Edition Bee|First Edition]] Festive Bee's flag is offset and is placed behind
        where it normally is to make room for the bee's bow.
        * Due to a bug, Festive Bees obtained from the Ticket Tent before the
        [[Updates#2019.09.28|9/28/2019 Update]] might have received a First Edition Flag in that
        update despite not being a true First Edition Bee.
        * It was the only Event bee that could have been obtained in two different ways (excluding
        robux packs) at the same time: The Festive Present next to Bee Bear and in the Ticket Tent
        for 500 tickets during Beesmas 2019.
        * This bee, along with [[Windy Bee]], are the only bees with tinted wings when not gifted.
        * According to Bee Bear, Festive Bee is a female.
        * It could be received during [[:Category:Beesmas|Beesmas 2018]] by completing the Bee Bear
        questline or by purchasing the Festive Bee Pack for 1600 robux in the [[Robux Shop]].
        * It could have been obtained through the [[Ornament Presents|Festive Present]], in the
        Beesmas 2019 Event. If the player already had Festive Bee, they would instead get 500
        tickets.
        * In Beesmas 2020, this bee can be obtained after finishing Bee Bear’s questline. If the
        player already has a Festive Bee, they are awarded 500 tickets instead.
        * This and [[Puppy Bee]] are the only [[Bees]] that have an exclusive [[Beequip]] for
        themselves.
        * Festive Bee is the only non-colorless bee capable of making Mark tokens.
        * After Beesmas 2020, the models of Festive and Puppy Bee, wearing their respective
        exclusive beequips, were moved outside of the main map, just behind [[Commando Chick's
        Hideout]].
        * Festive Bee and [[Riley Bee]] are the only Red bees to like three fields.
