        The '''Pink Eraser''' is a [[Bond|level]] 12 [[beequip]]. It has an equip limit of one.
        There is no color requirement. It can only be equipped to [[Bees|bees]] with a [[System
        Page#Convert Amount|Convert Amount]] [[Mutation|mutation]].

        ==Potential==
        *Convert Amount (Guaranteed) - +20 to +40 (up to +100 from waxing)
        *[[Instant Conversion]] (Guaranteed) - +15% to +20% (up to +30% from waxing)
        *Mark Duration (Guaranteed) - -90% to -99%
        *[Hive Bonus] [[System Page#Instant Bomb Conversion|Instant Red Bomb Conversion]]
        (Guaranteed) - +7% to +10% (up to +15% from waxing)
        *[Hive Bonus] [[Instant Conversion|Instant Bee Gather Conversion]] (Guaranteed) - +3% to +5%
        (up to +8% from waxing)
        *[Hive Bonus] [[Honey]] From [[Instant Conversion]] - +0.5% to +1% (up to 4% from waxing)
        *[Hive Bonus] Instant Conversion - x1.01 to x1.05 (from waxing, extremely rare, caustic
        only)

        ==Ways to obtain==
        *Can be purchased from [[Dapper Bear's Shop|Dapper Bear's Beequip Shop.]]
        *As a reward from the [[Robo Bear Challenge]].
