        {{Color|Honey}} is the main [[:Category:Currency|currency]] in [[Bee Swarm Simulator]].

        == Overview ==
        === Usage ===
        Honey can be used to purchase many [[items]] such as [[tools]], [[bags]], and in-game
        [[:Category:Accessories|accessories]] such as [[:Category:Guards|guards]] and
        [[:Category:Gliding Tools|gliding tools]], and to play [[Memory Match]]. Honey can also be
        used to purchase products such as [[ticket]]s, [[Egg#Basic Egg|basic eggs]], [[Royal
        Jelly|royal jellies]], [[treats]], and [[Hive Slot|hive slots]]. In addition, honey used
        along with tickets, could have been used to purchase [[Buffs & Debuffs#From Areas|Market
        Boosts]] from the [[Boost Market]], which is now removed. The price of many
        [[:Category:Consumables|consumables]] increases with each purchase, for example, basic eggs.
        === Obtaining Honey ===
        Honey is usually obtained by converting [[pollen]] into it. It can be converted at the
        [[hive]] or (partly) instantly converted using an [[Instant Converter]] or with a
        [[Micro-Converter|micro-converter]], catching a falling [[coconut]] or Science Bear's
        falling [[Beesmas Lights|Beesmas Light]] (only during Beesmas), activating [[Passive
        Abilities#Coin Scatter|Coin Scatter]] and/or [[Passive Abilities#Diamond Drain|Diamond
        Drain]]. Honey can also be obtained by completing [[quests]], redeeming [[codes]], defeating
        [[mobs]], and collecting [[sparkles]] and [[goo]] from [[fields]]. [[Vicious Bee|Vicious
        Bee's]] [[Ability Tokens#Impale|impale]] ability spawns a honey token if it hits a [[mob]].
        [[Festive Bee|Festive Bee's]] [[Ability Tokens#Festive Gift|festive gift]] ability always
        spawns honey tokens. Some other [[bees]] have the ability to give a [[Ability Tokens#Honey
        Gift|honey gift]] or a [[Ability Tokens#Token Link|token link]]. Certain
        [[:Category:Hats|hats]], guards, and [[amulet]]s have the ability to [[Instant
        Conversion|instantly convert]] a percentage of pollen into honey. Popping [[bubble]]s,
        spawning a [[flame]], having field conversion such as [[Field Wind|field winds]], collecting
        a [[Ability Tokens#Bomb|bomb token]] produced by a bee can result in instant conversion as
        well. The player can also get honey for participating in the [[Ant Challenge]]. Honey tokens
        can appear when the player collects pollen in a [[Fields|field]] and can be obtained from
        [[honeystorms]]. The [[Mythic Meteor Shower]] awards honey tokens after catching a meteor.
        The [[Honey Dispenser]] is also an option, however, it is only available to those who are in
        the [https://www.roblox.com/groups/3982592/Bee-Swarm-Simulator-Club#!/about Bee Swarm
        Simulator Club].

        == [[System Page#Convert Rate|Convert Rate]] ==
        Bees convert pollen into honey at a set rate. For example, [[Basic Bee]] makes 80 honey in 4
        seconds, translating into a conversion rate of 20 honey per second. [[Badges]], accessories,
        [[Robux Shop#Gamepasses|gamepasses]], [[enzymes]], and ''[[Buffs & Debuffs#From
        NPCs|Science Enhancement]]'' can increase the convert rate. The production rate stacks and
        adds up. There is currently no convert rate cap in the game.
