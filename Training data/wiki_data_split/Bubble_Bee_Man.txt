        '''Bubble Bee Man''' is a temporary [[Quest Givers|Quest Giver]] on the inner top of the
        [[Bear Gate]] (only accessible during [[Day/Night Cycle|Nighttime]] due to the moon
        platforms). Bubble Bee Man is one of the five temporary quest givers, along with [[Sun
        Bear]], [[Gummy Bear]], [[Bee Bear]], and [[Stick Bug]].

        In Beesmas 2020 and 2021, after the second Update for the event, he also gives out a Quest
        until the deadline.

        ==Trivia==
        *Bubble Bee Man originated from Mah_Bucket's fan-event called \"Beesmas\", which [[Bee Swarm
        Simulator]] participated in.
        *Bubble Bee Man is the only NPC to own two different NPC pads for itself, the other being
        behind the [[Global Top Battlers|Global Top Battlers Leaderboard]] near Panda Bear. However,
        it was removed during [[Updates#2019.12.23|Beesmas 2019]].
        **During Beesmas 2018, Bubble Bee Man also gave the player a quest after they obtained the
        first [[Badges#Event Badges .5BCurrently Unobtainable.5D|Beesmas Badge]] (Beesmas Believer)
        from [[Bee Bear]].
        *Bubble Bee Man is the only NPC to have deliberate grammatical errors, typos and
        misspellings in their dialogue.
        *Bubble Bee Man is one of the two NPCs that rewards an [[:Category:Accessories|Accessory]]
        as a reward for completing his quests, along with [[Sun Bear]].
        *Bubble Bee Man is actually a [https://www.roblox.com/catalog/17735318/Bubble-Bee-Man Roblox
        Hat].
        **Strangely, as the Roblox hat, both the texture and the model have the name: Bubble Bee
        Man.
        *Bubble Bee Man is the only NPC whose dialogue contains references to other Roblox games.
        *Sometimes, the icon of the B.B.M. Mask will appear to be invisible, but hovering over where
        the icon should appear will still show its stats.
        *Bubble Bee Man is one of the six [[Quest Givers]] that are not bears, along with [[Onett]],
        [[Gifted Bucko Bee]], [[Gifted Riley Bee]], Honey Bee, and Stick Bug.
        *Bubble Bee Man is the only quest giver that requires the player to defeat the Stump Snail.
        *In old servers, you can still see Bubble Bee Man giving his dialogue even if you did not
        complete his quest.
        *Bubble Bee Man is the only quest giver that gives out two different kinds of hats.
        *After the [[Updates#2019.02.01|2/1/2019 Update]], there is a royal jelly token in front of
        Bubble Bee Man and an enzyme token on the other side of the platform where one of the
        [[present]] tokens was previously located.
        *Regarding the quests needed to get completed, including Bee Bear's quests, the five Gifted
        Riley Bee quests, the five Gifted Bucko Bee quests, and [[Science Bear|Science Bear's]]
        quests to get the translators for delivering the presents, you needed to complete at least
        100 quests in total in order to complete Bubble Bee Man’s Beesmas 2018 quest (B.B.M.
        Mission).
        *During Beesmas 2018, if the player tried to talk to Bubble Bee Man before making a present
        with Bee Bear, he would say to come back later when they have made a present with Bee Bear,
        meaning they would need to earn the first Beesmas 2018 Badge (Beesmas Beeliever) before
        being able to start his quest.
        *Bubble Bee Man’s quest during Beesmas 2019 (B.B.M's Ornament) was originally planned to
        require the player to pop 3,333 [[Passive Abilities#Gathering Bubbles|Bubbles]], but this
        got changed before the release of the quest since Onett thought it would be a \"great meme.\"
        *He is the only quest giver that is solid.
        *When finishing his Beesmas 2020 quest, he says that you can use the Naughty List every 22
        hours, but you can actually use it every 8 hours.
        *He is the NPC that gives the least and most amount of Honey when finishing a quest (14 and
        14,404,808,675,309 respectively).
        *All of Bubble Bee Man's quests contain his initials B.B.M. or BBM.
        *During Beesmas 2020, Bubble Bee Man stayed a week after the end of the event so some people
        could finish his quest (Snowbears, snowflakes, and Festive Nymphs stayed too as they were
        part of his Quest).
        *Before they were removed, Bubble Bee Man was the only quest-giver to reward [[Eviction]]s.
