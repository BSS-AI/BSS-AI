        '''Windy Bee''' is a [[:Category:Colorless|Colorless]] [[Bees/Event|Event bee]] that hatches
        from a [[Egg#Event Bee Eggs|Windy Bee egg]], which can be obtained from donating [[Cloud
        Vial]]s after previously donating a [[Spirit Petal]] to the [[Wind Shrine]].

        Like all other Event bees, this bee does not have a favorite Treat, and the only two ways to
        make it gifted is by feeding it a [[Star Treat|Star Treat]], [[Gingerbread Bear]]s or [[Aged
        Gingerbread Bear]]s.

        Windy Bee likes the [[Dandelion Field]] and the [[Coconut Field]]. It dislikes the [[Bamboo
        Field]] and the [[Strawberry Field]].

        == Stats ==
        * Collects 10 [[Pollen]] in 3 seconds.
        * Makes 180 [[Honey]] in 2 seconds.
        * +40% [[Stats#Speed|Movespeed]], +50% Convert Speed, +25% Gather Speed, +100 [[System
        Page#Convert Amount|Convert Amount]], +3 [[Stats#Attack|Attack]].
        * 🌟 [[Gifted Bee#List of Hive Bonuses|Gifted Hive Bonus]]: +15% [[System Page#Instant White
        Conversion|Instant White Conversion]], x2 Boosts From [[Clouds]].

        === Abilities ===
        * '''[[Ability Tokens#Boost|[White Boost]]]''' Grants x1.2 pollen from White [[Flowers]] for
        15s. Stacks up to 10 times.
        * '''[[Ability Tokens#Rain Cloud|[Rain Cloud]]]''' Summons a Cloud in a different field that
        lasts for 60s (+10s per level). Clouds make flowers grow and grant +25% Pollen and 10%
        Unique Instant Conversion to nearby players (50% Pollen and 20% Unique IC if the player has
        a gifted Windy Bee). If another player's Windy Bee spawns a cloud in the field the player is
        on, a message will appear announcing \"<span style=\"background:grey;color:white; \">🌧️
        [Username]'s Windy Bee summoned a Cloud in this field. 🌧️</span>\"
        * '''[[Ability Tokens#Tornado|[Tornado]]]''' Spends your \"[[Ability Tokens#Haste|Haste]]\"
        stacks to summon a Tornado which damages enemies and collects [[Tokens]] and Pollen.
        Lifespan, speed, and damage are increased by the number of Haste stacks spent and bee level.

        == Trivia ==
        * It was formerly available to purchase for 800 Robux until the deadline, October 27 of
        2019.
        ** This was the 3rd bee available to be bought for Robux, with the eighth being [[Digital
        Bee]], sixth and seventh being [[Buoyant Bee|Buoyant]] and [[Precise Bee]], the fifth being
        [[Puppy Bee]], the fourth being [[Fuzzy Bee]], the second being [[Festive Bee]] and the
        first being [[Bear Bee]].
        * There is a gate dedicated to it, known as the [[Windy Bee Gate]] or the 35 bee gate.
        * This, and [[Vicious Bee]] are the only bees to have a hostile variant.
        * This bee, [[Music Bee]], [[Vector Bee]], [[Tadpole Bee]], [[Spicy Bee]], Fuzzy Bee,
        Buoyant Bee, Festive Bee and [[Digital Bee]] are the only bees with three different ability
        tokens, the most of any bee.
        * This bee, [[Ninja Bee]], [[Shy Bee]], and Fuzzy Bee are the only bees that have only one
        skin color.
        * This bee is the only bee to have the same skin color when it turns [[Gifted Bee|gifted]].
        * This bee, [[Cobalt Bee]], [[Crimson Bee]], and Puppy Bee are the only event bees that
        don't have an 'enhanced' ability when gifted.
        * This bee is the only bee to have 2 gifted bonuses, excluding other Event bees who enhance
        their ability.
        * Currently, this bee, Bear Bee, [[Gummy Bee]], Digital Bee and Vicious Bee are the only
        event bees that can't be bought in the [[Ticket Tent]].
        * This is the only Event Bee to dislike 2 fields.
        * This, along with Buoyant Bee, are the only bees to be able to produce a White Boost token.
        However Buoyant Bee only produces it through Surprise Parties.
        * The Tornado ability, [[frog]]s, [[Puppy Bee|Puppy Bee's]] [[Ability Tokens#Fetch|Fetch]]
        ability, the [[Cub Buddy]] and [[Passive Abilities#Star Saw|Star Saw]] are the only entities
        that can pick up [[items]] outside of personal tokens for the player.
        * This bee and Festive Bee are the only non-gifted bees that have tinted wings.
        * This bee was hinted at during the [[Black Bear]] quest \"Black Bear, Why?\" when he says
        this: \"Vicious Bee isn't the only bee that causes trouble! One time, I saw a strange bee
        destroy a whole field with powerful gusts of wind! I wonder if there are any other
        mysterious bees roaming free out there?\"
        * At one point, a model of a Windy Bee could be found outside the map, alongside with a
        model of a Vicious Bee.
        * Supposedly, Windy Bee likes the [[Dandelion Field]] because dandelion seeds 'go wherever
        the wind takes them.'
        * Spirit Bear referred Windy Bee as a \"She\".
