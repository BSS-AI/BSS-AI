{"text": "'''Ornaments''' are limited-time [[items]] that are obtainable during
        [[:Category:Beesmas|Beesmas]] 2019, 2020, 2021, and 2022. Ornaments can be obtained from
        most [[Quest Givers|quest givers]]. When the player receives an ornament, it gets placed on
        the [[Beesmas Tree]]. The ornaments will also grant some additional buffs, always with one
        field capacity buff, with the exception of Gifted Riley Bee, Gifted Bucko Bee, and Stick
        Bug, who gives two. Honey Bee just gives extra capacity, no field capacity. Every NPC's
        ornament would also give Convert Rate, except Gummy Bear and Bubble Bee Man.

        == Beesmas 2020 ==
        {{RemovedContent}}Ornaments returned in 2020, functioning the same. However, in order to
        obtain ornaments, the player needed to collect and give [[present]]s to an NPC in exchange
        for their ornament, along with a few other rewards. Some quest givers also required things
        completed or owned such as the [[Gummy Boots]] and the [[Honey Mask]]. Some buffs from
        ornaments were changed.

        == Beesmas 2019 ==
        {{RemovedContent}}In 2019, ornaments could be obtained from most quest givers by completing
        their special ornament quest. Every ornament would give the player extra capacity, a
        [[pollen]] boost from one or two specific [[fields]], and a bonus stat [[Buffs &
        Debuffs|buff]]. Unlocking ornaments were required for completing [[Bee Bear|Bee Bear's]]
        quests during his second visit and opening up [[Ornament Presents|ornament presents]].
