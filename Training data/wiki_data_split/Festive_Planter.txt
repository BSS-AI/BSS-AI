        The '''Festive Planter''' is a [[:Category:Beesmas|Beesmas]] exclusive single-use planter
        added in the [[Updates#2021.12.26|12/26/21 Beesmas update]]. It grows in 4 hours of
        playtime, and grants 3x more nectar. The chances of a Festive Planter spawning a
        [[Puffshroom]] is 1/10 (10%).

        Only 1 Festive Planter can be active at a time.

        == Ways to Obtain==
        ''There are currently no methods to obtain the Festive Planter.''

        ===The methods below are no longer possible.===

        *By purchasing certain items/bundles from [[Bee Bear's Catalog]]:
        **Buying the Festive Planter for 100 [[Snowflake]]s and 10 [[Gingerbread Bear]]s during the
        2021 and 2022 [[Beesmas]] events.
        **Buying the Merry Mondo Bundle for 25,000 Snowflakes and 250 Gingerbread Bears gave 10
        Festive Planters and other items.
        **Buying the Hefty Honeyday Bundle in Bee Bear's Catalog for 25,000 Snowflakes and 250
        Gingerbread Bears gave 10 Festive Planters and other items.
        *Completing [[Bee Bear|Bee Bear's]] 5th and 10th quest of Snow Cub Reformation.
        *By opening certain [[Gift Boxes]]:
        **Opening the Festive Gift Box from 2021 gave 1 Festive Planter, 2 if the player already had
        the [[Festive Wreath]], it also gave other items.
        **Opening the Festive Gift Box from 2022 gave 2 Festive Planters and other items.
        **Opening the Mondo Gift Box from 2021 and 2022 gave 5 Festive Planters and other items.
        *By purchasing certain offers in the [[Robux Shop]]:
        **Purchasing the Festive Frenzy Haul for 4,500 robux gave 10 Festive Planters and other
        items.
        **Purchasing the Mondo Robo Bundle gave 10 Festive Planters and other items.
        *Completing [[Spirit Bear|Spirit Bear's]] Beesmas quest rewarded 1 Festive Planter.
        *As a reward for completing Onett's Beesmas 2022 Quest.
        * As a reward for completing Spirit Bear's Beesmas 2022 Quest.
        *Completing Bee Bear's 10th and 20th quest rewards the player with a Festive Planter.
        * As a reward for completing your own Robo Party (Rank 40).

        ==Trivia ==
        *At this time, the max possible amount of Festive Planters a player can obtain is 101.
        *This is the only planter that can drop Magic Beans.
        *This planter gives the best Nectar multiplier in the game (3x), overtaking the [[Ticket
        Planter]] and [[Sticker Planter]] that give a x2 nectar multiplier.
        *This is one of the four planters that aren't reusable. Others being the [[Ticket Planter]],
        [[Sticker Planter]] and the [[Paper Planter]].
