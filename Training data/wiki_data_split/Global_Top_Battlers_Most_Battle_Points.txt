        The '''Global Top Battlers Leaderboard''' (also called the '''[[Battle Points]]
        Leaderboard''') is one of the 62 [[leaderboards]] in the game. This leaderboard shows how
        many battle points players have collected over time. It shows 10 leaderboard positions at
        one time. The player can scroll down the leaderboard to see more players, all the way to the
        100th place. Only legitimate players appear on the leaderboards for lengths of time
        (hackers/exploiters will be removed). Battle points are collected from certain mobs in the
        game.

        If the player steps on the white pad in front of it, the message, <span
        style=\"background-color:#0090BF; color:white;\">\"You have x Battle Points\"</span>, x
        being the number of battle points, will appear.

        == Location ==
        It is located on the yellow platform, besides [[Panda Bear]], in front of the [[Bamboo
        Field]], and behind the [[Blue Flower Field]].
