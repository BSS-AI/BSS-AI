        }}The '''Noob Shop''' is a [[:Category:Shops|shop]] run by [[Noob Bear]]. This shop sells
        four [[bags]], five [[tools]], two [[:Category:Hats|hats]], one pair of
        [[:Category:Boots|boots]], and one [[:Category:Belts|belt]]. It is located right next to the
        [[Dandelion Field]], and below the entrance of the [[Star Hall]]. It is the first shop,
        other than the [[Ticket Tent]], players have access to.

        [[Onett (Developer)|Onett]] has not yet revealed the official name of this shop; it has been
        named \"Noob Shop\" by the players because the bear inside is labeled \"Noob Bear\". However,
        when [[Mother Bear]] talks about [[amulet]]s with the player, she calls the shop, \"Noob
        Bear's Shop.\"

        Outside of the shop, there is a [[Honey Dispenser]], which can only be used by members of
        the [https://www.roblox.com/groups/3982592/Bee-Swarm-Simulator-Club#!/about Bee Swarm
        Simulator Club].

        Behind the Honey Dispenser is a hidden [[Obstacle Courses#Lava Obby|obstacle course]]. There
        is a [[Royal Jelly|royal jelly]] token at the end of it, located inside the Noob Shop. This
        obstacle course also has the [[Demon Mask]] shop in it. During the [[Egg Hunt 2019]] event,
        a [[Plastic Egg|plastic egg]] token was added to this course as a part of [[Brown Bear#Egg
        Hunt Quest 2019|Brown Bear's Egg Hunt 2019 Quest]]; but, this token was removed after the
        event and replaced with a token that gives 3 [[ticket]]s.

        [[Chicks#Spotted Chick|Spotted Chicks]] could have spawned here during the [[Egg Hunt
        2020|2020 Egg Hunt Event]], however this was removed.

        *After the [[Updates#5 12 2018|5/12/2018 update]], a green cog appeared on top of this shop.

        * The Helmet is the most expensive item in the Noob Shop.
