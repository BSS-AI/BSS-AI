        The '''Ticket Shop''' is a [[:Category:Shops|shop]] located outside the [[Mountain Top
        Shop]] to the right of the [[Field Booster]]. It sells [[ticket]]s for [[honey]] in
        increments of 1, 10, 100, and 500 tickets. It has a similar appearance to other shops like
        the [[Basic Egg Shop]], [[Royal Jelly Shop]] and [[Treat Shop]], but has a ticket on top,
        along with its icon on the front and sides. If you buy a ticket, the price to buy another
        will go up. The maximum tickets you can purchase in total is 50,000 at the max price of
        47.3T honey for 500 tickets.

        The shop also contains a black semicircle with a grey separator as the vending mechanism,
        which seems to make it a rotary dispenser.
