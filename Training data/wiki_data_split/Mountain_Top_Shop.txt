        The '''Mountain Top Shop''', also called the '''Top Shop''', is a [[:Category:Shops|shop]]
        located past the [[Lion Bee Gate]]. Before the [[Mountain Top Field|Mountain Top Field's]]
        name was known, this shop used to be called the '''25 Bee Shop'''. The shop sells various
        high-end [[items]] and is run by [[Top Bear]].

        There is a [[Royal Jelly]] token on the roof and a [[Ticket]] token in a corner inside the
        shop. In addition, the respawn timer for the [[Mondo Chick]] can be found on the side facing
        the Mountain Top Field.

        ==Trivia==
        *[[Mondo Belt Bag]] and [[Beekeeper's Boots]] could've also been obtained by completing
        [[Sun Bear|Sun Bear's]] quests before he left during his first visit.
        *This shop is the only place in the game where the player can purchase additional [[Hive
        Slot|hive slots]].
        *Top Bear was introduced in [[Updates#2018.07.11|7/11/2018 update]], which means this shop
        was once not run by any bear before said update.
        *This shop used to have the eviction for sale until the [[Updates#2019.09.28|9/28/2019
        update]], when it was removed.
        *When the game was first released, the only item in this shop was the Glider until the
        [[Updates#2018.04.10|4/10/2018 update]] added the [[Beekeeper's Mask]] and Hive Slots.
