        It collects 4 [[pollen]] from 5 [[flowers]] in a line every 0.5 seconds, which comes out to
        a base pollen collection rate of 40 pollen per second.

        == Appearance ==
        The Super Scooper is the advanced version of the [[Scooper]]. Its blade is noticeably bigger
        than the Scooper and has a more rectangular shape along with it's blue and yellow scheme.
        The handle is the same grey as the Scooper with dark red in the middle. The pole is dark red
        and has 6 yellow and blue attachments on it. The player holds the Super Scooper so that it
        appears to be pointing upwards.
