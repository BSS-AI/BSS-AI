        == Trivia ==
        * If the ingredients to craft this item were to be crafted, a total of 15,000,000,000 Honey,
        1 [[Spirit Petal]], 61,875 [[Strawberry|Strawberries]], 61,875 [[Blueberries|Blueberries]],
        77,500 [[Pineapple|Pineapples]], 125 [[Magic Bean]]s and 6,625 [[Royal Jelly|Royal Jellies]]
        are needed.
        * This is the 9th most expensive [[Items|item]] in terms of honey (excluding [[Hive
        Slot|hive slots]]).
        *This item emits a yellow particle effect in the center of the belt, similarly to the
        [[Petal Wand]].
        * This is one of the three belt accessories that were not obtainable from [[Sun Bear]].
        * This is one of many accessories to be worn by an [[:Category:NPC|NPC]], following the
        [[Mondo Belt Bag]] and the [[Beekeeper's Boots]] (both worn by [[Sun Bear]]), and the
        Porcelain Port-O-Hive, worn by [[Onett]].
        * The Petal Belt worn by [[Spirit Bear]] is much bigger than the one worn by a player.
        * The Petal Shurikens can pop [[Passive Abilities#Gathering Bubbles|bubbles]] and [[Ability
        Tokens#Fuzz Bombs|fuzz bombs]].
        *This is the only belt on display which doesn't have a hitbox.
        *This is one of two belts to have a [[Passive Abilities|passive]], the other being the
        [[Coconut Belt]].
