        The '''Porcelain Dipper''' is a [[Tools|tool]] that was added in the
        [[Updates#2018.06.02|6/2/2018 update]]. It can be purchased in the [[Mountain Top Shop]].

        {| class=\"article-table\"
        !Crafting Ingredients
        |-
        |{{Cost|Honey|150,000,000}}
        |}

        It collects 3 pollen from 49 surrounding patches in 0.7 seconds, increasing white pollen by
        50%. Its average base pollen collection rate is 245 pollen per second: 315 from
        [[:Category:Colorless|white]] [[flowers]] and 210 from [[:Category:Red|red]] and
        [[:Category:Blue|blue]] flowers (the ability is not counted, assuming all flowers are
        single-size). It has a maximum base [[pollen]] collection rate of 315 pollen per second,
        which is achievable only in the [[Spider Field]]. It emits bubbles and sparkles.

        Every 10th swing will summon a large decal of this tool rising up, it then summons a pillar
        of light collecting lots of pollen from 29 flowers (in a 5x5 area around the player and 4
        other flowers 3 tiles ahead, to the side and behind the player) and plays the following
        audio:

        [[File:PDAbility.ogg]]

        == Trivia ==
        *The price was increased from 100,000,000 (100 million) honey to 150,000,000 (150 million)
        honey after the [[Updates#2019.04.05|4/5/2019 update]], because an ability was added to it.
        *It used to collect more patches prior to the [[Updates#2019.04.05|4/5/2019 update]].
        *The Porcelain Dipper is an upgraded version of the [[Honey Dipper]], which can be bought in
        the [[Pro Shop]].
        *This was the second tool to have an ability, first being the [[Golden Rake]].
        *Some decals aren't visible through the glass of a porcelain dipper (such as bee faces in a
        hive, or the flame effect on the scythe) similar to the water of the sprinklers.
        **However, if positioned correctly, it can be used to view certain things in areas, like the
        [[Coconut Canister]], [[Coconut Clogs]], and the [[Coconut Belt]] in the [[Coconut Cave]].
        *The average collection rate of this tool is 41% better than the [[Scythe|Scythe's]] and the
        [[Bubble Wand|Bubble Wand's]] average rate.
        **However, the Scythe is 48% better at red flowers, and the Bubble Wand is 48% better at
        blue flowers. This is tempered by the fact that there are no fields that are entirely blue
        or entirely red, meaning the Bubble Wand and Scythe never actually achieve their theoretical
        maximum collection rate.
        *The Porcelain Dipper glows white during a [[honeystorm]].
        *The ability still activates outside of a [[Fields|field]].
        **The Porcelain Dipper’s light pillar will not collect pollen if the player is above the
        field in any way (e.g. jumping or gliding above the field or standing outside at the edge),
        although the pillar visually touches the flowers.
        **If a player goes in the [[King Beetle's Lair]] and jumps right before the beam effect,
        they can collect pollen from the Clover Field.
