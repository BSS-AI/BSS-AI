{"text": "'''Emoticons''' are a mechanic for showing a bee's mood. The mood can be affect how much
        [[bond]] a [[Bees|bee]] gets when it collects [[pollen]], how fast the bee flies, and can
        even enable some bees to find certain [[sticker]]s when collecting. It does not affect other
        [[stats]], such as [[Stats#Gather Amount|pollen collection rate]] or [[Stats#Production
        Amount|honey conversion speed]].

        == Bee Moods ==
        [[File:Happy.png|left|thumb|85x85px]]
        A bee will express happiness if it is fed a [[Treats|treat]] or if it is working (or most
        recently was working) in a [[fields|field]] that it likes. Bees gain extra bond from
        gathering when working in a field they like and also move slightly faster than usual.
        [[Precise Bee]] will also express happiness when it shoots all the targets during it's
        Target Practice ability.

        {{clear}}
        [[File:Sad.png|left|thumb|85x85px]]
        A bee will express unhappiness if it is working in a field it dislikes or if the last field
        it worked in was one it dislikes. Bees gain less bond from gathering in fields they dislike,
        and move slower than usual.
