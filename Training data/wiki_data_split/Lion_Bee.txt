        == Trivia ==
        * The Lion Bee has the highest '''base''' attack stat in the game.
        * [[Panda Bear|Panda]] calls Lion Bee “he,” it has a mane, and is referred to as a king in
        its description. This makes him, [[Rad Bee]] and [[Bucko Bee]] the only confirmed male bees
        in the game.
        * The Lion Bee is tied for the third-highest amount of base [[Energy]] in the game; it is
        only beaten by infinite stamina bees such as [[Photon Bee]] or [[Exhausted Bee]], and is
        tied with [[Buoyant Bee]].
        * There is a [[:Category:Gates|gate]] dedicated to it, known as the [[Lion Bee Gate]]. It is
        located near [[Polar Bear]].
        * Lion Bee, [[Tadpole Bee]], [[Tabby Bee]], [[Puppy Bee]], and [[Bear Bee]] are the only
        bees that are based on real-life animals that are not Bee Species.
        ** Lion Bee and Tabby Bee are the only bees that are based on felines.
        ** Lion Bee is the only Legendary bee that is based on a real-life non-bee species of
        animal.
        * Lion Bee is also the only bee in the game that likes the Ant Field.
        ** Lion Bee may like the Ant Field as a reference to [[Wikipedia:Antlion|antlions]] in real
        life.
        ** It might also like the Ant Field because it has the highest attack stat in the game and
        the Ant Challenge is about fighting [[ants]].
