        The '''System Page''' is shown by clicking the cog in the menu bar. It is in between the
        [[Badges]] page and the [[Robux Shop]] page. Here, the player can redeem [[codes]], send
        [[Trading|Trade Requests]], view the [[Leaderboards|Global Leaderboard]], use the [[Cub
        Console]], turn on and off in-game [[music]], toggle Pollen Text, Abbreviate Pollen, allow
        Trade Requests, display and hide other players' [[bees]], toggle Reducing Strobbing Vfx, and
        toggle Auto-Jelly. Turning off all in-game music only silences the music, not the sound
        effects (such as the sounds of [[Ability Tokens]] and obtaining
        [[Bees/Legendary|Legendary]], [[Bees/Mythic|Mythic]], [[Gifted Bee|Gifted]], and
        [[Bees/Event|Event]] bees to name a few). You can mute all sound by turning off the in-game
        sound on the Roblox menu.

        == Character Stats ==
        The tab also lists many of the player's active stat values, though not all of them. The
        following values are listed and explained:


        === Convert Amount ===
        Boosts the base conversion of your bees before multipliers by a certain amount.<br
        />Example: A [[Basic Bee]] with 20 Convert Amount will convert 100 [[honey]] in 4 seconds
        instead of 80 honey in 4 seconds.<br />It can be boosted with [[amulet]]s, the
        [[B.B.M. Mask]], the [[Honey Mask]] and certain [[:Category:Belts|belts]]. This stat was
        formerly named 'Conversion Amount' until it was changed in the [[Updates#2019.12.23|Beesmas
        2019 update]].


        === Convert Rate ===
        Boosts your bees conversion by the following percentage.<br />Example: A Basic Bee
        with 200% Convert Rate will convert 160 honey in 4 seconds instead of 80 honey in 4
        seconds.<br />It can be boosted with amulets, [[enzymes]], [[Buffs & Debuffs#From
        NPCs|Science Enhancement]], the Conversion Buff, the [[Buffs & Debuffs#From
        Other|Honeyday Event buff]], the [[Badges#Honey Badge|Honey Badge]], [[Gifted Bee|Gifted]]
        [[Diamond Bee]] and certain [[items]]. This stat was formerly named 'Conversion Rate' until
        it was changed in the Beesmas 2019 update.


        === Convert Rate At Hive ===
        Boosts your bees’ conversion by the following percentage at the [[hive]]. This stat can
        stack with Convert Rate.<br />Example: A level 1 Basic Bee with 200% Convert Rate and
        200% Hive Convert Rate will convert 320 honey in 4 seconds while converting at the hive, but
        it will only convert 160 honey outside of the hive.<br />It can be increased with the
        [[Badges#Pumpkin Badge|Pumpkin Badge]], Bubble Bloat and certain items. This buff was used
        to be \"Hive Convert Rate\" until the 12/26/21 update.


        === Bonus Convert Amount ===
        Increases the base conversion of the player's bees. It can be increased by certain amulets
        and items.


        === [[Loot Luck]] ===
        Loot Luck is a passive attribute that increases the chances of receiving rewards from
        defeated [[mobs]]. <br />It can be increased with [[Ability Tokens#Baby Love|Baby
        Love]], the [[Badges#Clover Badge|Clover Badge]], [[Baby Bee|Baby Bee’s]] Gifted Hive Bonus
        and certain items.<br />This stat was formerly named 'Luck' until it was changed in
        the [[Updates#2019.04.05|4/5/2019 update]].


        === [[Critical Hits#Critical Chance|Critical Chance]] ===
        The chance of your bees hitting stronger or collecting more pollen. More accurately - by how
        big your Critical Power is.<br />Example: A Basic Bee with 50% Critical Chance will
        hit with increased damage half of the time.<br />It can be boosted with amulets,
        [[Tropical Drink|tropical drinks]], [[Ability Tokens#Focus|Focus]], Gifted [[Commander Bee]]
        and certain items. Currently, the highest critical chance a player can have at a time is
        88%.


        === [[Critical Hits#Critical Power|Critical Power]] ===
        This stat multiplies how strong a Critical Hit will be.
        It applies to fighting and pollen collecting.<br />Example: Basic Bee with 300%
        Critical Power (on critical hit) will hit with the power of 3 Basic Bees (3 damage).<br
        />It can be boosted with the Ant Amulet, [[Ability Tokens#Melody|Melody]], the
        [[Badges#Battle Badge|Battle Badge]], Gifted [[Tabby Bee]], Gifted [[Looker Bee]] and with
        certain items. The calculated max Critical Power is 695%.


        === [[Critical Hits#Super-Crit Chance|Super-Crit Chance]] ===
        The possibility for a critical hit to Super-Crit. Super-Crits deals 33% more damage and
        collect twice as much pollen as a regular critical hit, and are instantly converted. When a
        hit is a Super-Crit, there will be a purple color indicating the damage and the '''!''' sign
        next to it. It can be boosted by having a Gifted Precise Bee or temporarily boost it by
        hitting all targets of target practice, a precise mark, or using a super smoothie. The max
        Super-Crit Chance is 45%.


        === [[Critical Hits#Super-Crit Power|Super-Crit Power]] ===
        The amount pollen is multiplied by when performing Super-Crits (after applying Critical
        Power). Super-crit attacks are only increased by 33% as much as Pollen Super-crits. It can
        only be boosted by the [[Whistle]] Beequip, a [[Cog Amulet]] or temporarily boosted by the
        [[Dark Scythe]].


        === Bee Ability Rate ===
        Increases the speed at which the player's bees create [[Ability Tokens]].<br
        />Example: If it takes a bee about 20 seconds to produce a token, with 20% Bee Ability
        Rate, the bee is likely to take ~16 seconds to produce the token.<br />Bee ability
        rate can be boosted by Star Amulets, Motivating Nectar, certain masks, items, bee mutations,
        and Beequips.


        === [[Pollen]] ===
        Increases pollen from all sources (both from [[bees]] and [[:Category:Tools|tools]]).<br
        />Example: With 100% pollen, the player can collect 1,000 pollen, and with 110% pollen,
        it will able to collect 1,100 pollen from the same amount of time and [[flowers]].<br
        />It can be increased with amulets, [[Ability Tokens#Bear Morph|Bear Morph]], [[Ability
        Tokens#Inspire|Inspire]], Baby Love, [[Ability Tokens#Mark|Pollen Marks]], the [[Buffs &
        Debuffs#From Other|Honeyday Event buff]], from [[clouds]], Gifted Basic Bee, or with certain
        items.


        === White Pollen ===
        Boosts your white pollen collection. It can be increased with amulets, tropical drink,
        [[Ability Tokens#Boost|White Boost]], the [[Badges#Spider Badge|Spider Badge]], the
        [[Badges#Coconut Badge|Coconut Badge]], Gifted [[Shocked Bee]], or with certain items.


        === Red Pollen ===
        Boosts your red pollen collection. It can be increased with amulets, [[Red Extract|red
        extracts]], [[Ability Tokens#Boost|Red Boost]], [[Buffs & Debuffs#From Passives|Flame
        Heat]], the [[Badges#Strawberry Badge|Strawberry Badge]], certain gifted
        [[:Category:Red|Red]] bees, or with certain items.


        === Blue Pollen ===
        Boosts your blue pollen collection. It can be increased with Amulets, [[Blue Extract|blue
        extracts]], [[Ability Tokens#Boost|Blue Boost]], with the [[Badges#Bamboo Badge|Bamboo
        Badge]], certain gifted [[:Category:Blue|Blue]] bees or with certain items.


        === Bomb Pollen ===
        Boosts your pollen collection from all bombs.<br />Example: If the player collects
        5,000 pollen with a Buzz Bomb token, with 200% Bomb Pollen they will collect 10,000 pollen
        from the same flowers.<br />It can be increased with Amulets, with [[Buffs &
        Debuffs#From Ability Tokens|Bomb Combos]], certain gifted Colorless bees, or with certain
        items.

        === Buzz Bomb Pollen ===
        Boosts your pollen collection from Buzz Bombs and [[Ability Tokens#Fuzz Bombs|Fuzz Bombs]].
        It can be increased with amulets, the [[Badges#Cactus Badge|Cactus Badge]] or with certain
        items.


        === Red Bomb Pollen ===
        Boosts your pollen collection from Red Bombs. It can be increased with Amulets, certain
        gifted Red bees or with certain items.


        === Blue Bomb Pollen ===
        Boosts your pollen collection from Blue Bombs. It can be increased with Amulets, certain
        gifted Blue bees, or with certain items.


        === [[Bubble#Bubble Pollen|Bubble Pollen]] ===
        Boosts your pollen collection from [[bubble]]s.<br />Example: If the player collects
        5,000 pollen with a bubble, with 200% Pollen from Bubbles they will collect 10,000 pollen
        from the same flowers.<br />It can be increased with Gifted [[Bubble Bee]], [[Bubble
        Mask]] or the [[Diamond Mask]].


        === [[Flame#Flame Pollen|Flame Pollen]] ===
        Boosts your pollen collection from [[flame]]s created by bees, [[Ability Tokens|ability
        tokens]], and [[Passive Abilities|passive abilities]].<br />Example: If the flames
        collect 1,000 over time, with 200% Pollen from Flames they will collect 2,000 pollen from
        the same flowers.<br />It can be increased with Gifted [[Fire Bee]] or the [[Demon
        Mask]].


        === Bee Gather Pollen ===
        Boosts pollen collection for all bees.<br />Example: With 200% Bee Gather Pollen,
        Basic Bee will collect 20 pollen in 4 seconds.<br />It can be increased with amulets,
        [[glue]], Gifted [[Music Bee]], with the [[Robux Shop|x2 Pollen from Bees Gamepass]] or with
        certain items.


        === Pollen From Tools ===
        Boosts your pollen collection from your tool.<br />Example: With 100% (base) Pollen
        From Tools, the player will collect 1,000 pollen with their tool at once. With 125% Pollen
        From Tools player will collect 1,250 pollen with their tool at once at the same place.<br
        />It can be increased with amulets, glue, Gifted [[Carpenter Bee]], with the
        [[Badges#Stump Badge|Stump Badge]] or with certain items.


        === [[Instant Conversion]] ===
        Instantly converts your pollen when you are collecting pollen on a field.<br
        />Example: a Basic Bee with 50% Instant Conversion that collects 10 pollen will make half
        of it instantly convert into honey. You will have 5 honey and 5 pollen, so you can collect
        more pollen.<br />It can be boosted with amulets, enzymes, the [[Badges#Dandelion
        Badge|Dandelion Badge]], Gifted [[Photon Bee]], [[Ability Tokens#Festive Blessing|Festive
        Blessing]] buff, or with certain items.


        === Instant White Conversion ===
        Instantly converts white pollen you collect, in addition to Instant Conversion. It can be
        boosted with amulets, the [[Badges#Pineapple Badge|Pineapple Badge]], Gifted [[Windy Bee]],
        or with the [[Coconut Canister]].


        === Instant Red Conversion ===
        Instantly converts red pollen you collect, in addition to Instant Conversion. It can be
        boosted with amulets, Flame Heat, the [[Badges#Mushroom Badge|Mushroom Badge]], Gifted
        [[Crimson Bee]], or with certain items.


        === Instant Blue Conversion ===
        Instantly converts blue pollen you collect, in addition to Instant Conversion. It can be
        boosted with amulets, the [[Badges#Blue Flower Badge|Blue Flower Badge]], Gifted [[Cobalt
        Bee]], [[Diamond Mask]], or with certain items.


        === Instant Bomb Conversion ===
        Instantly converts pollen collected by the player's bomb. It can be temporarily boosted by
        standing in a [[Ability Tokens#Festive Mark|festive mark]], or can be permanently increased
        by owning a Gifted [[Demon Bee]].


        === [[Flame#Instant Flame Conversion|Instant Flame Conversion]] ===
        Instantly converts pollen collected by the player's summoned flames. It can be boosted by
        having the Demon Mask equipped, or you can temporarily get 50% Instant Flame Conversion by
        triggering the [[Passive Abilities#Scorching Star|Scorching Star Passive]].


        === [[Bubble#Instant Bubble Conversion|Instant Bubble Conversion]] ===
        Instantly converts pollen collected by the player's popped bubbles. There are currently no
        ways to obtain it.


        ===[[Honey Per Pollen]]===
        Allows you to make more Honey from the same amount of pollen, which works on conversion,
        [[Instant Converter]]s, [[Micro-Converter|micro-converters]], and Instant Conversion.<br
        />Example: With 200% Honey Per Pollen, a player will make 2,000 honey from 1,000
        Pollen.<br />It can be increased with the [[Moon Amulet]], the [[Wealth Clock]], the
        [[Badges#Goo Badge|Goo Badge]], Gifted [[Gummy Bee]] or Nectar.


        === Honey At Hive ===
        Increases the honey you convert when converting at hive. You can boost this stat with
        Satisfying Nectar and Balloon Blessing. Diamond Bee's Shimmering Honey grants bonus honey
        when converting at the hive, but it is different to this stat.


        === Movespeed ===
        Movespeed of your character in studs. It can be increased with amulets, [[oil]], [[Ability
        Token#Haste|Haste or Haste+]], [[Ability Token#Bear morph|Bear Morph,]] [[Passive
        Abilities#Coconut Haste|Coconut Haste]], Gifted [[Hasty Bee]], or with
        [[:Category:Boots|boots]].


        === Movement Collection ===
        Allows the player to collect [[pollen]] when walking on [[flowers]]. The player can get this
        boost from wearing [[:Category:Boots|boots]] or from a [[Moon Amulet]]. The higher the
        movement collection, the greater amount of pollen the player collects from walking on
        flowers. This boost also collects [[goo]] if the player walks on any goo patches.


        === Jump Power ===
        Jump power of your character in studs. It can be increased with [[Ability Token#Bear
        Morph|Bear Morph,]] all boots (except for the [[Basic Boots]]), or the [[Propeller Hat]].
        The minimum jump power is 55 and the maximum jump power is 97.


        === Defense ===
        Reduces the damage that you take.<br />Example: If a mob deals 50 damage, with 50%
        Defense it will deal 25 damage instead.<br />It can be increased with the [[Shell
        Amulet]], [[:Category:Hats|hats]], or with [[:Category:Guards|guards]]. Having 100% defense
        means that you take no damage and can be achieved by activating the [[Passive
        Abilities#Emergency Coconut Shield|emergency coconut shield]] passive. The maximum defense
        without the emergency coconut shield is 60%. This is achieved by having the [[Demon Mask]]
        or [[Diamond Mask]] (+35%), both the [[Crimson Guard|Crimson]] and [[Cobalt Guard]]s (+10%
        each), and +5% on a Shell Amulet. Defense was originally Damage Reduction.


        === Bond From Treats ===
        Gives additional [[bond]] for your bees when you give them [[treats]].<br />Example:
        If you feed 100 normal treats (1,000 total bond) to your bee, with 125% Bond From Treats it
        will grant 1,250 bond instead.<br />It can be increased with the [[Moon Amulet]] or
        with Gifted [[Puppy Bee]].


        === Bond From Battle ===
        Gives additional bond for your bees when you are defeating a [[mob]].<br />Example:
        Normally, [[scorpion]]s give 15 bond for each of your bees when you defeat it. With 150%
        Bond From Battle, your bees will receive 22.5 Bond instead.<br />It can be increased
        with amulets.


        === Honey From Tokens ===
        Boosts honey that you receive from all [[Ability Tokens#Honey Gift|honey giving tokens]]
        (Honey Gift, Honey Gift+, [[Ability Tokens#Impale|Impale]], mob loot, [[Passive
        Abilities#Coin Scatter|Coin Scatter]], [[Honeystorm]], and others).<br />Example: A
        level 2 [[Diamond Bee]] will give 2,000 honey with its Honey Gift+ token. With 200% Honey
        From Tokens, it will give 4,000 instead.<br />It can be increased with amulets, Gifted
        [[Honey Bee]], or with certain items.


        === [[Bee Attack#Regular Bee Attack|Bee Attack]] ===
        Makes all your bees, regardless of their colors, deal more damage per hit. It will add one
        bee attack (Damage) per bee attack stat that you have. <br />Example: With +4 Bee
        Attack, Basic Bee will attack with the power of 5 Basic Bees (Base Attack + Bee Attack
        multiplier).<br />It can be increased with amulets, with [[Ability Token#Rage|Rage]],
        Gifted [[Rage Bee]] or [[Brave Bee]], or with certain items and buffs.


        === [[Bee Attack#Red Bee Attack|Red Attack]] ===
        Same as Bee Attack, but applies only for Red bees. It can be boosted with amulets or certain
        items.


        === [[Bee Attack#Blue Bee Attack|Blue Attack]] ===
        Same as Bee Attack, but applies only for Blue bees. It can be boosted with amulets or
        certain items.


        === [[Bee Attack#Colorless Bee Attack|Colorless Attack]] ===
        Same as Bee Attack, but applies only for Colorless bees. It can be boosted with amulets,
        Gifted [[Lion Bee]], or certain items.


        === [[Bee Attack#Bee Attack Multiplier|Attack Multiplier]] ===
        Multiplies attack of all bees, but with a percentage. It can be increased with Amulets,
        [[stinger]]s, Flame Heat, [[Passive Abilities#Emergency Coconut Field|Emergency Coconut
        Shield]] or with the [[Demon Mask]].


        === [[Goo]] ===
        Multiplies all collected goo. It can be increased with the Shell Amulet, [[Passive
        Abilities#Gummy Morph|Gummy Morph]], [[Gummy Mask]], [[Gummy Boots]], [[Passive
        Abilities#Gummy Star|Gummy Star]], or [[Gummyballer]].


        === Goo Conversion ===
        Instantly converts pollen collected from flowers covered in goo, similar to Instant
        Conversion. It can be increased by amulets, Gummy Mask, Gummy Morph, or Gummy Star.


        === [[Capacity|Capacity Multiplier]] ===
        Gives additional pollen storage by the amount of the percentages.<br />Example: If
        your backpack can hold up to 200,000 pollen, with 200% Capacity Multiplier, your backpack
        can hold up to 400,000 instead.<br />It can be increased with amulets,
        [[Badges#Sunflower Badge|Sunflower Badge]], [[Badges#Quest Badge|Quest Badge]], [[Bumble
        Bee]]'s Gifted Hive Bonus, [[Buoyant Bee]]'s Gifted Hive Bonus and with certain items. If
        your capacity multiplier is a decimal percentage, it doesn't show the decimal and rounds the
        number, but the rounding doesn't affect your capacity.


        === [[Field Capacity|White Field Capacity]] ===
        Gives additional pollen storage by the amount of percentages based on the percentage of
        white flowers in a field.


        Example : If you have 15,000,000 Capacity (With Capacity Multiplier) and your White Field
        Capacity is 180%, your total capacity if in the Spider Field is 27,000,000. It can be
        increased with things like [[Gummy Mask]], Exhausted Bee's Gifted Hive Bonus, [[Polar
        Bear]]'s Ornament and other inventory items. If your capacity multiplier is a decimal
        percentage, it doesn't show the decimal and rounds the number, but the rounding doesn't
        affect your capacity.


        === [[Field Capacity|Red Field Capacity]] ===
        Gives additional pollen storage by the amount of percentages based on the percentage of red
        flowers in a field.

        Example : If you have 15,000,000 Capacity (With Capacity Multiplier) and your Red Field
        Capacity is 150%, your total capacity if on a field with all flowers being red is
        22,500,000. It can be increased with things like [[Demon Mask]], Riley Bee's Gifted Hive
        Bonus, [[Gifted Riley Bee|Gifted Riley Bee (NPC)]] Ornament, the Rose Badge and other
        inventory items. If your capacity multiplier is a decimal percentage, it doesn't show the
        decimal and rounds the number, but the rounding doesn't affect your capacity.


        === [[Field Capacity|Blue Field Capacity]] ===
        Gives additional pollen storage by the amount of percentage based on the percentage of blue
        flowers in a field.

        Blue Fields being Stump Field, Blue Flower Field, Bamboo Field and Pine Tree Forest.

        Example : If you have 15,000,000 Capacity (With Capacity Multiplier) and your Blue Field
        Capacity is 200%, your total capacity if on a field with only blue flowers is 30,000,000. It
        can be increased with things like Bucko Bee's Gifted Hive Bonus, [[Gifted Bucko Bee|Gifted
        Bucko Bee (NPC)]] Ornament, the Pine Tree Badge, and other inventory items. If your capacity
        multiplier is a decimal percentage, it doesn't show the decimal and rounds the number, but
        the rounding doesn't affect your capacity.


        === Convert Total ===
        The total convert amounts of all of your bees combined (after conversion modifiers).


        === Gather Total ===
        The total gather amounts of all of your bees combined (before pollen modifiers).


        === Ticket Chance ===
        A stat boost which increases chances of obtaining tickets, can be obtained through the
        [[Moon Amulet]], and the now unobtainable ticket chance gamepass.


        === Attack Total ===
        The total attack of all of your bees combined (after attack modifiers).


        == Auto-Jelly Settings ==
        [[File:3CDE71CC-E05C-4EAA-A98E-BD3E71C85BCB.jpeg|thumb|right|The Auto-Jelly settings.]]
        [[File:AutoJellyReminder.png|thumb|The message when an auto-jelly setting is on.]]
        The '''Auto-Jelly Settings''' can be found at the bottom of the System Page. There are
        options for [[Royal Jelly|royal jellies]] or [[Royal Jelly#Royal Jelly Variants|star
        jellies]] to auto roll until the requirements are fulfilled. It can be used to help players
        obtain a bee they desire quickly. There are options for the requirement the jelly will roll
        into:
        * Until Legendary: Jellies will auto roll until you get a [[Bees/Legendary|Legendary]] or
        [[Bees/Mythic|Mythic]] Bee.
        * Until Mythic: Jellies will auto roll until you get a Mythic Bee.
        * Require Gifted: Jellies will auto roll until you get a [[Gifted Bee|Gifted]] or Mythic
        Bee.
        * Require Mutation: Jellies will auto roll until you get a [[mutation|Mutated]] or Mythic
        Bee.
        Multiple options can be turned on at a time, whilst also making the auto jelly stop on the
        combined requirement.<br />Example: When the Legendary and Gifted options are turned
        on, the jellies will auto roll until you get a Gifted Legendary Bee. When the Legendary,
        Gifted, and Mutation options are turned on, the jellies will auto roll until you get a
        Gifted Legendary Bee with a Mutation.

        If the Until Legendary option or both the Until Legendary and Until Mythic options are
        turned on, it will auto roll until you get a Legendary Bee or Mythic Bee. If only the
        Require Gifted option is turned on, it will auto roll until you get a Gifted Bee or a Mythic
        Bee. If only the Require Mutation option is turned on, it will auto roll until you get a
        Gifted Bee or a Mutated Bee. If both the Require Gifted and Require Mutation options are
        turned on, it will auto roll until you get a Gifted Mutated Bee or Mythic Bee.

        If the Mutation option is turned on, but the bee you selected isn't radioactive, a pop-up
        after using a royal jelly will say that you can't auto-roll royal jellies for mutation.
        Unless the bee is radioactive, you will have to use the royal jellies one at a time.

        When using a royal jelly on a bee with an auto-jelly option enabled, a reminder appears
        after the message that the auto-jelly option is on.
        [[File:Extremelyrareautojelly.png|thumb|The extra warning pop-up when selecting extremely
        rare options.]]
        If you turn on the following options, an extra pop-up will warn the player that the options
        they selected are very rare:
        * Mythic and Gifted.
        * Mythic and Mutation.
        * Mythic, Gifted, and Mutation.
        * Legendary, Gifted, and Mutation.
        [[File:Autostarjelly.png|thumb|An extra warning pop-up that appears when you try to
        auto-jelly with star jellies.]]
        When using a star jelly onto a bee, another pop-up will appear, reminding the player that an
        auto-jelly setting is on and will use as many star jellies as it needs in order to fulfill
        its requirement. This pop-up will appear even if only the Until Gifted option is turned on.
        If you are using the Require Gifted option with star jelly, you will still receive the
        warning, but nothing will happen.
