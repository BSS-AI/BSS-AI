        A '''Pinecone''' is a [[Bond|level]] 9 [[:Category:Beesmas|Beesmas]] [[beequip]]. It has an
        equip limit of two. There is no color requirement. It can only be equipped to [[Bumble
        Bee|Bumble]], [[Stubborn Bee|Stubborn]], [[Bucko Bee|Bucko]], [[Frosty Bee|Frosty]],
        [[Carpenter Bee|Carpenter]], or [[Bear Bee|Bear]] [[bees]].

        == Possible Stats ==
        * [[System Page#Convert Amount|Convert Amount]] (Guaranteed) - +20% to +30%. (up to +70%
        from [[Waxes|waxing]])
        * [[System Page#Bee Ability Rate|Ability Rate]] (rare) - +1%. (up to 2% from waxing)
        * [Hive Bonus] [[Pine Tree Forest]] [[Field Capacity|Capacity]] (Guaranteed) - +8% to +15%.
        (up to +25% from waxing)
        * [Hive Bonus] Pine Tree Forest [[Pollen]] (Guaranteed) - +5% to +10%. (up to +20% from
        waxing)

        == Ways to Obtain ==

        * Opening the [[Gift Boxes#2021|Earthen Gift Box]] dropped 1 Pinecone + other stuff.
        * Opening the [[Gift Boxes#2022|Chestnut Gift Box]] dropped 1 Pinecone + other stuff.
        * Buying the [[Robux Shop|Merry Mythic Pack]] granted 1 Pinecone + other stuff.
        * As a reward from completing Stick Bug's Beesmas 2020 quest.
        * Buying it from 2020 [[Bee Bear's Catalog]].
        * As a rare drop from [[Werewolf]] during Beesmas.
        * As a reward for participating in the [[Stick Bug Challenge]] during Beesmas.
        * As a rare drop from a [[Festive Nymph]].
