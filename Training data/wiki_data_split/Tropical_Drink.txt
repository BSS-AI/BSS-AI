        |cooldown = 1 second
        }}'''Tropical Drink''' is a [[:Category:Crafted|craftable]] [[:Category:Inventory|inventory
        item]] added in the [[Updates#2019.09.28|9/28/2019 update]]. When used, it grants x1.25
        [[System Page#White Pollen|white pollen]], +10% [[Instant Conversion|instant conversion]]
        and +5% [[System Page#Critical Chance|critical chance]] for 10 minutes. It can also be used
        as a material to craft certain items.
        == Ways to Obtain ==
        * Crafting it in the [[Blender]] for 10 [[coconut]]s, 2 [[enzymes]], and 2 [[oil]].
        * Defeating the [[Coconut Crab]] guarantees tropical drinks.
        * Completing certain [[Black Bear]] [[quests]] will award tropical drinks:
        ** Boss Battles rewards the player with 3 tropical drinks after completion.
        ** [[File:Tropical drink.jpg|thumb|A tropical drink inside the player’s inventory]]Black
        Bear has a rare chance to reward tropical drinks after the completion of one of his
        repeatable quests.
        * As a very rare drop from [[leaves]] in the [[Coconut Field]].
        * As a drop from [[sprout]]s in the Coconut Field (the odds increase depending on the rarity
        of the sprout).
        * Matching a pair of Tropical Drinks in [[Memory Match#Extreme Memory Match|Extreme Memory
        Match]] or [[Memory Match#Mega Memory Match|Mega Memory Match]].
        * As a drop from [[Wild Windy Bee]].
        * As a very rare drop from a [[Cub Buddy]] gift.
        * As a drop from [[Aphid#Armored Aphid|Armored Aphid]].
        * As a drop from fully-grown [[Planter]]s harvested on Coconut Field.

        === The methods below are no longer possible. ===

        * Matching a pair of Tropical Drinks in [[Memory Match#Winter Memory Match|Winter Memory
        Match]].
        * Purchasing the Kazoo Bundle from the [[Bee Bear's Catalog]] grants 10 tropical drinks.
        * Completing [[Honey Bee (NPC)|Honey Bee's (NPC)]] [[Beesmas|Beesmas 2022]] quests gives 9
        Tropical Drinks.
        * Purchasing packs in the [[Robux Shop]].
        * Redeeming certain expired [[codes]]:
        ** Using the code \"2YearsAfterParty\" (granted the buff and other stuff).
        ** Using the code \"PineappleParty\" (gave 1 tropical drink and other stuff).
        ** Using the code \"Strawbeary\" (gave 1 tropical drink and other stuff).
