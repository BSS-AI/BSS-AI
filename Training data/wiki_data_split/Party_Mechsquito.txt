        == Strategies ==
        Since they are essentially the same as their normal counterparts, Mechsquitos, the same
        methods used for them can be followed.

        * Party Mechsquitos aim for where the player is, not where the player will be. Because of
        this, moving around in circles will often result in Party Mechsquitos missing their attack.
        * Stand near the Party Mechsquito, and this will allow your bees to lock on to the Party
        Mechsquito and quickly deal damage.
        * [[Vicious Bee]] and [[Windy Bee]] provide excellent crowd control, especially since Party
        Mechsquitos often come in pairs or packs. [[Digital Bee]] can also stun many Party
        Mechsquitos at once and allow them to take extra damage via [[Ability Tokens#Mind Hack|Mind
        Hack]].
        * Unlike their normal counterparts, they stay in fields which can be used for the players
        advantage. Stand right outside the field they are in and your bees will attack it while it
        doesn't aim for you. Note that this can be a bit risky since they can hover around the other
        parts of the field and don't come close enough for your bees to target them.
