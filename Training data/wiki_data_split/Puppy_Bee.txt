        }}'''Puppy Bee''' is a [[:Category:Colorless|Colorless]] [[Bees/Event|Event bee]]. It
        hatches out of a [[Eggs#Event Bee Eggs|Puppy Bee egg]], which is available in the [[Ticket
        Tent]] for 500 [[Ticket]]s.

        Like all other Event bees, this bee does not have a favorite treat, and the only way to make
        it gifted is by feeding it a [[Star Treat|Star Treat]], [[Gingerbread Bear]]s or [[Aged
        Gingerbread Bear]]s.

        Puppy Bee likes the [[Clover Field]] and dislikes the [[Rose Field]].

        ==Stats==
        *Collects 25 [[Pollen]] in 4 seconds.
        *Makes 280 [[Honey]] in 4 seconds.
        *+100% [[Energy]], +15% [[Stats#Speed|Movespeed]], +15 Gather Amount, +200 [[System
        Page#Convert Amount|Convert Amount]], +1 [[Stats#Attack|Attack]].
        *🌟 [[Gifted Bee|Gifted Hive Bonus]]: +20% [[System Page#Bond From Treats|Bond from
        Treats]].

        ===Abilities===
        *'''[[Ability Tokens#Fetch|[Fetch]]]''' Creates a ball that collects tokens and increases
        pollen by 100% (+15% per [[Bond|Level]]) as you and Puppy Bee kick it to each other.
        Collection increases per kick. Combo hits to win [[Treat|Treats]].
        *'''[[Ability Tokens#Puppy Love|[Puppy Love]]]''' Increases your bond with up to 10 nearby
        bees by 60 (+20 per lvl). Grants +10% Bee Movespeed and +50% Bee Gather Amount for 10
        seconds (+1s per lvl).

        ===Reindeer Antlers Abilities===
        (Puppy Bee can summon these abilities if it has the [[Reindeer Antlers]] [[Beequip]]
        equipped)

        *'''[[Ability Tokens#Fetch|[Reindeer Fetch]]]''' Replaces Puppy Bee's \"[[Ability
        Tokens#Fetch|Fetch]]\" ability. Puppy's ball will glow bright red, and every 3rd time Puppy
        hits it you'll receive \"[[Buffs & Debuffs#From Ability Tokens|Reindeer Guidance]]\",
        which grants x1.01 Capacity for 15 minutes and stacks up to 10 times. Additionally, the ball
        has a small chance to spawn [[Gingerbread Bear]]s at high combos (max 1 per day). The chance
        is increased during [[Beesmas]] Events.
        *'''[[Ability Tokens#Focus|[Focus]]]''' Grants +3% [[Critical Hits|Critical Chance]] for 20
        seconds. Stacks up to 10 times. Critical hits increase pollen and damage by 100%.

        ==Trivia==
        *When Puppy Bee came out on the [[Updates#2018.09.10|9/10/2018 Update]], it was on sale in
        the Ticket Tent for 250 tickets until September 22, 2018.
        * It takes 3 hits of the ball by the owner and 3 hits by the Puppy Bee (3 volleys) to drop a
        [[Treat|treat token]]. The more times the ball is hit in a row, the more treats are earned
        from the treat token (Doubled every time a combo is done, starting at 1, capping at 256).
        **Once the treat token reaches the capped amount of treats, the ball will permanently stay
        light green and will not be able to produce any more treat tokens.
        **It takes a total of 54 hits to reach the max amount of treats, and 60 to get 10x Reindeer
        Guidance.
        **When a treat token of this kind is collected, even if more than 1 treat is earned from the
        treat token, it will always count as 1 treat token collected in quests.
        *Puppy Bee was the first bee to be able to create treat tokens, the second being [[Festive
        Bee]] with its [[Ability Tokens#Festive Gift|festive gift]] ability.
        * Puppy Bee was likely made to be [[Tabby Bee|Tabby Bee's]] canine counterpart.
        *Sometimes, the ball spawned by the fetch token can get stuck, allowing the player and Puppy
        Bee to easily pass it back and forth.
        *Puppy Bee was the first [[Bees|Bee]] that has a 3D model on its face (its tongue).
        *Puppy Bee, [[Bear Bee]], and Tabby Bee are currently the only bees that were sold at a
        discount for a limited amount of time.
        *This bee, Bear Bee, [[Gummy Bee]], Tabby Bee, Festive Bee, [[Digital Bee]], and [[Windy
        Bee]] are the only Event bees that can be a [[First Edition Bee]].
        **First Edition Puppy Bee could have been obtained by buying it before September 22, 2018.
        * Treat tokens produced by Puppy Bee's fetch can be collected with a [[Ability Token#Token
        Link|Token Link]], similar to [[Ability Tokens#Honey Gift|Honey Gifts]].
        *Puppy Bee is the third bee to produce a token that has its face on it, the second being
        Tabby Bee, and the first being [[Rage Bee]].
        *This is the only bee that is able to raise the bond of other bees, through its puppy love
        token.
        * This bee, [[Vicious Bee]], [[Crimson Bee]], [[Cobalt Bee]], and Windy Bee are the only
        Event bees that don't have an 'enhanced' ability if gifted.
        *The puppy love token glows when collected.
        *Puppy Bee's wings clip through its ears.
        *According to Bee Bear, Puppy Bee is a male.
        *This and Festive Bee are the only bees that can produce gingerbread bears.
        * This bee, along with Festive Bee, are the only bees that have an exclusive beequip for
        themselves.
        *During the Beesmas 2020 Event, players could purchase a special bundle for 800 Robux in the
        [[Robux Shop]] that would grant them a Puppy Bee Egg, the special Reindeer Antlers Beequip
        specifically made for the Puppy Bee, and 50 Gingerbread Bears as a bonus.
        **If the player already had a Puppy Bee when they bought the bundle, they would get 500
        tickets instead.
        *The player may also see a model of a Puppy Bee wearing the Reindeer Antlers beequip next to
        a Festive Bee model wearing the [[Festive Wreath]] beequip if they go far out of bounds.
        *This bee, along with [[Tadpole Bee]], Tabby Bee, [[Lion Bee]], and Bear Bee are the only
        bees based on real animals.
