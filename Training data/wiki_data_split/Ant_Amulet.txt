{"text": "The '''Ant Amulet''' is an [[amulet]] that is always obtained from participating in the
        [[Ant Challenge]].

        The amulet gives the player buffs and will always boost [[System Page#Convert Rate|Convert
        Rate]]. There are five tiers of Ant Amulets, each awarded at a certain amount of points in
        the Ant Challenge:

        The buffs for each amulet are generated randomly. Higher tiers of amulets award more and
        stronger buffs, starting with three buffs for Bronze and reaching six buffs for Supreme. The
        quality of the buffs increase with the player’s score until a score of 400, so a score of 99
        will have better buffs than a score of 55.
