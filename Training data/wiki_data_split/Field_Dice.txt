        {{Color|Field Dice}} is an [[:Category:Inventory|inventory item]] added in the
        [[Updates#2019.04.05|4/5/2019 update]]. When used, It boosts a random [[Fields|field]] the
        player has access to, granting a +100% [[pollen]] [[Field Boost]] for that field for 15
        minutes. If the player uses another field dice, or any other type of dice while a Field
        Boost from a dice is still active, the new field boost will override the old one, preventing
        the Field Boost from stacking up.

        As of the 12/26/2021 Update, there are currently 3 types of dice in the game, those being
        Field Dice, [[Smooth Dice]] and [[Loaded Dice]].

        The following audio plays when a field dice is used:[[File:Fdice.ogg]]

        == Trivia ==
        * Field Dice's raw ingredients cost:
        ** {{Cost|Whirligig|1}}
        ** {{Cost|Honeysuckle|5}}
        ** {{Cost|Sunflower Seed|50}}
        ** {{Cost|Pineapple|50}}
        ** {{Cost|Blueberry|50}}
        ** {{Cost|Strawberry|50}}
        ** {{Cost|Royal Jelly|50}} or {{Cost|Honey|50,000,000}}
        * The field icons shown on the dice are the [[Pine Tree Forest]], the [[Strawberry Field]],
        and the [[Blue Flower Field]].
        * This item is similar to {{Cost|Glitter}}, because they both give a 100% pollen boost on a
        field. However, it can only be used every 15 minutes, and the player can choose which field
        to boost.
        * Field Dice is actually a plural name. The singular version of this item would be Field
        Die.
        * It is possible to get a [[Mountain Top Field]] Boost, [[Stump Field]] Boost, [[Coconut
        Field]] Boost, and [[Pepper Patch]] Boost from field dice.
        ** Unlike this, [[Field Booster]]s can’t give a boost to said fields.
        * Donating 3 field dice to the [[Wind Shrine]] is almost guaranteed to summon [[Wild Windy
        Bee]].
        * There used to be a limit of 25 field dices, but it was removed in the 12/26/21 update.
        * The Field Dice used to be able to stack field boost allowing player to get up to 300%
        field boosts from it, it was later reworked in 12/26/21 update, where dice system was
        reworked and with the introduction of new dice types.
