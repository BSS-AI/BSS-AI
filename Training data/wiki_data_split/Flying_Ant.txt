        This is one of the first Ant Challenge [[mobs]] that the player will encounter, capable of
        appearing on the very first wave. Despite being able to appear on the first wave, Flying
        Ants usually start appearing at around the second or third waves.

        Flying Ants are ants with wings and they will pursue the player. They have the ability to
        pounce on players from quite a distance are the fastest out of the five ant types. However,
        they also have low health, much lower than any other ant type.

        A Flying Ant's level can increase depending on what wave of the Ant Challenge the player is
        on. They can range from level 1 up to level 16.

        == Strategies ==

        * Due to their quick speed, the player will need to react more quickly in order to dodge
        Flying Ants trying to pounce them.
        * Having [[bees]] that can produce [[Ability Tokens#Haste|Haste tokens]] can help the player
        gain more speed needed to maneuver around Flying Ants.
        * Flying Ants occasionally spawn in waves which could overwhelm the player. [[Vicious Bee]]
        and [[Windy Bee]] provide excellent crowd control with their abilities which could greatly
        help counter the large amounts of Flying Ants landing on the field. [[Digital Bee]] can also
        stun many Flying Ants at once and allow them to take extra damage via [[Ability Tokens#Mind
        Hack|Mind Hack]].
