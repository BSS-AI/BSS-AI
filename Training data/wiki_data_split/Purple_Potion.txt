        A '''Purple Potion''' is a [[:Category:Crafted|craftable]] [[:Category:Inventory|inventory
        item]] added in the [[Updates#2020.04.06|4/6/2020 update]]. When used, it grants the
        following for 15 minutes:
        * x1.25 [[System Page#Capacity Multiplier|Capacity]].
        * x1.25 [[System Page#Convert Rate At Hive|Convert Rate At Hive]].
        * x1.5 [[System Page#Red Pollen|Red Pollen]].
        * x1.5 [[System Page#Blue Pollen|Blue Pollen]].
        * x1.3 [[System Page#Bee Gather Pollen|Bee Gather Pollen]].
        * x1.3 [[System Page#Pollen From Tools|Pollen From Tools]].

        A purple potion buff and a [[Red Extract|red extract]], [[Blue Extract|blue extract]], or a
        [[glue]] buff cannot be active simultaneously. If any of these buffs are active when a
        purple potion is used, they will be overridden. Using a [[Super Smoothie|super smoothie]]
        will override the buff.

        == Ways to Obtain ==
        * A rare reward from [[Aphid#Rage Aphid|Rage Aphids]], [[King Beetle]], and [[Tunnel Bear]].
        * [[Crafting]] it in the [[Blender]] for 3 red extracts, 3 blue extracts, 3 glue, and 3
        [[Neonberry|neonberries]].
        * Completing [[Black Bear|Black Bear's]] \"Mountain Mix\" [[Quests|quest]].
        * Completing [[Dapper Bear|Dapper Bear's]] 6th, 7th and 9th quest.
        * A very rare chance from [[Mythic Meteor Shower|Mythic Meteors]].
        * An exceptionally rare chance from [[Wild Windy Bee]]s.
        * As a reward from the [[Robo Bear Challenge]].
        * As a drop from the [[Paper Planter]], [[Plastic Planter]], [[Candy Planter]], [[Red Clay
        Planter]], [[Blue Clay Planter]], [[Pesticide Planter]], [[Hydroponic Planter]], [[Petal
        Planter]], and [[Festive Planter]].
        ** Planting a Red Clay Planter in a red field, then to a blue field or vice versa has a high
        chance to drop a Purple Potion.
        * As a Stack Reward for adding certain [[stickers]] to the [[Sticker Stack]]:
        ** The Abstract Color Painting rewards a purple potion.
        ** The Purple Fleuron rewards a purple potion.
        ** The Royal Symbol rewards a purple potion.
        ** The Royal Bear rewards a purple potion.

        ==Trivia==

        *It is one of four [[:Category:Consumables|consumables]] that can boost capacity, with the
        first being the [[Marshmallow Bee|marshmallow bee]], the third being the [[Super
        Smoothie|super smoothie]] and the fourth being the [[snowflake]].
        *Prior to the purple potion being officially added to the game, there was a
        [[Glitches|glitch]] that caused it to be obtainable from King Beetle and Tunnel Bear early.
        The item could not be seen in the inventory, but could be donated to the Wind Shrine, where
        it had the appearance of a [[Egg#Basic Egg|basic egg]] and had no donation value.
        *Donating one purple potion to the wind shrine can give winds ranging from 5x-9x for 4-6
        red/blue fields.
