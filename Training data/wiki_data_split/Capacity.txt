{"text": "'''Capacity''' is a [[Mechanics|mechanic]] that shows how much [[pollen]] the player can
        store at a time. Capacity can be increased in a variety of ways including [[Buffs &
        Debuffs|buffs]], [[items]], [[sticker]]s and [[amulet]]s. Capacity can be increased by
        static amounts or by multipliers. Static increases can be shown simply through addition
        symbols (+) while multipliers can be shown through multiplication symbols (x) or percentages
        (%). The player's Capacity is displayed in the top-middle section, labelled as \"Pollen\" and
        also on the player's bag. The player can see how much their capacity is multiplied by
        looking for the Capacity Multiplier stat in the [[System Page]].
        [[File:PollenCapacity1.png|frame]]

        ==Ways to Obtain==

        ===Bags===
        Each [[Bags|bag]] adds a static amount of Capacity for the player. These are typically the
        bulk of the static amount of Capacity that the player can hold at once.

        * The [[Pouch]] gives +200 Capacity.
        * The [[Jar]] gives +750 Capacity.
        * The [[Backpack]] gives +3,500 Capacity.
        * The [[Canister]] gives +10,000 Capacity.
        * The [[Mega-Jug]] gives +25,000 Capacity.
        * The [[Compressor]] gives +50,000 Capacity.
        * The [[Elite Barrel]] gives +125,000 Capacity.
        * The [[Port-O-Hive]] gives +250,000 Capacity.
        * The [[Red Port-O-Hive]] gives +350,000 Capacity.
        * The [[Blue Port-O-Hive]] gives +350,000 Capacity.
        * The [[Porcelain Port-O-Hive]] gives +500,000 Capacity.
        * The [[Coconut Canister]] gives +1,000,000 Capacity.

        ===Belts===
        Each belt adds a static amount of Capacity for the player. Two of the belts also give a
        Capacity Multiplier.

        * The [[Belt Pocket]] gives +5,000 Capacity.
        * The [[Belt Bag]] gives +25,000 Capacity.
        * The [[Mondo Belt Bag]] gives +100,000 Capacity.
        * The [[Honeycomb Belt]] gives +150,000 capacity. It also gives +25% Capacity.
        * The [[Petal Belt]] gives +250,000 capacity. It also gives +50% Capacity.
        * The [[Coconut Belt]] gives +350,000 capacity. It also gives +75% Capacity.

        ===Guards===
        Each guard adds a static amount of Capacity for the player.

        * The [[Brave Guard]] gives +5,000 Capacity.
        * The [[Hasty Guard]] gives +5,000 Capacity.
        * The [[Bomber Guard]] gives +5,000 Capacity.
        * The [[Looker Guard]] gives +5,000 Capacity.
        * The [[Red Guard]] gives +25,000 Capacity.
        * The [[Elite Red Guard]] gives +75,000 Capacity.
        * The [[Riley Guard]] gives +150,000 Capacity.
        * The [[Blue Guard]] gives +25,000 Capacity.
        * The [[Elite Blue Guard]] gives +75,000 Capacity.
        * The [[Bucko Guard]] gives +150,000 Capacity.
        * The [[Crimson Guard]] gives +250,000 Capacity.
        * The [[Cobalt Guard]] gives +250,000 Capacity.

        ===Hats===
        There are several hats/masks that add a static amount of Capacity or give a Capacity
        Multiplier for the player.

        * The [[Strange Goggles]] give +77 Capacity.
        * The [[B.B.M. Mask]] gives +75,000 Capacity.
        * The [[Mondo B.B.M. Mask]] gives +250,000 Capacity.
        * The [[Honey Mask]] gives a x1.25 Capacity Multiplier.
        * The [[Fire Mask]] gives a x1.25 Capacity Multiplier.
        * The [[Bubble Mask]] gives a x1.5 Capacity Multiplier.
        * The [[Gummy Mask]] gives a x1.5 Capacity Multiplier.
        * The [[Demon Mask]] gives a x1.5 Capacity Multiplier.
        * The [[Diamond Mask]] gives a x3 Capacity Multiplier.

        ===Amulets===
        There are several amulets that add a static amount of Capacity for the player. Star Amulets
        give a Capacity Multiplier.

        * The [[Star Amulet]] gives a Capacity Multiplier depending on the tier.
        ** The Bronze Star Amulet gives a x1.25 Capacity Multiplier.
        ** The Silver Star Amulet gives a x1.5 Capacity Multiplier.
        ** The Gold Star Amulet gives a x1.75 Capacity Multiplier.
        ** The Diamond Star Amulet gives a x2 Capacity Multiplier.
        ** The Supreme Star Amulet gives a x2.5 Capacity Multiplier.
        * The [[Moon Amulet]] gives +25,000 Capacity which can be upgraded to +250,000 Capacity.
        * The [[Stick Bug Amulet]] can give up to +300,000 Capacity depending on the tier.
        ** The Bronze Stick Bug Amulet gives from +25,000 Capacity up to +100,000 Capacity.
        ** The Silver Stick Bug Amulet gives from +100,000 Capacity up to +150,000 Capacity.
        ** The Gold Stick Bug Amulet gives from +150,000 Capacity up to +200,000 Capacity.
        ** The Diamond Stick Bug Amulet gives from +200,000 Capacity up to +300,000 Capacity.
        * The [[Cog Amulet]] can give up to +250,000 Capacity depending on the tier.
        ** The Bronze Cog Amulet gives from +22,500 Capacity up to +26,500 Capacity.
        ** The Silver Cog Amulet gives from +45,000 Capacity up to +51,500 Capacity.
        ** The Gold Cog Amulet gives from +90,000 Capacity up to +108,000 Capacity.
        ** The Diamond Cog Amulet gives from +127,500 Capacity up to +162,500 Capacity.
        ** The Supreme Cog Amulet gives from +200,000 Capacity up to +250,000 Capacity.

        ===Gifted Hive Bonuses===
        There are two [[bees]] that give a Capacity Multiplier if [[Gifted Bee|gifted]].

        * Gifted [[Bumble Bee]] gives a x1.1 Capacity Multiplier.
        * Gifted [[Buoyant Bee]] gives a x1.2 Capacity Multiplier.

        ===Beequips===
        There are a handful of [[beequip]]s that add a static amount of Capacity or give a Capacity
        Multiplier.

        * A {{Cost|Beret|||0}} gives from +6,000 Capacity up to +13,500 Capacity.
        * A {{Cost|Single Mitten|||0}} gives from +7,500 Capacity up to +10,000 Capacity. Using
        [[waxes]] can increase the amount up to +15,000 Capacity.
        * A {{Cost|Warm Scarf|||0}} can give from +1% Capacity to +10% Capacity from waxing.
        * A {{Cost|Beesmas Tree Hat|||0}} gives from +10,000 Capacity up to +25,000 Capacity. Using
        waxes can increase the amount up to +35,000 Capacity.
        * The {{Cost|Reindeer Antlers|||0}} can give from +1% Capacity to +20% Capacity from waxing.
        * The {{Cost|Festive Wreath|||0}} gives from +5,000 Capacity up to +10,000 Capacity. Using
        waxes can increase the amount up to +110,000 Capacity.

        ===Passives===
        There are two [[Passive Abilities|passives]] that give a Capacity Multiplier. They can be
        obtained via a Diamond Star Amulet or Supreme Star Amulet. The Guiding Star Aura could also
        be obtained from [[Onett's Lid Art]] during Beesmas.

        * The Guiding Star passive gives a x1.25 Capacity Multiplier.
        ** The Guiding Star Aura gives x2.5 Capacity to the player who summoned the star and x1.25
        Capacity to other players.
        * The Star Shower passive give a x1.25 Capacity Multiplier.

        ===Buffs===
        There are several ways to get a buff to capacity such as inventory items, [[Ability
        Tokens|ability tokens]], defeating [[mobs]], and redeeming [[codes]].

        * Using a {{Cost|Purple Potion|||0}} gives a x1.25 Capacity Multiplier. The buff can also be
        given from certain codes.
        * Using a {{Cost|Super Smoothie|||0}} gives a x1.5 Capacity Multiplier. The buff can also be
        given from certain codes.
        * Using a {{Cost|Marshmallow Bee|||0}} gives +50% Capacity. The buff can also be given from
        certain codes.
        * Using a {{Cost|Snowflake|||0}} or collecting the [[Ability Tokens#Snowglobe
        Shake|Snowglobe Shake]] ability token from the {{Cost|Snowglobe|||0}} Beequip gives a x1.25
        Capacity Multiplier.
        * Using a {{Cost|Glitched Drive|||0}} during the [[Robo Bear Challenge]] gives a x1.25
        Capacity Multiplier.
        * Making combos with [[Ability Tokens#Fetch|Reindeer Fetch]] from [[Puppy Bee]] with the
        {{Cost|Reindeer Antlers}} Beequip equipped can give from a x1.01 Capacity Multiplier up to a
        x1.1 Capacity Multiplier.
        * Defeating the [[Mondo Chick]] for the Mondo Chick Blessing can give from a x1.02 Capacity
        Multiplier up to a x1.2 Capacity Multiplier.
        * Balloon Blessing can give from a x1.03 capacity multiplier up to a x4 Capacity Multiplier.
        * Galentine's Blessing (Beesmas only) gives +50% Capacity.
        * Beesmas Repentance (Beesmas only) gives a x1.05 Capacity Multiplier.
        * Defeating [[Festive Nymph]]s during Beesmas will guarantee a Festive Nymph Blessing token
        drop which can stack up to give from +1,000 Capacity up to +250,000 Capacity. The buff can
        also be given from certain codes.
        * Defeating Party Robots or completing a [[Robo Party]] during Beesmas can give Robo Party
        Blessing which can stack up to give from +1,000 Capacity up to +100,000 Capacity. The buff
        can also be given from certain codes.
        * The Guiding Star Aura gives x2.5 Capacity to the player who summoned the star and x1.25
        Capacity to other players.
        * The Capacity Code buff, which is given by certain codes, gives a x2 Capacity Multiplier.
        ===Robo Bear Challenge Upgrades===
        There are several upgrades that can be bought with [[cog]]s during the Robo Bear Challenge
        which can upgrade Capacity.

        * Common Upgrades:
        ** Defragment: This upgrades starts at x1.5 Capacity and maxes at 10 upgrades with x6
        Capacity.
        * Rare Upgrades:
        ** Expansion: This upgrades starts at x1.25 Capacity and maxes at 100 upgrades with x25
        Capacity.
        ** RAM: This upgrades starts at +75,000 Capacity and maxes at 10 upgrades with +750,000
        Capacity.
        * Epic Upgrades:
        ** HDD: This upgrade gives x3 Capacity.
        * Legendary Upgrades:
        ** Optimize: This upgrades starts at x1.1 Capacity and maxes at 25 upgrades with x2.5
        Capacity.
        ** The Cloud: This upgrades starts at x2.5 Capacity and maxes at 3 upgrades with x5.5
        Capacity.

        ===Badges===
        There are a handful of [[badges]] that add a static amount of capacity or give a Capacity
        Multiplier.

        * The [[Quests|Quest]] Badge gives a Capacity Multiplier depending on the badge rank.
        ** The Cadet Quest Badge gives a x1.25 Capacity Multiplier.
        ** The Hotshot Quest Badge gives a x1.5 Capacity Multiplier.
        ** The Ace Quest Badge gives a x1.75 Capacity Multiplier.
        ** The Master Quest Badge gives a x2 Capacity Multiplier.
        ** The Grandmaster Quest Badge gives a x2.25 Capacity Multiplier.
        * The Ability Badge can give up to +250,000 Capacity depending on the badge rank.
        ** The Cadet Ability Badge gives +25,000 Capacity.
        ** The Hotshot Ability Badge gives +50,000 Capacity.
        ** The Ace Ability Badge gives +100,000 Capacity.
        ** The Master Ability Badge gives +150,000 Capacity.
        ** The Grandmaster Ability Badge gives +250,000 Capacity.
        * The [[Sunflower Field|Sunflower]] Badge gives a Capacity Multiplier depending on the badge
        rank.
        ** The Cadet Sunflower Badge gives +10% Capacity.
        ** The Hotshot Sunflower Badge gives +20% Capacity.
        ** The Ace Sunflower Badge gives +30% Capacity.
        ** The Master Sunflower Badge gives +40% Capacity.
        ** The Grandmaster Sunflower Badge gives +50% Capacity.

        ==Downgrades==
        There is a debuff that reduces the Capacity of the player.

        * The Robo Bear Challenge Debuff reduces the Capacity by a depending on what round the
        player is on in the Robo Bear Challenge. At Round 1, it has a x0.1 Capacity Multiplier and
        increases by 0.015 per round to a total of a x0.46 Capacity Multiplier.
        There are certain upgrades that can be bought with cogs during the Robo Bear Challenge which
        can downgrade Capacity.

        * Common Upgrades:
        ** Overfit: Red: This upgrades starts at x0.9 Capacity and maxes at 10 upgrades with x0.5
        Capacity.
        ** Sharpen: This upgrades starts at x0.95 Capacity and maxes at 10 upgrades with x0.75
        Capacity.
        * Rare Upgrades:
        ** Crypto: This upgrades starts at x0.85 Capacity and maxes at 3 upgrades with x0.55
        Capacity.
        * Epic Upgrades:
        ** Malware: This upgrades starts at x0.75 Capacity and maxes at 3 upgrades with x0.25
        Capacity.
        * Legendary Upgrades:
        ** Overclock: This upgrade gives x0.75 Capacity.
