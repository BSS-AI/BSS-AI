        == Trivia ==
        * This bee and [[Carpenter Bee]] are the only Legendary bees added in an [[Updates|Update]].
        * It has a tail that symbolizes a quaver, also known as an eighth note.
        ** In musical notation terms, its face is a slur between an eighth note and a dotted quarter
        note.
        ** These are also known as a quaver and a dotted crotchet.
        * Music bee is the only legendary bee with 3 abilities.
        * Despite it being colorless, it favors blueberries.
        ** This makes it the only non-[[:Category:Blue|Blue]] bee to like blueberries.
        **Music Bee and [[Shocked Bee]] have the same skin pattern.
        * This is the only bee that generates a melody token without beequips. However, some bees
        can generate a melody token if they have the [[Toy Horn|toy horn]] or [[Kazoo]] beequips
        equipped to them.
        * A [[Royal Jelly#Specific Bee Jelly|Special Jelly]] that changes a bee into a Music Bee
        could be obtained by opening the [[Ornament Presents|Funky Present]] during the
        [[Updates#2019.12.23|Beesmas 2019 event]]. However, as the event has ended, the jelly was
        unobtainable until the Beesmas 2020 event where it could be purchased in the Beesmas Tree
        Bundle along with 5 [[Festive Bean]]s, and a [[Beesmas Tree Hat]] [[Beequip]].
