        {{Color|Caustic Wax}} is an [[:Category:Inventory|inventory item]] and a type of
        [[Waxes|wax]] added in the [[Updates#2021.12.26|12/26/2021 update]] that can be used on a
        [[beequip]] to boost its stats. Upon usage, it has a 25% chance of greatly improving the
        beequip's stats; however, it has a 75% chance of permanently destroying the beequip instead,
        destroying the beequip and also wasting the Caustic Wax. This wax cannot be used on
        permanent beequips.

        It also can be used to craft a variety of different items, such as [[planter]]s and
        [[tools]].

        When successfully applying Caustic Wax to a bee's beequip, a message pops up saying,
        \"<span style=\"background-color:#2866a8;color:white\">The Wax improved the
        Beequip!</span>”.

        If the wax fails to upgrade the beequip, a message will pop up saying, \"<span
        style=\"background-color:#a61e19;color:white\">The Wax has Destroyed the
        Beequip...</span>\".

        == Trivia ==
        * In order to craft this item, a total of {{Cost|Honeysuckle|75}}, {{Cost|Sunflower
        Seed|750}}, {{Cost|Pineapple|1,750}}, {{Cost|Bitterberry|190}}, {{Cost|Neonberry|25}} and
        {{Cost|Royal Jelly|5,252}} are needed.
        * The probability of Caustic Wax improving a beequip 5 times in a row is 1/1024 or ~0.1%.
        Because of its low chances and its ability to destroy the beequip, it is not recommended to
        try and use 5 Caustic Waxes on a beequip when you are not prepared yet.
        * This is the only wax that cannot be used on permanent beequips.
        * This is the only wax that can destroy beequips.
