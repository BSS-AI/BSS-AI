        ==Trivia==

        *This and the [[Porcelain Port-O-Hive]] is the only container in the game that requires
        [[Crafting|Materials]] other than honey to craft it.
        **If its items were to be crafted using the [[Blender]], it would cost 25 Billion Honey,
        1650 coconuts, 15000 [[Pineapple|Pineapples]], 15000 [[Sunflower Seed|Sunflower Seeds]],
        7500 [[Strawberry|Strawberries]], 7500 [[Blueberry|Blueberries]], and 9000 [[Royal
        Jelly|Royal Jellies]] to craft. It would also take 87.5 hours of Playtime to craft the
        Materials, or 1050 [[Ticket]]s to speed up crafting.
        *As of now, this is the 11th most expensive item in-game in terms of honey (excluding [[Hive
        Slot]]s), with the [[The Supreme Saturator|Supreme Saturator]] (55.555 billion), the [[Gummy
        Boots]] (100 billion), the [[Heat-Treated Planter]] (750 billion), the [[Hydroponic
        Planter]] (750 billion), the [[Dark Scythe]] (2.5 trillion), the [[Tide Popper]] (2.5
        trillion), the [[Petal Planter]] (5 trillion), the [[Coconut Belt]] (7.5 trillion), the
        [[Gummyballer]] (10 trillion), and [[The Planter Of Plenty]] (100 trillion) being more
        expensive.
        *This is the only container that gives [[Passive Abilities]].
        **This is the only item in the game that gives 2 passives without having to buy another
        [[Items|Item]].
        *The red and blue \"buttons\" on each side of the canister repeatedly glow, alternating to
        represent the lights on ambulances. This was likely added to fit with the passive Emergency
        Coconut Shield, which also gives off a siren sound upon activation.
        **Note that the player must take damage directly from a mob to activate Emergency Coconut
        Shield. For example, taking damage from the fire trail of [[Ants#Fire Ant|Fire Ants]], from
        the lawn mower or dying from an [[Obstacle Courses|Obby]] does not activate this ability.
        **When Emergency Coconut Shield is activated, it does not protect you from the 'Twigs'
        ability from [[Stick Bug]].
        **There is a rare bug that makes it when the Emergency Coconut Shield is activated when you
        should've died, you will have a very small amount of health instead.
        **The buttons' flashing alternation is synced with every player who has the item equipped.
