        The '''Red HQ''' is a large, red building with a portal, a shop, and other features. It is
        located next to the [[Rose Field]] and near the [[Special Sprout Summoner]]. It has a
        [[Riley Bee]] model above the entrance. To enter, the player must have discovered 4
        different red [[Bees|bee]] types. The Red HQ is the counterpart to the [[Blue HQ]].

        On the roof, there is an enlarged [[Scythe]]. Unlike the actual Scythe, the one on the Red
        HQ doesn't emit flame particles.

        == Interior ==
        On the first floor, there are five things that can be interacted with:
        * A [[Red Teleporter]] that can teleport the player to the rock behind [[Science Bear]];
        * The [[Red Field Booster]] that gives a 15-minute boost for either the [[Mushroom Field]],
        [[Strawberry Field]], [[Rose Field]], or the [[Pepper Patch]];
        * The [[All-Time Top Red Collectors]] and [[Daily Top Red Collectors]] [[leaderboards]],
        which show the top 100 players who have collected the most red pollen and the top 100
        players who have collected the most red pollen in a day respectively;
        * A set of red [[items]] for sale. (See the 'Store' section below.)

        There is a [[ticket]] token hidden near the back of the ceiling visible from the ladder.
        There are 3 possible ways to reach it:
        * Climb the Red Teleporter and jump/glide onto the token.
        * From outside the HQ, get on top of the All-Time Top Red Collectors leaderboard and then
        jump to the token.
        ** If it's too difficult to land on the leaderboard, the player may need to glide to the
        token instead.
        The player may need boots and/or the parachute/glider for some of these.

        The player can climb two ladders to reach the other sections of the HQ. The first ladder
        leads to a [[Royal Jelly|royal jelly]] token on the roof next to the base, as well as the
        [[Strawberry Dispenser]]. The second ladder leads to the top, where the player will find the
        [[Top Riley Bee Helpers]] Leaderboard and [[Gifted Riley Bee]], who requires a
        [[translator]] to be able to be communicated with. After the player gets a translator from
        Science Bear and uses it on Gifted Riley Bee, they can complete [[quests]] for them. The
        player will also find the [[Codes|code]] \"ROOF\" on the back side of the white facade, near
        the large Scythe.

        == Store ==
        There is a store in the Red HQ that sells nine [[items]]. To open the store, press E when
        ''not'' on the Red Teleporter, the Red Field Booster, or the Leaderboards.

        ==Trivia ==
        *[[Rogue Vicious Bee]] can target the player if they are close enough to the wall, next to
        the Rose Field.
        *The Dark Scythe is currently the most expensive item able to be bought in the Red HQ.
        *After the [[Updates#2019.04.05|4/5/2019 update]], a glass roof was added, preventing
        players from jumping out from the side and into the Rose Field. As of the
        [[Updates#2019.09.28|9/28/2019 update]], the roof is no longer there, due to a new mechanic
        that teleported players back to spawn if they entered a restricted area.
        **Bees (including Gifted Riley Bee) had no wings when viewed through the roof. This was
        because transparent parts seen through glass materials do not display properly, similar to
        the glass of the [[Porcelain Dipper]].
        *Moving into the red field booster just as the shop was opened would cause the
        [[Glitches#Shop Trap|Shop Trap glitch]].
        **The only way to reverse this is to disconnect and rejoin the game.
        *If the player uses Shift-Lock, they have the ability to look through the white wall behind
        the Dark Scythe, revealing a pad with the number 250 on it.
        *There is a [[Loaded Dice|loaded dice]] token on the far end of the roof near [[Robo Bear]]
        which the player can obtain by jumping on top of the [[Top Riley Bee Helpers]], then jumping
        onto the roof, and then walk/glide to the token.
        **Before Beesmas 2022, this token was on a rock next to the roof that the player could climb
        using Shift-Lock.
        *The Red HQ is also known as the \"Red Clubhouse.\" If the player does not have 4 red bee
        types discovered, the top of their screen will say, \"Discover 4 Red Bee types to enter the
        '''Red Clubhouse'''.\"
