        A {{Color|Strawberry}} is a type of [[Treats|Treat]] that can be fed to a [[Bees|bee]] to
        raise its bond by 25. However, when fed to a [[Rascal Bee|Rascal]], [[Rad Bee|Rad]], [[Rage
        Bee|Rage]], [[Riley Bee|Riley]], [[Baby Bee|Baby]], [[Stubborn Bee|Stubborn]], [[Shy
        Bee|Shy]], or [[Spicy Bee|Spicy]] Bee, the amount of [[bond]] they gain is doubled. These
        bees also have a small chance of becoming gifted when fed strawberries.

        == Ways to Obtain ==
        * There is a strawberry token hidden behind the largest strawberry in the corner of
        [[Strawberry Field]].
        * From using the [[Strawberry Dispenser]] near the [[Red HQ]].
        * As a reward from the [[Ant Challenge]].
        * Redeeming certain valid [[codes]]:
        ** 1MLikes (Gives 1 strawberry + other stuff).
        ** 15MMembers (Gives 15 strawberries + other stuff).
        * As a reward from [[Memory Match|memory matches]].
        * As a stack reward for adding certain [[stickers]] to the [[Sticker Stack]]:
        ** {{Cost|Pink Cupcake Sticker|||0}} rewards 50 strawberries.
        ** {{Cost|Pale Heart Sticker|||0}} rewards 50 strawberries.

        === Drops ===
        * Farming in the [[Mushroom Field]], [[Strawberry Field]], [[Rose Field]], [[Cactus Field]],
        and the [[Pepper Patch]].
        ** Harvesting [[Leaves]] in these fields may also yield strawberries.
        * As a drop from [[Ladybug|Ladybugs]], [[Scorpion|Scorpions]], and [[King Beetle]].
        * As a drop from a [[Ability Tokens#Festive Gift|Festive Gift]] or [[Ability Tokens#Festive
        Mark|Festive Mark]].
        * As a gift from a [[Cub Buddy|cub buddy]].
        * As a reward for completely growing any kind of [[Sprout]] except for Moon, Gummy, and
        Debug sprouts.
        ** Planting a sprout in the [[Mushroom Field]], [[Rose Field]], and [[Pepper Patch]] yields
        4x more strawberries than other fields. Planting a sprout in the [[Strawberry Field]] yields
        8x more strawberries than other fields.
        * Harvesting [[Sparkles]] (Higher chances in red fields).
        * As a drop from [[Puffshroom]]s in red fields or fields with high amounts of red flowers
        ([[Clover Field]], [[Cactus Field]], and [[Mountain Top Field]]).
        * As a drop from planters (besides {{Cost|Tacky Planter|||0}}, {{Cost|Blue Clay
        Planter|||0}} and {{Cost|The Planter Of Plenty|||0}}), most likely in red fields.
        ** The {{Cost|Red Clay Planter|||0}} Always drop strawberries upon harvest at full growth.
