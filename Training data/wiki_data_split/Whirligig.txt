        {{Color|Whirligig|Whirligigs}} are inventory [[items]] added in the
        [[Updates#2021.12.26|Beesmas 2021 update]]. Using one simply teleports the player and their
        [[bees]] back to their [[hive]], aswell as temporarily increasing Convert Rate.

        Whirligigs have a limit of 100. Obtaining any while having more than 100 whirligigs will not
        add them to the player's inventory, unless they are obtained through [[quests]] or
        [[codes]].

        Attempting to use a Whirligig without claiming a hive will prompt the message: \"You must
        claim a hive to use a Whirligig.\"

        ==Ways to Obtain==

        * A common drop from the [[Robo Bear Challenge]].
        * A reward from [[Retro Swarm Challenge]]
        * As a rare drop from [[mobs]] such as [[Rhino Beetle]]s and [[Mantis]]es.
        * As a drop from certain [[Planter]]s.
        ** Planters harvested in [[Pine Tree Forest]] have a higher chance to drop whirligigs than
        other [[fields]]. [[Petal Planter]] gives the most whirligigs, although [[Blue Clay
        Planter]] has the highest chance of dropping them.
        * As a Stack Reward for adding certain [[stickers]] to the [[Sticker Stack]]:
        ** The Launching Rocket rewards 10 whirligigs.
        ** The Simple Mountain rewards 10 whirligigs.

        == Trivia ==
        *Using a whirligig during an [[Ant Challenge]] will teleport the player to the starting
        platform after teleporting to the hive, thus putting an end to the challenge.
        *This is also one of the ways to exit the [[White Tunnel]] other than defeating or being
        killed by [[Tunnel Bear]], leaving and rejoining the game, and using the gray plate at the
        middle of the tunnel.
        *They are based on maple tree seeds (nicknamed whirligigs), which, when falling, spin, thus
        traveling further from the tree. This may have inspired the whirligig's mechanic of
        teleporting or rather \"carrying\" you back to your hive.
        *Before the [[Updates|12/26/22]] update, Whirligigs had a cooldown of 5 minutes, a minor
        change compared to the current 3 minute cooldown.
        *If the player is lagging, the whirligig may only teleport the player's bees.
