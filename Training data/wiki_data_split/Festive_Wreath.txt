        It has an equip limit of one. It has a red color requirement. It can only be equipped to
        [[Festive Bee]].

        ==Trivia==
        *The Festive Wreath and the [[Reindeer Antlers]] share many similarities:
        **This and the Reindeer Antlers are the only permanent beequips in the game.
        ***This is the second permanent beequip added to the game, preceded by the Reindeer Antlers.
        **The two are the only beequips to have models visible on the bee that they are equipped to.
        **They are the only beequips to have a set potential, with this beequip always having max
        potential and the Reindeer Antlers always having a potential of 3.
        **They are the only beequips that cannot be discarded.
        **[[Caustic Wax]] is unable to be used on these two beequips.
        *A model of a Festive Bee with the Festive Wreath can be found outside the map, next to a
        model of a [[Puppy Bee]] with Reindeer Antlers.
        *This is the one of three beequips to give a unique ability.
        **The other two are [[Snowglobe]] and Reindeer Antlers.
        *The model of the Festive Wreath on Festive Bee is the
        [https://www.roblox.com/catalog/191097654/Deluxe-Christmas-Wreath Deluxe Christmas Wreath]
        Roblox accessory.
