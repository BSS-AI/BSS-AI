        A {{Color|Treat}} is a type of [[Treats|Treat]] that can be fed to a [[Bees|bee]] to raise
        its bond by 10. They are the easiest treat to obtain but are not the favorite treat of any
        bee. They can also be used to craft the [[Honey Mask]] where 9,999 '''treats''' are needed.
        [[File:Treat token.png|thumb|A treat token.]]
        == Ways to Obtain ==
        * Using the [[Treat Dispenser]] near the [[Pro Shop]]/[[Pineapple Patch]] (Like the [[Honey
        Dispenser]], this requires being a member of the
        [https://www.roblox.com/groups/3982592/Bee-Swarm-Simulator-Club#!/about BSS Club]).
        * Buying from the Treat Shop next to under Mother Bear's awning for {{Cost|Honey|100}} per
        treat and capping out at {{Cost|Honey|10,000}} per treat.
        * Redeeming certain valid [[codes]]:
        ** 1MLikes (Gives 1 treat + other stuff).
        * As a reward from [[Memory Match|memory matches]].
        * As a stack reward for adding certain [[stickers]] to the [[Sticker Stack]]:
        ** The Fork And Knife rewards 500 treats.

        === Drops ===
        * Farming in the [[Dandelion Field|Dandelion]], [[Mushroom Field|Mushroom]], [[Clover
        Field|Clover]], [[Spider Field|Spider]], [[Cactus Field|Cactus]], [[Pumpkin Patch|Pumpkin]],
        [[Coconut Field|Coconut]], or [[Stump Field|Stump]] Fields.
        ** Drops from [[leaves]] in these fields may also yield treats.
        * A relatively high chance of dropping from [[Rhino Beetle|rhino beetles]], [[ladybug]]s,
        [[spider]]s, [[mantis]]es, [[scorpion]]s, [[Werewolf|werewolves]], [[aphid]]s, [[Stick
        Bug]], [[Stick Nymph]]s, [[Tunnel Bear]], [[King Beetle]], and [[Stump Snail|stump snail]].
        * A guaranteed drop from [[Mondo Chick]]s.
        * 2nd and 3rd capture of [[Commando Chick]] rewards 2 and 5 treats respectively.
        * \"Playing\" [[Ability Tokens#Fetch|Fetch]] with [[Puppy Bee]].
        * As a drop from a [[Ability Tokens#Festive Gift|Festive Gift]] or [[Ability Tokens#Festive
        Mark|Festive Mark]].
        * As a gift from a [[Cub Buddy|cub buddy]].
        * As a reward for completely growing any kind of [[sprout]] except for Gummy and Debug
        sprouts.
        * Harvesting [[sparkles]].
        * As a drop from [[Puffshroom]]s.
        * As a drop from Planters, which include:
        ** {{Cost|Plastic Planter}}
        ** {{Cost|Candy Planter}}
        ** {{Cost|Red Clay Planter}}
        ** {{Cost|Tacky Planter|}}

        === Quests ===
        *Black Bear gives treats on the following quests:
        **Get Red-y gives 100 treats.
        **One Stop On The Tip Top gives 200 treats.
        **Blue Mushrooms 2 gives 300 treats.
        **Pretty Pumpkins gives 400 treats.
        **Black Bear, Why? gives 500 treats.
        **Bee A Star gives 1,000 treats.
        **The [[Egg#Mythic_Egg|Mythic Egg]] questline gives treats on all quests, at a sum of
        195,500 treats.
        **Black Bear's repeatable quests also reward treats.
        *[[Science Bear]] gives treats on the following quests:
        **Mark Mechanics gives 3,000 treats.
        **Epistemological Endeavor gives 10,000 treats.
        *All of Mother Bear's quests give treats, 4,020 treats on total.
        *All of Polar Bear's quests give 10 treats, 180 treats on total.
        *All of Onett's quests give treats, 25,000 treats on total.
        *Every 5th completion of Honey Bee's quest may give treats.
