        '''Tunnel Bear''' is a hostile level 9 [[:Category:Bosses|Boss]] Mob.

        Tunnel Bear spawns in the [[White Tunnel]]. The entrance to the White Tunnel sits underneath
        the [[Instant Converter]] near [[Science Bear]].

        Tunnel Bear respawns every 48 hours (2 days), but it can instead respawn every 40 hours and
        48 minutes (1.7 days) if [[Gifted Bee|Gifted]] [[Vicious Bee]] is in the player's hive.

        == The White Tunnel ==
        {{Main|White Tunnel}}
        To enter the White Tunnel, go to the Instant Converter near the [[Pineapple Patch]]. Players
        can either jump through the chimney on the top or go in the right end of the Instant
        Converter and then drop down into the chute. Alternatively, players can climb up the hill to
        the [[Yellow Cannon]]/Science Bear and jump down from there.

        Upon entering the White Tunnel, players will land in a small entryway with two green walls
        and two white walls. One of the white walls looks semi-transparent. Go through the
        semi-transparent white wall to find the tunnel. You have to move a little bit after going
        through the white wall as Tunnel Bear will quickly drop down through the roof near the
        entrance. The walls will also gradually get narrower, making it harder to turn back as
        players head deeper into the White Tunnel.

        If Tunnel Bear does not appear and instead a countdown timer floats in mid-air, then that
        means the player will need to wait for the countdown on the respawn timer to finish before
        battling Tunnel Bear again.

        To exit the White Tunnel, stand on top of the Teleport Pad and use it. The Teleport Pad will
        teleport the player on top of the Instant Converter near the [[Sunflower Field]]. The
        Teleport Pad looks like a flat gray circle that sits in the middle of the White Tunnel.
        ==Basic Strategies==
        Tunnel Bear possesses 10,000 health and can instantly kill players if they touch/get too
        close to Tunnel Bear (\"Too close\" is not the same as \"touching,\" as the hitbox is bigger
        than its body). However, Tunnel Bear moves very slowly at a constant speed without ever
        speeding up, and on a very predictable path as it heads straight towards the player's
        location. Because of this, players could follow the recommended strategy of walking in front
        of Tunnel Bear and staying just close enough for their bees to keep attacking while leading
        it down the tunnel.

        When entering the tunnel, keep Tunnel Bear near the beginning of the White Tunnel and keep
        close to one of the walls on the sides (this will only slow the bear a little bit because
        now he has no collisions), and occasionally jump around Tunnel Bear. This alternative will
        only work at the beginning (before the exit) because the White Tunnel narrows down the
        further players go down it, trapping them if they travel too far in. However, this should be
        the chosen strategy for the first defeat as to ensure getting the gifted egg. If the
        player's bees are mostly level 9, they will not have to jump around it, as their bees will
        most likely kill it first.

        When Tunnel Bear spawns, players will only get 2 minutes and 30 seconds to defeat it. The
        timer is directly above its head, showing how much time remains for players to defeat it
        until it despawns. Note that it takes Tunnel Bear less than two and a half minutes to
        traverse the entire length of the White Tunnel.

        If the player defeats Tunnel Bear too late, some items may appear outside of the tunnel past
        the winking eye face. This isn't an issue however, as Tunnel Bear's tokens automatically
        collect before they despawn.

        If needed, the player can pre-activate [[Ability Tokens#Rage|Rage]], [[Ability
        Tokens#Focus|Focus]] and precision using the [[Pineapple Patch]] enemies or [[Stump Snail]]
        in the [[Stump Field]] to get certain bees to spawn the tokens.

        ==Tips to Defeat==
        *'''Own bees with a high [[Bee Attack|attack]] stat.''' Bees such as [[Cobalt Bee]], [[Brave
        Bee]], [[Crimson Bee]], [[Demon Bee]], [[Vicious Bee]], [[Lion Bee]], [[Riley Bee]], [[Bucko
        Bee]], [[Precise Bee]], [[Spicy Bee]], and [[Rage Bee]] deal higher amounts of damage than
        other [[bees]], making them more useful when fighting Tunnel Bear (or any other [[mob]]).
        *'''Own bees that boost fighting stats.''' Some specific bees will raise the effectiveness
        of the entire [[hive]], either by boosting Attack ([[Gifted Bee|Gifted]] Rage Bee, Gifted
        Brave Bee or Gifted Lion Bee), by boosting [[Critical Hits|Critical Chance]] (Looker Bee,
        [[Music Bee]] or Gifted [[Commander Bee]]), by boosting [[Critical Hits|Critical Power]]
        (Gifted [[Looker Bee]], Gifted [[Tabby Bee]], or Music Bee) or by boosting [[Critical
        Hits#Super-Crit|Super-Crit]] Chance and Power (Gifted Precise Bee).
        *'''Own bees that create [[Ability Tokens#Token Link|Token Link]] tokens.''' [[Music Bee]],
        [[Rage Bee]], [[Exhausted Bee]], and [[Shocked Bee]] possess the Token Link ability.
        Equipping the [[Paper Angel]] [[beequip]] on a valid bee teaches it Token Link as well. If a
        token appears too close to Tunnel Bear, use the Token Link instead of going back to get said
        token. That way, the player will not risk dying to Tunnel Bear.
        *'''Have high [[energy]] bees.''' Bees such as [[Photon Bee]] and [[Exhausted Bee]] have
        endless energy, and [[Bumble Bee]], [[Shy Bee]], Lion Bee, [[Buoyant Bee]], [[Fuzzy Bee]],
        Precise Bee, [[Vector Bee]], [[Gummy Bee]], and [[Vicious Bee]] have at least double the
        energy of a typical bee. If the player's bees have low energy, then their bees will most
        likely go to sleep after just a few attacks. One method to refill essential bees' energy
        levels is to fight mobs until they go to sleep and recharge, as this will increase the
        player's odds of defeating Tunnel Bear faster. The player can also use the [[Glitches#Sleepy
        Bees|sleep glitch]] so their bees start with their max energy. Completing [[Polar Bear]]’s
        quests can also help increase bee energy.
        *'''[[Bond|Level]] up bees to Level 9 or higher.''' Bees with a lower level than the mob
        will most likely miss when they attack. Bees with levels much lower than the level of the
        mob will have a higher chance of missing their attacks on the mob. Bees with an equal Level
        or higher Level than the mob will never miss it. During Beesmas 2020 and 2021, bees summoned
        when gandering at [[Onett's Lid Art]], using [[Honeyday Candles]], or using the [[Gummy
        Siege]] might be able to help defeat Tunnel Bear.
        *'''Stay logical.''' If players know that they cannot beat Tunnel Bear with the length of
        the tunnel, or if they keep dying, they should not keep trying to continue battling it.
        Instead, they should focus on getting better bees and equipment that can help them defeat
        it. The player should also wait until they can defeat it fast enough before it reaches the
        end of the tunnel since the tokens can despawn outside of the tunnel.
        *'''Move a distance from the start of the White Tunnel.''' Tunnel Bear can drop down at
        unexpected moments, instantly crushing players upon impact. If they make sure to keep a safe
        distance, Tunnel Bear will instantly start walking toward the player once he gets down.
        *'''When Tunnel Bear gets to low health, do not do risky actions.''' Whenever Tunnel Bear
        gets to low health, DO NOT attempt to go for [[Ability Tokens|tokens]], such as rage tokens
        that sit near Tunnel Bear. Just keep going until it is defeated.
        * '''Keep a safe distance away from the Tunnel Bear.''' Players often die because they do
        not keep their distance. The farther players stay away from Tunnel Bear, the more space they
        get to collect tokens, but they should not stay out of the range that bees attack within, or
        the bees will stop fighting and just follow the player.
        *'''Know a method.''' If players decide to do the turn around method, they should remember
        to avoid going past the Teleport Pad or back into the drop location since the Teleport Pad
        will prevent players from successfully turning around, while the entryway will cause Tunnel
        Bear to despawn.
        *'''Own a [[Cub Buddy]].''' Cub Buddies can help by collecting tokens, so owning a Cub Buddy
        can let the tokens that sit behind Tunnel Bear get collected in order to make the battle
        easier. Players can also use the alternative strategy, though it's riskier.
        *'''Use [[stinger]]s.''' This is not recommended, as players should save them for Vicious
        Bee, but stingers can help to get the last bit of damage needed before Tunnel Bear despawns.
        *'''Have a Vicious Bee.''' Having a Vicious Bee is helpful, as its [[Ability
        Tokens#Impale|Impale]] can deal massive amounts of damage to Tunnel Bear.
        *'''Equipping the [[Fire Mask]] or [[Demon Mask]].''' Both [[:Category:Hats|masks]] boosts
        the player's [[Bee Attack|bees' attack]].
        *'''Get attack [[mutation]]s.''' Players can either feed [[Treat#Atomic Treat|atomic
        treats]] to guarantee a mutation, or feed [[Treat#Neonberry|neonberries]] first to make it
        [[Radiation|radioactive]], and then use [[Treat#Bitterberry|bitterberries]] to have a higher
        chance of mutating a bee.
        *'''Empty your bag.''' If the player is going to try and defeat it, there's a chance they
        might die and lose their pollen. The player should empty their bag before attempting to
        defeat Tunnel Bear as a precaution.
        *'''Let your bees tell you how far to stay from Tunnel Bear.''' When the player's bees are
        far enough away to head back to the player, they should move a bit closer. The bees can help
        determine how far away the player is from collecting tokens.
        *'''Abuse a glitch method.''' When Tunnel Bear drops down into the tunnel, purposely walk to
        the left side of the tunnel. When Tunnel Bear attempts to follow you, his arm will clip into
        the wall, and as a result he will become stuck. This makes it extremely easy to defeat
        Tunnel Bear.

        length.
