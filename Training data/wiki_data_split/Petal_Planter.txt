        The '''Petal Planter''' is a reusable [[planter]] added in the [[Updates|12/26/2021
        update]], it grows in about 14 hours and stores 100 billion [[Pollen]]. When harvested, it
        grants bonus Jelly Beans, Field Dice, and Glitter. It can be purchased at the [[Petal
        Shop]].

        The planter grows 50% faster in [[fields]] with white flowers, and grants 33% bonus white
        pollen on harvest. Colorless bees are 50% more likely to sip [[nectar]] from this planter,
        and it grants 50% more Comforting and Satisfying nectar. Harvesting a fully grown Petal
        Planter has a 1/5 (20%) chance to spawn a [[puffshroom]].

        ==Trivia==

        *This is the fourth most expensive item in the game in terms of honey, only behind the
        [[Coconut Belt]], which costs 7.5 trillion honey, the [[Gummyballer]], which costs 10
        trillion honey, and [[The Planter Of Plenty]], which costs 100 trillion honey.
        *Its red counterpart is the [[Heat-Treated Planter]] and its blue counterpart is the
        [[Hydroponic Planter]].
        *The Petal Planter is currently the only item in the Petal Shop not to require a [[Spirit
        Petal]].
        *It is the only item in the Petal Shop added after the shop was added.
        * If the ingredients for item were to be crafted, it will cost 5,000,000,000,000 Honey, 425
        Magic Beans, 346,875 [[Strawberry|Strawberries]], 346,875 [[Blueberry|Blueberries]], 426,250
        [[Pineapple|Pineapples]], 72,500 Sunflower Seeds, 1,200 [[Neonberry|Neonberries]], 4,950
        [[Bitterberry|Bitterberries]], 5,650 Honeysuckles, 1,500 [[Coconut]]s, and 97,990 Royal
        Jellies.
        * This is currently one of the five reusable planters to not be sold in [[Dapper Bear's
        Shop]], the others being the [[Red Clay Planter]], [[Blue Clay Planter]], [[Hydroponic
        Planter]], and the [[Heat-Treated Planter]].
        ** Of those, this is the only planter to not be sold in a headquarters shop.
