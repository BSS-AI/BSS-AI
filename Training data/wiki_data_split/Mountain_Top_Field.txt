        The [[Mountain Top Field]] is a [[Fields|field]] located behind the [[Lion Bee Gate]]. The
        [[Mondo Chick]] protects this field and respawns every hour.

        This is a 24×28 field, containing 672 [[flowers]]. The flowers are red and blue, and the
        natural size is triple.

        This field can spawn [[Ability Tokens#Boost|Red Boost]], [[Ability Tokens#Boost|Blue
        Boost]], [[Honey]], [[Magic Bean]], [[Ticket]], [[Bitterberry|Bitterberry]], and
        [[Neonberry|Neonberry]] Tokens while collecting pollen.

        [[Bees]] that like the Mountain Top Field are [[Basic Bee]], [[Looker Bee]], [[Frosty Bee]],
        [[Honey Bee]], [[Precise Bee]], [[Buoyant Bee]], [[Digital Bee]], [[Festive Bee]] and
        [[Gummy Bee]]. Bees that dislike the Mountain Top Field are [[Carpenter Bee]] and [[Demon
        Bee]].

        This field gives Invigorating [[Nectar]] when a planter is placed here.

        A level 6-8 [[Rogue Vicious Bee]] can spawn here.

        A level 1-20 [[Wild Windy Bee]] can spawn here.

        ==Trivia==
        *This is the only field that doesn't produce [[treats]] from farming. However, harvesting
        [[sprout]]s in the Mountain Top Field will still drop normal rewards.
        *Leaves in this field are more rare, and always either spawn bitterberries, [[aphid]]s, or a
        [[Sticker]].
        *This is the only field past the Lion Bee Gate.
        *This was the only field that wasn't targeted by the [[Gummy Cannon]] during the
        [[:Category:Gummy Invasion|Gummy Invasion]] event. (The [[Stump Field]], [[Coconut Field]],
        [[Pepper Patch]], and the [[Ant Field]] were not in the game at that time.)
        *The Mountain Top Field and the Ant Field were the only two fields that did not have their
        own [[badges]].
        **This was changed in the 1/12/2024 Update, when the Mountain Top Field received a badge,
        thus making the Ant Field the only field without a badge.
        *This is the first field that consists of only triple flowers, the second being the Stump
        Field, the third and fourth being the Pepper Patch and the Coconut Field.
        **This is also the only field without any white flowers.
        *Following the [[Roblox: The Classic|Classic Event]],<ref>[[Roblox: The
        Classic|Roblox: The Classic | Bee Swarm Simulator Wiki | Fandom]]</ref> this field,
        the Ant Field, and Coconut Field cannot be boosted by traditional boosters.
        **The Coconut Field can only be boosted with the [[Coconut Dispenser]].
        *Before the [[Updates#2018.11.25|11/25/2018 update]], the [[Red Cannon]] required 22
        discovered [[Bees|bee]] types, but now it requires 25 instead, so the player can't bypass
        the Lion Bee Gate without 25 bees or 25 bee types discovered.
        **After the 9/28/2019 update, even by bypassing the Lion Bee Gate with 25 bee types
        discovered but not 25 bees, the player will be teleported back to spawn.
