        A '''Charm Bracelet''' is a [[Bond|level]] 12 [[beequip]]. It has an equip limit of one.
        There is no color requirement. It can only be equipped to [[Bees/Legendary|legendary]]
        [[bees]].

        ==Potential==
        * [[System Page#Convert Amount|Convert Amount]] (Guaranteed) - +15% to +20%.
        * [[Critical Hits|Critical Chance]] (Guaranteed) - +1% to +15%.
        * Mark Duration (Guaranteed) - +4% to +8%.
        * [[System Page#Bee Ability Rate|Ability Rate]] (Guaranteed) - +3% to +6%.
        * [[Ability Tokens#Melody|Ability: Melody]] (from [[Waxes|waxing]], rare, caustic only).
        * [Hive Bonus] [[System Page#Loot Luck|Loot Luck]] (Guaranteed) - +1%.
        * [Hive Bonus] [[System Page#Honey At Hive|Honey At Hive]] - +1%.
        * [Hive Bonus] Honey From Tokens - +1%.
        * [Hive Bonus] Convert Rate At Hive - +1%.
        * [Hive Bonus] [[Mushroom Field]] Pollen - +1%. (up to +41% from waxing)
        * [Hive Bonus] [[Blue Flower Field]] Pollen - +1%. (up to +41% from waxing)
        * [Hive Bonus] [[Sunflower Field]] Pollen - +1%. (up to +41% from waxing)
        * [Hive Bonus] [[Dandelion Field]] Pollen - +1%. (up to +41% from waxing)

        == Ways to obtain ==
        *As a very rare drop from the [[Petal Planter]].
        *Can be an available product in [[Dapper Bear's Shop|Dapper Bear's Beequip Shop.]]

        === These methods are no longer possible. ===

        * Buying the Charming Bundle from [[Bee Bear's Catalog]].
