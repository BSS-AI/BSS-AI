        The '''All-Time Top White Pollen Collectors''' is one of the 62 [[leaderboards]] in the
        game, and one of the 20 leaderboards with an in-game model. This leaderboard shows how much
        white [[pollen]] players have collected over time. It shows 10 leaderboard positions at a
        time. The player can scroll down the leaderboard to see more players, all the way to the
        100th place. Only legitimate players appear on the leaderboards for lengths of time
        (hackers/exploiters will be removed).

        If the player steps on the white pad in front of it, the message <span
        style=\"background-color:#0090BF; color:white;\">\"You've collected \"x\" White
        Pollen\"</span> will appear. \"x\" being the amount of white pollen you have collected.

        == Location ==
        It is located inside the 35 Bee zone on the left wall of the [[Petal Shop]], near the
        entrance.

        == Achievements ==

        * MrMocito was the first user to reach 10 trillion total white pollen collected.
        * MrMocito was the first user to reach 100 trillion total white pollen collected.
        * MrMocito was the first user to reach 1 quadrillion total white pollen collected.
