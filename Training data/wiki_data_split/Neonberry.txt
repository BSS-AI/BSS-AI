        A {{Color|Neonberry}} is a type of [[Treats|Treat]] that, when fed to a [[Bees|bee]], raises
        its [[bond]] by 500 and makes it [[Radiation|radioactive]] for a period of time. This is the
        only treat to have guaranteed chance of making the bee radioactive. These are not the
        favorite treat of any bee.

        == Ways to Obtain ==
        * As a reward from the [[Ant Challenge]].
        * As a common reward from [[Robo Bear Challenge]].
        * Redeeming certain valid codes:
        ** There are currently no valid codes that give neonberries.
        * As a Stack Reward for adding certain stickers to the Sticker Stack:
        ** {{Cost|Wobbly Looker Bee Sticker|||0}} rewards 3 neonberries.
        ** {{Cost|Traffic Light Sticker|||0}} rewards 3 neonberries.
        ** {{Cost|Atom Symbol Sticker|||0}} rewards 5 neonberries.
        <!-- not obtainable yet
        ** Every colored [[Beesmas Lights|Beesmas Light]] sticker rewards 3 neonberries each.
        -->
        === Drops ===
        * Has a low chance of dropping from [[Rhino Beetle|rhino beetles]], [[Ladybug|ladybugs]],
        [[Mantis|mantises]], [[King Beetle]], [[Stump Snail]], [[Mondo Chick]], and [[Coconut
        Crab]].
        * Farming in the [[Blue Flower Field]], [[Pineapple Patch]], [[Mountain Top Field]], and the
        [[Pine Tree Forest]] has a rare chance to drop neonberries.
        ** Harvesting [[Leaves]] in these fields may also yield neonberries.
        * As a rare drop from a [[Ability Tokens#Festive Mark|Festive Mark]].
        * As a gift from a [[Cub Buddy|cub buddy]].
        * As an uncommon drop from all [[sprout]]s.
        ** Debug sprouts are guaranteed to drop neonberries.
        * As an uncommon drop from [[Mythic Meteor Shower|Mythic Meteors]].
        * As an uncommon drop from [[Puffshroom|Puffshrooms]].
        * As a drop from the {{Cost|Pesticide Planter|||0}} and {{Cost|The Planter Of Plenty|||0}}.
        ** The {{Color|Pesticide Planter}} drops extra neonberries in the [[Stump Field]],
        [[Mountain Top Field]], and the [[Coconut Field]].
        ** Planting the {{Color|Pesticide Planter}} in the [[Stump Field]], then the [[Mountain Top
        Field]], then the [[Coconut Field]] results in an additional 25 neonberries (1 week
        cooldown).

        === Quests ===
        * Black Bear's Triple Treck gives 1 neonberry.
        * Brown Bear's 420th quest gives 4 neonberries.
