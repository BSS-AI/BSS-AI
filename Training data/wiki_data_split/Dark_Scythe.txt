        The '''Dark Scythe''' is a [[Tools|tool]] that can be crafted in the [[Red HQ]]. The player
        needs to have completed at least 250 [[Gifted Riley Bee]] quests to unlock it.
        {| class=\"article-table\"
        !Crafting Ingredients

        |}

        It collects 13 pollen from 35 patches in 0.5 seconds. It has an average base pollen
        collection rate of 910 pollen per second.

        If a [[flame]] gets hit by the Dark Scythe's collection, it will turn it into a [[Flame#Dark
        Flames|dark flame]] and [[Ability Tokens#Flame Fuel|flame fuel]] (if active) is reapplied,
        as well as its lifespan being reset.

        A dark flame grants a stack of [[Buffs & Debuffs#From Others|Dark Heat]], which grants
        +10% Instant Red Ability Conversion and x1.05 Super-Crit Power, with each additional dark
        flame granting x0.05 Super-Crit Power and +1% Instant Red Ability Conversion. An active dark
        flame always gives the player a stack of dark heat whether they are touching the flame or
        not.

        Touching a dark flame grants 33% more growth to [[Passive Abilities#Scorching Star|Scorching
        Stars]] than normal flames. Dark Flames also deal 50% more damage than regular flames.

        ==Trivia==
        *The Dark Scythe's blue counterpart is the [[Tide Popper]], and its white counterpart is the
        [[Gummyballer]].
        *The Dark Scythe's scooping trail can pop [[bubble]]s.
        *If the player's backpack is full and scooped their Dark Scythe while in a field, it will
        prompt the full backpack message three times.
        *This is the only red collector that doesn't multiply the amount of red pollen collected.
        This is the same case as [[Tide Popper]].
        *This is tied with the Tide Popper for being the fifth most expensive item in the game, both
        having a cost of {{Hover|2.5T|2,500,000,000,000}} Honey. In front of the Dark Scythe and
        Tide Popper is the [[Petal Planter]], costing {{Hover|5T|5,000,000,000,000}} Honey, Coconut
        Belt costing 7.5T Honey, the Gummyballer, costing {{Hover|10T|10,000,000,000,000}} Honey,
        and [[The Planter Of Plenty]], costing {{Hover|100T|100,000,000,000,000}} Honey.
        *If the ingredients for the item were to be crafted, a total of
        {{Hover|2.5T|2,500,000,000,000}} [[Honey]], 3 [[Invigorating Vial]]s 322,500
        [[Strawberry|Strawberries]], 247,500 [[Blueberry|Blueberries]], 82,500 [[Sunflower
        Seed|Sunflower Seeds]], 421,750 [[Pineapple|Pineapples]], 5,250 [[Honeysuckle]]s, 11,550
        [[Bitterberry|Bitterberries]], 1700 [[Neonberry|Neonberries]], 200 [[Stinger]]s, 3,000
        [[Coconut]]s, and 374,850 [[Royal Jelly|Royal Jellies]] are needed.
        *On December 28, 2021, there was an exploit that could have been used to obtain the Dark
        Scythe, as well as Tide Popper and Gummyballer for free. This was later patched by [[Onett
        (Developer)|Onett]], and everybody who used the exploit got a full reset on their account.
        * There was a glitch allowing players to buy the Dark Scythe without completing 250 [[Gifted
        Riley Bee]] quests. If the Dark Scythe was bought without doing 250 [[Gifted Riley Bee]]
        quests, the player could equip the item after doing 250 [[Gifted Riley Bee]] quests.
        **This was also the case with the Tide Popper.
