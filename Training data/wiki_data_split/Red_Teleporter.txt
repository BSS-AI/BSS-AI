        The [[Red Teleporter]] is a portal located inside the [[Red HQ]]. When activated, it will
        instantly teleport the player to the top of the rock directly behind [[Science Bear]]. The
        transportation is one-way; thus, the destination teleporter cannot be used to travel back
        into the Red HQ.

        To activate the Red Teleporter, the player must walk onto the white teleport pad and press
        the \"E\" key on their keyboard if they are on PC or Laptop. However, if they are on mobile,
        then they must walk onto the pad and tap on the button that appears at the top of the
        screen.

        == Trivia ==
        * Similar to its [[Blue Teleporter|blue counterpart]], the Red Teleporter uses an animation
        where each of the rings will revolve in circles. However, this can get buggy since the rings
        will start to spin, but then they will slow down until they reach the point of completely
        stopping.
        * A [[ticket]] token is located near the ceiling of the Red HQ. This can be collected by
        jumping up on top of the Red Teleporter, then jumping or parachuting to the token. Enhanced
        [[System Page#Jump Power|Jump Power]], such as from [[Ability Tokens#Bear Morph|bear
        morph]], or [[:Category:Boots|boots]], is required to jump high enough.
        *The 7 circles on the Red Portal near Science Bear are solid, and with enhanced Jump Power,
        you can jump out of the map on some circles.
