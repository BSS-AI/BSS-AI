        }}'''Cobalt Bee''' is a [[:Category:Blue|Blue]] [[Bees/Event|Event bee]]. It can be obtained
        by purchasing a [[Egg#Event Bee Eggs|Cobalt Bee egg]] in the [[Ticket Tent]] for 250
        [[ticket]]s.

        This bee is better with a [[Hive]] with more blue bees than
        [[:Category:Red|Red]]/[[:Category:Colorless|Colorless]] bees. Its abilities are enhanced
        when [[Crimson Bee]] is also in the hive.

        Like all other Event bees, this bee does not have a favorite Treat, and the two ways to make
        it gifted is by feeding it a [[Star Treat|Star Treat]] or [[Gingerbread Bear]]s.

        Cobalt Bee likes the [[Clover Field]] and the [[Pine Tree Forest]]. It dislikes the
        [[Pineapple Patch]].

        == Stats ==
        *Collects 10 [[Pollen]] in 4 seconds.
        *Makes 140 [[Honey]] in 3 seconds.
        *+75% [[Energy]], +30% [[Stats#Speed|Movespeed]], +25% Convert Speed, +60 [[System
        Page#Convert Amount|Convert Amount]], 10% [[Instant Conversion]], +6
        [[Stats#Attack|Attack]], 5% [[Critical Hits|Critical Chance]].
        *🌟 [[Gifted Bee|Gifted Hive Bonus]]: +15% [[System Page#Instant Blue Conversion|Instant
        Blue Conversion]].

        === Abilities ===
        *'''[[Ability Tokens#Pulse|[Blue Pulse]]]''' Fires a pulse that hops between your blue bees,
        collecting pollen around them (+10% per Level). Power increases with each hop. If you own a
        Crimson Bee, fires a [[Ability Tokens#Pulse|Red Pulse]] as well.
        **If Gifted, the pulse also causes bees to instantly convert pollen equal to 25% of the
        bee's Convert Amount (+25% per Field Boost stack).
        *'''[[Ability Tokens#Bomb Sync|[Blue Bomb Sync]]]''' Grants 10% Blue Pollen and Instant Blue
        Conversion allows [[Ability Tokens#Bomb|Blue Bombs]] to collect from white [[Flowers]] for
        30 sec. If red bomb sync is active, this applies to red flowers as well.

        ==Trivia==
        *The only difference between Cobalt Bee and Crimson Bee's [[Fields|Field]] preferences is
        that the Cobalt Bee likes the Pine Tree Forest, whereas Crimson Bee prefers the [[Rose
        Field]].
        *Cobalt Bee and Crimson Bee have the same [[Stats]].
        *Cobalt Bee and Crimson Bee are the first colored Event bees, followed by [[Vicious Bee]]
        and [[Festive Bee]].
        *Cobalt Bee and Crimson Bee are the only [[Bees]] that can activate another [[Ability
        Tokens|Ability]] apart from their own.
        *When Crimson and Cobalt Bee are both in the same hive, the player will get the following
        bonuses from these ability tokens:
        **Blue pulse additionally fires a red pulse to collect from red bees.
        **If both red bomb sync and blue bomb sync are activated simultaneously, blue bombs can
        collect from red and white flowers.
        *This is one of the Event bees who have different color faces on the hive rather than the
        bee itself, the others being Crimson Bee, Festive Bee, and Vicious Bee.
        *This was the first Blue Event bee, with Vicious Bee being the second.
        *Cobalt Bee and Crimson Bee are currently the only event bees to cost 250 tickets.
        **The [[First Edition Bee|first edition]] [[Tabby Bee]] and [[Puppy Bee]] could once be
        purchased with 250 tickets as well.
        *This bee, Crimson Bee, [[Precise Bee]], [[Photon Bee]], [[Windy Bee]], [[Shy Bee]],
        [[Tadpole Bee]], and [[Ninja Bee]] are the only bees that leave a trail.
        *The [[Cobalt Guard]], which can be [[Crafting|crafted]] in the [[Badge Bearer's Guild]],
        has a special design, name, and stats dedicated to Cobalt Bee.
        **The Cobalt Guard has a [[Passive Abilities#Haste Pulser|Passive Ability]] that looks
        similar to Cobalt Bee's blue pulse ability.
        *This bee, Crimson Bee, Puppy Bee, and Windy Bee are the only Event bees that don't have an
        'enhanced' ability if gifted.
        *Cobalt Bee's original face decal had a mouth that strongly resembled that of the [[Cool
        Bee]]. The decal was scrapped before the release of the Bee.
