        '''Stick Bug''' is an [[:Category:NPC|NPC]] that is found on the tallest mushroom in the
        [[Mushroom Field]] and is accessible by using the [[Red Cannon]]/[[Yellow Cannon]]/[[Blue
        Cannon]] with the [[Parachute]]/[[Glider]], or using the [[Beekeeper's Boots]] and jumping
        from the second mushroom to the one with the [[Royal Jelly]]. Players will need a
        [[Translator]] to talk to Stick Bug, where they can then ask it to start the [[Stick Bug
        Challenge]]. Stick Bug then appears as a Boss [[Mobs|Mob]] and always spawns in the
        [[Sunflower Field]] first. Upon defeat, it flees to a random field from this list:

        * [[Sunflower Field]].
        * [[Clover Field]].
        * [[Strawberry Field]].
        * [[Bamboo Field]].
        * [[Pineapple Patch]].
        * [[Cactus Field]].
        * [[Pumpkin Patch]].
        * [[Mountain Top Field]].

        Stick Bug jumps into the Sunflower Field when the Stick Bug Challenge is started, and will
        not return to the Mushroom until the challenge ends. Near it is the [[Top Stick Bug
        Fighters]] Leaderboard. The player can start the Stick Bug Challenge for free every 1 and a
        half day (1d:12hrs:00m:00s), by initiating it anytime for 50 [[Ticket]]s, or by giving Stick
        Bug a [[Present]] during the 2018, 2020, 2021 and 2022 Beesmas Event.

        When the challenge ends, player will be awarded an [[Stick Bug Amulet|amulet]] depending of
        his score (''More informations in the [[Stick Bug Amulet|Stick Bug's Amulet]] page'').

        During the [[Updates#2019.04.17|Egg Hunt 2019 Event Update]], Stick Bug would plant an Epic,
        Legendary, and Supreme [[Sprout]] after finishing its [[Egg Hunt 2019|Egg Hunt]] quest.

        ==Trivia==
        *If the player gave Stick Bug a Present, started its Egg Hunt quest, started its 2019
        Beesmas Quest, or finished its 2020 Beesmas Quest, the Stick Bug Challenge would
        automatically start.
        *Stick Bug, [[Shadow Bear]], the [[Cub Buddy|Cub Buddies]], [[Bee Bear]] and [[Spirit Bear]]
        are the only NPCs that dance.
        *There is a [[Glitches#Stick Bug Falls Down|Glitch]] where Stick Bug may fall over from its
        regular location on top of the tallest mushroom. If this occurs, it will either be in the
        [[Dandelion Field]], the Mushroom Field, or in between. Another glitch is on the [[Brave Bee
        Gate]] ramp you can walk to the bottom of the ramp and corner of the Bamboo Field where
        Stick Bug will get stuck and must teleport out.
        *Stick Bug gave the most honey when given a present (33,333,333 [[Honey]]), the next being
        [[Panda Bear]] at only 5,000 Honey.
        *If Stick Bug moves to the Mountain Top Field, it has a chance of falling and getting stuck
        in the Pineapple Patch or Pumpkin Patch.
        *Stick Bug and Wild Windy Bee are the only mobs that are impossible to kill, as depleting
        all of their HP will only level them up and regain their HP.
        *Stick Bug waves when the challenge ends.
        *Stick Bug is one of the three NPCs that requires a translator to interact with, the others
        being [[Gifted Riley Bee]] and [[Gifted Bucko Bee]].
        *Stick Bug's boss fight theme became a popular bait-and-switch meme, growing far out from
        the BSS Community.
        **Stick Bug is aware that it is the \"Get Stickbugged lol\" meme according to its Beesmas 2020
        dialogue when you were to give him a present during Beesmas 2020.
        *In [[Spirit Bear|Spirit Bear's]] dialogue, she states that Stick Bug scared her as a cub,
        which indicates that Stick Bug has been around for a long time.
        *Stick Bug and [[Chicks]] are the only mobs that have their own form of defense.
        *Stick Bug's Beesmas 2022 Quest originally gave 1 Caustic Wax, no Swirled Wax, no Ticket
        Planters, and 1 Invigorating Vial. This was soon buffed to 3 Caustic Wax, 3 Swirled Wax, 1
        Ticket Planter, and 2 Invigorating Vials.
        *Stick Bug is the only NPC that can spawn Sprouts.
        *As stated by [[Spirit Bear]], Stick Bug loves [[Pineapple|Pineapples]]
        **Stick Bug was also the one that suggested the [[Pineapple Patch]]
