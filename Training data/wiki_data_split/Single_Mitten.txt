        [[Bees/Rare|rare]], [[Bees/Epic|epic]], or [[Bees/Legendary|legendary]] [[bees]].

        == Possible Stats ==
        *[[Stats#Gather Amount|Gather Amount]] (Guaranteed) - +5 to +10. (up to +50 from
        [[Waxes|waxing]])
        *[[System Page#Bomb Pollen|Bomb Pollen]] (Guaranteed) - +10% to +15%. (up to +75% from
        waxing)
        *[Hive Bonus] [[Capacity]] (Guaranteed) - +7,500 to +10,000. (up to +15,000 from waxing)
        *[Hive Bonus] Red [[Pollen]] (from waxing) - +1% to +9%.
