        The '''Strawberry Dispenser''' is a [[dispenser]] located in the [[Red HQ]]. It can be
        accessed by climbing the ladder inside of the Red HQ, or by jumping on the fence near the
        [[Special Sprout Summoner]]. When used, it grants the player [[honey]], [[Ability
        Tokens#Haste|haste]] x5, [[Ability Tokens#Boost|red boost]] x10, and 1
        [[Strawberry|strawberry]] for every [[:Category:Red|red]] [[bees|bee]] in the player’s
        [[hive]].

        It has a cooldown of 4 hours.

        If the player doesn't have any red bees in their hive, it will not grant any strawberries.
        The player must be in the [[Bee Swarm Simulator Club]] in order to use this.

        == Trivia ==
        * The only time this dispenser appears in a quest is in [[Science Bear]]'s ''\"The Power of
        Information\"'', in which it requires the player to use this machine 3 times.
        * This dispenser gives the same honey per bee as the [[Blueberry Dispenser]].
        * This is one of three dispensers that give [[treats]] in the game with the other two being
        the [[Treat Dispenser]] and the Blueberry Dispenser.
        * This is one of four dispensers requiring the player to be part of the Bee Swarm Simulator
        Club with the others being the [[Honey Dispenser]], the Treat Dispenser, and the Blueberry
        Dispenser.
