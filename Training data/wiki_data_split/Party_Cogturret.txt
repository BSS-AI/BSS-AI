        The '''Party Cogturret''' was one of the mobs exclusive to the [[Robo Party]]. It behaved
        similarly to a normal [[Cogturret]] but spawned on a random field instead of following the
        player on the field they are standing on. They first appeared on Cake Rank 5 and continue to
        spawn in the following ranks.

        == Strategies ==

        * Party Cogturrets aim for where the player is, not where the player will be. Because of
        this, moving to the side after they fire a cog will often result in Party Cogturrets missing
        their attack.
        * Party Cogturrets will always shoot straight at where they are facing so you can avoid
        their attacks simply by not being in front of them.
        * Stand near the Party Cogturrets, this will allow your bees to lock on to the Party
        Cogturrets and quickly deal damage.
        * [[Vicious Bee]] and [[Windy Bee]] provide excellent crowd control, especially since Party
        Cogturrets often come in pairs or packs. [[Digital Bee]] can also stun many Party Cogturrets
        at once and allow them to take extra damage via [[Ability Tokens#Mind Hack|Mind Hack]].
