{"text": "'''Tools''' are items that can gather [[Pollen]]. Some tools have special abilities that
        help assist in gathering pollen, while some collect better from certain colored [[flowers]],
        which include white, red, and blue. All tools can be boosted through the [[System
        Page#Pollen From Tools|Pollen From Tools]] stat. The newest tool in the game was the
        [[Sticker-Seeker]]. There are currently twenty tools in the game.

        Every player starts out with the [[Scooper]]. Better tools can be bought in different
        [[:Category:Shops|Shops]]. The [[Noob Shop]] sells the cheapest/worst tools, ranging from 0
        - 14 thousand [[honey]]. The [[Pro Shop]] follows with tools ranging from 40 thousand - 1.5
        million honey, while the [[Red HQ]] and [[Blue HQ]] both have 2 tools each that can range
        from 3.5 million honey - 2.5 trillion honey. The [[Mountain Top Shop]] has the fourth most
        expensive tools, with prices that range from 20 million - 150 million honey. The [[Petal
        Shop]] has the third most expensive tool at 1.5 billion honey. Currently, the most expensive
        tool in the game is the [[Gummyballer]], which can be bought for 10 trillion honey and other
        various [[Crafting|crafting materials]] in the [[Gummy Bear's Lair]]. Unlike any other tool,
        the Gummyballer can directly take [[Goo]] off a field.

        The player can auto-click their pollen collector by holding down the mouse button on a
        computer. On a mobile device, holding on an open part of the screen does the same. Using
        [[Glue]] gives a 10 minute boost that grants x1.25 pollen from tools and bees. Using a
        [[Purple Potion]] can also give x1.25 [[System Page#Capacity Multiplier|Capacity]], x1.25
        [[System Page#Convert Rate At Hive|Convert Rate At Hive]], x1.5 [[System Page#Red Pollen|Red
        Pollen]], x1.5 [[System Page#Blue Pollen|Blue Pollen]], x1.3 [[System Page#Pollen From
        Bees|Bee Gather Pollen]], and x1.3 [[System Page#Pollen From Tools|Pollen From Tools]] for
        15 minutes. There is also a glitch for computer in which holding down click then Ctrl + Esc
        or Alt + tab automatically uses your tool, but clicking the screen removes it. This can also
        be achieved by moving the mouse outside of the boundaries in windowed mode, but it can only
        be done on the launcher version of the app. If the player presses backspace while a tool is
        equipped the tool will fall into the ground and the player appears to have no tool in their
        hand.
