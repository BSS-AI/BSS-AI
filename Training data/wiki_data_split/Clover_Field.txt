        This field can spawn [[Treat|treats]], [[Ability Tokens#Boost|red boost]], blue boost,
        [[ticket]], [[Magic Bean|magic bean]], [[Royal Jelly|royal jelly]], non-gifted gold or
        diamond [[eggs]] and [[Royal Jelly#Star Jelly|star jelly]] tokens when collecting
        [[pollen]].

        [[Bees]] that like the Clover Field are [[Basic Bee]], [[Cobalt Bee]], [[Crimson Bee]],
        [[Brave Bee]], [[Looker Bee]], [[Stubborn Bee]], [[Music Bee]], [[Puppy Bee]], and [[Tabby
        Bee]]. Bees that dislike the Clover Field are [[Lion Bee]] and [[Photon Bee]].

        Planters placed here produce Invigorating [[Nectar]].

        A level 1-3 [[Rogue Vicious Bee]] can spawn here.

        ==Obtaining Boosts==
        *Using the [[Field Booster]] may give a Clover [[Field Boost]]. When it does, it will give
        +200% Clover Field pollen for 15 minutes. [[File:Fis_clover.png|thumb|75px|The Clover Field
        Boost icon.]]
        *Using [[glitter]] will add 100% Clover Field pollen onto the current boost for 15 minutes,
        if it isn't already maxed out.
        *Using [[Field Dice|field dice]], [[Smooth Dice|smooth dice]], or [[Loaded Dice|loaded
        dice]] may add +100% - 300% Clover Field pollen for 15 minutes if the boost isn't maxed out
        yet.
        *Clover [[Field Wind]]s can be obtained through the [[Wind Shrine]] or [[codes]]. They have
        a base of +20% instant Clover Field conversion and give +5% instant Clover Field conversion
        and +20% Clover Field pollen for each stack. All Field Winds (Including Clover Field Winds)
        can stack up to a total of 13 times.
        *Redeeming certain valid codes:
        **1MLikes (Gives Clover Field Boost x1 + other stuff).
        **Troggles (Gives Clover Field Code Buff + other stuff).
        **DarzethDoodads (Gives Clover Field Code Buff + other stuff).
        *It can be boosted by the [[King Beetle Amulet]]. If it has a Clover Field Boost, it can
        boost the field by +1% to +100%.
        *It can be boosted by the [[Shell Amulet]]. If it has a Clover Field Boost, it can boost the
        field by +5% to +30% depending on the amulet rarity.

        ==Trivia==
        *The field booster for the Clover Field has been decreased three times. In the
        [[Updates#2018.07.11|7/11/2018 update]], it was decreased from x5 to x4, in the
        [[Updates#2019.04.05|4/5/2019 update]] it was decreased to x3, and in the
        [[Updates#2019.09.28|9/28/2019 update]], it was decreased to x2.
        *Before the 7/11/2018 update, the Clover Field had 29 × 39 = 1131 flowers and it was the
        largest field. It is no longer the largest field in the game, with only 5 less flowers than
        the new largest, the [[Pineapple Patch]].
        *The Bubble Wand on top of the [[Blue HQ]] will sometimes blow [[sparkles]] on to this
        field, along with the [[Mushroom Field]], [[Blue Flower Field]] and [[Dandelion Field]].
        *The [[Honeystorm]] Summoner used to be where the [[Wealth Clock]] is currently, but when
        the Wealth Clock was added, the Honeystorm Pad was moved to right beside the bush in the
        Clover Field where the Rhino Beetle spawns.
        *The [[King Beetle Lair]] is right under this field.
        *This is the first field with 2 [[mobs]] that can be accessed in the game.
        *When a [[Rogue Vicious Bee]] spawns in the Clover Field, a spike can be seen in King
        Beetle's Lair, which can still be activated.
        **If a [[sprout]] is in the Clover Field, the stem and the light can be seen in King
        Beetle's Lair.
        *Clover Field has a higher chance than any other field of dropping royal jelly when a sprout
        pops.
        **This field also has a low chance of spawning a star jelly.
        *If the player has the [[Spark Staff]], is in the King Beetle's Lair, and jumps, they are
        able to collect pollen from the Clover Field.
        *This is the only field that gives eggs.
        *This is the only starting field with roughly the same amount of Single and Double flowers,
        while the other 4 fields in the Starter Zone all have around 90% Single flowers, and the
        rest being Double.
