        '''Basic Bee''' is a [[:Category:Colorless|Colorless]] [[Bees/Common|Common bee]]. It is
        usually the first bee to be discovered and hatched.

        Basic Bee can be only hatched from a [[Egg#Basic Egg|Basic Egg]], a [[Egg#Star Egg|Star
        Egg]], or obtained through the now unobtainable Basic Bee Jelly. It cannot be obtained from
        a normal [[Royal Jelly]] or [[Royal Jelly#Star Jelly|Star Jelly]].

        Basic Bee's favorite type of treat is [[Sunflower Seed|Sunflower seeds]].

        Basic Bee likes the [[Sunflower Field]], [[Clover Field]], and [[Mountain Top Field]],
        though it dislikes the [[Spider Field]].

        ==Stats==
        *Collects 10 [[pollen]] in 4 seconds.
        *Makes 80 [[honey]] in 4 seconds.
        *🌟 [[Gifted Bee|Gifted Hive Bonus]]: x1.2 Pollen.

        ===Abilities===
        ''None.''

        ==Trivia==
        *Basic Bee is the only type of Common bee.
        *Due to the large number of appearances Basic Bee makes within the game and thumbnails, it
        is considered the main mascot of Bee Swarm Simulator.
        *Basic Bee is one of three bees to have an official plushie on the
        [https://shop.beeswarm.com/ Official Bee Swarm Simulator Website], the others being Tabby
        Bee and Vicious Bee.
        *The statistics for all other [[bees]] are described in terms of their difference from Basic
        Bee’s statistics. For example, +20 collection means that the bee it's describing has
        collected 20 more pollen than Basic Bee.
        *Basic Bee and [[Brave Bee]] are the only bees without abilities.
        **However, if they are in their [[Gifted Bee|gifted]] forms, they still can produce
        [[Ability Tokens#Inspire|Inspire tokens]].
        **And specific [[Beequip]]s grants abilities like [[Ability Tokens#Focus|Focus]] or
        [[Ability Tokens#Melody|Melody]] tokens.
        *Basic Bee and [[Bees/Event|Event Bees]] are the only types of bee that cannot be obtained
        from a normal royal jelly since Basic Bee is a Common Bee.
        *The [[Codes|code]], \"DontUseThisJelly\" (expired) gave a [[Royal Jelly#Royal Jelly
        Variants|Basic Bee jelly]] that could be used on a bee to transform any bee into a Basic
        Bee.
        *Basic Bee is usually the first bee to be hatched and discovered.
        *Basic Bee and [[Demo Bee]] share the same skin color pattern when they are not in their
        gifted forms.
        *Basic Bee and [[Honey Bee]] possess the same colors but in a different pattern.
        *Basic Bee's gifted bonus used to give +20% [[System Page#Pollen|Pollen]], but it later got
        buffed after the [[Updates#2019.04.05|4/5/2019 update]] into x1.2 Pollen, so it can now
        increase with any additional pollen multipliers that the player has.
        *An image of a winking Basic Bee face appears at the end of the White Tunnel.
        **This may refer to the currently valid code called \"Wink\".
        **This also appeared in the thumbnail of the [[Updates#2018.06.02|6/2/2018 update.]]
        *Basic Bee is the only kind of bee to appear in every different version of the thumbnail for
        the game.
        *Basic Bee, along with Tabby Bee and [[Rascal Bee]], are all of [[Onett
        (Developer)|Onett's]] favorite bees.
        **This was revealed through Onett's dialogue.
        *Basic Bee's face appears on the bee menu icon, found near the top left corner of the
        screen.
        **Its face is also visible on [[Treat|treats]], [[ticket]]s, [[Beequip|beequip case]] and
        occasionally appears on select objects that produce [[sparkles]]. However, the faces have
        curved mouths unlike Basic Bee's mouth.
        *Basic Bee can only be obtained from [[Egg#Basic Egg|basic eggs]], [[Egg#Star Egg|star
        eggs]], and the Basic Bee jelly, which is now unobtainable.
        *The chances of getting a Gifted Basic Bee from sunflower seeds is 1 in 12,000.
        ** The same chances apply to Legendary Bees if you feed them their favorite treats.
        *The chances of hatching a Gifted Basic Bee from a Basic Egg is 0.348% (1 out of
        287.35632184).
        *On very rare occasions, Basic Bee's face would disappear on most surfaces that use its
        texture.
        *Basic Bee, [[Tadpole Bee]], Tabby Bee, [[Lion Bee]], Puppy Bee, and Bear Bee are the only
        bees to represent realistic animals.
