        '''Field Capacity''' is a [[Mechanics|mechanic]] that increases the player's
        [[Capacity|Capacity Multiplier]] depending on what kind of [[Fields|field]] they are in.
        There are 2 types of field capacity: colored field capacity (shown as a stat) and field
        capacity for a specific field (shown as a buff).

        == Ways to Obtain ==
        * Colored field capacity multiplies the player's capacity depending on the percentage of the
        specific [[pollen]] type in the field.
        ** Red field capacity can be obtained from [[Riley Bee|Riley Bee's]] gifted hive bonus, the
        [[Demon Mask]] and [[Passive Abilities#Scorching Star|Scorching Star]].
        ** Blue field capacity can be obtained from [[Bucko Bee|Bucko Bee's]] gifted hive bonus,
        [[Buffs & Debuffs#From Passives|Bubble Bloat]] (Bubble Bloat can only be obtained if the
        player has [[Passive Abilities|Pop Star]] passive on [[Star Amulet|Supreme Star Amulet]]).
        ** White field capacity can be obtained from [[Exhausted Bee|Exhausted Bee's]] gifted hive
        bonus, the [[Gummy Mask]] and [[Passive Abilities#Gummy Star|Gummy Star]].
        * Redeeming certain valid [[codes]]:
        ** BANNED (Gives [[Spider Field]] Code Buff [+100% Spider Field pollen, +100% Spider Field
        Capacity] + other stuff).
        ** All [[Ready Player Two]] codes give Code Buffs on different fields.
        * Using certain [[beequip]]s that include a Field Capacity boost stat.
        **The [[Warm Scarf]] grants +1% to +3% Red and White Field Capacity.
        **The [[Pinecone]] grants +8% to +15% Pine Tree Forest Capacity.
        **The [[Snow Tiara]] grants +2% to +5% Blue Field Capacity and +3% to +5% White Field
        Capacity.

        ===The methods below are no longer possible.===
        * Every [[Buffs & Debuffs#From Areas|Market Boost]] grants x1.5 field capacity for 30
        minutes to the boosted field (only obtainable from codes).
        *All [[Ornaments|Beesmas 2020]] and [[Ornaments|2021 Ornaments]] (excluding [[Honey Bee
        (NPC)|Honey Bee]]'s ornament) gave field capacity as a stat.
        *Redeeming certain expired codes:
        ** RebootXmas (Gave +100% [[Clover Field]] Capacity + other stuff).
        ** Buoyant (Gave [[Blue Flower Field]] Capacity x3 + other stuff).
        ** WintersEnd (Gave +100% Spider Field Capacity, +100% Pine Tree Forest Capacity + and other
        stuff).
        **3YearParty (Gave [[Mountain Top Field]] Capacity x3 + other stuff).
        **5mMembers (Gives [[Pine Tree Forest]] Capacity x3 and [[Rose Field]] Capacity x3 + other
        stuff).
        **RedMarket (Gives [[Pepper Patch]] Capacity x1 + other stuff).
        ** Mocito100T (Gives [[Coconut Field]] Capacity x2 + other stuff).

        == Colored Field Capacity Formula ==
        The following formula shows how much capacity multiplier a player gets in a field:

        Let x equal the colored capacity multiplier.<br />Let y equal the percentage of that
        [[pollen]] type in the field.

        Capacity multiplier in the field =

        <nowiki>{{</nowiki><math>{{x ~ - ~ 1\} ~ \times ~ \{{\frac{y}{100}}\} ~ +
        ~ 1}}</math>}

        ==Trivia==
        *The [[Spider Field]] is the only field where the player is guaranteed to get 100% of a
        colored capacity bonus, specifically white field capacity.
        **This is achievable with Exhausted Bee's gifted hive bonus, by using the [[Gummy Mask]],
        activating Gummy Star, the Warm Scarf's hive bonus, or the Snow Tiara's hive bonus.
        **The only opposite version of this is in the [[Mountain Top Field]], it generates no white
        flowers and therefore doesn't give white field capacity.
        **It is also possible to get 100% of a colored capacity bonus on any other field if they
        generate enitrely with one color of flowers, but the odds of a field generating like that is
        exceptionally rare.
        *The Field Capacity Buff, Field Code Buff, Capacity Buff, and the x2 Pollen Boost are the
        only code-exclusive buffs.
