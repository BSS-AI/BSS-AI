        '''Pollination''' is a [[Mechanics|mechanic]] that causes [[flowers]] to grow to larger
        sizes, increasing their total [[pollen]] and multiplier. Flowers that are lower tier than
        other flowers in the [[Fields|field]] have a higher chance of being pollinated. When flowers
        are pollinated, they will burst flower particles.

        Flowers only stay pollinated for 1 minute, but this can be stacked up to 5 times for 5
        minutes. Every minute that an already pollinated flower is not pollinated, it will go down a
        tier, until it has reverted to its original state.

        Pollination is the only method to achieve large and star flowers, as the highest natural
        tier is triple.

        == Ways To Pollinate Flowers ==
        * [[Fuzzy Bee|Fuzzy Bee's]] [[Ability Tokens#Fuzz Bombs|fuzz bombs]] can pollinate flowers
        upon popping it.
        * Fuzzy Bee‘s [[Passive Abilities#Fuzzy Coat|fuzzy coat]] passive has a chance to pollinate
        up to 5 surrounding flowers while collecting pollen. This increases to 9 when gifted.
        *Fuzzy Bee's [[Ability Tokens#Pollen Haze|Pollen haze]] pollinates up to 30 flowers every
        second. [[Ability Tokens#Bomb|Bomb]] tokens produced in a field with pollen haze are
        automatically collected and pollinate flowers.
        * [[Diamond Mask|Diamond Mask's]] [[Passive Abilities#Diamond Drain|Diamond Drain]]
        pollinates 10 blue flowers when activated. The number of flowers pollinated increases by 3
        for every blue bee the player has.
        * If [[Star Amulet#Supreme Star Amulet|Guiding Star]] is active, the star will pollinate 15
        (+1 per [[Gifted Bee|gifted bee]] type) flowers every 15 seconds.
        * A bee with the [[Poinsettia]] beequip equipped has an 11% to 20% chance to pollinate
        flowers while gathering.
        *A bee with the [[Lei]] Beequip equipped has a 20% to 50% chance to pollinate flowers while
        gathering.

        == Trivia ==
        * This is the only way to directly change flowers in a field.
        * Fuzzy Bee is the only bee in the game to pollinate flowers.
        ** However, bees with the [[Poinsettia]] or Lei beequip have a low chance to pollinate
        flowers.
