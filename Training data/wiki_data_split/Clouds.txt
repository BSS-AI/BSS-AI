{"text": "{{A|title1 = Cloud|image1 = Screenshot 2019-09-28 at 12.06.56 PM.png|caption1 = An entity
        that regrows flowers and grants short pollen buffs.}}'''Clouds''' are small, white rain
        clouds that can be summoned through various methods (listed below). They move around the
        [[field]] aimlessly over a 1-5 minute time span, depending on the source of the cloud,
        raining over 49 surrounding flowers and functioning as secondary [[sprinklers]]. Clouds can
        also help grow a [[sprout]] if there is a player in the same field although the [[pollen]]
        given to the sprout depends on the type of sprout and the field. If a player is under a
        cloud, they will receive the Cloud Boost buff which gives +25% pollen and +10% Unique
        Instant Conversion (+50% Pollen and +20% Unique IC if the player has a [[Gifted Bee|Gifted]]
        [[Windy Bee]]) and lasts for up to 8 seconds. Clouds can also accelerate a [[planter]] by up
        to 20% with 6 clouds (around 8% with 1 cloud), however keeping a cloud in a field for hours
        is expensive making this method not viable for buffing planters.

        There can only be a maximum of 6 clouds on a field at a time. If the player attempts to use
        a [[Cloud Vial|cloud vial]] to summon another cloud, a message will pop up saying, <span
        style=\"background-color:red; color:white;\">\"There are too many clouds in this
        field.\"</span>

        Clouds cannot be summoned in the [[Ant Field]]. Attempting to do so will result in the
        following message: <span style=\"background-color:red; color:white;\">\"You can't make
        clouds in a challenge field.\"</span>

        ==Methods To Summon==

        *Using a cloud vial (3 minute duration).
        *Receiving field winds from the [[Wind Shrine]] (5 minute duration).
        *Activating/Defeating [[Wild Windy Bee]] (3 minute duration).
        *Summoned by a player's [[Windy Bee]] (1 min duration + 10 sec per level of Windy Bee)
        ==Types==
        There are two different types of clouds: The Rain Cloud and the Windy Cloud.

        Rain Cloud: The rain clouds are the ones that are summoned through donations to the Wind
        Shrine, by using a cloud vial and while fighting Wild Windy Bee. They are beneficial to
        players and give the cloud buff, grow sprouts, and regenerate flowers. These clouds are also
        spawned by tamed Windy Bees.

        Windy Cloud: The Windy Cloud is a cloud that is only seen when a Wild Windy Bee has
        appeared. The cloud floats over a field and contains Wild Windy Bee inside it. Once a player
        approaches it and jumps up into the cloud, it will become a rain cloud and Wild Windy Bee
        starts to attack. Like rain clouds, it is still able to give the cloud buff, grow sprouts,
        and regenerate flowers.

        Collecting a [[Ability Token#Cloud|Cloud]] token will give the player the message \"<span
        style=\"background:grey;color:white\">🌧️ Windy Bee summoned a Cloud in {Field Name}.
        🌧️</span>\"
        If another player's Windy Bee spawns a cloud in your field, a message will appear announcing
        \"<span style=\"background:grey;color:white\">🌧️ {Username}'s Windy Bee summoned a Cloud
        in this field. 🌧️</span>\"
