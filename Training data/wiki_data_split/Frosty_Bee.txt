        '''Frosty Bee''' is a [[:Category:Blue|Blue]] [[Bees/Epic|Epic bee]].

        Frosty Bee's favorite type of treat are [[Blueberry|Blueberries]].

        Frosty Bee likes the [[Blue Flower Field]], and the [[Mountain Top Field]]. It dislikes the
        [[Mushroom Field]].

        == Stats ==
        * Collects 10 [[Pollen]] in 4 seconds.
        * Makes 80 [[Honey]] in 4 seconds.
        * +25% [[Energy]], -20% [[Stats#Speed|Movespeed]].
        * 🌟 [[Gifted Bee|Gifted Hive Bonus]]: x1.25 [[System Page#Blue Bomb Pollen|Blue Bomb
        Pollen]].

        === Abilities ===
        * '''[[Ability Tokens#Boost|[Blue Boost]]]''' Grants x1.2 pollen from blue [[Flowers]] for
        15s. Stacks up to 10 times.
        * '''[[Ability Tokens#Bomb|[Blue Bomb+]]]''' Collects 10 pollen from 29 surrounding blue
        flowers (+10% pollen per Level). Combo with other bombs to increase power.

        == Trivia ==
        *Frosty Bee, [[Commander Bee]], and [[Buoyant Bee]] are the only [[Bees]] that wear hats.
        *Frosty Bee is a reference to Frosty the Snowman.
        *Because of the same abilities, the red counterpart of this bee is [[Shy Bee]].
        **Ironically, Frosty Bee's bombs are more powerful than Shy Bee's despite being a lower
        rarity.
        *A Frosty Bee Jelly could have been obtained from one of the bundles/packs in [[Bee Bear’s
        Catalog|Bee Bear's Catalog]].
        *This bee's name is the placeholder's name for any bee in a hive slot that hasn't loaded
        yet.
        *The bee may like mountain top field because it is cold on high mountains.
