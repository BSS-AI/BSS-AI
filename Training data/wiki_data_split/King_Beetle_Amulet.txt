        The '''King Beetle Amulet''' is an [[amulet]] with a {{texttip|one in seven|~14%}} chance of
        dropping upon defeating the [[King Beetle]] ([[Loot Luck]] does ''not'' affect the
        probability or stats).

        If the player has three or more types of amulets, the King Beetle Amulet will be above the
        player's right shoulder. If the player has two types, it will be above their left shoulder,
        and if it's the only type, it will be above their head.

        The amulet grants a random number of [[Buffs & Debuffs|buffs]]. The buffs can be
        field-specific [[Field Boost|pollen boosts]], [[System Page#Convert Rate|Convert Rate]]
        bonuses, and/or [[System Page#Bee Attack|Bee Attack]] bonuses. The quality of the amulet
        (the number of buffs it gives as well as the amount of those buffs) has the tendency to
        improve with the number of times the King Beetle is defeated, up to a maximum of 100
        defeats, though a guaranteed improvement on every occasion should not be expected.

        == Possible Buffs ==
        * +1% to +100% Convert Rate (Guaranteed).
        * One of the following (Possible):
        ** +1 [[Bee Attack#Regular Bee Attack|Bee Attack]]
        ** +1 [[Bee Attack#Red Bee Attack|Red Bee Attack]]
        ** +1 [[Bee Attack#Blue Bee Attack|Blue Bee Attack]]
        * Two of the following:
        ** +1% to +100% [[Bamboo Field]] Pollen
        ** +1% to +100% [[Mushroom Field]] Pollen
        ** +1% to +100% [[Blue Flower Field]] Pollen
        ** +1% to +100% [[Clover Field]] Pollen
        ** +1% to +100% [[Strawberry Field]] Pollen

        == Trivia ==
        * The King Beetle Amulet used to be a guaranteed drop by King Beetle but was removed some
        time after.
        * The amulet looks like King Beetle's head, but with a 45 degree tilt.
        * The King Beetle Amulet is the first amulet that is a dropped from a [[Mobs|mob]], the
        second being the [[Shell Amulet]] that is dropped by [[Stump Snail]].
        * This is the only amulet in the game to not be guaranteed to drop from its source.
        * All rewards from King Beetle will automatically be added into the player's inventory if
        they receive a King Beetle Amulet.
