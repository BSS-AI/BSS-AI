        This field can spawn [[honey]], [[Pineapple|pineapples]], [[Bitterberry|bitterberries]],
        [[Neonberry|neonberries]], [[enzymes]], [[ticket]]s, [[Magic Bean|magic beans]], [[glue]]
        and [[glitter]] tokens when collecting [[pollen]].

        This field gives Satisfying [[Nectar]] when a planter is placed here.

        [[Bees]] that like the Pineapple Patch are [[Shocked Bee]], [[Diamond Bee]], [[Lion Bee]],
        [[Gummy Bee]], and [[Photon Bee]]. Bees that dislike the Pineapple Patch are [[Vector Bee]],
        [[Cobalt Bee]], and [[Crimson Bee]].

        A level 1-20 [[Wild Windy Bee]] can spawn here.

        == Obtaining Boosts ==
        * Using the [[Field Booster]] may give a [[Field Boost|Pineapple Patch Boost]]. When it
        does, it will give +200% Pineapple Patch pollen for 15 minutes.
        [[File:Fis_pineapple.png|thumb|75px|The Pineapple Patch Boost icon.]]
        * Using [[glitter]] will add 100% Pineapple Patch pollen onto the current boost for 15
        minutes if it isn't already maxed out.
        * Using [[Field Dice|field dice]], [[Smooth Dice|smooth dice]], or [[Loaded Dice|loaded
        dice]] may add +100% - +300% Pineapple Patch pollen for 15 minutes if the current boost
        isn't already maxed out.
        * Pineapple Patch [[Field Wind|winds]] can be obtained through the [[Wind Shrine]] or
        [[codes]]. They have a base of +20% instant Pineapple Patch conversion, and give +5% instant
        Pineapple Patch conversion and +20% Pineapple Patch pollen for each stack. Pineapple Patch
        Winds can stack up to a total of 13 times.
        * Redeeming certain valid codes:
        ** ClubBean (Gives Pineapple Patch Boost x2 + other stuff).
        ** 1MLikes (Gives Pineapple Patch Boost x1 + other stuff).
        **10MilMembers (Gives Pineapple Patch code buff + other stuff).
        **WalmartToys (Gives Pineapple Patch code buff, boost x4, and winds x10 + other stuff).
        * The [[Moon Amulet]] can give a pollen boost for the Pineapple Patch between +1% and +150%.
        * It can be boosted by the [[Shell Amulet]]. If it has a Pineapple Patch Boost, it can boost
        the field by +7% to +30% depending on the amulet rarity.
        *Donating either the Menacing Mantis or Pineapple Patch Stamp [[Sticker]] to the [[Sticker
        Stack]] boosts the Pineapple Patch by 5% and 10% respectively.

        == Trivia ==
        * Oddly, the mobs in this field ([[Rhino Beetle]] and [[Mantis]]) are affiliated with blue,
        despite the field being white.
        * There is a [[Pineapple|pineapple]] token behind the pineapple closest to the shop.
        Collecting it grants the player five pineapples.
        * There is a [[ticket]] token worth three tickets on top of the giant pineapple. It can be
        obtain by using the [[Red Cannon]] and the [[Parachute]] or [[Glider]], or by jumping up
        from the side of the pineapple.
        * There is a [[glue]] token behind the biggest pineapple between the [[Stump Field]].
        * This was the third biggest field in the game before the [[Updates#2018.07.11|7/11/2018
        update]].
        * This field is one of the three fields to have the word \"Patch\" in their name instead of
        \"Field,\" along with the [[Pumpkin Patch]] and the [[Pepper Patch]].
        ** The [[Pine Tree Forest]] is another field without the word \"Field\" in it, having \"Forest\"
        in its name instead.
        * This is one of six fields that [[fireflies]] can travel to, with the others being [[Bamboo
        Field]], [[Cactus Field]], [[Rose Field]], [[Spider Field]], and [[Strawberry Field]].
        * This is one of the fields that the [[Stick Bug]] can move to during the [[Stick Bug
        Challenge]].
        * This field along with the [[Sunflower Field]] and the [[Pumpkin Patch]] are the only
        fields where an object may come to life and shoot sparkles.
        * The biggest pineapple will occasionally grow a face, where it will blow bubbles at patches
        of flowers, producing [[sparkles]] (in diamond formation) upon contact. Collecting pollen on
        the affected flowers can produce tickets, pineapples, [[Moon Charm|moon charm]], [[Royal
        Jelly|royal jelly]], [[enzymes]], or honey tokens.
        * While doing one of [[Spirit Bear]]'s quests, Spirit Bear states that the Pineapple Patch
        was originally supposed to be a Rhubarb Field. Instead, she and Stick Bug planted
        pineapples.
        *This is the only field to be guarded by two mobs of different tiers (Rhino Beetle and
        Mantis), if you don't include the [[Werewolf]] guarding the Pine Tree Forest as it merely
        happens to be located in the Werewolf's attack territory.
        *This is the currently the biggest field in the game.
