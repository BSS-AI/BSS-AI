        '''Aphids''' are hostile [[Mobs]] that have a low chance of spawning from collecting
        [[Leaves]], with the chances rising when a Field is experiencing a period of low activity
        (i.e. players are not visiting said field very often). Their level and health correlate to
        which [[Field]] the aphid spawns in, like [[Rogue Vicious Bee]]. Sharing a similar trait
        with [[Tunnel Bear]], [[Coconut Crab]], [[Chicks#Chick|Chicks]], [[Chicks#Hostage
        Chick|Hostage Chicks]], [[Wild Windy Bee]], [[Chicks#Mondo Chick|Mondo Chick]], and
        [[Puffshroom]]s, Aphids despawn after 30 seconds. The timer is shown above its health.
        Aphids attack in a similar matter to [[Stick Nymph]]s and [[Ants|Giant Ants]], following the
        nearest player around slowly. Aphids are public mobs, meaning that anyone can collect the
        drops regardless of whether or not they attacked it.

        Defeating an Aphid awards 10 [[Battle Points]] (20 if defeating one of the 3 rare types),
        [[Bond]], a Magic Bean, and various other rewards. Some rewards have a cooldown, meaning
        that they will not be obtainable for a while after receiving them once, regardless of the
        level or type. However, you can still get them through other players spawning an Aphid with
        said rewards at the expense of applying the same cooldown to them also.

        == Types ==

        Aphids always appear as 1 of 4 different variants: Normal, Rage, Armored, and Diamond.
        Different types of aphids may spawn at different encounter rates. Rewards vary between these
        types. Honey amounts depend on the aphid's level and player's honey per token boost.

        ==Tips==
        *Jumping and/or running around aphids will work effectively since they slowly follow
        players.
        **If players cannot defeat an aphid due to higher level and health, upgrading bees and
        having a [[Vicious Bee]] will help kill the aphid faster.
        *Using various [[:Category:Consumables|consumables]] and [[Passive Abilities|passives]] such
        as [[Stinger]]s, Oils, or the [[Passive Abilities|Star Saw]] can help defeat aphids faster.
