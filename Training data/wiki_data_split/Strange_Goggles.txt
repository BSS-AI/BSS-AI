        This accessory was made for the Ready Player Two event in [[Bee Swarm Simulator]]. As such,
        it is very distinct from other accessories in this game. and it requires 7 [[Codes]] to be
        redeemed. The codes to obtain all 7 of the 7-Pronged Cogs are: Dysentery, Luther, Millie,
        CarmenSanDiego, Jumpstart, Troggles and WordFactory. Upon equipping this item, the player
        will be able to interact with [[Digital Bee (NPC)]] in [[the Computer]].

        *+77 [[Capacity]].

        ==Trivia==
        *This is the cheapest item in the game in terms of honey besides the [[Pouch]] and
        [[Scooper]], which are automatically received for free.
        *This is the only Item that doesn't show up in the item/gear bar at the top of the screen.
        *This is the first Item that is required to enter a specific area, the second being the
        [[Propeller Hat]] and the [[Hiking Boots]].
        *While wearing these and doing the [[Digital Bee (NPC)|Digital Bee]]'s quests, the objective
        field will occasionally \"Glitch\" with brightly colored textures appearing on the Field
        decorations. This is purely cosmetic and does not affect gameplay.
