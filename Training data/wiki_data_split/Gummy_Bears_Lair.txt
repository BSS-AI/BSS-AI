        [[Gummy Bear&#39;s Lair]] is a location that can only be accessed by having a
        [[Badges#Goo Badge|Goo Hotshot Badge]] or above.
        [[File:Eee4a72469aa45acd97d01685d93a3e1.png|thumb|250px|The prompt to enter the Gummy
        Lair.]]

        To get into the lair, touch the [[Gummy Bee]] model on the [[Gummy Bee Egg Claim]] and use a
        [[Gumdrops|gumdrop]]. The player can reach the egg claim by going behind the [[Top Ant
        Exterminators|Ant Leaderboard]] and following the path (where there's also an [[Ant Pass|ant
        pass]] token). They can also use a cannon, such as the [[Red Cannon]], in combination with
        the [[Parachute]]/[[Glider]].

        If the player doesn't have the Goo Hotshot Badge, then the following message will prompt:
        <div
        style=\"background-color:rgb(93,223,193);margin:6px;width:300px;text-align:center;color:black\">\"Only
        Goo Hotshots can hear Gummy Bee...\"</div>

        [[File:Capture73762.jpg|thumb|250px|The notification that appears when the player is on top
        of the Gummy Bee model and does not have the Goo Hotshot Badge.]]Otherwise, the player will
        be prompted to use gumdrops to enter the Lair with this message:<div
        style=\"background-color:rgb(93,223,193);margin:6px;width:300px;text-align:center;color:black\">The
        Gummy Bee wants Gumdrops</div>

        == Shop ==
        When entered, you are greeted with [[Gummy Bear]] and [[Gummy Bee]]. The lair contains the
        [[Glue Dispenser]], which activates the \"[[Passive Abilities|Unlimited Gumdrops]]\" passive,
        with 3 minutes of unlimited [[gumdrops]] when using them, as well as a shop that sells the
        [[Gummy Mask]], [[Gummy Boots]] and the [[Gummyballer]].

        ==Notes ==
        *Players should avoid going to Gummy Bear&#39;s Lair if they have a lot of [[pollen]] in
        their [[Bags|container]], as they will eventually die due to Gummy Bear killing them or by
        the player needing to reset their character. However, the player can avoid losing pollen by
        rejoining the game, using a [[Micro-Converter]] to convert all of the pollen beforehand, or
        using a [[Whirligig]].
        **During Beesmas 2020 and 2021, the player would be able to exit the lair if they have given
        Gummy Bear a present.
        * The player’s bees do not go to the lair with the player. This is because the lair is high
        up in the sky, so the bees will take a while to get there.

        ==Glitches==
        *When the player walks up to Gummy Bear, he sometimes fails to kill the player after he
        finishes his speech.
        * After Gummy Bear kills the player, their body parts will slip down until they reach the
        end of the shop. If the player presses E at the right time, they will be stuck in the shop,
        resulting in the [[Glitches#Shop Trap|Shop Trap glitch]]. Only leaving the game could fix
        this glitch.
        *On rare occasions, [[Wild Windy Bee]] can push the player to Gummy Bear&#39;s Lair,
        making them glitch into it.

        == Token locations==
        * A glitter token behind the Gummy Boots case. (Contains 1 Glitter)
        *A [[Royal Jelly#Star Jelly|star jelly]] token in front of Gummy Bear. (Contains 1 Star
        Jelly)
        *An enzymes token in the corner of the lair. (Contains 1 Enzymes)

        ==Trivia==
        *Before Beesmas 2020, the only way to leave the lair without exiting or losing connection to
        the game is to die. The player can die by either courtesy of [[Gummy Bear]] or by resetting
        their character.
        *This is currently the only location not connected to the mountain; instead, it is high up
        in the sky. The player is able to see the silhouette of the lair if they look closely at the
        sky.
        *[[Bee Bear]] refers to [[Gummy Bear]] as '''naughty''' in 2018, most likely because he
        kills the player upon finishing his dialogue, and the fact that he tried to \"invade\" the
        mountain.
        *Originally, the lair required the Goo Ace Badge or above, which was a mistake on [[Onett
        (Developer)|Onett]]'s end. It was quickly fixed to allow people with Goo Hotshot as well, so
        there could be more players that could actually access the lair.
        *This and the [[Badge Bearer's Guild]] are the only places that require [[badges]] to enter
        them.
        **Gummy Bear&#39;s Lair is the only location that requires a specific badge to enter.
        * Before the [[Updates#2019.04.05|4/5/2019 update]], the [[Gummy Bee]] that was used to
        teleport was on top of the [[Ticket Tent]]. Now [[Gummy Bee]] is replaced with a model of
        [[Festive Bee]] and was instead put at the [[Fields|Ant Field]].
        *WhenGummy Bear&#39;s Lair first came out, it said, “Gummy Bee wants 1
        [[Gumdrops|Gumdrop]].” Now, it says “The Gummy Bee wants Gumdrops.”
        **However, if the player is visiting Gummy Bear&#39;s Lair for the first time, standing
        on the [[Gummy Bee]] model will say \"The Gummy Bee wants 1 Gumdrops.\"
        *[[Spirit Bear]] mentions how to get to the lair in her quest dialogue.
        *Some players may experience lag when they are getting close to [[Gummy Bear]].
        *This and the [[Petal Shop]] are the only shops with items in a triangular formation.
