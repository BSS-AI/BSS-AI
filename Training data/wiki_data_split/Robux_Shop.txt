        The '''Robux Shop''' is a shop that requires robux () to purchase items. It can be accessed
        through the Shop tab on the menu bar. It is directly next to the [[System Page|System Tab]].
        This shop sells gamepasses, [[Egg]]s, [[Royal Jelly]], [[Honey]], [[Night Bell]]s, [[Magic
        Bean]]s, and [[Ticket]]s. It sometimes sells limited-edition items, such as [[Festive
        Bean]]s, and limited-time packs.

        ==Trivia ==
        *Those who bought the x2 Ticket Chance Pass before it was removed got to keep it.
        *With the exception of the gamepasses, Bear Bee, and the Noob Cub Buddy skin, everything
        else in the shop can be earned without spending robux.
        *The Robux Shop and [[Bee Bear's Catalog]] are the only shops that are in a menu.
        *The Robux Shop has more items than any other shop in-game.
        *The Festive Bee Pack, Fuzzy Bee Pack, Buoyant Pack, Precise Pack, and Digital Bee Pack are
        the only packs in the Robux Shop that gave a [[Egg#Specific Bee Eggs|Bee Egg]], as Bear Bee
        is not a pack, nor was the Windy Bee deal.
