        '''Carpenter Bee''' is a [[:Category:Colorless|Colorless]] [[Bees/Legendary|Legendary bee]].
        Carpenter Bee's favorite type of treat is [[Sunflower Seeds|Sunflower Seeds]].

        Carpenter Bee likes the [[Bamboo Field]], and [[Pine Tree Forest]]. It dislikes the
        [[Mountain Top Field]].

        == Stats ==
        * Collects 10 [[Pollen]] in 3 seconds.
        * Makes 120 [[honey]] in 4 seconds.
        * -20% [[Stats#Speed|Movespeed]], +25% [[Energy]], +25% gather speed, +40 [[System
        Page#Convert Amount|Convert Amount]], +4 [[Stats#Attack|Attack]].
        * 🌟[[Gifted Bee|Gifted Hive Bonus]]: +25% [[System Page#Pollen From Tools|Pollen From
        Tools]].

        == Abilities ==
        * [[Ability Tokens#Mark|'''[Honey Mark+]''']] Marks a large random area on the
        [[Fields|Field]] for 8 seconds (+0.25s per level) that grants 2 Conversion Links and x1.25
        [[System Page#Convert Rate|Convert Rate]] while you stand on it. Stacks up to 3 times.
        * '''[[Ability Tokens#Mark|[Pollen Mark]]]''' Marks a random area on the field for 7 seconds
        (+0.2s per level) that increases all pollen up to 50% while you stand in it. Stacks up to 3
        times.

        == Trivia ==
        * Just like [[Music Bee]], this bee originated from Number1Riker's Bee Swarm showcase.
        ** Carpenter Bee and Music Bee are also the only Legendary bees added in an
        [[Updates|Update]].
        * This bee uses the Wood Texture and has a Hammer Mesh in its body.
        * This bee, [[Spicy Bee]], and [[Vector Bee]] are the only non-[[Bees/Event|Event Bees]]
        that have special body parts (Hammer Mesh).
        * This is one of two bees added in the [[Updates#2018.11.25|11/25/2018 Update]], along with
        [[Stubborn Bee]].
        * One reason Carpenter Bee likes the Pine Tree Forest might be because pine trees are made
        of wood.
        ** One reason Carpenter Bee dislikes the [[Mountain Top Field]] might be because on the tops
        of tall mountains, there are no trees.
        * Carpenter Bee, along with [[Honey Bee]] and [[Bumble Bee]], are the only bees named after
        their real-life counterparts.
        * Carpenter Bee is the only bee that can generate the Honey Mark+ ability token.
        * This bee was one of the bees to have a specific jelly in the [[Bee Bear's Catalog]]. The
        jelly was given as part of the Pinecone Bundle.
