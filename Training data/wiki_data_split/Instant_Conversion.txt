{"text": "'''Instant Conversion''' is a [[Mechanics|mechanic]] that instantly converts some of the
        player's collected [[pollen]] into [[honey]]. The amount of the converted pollen depends on
        how much % of instant conversion you have with the support of the respective type of instant
        conversion (white, red, and blue instant conversion etc.). This effectively makes the
        player's [[Bags|bag]] bigger, in the sense that the player can collect more pollen than the
        bag will hold. The player's [[Honey Per Pollen]] stat will boost the amount of honey
        received from instant conversion. There are also color variants for each [[Flowers|flower]]
        color ([[:Category:Blue|blue]], [[:Category:Red|red]], [[:Category:Colorless|white]]) that
        only instantly convert from their respective pollen colors.

        == Instant Conversion Variants ==
        Instant Conversion has 7 variants, consisting of Flame Conversion, Field Conversion (e.g.
        [[Field Wind|field winds]]), Colored Conversion, Goo Conversion, Bomb Conversion, Unique
        Instant Conversion, and during the Beesmas 2020 update, Bee Gather Conversion. Instant
        Conversion functions in the order of Goo Conversion, Field Conversion, Colored Conversion,
        then finally regular Instant Conversion, For example, if a player were to have 50% of the 4
        variants of conversion, and they collected 10,000 red pollen, it would convert 50% of that,
        then another 50% of that outcome, and so on. Goo conversion converts a flat amount of pollen
        equal to a percentage of the [[goo]] collected from a flower. Unique Instant Conversion is
        applied independently of all other instant conversion, for example if you have 2 different
        sources of 50% Unique Instant Conversion it would apply the 50% conversion twice giving you
        effectively 75% Instant Conversion instead of 100%.

        == Increasing Instant Conversion ==

        * Instant Conversion is increased by [[Enzymes]], [[Tropical Drink]]s, the [[Badge#Cactus
        Badges|Cactus Badge]], [[Gifted Bee|Gifted]] [[Photon Bee]] or with certain [[items]].
        * Instant White Conversion is increased by amulets, the [[Badges#Pineapple Badge|Pineapple
        Badge]], Gifted [[Windy Bee]] or with the [[Coconut Canister]].
        * Instant Red Conversion is increased by the [[Badges#Mushroom Badge|Mushroom Badge]],
        Gifted [[Crimson Bee]] or with certain items.
        * Instant Blue Conversion is increased by the [[Badges#Blue Flower Badge|Blue Flower
        Badge]], Gifted [[Cobalt Bee]] or with certain items.
        * Instant Field Conversion (field winds) are granted by donating to the [[Wind Shrine]] or
        from certain [[codes]].
        * Goo Conversion is increased by the [[Shell Amulet]], [[Passive Abilities#Gummy Morph|Gummy
        Morph]], or [[Passive Abilities#Gummy Star|Gummy Star]].
        * Instant Flame Conversion is granted by the [[Demon Mask]] and [[Passive
        Abilities#Scorching Star|Scorching Star]].
        * Instant Bomb Conversion is granted by [[Ability Tokens#Festive Mark|Festive Mark]] and
        Gifted [[Demon Bee]].
        * Unique Instant Conversion is granted by Coconut Combo, [[Clouds]], Puffshroom Blessing,
        [[Beequip]]s and Refreshing [[Nectar]].

        == Effects ==

        * When instant conversion is in effect, and pollen text is turned on, the player will see
        yellow numbers in addition to the blue/red/white pollen numbers while they're working in the
        [[fields]]. Those yellow numbers are the amount of pollen that instantly became honey;
        Collected honey amounts also show up in yellow, but those are also listed in the bottom
        right corner of the player's screen.

        * The formula for the effective increase in capacity given by instant conversion is:
        (x/(100-x)), where x is the player's instant conversion.
