{"text": "{{Consumables
        |image = Red Extract.png
        |description = Grants x1.25 Red Pollen for 10 minutes.
        |cooldown = 1 second
        }}'''Red Extract''' is an [[:Category:Inventory|inventory item]] that was added in the
        [[Updates#2018.11.25|11/25/2018 update]]. The purpose of it is to use it as a material to
        craft [[:Category:Accessories|accessories]], or for a 10-minute boost that grants x1.25
        [[System Page#Red Pollen|Red Pollen]]. The [[Buffs & Debuffs#From Items|buff]] cannot
        stack and will only reset its timer if you used a Red Extract when there is another one
        active. Using a [[Purple Potion|purple potion]] or [[Super Smoothie|super smoothie]] will
        override the buff.
        [[File:Red extract token bss.png|thumb|A red extract token.]]

        == Ways to Obtain ==
        * [[Crafting]] it via [[Blender]] for:
        ** {{Cost|Strawberry|50}}
        ** {{Cost|Royal Jelly|10}}
        * Matching a pair of red extracts in [[Memory Match#Mega Memory Match|Mega Memory Match]]
        (worth 1 red extract) or [[Memory Match#Extreme Memory Match|Extreme Memory Match]] (worth 3
        red extracts).
        * As a reward from the [[Wind Shrine]].
        * As a Stack Reward for adding certain [[Stickers]] to the [[Sticker Stack]]:
        ** Flying Rad Bee rewards 1 red extract.
        ** Round Rascal Bee rewards 1 red extract.
        ** Little Scorpion rewards 1 red extract.
        ** Red X rewards 1 red extract.
        ** AFK rewards 1 red extract.
        ** Small Flame rewards 1 red extract.
        ** Shrugging Heart rewards 1 red extract.
        ** Red Doodle Person rewards 1 red extract.
        ** Scythe rewards 1 red extract.
        ** Dark Scythe rewards 1 red extract.

        === Drops ===
        * As a rare drop from [[ladybug]]s and [[scorpion]]s.
        * As a common drop from the [[King Beetle]] and [[Aphid#Rage Aphid|Rage Aphids]].
        * Farming in the [[Mushroom Field]], [[Strawberry Field]], [[Rose Field]], or the [[Pepper
        Patch]] can rarely drop red extracts.
        * A rare drop from [[Leaves]] in the Mushroom Field, Strawberry Field, or the Rose Field.
        * As a rare drop from [[Sprout]]s (the odds increase depending on the rarity of the sprout
        and if the sprout is located in red fields).
        * As a drop from a [[Ability Tokens#Festive Gift|Festive Gift]] (if [[Festive Bee]] is
        [[Gifted Bee|gifted]]) or [[Ability Tokens#Festive%20Mark|Festive Mark]].
        * As a rare gift from a [[Cub Buddy|cub buddy]].
        * As a drop from the certain [[Planters]]:
        ** The {{Cost|Red Clay Planter|||0}}, and {{Cost|Heat-Treated Planter|||0}} are guaranteed
        to drop red extracts.
        *** The {{Cost|Heat-Treated Planter|||0}} drops extra red extracts in red fields (the
        Mushroom Field, Strawberry Field, Rose Field, and the Pepper Patch).
        *** Planting the {{Cost|Heat-Treated Planter|||0}} in the Mushroom Field, then the
        Strawberry Field, then the Rose Field, then the Pepper Patch results in an additional 100
        Red Extracts (36 day cooldown).
        ** Planters harvested in red fields may drop red extracts.

        === Quests ===
        * Completing every [[Gifted Riley Bee|Gifted Riley Bee's]] [[quests]] will always reward one
        Red Extract.
        * [[Black Bear]] gives Red Extracts on the following quests:
        ** Get Red-y gives 1 Red Extract and other items.
        ** Rocky Red Mountain gives 1 Red Extract and other items.
        ** Capsaicin Collector gives 1 Red Extract and other items.
        ** Black Bear's repeatable quests occasionally give Red Extracts.
        * [[Science Bear]] gives red extracts on the following quests:
        ** Meticulously Crafted gives 1 Red Extract and other items.
        ** Mushroom Measurement Monotony gives 3 Red Extracts and other items.
