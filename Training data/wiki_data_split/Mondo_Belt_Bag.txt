        ==Trivia==

        *This is the second belt accessory awarded by [[Sun Bear]] on his first visit, the first
        being the Belt Bag.
        *In the [[Updates#2018.09.10|9/10/2018 update]], Sun Bear returned for a second visit
        wearing the Mondo Belt Bag and the [[Beekeeper's Boots]]. The Mondo Belt Bag worn by Sun
        Bear is much bigger than the player's.
        *Before the [[Updates#2018.11.25|11/25/2018 update]], the Mondo Belt Bag didn't require
        stingers, pineapples, or sunflower seeds to be purchased.
        *This is the 4th strongest belt accessory in the game, behind the [[Honeycomb Belt]],
        [[Petal Belt]] and the [[Coconut Belt]].
        *This is one of the few items that went from being a quest reward to being purchasable in a
        [[:Category:Shops|shop]].
