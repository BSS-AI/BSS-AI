        '''Gummy Bear''' is an [[:Category:NPC|NPC]] that resides in [[Gummy Bear's Lair|his Lair]],
        killing those who go near him without purchasing the [[Gummy Mask]] or the [[Gummy Boots]].
        When the player has the Gummy Mask and/or Boots crafted, the player doesn't need them
        equipped to talk to him. The player must have the [[Badges#Goo Badge|Goo Hotshot Badge]] or
        above to enter [[Gummy Bear's Lair]], plus have 20 bees to access the [[Ant Gate|20 Bee
        Zone]] as the [[Gummy Bee Egg Claim]] is at the entrance of it.

        His first appearance on the mountain was during the Gummy Invasion on May 26, 2018. He gave
        15 quests that could have rewarded the player a [[Eggs#Event Bee Eggs|Gummy Bee egg]] at the
        end of the questline. Gummy Bear left on June 24, 2018.

        During the Part 2 of [[:Category:Beesmas|Beesmas]] 2020, 2021, and 2022, he once again
        became a quest giving NPC, this time with a single quest, Gummy Bear's Goo Years Beacon.
        Currently, the player can either talk to him, which gives a short paragraph of dialogue
        about the Gummy Army or claim his Beesmas quest or redeem its rewards, give him a present if
        you have one or by having him teleporting the player back to their [[Hive]].

        Before Gummy Bear left on June 24, 2018, he had his [[Gummy Cannon]] which he used to shoot
        [[gumdrops]] at fields to cover flowers with [[Goo]]. He targeted every field except the
        [[Mountain Top Field]] (and the [[Stump Field]], [[Coconut Field]], and [[Pepper Patch]], as
        they weren't in the game at that time). In Beesmas 2020 and Beesmas 2021, the Gummy Siege
        can drop gumdrops at the [[Mountain Top Field]], Stump Field, Coconut Field, and Pepper
        Patch.

        If the player would like to see Gummy Bear without losing their pollen, the player should
        empty out their bag before going into the lair. If the player is already inside the lair,
        the player can use a [[Micro-Converter]] or a [[Whirligig]]. If the player does not favor
        this tactic, the player can convert some of the pollen in their bag via conversion links,
        which can happen with a [[Ability Tokens#Bear Morph|Science Bear Morph]] or a [[Passive
        Abilities#Scorching Star|Scorching Star]]. However, this must be activated before seeing
        him. <br/>

        ==Trivia==

        *Gummy Bear was originally a [[:Category:Traveling Bears|Traveling Bear]] who arrived on May
        26, 2018, and left on June 24, 2018 (30 days, or 4 weeks and 2 days). He gave 15 [[quests]]
        with a final reward of a [[Eggs#Event Bee Eggs|Gummy Bee Egg]]. He came back in the
        [[Updates#2018.11.25|11/25/2018 Update]].
        *Gummy Bear brought the following during his visit to the mountain:
        **A Gummy Bee, which could have been seen flying around him.
        ***Gummy Bear was the first bear to introduce his own bee, with [[Bee Bear]] being the
        second, introducing [[Festive Bee]].
        **A [[Gummy Cannon]], which he used to fire gumdrops at [[Fields]].
        **The [[Gumdrop Shop]] where the player could buy gumdrops with [[Ticket]]s (which is
        currently near [[Panda Bear]]).
        *Gummy Bear is translucent, like the candy he is named after. (His colors of pink and teal,
        on the other hand, don't resemble any of the colors of [[Wikipedia:Gummy Bear|Classic Gummy
        Bear Candy]].)
        **Gummy Bear is the first and currently only [[:Category:Bears|Bear]] to have unnatural
        colors.
        ***While Robo Bear also has artificial fur colors, they are a robot.
        * He and the Gummy Bee are made of the same goo, according to his dialogue.
        *Gummy Bear is one of two bears (the other being [[Shadow Bear]]) to float, rather than
        stand on the ground.
        *Gummy Bear appears in the [[Badges#Goo Badge|Goo Badge]] for its icon.
        *Gummy Bear uses the [https://www.roblox.com/catalog/619547874/Levitation-Animation-Pack
        Levitation Animation Pack].
        *Gummy Bear was featured in the \"Dance Party\" advert.
        * After the Gummy Invasion, he returned in the Nov. 25 2018 update and resides in his lair
        that can be accessed through the Gummy Bee Egg Claim near the [[Ant Challenge]].
        ** He is the second bear out of three bears that can kill the player, the first being
        [[Tunnel Bear]] and the third being [[Snowbear]].
        **He is also the first and only quest-giver that can kill the player.
        *Gummy Bear, [[Tunnel Bear]] and [[Onett]] were \"Naughty\" during the Beesmas Event, as
        stated by Bee Bear.
        *Like all of the permanent bears (except [[Spirit Bear]] and [[Dapper Bear]]), he also has
        his own Bear Morph. Unlike them, the player doesn't need a [[Bear Bee]] to get it, but
        rather it can be activated by Gummy Mask's [[Passive Abilities#Gummy Morph|Gummy Morph
        Passive Ability]].
        **Gummy Bear is the only traveling bear to have a morph of his own.
        *Spirit Bear has stated that Gummy Bear was made out of gumdrops. She also stated that he
        was the one who introduced goo to the game.
        *Gummy Bear is “Faux Affably Evil” because he talks about evil plans while also pretending
        to be your friend. You only participated in his evil plans to get the egg.
        * According to [[Science Bear]], Gummy Bear is in fact an alien.
        *Gummy Bear, [[Sun Bear]] and Bee Bear have all had a second visit to the mountain, but
        Gummy Bear is the only one to stay permanently (for now).
        *Gummy Bear is the only bear to have used a cannon (the Gummy Cannon).
        *Gummy Bear used goo collection as the main requirement for his quests.
        *Gummy Bear was the first bear that requires the usage of
        [[:Category:Transport|transportation]] devices (cannons and the [[Slingshot]]) to complete
        his quests.
        *In every single quest, Gummy Bear made a goo-related pun.
        *Gummy Bear gave a [[Codes|Code]] in the dialogue of one of his last quests, which was the
        \"Tapioca\" code.
        *In total, the players could have received up to 31 tickets from him.
        *[[Onett (Developer)|Onett]] added 1 more week to the Gummy Bear's timer when he first
        arrived because many players requested an extension.
        *Gummy Bear is one of three NPCs to have moved locations, with the other two being [[Honey
        Bee (NPC)|Honey Bee]] and [[Bubble Bee Man]].
        *Ever since the Beesmas 2020 Event, Gummy Bear returned as an NPC in his lair.
        *He was voted as the strongest [[:Category:Bears|Bear]] in Onett’s StrawPoll
        voting.<ref>[https://twitter.com/OnettDev/status/1611171069540409345] Tweet posted by
        Onett linking the poll</ref>
