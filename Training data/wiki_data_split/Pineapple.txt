        [[File:Pineapple Token.png|thumb|A Pineapple token.|130px]]
        A {{Color|Pineapple}} is a type of [[Treats|Treat]] that can be fed to a [[Bees|bee]] to
        raise its bond by 25. However, when fed to a [[Hasty Bee|Hasty]], [[Brave Bee|Brave]],
        [[Honey Bee]], [[Exhausted Bee|Exhausted]], [[Shocked Bee|Shocked]], [[Fire Bee|Fire]],
        [[Demon Bee|Demon]], [[Lion Bee|Lion]], or [[Fuzzy Bee|Fuzzy]] Bee, the amount of [[bond]]
        they gain is doubled. These bees also have a small chance of becoming gifted when fed
        pineapples.

        == Ways to Obtain ==
        * 5 pineapples can be found behind the pineapple near the [[Treat Dispenser]].
        * 1 pineapple is given to the player every time they use the Treat Dispenser.
        * As a reward from the [[Ant Challenge]].
        * Redeeming certain valid [[codes]]:
        ** 1MLikes (Gives 1 pineapple + other stuff).
        ** 10mMembers (Gives 10 pineapples + other stuff).
        * As a reward from [[Memory Match|Memory Matches]] (Every type except [[Memory Match#Night
        Memory Match|Night memory match]] and the [[Memory Match#Winter Memory Match|Winter memory
        match]]).
        * As a stack reward for adding certain [[Stickers]] to the [[Sticker Stack]]:
        ** {{Cost|Shocked Hive Slot Sticker|||0}} rewards 50 pineapples.
        ** {{Cost|Rhubarb Sticker|||0}} rewards 50 pineapples.

        ===Drops===
        *Farming in the [[Pineapple Patch]].
        **Harvesting [[leaves]] in this field may also yield pineapples.
        *As a drop from [[Mantis|mantises]], [[scorpion]]s, [[spider]]s, [[Werewolf|werewolves]],
        [[Commando Chick]], [[Stick Nymph]]s, [[Stick Bug]], [[King Beetle]], and [[Tunnel Bear]].
        *As a drop from a [[Ability Tokens#Festive Gift|Festive Gift]] or [[Ability Tokens#Festive
        Mark|Festive Mark]].
        *As a gift from a [[Cub Buddy|cub buddy]].
        *As a reward for completely growing any kind of [[sprout]] except for Moon, Gummy, and Debug
        sprouts.
        **Planting a sprout in the Pineapple Patch yields 7x more pineapples than other fields.
        *Harvesting [[sparkles]] (Higher chances in the Pineapple Patch).
        *As a drop from [[Puffshroom]]s.
        *As a drop from planters, which include:
        **{{Cost|Ticket Planter}}
        **{{Cost|Festive Planter}}
        **{{Cost|Plastic Planter}}
        **{{Cost|Candy Planter}}
        **{{Cost|Blue Clay Planter}}
        **{{Cost|Red Clay Planter}}
        **{{Cost|Pesticide Planter}}
        **{{Cost|The Planter Of Plenty}}

        ===Quests===
        *[[Onett]] gives pineapples on the following quests:
        **Star Journey 1 gives 25 pineapples.
        *Spirit Bear gives pineapples on the following quests:
        **Witness Grandeur gives 100 pineapples.
        **Nature's Lessons gives 250 pineapples.
        **Rhubarb That Could Have Been gives 250 pineapples.
        **Do You Bee-lieve in Magic? gives 300 pineapples.
        **Beans Becoming gives 500 pineapples.
        **Echoing Call gives 500 pineapples upon receiving the quest.
