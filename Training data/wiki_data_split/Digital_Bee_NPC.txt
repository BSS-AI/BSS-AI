        '''Digital Bee''' (also known as '''?NULL''', '''0x4C4F4C''', and '''>> ☺''') is an
        NPC located inside [[The Computer]]. Digital Bee is involved in the [[Ready Player Two]]
        event, acting as a quest NPC, giving out three puzzles for the player to complete.

        The player cannot interact with Digital Bee until they buy, and equip, the [[Strange
        Goggles]] that can be found in the [[Noob Shop]].

        Upon completing puzzle 3, the player can walk into the screen where Digital Bee originally
        was and claim a [[Royal Jelly#Star Jelly|Star Jelly]]. The “[[How to Program BASIC]]” book
        also takes Digital Bee’s place until the player leaves the game.

        Its appearance is a computer with a pixelated smiley face (or the symbols it gives). It has
        an antenna and a body and wings similar to a regular bee.

        ==Trivia==
        *Digital Bee is the only NPC that doesn't communicate using words/dialogue and uses symbols
        instead.
        *Some of the sounds it makes are Windows XP sounds but modified slightly. When it first
        appears, the (slightly modified) Windows XP startup sound will play.
        *Before the [[Updates|12/26/22]] update, Digital Bee and [[Marshmallow Bee]] were the only
        bees that the player couldn't have in their hive.
        **Since Digital Bee has now been added in as an obtainable bee for the player's hive, this
        only applies to Marshmallow Bee now.
        *This bee is the only NPC bee that gives puzzles rather than quests.
        *This is one of the two bees that give star jellies for completing quests, the other one
        being [[Honey Bee (NPC)|Honey Bee.]]
