        The '''Naughty List''' is a recurring [[:Category:Beesmas|Beesmas]] machine that could be
        unlocked after completing [[Bubble Bee Man]]'s Beesmas quest. During Beesmas, it would be
        located on top of the [[Bear Gate|30 Bee Gate]], between [[Memory Match|Night Memory Match]]
        and Bubble Bee Man.

        It could be used to try and kill all the players in the server by summoning falling coal on
        top of them; this could easily be avoided by getting out of the area (which is marked with a
        giant green circle) where the coal is going to fall. Shall a player be killed by the falling
        coal, the player would receive a beequip called [[Lump Of Coal|Lump of Coal]] and a [[Buffs
        & Debuffs|buff]] called Beesmas Repentance, which grants 1.05x Capacity, +1% Bubble
        Pollen, +1% Blue Pollen, +1% [[Blue Flower Field]] Pollen, +1% [[Pine Tree Forest]] Pollen,
        +1% [[Stump Field]] Pollen, +1% Movement Collection Pollen, +1% [[Bee Attack]] and +1%
        [[Loot Luck]] for 2 hours. Players could have used the Naughty List every 8 hours.

        == Trivia ==

        * If a player attempted to use it without completing Bubble Bee Man's quest, it will say,
        \"''There aren't any names on the Naughty List. That can't be right...''\"
        * If a player stood on a mob while the coal was falling, it would kill the player and deal
        100 damage to the [[Mobs|mob]].
        * This was initially meant to kill AFK players as Onett stated on Discord.
        * The names that the player could see that are stated are: Aardvark Joe, Apple BEe
        ''[sic]'', and Applebee's Cofounder 1.
        * The Naughty List had the same longest cooldown for Beesmas 2020, tied with [[Onett's Lid
        Art]] and [[Gummy Siege]].
        * This was the only machine to kill both players and hostile mobs.
        * The Beesmas Repentance Buff does not stack. However, the duration of the buff stacks.
        * After finishing Bubble Bee Man's quest, he said that the player could use it every 22
        hours. However, the player could actually use it every 8 hours.
