        The '''Satisfying Vial''' is a consumable [[Nectar Vial|nectar vial]] [[Items|item]] that
        was added in the [[Updates#2022.12.26|12/26/2022 update]]. Once used, it grants the player 4
        hours of the [[Nectar|Satisfying Nectar buff]] instantly. It can also be used to craft other
        [[:Category:Accessories|accessories]] and [[tools]].

        == Ways to Obtain ==
        * Completing [[Dapper Bear|Dapper Bear's]] 5th, 11th and 15th quests grants 1 Satisfying
        Vial and other items.
        * Using the [[Nectar Condenser]] to condense Satisfying Nectar.
        * Adding the Satisfying Nectar Icon to the [[Sticker Stack]] grants 1 Satisfying Vial.

        === The methods below are no longer possible. ===
        * Purchasing the Hefty Honeyday Bundle from [[Bee Bear's Catalog]] granted 5 Satisfying
        Vials and other items.
        * Giving [[Polar Bear]] a [[Present]] granted 1 Satisfying Vial and other items.
        * Opening the [[Gift Boxes#2022|Gooey Gift Box]] granted 1 Satisfying Vial and other items.
        * Purchasing the Angelic Bundle from [[Bee Bear's Catalog]] granted 1 Satisfying Vial and
        other items.
        * Purchasing the Nighttime Nectar Pack in the [[Robux Shop]], granting 1 of each Nectar
        Vial.
        * Completing [[Bee Bear|Bee Bear's]] [[Beesmas|Beesmas 2022]] 20th quest granted 5
        Satisfying Vials.
