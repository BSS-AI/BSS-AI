        The '''Petal''' '''Wand''' is a [[Tools|tool]] added in the [[Updates|9/28/2019 update]]
        that can be crafted in the [[Petal Shop]].

        It collects 6 [[pollen]] from 37 patches in 0.7 seconds, and boosts pollen collected by
        100%. It has an average base pollen collection rate of 634.28 pollen per second. It emits a
        yellow particle effect at the top of the tool, similar to the [[Petal Belt]].

        Every 3rd swing, it fires a Petal Shuriken, which causes [[bees]] to instantly convert
        pollen if it touches their hitbox. The shuriken instantly converts 10,000 + 6% of the bees'
        [[System Page#Convert Amount|convert amount]] in pollen to honey, + an additional 6% per
        [[Field Boost|field boost]] stack of the field you're standing in. Shurikens can also pop
        [[Passive Abilities#Gathering Bubbles|bubbles]] and collect [[Ability Tokens#Fuzz Bombs|fuzz
        bombs]].
