        The '''Tacky Planter''' is a reusable planter added in 12/26/21 update. It can be purchased
        in [[Dapper Bear's Shop]].

        {| class=\"article-table\"
        !Crafting Ingredients
        |-
        |{{Cost|Honey|50,000,000}}<br>{{Cost|Magic Bean|10}}<br>{{Cost|Purple
        Potion|1}}<br>{{Cost|Hard Wax|5}}<br>{{Cost|Soft Wax|20}}
        |}

        It grows 25% faster (6 hours), grants 100% more pollen on harvest in the [[Starter Zone]],
        and grants 25% more Satisfying and Comforting Nectar. Harvesting a fully grown Tacky Planter
        has a 1/7 (14.28%) chance to spawn a [[Puffshroom]].
