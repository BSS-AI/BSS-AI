        '''Bear Bee''' is a [[:Category:Colorless|Colorless]] [[Bees/Event|Event bee]]. It can be
        purchased in the [[Robux Shop]] for 800 Robux.

        Similar to all other Event bees, this bee does not have a favorite treat, and can only
        become [[Gifted Bee|Gifted]] if the player bought it during its release, by feeding it a
        [[Star Treat|Star Treat]], [[Gingerbread Bear]]s or [[Aged Gingerbread Bear]]s.

        Bear Bee likes the [[Pumpkin Patch]] and the [[Pine Tree Forest]]. It dislikes the [[Blue
        Flower Field]].

        == Stats ==
        * Collects 15 [[Pollen]] in 2 seconds.
        * Makes 200 [[Honey]] in 2 seconds.
        * +75% [[Energy]], +50% gather and convert speed, +5 [[Stats#Gather Amount|Gather Amount]],
        +120 [[System Page#Convert Amount|Convert Amount]], +4 [[Stats#Attack|Attack]].
        * 🌟 [[Gifted Bee|Gifted Hive Bonus]]: +10% [[System Page#Pollen|Pollen]].

        === Abilities ===
        * '''[[Ability Tokens#Bear Morph|[Bear Morph]]]''' Transforms you into a random bear! Grants
        ×2 Pollen and boosts [[System Page#Movespeed|Speed]] and [[System Page#Jump Power|Jump
        Power]]. If Gifted, has a 20% chance to transform the player into a rare bear that gives
        special boosts (either [[Mother Bear]], which gives x2.5 Pollen and an additional x1.5
        [[System Page#Bee Gather Pollen|Pollen from Bee Gathering]] or [[Science Bear]], which adds
        +1 Conversion Link).

        == Trivia ==
        *Bear Morph’s extra jump power and speed can give early access to things such as [[Royal
        Jelly|royal jellies]] and [[ticket]]s that can normally only be accessed later in the game.
        * The First-edition [[Robux Shop|Bear Bee Pack]] was available in the first month of the
        game's release for 650 Robux. Later, when it went on sale again, it cost 1,000 Robux. It
        currently costs 800 Robux.
        *This was the first Event bee added to the game and the only Event bee which was in the
        original release.
        *This was the first bee that is obtainable with Robux, the second being [[Festive Bee]], the
        third being [[Windy Bee]], the fourth being [[Fuzzy Bee]], the fifth being [[Puppy Bee]],
        the sixth and seventh being [[Precise Bee]] and [[Buoyant Bee]], the eighth being [[Digital
        Bee]]. However, it is the only bee that is exclusively obtainable with Robux, as Festive Bee
        was also obtainable through [[quests]], tickets, and the [[Ornament Presents|Festive
        Present]], Windy Bee is obtainable from the [[Wind Shrine]], Puppy Bee is bought with
        tickets, Precise, Fuzzy and Buoyant can be hatched from eggs or royal jelly, Digital Bee is
        bought with drives, cogs, and honey.
        *This bee, Festive Bee, [[Gummy Bee]], [[Vicious Bee]], Windy Bee, and Digital Bee are the
        only Event bees obtainable without spending tickets.
        **Gummy Bee was previously purchasable with tickets and the Festive Bee is currently
        purchasable for 500 tickets.
        *This bee, Gummy Bee, [[Tabby Bee]], Puppy Bee, Festive Bee, Windy Bee, and Digital Bee are
        the only bees that can be a First Edition Bee.
        *Before the [[Updates#2018.11.25|11/25/2018 update]], Bear Bee's [[Gifted Bee#List of Hive
        Bonuses|Gifted Hive Bonus]] was +20% [[System Page#White Pollen|White Pollen]]. It was later
        changed to +40% White Pollen and then to +5% Pollen. The current [[Gifted Bee#List of Hive
        Bonuses|Gifted Hive Bonus]] is +10% Pollen.
        *This bee, Tabby Bee, Puppy Bee, [[Tadpole Bee]], and [[Lion Bee]] are the only bees based
        on real life animals other than bees.
        *This bee, Festive Bee, Gummy Bee, [[Photon Bee]], and Tabby Bee are the only bees to have a
        gifted bonus that affects its signature ability.
        **This enhanced Bear Morph in the gifted hive bonus, adding Science Bear and Mother Bear
        morphs, was added in the [[Updates#2019.04.17|4/17/2019 Update]].
        *This bee is the only bee who can transform the player into a bear, and one of three ways to
        do so. The other ways are by using the [[Gummy Mask]], which can transform the player into
        [[Gummy Bear]], and redeeming certain valid [[Codes]].
        *This is the only Event bee that can become gifted without the use of a star treat or
        gingerbread bear, as all First Edition Bear Bees become automatically gifted when the owner
        joins the game if they are not gifted yet.
        *There is a glitch in which dying with bear morph active will leave the player's character
        without a head when it deactivates.
