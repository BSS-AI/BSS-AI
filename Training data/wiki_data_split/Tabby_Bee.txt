        bee]] that hatches out of a [[Eggs#Event Bee Eggs|Tabby Bee egg]], which is currently
        available in the [[Ticket Tent]] for 500 [[Ticket]]s.

        Like all other Event bees, this bee does not have a favorite Treat, and the only way to make
        it gifted is by feeding it a [[Star Treat|Star Treat]] or [[Gingerbread Bear]]s.

        Tabby Bee likes the [[Spider Field]] and [[Clover Field]]. It dislikes the [[Cactus Field]].
        == Stats ==
        * Collects 10 [[Pollen]] in 4 seconds.
        * Makes 160 [[Honey]] in 3 seconds.
        * +50% [[Energy]], +15% [[Stats#Speed|Movespeed]], +25% Convert Speed, +3
        [[Stats#Attack|Attack]], up to +1680 [[System Page#Convert Amount|Convert Amount]], up to
        +100 [[Stats#Gather Amount|Gather Amount]].
        * 🌟 [[Gifted Bee|Gifted Hive Bonus]]: +50% [[System Page#Critical Power|Critical Power]].
        Unlike other bees, this Bee has a unique perk: its stats increase permanently with the
        collection of tabby love tokens. At 1,000 Tabby Love, this bee has the best Conversion Rate
        in the game, with its gather amount being surpassed by only [[Digital Bee]]. Its maximum
        stats are:
        * Collects 110 Pollen in 4 seconds.
        * Makes 1760 Honey in 3 seconds.
        * Collects 88 Pollen from 3 lines of 4 flowers with Scratch (1,056 pollen).

        === Abilities ===
        * '''[[Ability Tokens#Scratch|[Scratch]]]''' Collects 7 (+1 per lvl) Pollen from 3 lines of
        4 [[Flowers]]. If Gifted, Scratch always does Critical hits.
        * '''[[Ability Tokens#Tabby Love|[Tabby Love]]]''' Permanently grants Tabby Bee +1% Gather
        Amount, Convert Amount, and pollen from Scratch. Stacks up to 1,000 times.
        ** If at max stacks, Tabby Love grants Tabby Blessing, giving +1% Critical Chance and +25%
        Critical Power for 20 seconds (+2s per level). If Gifted, it grants Tabby Blessing+, which
        adds +1% Super-Crit Chance as well.

        == Trivia ==
        * When Tabby Bee was added, the maximum tabby love stack was mistakenly set to 500. This
        mistake was fixed in a later update.
        * The price of Tabby Bee was 250 tickets when it was originally released.
        * There used to be an [[Easter Eggs#Tabby Bee Block (Removed)|Easter Egg]] behind the hives,
        which was a block with a distorted photo of Tabby Bee on it. It was removed around 2 June
        2018. This was possibly a hint for one of the [[codes]], more specifically, \"Meow\", which
        gave five tickets before it expired.
        * Tabby Bee is one of three bees to have an official plush on the
        [https://shop.beeswarm.com/ Official Bee Swarm Simulator Website], the others being [[Basic
        Bee]] and [[Vicious Bee]].
        * Because of a timer glitch that stopped players from buying it early, Tabby Bee returned on
        sale between June 2 and June 6, 2018.
        * Tabby Bee was the third Event bee to be added to the game, after [[Photon Bee]] and [[Bear
        Bee]] respectively.
        * This bee, Bear Bee, [[Gummy Bee]], [[Puppy Bee]], [[Festive Bee]], and [[Windy Bee]] are
        the only Event bees which can be a first edition bee when hatching them during its first
        release in-game.
        * Tabby Bee, Bear Bee, Gummy Bee, Festive Bee, and Photon Bee are the only bees to have a
        gifted bonus that affects its signature ability.
        * The player’s tabby love stack will not be removed if the player transforms Tabby Bee in
        any way.
        * Before the May 23, 2024 update, if the player already had max Tabby Love stacks and
        collected another one, it would neither increase the existing buff nor have any effect.
        * Tabby Bee is a reference to [[Onett (Developer)|Onett's]] real-life cat named Sam. This is
        hinted in one of [[Onett|Onett's]] dialogues in his star journey [[quests]], and later
        confirmed on Discord.
        * Tabby Bee, along with Basic Bee and [[Rascal Bee]], are Onett's favorite bees.
        * Tabby Bee is mentioned in [[Panda Bear]]'s dialogue during his Ultimate Ant Annihilation 1
        quest. Like Tabby Bee's description, he said that Tabby Bee was raised by cats.
        * Tabby Bee is one of the few bees based off from animals other than just bees, the others
        being [[Lion Bee]], [[Tadpole Bee]], Puppy Bee, and Bear Bee.
        *Tabby Bee, despite having a 50% energy increase compared to Basic bee listed in its stats,
        has a base energy of 28, which is only comparable to a 40% energy increase.
        * This was one of the few bees that could have been summoned from [[Onett's Lid Art]].
        ** If a Tabby Bee from Onett's Lid Art produced a tabby love token, the buff would not be
        removed after the Tabby Bee despawns.
