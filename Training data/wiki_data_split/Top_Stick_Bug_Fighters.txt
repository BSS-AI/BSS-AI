        The '''Global Top Stick Bug Fighters Leaderboard''' is one of the 62 [[leaderboards]] in the
        game. It can display 10 leaderboard positions at a time
        If the player stands on the white pad in front of the leaderboard, <span
        style=\"background-color:#0090BF; color:white;\">\"Your highscore:
        \"..ScoreVariable..\"\"</span> \"ScoreVariable\" being the score obtained from the [[Stick
        Bug Challenge]] This allows for players to view their current Highscore for the Stick Bug
        Challenge. This leaderboard tracks the top 100 scores from fighting [[Stick Bug]] in the
        Stick Bug Challenge. The player can scroll down the leaderboard to see more players, all the
        way to the 100th place.

        The Top Stick Bug Fighters leaderboard was reset at one point due to glitches with the
        fight. The glitches were fixed on 9/27/19

        It was later reset again on 12/26/2022[[File:Stickbug and Stickbug
        Leaderboard.png|thumb|Stick Bug next to the Top Stick Bug Fighters leaderboard.]]

        The leaderboard was recently reset once more in 1/12/2024 due to the technique of using
        attack buffs in Robo Bear's Challenge to increase damage drastically.

        ==Location==
        It is located right behind Stick Bug, on top of the biggest mushroom in the [[Mushroom
        Field]]. One method to get there is to use the [[Red Cannon|Red]], [[Blue Cannon|Blue]], or
        [[Yellow Cannon]]s paired with either the [[Parachute]] or [[Glider]]. Another way to get
        there is to parachute or glide from a tall place, like near the [[Lion Bee Gate]]. Doing the
        [[Obstacle Courses#Mushroom Field Obby|Mushroom Field Obby]] and jumping and gliding to the
        tallest mushroom is also a viable solution.

        ==Achievements==
        *zlib was the first player to reach a score of 100 million or more.
        *Rara0w0x3 was the first player to reach a score of 1 billion or more.

        == Trivia ==
        * This leaderboard, the [[Highest Snowbear Level]] leaderboard, and the [[Fastest Crab
        Slayers]] leaderboard are the only three leaderboards to rank \"[[:Category:Bosses|bosses]]\".
        * After the leaderboard got reset again in 1/12/2024, the score displayed when stepping on
        the podium is your score before the reset. But it does not display in the leaderboard.
