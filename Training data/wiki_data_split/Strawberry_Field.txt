        This field can spawn [[honey]], [[Strawberry|Strawberry]], [[Ability Tokens#Boost|Red
        Boost]], [[Red Extract]], [[Bitterberry]], [[Ticket]] and [[Royal Jelly]] tokens while
        collecting [[pollen]].

        [[Bees]] that like the Strawberry Field are [[Fire Bee]], [[Riley Bee]], and [[Shy Bee]].
        Bees that dislike the Strawberry Field are [[Cool Bee]], [[Bubble Bee]], [[Bucko Bee]], and
        [[Windy Bee]].

        This field gives Refreshing [[Nectar]] when a planter is placed here.

        == Obtaining Boosts ==
        * Using the [[Red Field Booster]] may give a Strawberry [[Field Boost]]. When it does, it
        will give +100% Strawberry Field pollen for 15 minutes.
        [[File:Fis_strawberry.png|thumb|75px|The Strawberry Field boost icon.]]
        * Using [[glitter]] in the Strawberry Field will add 100% Strawberry Field pollen onto the
        current boost for 15 minutes if the boost isn't already maxed out.
        * Using [[Field Dice|field dice]], [[Smooth Dice|smooth dice]], or [[Loaded Dice|loaded
        dice]] may add 100% - 300% Strawberry Field pollen for 15 minutes if the current boost isn't
        already maxed out and also what type of dice is used.
        *Strawberry [[Field Wind]]s can be obtained through the [[Wind Shrine]] or [[codes]]. They
        have a base of +20% instant Strawberry Field conversion, and give +5% instant Strawberry
        Field conversion and +15% Strawberry Field pollen for each stack. Strawberry Field Winds can
        stack up to a total of 15 times.
        *Redeeming certain valid [[codes]]:
        **1MLikes (Gives Strawberry Field Boost x1 + other stuff).
        **WeekExtension (Gives Strawberry Field Boost x3 + Strawberry Field Winds x10 + other
        stuff).
        *The field can be boosted by the [[King Beetle Amulet]]. If it has a Strawberry Field Boost,
        it can boost the field by +1% up to a max of +100% pollen.

        ===The methods below are no longer possible.===

        *Giving a [[present]] to certain NPCs:
        **[[Gifted Riley Bee]] gave Strawberry Field Boost x3 and other boosts and [[items]].
        ** [[Onett]] gave Strawberry Field Boost x1 and other boosts and items.
        *Purchasing a Strawberry Field [[Buffs & Debuffs#From Areas|Market Boost]] from the
        [[Boost Market]] will give x1.5 Strawberry [[Field Capacity]], x1.25 Strawberry Field
        Pollen, x1.25 [[System Page#Red Bomb Pollen|Red Bomb Pollen]], and x1.25 [[System Page#Hive
        Convert Rate|Convert Rate At Hive]].
        *Redeeming certain expired codes:
        **Friday (Gave Strawberry Field Boost x5 + other stuff).
        **SugarRush (Gave Strawberry Field Boost x3 + other stuff).
        **FruitMachine (Gave Strawberry Field Boost x1 + other stuff).
        **Berlin (Gave Strawberry Field Boost x4 + other stuff).
        ** 1mFavorites (Gave Strawberry Field Boost x1 + other stuff).
        **WikiHonor (Gave Strawberry Field Boost x3 + other stuff).
        **JollyJelly (Gave Strawberry Field Boost x3 and Strawberry Field Winds x3 + other stuff).
        ** FestiveFinale (Gave Strawberry Field Boost x3 and Strawberry Field Winds x2 + other

        ==Trivia==

        *This is the third smallest field in the game, outranked by the [[Stump Field]] and
        [[Pumpkin Patch]].
        *There is a strawberry token behind the biggest strawberry in the top left of the field.
        *This is one of six fields that [[fireflies]] can travel to during [[Day/Night
        Cycle|nighttime]], with the others being [[Bamboo Field]], [[Cactus Field]], [[Pineapple
        Patch]], [[Rose Field]], and Spider Field.
        *[[Mother Bear]] states that finding strawberry tokens are much more common in this field
        than in any other field.
        *According to [[Spirit Bear]], the strawberries in the field were planted from seeds
        imported from Sweden. She also states that [[sprout]]s in this field spawn the most
        strawberry tokens, 8 times more than normal sprouts.
        *If you run towards the strawberry on the corner fast enough, you may glitch inside of the
        strawberry. If this happens you will have to reset your character, rejoin the game, or use a
        [[Whirligig]].
        *This the only red field that gives [[Nectar|Refreshing Nectar]].
