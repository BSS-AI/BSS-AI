        The '''Blue Cannon''' is located on a rock between the [[Pine Tree Forest]] and the [[Rose
        Field]], in front of the [[Royal Jelly Shop]]. It is available to use once the player has
        discovered 16 [[Bees|bee]] types. If used without certain [[Buffs & Debuffs|buffs]] or
        [[items]], such as the [[Parachute]] or [[Glider]], it will usually shoot the player to the
        1 layer of flowers of the [[Clover Field]] closest to [[Brown Bear]]. However, it may
        occasionally undershoot the player to the [[Slingshot]] or between the [[Mushroom
        Field|Mushroom]] and the [[Dandelion Field]]. It has a 5 second cooldown period.

        It can be used (with either the Parachute or Glider) to collect the [[Royal Jelly|royal
        jelly]] token on top of the tallest dandelion in the Dandelion Field. Players (with either
        the Parachute or Glider) can also use the Blue Cannon to reach the [[Star Hall]].

        == Trivia ==

        * This is one of the four cannons in the game, which the other three cannons are [[Yellow
        Cannon]], [[Red Cannon]], and [[Gummy Cannon]].
        ** This is one of the three transportational cannons in the game, which the other two
        cannons are [[Yellow Cannon]] and [[Red Cannon]].
        * The Blue Cannon has a cooldown of 5 seconds. While this is not normally possible to see
        during regular gameplay as the cannon launches the player too far to return in 5 seconds,
        the cooldown can be seen by using the Glider/Parachute glitch to stop all momentum as soon
        as the player exits the cannon.
        **This cooldown is shared by all the transportational cannons.
