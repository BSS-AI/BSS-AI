        This field can spawn [[Sunflower Seed|sunflower seeds]], [[ticket]]s, [[Ability
        Tokens#Boost|red boosts]], blue boosts, [[oil]], and [[Magic Bean|magic bean]] tokens when
        collecting [[pollen]].

        This field gives Satisfying [[Nectar]] when a planter is placed here.

        [[Bees]] that like the Sunflower Field are [[Basic Bee]], [[Baby Bee]], [[Hasty Bee]], and
        [[Exhausted Bee]]. The only bee that dislikes the Sunflower Field is [[Looker Bee]].

        == Obtaining Boosts ==
        * Using the [[Field Booster]] may give a Sunflower [[Field Boost]]. When it does, it will
        give +300% Sunflower Field pollen for 15 minutes. [[File:Fis sunflower.png|thumb|75px|The
        Sunflower Field boost icon.]]
        * Using [[glitter]] will add 100% Sunflower Field pollen onto the current boost for 15
        minutes, if the current boost isn't already maxed out.
        * Using [[Field Dice|field dice]], [[Smooth Dice|smooth dice]], or [[Loaded Dice|loaded
        dice]] may add +100% Sunflower Field pollen for 15 minutes if the current boost isn't
        already maxed out.
        * Sunflower [[Field Wind]]s can be obtained through the [[Wind Shrine]] or [[codes]]. They
        have a base of 20% instant Sunflower Field conversion, and give +5% instant Sunflower Field
        conversion and +25% Sunflower Field pollen for each stack. Sunflower Field Winds can stack
        up to a total of 15 times.
        * Redeeming certain valid [[codes]]:
        ** Millie (Activates Sunflower Field Code Buff + other stuff).
        ** 1MLikes (Gives Sunflower Field Boost x1 + other stuff) (Have to be in group to redeem).
        * It can be boosted by the [[Shell Amulet]]. If it has a Sunflower Field Boost, it can boost
        the field by +1% to +27% depending on the amulet rarity.
        *Giving a Present to Black Bear grants 3x Sunflower Field Boost + other stuff.

        == Trivia ==
        * This is the only field in the game where the player can find sunflower seeds that spawn
        naturally, without the need of [[sprout]]s.
        * This is the first field [[Black Bear]] assigns a [[Quests|quest]] to.
        * The [[Badges#Sunflower Badge|Sunflower badges]] and the [[Badges#Pumpkin Badge|Pumpkin
        badges]] are used 3 times for chances in [[Memory Match]], the most for any field badges.
        * [[Stick Bug]] will always spawn here when the [[Stick Bug Challenge]] is activated. Stick
        Bug may return after moving to other fields.
        * This was the second-biggest field in the game, the biggest one being the [[Clover Field]].
        After the [[Updates#2018.07.11|7/11/2018 update]], this isn't the case anymore.
        ** Similar to why the Clover Field was shrunk, this field was decreased in size to make room
        for [[Mother Bear]] and the [[Treat Shop]], which can be found at the end of the field.
        * This field along with the [[Pineapple Patch]] and the [[Pumpkin Patch]] are the only
        fields where an object may come to life and shoot sparkles.
        * Once in a while, a face will appear on the sunflower closest to the [[Basic Egg Shop]],
        where it will proceed to shoot magic onto a group of 13 random flowers (in diamond
        formation), thus generating sparkles. Flowers with sparkles will give random rewards such as
        [[honey]], treats, and sunflower seeds. (The sparkles are just like the
        [[Fireflies|firefly]] sparkles.)
