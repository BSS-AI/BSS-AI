        {{Quote|The official mask of a Gummy Soldier.}}
        The '''Gummy Mask''' is a [[:Category:Masks|Mask]] available for purchase in [[Gummy Bear's
        Lair]]. It can be reached by touching the [[Gummy Bee]] model on the [[Gummy Bee Egg Claim]]
        and using a [[Gumdrops|Gumdrop]] (This only works if the [[Badges#Goo Badge|Goo Hotshot]]
        badge is/was owned).

        Once 30 regular gumdrops are used or 10 [[Ability Tokens]] from Gummy Bee are collected,
        Gummy Mask's passive ability (Gummy Morph) activates with the following sound file:
        [[File:gbm.ogg]]

        The appearance of the Gummy Mask has a teal gumdrop on top emitting tiny bubbles. Below that
        is a pink dome with a neon teal disc and a pink disc underneath. The Gummy Bee face is
        placed on a pink barrier surrounding the player's face.

        ==Stats==
        *+75% [[Goo]]
        * +10% [[System Page#Goo Conversion|Instant Goo Conversion]]
        *x1.25 [[System Page#White Pollen|White Pollen]]
        *+25% [[Pollen]]
        *x1.5 [[Capacity]]
        *x1.5 [[Field Capacity|White Field Capacity]]
        *+30% [[System Page#Defense|Defense]]
        * +20% [[System Page#Bee Ability Rate|Bee Ability Rate]]
        *[[Passive Abilities#Coin Scatter|+Passive: Coin Scatter]] (Requires [[Honey Mask]] in order
        to use):
        **The passive is activated every 20 [[Ability Tokens#Mark|Mark Tokens]]. When it does, 300%
        of the player's total [[System Page#Convert Total|Hive Convert Amount]] will be converted
        and scattered into honey tokens, which cannot be collected through a [[Ability Tokens#Token
        Link|Token Link]] (visible only to the player, Honey Tokens last one minute). The passive
        has a cooldown of two minutes before re-use.
        *[[Passive Abilities#Gummy Morph|+Passive: Gummy Morph]]:
        **The Gummy Morph will trigger every 30 gumdrops used OR 10 Gummy Bee ability tokens
        collected (+1 stack per gumdrops used, +3 stacks every Gummy Bee ability). When it triggers,
        it will turn you into [[Gummy Bear]], your Gummy Bee will glow and leave a trail. In
        addition, the field will be fully covered in goo. As Gummy Bear, you will gain +75% goo,
        instantly converting all of it, and have increased jump power and player speed. As Gummy Bee
        glows, it will gather 1000% more pollen, gain +300 attack, and move 200% faster for 10
        seconds. This passive does not have any cooldown time.

        ==Trivia==
        * If the ingredients to craft this item were to be crafted, a total of 5,000,000,000 Honey,
        75,000 [[Bluberry|Blueberries]], 75,000 [[Strawberry|Strawberries]], 92,500
        [[Pineapple|Pineapples]], 5,000 [[Sunflower Seed|Sunflower Seeds]], 7,000 [[Royal
        Jelly|Royal Jellies]], 100 [[Magic Bean]]s and 1 [[Satisfying Vial]] of nectar are needed.
        *Upon activating the Gummy Morph, [[Gummy Bee]] will have the highest Base Attack Power of
        303 Damage in total.
        * This is the only mask that represents an [[Bees/Event|Event bee]] (Gummy Bee), as the
        other masks are based on [[Bees/Epic|Epic]] and [[Bees/Legendary|Legendary Bees]].
        *The Gummy Mask was nerfed in the [[Updates#2019.04.05|4/5/2019 Update]] because it was too
        powerful.
        **It was later buffed a little in the [[Updates#2019.04.17|4/17/2019 Update]], as people
        were complaining that it was extremely bad with the nerf.
        *Before the [[Updates#2019.09.28|9/28/2019 Update]], if the [[Buffs & Debuffs#From
        Areas|Unlimited Gumdrops]] buff is active, the passive ability would not be activated from
        gumdrops. This is because gumdrops in the inventory weren't technically consumed upon use
        with the Unlimited Gumdrops buff.
        **After the update, it can now be activated with Unlimited Gumdrops.
        * The [[Music]] that plays when the Gummy Bear Morph is active is a slight rendition of
        [[Gummy Bear|Gummy Bear's]] theme that plays in the Gummy Bear's Lair.
        *This and the [[Honey Mask]] are the only masks in the game that requires a badge to access
        and purchase.
        ** Coincidentally, the [[Honey Mask]] is also required to unlock the coin scatter ability on
        this mask.
        *The Gummy Mask is the only mask in the game that requires a specific badge to access it.
        * This mask is the only way to get the Gummy Morph ability, and the only way to get a Bear
        Morph ability without Robux and codes.
        *Either this mask or the [[Gummy Boots]] were needed in order to receive Gummy Bear's
        [[Beesmas]] quest.
        *The Gummy Morph icon is the same icon as the one for the Goo badge.
        *This and the [[Strange Goggles]] are the only items that are required to interact with
        NPCs. However, during Beesmas, the Honey Mask was also needed to give [[Honey Bee
        (NPC)|Honey Bee]] a present and to receive its Beesmas quest.
        *This, the [[Demon Mask]] and the [[Diamond Mask]] are the only masks with decorative
        accessories (Gummy Mask has a teal gumdrop).
        *This is the 3rd most expensive item requiring [[Glue]]. In front of the Gummy Mask is
        [[Gummy Boots]] (2nd) and [[Gummyballer]] (1st).
