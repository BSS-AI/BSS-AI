        The '''Invigorating Vial''' is a consumable [[Nectar Vial|nectar vial]] [[Items|item]] that
        was added in the [[Updates#2022.12.26|12/26/2022 update]]. Once used, it grants the player 4
        hours of the [[Nectar|Invigorating Nectar]] [[Buffs & Debuffs|buff]] instantly. It can
        also be used to craft other [[:Category:Accessories|accessories]] and [[tools]].

        == Ways to Obtain ==
        * Completing [[Dapper Bear|Dapper Bear's]] 9th and 12th quests.
        * Using the [[Nectar Condenser]] to condense Invigorating Nectar.
        * Adding the Invigorating Nectar Icon to the [[Sticker Stack]] grants 1 Invigorating Vial.
        === The methods below are no longer possible. ===
        * Purchasing the Charming Bundle in [[Bee Bear's Catalog]] gave an Invigorating Vial and
        other items.
        * Purchasing the Hefty Honeyday Bundle in Bee Bear’s Catalog gave 5 Invigorating Vials and
        other items.
        * Opening the [[Gift Boxes#2022|Obnoxious Gift Box]] gave an Invigorating Vial and other
        items.
        * Giving a [[present]] to [[Gifted Riley Bee]].
        * Completing [[Stick Bug#2022 Beesmas Quest - Nymph Hats|Stick Bug's Beesmas 2022 quest]].
        * Dropped by a Level 10 [[Snowbear]].
        * Purchasing the Nighttime Nectar Pack in the [[Robux Shop]] granted 1 of each Nectar Vial.
