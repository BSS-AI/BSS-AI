        }}'''Crimson Bee''' is a [[:Category:Red|Red]] [[Bees/Event|Event Bee]]. It can be obtained
        by purchasing a [[Egg#Event Bee Eggs|Crimson Bee egg]] in the [[Ticket Tent]] for 250
        [[Ticket]]s.

        This bee is better with a [[Hive]] with more red [[Bees]] than
        [[:Category:Blue|Blue]]/[[:Category:Colorless|Colorless]] bees. Its abilities are enhanced
        when [[Cobalt Bee]] is also in the hive.

        Like all other Event bees, this bee does not have a favorite treat, and the two known ways
        to make it gifted is by feeding it a [[Star Treat|Star Treat]] or by feeding it
        [[Gingerbread Bear|Gingerbread Bears.]]

        Crimson Bee likes the [[Clover Field]] and the [[Rose Field]]. It dislikes the [[Pineapple
        Patch]].

        == Stats ==
        * Collects 10 [[Pollen]] in 4 seconds.
        * Makes 140 [[Honey]] in 3 seconds.
        * +75% [[Energy]], +30% [[Stats#Speed|Movespeed]], +25% Convert Speed, +60 [[System
        Page#Convert Amount|Convert Amount]], 10% [[Instant Conversion]], +6
        [[Stats#Attack|Attack]], 5% [[Critical Hits|Critical Chance]].
        * 🌟 [[Gifted Bee|Gifted Hive Bonus]]: +15% [[System Page#Instant Red Conversion|Instant Red
        Conversion]].

        === Abilities ===
        * '''[[Ability Tokens#Pulse|[Red Pulse]]]''' Fires a pulse that hops between your red bees,
        collecting pollen around them (+10% per level). Power increases with each hop. If you own
        Cobalt Bee, fires a [[Ability Tokens#Pulse|Blue Pulse]] as well.
        ** If Gifted, the pulse also causes bees to instantly convert pollen equal to 25% of the
        bee's Convert Amount (+25% per Field Boost stack).
        * '''[[Ability Tokens#Bomb Sync|[Red Bomb Sync]]]''' Grants 10% Red Pollen and Instant Red
        Conversion and allows [[Ability Tokens#Bomb|Red Bombs]] to collect from white [[Flowers]]
        for 30 sec. If [[Ability Tokens#Bomb Sync|Blue Bomb Sync]] is active, applies to blue
        flowers as well.

        == Trivia ==
        * The only difference between Crimson Bee and Cobalt Bee's [[Fields|Field]] preferences is
        that the Crimson Bee likes the Rose Field, whereas Cobalt Bee prefers the [[Pine Tree
        Forest]].
        * This is one of the two [[Bees]] which have [[Ability Tokens|Abilities]] that can trigger
        another bee's ability, the other being its counterpart, Cobalt Bee.
        * This is one of the four Event bees who have different color faces on the hive rather than
        the bee itself, the others being Cobalt Bee, [[Festive Bee]], and [[Vicious Bee]].
        ** This is the first bee that is both a red bee and an Event bee, with Festive Bee being the
        second.
        * When Crimson and Cobalt Bee are both in the same hive, the player gets the following
        bonuses from these ability tokens:
        ** Red pulse additionally fires a Blue Pulse to collect from blue bees.
        ** If both red bomb sync and blue bomb sync are activated, red bombs can collect from blue
        and white flowers.
        * This bee, along with Cobalt Bee, are currently the only Event bees to cost 250 tickets.
        ** The [[First Edition Bee|first edition]] [[Tabby Bee]] and [[Puppy Bee]] could once be
        purchased with 250 tickets as well.
        [[File:Apples.png|thumb]]
        * The description of the Red Bomb Sync ability while it is active is spelled incorrectly as
        \"apples\" instead of \"applies\".
        **[[Onett (Developer)|Onett]] was aware of this and instead changed the \"apples\" to \"🍎 s\".
        **This mistake has recently been corrected.
        * This bee, Cobalt Bee, [[Photon Bee]], [[Windy Bee]], [[Tadpole Bee]], [[Ninja Bee]] and
        [[Shy Bee]] are the only bees that create a trail.
        * The [[Crimson Guard]], which can be purchased in the [[Badge Bearer's Guild]], has a
        special design, name, and stats dedicated to Crimson Bee.
        ** The Crimson Guard has a [[Passive Abilities|Passive Ability]] similar to Crimson Bee's
        red pulse ability.
        * This bee, [[Cobalt Bee]], [[Puppy Bee]], and [[Windy Bee]] are the only event bees that
        don't have an 'enhanced' ability if gifted.
