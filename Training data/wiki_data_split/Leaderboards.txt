        '''Leaderboards''' are a feature within the game. Each leaderboard displays the ranks of the
        top 100 players, showing ten ranks at a time. Leaderboards are usually not in real-time, but
        update very often. Upon standing on the white pad in front of a leaderboard, it will show a
        message stating the player's score for the corresponding leaderboard.

        Out of the 62 total leaderboards, 22 are able to be viewed at various locations around the
        map, and the rest are available to view in the [[System Page]], with the exception of the
        [[Daily Top Ant Field Collectors]] leaderboard which cannot be viewed but still provides
        rewards. There are 3 leaderboards that have been removed; [[Most Commando Captures]],

        ==Trivia==
        *All leaderboards that reset on a daily basis reset at 12:00am GMT-5.
        *[[Highest Snowbear Level|The Highest Snowbear Level leaderboard]] was added on Christmas
        Day 2020 with the Beesmas update. This was removed 1 week after Beesmas 2020 ended.
        *The [[Top Brown Bear Helpers]] leaderboard is the only leaderboard that does not show the
        player's current amount of the stated objective.
        **The player can still view their score either in the leaderboard menu in the System Page or
        by speaking to [[Brown Bear]] when claiming or turning in a quest.
        **The [[Most Commando Captures Leaderboard]] also shared this property, but has since been
        removed from the game entirely.
        *On 3/17/2022, [[Onett (Developer)|Onett]] abbreviated the amount of honey for the Daily Top
        Honeymakers and the All-Time Top Honeymakers leaderboard due to players having massive
        amounts of honey.
