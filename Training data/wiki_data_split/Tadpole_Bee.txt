        causing them to replenish.

        == Trivia ==
        * The Tadpole Bee, [[Lion Bee]], [[Tabby Bee]], [[Bear Bee]], and [[Puppy Bee]] are the only
        [[bees]] to represent realistic animals.
        ** The Tadpole Bee is the only Mythic bee that represents a realistic animal.
        * Tadpole Bee and [[Baby Bee]] are the only bees with the Baby Love ability.
        ** Tadpole Bee and Baby Bee are also the only bees that appear smaller than the normal bee
        size.
        * Tadpole Bee and [[Windy Bee]] are the only bees with 2 gifted bonuses, excluding other
        Event Bees who enhance their ability.
        * Tadpole Bee, [[Vector Bee]], [[Fuzzy Bee]], [[Spicy Bee]], [[Buoyant Bee]] and [[Digital
        Bee]] are the only bees with gifted exclusive abilities.
        * This bee and Spicy Bee are the only bees who can summon other entities.
        ** This is the only bee that can summon a [[mobs|Mob]].
        * Tadpole Bee and [[Bubble Bee]] are the only bees with the Gathering Bubbles passive
        ability.
        * Tadpole Bee, Fuzzy Bee, Spicy Bee, Buoyant Bee, and Digital Bee all have four different
        [[Ability Tokens|Abilities]], the most for any bee.
        * Tadpole Bee used to be the only bee in the game that had a decimal base attack (0.5).
        * Tadpole Bee is the only Mythic bee that has its Gifted-exclusive ability token shared with
        another bee.
        * Along with Vector Bee and Spicy Bee, Tadpole Bee was one of the first [[Bees/Mythic|Mythic
        Bees]] to be introduced to the game.
        * A [[Egg#Specific Bee Eggs|Tadpole Bee Egg]] could be obtained by giving [[Bubble Bee Man]]
        a [[Present]] during the Beesmas 2020 event.
        ** Tadpole Bee is the second mythic bee to have an egg of its own, the first being [[Fuzzy
        Bee]].
        * Tadpole Bee is one of the 8 other bees with a unique trail.
        * Tadpole Bee is the blue counterpart of Spicy Bee.
        * Tadpole Bee and Fuzzy Bee are the only Mythics, when gifted, to not have any glowing
        parts.
