        can be reached by going to the [[Obstacle Courses#Lava Obby|Lava Obby]] (located behind the
        [[Honey Dispenser]] or by going to the passage in the top of the stall in the [[Noob Shop]])
        and following the course to the far back wall.

        == Trivia ==
        * If a player were to craft the ingredients to craft the Demon Mask, a total of
        {{Cost|Honey|5,000,000,000}}, {{Cost|Stinger|500}}, {{Cost|Royal Jelly|5,000}},
        {{Cost|Strawberry|27,500}}, {{Cost|Pineapple|22,500}}, and {{Cost|Gumdrops|15,000}} are
        needed. Crafting all the required materials takes a total of 2,500 minutes (41 hours and 40
        minutes) of playtime.
        * This mask and the Diamond Mask both represent [[Bees/Legendary|Legendary bees]].
        ** The Demon Mask is based on Demon Bee and uses the same face decal as Demon Bee.
        * The sound effect that plays when the X-Flame passive ability is activated is a distorted
        version of the music \"[[Music|bpatrol]]\".
        * The Demon Mask's stats have gone through multiple changes.
        ** Originally, the Demon Mask's stats were:
        *** +50% [[System Page#Pollen from Bees|Bee Gather Pollen]]
        *** +26% [[System Page#Instant Conversion|Instant Conversion]]
        *** +35% Defense
        *** +12% Bee Ability Rate
        *** +125% [[Loot Luck|Luck]] (Loot Luck)
        *** +25% Bee Attack Power (Bee Attack)
        *** +30% [[System Page#Bomb Pollen|Bomb Pollen]]
        *** x1.2 Red Pollen
        *** +Passive: X-Flame
        ** When the [[Updates#2019.04.05|4/5/2019 Update]] came out, its stats changed to:
        *** x2 Red Pollen
        *** +50% Bomb Pollen
        *** +25% Bee Attack
        *** +35% Defense
        *** +12% Bee Ability Rate
        *** +Passive: X-Flame
        * The Diamond Mask shop and the Demon Mask shop are the only shops in the game that have a
        total of 1 item.
        * This, the [[Diamond Mask]] and the [[Gummy Mask]] are the only masks with decorative
        accessories (the Demon Mask has flame producing devil horns).
        * Abilities that trigger “X-Flame” include the following:
