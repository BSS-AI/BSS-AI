        The '''Instant Converter''' is a [[:Category:Machines|machine]] used to immediately convert
        [[pollen]] into [[honey]], similar to a [[hive]], but instantly and without the use of
        [[bees]].

        There are three Instant Converters that can be found in the game:
        #Behind the [[Basic Egg Shop]], next to the [[Sunflower Field]] and in front of the
        [[Mushroom Field]]; this one is available to all players.
        #Just around the corner from the [[Brave Bee Gate]]; the player will need 10 bees to access
        it.
        #Next to the [[Mountain Top Field]]; the player will need 25 bees to access it.

        Each machine costs {{Cost|Ticket|1}} to use and has a cooldown of 15 minutes.

        The player's [[Honey Per Pollen]] rate also applies to the Instant Converter, just like it
        would at the hive.

        ==Tips==
        *The player can use an Instant Converter to squeeze in one more trip to a [[Field
        Boost|boosted field]]. This can be useful when they know that going back to their hive and
        waiting for their bees to convert their pollen would take longer than the time left on a
        boost.
        *It is more cost-effective to use an Instant Converter when the player's [[Bags|bag]] is
        full since it's a free cost.
        *Keep in mind that the cooldown of an Instant Converter is the same as the duration of a
        field boost, which means the same converter cannot be used twice during a field boost unless
        the player increases the duration of it.

        ==Trivia==

        *Before the 7/11/2018 update, the conveyor belt in the Instant Converter would push the
