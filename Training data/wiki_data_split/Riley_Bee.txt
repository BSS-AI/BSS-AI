        '''Riley Bee''' is a [[:Category:Red|Red]] [[Bees/Epic|Epic bee]].

        Riley Bee's favorite type of [[Treats|Treat]] is [[Strawberry|Strawberries]].

        Riley Bee likes the [[Mushroom Field]], [[Strawberry Field]], and [[Rose Field]]. It
        dislikes the [[Bamboo Field]] and the [[Pine Tree Forest]].

        == Stats ==
        * Collects 10 [[Pollen]] in 2 seconds.
        * Makes 140 [[Honey]] in 4 seconds.
        * +10% [[Stats#Speed|Movespeed]], +25% [[Energy]], +4 [[Stats#Attack|Attack]], +50% gather
        speed, +60 [[System Page#Convert Amount|Convert Amount]].
        * 🌟 [[Gifted Bee|Gifted Hive Bonus]]: +20% Red Field Capacity.

        === Abilities ===
        * '''[[Ability Tokens#Boost|[Red Boost]]]''' Grants x1.2 pollen from red [[Flowers]] for 15
        seconds. Stacks up to 10 times.
