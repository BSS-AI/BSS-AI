{"text": "A '''Dispenser''' is a [[:Category:Machines|machine]] whose main purpose is to dispense
        various [[items]] or boosts to the player typically under a cooldown.

        To use a dispenser, simply stand on the pad in front of the dispenser to use it. Some of the
        dispensers have certain requirements in order to be used such as having enough [[ticket]]s
        or being a part of the [[Bee Swarm Simulator Club]]. While there are some dispensers that
        don't directly have any requirements to be used, there are some that have indirect
        requirements such as the [[Glue Dispenser]] requiring the player have a certain
        [[Badges|badge]] to access, as the Gummy Bear's Lair requires the player to have
        [[Badges#Goo Badge|Goo Hotshot]] or above to enter, or the [[Coconut Dispenser]] needing the
        player to defeat the [[Coconut Crab]] to gain access to it, similar to the rest of the
        content in the [[Coconut Cave]].

        == Trivia ==

        * Of all the machines in the game, dispensers have the most variants, with a total of 11
        different types.
        *The [[Honey Dispenser]], the [[Strawberry Dispenser]], the [[Blueberry Dispenser]], the
        [[Royal Jelly Dispenser]], and the Glue Dispenser are the only dispensers that are required
        to be used for a [[Quests|quest]] (\"Honey Bee's Honey Wreath?\" from [[Honey Bee (NPC)|Honey
        Bee]] requires the player to use the Honey Dispenser, \"BBM's Naughty List\" from [[Bubble Bee
        Man]] requires the player to use the Royal Jelly Ticket Dispenser, and \"The Power of
        Information\" from [[Science Bear]] requires the player to use the Strawberry Dispenser, the
        Blueberry Dispenser, and the Glue Dispenser).
        *There are three dispensers that give [[treats]] in the game being the [[Treat Dispenser]],
        the Blueberry Dispenser, and the Strawberry Dispenser.
        *The Glue Dispenser, the [[Free Ant Pass Dispenser]], [[Ant Pass Dispenser]], [[Free Robo
        Pass Dispenser]], [[Robo Pass Dispenser]], and the Coconut Dispenser are the only dispensers
        to not grant [[Ability Tokens#Haste|haste]].
        *The Glue Dispenser, the Free Robo Pass Dispenser, and the [[Free Royal Jelly Dispenser]]
        are the only dispensers with a 22 hour cooldown, the longest in the game.
        *The Free Ant Pass Dispenser, the Ant Pass Dispenser, the Robo Pass Dispenser, and the Free
        Robo Pass Dispenser are the only dispensers that don't have any visible faucets, and instead
        have a small compartment, like a vending machine that gives out tickets.
        *The Glue Dispenser and the Coconut Dispenser are the only dispensers that give more items
        when the player upgrades the badge that corresponds to it.
        *The Coconut Dispenser is the only dispenser to give a field boost.
