        <nowiki> </nowiki>A '''Honeystorm''' is a server-wide event that can be summoned
        by using the pad near [[Brown Bear]] by anyone who has discovered four
        [[Bees/Legendary|Legendary Bee]] types. When summoned, a server-wide announcement will
        state, \"<span style=\"background-color:#F4A90D; color:white;\">[Username] has summoned a
        Honeystorm!</span>\". Upon being summoned, a golden-yellow fog will appear along with a
        multitude of [[Honey|honey tokens]] and some [[ticket]] tokens that will begin to randomly
        appear across all [[fields]], even the [[Ant Challenge|Ant Field]]. The storm lasts for 20
        seconds and has a cooldown of 4 hours. It can also be randomly triggered, in which the
        announcement says, \"<span style=\"background-color:#F4A90D; color:white;\">A Honeystorm
        has been summoned!</span>\".

        When a honeystorm is summoned, the honey tokens make raindrop-like popping noises when they
        appear. Like with the [[Mythic Meteor Shower]], the distorted voice clip of [[BSS
        Staff#IcedTeaLatte|IcedTeaLatte]] saying \"Honeystorm\" indicates that a Honeystorm has been
        summoned.

        Fields with bigger [[flowers]] give higher amounts of honey per token, with their totals
        being increased by the [[System Page#Honey From Tokens|Honey From Tokens]] stat. These honey
        tokens also despawn faster than normal honey tokens.

        == Trivia ==
        * The honeystorm fog is normally tinted yellow, but during Beesmas in 2018 and 2019, the fog
        is white.
        ** As of Beesmas 2020, this is no longer the case, since [[Snowstorm]]s use the white fog
        instead.
        * This and the [[Special Sprout Summoner]] are the only [[:Category:Machines|machines]] that
        require the player to discover Legendary bee types to be activated, with this requiring 4
        and the Special Sprout Summoner requiring 8.
        * A honeystorm's yellow tint could generate enough light to make the [[Cave Monster|cave
        monsters]] in the Werewolf's cave visible.
        * There is a [[Glitches|glitch]] where if a switch between day and [[Day/Night Cycle|night]]
        occurs while a honeystorm is active, the fog will disappear. This is purely visual and the
        honeystorm will still function as normal.
        * There was a short-lived issue around 5/26/2018 that caused the Honeystorm Summoner to be
        removed. If a player encountered a server without a Honeystorm Summoner, they could simply
        leave the game and come back. This was, of course, fixed later.
        * After the [[Updates#2018.09.10|9/10/2018 update]], the Honeystorm Summoner was moved and
        the [[Wealth Clock]] now sits in its former spot. Now it is located between the [[Clover
        Field]] and [[Blue HQ]].
        * Upon receiving the quest \"Witness Grandeur\", [[Spirit Bear]] summons a honeystorm.
        * According to [[Bee Bear]], [[Bubble Bee Man]] summons the honeystorms.
        * If you summon honeystorms at night, the fog will stay even after the storm until daytime.
        * Honeystorms can stack with other honeystorms. This does not affect the intensity of the
        honeystorms, but instead increases the duration.
