        The '''Fastest Crab Slayers Leaderboard''' is one of the 62 [[leaderboards]] in the game.
        This leaderboard shows the least time in which the player has defeated the [[Coconut Crab]].
        It shows 10 leaderboard positions at one time. The player can scroll down the leaderboard to
        see more players, all the way to the 100th place.

        If the player has never defeated the Coconut Crab the pad will prompt, \"You haven't defeated
        the Coconut Crab\".

        ==Location==
        It is located past the [[Windy Bee Gate]] inside the [[Coconut Cave]]. The player will need
        to defeat the Coconut Crab to enter the Cave and see their score.

        ==Achievements==
        * Pepega90 was the first user to defeat the Coconut Crab in 30 seconds or less.
        * Pepega90 was the first user to defeat the Coconut Crab in 25 seconds or less.
        * McProsephYouTube was the first user to defeat the Coconut Crab in 20 seconds or less.
        * Miracle_Mike was the first user to defeat the Coconut Crab in 15 seconds or less.
        * e_IoI was the first user to defeat the Coconut Crab in 10 seconds or less.
        * HavingFun2228 was the first user to defeat the Coconut Crab in 5 seconds or less.
        ==Trivia==
        *This is the first and only [[:Category:Leaderboards|leaderboard]] to rank by time (time
        before defeated).
        **This leaderboard, the [[Highest Snowbear Level]] leaderboard, and the [[Top Stick Bug
        Fighters]] leaderboard are the only three leaderboards to rank
        \"[[:Category:Bosses|bosses]]\".
        **This is the only leaderboard for the shortest amount of time to accomplish a goal.
        *This leaderboard is currently updated and has been repolished.
        *Onett accidentally put Coconut Crab's Health to only 20 and fixed it later, in the duration
        of the oversight, many players were able to reach faster times than usual. The leaderboard
        was later reset in June ? 2024.
