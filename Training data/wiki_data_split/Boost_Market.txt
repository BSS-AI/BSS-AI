        The '''Boost Market''' was a [[:Category:Shops|shop]] that could have been found in the
        [[Bear Gate|30 Bee Zone]]. Since the [[:Category:Beesmas|Beesmas]] 2021 [[Updates|update]],
        it has been replaced by the [[Nectar Pot]].

        Every 30 minutes, the shop allowed the player to purchase a market boost at a certain price.
        The price depended on the number of people who have purchased that specific boost, much like
        a real [[Wikipedia:Stock Market|stock market]]. It cost both [[ticket]]s and [[honey]] to
        purchase a boost. These boosts lasted for 30 minutes. Market boosts could also have been
        obtained from certain [[codes]], like RedMarket, or the ones used in the [[Ready Player
        Two]] event.
