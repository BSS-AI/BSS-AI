        The '''Coconut Dispenser''' is a [[dispenser]] located on the right side of the [[Coconut
        Cave]]. The player will need to defeat the [[Coconut Crab]] to gain access to it, similar to
        the rest of the content in the Coconut Cave.

        The dispenser has a cooldown of 4 hours. When used, the player will receive a certain number
        of [[coconut]]s, based on the quality of their [[Badges#Coconut Badges|Coconut Badge]]. They
        will also be granted a x1 [[Coconut Field]] [[Field Boost|Boost]]. This remains static,
        meaning the Coconut Badge will not affect the field boost multiplier.

        There is a secret entrance that leads to a tunnel that can be entered by jumping on top of
        the dispenser and into a small portion of the wall. Inside the tunnel, there are 3 tokens
        that grant 5 [[ticket]]s, 5 [[Jelly Beans|jelly beans]], and 1 [[enzymes|enzyme]] each.

        == Trivia ==
        * This, the [[Free Ant Pass Dispenser]], the [[Ant Pass Dispenser]], the [[Glue Dispenser]],
        the [[Free Robo Pass Dispenser]], and the [[Robo Pass Dispenser]] are the only dispensers
        that don't grant [[Ability Tokens#Haste|Haste]].
        * This, the Free Ant Pass Dispenser, the Ant Pass Dispenser, the Free Robo Pass Dispenser
        and the Robo Pass Dispenser are the only dispensers that grant [[items]] with a capacity
        limit.
        * This is the only dispenser that grants a Field Boost.
        * This is the second mechanic that guarantees a certain field boost, the first one being
        [[glitter]].
        * This is the only dispenser that gives a different message after using: \"Earn coconut
        badges to receive more coconuts!\"
        * This and the Glue Dispenser are the only dispensers to give more items based on the rank
        of a badge.
