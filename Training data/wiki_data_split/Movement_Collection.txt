        '''Movement Collection''' is a type of boost that allows the player to collect [[pollen]]
        when walking on [[flowers]]. The higher the movement collection, the greater amount of
        pollen the player collects from walking on flowers. This boost also collects [[goo]] if the
        player walks on any goo patches.

        == Ways to Increase ==
        The player can get this boost from wearing [[:Category:Boots|boots]] or from a [[Moon
        Amulet]].

        * The Moon Amulet can give from +1 Movement Collection to +3 Movement Collection.
        * Boots:
        ** The [[Basic Boots]] give +2 Movement Collection.
        ** The [[Hiking Boots]] give +6 Movement Collection.
        ** The [[Beekeeper's Boots]] give +10 Movement Collection.
        ** The [[Coconut Clogs]] give +12 Movement Collection.
        ** The [[Gummy Boots]] give +15 Movement Collection.

        == Trivia ==
        * Movement Collection isn't shown in the [[System Page]], unlike most other boosts, such as
        [[Loot Luck]] and [[Instant Conversion]].
        *If the player has movement collection on their Moon Amulet, they can still collect pollen
        even without boots equipped.
        *The maximum Movement Collection a player can have is 18. This can be achieved by having a
        Gummy Boots (+15) and a Moon Amulet (+3).
