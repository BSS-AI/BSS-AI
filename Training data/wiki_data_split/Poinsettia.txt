        bees that know the [[Ability Tokens#Boost|Boost]] ability.

        == Possible Stats ==
        * Red Gather Amount (Guaranteed) - +10% to +25%. (up to +60% from [[Waxes|waxing]])
        * Gather Pollination Chance (Guaranteed) - +10% to +20%. (up to +40% from waxing)
        * [Hive Bonus] Bee Gather Pollen - +1%. (up to +31% from waxing)
        * [Hive Bonus] Red Pollen (From waxing) - +2% to +25%.

        == Ways To Obtain ==
        *As a rare reward from the [[Ant Challenge]]. A higher score has a higher chance to drop a
        Poinsettia.

        ===These methods are no longer possible. ===
        * Purchasing the Beequip Bundle in [[Bee Bear’s Catalog|Bee Bear's Catalog]] for 75
        [[Snowflake]]s and 15 [[Gingerbread Bear]]s (Gave 1 Poinsettia + other items).
        * As a reward from [[Spirit Bear|Spirit Bear's]] Galentine's Shrine quest in Beesmas 2021
        (Gave 1 Poinsettia + other items).
