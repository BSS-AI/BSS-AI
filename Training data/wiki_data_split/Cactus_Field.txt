        This field can spawn [[honey]], [[Treat|treat]], [[Strawberry|strawberry]],
        [[Blueberry|blueberry]], [[ticket]], [[Magic Bean|magic bean]], [[Bitterberry|bitterberry]],
        and [[stinger]] tokens when collecting [[pollen]].

        [[Bees]] that like the Cactus Field are [[Bomber Bee]], [[Hasty Bee]], [[Commander Bee]],
        [[Demo Bee]], and [[Vicious Bee]]. Bees that dislike the Cactus Field are [[Exhausted Bee]],
        [[Baby Bee]], [[Music Bee]], [[Tadpole Bee]], and [[Tabby Bee]].

        This field gives Invigorating [[Nectar]] when a planter is placed here.

        A Level 4-7 [[Rogue Vicious Bee]] can spawn in this field.

        == Obtaining Boosts ==
        * Using the [[Field Booster]] may give a Cactus [[Field Boost]]. When it does, it will give
        +100% Cactus Field pollen for 15 minutes. [[File:Fis cactus.png|thumb|75px|The Cactus Field
        Boost icon.]]
        * Using [[glitter]] will add 100% Cactus Field pollen onto the current boost for 15 minutes
        if it isn't already maxed out.
        * Using [[Field Dice|field dice]], [[Smooth Dice|smooth dice]], and [[Loaded Dice|loaded
        dice]] may add +100% - 300% Cactus Field pollen for 15 minutes if the current boost isn't
        maxed out and is based on the type of dice used.
        * Cactus [[Field Wind]]s can be obtained through the [[Wind Shrine]] or [[codes]]. They have
        a base of +20% instant Cactus Field conversion, and give +5% instant Cactus Field conversion
        and +15% Cactus Field pollen for each stack. Cactus Field Winds can stack up to a total of
        13 times.
        *The [[Icicles]] [[Beequip]] can grant up to +10% Cactus Field pollen.
        * Redeeming certain valid codes:
        ** FourYearFiesta (Gives Cactus Field Boost x4 and Cactus Field Winds x15 + other items).
        * It can be boosted by the [[Shell Amulet]]. If it has a Cactus Field Boost, it can boost
        the field by +5% to +27% depending on the amulet rarity.

        == Trivia ==
        * This is the only field where three types of [[treats]] can be found naturally in the same
        place.
        * This is the fourth-smallest field in the game, the smaller ones being the [[Pumpkin
        Patch]], [[Strawberry Field]], and the [[Stump Field]].
        * It is possible to hide behind the cactus closest to the [[Pine Tree Forest]] so the
        werewolf cannot attack the player.
        * This is one of six fields where [[fireflies]] travel to, with the others being [[Bamboo
        Field]], [[Pineapple Patch]], [[Rose Field]], [[Spider Field]], and Strawberry Field.
        * This is one of three fields that is protected by the werewolf, the others being the
        Pumpkin Patch and the Pine Tree Forest.
        * This is the only field in the Honey Bee gate which gives invigorating nectar.
