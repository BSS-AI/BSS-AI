        The '''Pink Shades''' is a [[Bond|level]] 10 [[beequip]]. It has an equip limit of one.
        There is no color requirement. It can only be equipped to [[Basic Bee|Basic]], [[Rad
        Bee|Rad]], [[Bomber Bee|Bomber]], [[Demo Bee|Demo]], [[Honey Bee|Honey]], and [[Shy
        Bee|Shy]] [[bees]], but they must be [[Gifted Bee|gifted]].
        == Potential ==

        * Ability [[Pollen]] - +15% to +25%. (up to +65% from [[Waxes|waxing]])
        * [[Critical Hits#Critical Power|Critical Power]] - +25% to +50%.
        * [[Critical Hits#Critical Chance|Critical Chance]] - +20% to +30%. (up to +68% from waxing)
        * [[Critical Hits#Super-Crit Chance|Super-Crit Chance]] - +10% to +15%. (up to +55% from
        waxing)
        * Ability: [[Ability Tokens#Focus|Focus]] (From waxing, caustic only)
        * [Hive Bonus] Super-Crit Chance (from waxing, caustic only) - +1%.
        * [Hive Bonus] [[Critical Hits#Super-Crit Power|Super-Crit Power]] (from waxing) - +2% to
        +30%.

        == Ways to obtain ==
        * As an exceptionally rare drop from [[Tacky Planter]] (1 in 100,000 at full growth).
        * As an exceptionally rare drop from The Planter of Plenty.
        * As an exceptionally rare reward from the [[Stick Bug Challenge]].

        === These methods are no longer possible. ===
        * As a reward from completing [[Honey Bee (NPC)|Honey Bee's]] [[:Category:Beesmas|Beesmas]]
        2022 quest.
