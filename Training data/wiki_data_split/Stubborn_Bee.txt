        Stubborn Bee's favorite type of treat are [[Strawberry|strawberries]]. 

        Stubborn Bee likes the [[Dandelion Field]] and the [[Clover Field]]. It dislikes the [[Rose
        Field]].

        == Stats ==
        * Collects 10 [[pollen]] in 4 seconds.
        * Makes 80 [[honey]] in 3 seconds.
        * -15% [[Stats#Speed|movespeed]], +25% convert speed, +1 [[Stats#Attack|attack]].
        * 🌟 [[Gifted Bee|Gifted Hive Bonus]]: +15% [[Ability Tokens|Ability Token]] Lifespan.

        == Abilities ==
        * '''[[Ability_Tokens#Mark|[Pollen Mark]]]''' Marks a random area on the [[Fields|field]]
        for 7 seconds (+0.25s per level) that increases all pollen up to 50% while you stand in it.
        Stacks up to 3 times.

        == Trivia ==
        * Stubborn Bee was one of the two bees added in the [[Updates#2018.11.25|11/25/2018
        update]], the other being [[Carpenter Bee]].
        * Stubborn Bee and [[Looker Bee]] are the only Rare bees added after the game launch.
        *The gifted hive bonus (+20% ability token lifespan) used to be broken. The player could
        have a Gifted Stubborn Bee in their hive, but the ability token lifespan would not apply.
        This was fixed on an unknown update in February 2021.
        *Stubborn Bee is the only Colorless Rare Bee that does not have its own
        [[:Category:Guards|guard]] in the [[Pro Shop]]. This is because it was added in the
        [[Updates#2018.11.25|11/25/2018]] update, after the guards were added.
