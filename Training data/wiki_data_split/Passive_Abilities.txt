        '''Passive Abilities,''' or simply '''Passives''' are effects found on some
        [[:Category:Accessories|accessories]], [[amulet]]s, or [[bees]]. These abilities are active
        all the time or only activate when specific actions are done, as an additional effect of
        those actions.

        Passive abilities granted by an accessory or an amulet are displayed near the hotbar. Some
        passive abilities require the player to do the same action multiple times as shown by the
        number displayed on the passive icon, indicating how close it is to activating the ability.
        When the ability is activated, the number will reset.

        Some passive abilities have a cooldown. When in cooldown, the number of seconds left will be
        displayed and will turn pale red. No trigger will activate the ability, nor increase the
        trigger counter. These passive abilities also have an initial cooldown when the player
        equips items that have those passives to prevent bypassing the cooldown.
        == Accessory Passives ==
        === Haste Pulser ===
        [[File:HastePulser.png|thumb|left|100px|The icon for Haste Pulser.]]
        '''Haste Pulser''' activates every 30th [[Ability Tokens#Haste|haste token]] collected. When
        activated, it fires a blue pulse that hops to each blue bee. When the pulse hops between
        bees, it collects [[pollen]] from 9 surrounding [[flowers]] around them. Power increases
        with each hop.

        Haste Pulser is granted by equipping the [[Cobalt Guard]].

        This passive makes the same sound as a regular pulse from [[Cobalt Bee|Cobalt]] or [[Crimson
        Bee]].

        If the player has no blue bees, then nothing happens.

        === Bubble Bombs ===
        [[file:bubblebomb.png|thumb|left|100px|The icon for Bubble Bombs.]]
        '''Bubble Bombs''' activates every 25th [[Ability Tokens#Bomb|bomb token]] collected, with a
        2 minute cooldown. When activated, it spawns 25 [[bubbles]] randomly in the [[Fields|field]]
        the player is in. The bubbles last for 8 seconds. If any player, a petal shuriken, or a
        [[Ability Tokens#tornado|tornado]] from [[Windy Bee]] touches the bubble, it pops,
        collecting 4 red, 6 white, and 8 blue pollen from 29 surrounding flowers, increasing the
        amount of pollen collected by 15% per [[Gifted Bee|gifted blue bee]] type, while also
        replenishing the flowers.

        Bubble Bomb is only granted by equipping the [[Bubble Mask]] or [[Diamond Mask]] if the
        player owns the Bubble Mask.
