        The '''Egg Hunt Info''' was a [[Quest Givers|quest giver]] that was located in front of the
        [[Ant Gate]]. This was the quest location of the [[Egg Hunt 2019]] event that occurred in
        Roblox for [[Bee Swarm Simulator]]. The player could collect three [[Marshmallow
        Bee|marshmallow bees]] and turn in it here for various in-game rewards, the [[Flight of the
        Bumble Egg]], and a [[Badges|badge]]. If obtained before the event ended, the player could
        equip the Flight of the Bumble Egg in the avatar tab on the Roblox website.

        Each of the NPCs in game at the time, except for the [[Honey Bee (NPC)|Honey Bee NPC]],
        [[Onett]], gave a quest for a marshmallow bee.

        Note: If you completed the Egg Hunt quests before they were modified, you will be rewarded
        two [[Field Dice|field dice]], two marshmallow bees, and five [[Jelly Beans|jelly beans]].
