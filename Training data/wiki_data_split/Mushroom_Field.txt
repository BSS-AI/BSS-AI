        The [[Mushroom Field]] is one of the five [[Starter Zone|starting]] [[fields]] a new player
        can go to. It is the first [[:Category:Red|red]] field accessible to players in the game.
        The field is protected by a level 1 [[ladybug]].

        This is a 32x23 field made up of 736 [[flowers]]. The flowers are red and white, in sizes of
        single and double.

        This field can spawn [[Treat|treat,]] [[Strawberry|strawberry]],
        [[Bitterberry|bitterberry]], [[ticket]], [[Ability Tokens#Boost|red boost]], [[honey]],
        [[Royal Jelly|royal jelly]], [[Magic Bean|magic bean]], and [[Red Extract|red extract]]
        tokens when collecting [[pollen]].

        Bees that like the Mushroom Field include [[Rad Bee]], [[Rascal Bee]], [[Fire Bee]], [[Riley
        Bee]], [[Baby Bee]], [[Demon Bee]], and [[Festive Bee]]. Bees that dislike the Mushroom
        Field include [[Bumble Bee]], [[Frosty Bee]], [[Shocked Bee]], and [[Ninja Bee]].

        This field gives Motivating [[Nectar]] when a planter is placed here.

        == Obtaining Boosts==
        *Using the [[Red Field Booster]] may give a Mushroom [[Field Boost]]. When it does, it will
        give +300% Mushroom Field pollen for 15 minutes. [[File:Fis_mushroom.png|thumb|75px|The
        Mushroom Field Boost icon.]]
        *Using [[glitter]] in the field will add 100% Mushroom Field pollen onto the current boost
        if the boost isn't already maxed out.
        *Using [[Field Dice|field dice]], [[Smooth Dice|smooth dice]], or [[Loaded Dice|loaded
        dice]] may add +100% - +300% Mushroom Field pollen for 15 minutes if the current boost isn't
        already maxed out and based on the type used.
        * Mushroom [[Field Wind]]s can be obtained through the [[Wind Shrine]] or [[codes]]. They
        have a base of +20% instant Mushroom Field conversion and give +5% instantMushroom Field
        conversion and +25% Mushroom Field pollen for each stack. Mushroom Field Winds can stack up
        to a total of 15 times.
        *Redeeming certain valid codes:
        **Dysentery (Activates Mushroom Field Code Buff + other stuff).
        **1MLikes (Gives Mushroom Field Boost x1 + other stuff).
        *It can be boosted by the [[King Beetle Amulet]]. If it has a Mushroom Field Boost, it can
        boost the field by +1% to +100%.

        ===The methods below are no longer possible.===

        **[[Onett]] gave Mushroom Field Boost x1 and other boosts and items.
        **[[Bubble Bee Man]] gave Mushroom Field Boost x4 and other boosts and items during Beesmas
        2021.
        *Completing [[Gifted Riley Bee#Egg Hunt 2019 Quest|Gifted Riley Bee's Egg Hunt 2019 Quest]]

        ==Trivia==
        * There is a small [[Obstacle Courses#Mushroom Field Obby|parkour course]] in the Mushroom
        Field that can be done to obtain a royal jelly.
        *The [[Bubble Wand]] model on top of the [[Blue HQ]] can occasionally grow a face and blow
        [[sparkles]] onto this field, along with the [[Blue Flower Field|Blue Flower]], [[Clover
        Field|Clover]], and [[Dandelion Field]].
        *[[Stick Bug]] is found on the tallest mushroom, along with the [[Top Stick Bug Fighters]]
        leaderboard. This is where the player can start the [[Stick Bug Challenge]] if they have
        given Stick Bug a [[translator]].
        *After the [[Updates#2018.11.25|11/25/2018 update]] was released, Stick Bug would sometimes
        fall off the tallest mushroom and land in or near the [[Dandelion Field]]. This was a visual
        [[Glitches#Onett/Stick Bug/Mondo Chick Falling|glitch]] and didn't affect gameplay. This
        glitch was patched a few days after the update.
        *This is one of the two fields in the Starter Zone with a Ladybug in it, the other being the
        Clover Field.
