{"text": "[[File:New egg page.png|thumb|Eggs available inside the [[Robux Shop]].]]
        '''Eggs''' are a type of [[:Category:Inventory|inventory item]] where the player drags into
        their [[hive]] to hatch a [[Bees|bee]] or can be donated to the [[Wind Shrine]]. They are
        also the items that help players start playing in the [[Bee Swarm Simulator|game]]. Eggs
        come in many different types, some offering chances at different rarities of bees, and some
        hatching a specific bee. Currently, a total of 5 (or 10 if counting the [[Gifted
        Bee|gifted]] variants of the eggs) different types of eggs exist in the game, not including
        [[Plastic Egg]]s, specific bee eggs, the [[Flight of the Bumble Egg]], or the [[Swarming Egg
        of the Hive]].

        Ever since the [[Updates#2019.09.28|9/28/2019 update]], players do not need a vacant [[Hive
        Slot|hive slot]] to hatch an egg. Now, players can hatch an egg on top of an existing bee to
        replace it. The level of the bee remains the same, acting like a [[Royal Jelly]].

        Please also note that due to regulations in Netherlands and Belgium, any eggs in the Robux
        shop won't be available to purchase for Dutch and Belgian consumers. This restriction counts
        for every egg in the shop.

        Basic Egg=

        == {{Color|Basic Egg}} ==
        [[File:Basic_Egg.png|left|100px]] The '''Basic Egg''' is the most common type of egg found
        in the game. It will almost always hatch into a [[Basic Bee]] (87% chance), but in rare
        cases, it can hatch into a [[Bees/Rare|Rare Bee]] (10% chance), an [[Bees/Epic|Epic Bee]]
        (2.5% chance), a [[Bees/Legendary|Legendary Bee]] (0.5% chance), a [[Bees/Mythic|Mythic
        Bee]] (0% chance), or into a [[Gifted Bee]] (0.4% chance), with a 0.348% (1 out of
        287.35632184) chance of getting a Gifted Basic Bee from the egg. [[Bees/Event|Event bees]]
        cannot be hatched from this egg. This egg cannot be used on a Basic Bee.
        {{clear}}

        <u>Ways to obtain a Basic Egg:</u>

        * First-time players receive a free Basic Egg in their inventory.
        * Purchasing from the [[Basic Egg Shop]] located near the [[Sunflower Field]] for a base
        price of {{Cost|Honey|1,000}} and capping at {{Cost|Honey|10,000,000}}. Every time the
        player purchases a Basic Egg, the price of the next egg scales up.
        * Adding the Round Basic Bee [[Sticker]] to the [[Sticker Stack]] rewards 1 Basic Egg.

        ==Specific Bee Eggs==
        Instead of hatching into a random bee of a particular rarity, '''specific bee eggs''' will
        always hatch into the egg's respective bee. All of these eggs share the same appearance of a
        basic egg.
        *A [[Rascal Bee]] egg can drop from a [[ladybug]] during rare occasions.
        *A [[Bumble Bee]] egg can drop from a [[Rhino Beetle|rhino beetle]] during rare occasions.
        *A [[Baby Bee]] egg can drop from a [[Tunnel Bear]] during rare occasions.
        * A [[Honey Bee]] egg can drop from a [[Cave Monster]] during rare occasions.
        *A [[Fuzzy Bee]] egg could have been purchased from Fuzzy Pack in the Robux Shop for 800
        Robux, which gave 250 [[ticket]]s, 10 [[glue]], and 10 [[glitter]] along with the egg (no
        longer available).
        *A [[Tadpole Bee]] egg and other rewards were awarded upon giving [[Bubble Bee Man]] a
        [[Present]] during the Beesmas 2020 event (no longer obtainable).
        *A [[Bubble Bee]] egg could have been obtained by completing [[Bubble Bee Man|B.B.M's
        Naughty List]] quest from Bubble Bee Man (no longer obtainable).
        * A [[Commander Bee]] egg can be obtained by capturing the [[Commando Chick]] 200 times.
        *A [[Buoyant Bee]] egg could have been obtained by purchasing the Buoyant Pack in the Robux
        Shop for 800 Robux (no longer obtainable).
        *A [[Precise Bee]] egg could have been obtained by purchasing the Precise Pack in the Robux
        Shop for 1700 Robux (no longer obtainable).
        Attempting to use a specific bee egg on a bee that is already the one that's guaranteed by
        the egg will not work (Using a Honey Bee egg on a Honey Bee won't work and the egg won't be
        used).

        ===Event Bee Eggs===
        [[Bees/Event|Event bee]] eggs are a special type of egg that will always hatch into a
        specific type of Event bee. After an Event bee egg is hatched, the player will receive an
        exclusive type of [[Royal Jelly#Royal Jelly Variants|permanent royal jelly]] for that
        specific Event bee. The Event bee royal jelly will turn any bee into that kind of Event bee.
        It cannot be applied if the player currently has that Event bee in their hive though.
        * A [[Bear Bee]] egg can be obtained in the Robux Shop for 800 robux. This once cost 1000
        robux, and on the game's release and certain events, it was reduced to 650 or 600 robux for
        a short while.
        *A [[Cobalt Bee]] egg can be obtained at the [[Ticket Tent]] for 250 [[ticket]]s.
        *A [[Crimson Bee]] egg can be obtained at the [[Ticket Tent]] for 250 tickets.
        *A [[Digital Bee]] egg can be obtained at [[Robo Bear's Shop]] after getting a Diamond [[Cog
        Amulet]], 7,777,777 [[Honey]] and 5 of all [[Drives]]. This once could be bought from the
        [[Robux Shop|Digital Bee Pack]] for 2500 robux.
        *A [[Festive Bee]] egg can be obtained in the Ticket Tent for 500 tickets, from finishing
        the 15th quest of [[Bee Bear]] (Festive Fight Finale) during Beesmas 2020 (if the player
        doesn't have Festive Bee yet, otherwise it gives 500 [[ticket]]s), or from opening the
        [[Ornament Presents|Festive Present]] during Beesmas 2019. This could once get received from
        completing all of [[Bee Bear|Bee Bear's]] quests during his first visit, and it could once
        have been bought from the Festive Bee Pack for 800 robux.
        *A [[Gummy Bee]] egg can be obtained for 2500 [[gumdrops]] at the [[Gummy Bee Egg Claim]].
        This could once have been obtained from completing all of [[Gummy Bear|Gummy Bear's]]
        [[quests]] during the [[:Category:Gummy Invasion|Gummy Invasion event]], and it could once
        have been purchased in the Ticket Tent for 500 tickets.
        * A [[Photon Bee]] egg can be obtained at the Ticket Tent for 500 tickets.
        *A [[Puppy Bee]] egg can be obtained at the Ticket Tent for 500 tickets. This was once on
        sale for 250 tickets. This once could've been bought from the Reindeer Puppy Pack for 800
        robux during Beesmas 2020.
        *A [[Tabby Bee]] egg can be obtained at the [[Ticket Tent]] for 500 tickets. This was once
        on sale for 250 tickets.
        *A [[Vicious Bee]] egg can be obtained at the [[Vicious Bee Egg Claim]] for 250
        [[stingers]].
        *A [[Windy Bee]] egg can be obtained from the [[Wind Shrine]] if the player donates a
        variable amount of [[Cloud Vial|cloud vials]] after donating a [[Spirit Petal]]. This once
        could've been bought from the Windy Bee Pack for 800 robux.

        ==Trivia==

        *The [[Vicious Bee]], [[Windy Bee]], [[Festive Bee]], and [[Gummy Bee]] Eggs are the only
        types of [[Bees/Event|Event bee]] eggs that could or can be obtained without spending robux
        or [[ticket]]s. (The Festive Bee Egg can now be purchased in the [[Ticket Tent]] for 500
        tickets.)
        *Star Eggs and Basic Eggs are the only two eggs that can hatch into a [[Gifted Bee|Gifted]]
        [[Basic Bee]].
        *[[Plastic Egg]]s, the [[Flight of the Bumble Egg]] (Egg Hunt 2019), and the [[Swarming Egg
        of the Hive]] (Egg Hunt 2020) are the only types of eggs that do not hatch into a
        [[Bees|bee]].
        * Any kind of specific bee egg and event bee egg will have the same icon as a Basic Egg in
        the inventory.
        *The [[Bear Bee]], [[Festive Bee]], [[Puppy Bee]], and [[Windy Bee]] Eggs are the only type
        of Event bee eggs that can/could be obtained directly by spending robux.
        *Basic Eggs are the only type of eggs that can be obtained with honey.
        ** It is also tied with the Gifted Mythic Egg for the least number of ways to be obtained,
        being only 2.
        *The star that appears on the Star Egg is slightly larger than the stars on the other types
        of gifted eggs.
        *The Mythic Egg is the only type of egg with the name of the bee rarity that it hatches
        into.
        **The Mythic Egg is also the only egg that has a 100% of hatching into a certain bee rarity
        (except for Event bee eggs and specific bee eggs). However, before mythic bees were added,
        diamond eggs used to have a 100% chance of hatching into a legendary bee.
        **The Mythic Egg is also the only egg with a letter on it.
        **The letter on the Mythic Egg is Latin-Script.
        *The [[Fuzzy Bee]] egg, [[Buoyant Bee]] egg and the [[Precise Bee]] egg are the only
        non-event and the only specific bee eggs that could be bought from the Robux Shop.
        * The Gifted Silver Egg and the Star Egg are the only Gifted eggs that can be obtained by
        completing the main questlines from [[:Category:NPC|NPCs]] (Black Bear's \"Getting Stumped\",
        Mother Bear's \"Seven To Seven\", and Panda Bear's \"Royal Rumble\").
        * The player cannot hatch a specific bee egg on that specific bee. For example, a Bumble Bee
        egg cannot be hatched on a Bumble Bee.
        *The Basic Egg is the only egg without a gifted variant of itself, excluding specific bee
        eggs.
        **Similarly, the Star Egg is the only Gifted egg without a regular variant.
        *The Diamond and Basic Eggs are the only non-specific or event eggs that can hatch into a
        bee of their name (the [[Diamond Bee|Diamond]] and Basic Bees, respectively), though you can
        get other bees from these eggs.
        *The Bubble Bee Egg is the only non-Event specific bee egg that can be obtained from a quest
        (B.B.M's Naughty List).
        *The Tadpole Bee Egg is the only egg that can be obtained by giving a present (to Bubble Bee
        Man).
