        [[File:Free robo dispenser.png|thumb|The Free Robo Pass Dispenser.]]
        The '''Free Robo Pass Dispenser''' is a [[dispenser]] located in the [[Bear Gate|30 Bee
        Zone]], directly right of the [[Nectar Pot]]. When used, gives the player a free [[Robo
        Pass]] every 22 hours, which can be used to participate in the [[Robo Bear Challenge]].
        Players cannot use the dispenser if they already have 10 or more Robo Passes in their
        inventory.

        == Trivia ==
        * There used to be a free Robo Pass token behind the Free Robo Pass Dispenser, this has been
        replaced with the [[Broken Drive]].
        * This, the [[Royal Jelly Dispenser#Free Royal Jelly Dispenser|Free Royal Jelly Dispenser]]
        and the [[Glue Dispenser]], have a cooldown of 22 hours, being tied with the longest
        cooldown of any dispenser in the game.
        * This and the [[Free Ant Pass Dispenser]] are the only free dispensers in the game that do
        not give any [[Buffs & Debuffs|buffs]] when used.
        ** This, [[Robo Pass Dispenser]], the [[Free Ant Pass Dispenser]], the [[Ant Pass
        Dispenser]], the [[Coconut Dispenser]], and the [[Glue Dispenser]] are the only dispensers
        that do not grant [[Ability Tokens#Haste|Haste]] when used.
        * This, the [[Robo Pass Dispenser]], the [[Ant Pass Dispenser]], and the [[Free Ant Pass
        Dispenser]] are the only dispensers that don't have any visible faucets. Instead, they have
        a small compartment, similar to a vending machine or an ATM.
