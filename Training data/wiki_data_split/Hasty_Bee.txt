        * The [[Hasty Guard]], which can be bought in the [[Pro Shop]], has a special design, name,
        and stats dedicated to Hasty Bee.
        * This is the only [[Bees|bee]] to have a [[Gifted Bee#List of Hive Bonuses|gifted bonus]]

        * This bee, [[Shocked Bee]], and [[Shy Bee]] are the only bees that have either a spelling
        or grammar mistake in their description.
        * A [[Royal Jelly#Specific Bee Jelly|special jelly]] that changes a bee into a Hasty Bee can
        be obtained by using the [[Codes|code]], 'HasteHelper' (expired).
        *This bee, Shocked Bee, Ninja Bee, and [[Photon Bee]], are the only bees that grant the
        Haste token without any certain [[Beequip]]s.
        *Hasty Bee most likely dislikes the [[Stump Field]] because it is home to the [[Stump
        Snail]], who moves very slowly.
