        '''Battle Points''' are points used for [[Global Top Battlers Leaderboard]] rankings and the
        [[Badges#Battle Badge|Battle Badge]]. They are obtained by defeating certain [[mobs]].

        The player can see how many battle points they have by stepping on the pad in front of the
        Global Top Battlers Leaderboard or by looking in your [[Badges]] and scrolling down to the
        Battle Badge.

        == Battle Points per Mob ==
        * [[Robo Bear Challenge#Mobs|Robo Bear Challenge Mobs]], [[Ants]], [[Rogue Vicious Bee]],
        [[Stick Bug]], [[Stick Nymph|Stick nymphs]], [[Wild Windy Bee]], and [[chicks]]: No points.
        * [[Ladybug]]s, [[Rhino Beetle|rhino beetles]], and [[Cave Monster|cave monsters]]: 1 point.
        * [[Mantis]]es and [[scorpion]]s: 5 points.
        * [[Spider]]s: 10 points.
        * [[Aphid]]s: 10 points if normal, 20 if rage, armored, or diamond.
        * [[Werewolf|Werewolves]]: 25 points.
        * [[King Beetle]]: 150 points.
        * [[Tunnel Bear]]: 200 points.
        * [[Coconut Crab]]: 400 points.
        * [[Stump Snail]]: 500 points.

        == Trivia ==
        * The first player to get the Battle Master Badge was DB4444.
        * The first player to get the Battle Grandmaster Badge was MunkNull.
