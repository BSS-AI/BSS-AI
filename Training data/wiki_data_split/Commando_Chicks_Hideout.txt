        The '''Commando Chick’s Hideout''' is a location in the [[Starter Zone|starter zone]] that
        can be entered by completing the [[Obstacle Courses#Commando Chick&#39;s Hideout
        Obby|obby]] between the [[Blue HQ]] and the [[Wealth Clock]]. To enter the parkour, the
        player first needs to cut the vines with either the [[Clippers]], [[Scissors]], or the
        [[Scythe]]. It is home to the [[Commando Chick]].

        == Strategy ==
        Standing on the last stair, nearest to Commando Chick's hideout normally makes the chick
        flee. However, if the player stands on the edge of that stair (as close as possible to the
        hideout where one of your body parts is inside of the hideout), the Commando Chick does not
        flee; therefore, the player's bees can defeat the Commando Chick whilst they take no damage.
        This strategy does not 100% work as there could be a slight chance of the Commando Chick
        fleeing. The player could just move into the hideout and then back onto the stair again to
        fix this.

        == Music ==
        When in the hideout, the following audio plays: [[File:Crawlers.ogg]]<br />
        This audio also plays in [[King Beetle's Lair]] and the [[White Tunnel]].
