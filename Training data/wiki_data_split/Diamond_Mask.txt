        The '''Diamond Mask''' is a [[:Category:Masks|Mask]] and the [[:Category:Blue|Blue]]
        counterpart of the [[Demon Mask]]. It can be reached by finding the doorway near the end of
        the [[Obstacle Courses#Cloud Obby|Cloud Obby]], then completing yet another obstacle course.
        The player is able to skip the Cloud Obby by using a [[Parachute]] or [[Glider]] with enough
        height to steer themselves to the entrance to the [[Obstacle Courses#Diamond Room|Diamond
        Room]]. Prior to the [[Updates#2019.09.28|9/28/2019 Update]], the player is unable to jump
        on the moons during the day, similar to the moon platforms in the [[Bear Gate]] and behind
        [[Dapper Bear's Shop]]. Now, if the player owns the Diamond Mask, the moons in the Diamond
        Room are permanently solid and shaded a deep blue color.

        ==Trivia==
        *If the ingredients to craft this item were to be crafted, a total of 5,000,000,000 honey,
        4,000 [[Royal Jelly|Royal Jellies]], 12,500 [[Blueberry|Blueberries]], 7,500 [[Sunflower
        Seed|Sunflower Seeds]], 100 [[Magic Bean]]s, 2,500 Moon Charms, and 5 Diamond Eggs are
        needed.
        *This and the [[Honey Mask]] are the only [[Items]] that require [[Egg]]s to craft.
        *This mask and the Demon Mask both are the only masks in the game that resemble
        [[Bees/Legendary|Legendary bees]].
        **The Diamond Mask is based on [[Diamond Bee]] and uses the same face decal as it.
        *When receiving the quest 'Breath of Blue', [[Spirit Bear]] says that Diamond Drain is
        activated on every 25th [[Ability Tokens|Blue Boost]] token collected, despite it being
        every 30th blue ability token collected.
        **It is also stated by Spirit Bear that the amount of pollen converted by the ability
        decreases with each use, though this has not been confirmed.
        *This is one of the most expensive item in the game (in terms of honey, excluding [[Hive
        Slot|hive slots]]), tied with the Demon Mask and the [[Gummy Mask]], and being cheaper than
        [[The Supreme Saturator|Supreme]] [[The Supreme Saturator|Saturator]] (55.555 billion), the
        [[Gummy Boots|Gummy]] [[Gummy Boots|Boots]] (100 billion), the [[Heat-Treated
        Planter|Heat-Treated]] [[Heat-Treated Planter|Planter]] (750 billion), the [[Hydroponic
        Planter|Hydroponic]] [[Hydroponic Planter|Planter]] (750 billion), the [[Dark Scythe]] and
        the [[Tide Popper]] costing(2.5 trillion) each, the [[Petal Planter|Petal]] [[Petal
        Planter|Planter]] (5 trillion), the [[Coconut Belt]] (7.5 trillion), the [[Gummyballer]] (10
        trillion), and [[The Planter Of Plenty|The]] [[The Planter Of Plenty|Planter]] [[The Planter
        Of Plenty|Of]] [[The Planter Of Plenty|Plenty]] (100 trillion) being most expensive item.
        *The Diamond Mask shop, the [[Hub Field Shop]] and the [[Demon Mask]] Shop are the only
        shops in the game that have a total of 1 item.
        *This, the Demon Mask and the Gummy Mask are the only masks with decorative accessories (the
        Diamond Mask has a tiara).
        *Unlike the Demon Mask and Gummy Mask, the sound effect of its passive is not based on music
        in the game.
        *According to Spirit Bear, the amount of converted pollen from the Diamond Drain passive
        gets weaker with every use until the player returns to the hive.
        *This hat gives the most capacity.
