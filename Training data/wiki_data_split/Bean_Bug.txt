        }}The '''Bean Bug''' is a passive [[Mobs|Mob]] that can randomly appear in any [[Field]]
        (except the [[Ant Field]]), be spawned through the [[Wind Shrine]] or be manually spawned by
        [[Onett (Developer)|Onett]]. They often spawn in one field in groups of 3 or more, and later
        split up.

        When they spawn, they spit 1-3 {{Cost|Jelly Beans}} tokens in volleys over the field which
        any player can pick up. Then they hop to another location on the field and spit more. The
        tokens only last for a few seconds, much less time than the tokens spawned from regular
        Jelly Beans. After 7-15 volleys of Jelly Beans, the bean bugs hop to other fields to spit
        Jelly Beans. This process repeats until they despawn after a while.

        Bean bugs can aid the player in collecting [[Pollen]]/[[Honey]], as Jelly Bean tokens can
        boost the player’s pollen collection, [[System Page#Critical Chance|Critical Chance]],
        [[System Page#Instant Conversion|Instant Conversion]], and other stats.

        The beans were used for the limited time [[Sun Bear]] questline to receive 250 [[Ticket]]s
        and for the limited 2019 [[Bee Bear]] questlines. They are also used for [[Spirit
        Bear|Spirit Bear's]] questline, and also used for Bee Bear's questline. Sun Bear and Bee
        Bear's Bean Bug questlines could only be received as a part of an event.

        Players are unable to damage it, nor can it damage players, hence why it is a passive mob.

        == Trivia ==
        * Bean bugs, [[Frog]]s, [[Fireflies]], [[Chicks#Chick|Chicks]], [[Chicks#Spotted
        Chick|Spotted Chicks]], and [[Chicks#Hostage Chick|Hostage Chicks]] are the only passive
        mobs in the game, as they cannot deal damage to players.
        * They were first introduced in the [[Updates#2019.09.28|9/28/2019 Update]] when Sun Bear
        accidentally released the bean bugs.
        * Sometimes, they can get stuck in various areas, including the [[Mushroom Field]], [[Cactus
        Field]], [[Bamboo Field]], [[Basic Bee Gate]], and [[Fields]] that have solid object(s)
        while jumping. If they get stuck, they can’t change course and will remain useless until
        de-spawning. Onett joked about this being \"natural selection\".
        * These and fireflies are the only passive mobs that are able to produce [[Ability
        Tokens|Tokens.]]
        * This, [[Stick Bug]], [[Snowbear]]s and [[Mechsquito]]s are the only mobs being able to
        spawn a [[Buffs & Debuffs#Debuffs|debuff]]. Stick Bug can spawn the Splinter Trap,
        Snowbear can Freeze with its Snowballs, Mechsquitos can apply Mechsquito Toxin by firing at
        the player, and bean bugs can spawn spoiled Jelly Beans.
        * As confirmed by Bee Bear, bean bugs have small brains and cannot understand the concept of
        naughty or nice.
        * At the end of Beesmas 2020, Onett spawned them as \"The Bean Bug Brigade\".
        **They then returned at the end of Beesmas 2021 as they \"Awoke from Hibernation\".
        *Bean Bugs are the first Mob that jumps, with frogs being the second.
        **While technically Chicks jump when they go inside their egg, this is the only time they do
        so. They do not do this to get around, so they do not count.
