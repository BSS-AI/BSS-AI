        This field can spawn [[honey]], [[Blueberry|blueberry]], [[Neonberry|neonberry]],
        [[ticket]], [[Ability Tokens#Boost|blue boost]], [[Blue Extract|blue extract]], and [[Magic
        Bean|magic bean]] tokens when collecting [[pollen]].

        [[Bees]] that like the Blue Flower Field include [[Bumble Bee]], [[Bubble Bee]], [[Bucko
        Bee]], [[Frosty Bee]], [[Baby Bee]], [[Diamond Bee]], [[Ninja Bee]], and [[Buoyant Bee]].
        Bees that dislike the Blue Flower Field include [[Rage Bee]], [[Bear Bee]], and [[Festive
        Bee]].

        This field gives Refreshing [[Nectar]] when a planter is placed here.

        == Obtaining Boosts ==
        [[File:Fis blueflower.png|thumb|75px|The Blue Flower Field Boost icon.]]

        * Using the [[Blue Field Booster]] may give a Blue Flower [[Field Boost]]. When it does, it
        will give +300% Blue Flower Field pollen for 15 minutes.
        * Using [[glitter]] will add 100% Blue Flower Field pollen onto the current boost for 15
        minutes if it isn't already maxed out.
        * Using [[Field Dice|field dice]], [[Smooth Dice|smooth dice]], and [[Loaded Dice|loaded
        dice]] may add +100% - 300% Blue Flower Field pollen for 15 minutes if the current boost
        isn't already maxed out.
        * Blue Flower [[Field Wind]]s can be obtained through the [[Wind Shrine]] or [[codes]]. They
        have a base of +20% instant Blue Flower Field conversion, and give +5% instant Blue Flower
        Field conversion and +25% Blue Flower Field pollen for each stack. Blue Flower Field Winds
        can stack up to a total of 13 times.
        * Redeeming certain valid codes:
        **Luther (Activates Blue Flower Field Code Buff + other stuff).
        **1MLikes (Gives Blue Flower Field Boost x1 + other stuff).
        *It can be boosted by the [[King Beetle Amulet]]. If it has a Blue Flower Field Boost, it
        can boost the field by +1% to +100%.

        ===The methods below are no longer possible.===

        *Giving a [[present]] to certain NPCs:

        **[[Onett]] gave Blue Flower Field Boost x1 and other boosts and items.
        *Completing [[Gifted Bucko Bee#Egg Hunt Quest 2019|Gifted Bucko Bee's Egg Hunt 2019 Quest]]

        ** 1mFavorites (Gave Blue Flower Field Boost x1 + other stuff).
        **ClubParty (Gave Blue Flower Field Boost x6 + other stuff).
        **AceUnhackedLoot (Gave Blue Flower Field Boost x2 + other stuff).
        **PingBoost (Gave Blue Flower Field Boost x4 + other stuff).
        **Discord50k (Gave Blue Flower Field Boost x4 + other stuff).
        **Buzzy (Gave Blue Flower Field Boost x3 and Blue Flower Field Winds x3 + other stuff).

        Page#Hive Convert Rate|Convert Rate At Hive]].

        == Trivia ==
        * This field is adjacent to the [[Blue HQ]] and [[King Beetle's Lair]].
        * Sometimes, the giant [[Bubble Wand]] model on the Blue HQ gets a face and will blow
        bubbles towards this field, along with the [[Mushroom Field]], [[Clover Field]], and
        [[Dandelion Field]], to produce [[sparkles]].
        * In the early days of the game, the decorative flowers in this field used to face upwards.
        This was changed due to the player being able to stand on the flower and the Rhino Beetle
        only looking at them instead of attacking.
        ** However, you can still see the decorative flowers facing upwards in the tutorial.
        * According to [[Spirit Bear]], this field used to be the \"Blueberry Field.\"
        * This is the only blue field that gives [[Nectar|Refreshing Nectar]].
