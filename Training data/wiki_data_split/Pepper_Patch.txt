        The '''Pepper Patch''' is a [[field]] added in the [[Updates#2019.9.28|9/28/2019 update]],
        located past the [[Windy Bee Gate]], to the right of the [[Memory Match#Extreme Memory
        Match|Extreme Memory Match]] and to the left of the [[Wind Shrine]]. There are no [[mobs]]
        that protect this field.

        This is a 21x27 field, containing 567 [[flowers]]. The flowers are red and white, and the
        only natural size is triple.

        This field can spawn [[Strawberry|strawberry]], [[Bitterberry|bitterberry]], [[Royal
        Jelly|royal jelly]], [[Red Extract|red extract]], [[ticket]] and [[Magic Bean|magic bean]]
        tokens when collecting [[pollen]].
        The only [[Bees|bee]] that likes the Pepper Patch is [[Spicy Bee]]. The only bee that
        dislikes the Pepper Patch is [[Fuzzy Bee]].

        This field gives Invigorating [[Nectar]] when a planter is placed here.

        A level 9-12 [[Rogue Vicious Bee]] can spawn here.

        == Obtaining Boosts ==
        * Using the [[Red Field Booster]] may give a Pepper [[Field Boost|Patch Boost]] (only if the
        player has 35+ bees). When it does, it will give +100% Pepper Patch pollen for 15 minutes.
        * Using [[glitter]] will add 100% Pepper Patch pollen onto the current boost for 15 minutes
        if it isn't already maxed out.
        [[File:PepperFieldBoost.png|thumb|75px|The Pepper Patch boost icon.]]
        * Using [[Field Dice|field dice]], [[Smooth Dice|smooth dice]], or [[Loaded Dice|loaded
        dice]] may add +100% - +300% Pepper Patch pollen for 15 minutes if the current boost isn't
        already maxed out.
        * Pepper Patch [[Field Wind|Winds]] can be obtained through the [[Wind Shrine]] or
        [[codes]]. They have a base of +20% instant Pepper Patch conversion and give +5% instant
        Pepper Patch conversion and +10% Pepper Patch pollen for each stack. Pepper Patch Winds can
        stack up to a total of 15 times.
        * Redeeming certain valid [[codes]]:
        * There are no valid codes at this time.

        === The methods below are no longer possible. ===
        * Redeeming certain expired codes:
        ** Sriracha (Gave Pepper Patch Boost x2 and Pepper Patch Capacity x2 + other stuff).
        ** 10mMembers (Gives Pepper Patch Code Buff + other stuff).
        ** DemiDecade (Gives Pepper Patch Boost x4 + Pepper Patch Winds x15 + other stuff).
        ** Gumaden10T (Gave Pepper Patch Boost x1 and Pepper Patch Winds x3 + other stuff).
        ** RedMarket (Gave Pepper Patch Boost x1, Pepper Patch Market Boost, and Pepper Patch
        Capacity x1).
        **FrozenBugReboot (Gave Pepper Patch Boost x4 and Pepper Patch Code Buff + other stuff).
        * Purchasing a Pepper Patch Market Boost from the [[Boost Market]] or certain codes gave
        x1.25 Pepper Patch Pollen along with 1.5x [[Field Capacity|Pepper Patch Capacity]], x1.25
        [[System Page#Pollen From Flames|Flame Pollen]], and x1.25 [[System Page#Hive Convert
        Rate|Convert Rate At Hive]].

        == Trivia ==
        *This, the Coconut Field, the [[Stump Field]], and the [[Ant Challenge|Ant Field]] are
        currently the only fields released in updates.
        *This, the [[Mountain Top Field]], Stump Field, and Coconut Field are the only fields to
        solely have natural triple flowers.
        *The Pepper Patch is the only field past a bee gate with nothing guarding it.
        * This and the Coconut Field are the only fields where [[sprout]]s cannot appear naturally
        or be planted with the [[Special Sprout Summoner]].
        *The [[Badges#Pepper Badge|Pepper Badge]] and [[Badges#Coconut Badge|Coconut Badge]]
        requires more pollen collected than the other field
        badges.[[File:PepperPatch2.png|thumb|238x238px|Pepper Patch before being changed]]
        *The Pepper Patch used to cover the entire platform it is placed on. Now, the field has
        shrunk a little and the area was amplified. As a result, it does not cover the edges of the
        platform anymore.
        **The peppers used as decoration were also reorganized.
        *This is the only red field that gives [[Nectar|Invigorating Nectar]].
