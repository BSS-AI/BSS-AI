        (Bronze, Silver, Gold, Diamond, and Supreme) from machines in the [[Star Hall]]. They grant
        buffs to the owner and always increase [[capacity]] as they go up a tier. Higher tier
        amulets grant more and better buffs. Diamond and Supreme Star Amulets also have additional
        [[Passive Abilities#Star Amulet Passives|Star Passive]] abilities. The more [[gifted bee]]
        types the player has discovered, the more likely the stats from the Star Amulets will be at
        maxed value.

        Each Star Amulet requires the user to have a certain number of gifted bees discovered. After
        the user generates a Star Amulet, the user must wait 1 second to generate another one.

        If the player disconnects while deciding to keep old or replace a Star Amulet, they will
        have another chance to keep old or replace when they rejoin a server.

        When the player generates a Star Amulet, the following audio plays (The sound pitch when
        creating a Star Amulet decreases when going up in tiers):

        ==== Supreme Star Passives ====
        The Supreme Star Passives are the main feature of Supreme Star Amulets. There are 6 passives
        that the player can get, with a 1/6 chance of getting a specific one. There is also a 1/50
        chance to get another passive, resulting in two passive abilities. If the player has 500B
        honey or more when generating an amulet, they can choose to spend 500B to guarantee a double
        passive. If a double passive is generated, there is a 1/15 chance of getting a specific
        combination (1/6 * 1/5 * 2). This means there is a 1/750 chance to get a specific pair of
        passives from generating an amulet overall (1/15 * 1/50). The player can only have a maximum
        of two passives from a Supreme Star Amulet at a time. Listed below are the possible
        passives:

        ==Trivia==
        *The Supreme Star Amulet Generator takes the place of the original spot taken by Diamond
        Star Amulet Generator, only a bit more moved back.
        *The Diamond Star Amulet and Supreme Star Amulet are the only two amulets in the game that
        can have or always have passive abilities on them.
        * The Diamond Star Amulet can only give 2 types of [[Passive Abilities|passives]], being
        Guiding Star and Star Shower.
        *The Supreme Star Amulet is the only Star Amulet added after the original release of Star
        Amulets.
        *The Supreme Star Amulet is the only Star Amulet that can have double passive abilities.
        * The star of the Supreme Star Amulet passive abilities rotate counter-clockwise around the
        player.
        *The Star Amulets used to require the gifted bee types in the player's [[hive]]. However,
        this was changed to only requiring discovering gifted bee types in the
        [[Updates#2020.06.06|6/6/2020 update]].
        * An [[Ant Pass]] token is located behind the Supreme Star Amulet Generator.
        ** It used to be behind the Diamond Star Amulet Generator before the Supreme Star Amulet
        Generator took its place.
        * The Star Shower passive functions similarly to [[Science Bear|Science Bear's]] [[Beesmas
        Lights]].
        *The audio for obtaining a Star Amulet was the same audio for completing the [[Ready Player
        Two]] ROBLOX event (if the player has ALL the event badges).
        *When the Star Shower Passive is activated, the big star that indicates the area that the
        stars will fall has a slightly stretched top.
        *If a player dies while Scorching Star, Pop Star, or Gummy Star is active, the star will
        continue to hover wherever the player dies. It will be without the star’s model and will not
        follow the player, but it will still play its tune. It will also continue giving the buffs
        that the passive grants.
        *If the player has a little under 500 billion honey, it is still possible to buy the offer
        of granting a guaranteed double passive on a Supreme Star Amulet.
        *In some cases, if the player has activated Pop Star and reaches the time limit, sometimes
        it will activate another Pop Star buff. However, it won't count the number of popped
        bubbles.
        *The Diamond Star Amulet has a 1/1,000,000 chance of giving a Star Cub Skin.
