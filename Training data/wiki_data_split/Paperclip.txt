        no color requirement. It can only be equipped to [[Bees/Rare|rare]] or [[Bees/Epic|epic]]
        [[bees]].

        ==Potential==
        * [[System Page#Bee Ability Rate|Ability Rate]] (Guaranteed) - +1% to +3%. (up to +6% from
        [[Waxes|waxing]])
        * Ability Pollen (Guaranteed) - +5% to +15%. (up to +65% from waxing)
        * [[System Page#Convert Rate At Hive|Convert Rate At Hive]] (from waxing) - +2% to +30%.
        * Ability: [[Ability Tokens#Token Link|Token Link]] (from waxing, extremely rare, caustic
        only)
        ** Collects all [[Ability Tokens|ability tokens]], granting 25 [[Honey]] (+10 per lvl) per
        token collected.
        * [Hive Bonus] Ability Token Lifespan (Guaranteed) - +1% to +3%. (up to +9% from waxing)
        * [Hive Bonus] Bee Ability Pollen (from waxing, rare) - +1% to +3%.

        ==Ways to obtain==
        *As a very rare drop from the [[Petal Planter]].
        *Purchasing it from [[Dapper Bear's Shop]].
