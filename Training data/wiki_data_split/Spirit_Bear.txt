        [[File:Spirit bear.PNG|thumb|Spirit Bear standing in the 35 Bee zone.]]
        '''Spirit Bear''' is a [[Quest Givers|Quest Giver]] and is one of the eight permanent Quest
        [[Bears]] that can be accessed in the game, the others being [[Black Bear]], [[Brown Bear]],
        [[Mother Bear]], [[Panda Bear]], [[Science Bear]], [[Dapper Bear]], and [[Polar Bear]]. She
        holds a [[Petal Wand]] and wears a [[Petal Belt]]. The player must have 35 [[bees]] or more
        to reach Spirit Bear. She is located between the [[Coconut Field]] and the [[Pepper Patch]],
        and in front of the [[Memory Match#Extreme Memory Match|Extreme Memory Match]].

        Her [[Quests]] are far more difficult and take much longer to complete than the ones given
        by [[Science Bear]] or [[Onett]], as the later quests will require billions of [[Pollen]],
        Grandmaster [[Badges]], and huge amounts of [[Ability Tokens|Tokens]] collected. The main
        rewards from her quests are [[Spirit Petal]]s, which are granted every ten quests. In total,
        the player can obtain three, one to donate to the [[Wind Shrine]] (to get the [[Windy Bee]]
        or a [[Star Treat]] if the player already obtained a [[Windy Bee]] through buying it in an
        expired robux pack), another for the [[Petal Wand]], and another for the [[Petal
        Belt]].<br />

        == Trivia ==
        * She is one of the two female bears in the game, the other being [[Mother Bear]].
        * You can get to her without 35 bees by using the [[Red Cannon]], then using the
        [[Parachute]] or [[Glider]] to glide over, then make sure you have high ping so you are able
        to talk to her before you get kicked out of the 35 bee zone.
        * Her idle animation comes from the
        [https://www.roblox.com/bundles/48/Elder-Animation-Package Elder Animation Package].
        * She quite often refers to her sister, another bear who was very close to her.
        * She, [[Stick Bug]], the [[Cub Buddy|Cub Buddies]], Bee Bear and [[Shadow Bear]] are the
        only NPCs that dance.
        * She is the second NPC to hold a tool, the first being [[Onett]], which makes her the only
        bear holding a tool.
        * She is the third NPC to wear a belt, the other two being [[Sun Bear]] and [[Onett]].
        ** Spirit Bear, Sun Bear, and Dapper Bear are the only [[Bears]] to wear accessories, Spirit
        Bear and Sun Bear being the only bears to wear [[:Category:Accessories|in-game
        accessories]].
        * Spirit Bear is based off of [[Wikipedia:Kermode bear|Kermode Bears]], which are sometimes
        referred as spirit bears.
        ** Kermode bears are a rare subspecies of the black bear.
        * She mentions the [[Bee Swarm Simulator Wiki]] in the quest \"Spirit's Starter\".
        * Both she and [[Science Bear]] have [[items]] that are only obtainable from their quests,
        which are [[Spirit Petal]]s and [[Translator]]s respectively.
        * The [[Codes|Code]] \"BeesBuzz123\" is her account's password on her own website, proven from
        her dialogue in \"QR Quest\".
        * She, [[Panda Bear]], [[Black Bear]], [[Gummy Bear]] and [[Science Bear]] are the only
        bears that mention/have a code.
        * It is possible to glitch into Spirit Bear and get a quest from her. The player has to jump
        on the [[Windy Bee]] statue on top of the [[Windy Bee Gate|35 Bee Gate]] and glide or jump
        to her, or, if the player has enough ping, climb up the big rock left of the gate and
        nearest to the [[Coconut Field]] and talk to her.
        * She has the most words per quest in her dialogue, at around 2,000 per quest, even
        surpassing Science Bear with the most words per quest dialogue.
        ** She has around 21 thousand words in her whole dialogue.
        * She and [[Dapper Bear]] are the only permanent quest bears to not have a [[Ability
        Tokens#Bear Morph|Bear Morph]].
        * All of Spirit Bear's quests require collecting an increasing amount of white pollen. (With
        an exception of her Beesmas quests.)
        * Spirit Bear is the second bear to require the completion of another bear's quest, the
        first being [[Bee Bear]] and [[Sun Bear]] being third.
        * Spirit Bear is the only quest giver that can summon [[Nighttime]], a [[Wild Windy Bee]],
        and a [[Snowstorm]]. However, Spirit Bear, along with [[Honey Bee (NPC)|Honey Bee]], are the
        only quest givers that can summon a [[Honeystorm]].
        * She is self-aware that she is in a game, as she mentions \"game
        balance<nowiki>''</nowiki> in <nowiki>''</nowiki>Sway Away.\"
        ** This could be because of multiple reasons, such as her saying \"Ol' Stumpy\", her
        mentioning the wiki you are viewing this on, or her age.
        * She is the only permanent quest giver that requires the player to play [[Memory Match]]es.
        * She is the only permanent quest giver that requires the player to use the [[Wealth Clock]]
        for her quests.
        * Spirit Bear refers to the Stump Snail as <nowiki>''Ol' Stumpy''</nowiki>. This
        may be a reference to the youtuber [https://www.youtube.com/user/ThnxCya ThnxCya] as he
        references it as <nowiki>''Ol' Stumpy''</nowiki> too.
        * Sources confirm that she's a 1983's Memory Match tournament champion, and that explains
        why she gives quests that require players to play [[Memory Match]].
        * Some of her quests give items upon receiving the quest. These items include: [[Jelly
        Beans]], [[Royal Jelly#Star Jelly|Star Jelly]], [[Glitter]], [[Magic Bean]]s, [[Coconut]]s,
        [[Pineapple|Pineapples]], [[Sunflower Seed|Sunflower Seeds]] and [[Gumdrops]].
        * Her favorite holiday is Galentine's Day.
        * Her sleep talking during the quest \"Do You Bee-lieve In Magic?\" is probably a reference to
        the image of [[Basic Bee]] winking that can be found at the end of the White Tunnel, hinting
        to the code \"Wink\".
        * In the 2021 Beesmas Quest, when she tells the player where to get [[Nectar|Satisfying
        Nectar]], she mentions the wrong fields; The [[Dandelion Field]], the [[Bamboo Field]], and
        the [[Pine Tree Forest]] is where the player can get [[Nectar|Comforting Nectar]], not
        [[Nectar|Satisfying Nectar]].
        * In her first quest, Spirit Bear reveals that she planted the Sunflowers in the [[Sunflower
        Field]].
        ** The Sunflowers were requested by her Sister, who loves to snack on
        [https://bee-swarm-simulator.fandom.com/wiki/Treats#Sunflower_Seed Sunflower Seeds].

        * The dialogue of her quest \"Witness Grandeur\" reveals she has known [[Brown Bear]] since he
        was a cub.
