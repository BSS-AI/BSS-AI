        '''Robo Bear's Shop''' is a [https://bee-swarm-simulator.fandom.com/wiki/Category:Shops
        shop] run by [[Robo Bear]] located right of the [[Red HQ]], next to the [[Rose Field]]. This
        shop sells all of the [[drives]], and [[Digital Bee]]. It was added alongside the rest of
        Robo Bear's area in the [[Updates|12/26/22]] update.

        Robo Bear's Shop is not the official name of the shop; it is only called this (assumably)
        because the owner of the shop is Robo Bear.

        The formula for the price of each colored drive in honey is

        <math>P(n) = 250{,}000n^{2}</math>

        where ''n'' represents the ''n''th drive of that type to purchase (1st is 250,000, 2nd is
        1,000,000, 3rd is 2,250,000, etc). The price for [[Glitched Drive]]s begins at ''n + 1''.
