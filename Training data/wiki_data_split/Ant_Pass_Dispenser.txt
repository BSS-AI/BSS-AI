        The '''Ant Pass Dispenser''' is a [[dispenser]] that is located behind the [[Ant Gate]] and
        to the right of the [[Free Ant Pass Dispenser]]. It gives one [[Ant Pass|ant pass]] for 10
        [[ticket]]s, which can be used to access the [[Ant Challenge]]. The price does not increase
        and there is no cooldown on using the dispenser, unlike the Free Ant Pass Dispenser. Players
        cannot use the dispenser if they already have 10 or more ant passes in their inventory.

        == Trivia ==
        * This, the Free Ant Pass Dispenser, the [[Robo Pass Dispenser]], and the [[Free Robo Pass
        Dispenser]] are the only [[:Category:Dispenser|dispensers]] that don't have any visible
        faucets, and instead have a small compartment, like a vending machine that gives out
        tickets.
        * This, the [[Royal Jelly Dispenser]], and the Robo Pass Dispenser are the only dispensers
        that require tickets to use.
