{"text": "'''Energy''' represents the number of times a [[Bees|bee]] will gather [[pollen]] from a
        [[Flowers|flower]], sip [[nectar]], and [[Bee Attack|attack]] [[mobs]] before returning to
        the [[hive]] to rest. Each action costs one energy. Spawning [[Ability Tokens]] does not use
        up energy. The energy of all bees in a hive can be increased with stacks of [[Buffs &
        Debuffs#From NPCs|Polar Power]] from [[Polar Bear]]'s quests, certain [[mutation]]s, certain
        [[beequip]]s or by [[Bond|leveling up]] bees.

        It takes 30 seconds for a bee to rest and recover all its energy in the hive. However, there
        are some exceptions. [[Shocked Bee]] takes a lesser time to sleep, as well as [[Exhausted
        Bee]] and [[Photon Bee]] never sleeping at all. However, if the player dies or resets all
        bees will return to the hive to sleep regardless of any buffs, and they sleep for 30 seconds
        except Shocked Bee who sleeps for only 15 seconds.

        When a bee runs out of energy, a message will appear, stating,
        \"😴 [Type] Bee is out of energy! It's going to sleep.\"

        == Trivia==
        *The bees with the highest finite energy are the [[Lion Bee]] and [[Buoyant Bee]].
        *There was a [[Glitches|glitch]] where the energy of a bee would go to an absurd amount if
        the player used a [[Royal Jelly|royal jelly]] on an Exhausted Bee or Photon Bee.
        * There is a glitch where dying twice in quick succession causes the player's bees to go to
        sleep and recover energy, instead of just the 30 second cooldown after death. This includes
        Photon Bee and Exhausted bee, who both have unlimited energy, likely due to them not
        actually having \"infinite\" energy.
        *The energy for Exhausted Bee and Photon Bee is actually set to 280,000,000.
        *As a bee loses energy, it also loses convert amount.
        **When a bee wakes up, The Energy and Convert Amount will max.
