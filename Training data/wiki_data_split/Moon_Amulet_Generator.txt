{"text": "[[File:Moon_Amulet_Generator.jpg|thumb|214px|The Moon Amulet Generator.]] The '''Moon
        Amulet Generator''' is a [[:Category:Machines|machine]] located between [[Spider Field]] and
        [[Bamboo Field]] that allows the player to generate and/or replace a [[Moon Amulet]]. To use
        this machine, {{Cost|Moon Charm|100}} are needed to generate a moon amulet.

        It can be reached by traversing the [[Obstacle Courses#Bamboo Field Obby|obstacle course]]
        between the Spider Field and [[Bamboo Field]], by dropping in from above by cannon and using
        a [[:Category:Gliding Tools|gliding tool]], or by falling off the ramp leading to the [[Lion
        Bee Gate]] and onto the platform.

        Every time the player uses the generator, the capacity buff increases, starting at 25k and
        increasing by 25k each. This buff stops at the maximum of 250k capacity. However, to obtain
        the Moon Amulet at its highest potential, the player would have to use the generator 25
        times, being when the other (potential) stat boosts max out.

        When using the generator, it will say, \"🌙 The moon seems to grow brighter... 🌙\". If the
        player has reached the maximum [[System Page#Capacity Multiplier|capacity]] [[Buffs &
        Debuffs|buff]] (+250k), the chat instead states, \"🌙 The moon seems happy! 🌙\".

        When the player generates a Moon Amulet, the following audio plays:

        == Trivia ==
        * The Moon Amulet Generator glows at [[Day/Night Cycle|night]], although nothing changes
        mechanically.
        * The Moon Amulet Generator is one of the 6 amulet generators, the other 5 being the [[Star
        Amulet|star amulet]] generators in the [[Star Hall]].
        * This is the only amulet generator that requires something other than [[honey]] to
        activate.
        * This is the only Generator that increases the Capacity Buff and stops at maximum.
