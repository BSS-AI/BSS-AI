        [[File:Smile sticker icon.png|thumb|The icon located on top of the Sticker Stack.]]The
        '''Sticker Stack''' is located in the [[Honey Bee Gate|15 Bee Zone]] next to the [[Pine Tree
        Forest]] and the [[Honey Bee (NPC)]]. It allows players to donate a [[Sticker#Sticker Index
        (being finished 1/13/24-1/14/24)|Sticker]], Hive Skin, [[Cub Buddy|Cub Skin]] or Voucher and
        receive a boost and item depending on the sticker you donated. The boost lasts for 15
        minutes (+10 seconds for every sticker donated, maximum 1 hour.) Each type of sticker can
        only be donated once.

        == Usage ==
        To use the Sticker Stack, step into the podium in front of it and open the menu. The player
        can then choose to either pay 25 tickets or use a sticker that hasn't been stacked yet to
        activate the stack. Both ways lead to activating the sticker stack, granting the sticker
        stack buff with the duration and buffs depending on the past stacked stickers (15-minute
        base duration with an extra 10 seconds for each sticker stacked, 1-hour maximum duration).
        Stacking an unused sticker for activating the machine will grant a new buff depending on the
        stacked sticker and an extra 10 seconds duration to the buff permanently for future
        activations and the sticker that was stacked cannot be used to stack again. Upon stacking a
        sticker, the player will be granted rewards also depending on the sticker stacked.

        == Trivia ==
        *The sticker stack was added in the 1/12/2024 update, along with [[sticker]]s and the [[Hub
        Field|Hive Hub]].
        *If you get a reward from the sticker stack that contains a limit (like the
        [[Micro-Converter]]) the capacity limit is ignored and the reward is still given even if the
        player has the maximum amount.
        *The machine is used for the 5 sticker stack badges, but its Grandmaster variant is
        impossible because it requires 500 different stickers stacked and only 280 stickers
        currently exist in the game.
