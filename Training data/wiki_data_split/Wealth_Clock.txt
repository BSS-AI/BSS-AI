        The '''Wealth Clock''' is a [[:Category:Machines|machine]] that boosts [[System Page#Honey
        Per Pollen|Honey Per Pollen]] and grants [[ticket]]s to the player. The Wealth Clock is
        located next to [[Brown Bear]], the entrance to [[Commando Chick's Hideout]] and the
        [[Honeystorm|Honeystorm Summoner]].

        To use the clock, the player will need to have discovered 5 types of [[bees]]. The clock can
        only be used every hour of playtime. Each use grants x1.01 Honey Per Pollen, which stacks up
        to 5 times, with a maximum of x1.05 Honey Per Pollen. The Wealth Clock is also able to award
        {{Cost|Ticket}}, starting at 1 for the first use, increasing the amount of tickets awarded
        by 1 for every subsequent use until capping at {{Cost|Ticket|5}}, on which the Wealth Clock
        will only produce {{Cost|Ticket|5}} per use. Every subsequent use will award the player
        {{Cost|Ticket|5}}. Every time it does, it will say the following:

        (First four times)<br />
        \"[🕒 The Wealth clock has powered up... 🕒]<br />
        [It increases Honey made from pollen by #%.]<br />
        [It printed a Ticket/# Tickets!]<br />
        [Come back in 1 hour and it'll print #!]<br />
        [Remember, it resets if you leave the game.]<br />
        [+# Ticket (from Wealth Clock)]\"

        (Fifth Time Onward)<br />
        \"[🕒 The Wealth clock is fully powered! 🕒]<br />
        [It prints 5 Tickets per hour!]<br />
        [Remember, it resets if you leave the game.]<br />

        [+5 Tickets (from Wealth Clock)]\"

        == Sound Effect ==
        [[File:WealthClock.ogg]]

        == Notes ==
        * There is a 15-minute grace period (can be offline for 15 minutes) until the Wealth Clock
        buff resets. This means the player can exit and rejoin, while still maintaining the boost,
        as long as they join within the 15-minute grace period.
        ** However, if the player joins in that period of time, the Wealth Clock cooldown will reset
        back to an hour.
        * The player can obtain wealth clock boost by redeeming certain valid [[codes]]:
        ** 1MLikes (Activates Wealth Clock Buff x1 + other stuff).
        ** WalmartToys (Activates Wealth Clock Buff x1 + other stuff).

        == Trivia ==
        * The Wealth Clock is located where the Honeystorm Summoner used to be.
        * As of the [[Updates#2018.11.25|11/25/2018 update]], it increases the number of tickets it
        prints each interval. It will also start printing tickets the moment the player starts using
        it, but the player has to be in the server for 1 hour to use it instead of the previous 30
        minute cooldown.
        * The amount of tickets granted by the Wealth Clock is based on the player's current stack
        of the Wealth Clock buff rather than how many times the player has used it (e.g if the
        player has 1 stack of the Wealth Clock buff and then uses the Wealth Clock for the first
        time, it will grant 2 tickets instead of 1).
        * The [[Badges#Playtime Badge|Playtime Badge]] is represented by the Wealth Clock.
        * The Wealth Clock has a 1/250 (0.4%) chance to spawn a Hourglass [[Sticker#Sticker
        Index|Sticker]] in the [[Clover Field]].
