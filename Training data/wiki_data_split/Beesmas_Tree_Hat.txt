        A '''Beesmas Tree Hat''' is a [[Bond|level]] 10 [[:Category:Beesmas|Beesmas]] [[beequip]].
        It has an equip limit of three. There is no color requirement. It can only be equipped to
        [[bees]] with a [[mutation]].

        == Possible Stats ==
        * Convert Amount (Guaranteed) - +10% to +20%. (up to +60% from [[Waxes|waxing]])
        * [[Critical Hits|Critical Chance]] (Guaranteed) - +2% to +5%. (up to +10% from waxing)
        * Ability [[Pollen]] (Guaranteed) - +2% to +5%. (up to +10% from waxing)
        * Ability Rate - +1%. (up to +2% from waxing)
        * [Hive Bonus] [[Capacity]] (Guaranteed) - +10,000 to +25,000. (up to +35,000 from waxing)
        * [Hive Bonus] Convert Rate At Hive (Guaranteed) - +3% to +5%. (up to +15% from waxing)
        ==Ways to Obtain==
        ===These methods are no longer possible.===
        *Buying the Festive Bundle from [[Bee Bear’s Catalog]] (2021).
        *By completing [[Bee Bear|Bee Bear's]] Snow Cub Reformation (5/5).
        *From the Honeyday Mega-Haul pack in the [[Robux Shop]].
        *Opening the [[Gift Boxes|Merry Gift Box]].
        *From the [[Stockings]].
        *As a drop from [[Tunnel Bear]].
        *Completing 20th [[Bee Bear|Bee Bear's]] [[Beesmas|Beesmas 2022]] quest gives 1 Beesmas Tree
        Hat.
