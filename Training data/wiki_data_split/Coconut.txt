        A {{Color|Coconut}} is an [[:Category:Inventory|inventory item]] that was added in the
        [[Updates#2019.09.28|9/28/2019 update]]. It will spawn a large, green circle on the
        [[Fields|field]] you used it on. If caught, it will convert [[pollen]] into [[honey]]
        tokens. It can also collect pollen for the player that summoned it, damage [[mobs]], and can
        be used in certain [[crafting]] recipes.

        Pollen from coconuts can be increased with the [[Coconut Clogs]], [[Pollination]], and the
        [[Boost Market|Coconut Field Market Boost]] (Impossible to obtain as the Boost Market has
        been removed).

        A maximum of 500 coconuts can be held at a time, as the item’s highest capacity limit,
        excluding items that don’t have a capacity limit.

        There’s also an On/Off switch on the player’s hotbar (if Coconut Item is in the player’s
        hotbar). The Coconut will be used automatically when this switch is turned on.

        The following audio plays when a coconut hits a field: [[File:Cocothud.ogg]]

        * Growing [[sprout]]s in the Coconut Field.

        * As a drop from [[puffshroom]]s within the 35 bee zone.
        * The Palm Tree [[sticker]] rewards 10 coconuts when added to the [[Sticker Stack]].

        == Conversion ==
        When the coconut lands on a player, the player's total [[System Page#Convert Amount|hive
        convert amount]] is converted into a ring of honey tokens. For example, if a player has
        500,000 total hive convert amount and a coconut lands on them, 500,000 pollen would be
        converted into honey tokens. The amount of pollen stored beforehand is not taken into
        account of how much pollen is converted.

        The amount of honey received from the coconut is multiplied by [[System Page#Honey From
        Tokens|Honey From Tokens]].

        == Trivia ==
        *Before the 12/26/2021 update, you only could have a max of 150 Coconuts in your inventory.
        It has been increased to 500 after said update.
        *Getting hit by a coconut originally converted 75% of the player's hive convert amount into
        honey.
        *This is the only inventory item that can directly damage mobs.
        *If you have no [[pollen]] in your [[Bags|bag]], catching a coconut will still spawn honey
        tokens that give 1 [[honey]] each, (before Honey From Tokens is applied). This happens
        because a honey token needs to give you at least 1 honey.
        **The easiest way to see this is to use a Coconut while in the [[Ant Challenge]].
