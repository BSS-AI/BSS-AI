        '''Stockings''' is a Beesmas exclusive machine that can be unlocked after completing [[Brown
        Bear]]'s 2022 Beesmas quest. They were introduced on [[Updates#2020.12.25|Beesmas 2020]],
        returned on Beesmas 2021, and returned for Beesmas 2022. The Stockings is located at the
        bottom of the hill that Brown Bear is on, next to the [[Clover Field]]. When used, they
        spawn a random [[beequip]], as well as 2 random items in front of the stockings. It has a
        1-hour cooldown.
