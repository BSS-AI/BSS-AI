        The '''Mysterious Figure''' (named '''Eggwick''' or '''Evilwick''') was a short figure with
        a light gray hood, gray skin, and no face. He could be found in the [[Mazes#Blue Maze|Blue
        Maze]] behind the glass wall on the path to the right. He could neither be interacted with
        nor be collided with. There are also two tiny hallways to the right and left of where the
        figure was. The player could enter the hallway using a glitch to bypass the wall.

        Since the [[Updates#2019.09.28|9/28/2019 update]], there is a [[Royal Jelly#Star Jelly|star
        jelly]] token in his place and can only be obtained by entering a passage in the [[Petal
        Shop]] located in the [[Windy Bee Gate]].

        == Trivia ==
        * He, along with [[Onett]], [[Stick Bug]], [[Gifted Riley Bee]], [[Gifted Bucko Bee]],
        [[Honey Bee (NPC)|Honey Bee]], and [[Digital Bee (NPC)|Digital Bee]] are the only
        [[:Category:NPC|NPCs]] that aren't [[:Category:Bears|bears]].
        ** He and Onett are the only Robloxian-species NPCs.
        * This figure represents the Hooded Figure in the Roblox [[Egg Hunt 2019]] Event, who also
        appeared in other games that participated in the Egg Hunt, and in the hub world.
        * He would occasionally wave at the player.
        * This is the only NPC that the player normally couldn't get close to, as a glass wall
        prevented players from reaching him.
        * This is the third NPC to not originate in Bee Swarm Simulator, the first being Onett and
        the second being [[Bubble Bee Man]].
