        A {{Color|Magic Bean}} is an [[:Category:Inventory|inventory item]] that was added in the
        [[Updates#2018.11.25|11/25/2018 update]]. It can be used to summon a random sprout in the
        [[Fields|field]] the player is standing in. When one is planted, a server-wide message will
        appear saying, \"🌱{Username} has planted a (Sprout Type) Sprout...🌱\".

        == Ways to Obtain ==
        * Purchasing for {{Cost|Ticket|20}} each in the [[Magic Bean Shop]] on the right side of
        [[Stump Field]].
        * As a reward from the [[Stick Bug Challenge]].
        * As a reward from donating to the [[Wind Shrine]].
        * As a reward from [[Memory Match]] games.
        * As a stack reward for adding certain [[Stickers]] to the [[Sticker Stack]]:
        ** Forward Facing Aphid rewards 1 magic bean.
        ** Round Green Critter rewards 1 magic bean.
        ** Flying Magenta Critter rewards 1 magic bean.
        ** Blue Triangle Critter rewards 1 magic bean.
        ** Purple Pointed Critter rewards 1 magic bean.
        ** Orange Leg Critter rewards 1 magic bean.
        ** Sprout rewards 1 magic bean.
        ** Critter in a Stocking rewards 1 magic bean.
        ** Festive Pea rewards 1 magic bean.
        * Redeeming certain valid [[codes]].
        ** ClubBean (Gives 1 magic bean and other stuff, requires being a member of the
        [https://www.roblox.com/My/Groups.aspx?gid=3982592 Bee Swarm Simulator Club]).
        * Purchasing in the [[Robux Shop]] for {{Cost|Robux|30}} each.
        ** Additionally, a value pack of 10 Magic Beans are also purchasable for {{Cost|Robux|200}}
        ({{Cost|Robux|30}} each).

        === Drops ===
        * Unknown chance of dropping when collecting pollen in [[fields]].
        * As an unknown chance from [[Leaves]].
        * As a drop from [[aphid]]s and the [[Coconut Crab]].
        * As a rare drop from a [[Rhino Beetle|rhino beetle]], [[ladybug]], [[spider]], [[Stick
        Nymph]]s, [[Stick Bug]], [[King Beetle]], [[Wild Windy Bee]], [[Tunnel Bear]], [[Stump
        Snail]], and the [[Mondo Chick]].
        * As a rare drop from [[Ability Tokens#Festive Gift|Festive Gift]] and [[Ability
        Tokens#Festive Mark|Festive Mark]].
        * As a rare gift from a [[Cub Buddy|cub buddy]].
        * A chance of dropping from any [[sprout]] except for Moon, Gummy, and Debug sprouts.

        === Quests ===
        * [[Black Bear]] gives magic beans on the following quests:
        ** Pumpkins, Please! rewards 1 magic bean along with other items.
        ** Rocky Red Mountain rewards 1 magic bean along with other items.
        ** Rare Red Clover rewards 1 magic bean along with other items.
        ** Okey-Pokey rewards 1 magic bean along with other items.
        ** Capsaicin Collector rewards 1 magic bean along with other items.
        ** Mountain Mix rewards 1 magic bean along with other items.
        ** Variety Fetcher 2 rewards 1 magic bean along with other items.
        ** Weed Wacker 3 rewards 3 magic beans along with other items.
        ** Boss Battles rewards 3 magic beans along with other items.
        ** Black Bear has a chance to reward magic beans on some of his repeatable quests.
        * Completing [[Brown Bear]]'s 55th, 120th, 190th, 220th and 280th quest each reward 1 magic
        bean.
        * [[Science Bear]] gives magic beans on the following quests:
        ** Subsidized Agriculture yields 1 magic bean upon receiving the quest.
        ** Mark Mechanics rewards 1 magic bean and other items.
        ** Testing Teamwork rewards 3 magic beans and other items.
        * [[Polar Bear]] occasionally gives magic beans upon completing one of his quests.
        * [[Spirit Bear]] gives magic beans on the following quests:
        ** Out-Questing Questions rewards 1 magic bean and other items.
        ** Memories of Memories rewards 3 magic beans and other items.
        ** Beans Becoming gives the player a magic bean when they start the quest.
        ** Do You Bee-lieve In Magic? rewards 5 magic beans and other items.
        ** Beeternity rewards 5 magic beans and other items.
        ** A Breath Of Blue rewards 10 magic beans and other items.
        ** Rhubarb Could That Have Been rewards 5 magic beans and other items.
        ** Dreams Of Being A Bee rewards 5 magic beans and other items.
        ** The Sky Over The Stump rewards 5 magic beans and other items.
        ** Bear Without Despair rewards 10 magic beans and other items.
        ** Echoing Call rewards 50 magic beans and other items.
        * As a rare 5th quest reward from [[Gifted Riley Bee]] and [[Gifted Bucko Bee]].
