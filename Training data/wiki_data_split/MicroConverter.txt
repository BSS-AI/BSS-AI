{"text": "{{Consumables
        |image = Mconv.png
        |description = Instantly converts all Pollen in your bag to Honey.
        |cooldown = 5 seconds
        |cap = 15
        }}The '''Micro-Converter''' is an [[:Category:Inventory|inventory item]] added in the
        [[Updates#2019.04.05|4/5/2019 update]]. When used, it instantly converts all [[pollen]] in
        the player's [[Bags|bag]] into [[honey]]. It acts as an [[Instant Converter]] without the
        need for [[ticket]]s and the journey to the shop, a significantly shorter cooldown, and is
        portable.

        The amount of honey the player receives from a micro-converter is not affected by their
        [[System Page#Convert Rate|Convert Rate]], although it is affected by [[System Page#Honey
        Per Pollen|Honey Per Pollen]].

        The player can have a maximum of 15 micro-converters in their inventory at a time. Obtaining
        more than 15 will not add any micro-converters to their inventory unless purchased from the
        [[Robux Shop]], obtained from [[Quests]], or adding certain [[stickers]] to your [[Sticker
        Stack]].

        The following audio plays when a micro-converter is used: [[File:Mconv.ogg]]

        == Ways To Obtain ==
        * Rarely dropped when defeating [[mobs]], such as a [[Rhino Beetle|rhino beetle]],
        [[mantis]], [[werewolf]], [[Tunnel Bear]] and [[aphid]], or guaranteed drop from [[Coconut
        Crab]] and [[Mondo Chick]].
        * Completing some of [[Black Bear#Quests to Star Jelly (15)|Black Bear's Star Jelly quests]]
        and [[Black Bear#Quests to Mythic Egg#Mythic Egg quests|Black Bear]]'s Mythic Egg quests (15
        in total).
        * Completing some of [[Spirit Bear#Quests (30)|Spirit Bear's quests]].
        *Completing some of [[Bee Bear|Bee Bear's]] quests.
        *Completing [[Dapper Bear|Dapper Bear's]] 4th 9th, and 13th quest.
        * Completing 10, 30, 70, 155, 170, 215, and 270 [[Brown Bear]] quests will reward an
        additional 5 micro-converters, and completing 80 will reward an additional 10
        micro-converters.
        * Redeeming certain valid [[codes]]:
        ** Using the code \"WalmartToys\" (Gives 3 micro-converters + other stuff).
        ** Using the code \"ClubConverters\" (Gives 10 micro-converters).

        ** Using the code \"PlushFriday\" (Gives 1 micro-converter + other stuff).
        ** Using the code \"ThnxCyasToyBox\" (Gives 1 micro-converter + other stuff).
        ** Using the code \"5Years\" (Gives 5 micro-converters + other stuff).
        * A rare gift from [[Ability Tokens#Festive Gift|Festive Gifts]].
        * As a rare drop from [[Ability Tokens#Festive Mark|Festive Mark]].
        * As a reward for donating to the [[Wind Shrine]].
        * Matching a pair of micro-converters in [[Memory Match]]es (available in increments of 1,
        2, 6, or 5 depending on which Memory Match).
        * Rarely through a [[Cub Buddy]] gift in increments of 1 or 5.
        * A token behind [[Black Bear]] in between the [[Red HQ]] and the [[Windy Bee Gate]].
        * As a drop from a [[Blue Clay Planter]].
        ** Planting it in the [[Spider Field]] makes it drop at least 10 Micro-Converters.
        * As a reward from [[Robo Bear Challenge]].
        * As a Stack Reward for adding certain [[stickers]] to the [[Sticker Stack]]:
        ** The Alert Icon rewards 3 micro-converters.
        ** The Simple Skyscraper rewards 3 micro-converters.
        ** The Lightning rewards 3 micro-converters.
        ** The Grey Diamond Logo rewards 3 micro-converters.
        ** The Magnet rewards 3 micro-converters.
        ** The Spark Staff rewards 3 micro-converters.
