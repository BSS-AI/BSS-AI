{"text": "''<small>Are you looking for [[Pollen]] or [[Leaves]]?</small>''

        '''Flowers''' can be found in all [[fields]]. They can be interacted with using different
        sources to obtain [[pollen]] from and later be converted into [[honey]]. There are 5 flower
        sizes: single, double, triple, large, and star. Single, double, triple, large, and star
        flowers multiply pollen by 1, 2, 3, 4, and 5 per harvest, respectively. This number can be
        increased with better [[tools]], [[badges]], [[Ability Tokens|ability tokens]], [[Field
        Booster|field boosters]], etc. They also have base total pollen amounts of 30, 45, 60, 75,
        and 90 respectively. Total pollen is how much pollen the player can get before the flower's
        pollen is depleted and the flower has to regrow. In addition, larger flowers grow at a
        higher rate than smaller ones. [[Sprinklers]], [[Clouds]] and [[Passive Abilities#Gathering
        Bubbles|Bubbles]] can regenerate flowers while the [[Ability Tokens#Pollen Haze|pollen
        haze]] [[Ability Tokens|ability]] improves flower growth rate.

        There are 3 different flower colors: [[:Category:Colorless|white]], [[:Category:Red|red]],
        and [[:Category:Blue|blue]]. [[Bees]] also have these colors, and red/blue bees that harvest
        flowers of the same color get 50% bonus pollen from it. Also, the color of the pollen plays
        a large role in many [[quests]] and bee abilities.

        Sometimes, a flower patch might emit green [[leaves]]. Collecting pollen from that patch
        will either grant an [[Items|item]] or spawn an [[aphid]].

        == Trivia ==

        * There are decals of yellow flowers in [[Onett (Developer)|Onett's]] inventory, but they
        have never made an appearance in the game itself. They were most likely scrapped during the
        game's development.
        * White flowers are the most common in the game, as practically all fields have white
        flowers. The only field that doesn't have white flowers is the [[Mountain Top Field]].
        ** The only field with exactly one color of flowers is the [[Spider Field]], the color being
        white.
        * The [[Ant Field]], [[Stump Field]], Mountain Top Field, [[Coconut Field]], and [[Pepper
        Patch]] are the only fields with exactly one type of flower size (single for Ant Field,
        triple for the rest).
        * Star Flowers glow during [[Day/Night Cycle|nighttime]].
        * Ant Field flowers hold 50 pollen, plus 10 pollen per tier (up to 90 pollen).
        * Before the [[Updates#2020.04.06|4/6/2020 update]], the flower designs were different, and
        the sizes were only small, double, and large.
        *If the player looks at a field from a nearly flat angle, the flowers will appear to turn to
        their corresponding colors.
        *[[Fuzzy Bee|Fuzzy Bee's]] abilities and [[Passive Abilities#Fuzzy Coat|passive ability]],
        the [[Passive Abilities#Diamond Drain|Diamond Drain]] passive, the [[Passive
        Abilities#Guiding Star|Guiding Star]] passive, and a bee with the [[Poinsettia]] or [[Lei]]
        beequip are the only ways to pollinate flowers.
