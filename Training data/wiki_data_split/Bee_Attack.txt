        '''Bee Attack''' is a [[Mechanics|mechanic]] that causes [[bees]] to attack [[mobs]] in a
        radius. When attacking, bees will deploy a small red laser (presumably a stinger) to deal
        damage based on the amount of [[System Page#Bee Attack|bee attack power]] they possess. Bee
        attack boosts are split into five categories, regular bee attack, colorless bee attack, red
        bee attack, blue bee attack, and bee attack multiplier.

        Regular bee attack, or known as just 'bee attack,' improves bee attack to all bees. [[System
        Page#Colorless Attack|Colorless]], [[System Page#Red Attack|red]], and [[System Page#Blue
        Attack|blue]] bee attack improves bee attack to matching bees. [[System Page#Attack
        Multiplier|Bee attack multiplier]] improves bee attack to all bees by multiplication after
        the sum-up of other bee attack statistics. Base bee attack refers to the total attack of one
        bee before any multipliers but after add additive bonuses. For example a [[Lion Bee]] with
        10 bee attack with items that give +3 bee attack and +1 colorless bee attack has a base bee
        attack of 14.

        The starting bee attack of all bees ranges from 0 to 10, although attack can be increased
        beyond that. The bee with the highest starting bee attack is Lion Bee with 10 attack, and
        the bee with the lowest starting bee attack is [[Baby Bee]] with 0 attack, however even if
        Baby Bee's attack is raised, it will still be unable to attack mobs.

        For an increased bee attack stat, first the additives (+ bee attack and + colourless, blue
        or red bee attack) is added and then, the Bee attack multiplier is added. For example, for a
        gifted [[Brave Bee]] of a player wearing the [[Porcelain Port-O-Hive]] with no amulets,
        guards and belt, since the base attack of a Brave Bee is 5, the +2 bee attack from the said
        bee’s Gifted Hive Bonus and Porcelain Port-O-Hive are first added, making the bee attack
        till now 7. With the x1.5 bee attack of the Gifted buff, its bee attack is 10.5. Thus, the
        Brave Bee’s attack stat will be displayed as 10.5 in the stats menu of the said bee.

        == Improving Bee Attack ==
        Bee attack can be improved in many ways, every type of bee attack can be improved depending
        on the player's regular bee attack, colorless bee attack, red bee attack, blue bee attack,
        and bee attack multiplier.

        === Regular Bee Attack ===
        * Collecting a [[Ability Tokens#Rage|rage]] ability token grants a bonus +1 bee attack,
        stacking up to 4 times, for a maximum of +4 bee attack.
        * [[Brave Bee|Gifted Brave Bee]] grants a bonus +1 bee attack.
        * [[King Beetle Amulet]], [[Ant Amulet]], and [[Stick Bug Amulet]]s have a chance to grant
        +1 bee attack.
        * The [[Hiking Boots]] and [[Beekeeper's Boots]] both grant a bonus +1 bee attack.
        * The [[Coconut Clogs]] grant a bonus +2 bee attack.
        * The [[Gummy Boots]] grant a bonus +3 bee attack.

        === Colorless Bee Attack ===
        * The [[Mondo Belt Bag]], [[Honeycomb Belt]] and [[Petal Belt]] all grant a bonus +1
        colorless bee attack.
        * The [[Brave Guard]] grants a bonus +1 colorless bee attack.
        * The [[Cog Amulet]] (Gold+) has a chance to grant +1 or +2 colorless bee attack.

        === Red Bee Attack ===
        * The [[Red Guard]] and [[Elite Red Guard]] both grant a bonus +1 red bee attack, the
        [[Riley Guard]] grants +2 red bee attack, and the [[Crimson Guard]] grants +3 red bee
        attack.
        * The King Beetle Amulet has a chance to grant +1 red bee attack.
        * The Stick Bug Amulet has a chance to grant +1 or +2 red bee attack.
        * The Cog Amulet (Gold+) has a chance to grant +1 or +2 red bee attack.

        === Blue Bee Attack ===
        * The [[Blue Guard]] and [[Elite Blue Guard]] both grant a bonus +1 blue bee attack, the
        [[Bucko Guard]] grants +2 blue bee attack, and the [[Cobalt Guard]] grants a bonus +3 blue
        bee attack.
        * The King Beetle Amulet has a chance to grant +1 blue bee attack.
        * The Stick Bug Amulet has a chance to grant +1 or +2 blue bee attack.
        * The Cog Amulet (Gold+) has a chance to grant +1 or +2 blue bee attack.
        * Adding the Cop and Robber to the Sticker Stack grants +0.5 Blue Bee Attack.

        === Bee Attack Multiplier ===
        * [[Rage Bee|Gifted Rage Bee]] grants +10% bee attack multiplier.
        * Using a [[stinger]] grants x1.5 bee attack multiplier for 30 seconds.
        * [[Buffs & Debuffs#From Passives|Flame heat]] grants a bonus +5-25% bee attack
        multiplier depending on how high the stack is.
        * The [[Fire Mask]] grants a bonus +15% bee attack multiplier.
        * The [[Demon Mask]] grants a bonus +25% bee attack multiplier.
        * The Stick Bug Amulet has a chance to grant +1-10% bee attack depending on the tier of the
        amulet.
        * The Cog Amulet has a chance to grant +3-15% bee attack depending on the tier of the
        amulet.
        * Activating the [[Passive Abilities#Emergency Coconut Shield|Emergency Coconut Shield]]
        grants x1.25 bee attack multiplier for 10 seconds.
        * The Coconut Combo buff gives up to +50% bee attack at 100 stacks.
        * The [[Boost Market|Rose Field Market Boost]] grants +10% bee attack multiplier along with
        other buffs for 30 minutes. (unobtainable)
        * Invigorating Nectar grants a bonus of +1% to +10% bee attack multiplier.
        * Certain [[Beequip]]s. (Example: Icicles)
        * Adding the Foward Facing Aphid to the Sticker Stack grants +0.5% Bee Attack.
        * Adding the Walking Stick Nymph to the Sticker Stack grants +0.5% Bee Attack.
        * Adding the Theatrical Intruder to the Sticker Stack grants +0.5% Bee Attack.
        * Adding the Tough Potato to the Sticker Stack grants +0.5% Bee Attack
        * Adding the Festive Pea to the Sticker Stack grants +0.5% Bee Attack.
        * Adding the Cyan Hilted Sword to the Sticker Stack grants +1% Bee Attack.
        * Adding the Stick Cub Skin to the Sticker Stack grants +1% Bee Attack.
        * [[Buffs & Debuffs#From Other|Festive Nymph Blessing]] gives % bee attack, up to +5%.
        === Robo Bear Challenge Upgrades ===
        The [[Robo Bear Challenge]] provides increases or decreases Bee Attack in the form of adding
        or multiplying depending on which upgrade you purchase with Cogs. These buffs last until
        your Robo Bear Challenge ends, however they are only active when you are doing a round.

        * Common Upgrades:
        ** Sharpen: This upgrade starts at x1.2 Bee Attack and maxes at 10 upgrades with x3 Bee
        Attack.
        * Rare Upgrades:
        ** Blue Screen: This upgrade grants x1.03 Bee Attack and Blue Bee Attack for every blue
        boost stack you have, maxing out at x1.3 Bee Attack and Blue Bee Attack.
        ** Equalize: This upgrade grants +1 Bee Attack and x1.25 Ungifted Bee Attack.
        ** Saturate: This upgrade starts at x1.25 Blue and Colorless Bee Attack, but x0.8 Red Bee
        Attack, it maxes at 3 upgrades with x1.75 Blue and Colorless Bee Attack and x0.4 Red Bee
        Attack.
        ** SSD: White: This upgrade ''lowers'' Bee Attack, starting at x0.8 Bee Attack and maxes at
        3 upgrades with x0.6 Bee Attack.
        * Epic Upgrades:
        ** Fission: This upgrade grants +1 Bee Attack.
        ** Furnace: This upgrade does not improve Bee Attack, but it grants x1.5 Flame Damage.
        ** Inject: This upgrade grants +2 Blue Bee Attack, +1 Colorless Bee Attack, and x1.25 Impale
        Damage.
        ** Malware: This upgrade starts at +3 Bee Attack and maxes at 3 upgrades with +9 Bee Attack.
        ** NFT: This upgrade ''lowers'' Bee Attack, it grants -3 Bee Attack.
        ** Pop-Up: This upgrade grants +2 Blue Bee Attack.
        * Legendary Upgrades:
        ** Bluetooth: This upgrade gives x2 Blue Bee Attack.
        ** Codec: This upgrade ''lowers'' Bee Attack, it grants x0.8 Bee Attack.

        === Others ===
        These ways are to improve certain bees' attacks which don't count up to regular bee attack,
        colorless bee attack, red bee attack, blue bee attack, nor bee attack multiplier.
        * [[Critical Hits|Critical hits]] increase the damage outcome of bees by the amount of
        [[System Page#Critical Power|Critical Power]]. (minimum of 100% more damage)
        ** Super-crits increase the damage outcome of critical hits by the amount of Super-Crit
        Power, but it is only 33% as powerful as a pollen super-crit.
        * Every gifted bee gains x1.5 bee attack for themselves.
        * Certain [[mutation]]s increase the individual bee's attack by 1 or a multiplier (%).
        * Activating [[Passive Abilities#Gummy Morph|Gummy Morph]] grants +300 bonus bee attack only
        to [[Gummy Bee]], along with other buffs.
        * Adding the Flying Brave Bee to the Sticker Stack grants +2 Rare Bee Attack.
        * Adding the Coiled Snake to the Sticker Stack grants +1 Epic Bee Attack.
        * Adding the Evil Pig to the Sticker Stack grants +1 Epic Bee Attack.
        * Adding the Left Facing Ant to the Sticker Stack grants +1 Legendary Bee Attack.
