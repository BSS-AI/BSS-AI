        {{Color|Glitter}} is a craftable [[:Category:Inventory|inventory item]] that was added in
        the [[Updates#2018.11.25|11/25/2018 update]]. It grants a +100% [[pollen]] boost in the
        [[field]] the player is standing in for 15 minutes upon activation. If used in a field with
        a [[Planter]], the planter gains a 25% growth rate boost until it is harvested.
        '''Glitter''' has a 15 minute and 5 second cooldown between uses. This is to prevent the
        user from stacking the item's buff for one field.

        It can be used in all fields except the [[Ant Field]]. Attempting to do so will result in
        the following message: <span style=\"background-color:red; color:white;\">\"You can't
        boost a challenge field.\"</span>

        The following audio plays when glitter is used to boost a field: [[File:Glitteruse.ogg]]

        == Ways to Obtain ==
        * It can be crafted in the [[Blender]] for {{Cost|Moon Charm|25}} and {{Cost|Magic Bean|1}}.
        * Redeeming certain valid [[Codes]]. There are currently no valid codes that give glitter.
        * As a reward from donating to the [[Wind Shrine]].
        * Playing any type of [[Memory Match]] (increments of 1 or 5)
        * As a drop from [[Mobs]], such as the [[Aphid#Diamond Aphid|diamond aphid]], [[Werewolf]],
        [[Mantis]], [[Scorpion]], [[Stump Snail]], [[Tunnel Bear]], [[King Beetle]], [[Wild Windy
        Bee]] and [[Coconut Crab]].
        * All [[sprout]]s (excluding the Debug Sprout) can generate a glitter token (possibilities
        differ depending on the type of sprout);(especially in Moon [[Sprout]]).
        * Can be generated in place of a [[Moon Charm|moon charm]] by [[Fireflies]] during
        [[Day/Night Cycle|Nighttime]].
        * The [[Dandelion Field]], [[Spider Field]] and the [[Pineapple Patch]] have a small
        possibility of generating a glitter token while harvesting.
        * As a rare drop from [[Leaves]], [[Sparkles]], [[Mythic Meteor Shower|Meteors]], and
        [[Ability Tokens#Festive Gift|Festive Gifts]] ([[Gifted Bee|Gifted]] exclusive drop).
        * As a drop from the {{Cost|Candy Planter}}, {{Cost|Tacky Planter}}, {{Cost|Festive
        Planter}}, {{Cost|Pesticide Planter}} and {{Cost|Petal Planter}}.
        * As a rare drop from [[Puffshroom]]s.
        * As a Stack Reward for adding certain [[Stickers]] to the [[Sticker Stack]]:
        ** Sideways Spirit Bear rewards 1 glitter.
        ** Shining Halo rewards 1 glitter.
        ** Glowing Smile rewards 1 glitter.
        ** Every Star Sign sticker rewards 1 glitter.

        === Quests ===
        * [[Black Bear]] gives glitter on the following quests:
        **Pretty Pumpkins gives 1 glitter and other items.
        ** All-Whitey Then gives 1 glitter and other items.
        ** Black Bear can occasionally give glitter after completing a repeatable quest.
        * [[Science Bear]] gives glitter on the following quests:
        ** Smart, Not Hard gives 1 glitter and other items.
        ** Meditating On Phenomenon gives 1 glitter and other items.
        ** Hive Minded Bias gives 3 glitter and other items.
        ** Epistemological Endeavor gives 4 glitter and other items. Before its nerf, the quest gave
        5 glitter.
        * [[Dapper Bear]] gives glitter on the following quests:
        ** Unintended Intimidation gives 2 glitter and other items.
        ** The Trick To Tickets gives 1 glitter and other items.
        ** Ongoing Maintenance gives 5 glitter and other items.
        * All of [[Spirit Bear|Spirit Bear's]] quests except for her 10th and 17th reward glitter.
        * [[Polar Bear]] has a small chance of rewarding glitter after one of his quests.

        ==Trivia ==
        *Using it with a planter will make the planter grow 25% as fast.

        *One of [[Bee Bear|Bee Bear's]] quests required the player to use one glitter.

        *This is one of four consumable items to grant a [[Field Boost]], the others being [[Field
        Dice]], [[Smooth Dice]], and [[Loaded Dice]]
        ** It also is the one of the four consumables that shows glitter particles on the field when
        the said field is boosted.
        **This is the only consumable to grant a Field Boost of the player's choice.
        *Spirit Bear mentions in one of her quest dialogues, Spirit's Starter, that glitter was once
        so rare that 1 pouch of it costed 5 million honey.
