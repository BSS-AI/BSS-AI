        ==Tips/Strategies ==
        * Having bees oriented toward fighting, such as [[Rage Bee]], [[Demon Bee]], [[Lion Bee]],
        and [[Vicious Bee]], will help defeat the Stump Snail faster.
        * Two additional [[Fire Bee]]s from [[Spicy Bee]]'s [[Ability Tokens#Inferno|Inferno]] and
        [[Frog]]s from [[Tadpole Bee]]'s [[Ability Tokens#Summon Frog|Summon Frog]] may be summoned
        to deal further damage.
        * [[Precise Bee]] can deal more damage and provides [[System Page#Critical Chance|Critical]]
        and [[Critical Hits#Super-Crit Chance|Super-Crit chance]] to increase bee damage.
        * The player should wear equipment that boosts the bee attack, notably the [[Fire Mask|Fire
        mask]] or [[Demon Mask|Demon mask]].
        ** If the player has the [[Gummy Mask|Gummy mask]], they can intentionally trigger [[Passive
        Abilities#Gummy Morph|Gummy Morph]] to boost [[Gummy Bee]]'s attack for 10 seconds.
        * A player with the [[Coconut Canister]] can intentionally trigger the [[Passive
        Abilities#Emergency Coconut Shield|Emergency Coconut Shield]] to receive a short attack
        boost.
        * When AFK, a [[Cub Buddy|cub buddy]] is capable of collecting tokens the player would
        otherwise miss.
        * [[Stinger]]s greatly boost attack, [[Oil]] increases [[Stats#Speed|Bee Movespeed]], and
        [[Tropical Drink]]s increase critical chance.
        * During the [[:Category:Beesmas|Beesmas 2020]], [[:Category:Beesmas|2021]], and
        [[:Category:Beesmas|2022]] events, the summoned bees from [[Onett's Lid Art]], [[Honeyday
        Candles]], and [[Gummy Siege]] could be used to attack the Stump Snail.

        ==Trivia ==
        * It is one of two [[mob]]s where the damage dealt to it is saved over multiple sessions,
        the other being [[Chicks#Commando Chick|Commando Chick]].
        * Stump Snail, [[King Beetle]], and [[Stick Bug]] are the only mobs that can drop
        [[amulet]]s.
        ** Stump Snail is the only mob that is guaranteed to drop an amulet.
        * This is the only mob that guarantees to drop [[glue]] upon defeat.
        * As of the [[Updates#2019.02.01|01/02/2019 update]], the Stump Snail, [[Rogue Vicious
        Bee]], King Beetle (only if it drops a [[King Beetle Amulet]]), and [[ants]] are the only
        mobs that do not drop tokens when defeat; instead the loot directly goes to the player's
        inventory.
        ** Even though the Stump Snail does not drop tokens, the total amount of honey rewarded is
        still increased by [[System Page#Honey From Tokens|Honey From Tokens]].
        * [[Spirit Bear]] refers to the Stump Snail as \"Ol' Stumpy,\" which may be a reference to
        Youtuber [https://www.youtube.com/user/ThnxCya ThnxCya] as he is also known to refer to the
        Stump Snail as \"Ol' Stumpy.\"
        * It is possible to damage other players' snails with [[Passive Abilities#Gathering
        Flames|Flames]].
        * Stump Snail originally had 25,000,000 health prior to the [[Updates#2019.04.05|05/04/2019
        update]]. The change was due to most bees' attacks being buffed in said update.
        * Stump Snail has the most health and the longest respawn time among all mobs.
        * Previously, there was a bug where the Stump Snail would spawn at a lower position than
        usual, causing it to bob up before falling under the field.
        ** On rare occasions, it would fall significantly beneath the map beyond the player's bees'
        attack range. During such occurrences, bees would be unable to focus on it, flying up and
        down and rendering its defeat unrealistic.
        ** This has since been patched.
        * This is one of the three hostile mobs that require multiple days to respawn, the others
        being [[Tunnel Bear]] and [[Coconut Crab]].
        * When a player leaves the field, the snail returns to its cave without a specific target.
        In this state, it can damage any player that comes into contact with it.
