        The '''Tallest Sticker Stack''' is one of 62 [[leaderboards]] in the game. This leaderboard
        shows the amount of [[stickers]] players have donated to the [[Sticker Stack]]. It shows 10
        leaderboard positions at a time. The player can scroll down the leaderboard to see more
        players, all the way to the 100th place.

        == Location ==
        It is located past the [[Honey Bee Gate]] and behind the [[Pine Tree Forest]].

        == Achievements ==

        * On 01/12/2024, XSxriptX was the first player to earn the Sticker Stack Cadet Badge (25
        Stickers in Stack).
        * On 01/13/2024, mateusz8992 was the first player to earn the Sticker Stack Hotshot badge
        (50 Stickers in Stack).
        * On 01/14/2024, warnadowind was the first player to earn the Sticker Stack Ace Badge (100
        Stickers in Stack).
        * On 01/15/2024, warnadowind was the first player to add 150 stickers to the stack.
        * On 01/17/2024, NicolaiJee was the first player to add 200 stickers to the stack.
        * On 01/24/2024, NicolaiJee was the first player to earn the Sticker Stack Master Badge (250
        Stickers in Stack).
        * On 01/25/2024, NicolaiJee was the first player to add all 264 non-event exclusive stickers
        to the stack as of the [[Updates|01/12/2024 update]].

        == Trivia ==
        *The Tallest Sticker Stack is the only leaderboard in the game to have a maximum amount,
        being 264, meaning that the leaderboard will eventually become impossible to get on to.
