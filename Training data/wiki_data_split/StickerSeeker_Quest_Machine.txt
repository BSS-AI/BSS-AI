        The '''Sticker-Seeker Quest Machine''' is a [[Quest Givers|quest giver]] in the [[Hive
        Hub]]. To interact with it, the player must own and have the [[Sticker-Seeker]] equipped.
        The player must also have a total of 15 bees hatched to interact with the machine. It has a
        total of 500 quests.

        [[File:StickerQuest Giver.png|thumb|209x209px|The Sticker Quest Giver and the Sticker-Seeker
        leaderboard.]]

        ==Ranks ==
        A player's rank is the amount of Sticker-Seeker Quest Machine's quests they have completed.
        Ranks are also the way the [[Top Sticker-Seekers]] leaderboard is measured, and increases
        every time a player completes a quest. The higher the rank, the better the rewards. For
        example, a player is more likely to get Sticker Planters when they have achieved further
        ranks than players with a Rank of 5. Quest requirement amounts also scale to the ranks.
        ==Rewards==
        Every quest reward always includes [[Honey]], a [[Ticket]], [[Royal Jelly|Royal Jellies]],
        and a random [[Sticker]], as well as many other possible rewards, including [[Sticker
        Planter]]s or [[Balloon]]s. Both are determined by the Rank, or number of quests the player
        has completed. Every once in a while, the player reaches a Rank milestone, which rewards an
        [[Egg]], like a [[Egg#Silver Egg|Silver Egg]] at the 25th Rank completion.
