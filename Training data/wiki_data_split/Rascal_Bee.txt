        * Its blue counterpart is [[Bumble Bee]].

        * Rascal Bee is one of [[Onett (Developer)|Onett's]] favorite [[Bees|bee]] types because he

        different face.
        * In Rascal Bee's description, \"misch'''i'''evous\" is written as \"mischev'''i'''ous\", a
        common misspelling of the word.
        * Defeated [[ladybug]]s will sometimes drop [[Egg#Specific Bee Eggs|Rascal Bee eggs]].
        * Rascal Bee is the only Rare bee to have an attack of 3.
        * Rascal Bee has the second highest attack of all Rare bees, only behind [[Brave Bee]].
