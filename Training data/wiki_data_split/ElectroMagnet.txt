        ==Trivia==
        *This tool is the upgraded version of the [[Magnet]]. Their appearances are similar and they
        are the only tools to collect pollen in a square pattern. However, the two have their
        differences. The Electro-Magnet is yellow and white in color, and it has electricity running
        on its tips, while the Magnet is red and gray, without electricity. It collects faster and
        also has four tips, whereas the Magnet only has two.
        *The Electro-Magnet, [[Clippers]], [[Golden Rake]], [[Spark Staff]], [[Petal Wand]], [[Dark
        Scythe]], [[Tide Popper]] and the [[Gummyballer]] are the only tools added in an update.
        *The Electro-Magnet's associated sticker is strangely missing its handle.
