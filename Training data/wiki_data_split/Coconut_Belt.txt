        ==Stats ==
        *+350,000 Capacity
        *+75% Capacity
        *+120 Convert Amount
        *+102% Loot Luck
        *+100% Honey From Tokens
        *x1.1 Coconut Field Pollen
        *+10% Gifted Bee Pollen
        *+1 Bee Attack
        *+Passive: Petal Storm (Needs [[Petal Belt]])
        *+Passive: Combo Coconuts
        **Every 40th Coconut you drop onto a field (through items or abilities) becomes a Combo
        Coconut. When caught by any player. Combo Coconuts are kicked and fall again, onto your own
        field if caught on the first drop within the blue circle, or onto another players field.
        Every time they are kicked, they speed up, and gain stacks of Coconut Combo. Many more
        stacks are granted if caught by other players in fields with different colors. Catching the
        Coconut grants a buff that grants between 10% to 75% Unique Instant Conversion, between x1.1
        to x1.5 Pollen and between +1% to +50% Pollen, Red Pollen, Blue Pollen, White Pollen, Bee
        Attack, and Honey From Tokens. When the Coconut fails to be caught it despawns and reapplies
        the buff to all players who caught it at least once.
