        The '''Gummyballer''' is a [[Tools|tool]] that can be crafted in [[Gummy Bear's Lair]].

        It collects 8 [[pollen]] from 29 patches in 1 second and boosts it by x2 on red and blue
        flowers, or x3 on white flowers. It has an average base pollen collection rate of 541.33
        pollen per second: 696 from white flowers and 464 from red and blue flowers.

        Every swing absorbs any collected [[goo]] and removes it from the flower. In return, it
        gives 12 seconds of \"Gummyball\" buff with a max of 1000 stacks. The amount of Gummyball
        stacks per collected goo patch is proportional to how fresh the goo is (the more faded the
        goo is, the less it gives stack to the Gummyball). The more Gummyball stacks collected, the
        bigger the ball on the collector becomes, along with a stronger aura around it and up to a
        1.1x white pollen boost.

        Upon reaching the max stack of Gummyball (1000) or swinging with the collector above the
        ground (by jumping) while being inside a field, a Gummyball will launch forward from the
        player's faced direction and bounce around the border of the field. It leaves a trail of goo
        behind and collects pollen from the flowers it hits (collection and goo spread depends on
        the size of the Gummyball). It will also grab any ability or honey token and pop bubbles
        along the way. The larger the initial Gummyball stacks when it's launched, the larger the
        Gummyball will start as and the longer the Gummyball will last.

        The Gummyball rewards the player with \"Gummyball Combo\", giving 2 stacks for hitting a wall,
        1 per [[Ability Tokens|ability token]], 3 per honey token, 8 per [[Ability
        Tokens#mark|mark]] and 30 per precise mark for 8 seconds. Gummyball combo has a max stack of
        1000 and always provides a 1.1x white pollen boost and up to a 11x goo boost depending on
        the stack of Gummyball combo.

        ==Gummyball Combo Strategies==
        *Choose a narrow field, and align your character perpendicular to the field border that
        you're facing by using the \"<\" and \">\" keys with shift lock (rotates the camera by 45
        degrees) If on mobile, go in straight vertical lines across the field. When aligned, launch
        the Gummyball at a place with lots of active marks, including the Honey Mark(+), Pollen
        Mark(+), Festive Mark, and Precise Mark. This will cause the ball to go back and forth and
        hit the mark(s) and other things that give stacks along the way without the Gummyball going
        to the left/right.
        *Time the Gummyball before [[Passive Abilities#Gummy Morph|Gummy Morph]] activates (at
        around 21/30 if gumdrops are being dropped), so it has the time to stack up before the Gummy
        Morph activates.

        ==Trivia==
        *The Gummyballer is the second most expensive item in the game in terms of honey, only
        behind [[The Planter Of Plenty]] which costs {{Hover|100T|100,000,000,000,000}} honey.
        *Its red counterpart is the [[Dark Scythe]], and its blue counterpart is the [[Tide
        Popper]]. However, this tool is more expensive than its counterparts. This is likely because
        the Dark Scythe and Tide Popper require 250 quests to be finished from its corresponding
        shop owner, while the Gummyballer does not.
        *The Gummyballer is tied with the [[Pulsar]] for the slowest tool in the game (in terms of
        how long the cooldown of each collection lasts).
        *On December 28, 2021, the Gummyballer was obtainable through an exploit that gave the
        Gummyballer for free, along with the Tide Popper and Dark Scythe.
        **Onett later patched this, and everybody who used this exploit had their data reset on
        their account.
        * If the ingredients for the item were to be crafted, the total cost would be
        {{Hover|10T|10,000,000,000,000}} honey, 412,500 [[Blueberry|blueberries]], 412,500
        [[Strawberry|strawberries]], 902,500 [[Pineapple|pineapples]], 135,000 [[Sunflower
        Seed|sunflower seeds]], 812,700 [[Royal Jelly|royal jellies]], 16,500
        [[Bitterberry|bitterberries]], 3,700 [[Neonberry|neonberries]], 6,000 [[coconut]]s, 2,400
        [[Magic Bean|magic beans]], 12,500 [[honeysuckle]]s.
        **Behind The Planter Of Plenty, this requires the most base resources to craft.
        * The Gummyballer is the only item to be added to Gummy Bear's Lair in an update after the
        lair came out.
        *This is the only tool that undergoes visual changes with its ability.
        * This is the only tool that has the ability to take goo patches from flowers.
        *The Gummyball on the tool moves similarly to amulets, meaning it drags behind the
        collector's actual movement.
        **On occasion, the Gummyball will appear to be dislocated from the collector, this occurs if
        either the server or client ping (response delay) is at high levels.
        *The Gummyballer, along with The Planter Of Plenty, The Hydroponic Planter, and the
        Heat-Treated Planter are currently the only four items that require turpentine to craft,
        needing 5, 25, 1, and 1 respectively.
