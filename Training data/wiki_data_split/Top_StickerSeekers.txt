        The '''Top Sticker-Seekers''' was added in the 01/13/2024 [[Updates|update]] and is one of
        62 [[leaderboards]] in the game. This leaderboard shows how much [[Sticker-Seeker Quest
        Machine|Sticker-Seeker Quests]] players have completed. It shows 10 leaderboard positions at
        a time. The player can scroll down the leaderboard to see more players, all the way to the
        100th place.

        == Location ==
        It is located inside of the [[Hive Hub]] next to the [[Hub Field Shop]].

        == Achievements ==

        * XSxriptX was the first player to reach 25 Sticker-Seeker quests completed.
        * NoahvsZombie was the first player to reach 50 Sticker-Seeker quests completed.
        * MyloDaBoss was the first player to reach 75 Sticker-Seeker quests completed.
        * piterozero20001 was the first player to reach 100 Sticker-Seeker quests completed.
        * TorchyGem was the first player to reach 500 Sticker-Seeker quests completed.

        == Trivia ==

        * This is the only leaderboard located inside of the Hive Hub.
