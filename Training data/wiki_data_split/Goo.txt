        '''Goo''' is a purple-to-teal substance that gives the player bonus [[honey]] when spread
        across [[flowers]]. It is required for all of [[Onett|Onett’s]] and [[Spirit Bear|Spirit
        Bear's]] [[quests]] and was required for all of [[Gummy Bear|Gummy Bear's]] quests, as well
        as for some [[Gifted Riley Bee]] and [[Gifted Bucko Bee]] quests and some of [[Science
        Bear|Science Bear's]] quests. Using [[gumdrops]] is the easiest and most common way to
        manually spread goo until one gets the [[Gummy Boots]]. To collect goo, collect pollen as
        usual (with [[tools]], [[bees]], and/or their [[Ability Tokens|ability tokens]]) from
        flowers that are covered in goo.

        The following formula shows how much bonus honey a player gets from goo. Let ''p'' equal the
        amount of pollen collected from a puddle of goo, and ''f'' the amount of flowers inside the
        puddle.

        <math>p\sqrt{\frac{f}{200}}</math>

        This means that the larger the goo puddle, the more bonus honey the player gets. For
        example, collecting 500,000 pollen from a goo puddle of 80 flowers would grant roughly
        316,228 bonus honey.

        Unlike ability tokens, goo can be freely shared with other players. Collecting goo depletes
        the underlying flowers, but does not actually \"use up\" the goo itself, unless you collect
        goo with the [[Gummyballer]]. Goo puddles disappear on their own over a short period of
        time.

        ==Trivia==

        *Gummy Bear's [[Gummy Cannon]] used to shoot [[gumdrop]]s at all [[fields]] except the
        [[Mountain Top Field]] and the fields added after the event (which are [[Stump Field]],
        [[Coconut Field]], [[Pepper Patch]], and the [[Ant Field]]). When the gumdrops landed, they
        turned into goo. Gummy Bear used to continually cycle through all 13 fields excluding the
        [[Mountain Top Field]] in a random order. When he targeted a field, the server used to
        announce it in the bottom right corner as well as in the chat window.
        ** The Gummy Cannon didn’t always shoot in the center of fields. Sometimes, the gumdrops
        landed outside of the field, making them vanish and sometimes going to the surrounding
        fields.
        *If a gumdrop falls outside of a field and does not land on a flower, it disappears.
        *[[Gummy Bee|Gummy Bee's]] [[Ability Tokens#Glob|glob]] and [[Ability Tokens#Gumdrop
        Barrage|gumdrop barrage]] abilities both spread goo in fields.
        *Activating [[Gummy Mask|Gummy Mask’s]] [[Passive Abilities#Gummy Morph|Gummy Morph passive
        ability]] covers the entire field the wearer is in with goo.
