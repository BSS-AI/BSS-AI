        A '''Snow Storm''' was a server-wide event that could be summoned by using the [[Snow
        Machine]] or randomly in the game. It was similar to a [[Honeystorm]], but it spawned
        snowflakes instead. A snow storm would last for 20 seconds.

        When a player used the Snow Machine to summon a snow storm, a server-wide announcement would
        state: \"<span style=\"background-color:#FFFFFF; color:black;\">{Username} has activated
        the Snow Machine!</span>\" and [[Snowflake]]s would start to fall rapidly from the sky
        on every field. There would be no audio at the beginning of a snow storm, unlike a
        honeystorm or [[Mythic Meteor Shower|mythic meteor shower]].

        When a snow storm was randomly summoned, the message would state: \"<span
        style=\"background-color:#FFFFFF; color:black;\">A Snow Storm has begun...</span>\".

        ==Trivia ==
        *Occasionally, [[Onett (Developer)|Onett]] would spawn a snow storm, usually under an alias.
        This is similar to how he globally summons mythic meteor showers, [[Day/Night
        Cycle#Night|nighttime]], special [[sprout]]s, and [[mobs]].
        *Like honeystorms and mythic meteor showers, snow storms can light up the [[Werewolf's
        Cave]] enough to see the [[Cave Monster]]s and the Gold Egg at the end of the cave.
        **Unlike honeystorms, snow storms don't spawn snowflake tokens in the [[Ant Challenge|Ant
        Field]].
        * During Beesmas 2020 and 2021, giving [[Spirit Bear]] a [[Present]] would trigger a snow
        storm after the dialogue when the [[Ornaments|ornament]] was given.
        **Finishing Spirit Bear’s Beesmas quest would also summon a snow storm.
        * The snowflakes keep falling for a few seconds after a snow storm ends because it takes
        time for the snowflakes to reach the ground.
        *If a snow storm is summoned during [[Day/Night Cycle#Night|Nighttime]], the white fog will
        stay even after the snow storm has ended until nighttime ends.
