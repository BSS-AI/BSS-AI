        A '''Robo Pass''' is an [[:Category:Inventory|inventory item]] that was added in the
        [[Updates|12/26/2022 update]]. The player must use one in order to participate in the [[Robo
        Bear Challenge]]. Players can only have a maximum of 10 Robo Passes in their inventory at a
        time (unless they purchased them from [[Robux Shop|Robux packs]] or received them from
        quests).

        A robo pass can be used by talking to Robo Bear and initiating Round 1 of the Robo Bear
        Challenge.

        ==Methods of Obtaining==
        *There is a [[Free Robo Pass Dispenser]] past the [[Bear Gate]] which can be used every 22
        hours.
        *There is also a [[Robo Pass Dispenser]] to the right of the Free Robo Pass Dispenser which
        gives a Robo Pass for {{Cost|Ticket|50}} with no cooldown.
        *As a rare drop from [[Rhino Beetle]]s, [[Ladybug]]s, [[Spider]]s, [[Scorpion]]s, [[Coconut
        Crab]], [[Tunnel Bear]], [[King Beetle]], or [[Aphid#Armored Aphids|Armored Aphid]]s.
        *There is a Robo Pass token hidden behind a rock in the [[Coconut Cave]].
        *There is a Robo Pass token on the green cog near the [[Star Hall]].
        *There is a Robo Pass token hidden inside [[The Computer]].
        *As a possible reward from the [[Ant Challenge]].
        *As a possible drop from [[Puffshroom]]s.

        ===These methods below are no longer possible===
        *There was a Robo Pass token hidden behind the Robo Pass Dispensers. It was replaced by a
        Broken Drive token after Beesmas 2022 ended.
        *There was a Robo Pass token hidden inside the [[Beesmas Tree]].
        *Defeating Level 4, 11, and 18 [[Snowbear]]s.
        *The Digital Bee pack gave 10 Robo Passes + other items.
        *The Mondo Robo Bundle gave 25 Robo Passes + other items.
        *The Robotic Bundle in Bee Bears Catalog gave 10 Robo Passes, along with other items.
        *From certain quests:
        **Onett's Beesmas 2022 quest gives 3 Robo Passes.
        **Riley Bee's Beesmas 2022 quest gives 1 Robo Pass.
        **Bucko Bee's Beesmas 2022 quest gives 1 Robo Pass.
        **Stick Bug's Beesmas 2022 quest gives 1 Robo Pass.
        *As a reward from certain [[Gift Boxes]]:
        **The Charcoal Gift Box gave 1 Robo Pass.
        **The Obnoxious Gift Box gave 1 Robo Pass.
        **The Tropical Gift Box gave 1 Robo Pass.
        *From giving [[Present]]s in the Beesmas 2022 Event:
        **Giving [[Stick Bug]] a Present gave 1 Robo Pass.
        **Giving [[Onett]] a Present gave 1 Robo Pass.
        **Giving [[Robo Bear]] a Present gave 1 Robo Pass.
        **Giving [[Gummy Bear]] a Present gave 1 Robo Pass.

        *The Robo Pass took the longest time to get implemented out of all the items in the game,
        taking over 3 years after it was initially leaked on April 4, 2019.

        appear when attempting to select an item to donate.
