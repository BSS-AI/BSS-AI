        '''Trading''' is a mechanic added in the [[Updates|1/12/2024 update]], which allows players
        to trade items. Only non-permanent [[Beequip]]s, [[Cub Buddy|Cub Buddy Skins]] and
        [[Sticker]]s can be traded. The trade request menu can either be opened by going to [[System
        Page|settings]] or walking over a player's hive and accepting the prompt for a trade
        request. You can add or remove an item by interacting with the button in the top left corner
        of the item. When ready, both players select the accept button before waiting 5 seconds.
        Once the 5 seconds are over, the trade menu closes, and both players receive their items and
        get taken to the items' stats.

        Trading can be done an unlimited number of times to an unlimited number of players. But you
        cannot trade with multiple players at a time. A player has to accept or decline a trade
        request before being able to get a trade request from the same player again. You can also
        change whether you can receive trade requests by going into [[System Page|settings]] and
        finding \"Allow trade requests\". A player can be ineligible to receive trade requests for
        either not having unlocked trading yet or having their trade requests disabled. The
        requirements for unlocking trading are:

        *20 [[Bees]]
        *[https://bee-swarm-simulator.fandom.com/wiki/Badges#Playtime_Badges Playtime Cadet Badge]
        (8 hours of playtime)
        If a player is unable to receive trading requests, one of two messages will appear depending
        on which reason it is. If they don't have trading unlocked, then: \"[{Player} has not
        unlocked trading (20 Bees and Playtime Cadet Badge)]\" else if it's because they have trading
        requests disabled: \"[{Player} has disabled trading]\". If the player decides to cancel a
        trade, both players keep their items, and the trade menu closes before a popup appears
        telling both players that the trade has been cancelled.

        == Tips ==
        *'''Beware of scams involving trading and trust trades.''' Make sure that the items traded
        are of similar value before accepting the trade.
        *'''Make fair and cancel unfair trades.''' If you make an unfair trade, or if the other
        player makes an unfair trade, it is likely that the trade will be cancelled, and you should
        cancel an unfair trade.
        *'''Use the item inspector.''' You should use the item inspector to your advantage to make
        sure the item's balanced (if you are also a collector) also take the item's rarity into
        consideration. The item inspector is a must in ensuring that Beequips have the stats that
        you are looking for.
