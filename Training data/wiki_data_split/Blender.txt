{"text": "[[File:Blender.Png|thumb|217x217px|The blender in the Badge Bearer's Guild.]]
        The '''Blender''' is a [[:Category:Machines|machine]] located in the [[Badge Bearer's
        Guild]]. The machine's usage is to create [[:Category:Consumables|consumable]] [[items]]
        from pre-existing items. However, the player is only able to craft one type of item at a
        time. There is a total of 20 different items that the machine can craft.

        == Can Be Crafted ==
        [[File:Blendermenu.png|thumb|The Blender interface when crafting an item.]]
        Each item takes 5 minutes to craft and the timer only counts down when the player is
        in-game. The player can end the crafting process at any time by clicking on the \"End
        Crafting\" button. This will give the player all finished items, and all unfinished items
        will be turned back into their ingredients and given back to the player. If the button is
        green, then everything has been blended. If not (red button), then the Blender has not
        finished blending. The player can also speed up the blending for a varying amount of
        {{Cost|Ticket}}. For every 1 item being crafted, the Blender requires 1 more ticket to speed
        up. Except for {{Cost|Moon Charm}} and {{Cost|Gumdrops}}, for which each ticket serves for
        speeding up whole 10 items.

        == Trivia ==
        * There is a {{Cost|Star Jelly|||0}} token on top of the lid of the Blender. It can be
        obtained by repeatedly jumping on the handle with enough [[System Page#Jump Power|Jump
        Power]] or by standing on the gate to the Ace Badge section and gliding towards the top.
        * If you select to craft something but not actually ‘blend’ it, when you leave the blender
        and open it up again, it would show that you would select the number of red extracts you
        want to craft.
        * During the start of the Beesmas 2022 you could complete \"obtain items\" quests by canceling
        the crafting, however this got fixed.
