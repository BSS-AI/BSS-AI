        The '''Honey Wreath''' is one of the Beesmas machines which could be unlocked after
        completing [[Black Bear]]'s Honey Wreath [[Quests|quest]].

        When used, it would convert all of the [[pollen]] in the player's bag into [[honey]],
        multiplied by the [[Honey Per Pollen]], scattering 8 honey tokens around them to collect.
        The amount of honey obtained could also be multiplied by [[System Page#Honey From
        Tokens|Honey From Tokens]]. It has a cooldown of 30 minutes. Using the Honey Wreath was
        required for a few of [[Bee Bear|Bee Bear's]] quests during his 2020/2021/2022 visits, and
        it was also required for [[Honey Bee (NPC)|Honey Bee's]] Beesmas Quest.

        If the player attempted to use the Honey Wreath without any pollen in their bag, it would
        still scatter 8 honey tokens. Each one gives a base of 100 honey, totaling to 800 honey.

        The Honey Wreath could be upgraded by completing Honey Bee's Beesmas 2020 and 2021 quest.
        This would cause it to grant 50% more honey and also summon a level 20 [[Honey Bee]] that
        lasted for 30 minutes.

        == Appearance ==
        Before completing Black Bear's quest, it looks like an ordinary wreath and does not have the
        honey icons decorated around it. After completing his quest, the wreath is decorated with
        honey icons and the [[Flight of the Bumble Egg]]. It is located between Black Bear and the
        [[Ticket Tent]], making it available to any player that has completed the quest. If the
        player completed Honey Bee's \"Honey Bee's Honey Wreath?\" quest, it would have a Gifted Honey
        Bee face in the center of the wreath.

        == Trivia ==
        * The wreath model comes from the [https://www.roblox.com/catalog/42844877/Christmas-Wreath
        Christmas Wreath hat] on Roblox's catalog.
        * The egg that is hung on the wreath is the [[Flight of the Bumble Egg]]. It is a limited
        item that could be worn on your avatar. It was only obtainable during the [[Egg Hunt 2019]]
        event.
        * Similar to [[coconut]]s, the amount of honey received from the wreath is increased by
        [[System Page#Honey From Tokens|Honey From Tokens]].
        * If the player attempts to use the Honey Wreath before it is decorated, it will display
        text saying \"''This Wreath looks frankly uninspired...''\"
        * This was one of the 4 machines that granted the player temporary bees (only if the “Honey
        Bee’s Honey Wreath?” quest is completed), the others being the [[Honeyday Candles]], the
        [[Gummy Beacon]] and [[Onett's Lid Art]].
        * The Honey Bee given by Honey Wreath would collect pollen for the player and would also be
        able to attack the mobs the player's actual bees are attacking. e.g. if the player's bees
        attack the [[King Beetle]], the bee summoned from the Honey Wreath will also attack the King
        Beetle.
        ** However, if the player went to their hive to convert their pollen, the bee given by the
        Honey Wreath would not convert any pollen and would have just flown around the player.
        However, they are able to use a conversion link created by a [[Ability Tokens|Honey Mark]].
