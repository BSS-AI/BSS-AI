{"text": "{{Inaccurate}}
        '''Glitches''', or '''Bugs''', are situations that can occur in-game that are unintended,
        and thus likely to be patched in future [[updates]]. Some of these glitches can be
        detrimental to gameplay while others are mostly visual and won't affect players.

        ''Note: Glitches (mostly visual) with an asterisk (*) next to its name means this glitch can
        happen in other games, as well as Roblox Studio.''

        == Mob Glitches ==

        === Mobs Unable to Move ===
        [[File:Cococrab.png|thumb|356x356px|Coconut Crab stuck in the [[Coconut Cave]] during
        7/20/2022. Image taken from a video on
        [https://www.reddit.com/r/BeeSwarmSimulator/comments/w3fqwx/what_the_heck_is_up_with_my_coconut_crab_ive/
        Reddit].]]
        Occasionally, a [[mob]] will get trapped in the collision box of certain objects around it.
        This usually renders them unable to move or escape, which makes killing them easier. The mob
        can still be attacked and will still drop rewards normally. This glitch seems to happen
        primarily to [[Mantis]]es when they leap into objects, such as a wall. Sometimes, the [[King
        Beetle]] gets stuck in the corner which says \"Song Name\" near it, making it easy to kill
        him. This can also happen to [[Stick Bug]] in certain places.

        In mid-2022, during the days of 7/19-20, 7/22-31, 8/2, and 8/3, all mobs (Excluding the
        [[Werewolf]], Stick Bug, [[Tunnel Bear]], [[Wild Windy Bee]], [[Rogue Vicious Bee]], and
        [[Aphid]]s) would be frozen in place upon entering the area they spawn in. Affected bosses
        include the [[Coconut Crab]], [[King Beetle]], and [[Stump Snail]]. This makes killing them
        easier, as the mobs will not be able to voluntarily attack the player. On 7/21/2022, Onett
        would fix this glitch (temporarily) and added the code \"FrozenBugReboot\" to suffice for the
        glitch. The glitch would become fixed and unfixed for multiple weeks until 8/3/2022, when
        Onett would finally fix the glitch permanently in servers of version 448+.

        === Frozen Field Defenders ===
        When the player jumps while a mob is going towards them to attack, the mob freezes and does
        not move towards the player again until the player lands on the ground. This generally makes
        killing mobs easier. This does not apply to [[Stump Snail]], [[Ants]],
        [[:Category:Bosses|Bosses]], Aphids, and [[Cave Monster]]s.

        === Out of Bounds Mobs ===
        Mobs will occasionally go out of bounds (outside of the map). It is unknown why or how this
        happens, but when it does, mobs will attempt to walk to their desired spawn field. However,
        bees can still attack it, with it acting the same as if players were to kill it in the field
        it defends.

        === Collapsing Mobs ===
        [[File:Collapsed ladybug.png|alt=An example of a Collapsed Ladybug|thumb|A collapsed
        [[Ladybug]].]]
        This glitch is purely visual, but it can be triggered when a mob, such as a [[Ladybug]] or
        [[Rhino Beetle]] is defeated while it is trying to move. The mob's head or any other part
        can move inward. This glitch seems more common with the [[Ladybug]] and [[Spider]].

        === Invisible Meshes* ===
        If the meshes (notably the Chick egg shell and both of the Stump Snail's body and shell)
        doesn't load yet, they will appear invisible. This is purely a visual glitch. This glitch
        can be fixed by waiting until the meshes load or by rejoining the game.[[File:Mondo chick
        with no egg.png|thumb|193x193px|A Mondo Chick with an invisible egg.]]
        [[File:Commando chick with no egg.png|thumb|A Commando Chick with an invisible egg.]]

        === [[King Beetle]] in the Fields ===
        Sometimes, when a player dies or glitches their way out of [[King Beetle Lair|King Beetle's
        Lair]], King Beetle's head and health can be seen outside from the Blue Flower Field. This
        is not rare, as it happens very often when players die. This seems to be because the script
        that lets you see its health is still running.
        [[File:Invisible Stump Snail body.png|alt=An example of an invisible Stump Snail.|thumb|An
        example of an invisible Stump Snail.]]

        === [[King Beetle]] Stuck in Flower ===
        This glitch is quite rare. To activate it, the player should stand near the entrance to the
        King Beetle's Lair then let it attack them. When they die, there is a very small chance that
        it'll go out of its lair and get stuck at the flowers near the entrance. To fix this, either
        go inside the lair again or rejoin.

        === Spinning [[King Beetle]] ===
        This glitch can occur when fighting [[King Beetle]], causing it to spin around. This glitch
        is pretty rare. The cause is currently unknown.[[File:King beetle stuck.png|alt=King beetle
        stuck in flower|thumb|The King Beetle stuck in the flower below the lair entrance.]]

        === [[Stick Nymph]] Spawns ===
        Sometimes, when Stick Bug summons Stick Nymphs in a field with a wall beside it (the
        Strawberry, Bamboo, Spider, Clover, Mountain top, Blue Flower, and Pineapple fields) some of
        the Stick Nymphs get stuck inside the wall making them unable to move.

        === [[Stick Nymph]] Rain ===
        If the player stands on the instant converter by the [[Mountain Top Field]], they can trick
        all the Stick Nymphs into falling through the gap between the field and the platform, making
        them fall straight through to the inside of the [[Pro Shop]] (the Stick Nymphs no-clip right
        through the ceiling), and will stay by the containers in the Pro Shop, constantly trying to
        get back to the Mountain Top Field. They will remain there after Stick Bug switches fields,
        and can kill anyone who is in the Pro Shop. To fix this, simply kill the Stick Nymphs or
        just wait until Stick Bug despawns.

        === [[Cave Monster]] Death ===
        When Cave Monsters die, their body will remain in the location of defeat. The walking sound
        will repeatedly play as if they were still walking. Rejoining could be a method to stop the
        walking sound.

        === [[Cub Buddy]] Stuck in Bag ===
        When a cub buddy goes inside a bag in the Pro Shop or [[Mountain Top Shop]], it can’t get
        out. When in the bag, they can't be interacted with.
        [[File:40323E7A-67D3-4832-8811-70DA3B4FE0D2.jpeg|thumb|170px|An example of a Cub Buddy being
        stuck in a bag.]]

        === Enormous [[Mobs|Mob]] HP Bars ===
        This glitch causes a mob to have enormous health bar. This is because that HP is the HP that
        the mob loads with, and after the mob loads, the game changes the HP. However, if the
        player's internet is slow or lagging, the game doesn't change the HP and so the mob appears
        to gain a large amount of HP (usually 1,300,400,000). Its HP should return to normal after a
        short period of time.

        === Enormous Mob Time Bars ===
        The same as the Enormous Mob HP bars glitch, but this happens with the time bar instead.

        === [[Snowbear]] Leaving the Spider Field ===
        Sometimes, if the snowbear gets [[Ability Tokens|Mind Hacked]], it will wander out of the
        field, sometimes getting stuck. However, it can even potentially wander out of the 5 Bee
        Zone.

        === No Numbers on [[Rogue Vicious Bee]]'s Health Bar* ===
        This glitch causes the numbers on the Rogue Vicious Bee's health bar to go invisible. This
        is purely visual, and the moment Rogue Vicious Bee takes damage, the health bar will return
        to normal.[[File:GlitchAmountHP.png|thumb|150px|An example of a mob's enormous glitched HP
        bar.]]

        === Confused [[Coconut Crab]] ===

        If the player stands on the wall of the [[Coconut Field]], the Coconut Crab will think the
        player is standing on the field and try to attack the player with its claw attack but cannot
        hit the player. It should be noted that the falling coconut attack still can hit the player,
        even though this can be dodged by jumping.

        === Slow [[Coconut Crab]] Claws ===
        This glitch causes the Coconut Crab's claws to jab slowly but the crab is moving at normal
        speed, making it much harder to avoid the attack. If the Coconut Crab starts to jab with its
        claws again, this may result in it attacking with all of its claws. This usually happens
        when the player or server is lagging, and can be avoided by either changing your internet,
        rejoining, or joining a different server.

        === Commando Egg Transportation ===

        If a [[Commando Chick]] transforms into an egg in the corner north-west of the arena's
        entrance, it can briskly move to the corner south-west of the entrance.

        === Commando Wall Jump ===
        If a player is standing near the entrance to [[Commando Chick's Hideout]], when commando
        goes into its shell, it will sometimes jump onto the ledge. [[Commando Chick]] can still be
        harmed and will return to the hideout when it comes out of its shell.

        === Phantom [[Rogue Vicious Bee]] Attacks ===
        If a player touches the base of the Rogue Vicious Bee's dormant spike while in the [[King
        Beetle's Lair]], the Rogue Vicious Bee can start \"attacking\" a player in the [[Clover
        Field]]. A message will be sent to the server stating \"⚠ [Gifted] Vicious Bee is attacking
        [Player] in the Clover Field ⚠\" as normal, however, the players that would arrive at the
        Clover Field wouldn’t see anyone. The players would normally assume the \"victim\" of the
        [[Rogue Vicious Bee]] died, while those already viewing the Clover Field from up close will
        see a Vicious Bee get activated out of nowhere.

        === Slow Mobs Glitch ===
        This glitch causes field defenders to move at a slower speed. This includes the speed at
        which the mobs fall from a spawning location. The cause of this glitch is unknown though it
        happens at the same time as the Slow Bees Glitch, meaning it may be caused by bad internet
        connection or extreme lag.

        === Frozen [[Stump Snail]] ===
        [[File:Screen Shot 2021-01-01 at 10.07.30 PM.png|thumb|An example of a glitched Stump
        Snail.]]
        If a player with a cub buddy goes into the blue HQ and keeps on activating and deactivating
        the cub buddy it can get glitched inside of the elite blue guard and can then teleport away
        with the guard. If the player goes to the Stump Snail and positions the glitched Cub Buddy
        in one of the corners, when the snail reaches that corner, it will no longer be able to
        move.

        === Moving [[Commando Chick]] in Egg ===
        It is unknown how this glitch occurs, but it causes the Commando Chick to move around while
        still inside its egg. Here's a [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EB5rYJOJfSI video] as
        reference for this glitch.

        === Speedy Commando Chick Glitch* ===
        This glitch occurs randomly when Commando Chick spawns. Commando Chick's feet become stuck
        in the ground and it will move around extremely quickly only stopping when hiding in its
        egg. This bug can occur in conjunction with the Moving Commando in Egg glitch.

        === Dancing [[Stump Snail]] Glitch ===
        [[File:7719D772-2083-4C0D-A457-823B38F07D8C.jpg|thumb|A Stump Snail dancing in the field.]]
        This glitch occurs sometimes when fighting the Stump Snail. It causes the Stump Snail to
        start moving from side to side. Part of its body can go off the field itself. This happens
        occasionally when the Stump Snail changes its direction when it is moving.

        === Grenade Rain ===
        This glitch can be performed by constantly exiting and entering the [[Commando Chick's
        Hideout|Commando Chick's hideout]] repeatedly, causing the Commando Chick to spawn and
        despawn while summoning two grenades at a time. The grenades can kill players inside the
        hideout. This glitch can be annoying for people that are in the hideout that just want to
        defeat their Commando Chicks. Very rarely, if the player does this enough, it will knock the
        fleeing Commando Chick into the ground. It will stay there as the new Commando Chick does
        its normal behavior. After around 5 seconds, it will despawn.

        === Inanimate [[Coconut Crab]] ===
        Similar to the Mobs Unable to Move glitch, the Coconut Crab can sometimes go inanimate and
        would therefore be unable to move.

        === [[Fireflies]] Stuck Inside Walls ===
        Sometimes a firefly will get stuck inside a wall and the player will not be able to nudge
        the firefly. The rest of the fireflies will remain in air. Fireflies can get stuck in
        multiple different places such as the [[Honey Bee Gate]].
        [[File:RobloxScreenShot20210206 190536528.png|alt=Fireflies not moving glitch|thumb|Glitched
        Fireflies.]]

        === Fast Coconuts ===
        Sometimes, a coconut from the Coconut Crab will drop too early. This will cause many
        problems for the players because it drops too fast, and the player can't see it. Lag is the
        main cause of this occurring.
        [[File:RobloxScreenShot20210215 081453025 (2).png|thumb|A glitched Coconut Crab.]]

        === Moving Claws ===
        Sometimes the claws of a Coconut Crab will move with the Coconut Crab; it can make things
        harder for players as the glitch happens from time to time if a player's connection isn't
        working properly.

        === Fallen [[Mondo Chick]] ===
        Occasionally, [[Mondo Chick]] may fall off of the [[Mountain Top Field]] into the [[Bamboo
        Field]] or the [[Pineapple Patch]]. Loot dropped from Mondo Chick's defeat will spawn inside
        the field it was defeated in, and if it fails to reach a field before this happens it spawns
        no loot at all. This glitch is a result of Mondo entering its egg just close enough to the
        edge of the Mountain Top that the moment it leaves, if it moves in the correct direction, it
        falls out of the field.

        === Cub Buddy Loop Glitch ===
        When there's an [[Ability Tokens|ability token]] made by a [[Bees|bee]] in the air, the
        player's [[Cub Buddy]] might go for it and keep teleporting next to the player. This can
        also happen when standing on a ledge. The cub may walk off and constantly teleport to the
        same spot, then fall again.
        [[File:RobloxScreenShot20220224 161414390.png|thumb|193.981x193.981px|Another glitched
        coconut crab.]]

        === Invisible and Non-Attacking Coconut Crab ===
        This is a partly visual glitch occurs while you are in the middle of fighting the Coconut
        Crab. The only way that the Coconut Crab can damage you during this glitch is when you touch
        its hitbox or touch any of its visible claws. A purely visual glitch occurs when you either
        leave the Coconut Field, or defeat the Coconut Crab, where the remaining Coconut Crab arms
        float mid-air. The floating Coconut Crab arm is purely a visual glitch, and may only stop
        once the server is empty, and closes. It is currently unknown how either of these glitches
        occur.
        === Frozen Cub Buddy ===
        [[File:Weird cub buddy glitch.webp|thumb|The frozen cub buddy glitch|153x153px]]
        This glitch causes parts of the player's cub buddy to freeze wherever it goes. The cause of

        === Submerged Cogmowers ===
        [[File:CogmowerGlitch.png|thumb|A Cogmower mostly submerged in the Pineapple

        [[Cogmower]]s when rising up to follow the player will not fully rise up into the field

        It is unknown what causes this glitch.

        The [[Ant Challenge]] timer freezes and [[ants]] will stop spawning, it starts to spam lawn

        === Crashing Mondo ===
        This sometimes happens when you let Mondo Chick despawn, When It does its leaving jump

        === Frozen Mechsquitos ===
        [[File:LandMowersGoesCrazy.jpg|thumb|The Infinite Lawn Mowers Glitch|206x206px]]
        When the player dies during [[Robo Bear Challenge]], all [[Mechsquito]]s stop targeting the

        === Gifted [[Rogue Vicious Bee|Vicious Bee's]] Health Bar ===
        When a gifted Rogue Vicious Bee spawns, it's not always at maximum health. It is usually

        === [[Ant Challenge|Ants]] Stuck in Wall ===
        Rarely during an Ant Challenge, an [[Regular Ant|Ant]] or a [[Fire Ant]] can get stuck on

        challenge ends or they are defeated.

        === Glitched Fastest Time ===
        Since the [[Updates|12/26/2022 update]], the fastest time a player can defeat the Mondo
        Chick or the Coconut Crab is glitched and won't update if the player defeats them with a
        faster time than their previous record.

        === Flopping Cub Buddies ===
        [[File:Frozen Mobs and Flopping Cub Buddies Glitches Message.png|thumb|A message
        acknowledging the Flopping Cub Buddies.]]
        Sometimes, a Cub Buddy will get glitched and fall over nonstop. It can't summon any tokens
        or collect any tokens while this effect is active.

        === Other Players' Mobs Attacking You ===
        If a mob has no target (either because the target died or left the attack zone), the mob
        becomes capable of hitting anyone within its hitbox. This is noticeable when collecting
        pollen in the [[Coconut Field]] when your Coconut Crab has already been defeated. This
        glitch can be replicated when a player is fighting a Coconut Crab and you are inside the
        player’s Coconut Crab’s hitbox. If the player dies or leaves, the Coconut Crab can damage
        you while traveling back to its spawn location. This can occur with any mob that has a set
        spawn location. Occasionally, this can also occur with Tunnel Bear. The cause of this glitch
        is unknown.

        == Player Glitches ==

        === [[Quests|Quest]] Skipping ===
        This can happen to a player for unknown reasons, all of the [[Quest Givers]] will act like
        the player have done more [[quests]] than they have actually done, giving them quests from
        way farther ahead without giving them the rewards for the quests that the game thinks they
        have completed. This means that the player could miss out on [[Spirit Bear]] quests and not
        be able to get all of the [[Spirit Petal|spirit petals]] or miss out on [[Science Bear]]
        quests and not get all of the [[translator]]s.

        === Receiving/Completing Removed Quests ===
        Occasionally, if a player has completed the requirements for a quest that has been removed
        from the game, they can still get the rewards or possibly receive the next quest, this
        appears to be most common with [[Black Bear#Repeatable Quests .28Old.29|Black Bear's Old
        Repeatable Quests]].
        [[File:Black Bear Old Quests Glitch.png|thumb|An image showing a player receiving one of
        black bear's old quests.]]

        === Talking to [[Spirit Bear]] with less than 35 bees ===
        If the player has laggy internet or high ping, they can talk to Spirit Bear by gliding from
        the top of the Windy Bee Gate with less than 35 bees.

        === [[Glider]]/[[Parachute]] on while running glitch ===
        If the player lands on the ground with a glider/parachute on, still have it on until they
        jump, equip it, and unequip it before they touch the ground. This glitch happened on
        9/17/2020 but was patched the very same day. However, on 9/19/2020 it started to glitch
        again.

        === Shop Trap ===
        This glitch can occur in any shop. When players reset with the shop open, they are unable to
        close the interface, as there is no button to do so. Players are able to exit the shop by
        resetting their character and spamming the E key, but this won't work all the time so
        players may have to reset a few times or rejoin. This mostly happens in [[Gummy Bear's
        Lair]].

        === Bear Morph Body Split Glitch ===
        [[File:ZGLitch 2.png|thumb|120px|An example of the Bear Morph Body Split Glitch.]]
        One glitch can rarely occur when players die or enter a game with [[Bear Morph]] active. It
        causes the player to appear with no head and sometimes a few limbs. Players will not be able
        to equip items and they'll often move erratically. To fix it, wait until the bear morph buff
        expires, then leave and rejoin the game. Resetting also works, but players will still lose
        their pollen unless they use a micro-converter beforehand.

        === Wall Stuck Glitch ===
        [[File:Bear morph glitch-0.png|thumb|180px|alt=|An example of the Wall Stuck Glitch with
        Bear Morph on.]]
        If Bear Morph is active and players get too close to an outside wall, they can get stuck in
        the wall. They will remain stuck until the bear transformation ends, and the player will be
        able to move again. If players try to do this on purpose, they will likely achieve the
        desired glitch. If players don't get out of the wall, they should leave and rejoin the game
        rather than resetting, so they don't lose the [[pollen]] in their backpack unless they use
        an [[Instant Converter]] or a [[Micro-Converter|micro-converter]]. Rarely, players can get
        stuck in a wall even without Bear Morph. Using certain Rthro packages, like the Gothic
        Gargoyle, can lead to body parts becoming distorted.

        === Frozen Glitch ===
        This glitch makes the player frozen in place until the player resets their character or
        rejoins the game, however the player will still be able to move their tool to collect
        [[pollen]] if standing in a field. To cause this glitch is unknown but using a weak
        internet, high ping, being on low battery mode on mobile and using a complex
        [[Mechanics|mechanic]], (Memory match, Pollen Pot, purchasing high cost items, and
        [[Beesmas]] [[mechanics]]) will usually cause this glitch.

        === Invisible Splinter Trap ===
        If a player respawns right as Stick Bug uses his [[Stick Bug Challenge#Splinter
        Trap|Splinter Trap]], they would still get the debuff. However, players will not see the
        pollen requirement to remove it, and will continuously deal damage until death unless they
        fulfill the unknown pollen requirement.

        === Ant Challenge Haste+ Glitch ===
        If players start the Ant Challenge with Haste+ and run towards the ant hill, they will
        \"leave\" the [[Ant Field]] and will be taken out of the field given the amulet with the score
        they had at the time of leaving.

        === Empty Ant Challenge Glitch ===
        This glitch causes the Ant Challenge to be empty. It is unknown what causes this glitch, but
        it is theorized that this glitch happens when a player leaves the game while doing the Ant
        Challenge.
        [[File:BD8F04F9-9A82-4F43-85B5-AEA386C0A454.jpeg|thumb|The Empty Ant Challenge Glitch]]

        === Walking on Lava Glitch ===
        [[File:On lav.png|thumb|A player on top of lava.]]
        There are multiple [[Obstacle Courses]] that have instant-death floors. However, using a max
        scale Roblox character and one of the 2019 Rthro Football packages, it is possible to walk
        on top of these floors. However, certain emotes can cause the character to collide with the
        floor, such as /e dance3. This also works with the Levitation Animation Pack. Sometimes
        hitting the lava floor at a high enough speed (Such as jumping and landing) can cause the
        player to collide with it and die.

        === Stuck in Objects Glitch ===
        If players have enough speed and run into a solid object, such as a strawberry, they can get
        stuck inside of the object. Players can get out by resetting, rejoining, or they can run
        glitch back out by mass jumping and running.
        [[File:Stuck in objects glitch.png|thumb|A player stuck in a strawberry in the [[Strawberry
        Field]].]]

        === Sideways Ability ===
        [[File:Películas y TV 31 08 2020 09 21 23 a. m. (2).png|thumb|160px|The Sideways Ability in
        action.|alt=]]
        This glitch is a minor glitch and doesn't affect gameplay. Abilities such as the [[Porcelain
        Dipper]] ability, supercharged [[Golden Rake]], and petal shuriken are based on the player's
        orientation. Because of this, activating an ability can be summoned sideways instead of
        upright. Possible ways to achieve this include being flung by [[Wild Windy Bee]], or when
        the player is in a vertical facing position, or when the player is spamming wasd in a
        certain order when it is activated (people do this to also benefit from the boots collecting
        passive).

        === Glider Clip Glitch ===
        Sometimes, if a wall is thin enough, a player will be able to glide against it and clip
        through it.

        === Shop Trap 2 ===
        If the player activates the red field booster and quickly open the shop, the button for
        activating the red field booster will be on the screen instead of the \"Close Shop\" button.
        This will result in the player getting stuck inside the shop with no way to close it. The
        same glitch will occur if the player has a high speed boost and runs towards the blender at
        the correct angle then quickly opens the sprinkler shop.

        === Death Teleport ===
        Sometimes when the player dies, their head or other body parts will teleport to the [[King
        Beetle's Lair|King Beetle’s Lair]], no matter their location of death. This does not
        activate [[King Beetle]]. When the player is dead and teleports to King Beetle’s Lair after
        dying, they can’t leave the lair until they respawn.

        This is often caused by the player collecting the Bear Morph token from [[Bear Bee]] in any
        way (usually from their Cub Buddy) after the player dies or resets. If the token collected
        is a Black Bear, Brown Bear, or Polar Bear Morph, it will teleport the player into the King
        Beetle's Lair. In the case of a Science Bear Morph, it will teleport the player next to
        Science Bear.

        === Stuck in [[Ant Field]] Glitch ===
        If the player has slow internet connection and starts the ant challenge, there is a chance
        that the game will detect the player as being \"disconnected\" from the game when the internet
        connection acts up. This causes the player to get stuck in the Ant Field, due to the game
        thinking the player had disconnected from the server. This can be fixed when someone else
        starts the Ant Challenge, kicking the player out of the field. This glitch is beneficial to
        some quests requiring pollen from the field.

        === Invisible Pollen Collector ===
        [[File:My friend lost his collector LOL.png|thumb|alt=A player having no collector.]]
        There is a glitch where the player can lose their pollen collector, it is encountered when
        the player dies or it can happen if they click backspace. The player can fix this by
        rejoining or by equipping another collector and equipping their original one. If the player
        loses their pollen collector by pressing backspace, they can press backspace again within
        the next 5 seconds to get it back. This glitch can also happen when the inventory slot of
        the collector appears and is clicked.

        It can also happen if a player has slow internet and the inventory shows up on the \"Loading\"
        screen and the player unequips the pollen collector. When the player spawns, they will have
        no pollen collector. To fix this they have to rejoin the game or reset their character. It
        can also be fixed by equiping a different pollen collector and equiping back to your
        original pollen collector.

        === Invisible Twigs ===
        When [[Stick Bug]] moves to a different field and the player is not in flowers, the game
        will say they've been trapped in twigs, but the pollen to remove it and the twigs itself
        will not appear. This can be fixed by leaving the game. This can also be caused if the
        player has twigs on them when they die. You can also stop this glitch be leaving and
        rejoining.

        === Floor Gliding ===
        [[File:Stuck in floor glitch.png|thumb|147x147px|A player stuck in the floor.]]
        This can happen when landing on the ground with high ping, the player will get stuck in the
        floor and start moving around at high speeds without being able to control where they're are
        going. They can get out of the floor by moving off the edge of whatever surface they got
        stuck in or resetting. The player can partially control which direction they move in by
        equipping their glider. Note: If the player plays on computer, they can use shift lock and
        move with their glider equipped.

        === [[Tide Popper]] freeze glitch ===
        On 1/01/2022, going to the Tide Popper in the [[Blue HQ]] shop would cause a game freeze not
        letting you leave the shop anymore. Onett and the Bee Swarm Simulator crew left a warning
        sign for all users to not visualize the item.

        Sometimes bees will freeze in their position and not move. The cause of this glitch is
        unknown, but it happens fairly commonly while fighting mobs, especially Stump Snail.

        player can walk against the Spider Cave (where the Spider spawns), and the bees will think a
        hostile mob is around them. They will spawn [[Ability Tokens|tokens]] that will only be
        spawned when encountering an enemy (e.g: A Vicious Bee spawning an Impale token). This is
        for unknown reasons, though it may be because the Cave Monster spawn is above or too close
        to the Spider Cave, so the bees will spawn tokens that will only be spawned when attacking
        an enemy.

        extremely slowly.

        During the glitch, all bees will move at a significantly slower speed. The cause of this
        glitch is unknown however it happens at the same time as the Slow Mobs Glitch, meaning it

        ===Invisible Purple Potion/Super Smoothie Glitch===
        Prior to the items being officially added to the game, it was possible to obtain [[Purple

        temporarily stop using the item for a few seconds. To fix this, ether use the item manually,
        or wait a few seconds for it to work again.

        === False Cooldown ===
        When a cooldown occurs for an item in the hotbar, the time between each second is a bit more
        than what the game considers to be the cooldown of said item. This results in an item
        available to be used before the cooldown seen on the hotbar is completed. Usually, this is
        unnoticeable, but with long-cooldown items such as [[Glitter]] and [[Festive Bean]]s, the
        cooldown difference is visible.

        === Sprinkler Glitch ===
        When the player jumps and use a sprinkler multiple times then they can stack sprinklers on
        top of each other or put them close together, which can be useful. Be aware that if the
        player has too much jump boost (sources of jump boost include bear morph, boots, etc) then
        it will show a message saying \"You must be standing in a field to use this.\" This glitch is
        very powerful when the player has Golden Gushers or Diamond Drenchers sprinklers because
        they have a larger quantity of sprinklers.

        Sometimes, when you collected a Gifted Buoyant Bee's Surprise Party, it will summon Golden
        Balloons normally, but the balloons are invisible and only its area of effect can be seen,
        the area is also not moving. It is unknown how this glitch happens.
