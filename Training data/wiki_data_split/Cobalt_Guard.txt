        == Trivia ==
        * If the ingredients to craft this item were to be crafted, a total of 200,000,000 (200
        million) honey, 5000 [[Blueberry|blueberries]], 2500 [[Pineapple|pineapples]], 25 [[Magic
        Bean|magic beans]], 1500 [[Royal Jelly|royal jellies]] and 100 stingers are needed. The
        total crafting time is 13375 minutes (or ~223 hours) of playtime.
        * This is the only blue guard that isn't purchasable in the [[Blue HQ]].
        * This is one of the two guards that have a passive ability, the other being the [[Crimson
        Guard]].
        * Its [[:Category:Red|red]] counterpart is the Crimson Guard.
        * This and the Crimson Guard are the only guards to be based off an [[Bees/Event|Event
        bee]].
        * This is the only blue guard that leaves a trail.
        * The blue trail's ''min length value'' is higher than the [[Crimson Guard]]'s trail, making
        the blue trail to be less smoother than the red one. This could be to not show the trail
        when using a petal wand.
