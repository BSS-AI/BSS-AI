        The '''Global Top Ant Exterminators Leaderboard''' is one of the 62 [[leaderboards]] in the
        game. This leaderboard shows the top [[Ant Challenge]] scores of players. It shows 10
        leaderboard positions at one time. However, the player can scroll down the leaderboard to
        see more scores of other players, all the way down to the 100th place.

        If the player steps on the white pad in front of it, the message <span
        style=\"background-color:#0090BF; color:white;\">\"Your Highscore: X\"</span> will
        appear. (X = Your Highscore)

        The structure conceals a secret passage containing an [[Ant Pass|ant pass]], and the [[Gummy
        Bee Egg Claim]].

        == Location ==
        It is located in front of the Ant Challenge room near the [[Ant Pass Dispenser]] and the
        [[Free Ant Pass Dispenser]]s.

        == Achievements ==

        * On 8/1/2018, SDMittens was the first user to reach 200 Ant Exterminations.
        * On 12/26/2018, SDMittens was the first user to reach 300 Ant Exterminations.
        * On 12/14/2019, Lipsisas was the first user to reach 400 Ant Exterminations.
        * On 1/10/2020, Zlib was the first user to reach 500 Ant Exterminations.
