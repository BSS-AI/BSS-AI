        The '''Highest Snowbear Level Leaderboard''' was one of 18 [[leaderboards]] in the game. It
        was added on the [[Updates#2020.12.25|12/25/2020 update]] and returned on
        [[:Category:Beesmas|Beesmas]] 2021 and 2022. It is next to the [[Spider Field]], [[Bamboo
        Field]], [[Gumdrop Shop]], [[Snowbear Summoner]], and [[Panda Bear]]. To summon a
        [[Snowbear]], the player must complete Panda Bear's Beesmas [[Quests|quest]] to gain access
        to the summoner, allowing them to use the Snowbear Summoner. Each time the player defeats
        the Snowbear, its level increases by one. The leaderboard displays the players who have the
        highest top 100 Snowbear levels. This leaderboard does not have a white pad to stand on.

        == Trivia ==
        * This was the second leaderboard to be removed, the first being the [[Most Commando
        Captures]] leaderboard.
        ** This is one of two leaderboards that were temporarily removed, the other being the
        [[Highest Robo Party Cake Rank]] leaderboard.
