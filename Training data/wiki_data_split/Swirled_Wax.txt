        {{Color|Swirled Wax}} is an [[:Category:Inventory|inventory item]] and a type of
        [[Waxes|wax]] that can be used on a [[beequip]] to reroll its stats. Beequip's with higher
        potential have a greater chance of having better stats when rerolled.

        When used on a beequip, it will change its base stats. If the beequip have waxes applied on
        it, it will also change the waxes' enhancements as well.

        When a Swirled Wax is applied to a beequip, a message pops up saying: \"The Wax has Altered
        the Beequip.\"

        Sometimes, the stats won't show any changes when you use a swirled wax, most of the time you
        simply just have to rejoin to fix it.

        ==Ways to Obtain==
        * [[Crafting]] it via [[Blender]] for:
        ** {{Cost|Hard Wax|3}}
        ** {{Cost|Soft Wax|9}}
        ** {{Cost|Purple Potion|6}}
        ** {{Cost|Royal Jelly|3333}}
        * As a stack reward for adding certain [[stickers]] to the [[Sticker Stack]]:
        ** Auryn rewards 1 swirled wax.
        ** Pearl Girl rewards 1 swirled wax.

        ==Tips==
        * Crafting swirled waxes is recommended due to the low chances of being dropped from
        harvesting planters and defeating certain [[puffshroom]]s.
        * Having as much active planters on corresponding fields that can make them drop swirled
        waxes can be helpful on getting them.
        * Try to do cooperative [[puffshroom]] runs with other strong players, as it can maximize
        the potential of the puffshroom's levels and amounts summoned.

        ==Trivia==
        *Of all 4 [[waxes]], this was the first to be added into the game. It was added in Beesmas
        2020 while the rest were added in Beesmas 2021.
        *This and {{Cost|Soft Wax|||0}} are the only waxes that are guaranteed to change a beequip's
        stats.
        * Prior to Beesmas 2021, this item had no favor when donated to the [[Wind Shrine]].
        * If the items for it were to be crafted, a total of {{Cost|Honeysuckle|90}},
        {{Cost|Sunflower Seed|900}}, {{Cost|Pineapple|4050}}, {{Cost|Strawberry|3600}},
        {{Cost|Blueberry|3600}}, {{Cost|Bitterberry|99}}, {{Cost|Neonberry|10}}, and {{Cost|Royal
        Jelly|4,512}} are needed.
        *Swirled Wax used to have a limit of 5, but the limit was removed in the
        [[Updates#2021.12.26|12/26/21 update]].
        *Prior to the 12/26/21 update, Swirled Wax did not have a wick in its icon design.
        *This is the only wax that is not included in the Waxy Bundle.
