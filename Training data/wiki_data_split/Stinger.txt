        '''Stingers''' are an [[:Category:Inventory|inventory item]] added in the
        [[Updates#2018.09.10|9/10/2018 update]]. When used, it will grant x1.5 [[Bee Attack|Bee
        attack]] for 30 seconds. It can also be used to obtain [[Vicious Bee]] (costs 250 stingers).
        Stingers are also used in many [[crafting]] recipes.

        == Ways to Obtain ==
        * Defeating [[Rogue Vicious Bee]] always grants stingers. The number of stingers dropped
        depends on the Rogue Vicious Bee's level, and whether it is gifted or not.
        * A rare drop from a [[Aphid#Rage Aphid|Rage Aphid]], [[Aphid#Armored Aphid|Armored Aphid]],
        [[scorpion]], or [[spider]].
        * As a Stack Reward for adding certain [[Stickers]] to the [[Sticker Stack]]:
        ** Flying Brave Bee rewards 3 stingers.
        ** Walking Stick Nymph rewards 3 stingers.
        ** Coiled Snake rewards 3 stingers.
        ** Dark Flame rewards 5 stingers.
        ** Cyan Hilted Sword rewards 10 stingers.
        ** Red Wailing Cry rewards 3 stingers.
        ** Tough Potato rewards 3 stingers.
        * Capturing certain amounts of [[Commando Chick]]s:
        ** 3 captures give 1 stinger and other [[items]].
        ** 12 captures gives 3 stingers and other [[items]].
        ** 30 captures gives 10 stingers and other [[items]].
        ** 40 captures gives 10 stingers and other [[items]].
        * Buying one for 10 tickets in the [[Stinger Shop]].
        * A rare drop from [[leaves]] in the [[Cactus Field]].
        * Shivering certain [[badges]] in the [[Wind Shrine]] by donating certain items (Battle,
        Clover, Spider, Cactus, and Rose badge rewards Stinger + other stuff).
        * Donating 10 or less ant passes to the wind shrine may also give a large amount of
        stingers.
        * Completing certain [[quests]] ([[Black Bear]], [[Gifted Riley Bee]], and [[Spirit Bear]]).
        * Redeeming certain valid [[codes]]:
        ** Using the code \"1MLikes\" (Gives 1 stinger and other stuff).
        * Winning it from the [[Memory Match#Mega Memory Match|Mega]], [[Memory Match#Night Memory
        Match|Night]] or [[Memory Match#Extreme Memory Match|Extreme]] [[Memory Match]]es.
        * Very rarely, as a [[Cub Buddy]] gift.
        * As a drop from [[Planter]]s harvested in the [[Cactus Field]] or [[Rose Field]].
        * Fully growing [[Red Clay Planter]] in [[Clover Field]], [[Spider Field]] and [[Cactus
        Field]] (in that order) rewards 10 Stingers (2 weeks cooldown).

        === Looting from Rogue Vicious Bee===
        The Rogue Vicious Bee can be summoned by touching or being close to a spike in a field. It
        has a higher chance of spawn during the [[Day/Night Cycle|nighttime]], and is guaranteed to
        spawn when using a [[Night Bell|night bell]]. Depending on the level, if it’s gifted, and if
        it’s the first defeat of the day, a Rogue Vicious Bee can drop 1-20 stingers.

        A daily bonus of 5 stingers is also given for the first Vicious Bee defeated that day, with
        a cooldown of 22 hours after the daily reward. This also applies to Gifted Vicious Bees,
        having their own separate daily bonus.

        There are 6 fields where the [[Rogue Vicious Bee]] can spawn at. They are:
        *[[Clover Field]].
        *[[Spider Field]].
        *[[Cactus Field]].
        *[[Rose Field]].
        * [[Mountain Top Field]].
        *[[Pepper Patch]].
