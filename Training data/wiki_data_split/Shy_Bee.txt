        '''Shy Bee''' is a [[:Category:Red|Red]] [[Bees/Legendary|Legendary bee]].

        Shy Bee's favorite type of treat is [[Strawberry|Strawberries]].

        Shy Bee likes the [[Strawberry Field]] and the [[Pumpkin Patch]]. It dislikes the [[Pine
        Tree Forest]].

        == Stats ==
        * Collects 10 [[Pollen]] in 2 seconds.
        * Makes 320 [[Honey]] in 4 seconds.
        * +100% [[Energy]], +30% [[Stats#Speed|Movespeed]], +50% Gather Speed, +240 [[System
        Page#Convert Amount|Convert Amount]], +1 [[Stats#Attack|Attack]].
        * 🌟[[Gifted Bee|Gifted Hive Bonus]]: +5% Bee Ability Pollen.

        === Abilities ===
        * '''[[Ability Tokens#Boost|[Red Boost]]]''' Grants x1.2 pollen from red [[Flowers]] for
        15s. Stacks up to 10 times.
        * '''[[Ability Tokens#Bomb|[Red Bomb]]]''' Collects 10 pollen from 13 surrounding red
        flowers (+10% pollen per Level). Combo with other bombs to increase power.
        * '''[[Passive Abilities#Nectar Lover|[Passive: Nectar Lover]]]''' This bee is twice as
        likely to sip Nectar from Planters. When it does, it gathers twice as much Nectar and
        contributes twice as much growth to the Planter.

        == Trivia ==
        * This bee, [[Ninja Bee]], [[Fuzzy Bee]], and [[Windy Bee]] are the only bees that have only
        one skin color.
        * This bee used to have a spelling or grammar mistake in its description, along with [[Hasty
        Bee]] and [[Shocked Bee]], but it was fixed in an update.
        * This bee is the only bee that has a smoke effect.
        * This bee is the red counterpart of Ninja Bee.
        * A [[Royal Jelly#Specific Bee Jelly|Special Jelly]] that changes a bee into a Shy Bee could
        have been obtained from the code, 'NewMic' (expired).
        * This bee, [[Diamond Bee]] and [[Demon Bee]] are the only legendary bees with a passive
        ability.
