        The '''Cave Monster''' is a level 11 [[Mobs|mob]] that spawns inside the [[Werewolf's
        Cave]]. They look identical to a [[spider]], except that they lack eyes. Cave Monsters make
        eerie whispering noises when the player gets close to them. A [[Honeystorm]] and a [[Mythic
        Meteor Shower]] (and during the Beesmas events, a [[Snow Storm]]) can make it bright enough
        to see their bodies.

        Their primary purpose is to guard a [[Egg#Gold Egg|Gold egg]] located at the end of the
        cave. The best way to reach it is to have [[Ability Tokens#Haste|Haste+]], Haste x5 or
        higher, or by using the [[Glider]]. Be aware that the roof of the Cave is not very high, so
        it is easy to hit your head and slow you down.

        It is possible to defeat the cave monster. However, they move very fast, so some slower
        [[bees]] will be unable to reach them. The best way to defeat them is by activating the
        [[Passive Abilities#Emergency Coconut Shield|Emergency Coconut Shield]] and running quickly
        toward the gold egg token. Cave Monsters are the only mobs to (albeit rarely) drop a
        [[Egg#Specific Bee Eggs|Honey Bee egg]]. If the player does not have the [[Coconut
        Canister]], another method is to quickly move into and then out of their spawning zone. You
        can also use the [[Passive Abilities#Star Saw|Star Saw]] passive ability from the [[Star
        Amulet|Supreme Star Amulet]]. It takes 30 minutes for one to respawn and they don't count as
        spiders towards [[quests]] from [[bear]]s.

        ==Trivia==
        *[[Onett (Developer)|Onett]] had stated that cave monsters have a rare drop and can only be
        obtained by defeating them, but he also said that the drop is not very useful. This was
        later confirmed to be a Honey Bee egg. Onett had also jokingly said that \"Saving the [[Honey
        Bee|honey bee]] from the cave monsters was the ultimate goal of [[Bee Swarm Simulator]].\"
        *Their noises can be heard throughout the entire map, along with [[Mondo Chick]].
        *Cave monsters were the first mobs that is able to instantly kill the player and one of two
        mobs that instantly kill the player upon touching them (the other being [[Tunnel Bear]]).
        **This makes the cave monsters the most lethal non-boss mob in the game.
        *Cave monsters have the second highest base level of any mob in-game, with the [[Coconut
        Crab]] having the highest.
        *Cave monsters, [[King Beetle]], Tunnel Bear, [[Rogue Vicious Bee]], [[Stick Bug]], [[Robo
        Bear Challenge|RBC mobs]], [[Wild Windy Bee]], [[aphid]]s and [[Commando Chick]] are the
        only mobs who don't belong to any particular fields.
        *There used to be 4 cave monsters because the gold egg was once the rarest [[Items|item]] in
        the game.
        *Cave monsters have one of the fewest possible amounts of drops in the game, only behind
        [[ants]], and [[Robo Bear Challenge|RBC mobs]], which have none.
        **Cave monsters, Coconut Crab, [[Robo Bear Challenge|RBC Mobs]], [[Snowbear]], and ants are
        the only mobs that don't drop honey upon death.
        *Cave monsters are currently the only mobs with a set respawn time that [[Quest Givers|quest
        givers]] do not require the player to defeat.
        *Cave monsters are the only defeatable mobs that do not have a health bar.
        **Between the [[Updates#2018.11.25|11/25/18]] and the [[Updates#2018.12.25|12/25/18]]
        [[updates]], the Cave Monsters had a health bar that revealed they were level 11 and had 400
        health.
        *When donating the Honey Bee Egg to the [[Wind Shrine]], there is a decent chance it will
        spawn [[Bean Bug|bean bugs]]. This makes killing the Cave Monster somewhat useful as some
        quests require collecting tokens from Bean Bugs.
