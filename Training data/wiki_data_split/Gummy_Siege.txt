        The '''Gummy Siege''' is rewarded for completing [[Gummy Bear#2021 Beesmas Quest - Gummy
        Beacon|Gummy Bear's]] quest for [[Beesmas]]. It is activated by using the [[Gummy Beacon]]
        on the [[Star Hall]].

        Upon activating, a platform will appear with [[Gummy Bear]] and [[Gummy Bee]] in front of
        the Gummy Beacon and the [[Music|gummy morph tune]] will play. Then, Gummy Bear will begin
        to shoot [[goo]] puddles at random fields similar to the gummy invasion event. The targeted
        field also has a chance to spawn [[Gumdrops]]. Gummy Bear will pick five random fields
        (excluding the [[Ant Field]]), and shoots goo for one minute per field, making the Gummy
        Siege 5 minutes long.

        If a player stands on the Gummy Beacon who hasn't completed the Gummy Bear's Goo Year Beacon
        quest/don’t have it, the message will say: \"This Satellite dish isn't working, but it still
        emits bad vibes...\".
        [[File:Static-assets-upload566652386421731089.png|thumb|Three random leveled Gummy Bees
        being summoned.]]
        It will also summon three level 8-20 Gummy Bees for five minutes.

        When it starts, two server-wide messages will appear saying the following lines:
        [[File:GummySiegeInProcess.png|thumb|The message that appears when you step onto the Gummy
        Beacon and a Gummy Siege is in process.]]
        <span style=\"background:#30D5C8;color:black\">Gummy Bear: \"Artillery has arrived, Gummy
        Soldier {Username}.\"</span>

        <span style=\"background:#30D5C8;color:black\">Gummy Bear: \"Beesmas ends now. Commence
        GOO YEAR celebrations! \"</span>

        ==Trivia==
        *The Gummy Bees from the Gummy Siege are not affected by the [[Passive Abilities#Gummy
        Morph|Gummy Morph]] Passive from the [[Gummy Mask]], however their [[Ability
        Tokens|abilities]] will still contribute to activating the passive.
        *Gummy Siege has the same cooldown duration as [[Onett's Lid Art]] and the [[Naughty List]],
        all three of them having a total cooldown of 8 hours.
        *Although Gummy Bear is shown during the seige, he can still be seen in his lair.
