        The '''Wild Windy Bee''' is a [[:Category:Mini Bosses|Mini-Boss]] that usually spawns in the
        [[Dandelion Field]], [[Pineapple Patch]], [[Pumpkin Patch]], [[Mountain Top Field]], and
        [[Coconut Field]].

        During the [[Beesmas]] event, Wild Windy Bee could also spawn in the [[Clover Field]],
        [[Spider Field]], [[Bamboo Field]] and [[Pepper Patch]] in addition to the [[Fields]] that
        it normally appears in.

        A Wild Windy Bee may spawn naturally every two in-game [[Day/Night Cycle]]s or from an
        offering to the [[Wind Shrine]]. If the first line of dialogue from offering to the Wind
        Shrine is “A sudden breeze sweeps the {item} into the sky\" a Wild Windy Bee will spawn in a
        random [[Fields|Field]]. If the player does not touch the cloud Wild Windy Bee is in for 3
        minutes, it will de-spawn.

        It can also be spawned by receiving [[Spirit Bear|Spirit Bear's]] \"Tickle The Wind\" [20]
        [[Quests|Quest]] that summons 3 Wild Windy Bees at Mountain Top Field, by completing Spirit
        Bear's \"Space Oblivion\" [24] quest that summons a single Wild Windy Bee, And by receiving
        Spirit Bear's \"Spring Out Of The Mountain\" [30] Quest that summons a single Wild Windy Bee.
        When a Wild Windy Bee spawns, the player needs to find a floating [[Clouds|Cloud]] over a
        field with Windy Bee inside. It will be camouflaged inside the cloud and have a distinct
        white trail. Touching the Wild Windy Bee in time will initiate the fight. The starting level
        of Wild Windy Bee upon discovery is random but can only go up to level 6 (unless spawned by
        [[Onett (Developer)|Onett]]).

        When Windy Bee starts its attack, all players on the server receive the message: \"<span
        style=\"color:white;background-color:gray\">☁️ (Username of the Player) found Windy Bee in
        the (Dandelion Field/Pineapple Patch/Pumpkin Patch/Mountain Top Field/Coconut Field) (Clover
        Field/Spider Field/Bamboo Field/Pepper Patch Only for Beesmas)! ☁️</span>\". When the
        Windy Bee's HP reaches 0, the bee will drop rewards and immediately move to a random field,
        excluding the [[Ant Field]] and [[Stump Field]]. It leaves successively better rewards
        behind each time it's forced to move. When it leaves the map after its 5-minute timer
        elapses, the server states: \"<span style=\"color:white;background-color:gray\">☁️ Windy
        Bee is fleeing... ☁️</span>\"

        If the chimes on the Wind Shrine moves continuously from left to right, it indicates that
        Wild Windy Bee is on the map, either camouflaged in a cloud or fighting in the open.

        == Attack Pattern and Rewarding==
        Wild Windy Bee uses 2 different attacks. Its first attack is summoning 3 tornados that wipes
        [[Pollen]] off the field and deals damage every 0.5 seconds. The damage dealt depends on the
        proximity between the tornado and the player, plus the number of times Wild Windy Bee is
        defeated. The duration and speed of the tornadoes also depend on the number of times Wild
        Windy Bee is defeated. This attack can damage other mobs. The damage will vary from 1-20.
        This attack will happen first, then cycled after 3 Wind blows

        Wild Windy Bee's second attack is forming a gust of wind that blows players away with great
        force in a rectangular area. It will deal no damage, but the player(s) will be flung away if
        caught in the rectangular area, unable to deal damage for some time. A red attack indicator
        is shown for about one second to show where Wild Windy Bee is about to attack. The direction
        of attack depends on Wild Windy Bee's current position and the position of a randomly
        targeted player on the field. The size of the attack is based on how far the edge of the
        field is from where Wild Windy Bee is looking. If Wild Windy Bee cannot detect any players
        on the field, it will attack in a random direction. This attack is cycled in after Wild
        Windy Bee finishes the tornado attack.

        Upon defeat, Wild Windy Bee leaves behind a cloud in the middle of the field that regrows
        flowers. A ring of tokens then appears underneath Windy Bee like [[Stick Bug]], and each
        player gets their own loot ring unless the player does not deal enough damage. Afterward,
        Windy Bee flies off to a different field. Wild Windy Bee will continue doing this for 5
        minutes before fleeing.

        Every time Wild Windy Bee is defeated, it levels up with more health, similar to Stick Bug.
        It also has a higher chance of dropping Cloud Vials and rarer items.

        ==Tips==
        * When summoning one, 5 - 10 Field Dice/3-5 Oils/5-10 Glue is the best way if the player
        wants a cheap but reliable way.
        ** Other options include: 1 Star Jelly, 3-5 Glitters.
        *Kill your mobs beforehand, especially ones like the [[Coconut Crab]] and [[Chicks#Mondo
        Chick|Mondo Chick]] that tend to interfere the most.
        *[[Nectar]]s such as Invigorating Nectar grants x1.01 - x1.10 Bee attack and Motivating for
        1% - 5% Bee Ability Rate.
        * Equip accessories that improve bee attack, such as the [[Fire Mask]] or the [[Demon
        Mask]].
        **Equipping [[Coconut Clogs]] or [[Gummy Boots]] gives an extra 5% and 10% bee attack each.
        **You can use [[Stinger]]s for an extra 1.5x bee attack, though if you don’t have a Vicious
        Bee it is not recommended.
        *Use [[Oil]] and/or [[Tropical Drink]]s for 1.2x player and bee movespeed and 5% [[Critical
        Hits|critical chance]].
        **Use [[Super Smoothie]] for both improved movespeed, bee movespeed, critical chance and
        super-crit chance. Though it's not recommended due to its expensive crafting recipe and its
        rarity.
        *Try and build up a [[Ability Tokens#Focus|Focus]] stack before starting the battle as
        critical chance improves the chances of a critical hit, increasing attack damage.
        **Try and build up a precision stack before starting the battle as precision grants
        Super-Crit chance that are 33% stronger than a critical hit and also reduces the cooldown
        for your precise bees to attack again by 75%.
        **You can also collect Melody to gain 100% critical power for 30 seconds.
        *The player can use the sound effects created by the windy bee to tell what attack it is
        going to execute next.
        *During the wind gust attack, the player should go into an open space to avoid the attack
        easily.
        *If possible, use the decorations around the field to stand on. This makes the bee think
        there is no one in the field and it won't target the player.
        **Going out of the field but near the Wild Windy Bee makes the bee think there is no one in
        the field but player's bees will still attack.
        *Since the tokens from Wild Windy Bee have a relatively long lifespan, the player should
        follow the Windy Bee to it's next field first, and then collect the rewards using a
        [[Ability Tokens#Token Link|Token Link]] or get hit by a bullet or hitting all targets from
        [[Precise Bee|Precise Bee's]] target practice.
        *Use the [[:Category:Transport|Transportation Devices]] to get to certain fields quicker.

        == Trivia ==
        * The player can tell if a Wild Windy Bee is in a field when the wind chimes of the [[Wind
        Shrine]] are continuously moving.
        * If the timer ends before it generates reward tokens, it will leave without making any
        rewards. However, if it has already started producing them, it will finish spawning them as
        Windy Bee flees.
        *Like [[Mythic Meteor Shower]]s and [[Puffshroom]]s, the quality of its rewards are affected
        by the law of diminishing marginal utility. The more times the player receives loot from
        Wild Windy Bees in one day, the worse the rewards gradually get.
        *Windy Bee and [[Vicious Bee]] are the only bosses/mobs that are [[Bees]], Vicious Bee’s
        counterpart being [[Rogue Vicious Bee]].
        **Unlike Rogue Vicious Bee, the Wild Windy Bee does not have a gifted variant.
        * Similar to Rogue Vicious Bee's [[Ability Tokens#Impale|Impale]] attack, the tornado can
        deal damage to other mobs.
        *[[Onett (Developer)|Onett]] would sometimes spawn Wild Windy Bee in fields, including
        fields where it wouldn't naturally appear, usually to help grow [[Sprout]]s that he also
        spawns.
        ** If Onett spawns them in the [[Stump Field]], the Windy Bees freeze in the air upon defeat
        until the timer is up, as they are not programmed to fly to any other field from Stump
        Field.
        **At the end of Beesmas 2020, Onett spawned a couple of Wild Windy Bees called \"The Windy
        Gang\".
        **The Windy Gang made a return in Beesmas 2021, spawning a festive sprout and appearing in
        the [[Mountain Top Field]].
        *[[Black Bear]] mentioned this bee in one of his dialogues before it was added to the game,
        where he said \"A powerful bee destroyed fields with gusts of wind.\"
        *This is the only mob/enemy that can knock players back.
        *This is the fifth [[Mobs|Mob]] to have non-fixed levels, the first four being [[Ants]],
        [[Stick Bug]], and [[Stick Nymph|Stick/Festive Nymphs]].

        *It is possible to damage a Wild Windy Bee while it is summoning rewards, by using flames.
        *This, along with the [[Commando Chick]] and [[Snowbear]] are the only Mini-bosses that drop
        loot in a circle
        *This, [[King Beetle|King beetle]] and [[Commando Chick]] are the only mobs that drop loot
        only in the center of the location they are in. Even when they are not close to the center.
        *When Wild Windy Bee flees, it will face all players in the server before flying off. This
        most likely explains why when Wild Windy Bee flees, it starts to move in random directions.
        *If the player collides with Wild Windy Bee's body by jumping into it, they will take some
        damage.
