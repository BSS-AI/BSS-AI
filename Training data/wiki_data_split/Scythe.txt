        The '''Scythe''' is a [[Tools|tool]] that can be purchased in the [[Red HQ]].

        {| class=\"article-table\"
        !Crafting Ingredients
        |-
        |{{Cost|Honey|3,500,000}}
        |}

        The Scythe collects 8 [[pollen]] from 7 patches in 0.47 seconds and collects x2 pollen from
        [[:Category:Red|red]] [[flowers]]. Every 20th swing spawns a [[flame]]. Its average base
        pollen collection rate is 158.45 pollen per second: 238.29 from red flowers and 119.14 from
        white and blue flowers. It emits fire particles at the blade.
