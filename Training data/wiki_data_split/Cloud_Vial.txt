        }}A '''Cloud Vial''' is a [[:Category:Consumables|consumable]]
        [[:Category:Inventory|inventory item]] that was added in the [[Updates|9/28/2019 update]].
        Once used, it will spawn a [[Clouds|cloud]] in the field you are standing in.

        The player can have a maximum of 10 in their inventory at a time. Obtaining more than 10
        will not be added into the inventory unless obtained from a [[Quests|quest]], [[Sticker
        Stack]] reward, or [[Ornament Presents]].

        The summoned [[Clouds|cloud]] will move around the [[Fields|field]]. It grants a 25% (50%
        with [[Gifted Bee|Gifted]] [[Windy Bee]]) [[pollen]] boost if the player is under it, while
        causing [[flowers]] under the cloud and [[sprout]]s in the field to grow faster. If there is
        a sprout in the field, the clouds will help the sprout grow by connecting to the sprout with
        a straight blue line. The growing speed applied to the sprout increases with the pollen
        amount required for the sprout. Each field has a limit of clouds, with bigger and
        higher-tier fields having a higher limit. If the limit for clouds has reached, using anymore
        clouds will result in the following message: \"There are too many Clouds in this field\".
        Windy Bee's [[Ability Tokens#Rain Cloud|Rain Cloud]] ability will ignore this field and will
        not further summon anymore clouds into the field.

        It can't be used in the [[Ant Field]], attempting to do so will result in the following
        message: <span style=\"background-color:red; color:white;\">\"You can't make clouds in a
        challenge field.\".</span>

        It also can't be used in the [[Hub Field]], attempting to do so will result in the
        message:<span style=\"background-color:red; color:white;\">\"You can't make clouds in the
        Hub Field.\".</span>

        Donating a cloud vial to the [[Wind Shrine]] after having donated a [[Spirit Petal|spirit
        petal]] will give the player a chance to obtain a [[Egg#Event Bee Eggs|Windy Bee egg]] if
        they do not already own a [[Windy Bee]].

        The following audio plays when a cloud vial is used:[[File:Vialuse.ogg]]

        **\"1MLikes\" (Gives 1 cloud vial + other items).
        **\"ThnxCyasToyBox\" (Gives 1 cloud vial + other items).
        *As a Stack Reward for adding certain [[stickers]] to the [[Sticker Stack]]:
        **Yellow Umbrella rewards 1 cloud vial.
        **Simple Cloud rewards 1 cloud vial.
        **Grey Raining Cloud rewards 3 cloud vials.
        **Tornado rewards 3 cloud vials.
        **Blowing Leaf rewards 3 cloud vials.

        **The Beginner's Bean Bundle gave 5 Cloud Vials and other items.

        **Gumaden10T (Gave 1 Cloud Vial + other stuff).
        *As a reward from some of [[Sun Bear]]'s quests.
        *Purchasing the Refreshing Bundle in the [[Bee Bear's Catalog]] gives you 20 Cloud Vials and
        other items
        *Completing some of [[Bee Bear|Bee Bear's]] quests.

        ==Trivia==

        *Cloud vials do not spawn a Wild Windy Bee. Instead, if a spirit petal isn't donated yet, it
        will summon clouds. To summon a Wild Windy Bee, the player would need to donate items to the
        Wind Shrine.
        * Cloud vials are the only items that can directly help grow sprouts.
        *Clouds, [[Sprinklers]] and [[bubbles]] are the only ways to regenerate flowers without them
        naturally regenerating.
        *With high enough jump power, collecting the cloud vial token is possible without 35 bees.
        Jumping from the roof of the Red HQ to the area where the token is makes it possible.
