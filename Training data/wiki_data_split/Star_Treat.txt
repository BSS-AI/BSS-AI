        A {{Color|Star Treat}} is a special type of [[Treats|Treat]] that, when fed to a
        [[Bees|bee]], raises its [[bond]] by 1,000, along with the bee transforming into its
        [[Gifted Bee|gifted form]]. It is the only treat in the game which is guaranteed to turn a
        bee gifted. It is also one of the only ways to get gifted [[Bees/Event|Event bees]],
        including the {{Cost|Gingerbread Bear}} and [[Aged Gingerbread Bear|aged gingerbread bear]].
        == Ways to Obtain ==
        * Buying it with {{Cost|Ticket|1,000}} inside the [[Ticket Tent]].
        * Playing [[Memory Match#Night Memory Match|Night Memory Match]]/[[Memory Match#Extreme
        Memory Match|Extreme Memory Match]] (exceptionally rare).
        * Given by the [[Wind Shrine]] if you already have a [[Windy Bee]] after buying the [[Robux
        Shop|Windy Bee Pack]].

        === Drops ===
        * An exceptionally rare drop from the [[Mondo Chick]], [[Coconut Crab]], and [[Fireflies]].
        * Capturing [[Commando Chick]] for the 100th time guarantees a star treat.
        * An exceptionally low chance of dropping from [[Mythic Meteor Shower|Mythic Meteors]].
        * An extremely rare drop from {{Cost|Festive Planter}}.

        === Quests ===
        * Completing all of [[Onett|Onett's]] quests, [[Mother Bear|Mother Bear's]] quests, and
        [[Panda Bear|Panda Bear's]] quests.
        * Completing 750 [[Brown Bear]] quests rewards a star treat. Every 500 quests after the
        750th Brown Bear quest also rewards a star treat.

        * This and the {{Cost|Atomic Treat}} give the most [[bond]] (1,000 bond) out of any treat,
