        The '''Mondo B.B.M. Mask''', or '''Mondo Bubble Bee Man Mask''', is a
        [[:Category:Masks|Mask]] that is bigger version of the [[B.B.M. Mask]]. It was obtained by
        completing [[Bubble Bee Man|Bubble Bee Man's]] [[Quests|Quest]], \"B.B.M. Mission\", which was
        given out in the [[Bear Gate|30 Bee Zone]] during the [[:Category:Beesmas|Beesmas 2018
        Event]]. To obtain the quest the player would have needed to complete [[Bee Bear|Bee
        Bear’s]] entire Beesmas 2018 questline (unavailable). Otherwise, Bubble Bee Man would say,
        'uhh... ..ummm....'. After the completion of this quest, this hat would automatically
        replace the hat the player was wearing. Currently, players can reequip this mask by talking
        to BBM in the [[Bear Gate|30 Bee Zone]].
