        A '''Toy Horn''' is a [[Bond|level]] 9 [[:Category:Beesmas|Beesmas]] [[beequip]]. It has an
        equip limit of two. There is no color requirement. It can only be equipped to [[Brave
        Bee|Brave]], [[Rage Bee|Rage]], [[Lion Bee|Lion]], [[Cobalt Bee|Cobalt]], or [[Crimson
        Bee|Crimson]] [[bees]].

        [[Waxes|waxing]]

        *[[Energy]] (Guaranteed) - -10% to -20%.
        *[[System Page#Bee Ability Rate|Ability Rate]] - +1%. (up to +4% from waxing)
        * [[Ability Tokens#Melody|Ability: Melody]] (Guaranteed).

        +5%
