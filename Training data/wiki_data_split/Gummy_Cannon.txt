        The '''Gummy Cannon''' is [[Gummy Bear|Gummy Bear's]] personal cannon that he used to fire
        [[gumdrops]] onto [[fields]]. It was brought to the mountain in the
        [[Updates#2018.05.26|5/26/2018]] update, and was removed alongside Gummy Bear at a later
        date. The Gummy Cannon's color scheme is identical to the color of [[goo]], gumdrops,
        [[Gummy Bee]], and Gummy Bear, with a teal base and a pink barrel. It was found next to
        Gummy Bear and Gummy Bee.

        When Gummy Bear launched the cannon, all players in the server would get a notification that
        said: <span style=\"background-color:#33ffb7;\"><span style=\"color:black;\">\"Sights
        Set on the [Field Name]!\"</span></span> and gumdrops would be scattered all over
        the selected field until the field changed.

        During Beesmas 2020 and Beesmas 2021, it could be summoned by using the [[Gummy Beacon]] to
        summon a [[Gummy Siege]] after completing Gummy Bear's [[Gummy Bear#2021 Beesmas Quest -
        Gummy Beacon|Beesmas Quest]]. Each time gumdrops are scattered on a field, a gumdrops token
        appeared in its place.
        [[File:Gummy bear's plataform.png|thumb|200px|Gummy Bear next to the Gummy Cannon during a
        Gummy Siege.]]

        == Trivia ==
        * The Gummy Cannon was the only cannon that wasn't used for player
        [[:Category:Transport|transportation]]. It was only used for decoration.
        ** It was also the first and only cannon to launch [[items]].
        * When the Gummy Cannon launched gumdrops, the animation of the gumdrops falling onto the
        selected field was the same one as the player using gumdrops in the field.
        * The Gummy Cannon was not completely accurate; the gumdrops it launched could miss the
        fields it was aimed at, and possibly even splatter goo into nearby fields.
        * This is the only cannon that was removed in-game and returned.
        * The cannon shot 5-7 gumdrops before aiming at other fields.
        * There used to be a gumdrop token behind the cannon.
        ** After the [[:Category:Gummy Invasion|event]] ended, the gumdrop token was covered with a
        green path, which made it more difficult to get, although it is possible to get it if the
        player moves along the edge.
