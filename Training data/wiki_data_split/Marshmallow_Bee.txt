        The {{Color|Marshmallow Bee}} is an [[:Category:Inventory|inventory item]] that was added
        during the [[Egg Hunt 2019]] event. It has a capacity limit of 25, obtaining while having
        more than 25 will not be added into the player's inventory unless obtained from a
        [[Quests|quest]] reward. Collecting 3 Marshmallow Bees and handing them in to the [[Egg Hunt
        Info]] gave the player the [[Flight of the Bumble Egg]] and a [[Badges#Event Badges
        .5BCurrently Unobtainable.5D|badge]] during the Egg Hunt 2019 Event.

        When used, it grants the following [[Buffs & Debuffs#From Items|buffs]] for 30 minutes:
        *+50% [[System Page#White Pollen|White Pollen]].
        *+50% [[System Page#Capacity Multiplier|Capacity]].
        *+250% [[System Page#Convert Rate|Convert Rate]].

        == Trivia ==
        *This was the only item to use the word \"eat\" instead of \"use\" in the pop-up when clicked
        on.
        **Players cannot get this pop-up anymore, as in the [[Updates#2019.09.28|9/28/2019 update]],
        activating from the hot-bar will automatically trigger the item. On rare occasions, it still
        triggers the pop-up before activating.
        *This is one of the four [[:Category:Consumables|consumables]] that increase capacity, the
        others being the [[Purple Potion]], [[Super Smoothie]] and [[Snowflake]]s.
        *There are sugar crystals on the Marshmallow Bee, something most real life marshmallows
        don't have. This may have taken influence from or be a reference to the brand
        [[Wikipedia:Peeps|Peeps]], which are animal-shaped marshmallow candies coated in sugar.
        *Before the [[Updates#2020.04.06|4/6/2020 update]], there was no capacity limit on how many
        Marshmallow Bees could be stored in the player's inventory. This was likely intended because
        the player could only obtain a limited amount of Marshmallow Bees prior to this update.
        *Before the [[Updates#2022.12.26|12/26/22]] update, [[Digital Bee]] and Marshmallow Bee were
        the only bees that the player couldn't have in their hive.
        **Since Digital Bee has now been added in as an obtainable bee for the player's hive, this
        only applies to Marshmallow Bee now.
