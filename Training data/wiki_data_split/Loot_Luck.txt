{"text": "'''Loot Luck''' is a passive attribute that increases the chances of receiving certain
        rewards from defeated [[mobs]]. The formula to calculate the chance of receiving an
        [[Items|item]] from a mob when the player defeats it is: 1-((1-base chance)^luck). A general
        rule of thumb is that for every 100% Loot Luck the player has, the player gets an extra roll
        to get each drop.

        It also affects what type of token spawns in [[fields]]. Every time a [[honey]] token spawns
        in the field, it rolls a \"drop table\". Every [[Items|item]] has a chance to \"pass,\" with
        Loot Luck boosting the chance for it to pass. If it does, that item spawns. Otherwise, if it
        fails, it goes on to the next item. If every item fails, the honey token spawns as it is.
        Loot Luck does not affect [[amulet]] tiers, stats granted from amulets, drops from
        [[chicks]], or hatching and transforming [[bees]].

        == Ways to Increase ==
        Loot Luck can be increased through [[:Category:Belts|belts]], [[Buffs & Debuffs|buffs]],
        and other ways.

        === [[:Category:Belts|Belts]] ===
        Each belt gives an amount of Loot Luck.

        * The [[Belt Pocket]] gives +25% Loot Luck.
        * The [[Belt Bag]] gives +50% Loot Luck.
        * The [[Mondo Belt Bag]] gives +75% Loot Luck.
        * The [[Honeycomb Belt]] gives +100% Loot Luck.
        * The [[Petal Belt]] gives +101% Loot Luck.
        * The [[Coconut Belt]] gives +102% Loot Luck.

        === [[Gifted Bee|Gifted Hive Bonuses]] ===

        * Gifted [[Baby Bee]] gives +25% Loot Luck.

        === Buffs ===

        * [[Ability Tokens#Baby Love|Baby Love]] gives +50% Loot Luck.
        * [[Ability Tokens#Snowglobe Shake|Cool Breeze]] gives from 1% Loot Luck up to 10% Loot Luck
        at 100% Cool Breeze.

        === [[Badges]] ===
        *The [[Badges#Clover Badges|Clover Badge]] can give up to +50% Loot Luck depending on the
        badge rank.
        ** The Cadet Clover Badge gives +10% Loot Luck.
        ** The Hotshot Clover Badge gives +20% Loot Luck.
        ** The Ace Clover Badge gives +30% Loot Luck.
        ** The Master Clover Badge gives +40% Loot Luck.
        ** The Grandmaster Clover Badge gives +50% Loot Luck.
        == Trivia ==

        * Before the [[Updates#2019.04.05|4/5/2019 update]], Loot Luck was simply named Luck.
        * Loot Luck once affected [[Ability Tokens#Honey Gift|honey tokens]]. However, this feature
        was removed due to there already being a [[System Page#Honey From Tokens|Honey From Tokens]]
        stat.
        * Before the [[Updates#2019.09.28|9/28/2019 update]], the Clover Badge did not give any Loot
        Luck. This was a mistake, which was fixed after said update.
        * The [[B.B.M. Mask]] used to grant +50% Loot Luck.
