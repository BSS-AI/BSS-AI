        The '''Tide Popper''' is a [[Tools|tool]] that can be crafted in the [[Blue HQ]]. The player
        needs to have completed at least '''250''' [[Gifted Bucko Bee]] quests to unlock it.

        Every 3rd swing releases a wave of water that collects pollen, pops [[bubble]]s, and
        converts pollen when a bee is hit by the wave.

        When a bubble from the player is popped by any means, it grants a stack of \"Tide Power\" (2
        stacks if it was a [[Bubble#Golden Bubbles|golden bubble]]), which grants faster tool
        swinging speed and stronger waves. Higher stacks grants a stronger buff.

        When Tide Power reaches 495 stacks or greater, each swing of the tool will release a large
        wave. When Tide Power reaches 500 stacks, it is replaced with \"Tidal Surge\". It lasts for 10
        seconds and grants x2.5 to x1.5 tool swinging speed (starting at x2.5 and gradually
        decreasing until it reaches x1.5), and every swing releases a large tidal wave. Popping a
        bubble during a Tidal Surge extends the duration of it.

        Activating a Tidal Surge grants 1 minute of \"Tide Blessing\". Whenever a tidal wave hits a
        Balloon of the player's, it causes the balloon to convert up to 8% of your Convert Total
        into Honey Tokens worth x2.5 that (these tokens can not be collected by token link) and if
        the balloon is golden, grants 10 seconds of Tide Blessing. Tide Blessing stacks up to 4
        hours, and grants up to x1.2 Blue Pollen, Honey From Tokens, Convert Rate at Hive, Pollen
        from Bees, and Pollen from Tools.

        == Trivia==
        *The Tide Popper was originally called the \"Aqua Popper\".
        *Its red counterpart is the [[Dark Scythe]], and its white counterpart is the
        [[Gummyballer]].
        *If a wave is as high as a balloon, it can make the balloon convert pollen without Tidal
        Surge.
        *This is only blue collector that doesn't multiply the amount of blue pollen. It has same
        case as [[Dark Scythe]].
        *There is currently a glitch that makes the player holding the Tide Popper have black
        trails.
        *This is tied with the Dark Scythe for being the fifth most expensive item in the game, both
        having a cost of {{Hover|2.5T|2,500,000,000,000}} Honey. In front of the Tide Popper and
        Dark Scythe is the [[Petal Planter]], costing {{Hover|5T|5,000,000,000,000}} Honey, Coconut
        Belt costing 7.5T Honey, the Gummyballer, costing {{Hover|10T|10,000,000,000,000}} Honey,
        and [[The Planter Of Plenty]], costing {{Hover|100T|100,000,000,000,000}} Honey.
        *If the ingredients for the item were to be crafted, the total cost would be
        {{Hover|2.5T|2,500,000,000,000}} Honey, 6,750 [[Honeysuckle]]s, 112,500 [[Treat#Sunflower
        Seed|Sunflower Seeds]], 641,250 [[Pineapple|Pineapples]], 528,750
        [[Strawberry|Strawberries]], 603,750 [[Bluerberry|Blueberries]], 450 [[Magic Bean]]s, 4,500
