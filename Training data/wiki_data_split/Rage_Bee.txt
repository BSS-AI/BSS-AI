        the second being [[Tabby Bee]], and the third being [[Puppy Bee]].
        **This bee is also the only non-[[Bees/Event|Event bee]] to do so.
        *Rage Bee and [[Spicy Bee]] are the only bees that can produce rage tokens.
        *Rage Bee has three different skin colors, similar to [[Baby Bee]]. When gifted, it only has

        *A [[Royal Jelly#Specific Bee Jelly|Special Jelly]] that changes a bee into a Rage Bee could

        2018 event]] (no longer obtainable).
        *The [[Aphid#Rage Aphid|Rage Aphid]] and gifted Spicy Bee has a similar color scheme to Rage
        Bee's gifted form.
        *This is one of the only bees that can have an extra ability from [[Beequip]]s (specifically
