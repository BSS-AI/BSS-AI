        The '''Plastic Planter''' is a [[planter]] added in [[Updates|12/26/21 update]]. It takes 2
        in-game hours to fully grow unless boosted by using a [[Glitter]], [[Cloud Vial]]s, or the
        player's [[bees]] sipping on it. The planter can be bought from [[Dapper Bear's Shop]].
        Harvesting a fully grown Plastic Planter has a 1/10 (10%) chance to spawn a [[Puffshroom]]
        and will return the Planter back into the player's inventory.
