        '''Spicy Bee''' is a [[:Category:Red|Red]] [[Bees/Mythic|Mythic bee]].

        Spicy Bee’s favorite type of treat is [[Strawberry|Strawberries]].

        Spicy Bee likes the [[Pepper Patch]]. It dislikes the [[Stump Field]].

        == Stats ==
        * Collects 14 [[Pollen]] in 4 seconds.
        * Makes 200 [[honey]] in 2 seconds.
        * +220 [[System Page#Convert Amount|Convert Amount]], +50% Convert Speed, +4 Gather Amount,
        +4 [[System Page#Bee Attack|Attack]].
        * 🌟 [[Gifted Bee|Gifted Hive Bonus]]: +25% [[Flame#Flame Duration|Flame Duration]].

        === Abilities ===
        *'''[[Ability Tokens#Inferno|[Inferno]]]''' Summons a ring of 6 [[flame]]s, then summons 2
        [[Fire Bee]]s that last for 15s (+1s per Level) with levels equal to 2 less than this bee's
        level. If Gifted, each Fire Bee has a 10% (+1% per Level) chance of being Gifted as well.
        However, the gifted Fire Bees from inferno '''DO NOT''' grant their gifted hive bonus.
        *'''[[Ability Tokens#Rage|[Rage]]]''' Grants your bees +1 attack for 45s. Stacks up to 4
        times.
        *[[Ability Tokens#Flame Fuel|'''[🌟Gifted Ability: Flame Fuel]''']] For 15 seconds, Spicy
        Bee tosses oil into any flames that spawn, causing them to last 50% longer and instantly
        convert 50k pollen and (10 + 5 per Field Boost stack)% of your [[hive|Hive's]] [[System
        Page#Convert Amount|Convert Amount]]. Unless converted to a [[Flame#Dark Flames|dark
        flame]], Spicy Bee won't throw oil into flames currently on the field.
        *'''[[Passive Abilities#Steam Engine|[Passive: Steam Engine]]]''' This bee’s
        [[Stats#Speed|Movespeed]] and gather speed drastically increases up to 100% with [[Buffs
        & Debuffs#Flame Heat|Flame Heat]].

        ==Trivia==
        *Spicy Bee, [[Tadpole Bee]], [[Fuzzy Bee]], [[Buoyant Bee]] and [[Digital Bee]] all have
        four different [[Ability Tokens|Abilities]], the most for any [[Bees|Bee]].
        *This bee and [[Rage Bee]] are the only bees with the Rage Ability.
        *Spicy Bee’s gifted form is similar to Rage Bee’s gifted form and rage aphids.
        *This bee and Tadpole Bee are the only Bees who can summon other entities.
        **This is the only bee that can summon other Bees.
        *This bee was originally going to be themed like a train, hence the Steam Engine passive
        ability, train sounds the tokens make and exhaust pipes on the bee's
        body.<ref>[https://discord.com/channels/427553293862961153/427573109600550919/654002596050239546]
        Discord message from Onett</ref>
        *While Steam Engine is active, Spicy Bee will emit a continuous trail of steam.
        *This bee, along with Vector Bee, Buoyant Bee, and Precise bee emits light when gifted.
        *Unlike other gifted bees, the star on top of a gifted Spicy Bee is a different color to the
        star on its side, with a green star on the top and 2 black ones on the side.
        *This is the only Mythic bee to have completely whole numbered base [[Stats]].
        *The Fire Bees summoned by its Inferno ability cannot convert pollen in the player's hive.
        *At launch, this bee was insanely broken because of a typo. It was supposed to have each
        flame converting 1% of the player’s total conversion, but instead it converted 1% of their
        capacity. X-Flame converted 29% of the player's capacity ([[Demon Mask]] was required). It
        was quickly noticed and nerfed to the regular stats it has.
        *When Gifted, Spicy Bee's exhaust pipes emit more steam.
        *Spicy Bee and [[Puppy Bee]] are the only bees to like one field.
        *If Spicy Bee is level 1 or 2, the Fire Bees will be level 1 instead of two levels below.
        This is because there is no level 0 or negative levels.
