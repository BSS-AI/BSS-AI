        presence is not required to purchase from the shop, and players cannot interact with him.

        ==Trivia==
        *He is the first shop bear to be accessed in the game.
        *As with the other bears, Noob Bear can sometimes fall over or fall apart.
        **This [[Glitches|glitch]] has been mostly patched, so it occurs very rarely as of now.
        *This bear, [[Pro Bear]], [[Top Bear]], and [[Shadow Bear]] are the only bears without an
        actual function whatsoever.
        *Noob Bear uses the [https://www.roblox.com/bundles/56/Cartoony-Animation-Package Cartoony
        Animation Package].
