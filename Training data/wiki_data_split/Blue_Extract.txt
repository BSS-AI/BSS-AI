        |image = Blue Extract.png
        |description = Grants x1.25 Blue Pollen for 10 minutes.
        |cooldown = 1 second
        }}'''Blue Extract''' is an [[:Category:Inventory|inventory item]] added in the
        [[Updates#2018.11.25|11/25/2018 update]]. The purpose of it is to use it as material to
        craft equipment and accessories, or for a 10-minute boost that grants x1.25 [[System
        Page#Blue Pollen|Blue Pollen]]. The [[Buffs & Debuffs#From Items|buff]] cannot stack,
        and will only reset its timer if the player used a Blue Extract when the effect is already
        active. Using a [[Purple Potion|purple potion]] or [[Super Smoothie|super smoothie]] will
        override the buff.
        [[File:A blue extract token..png|thumb|right|A blue extract token.]]

        == Ways to Obtain ==
        * [[Crafting]] it via [[Blender]] for:
        ** {{Cost|Blueberry|50}}
        ** {{Cost|Royal Jelly|10}}
        * Matching a pair of blue extracts in [[Memory Match#Mega Memory Match|Mega Memory Match]]
        (worth 1 blue extract) or [[Memory Match#Extreme Memory Match|Extreme Memory Match]] (worth
        3 blue extracts).
        * As a reward from the [[Wind Shrine]].
        * As a stack reward for adding certain [[Stickers]] to the [[Sticker Stack]]:
        ** Flying Ninja Bee rewards 1 blue extract.
        ** Blob Bumble Bee rewards 1 blue extract.
        ** Menacing Mantis rewards 1 blue extract.
        ** Small Blue Chick rewards 1 blue extract.
        ** Tadpole rewards 1 blue extract.
        ** Cool Backpack rewards 1 blue extract.
        ** Bubble Wand rewards 1 blue extract.
        ** Tide Popper rewards 1 blue extract.

        === Drops ===
        * As a rare drop from [[Rhino Beetle|rhino beetles]] and [[Mantis|mantises]].
        * As a common drop from [[King Beetle]] and [[Aphid#Diamond Aphid|Diamond Aphids]].
        * Farming in the [[Blue Flower Field]], [[Bamboo Field]], [[Pine Tree Forest]], or the
        [[Stump Field]] can rarely drop blue extracts.
        * A rare drop from [[leaves]] in the Blue Flower Field, Bamboo Field, Pine Tree Forest, or
        the Stump Field.
        * As a rare drop from [[sprout]]s (the odds increase depending on the rarity of the sprout
        and if the sprout is located on the blue fields).
        * As a drop from a [[Ability Tokens#Festive Gift|Festive Gift]] (if [[Festive Bee]] is
        [[Gifted Bee|gifted]]) or [[Ability Tokens#Festive%20Mark|Festive Mark]].
        * As a rare gift from a [[Cub Buddy|cub buddy]].
        * As a drop from the certain [[Planters]]:
        ** The {{Cost|Blue Clay Planter|||0}}, and {{Cost|Hydroponic Planter|||0}} are guaranteed to
        drop blue extracts.
        *** The {{Cost|Hydroponic Planter|||0}} drops extra blue extracts in blue fields (the Blue
        Flower Field, Bamboo Field, Pine Tree Forest, and the Stump Field).
        *** Planting the {{Cost|Hydroponic Planter|||0}} in the Blue Flower Field, then the Bamboo
        Field, then the Pine Tree Forest, then the Stump Field results in an additional 100 Blue
        Extracts (36 day cooldown).
        ** Planters harvested in blue fields may drop blue extracts.

        === Quests ===
        * Completing every [[Gifted Bucko Bee|Gifted Bucko Bee's]] quests will always reward one
        blue extract.
        * [[Black Bear]] gives blue extracts on the following quests:
        ** Walk Through The Woods gives 1 blue extract and other items.
        ** Bamboo Boogie 2: Bamboo Boogaloo gives 1 blue extract and other items.
        ** You Blue It gives 1 blue extract and other items.
        ** Black Bear's repeatable quests occasionally gives blue extracts.
        * [[Science Bear]] gives blue extracts on the following quests:
        ** Meticulously Crafted gives 1 blue extract and other items.
