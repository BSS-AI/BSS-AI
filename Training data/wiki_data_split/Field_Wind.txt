        A '''Field Wind''' is a stacking buff that grants a bonus to [[pollen]] and [[Instant
        Conversion|instant conversion]] to a specific [[Fields|field]]. Each stack of a field wind
        increases pollen gained from its field by a set amount depending on the field, and also
        gives 3% instant conversion to the field starting with a base of 25%. Field winds last for
        30 minutes. Field winds are obtained by donating certain [[items]] to the [[Wind Shrine]] or
        using various [[codes]]. Field wind tokens from the Wind Shrine have a lifespan of 30
        seconds.

        With each application, the field winds stacks add up, and the duration resets to 30 minutes.
        If there's another code that gives a boost for that same field, this can be used to extend
        or reset the timer for another 30 minutes. The max stack for field winds is x15. Field winds
        can stack up to x15 if the item donated is rare enough and the player has enough [[Wind
        Shrine#Favor|favor]].

        Unlike its field boost counterpart, fields boosted with winds do not give off [[sparkles]].
        However, the icons used to identify field winds are the same as field boosts, except they
        have a white background.

        Lower level [[Fields]] tend to have more field pollen than other fields.
