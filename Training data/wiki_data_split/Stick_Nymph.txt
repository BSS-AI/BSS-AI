        == Drops ==

        Stick Nymphs will drop 5 random tokens in a pentagonal formation when defeated.<br
        />Rewards can include:

        * [[Honey]] calculated by the formula 200x, with x being the stick nymph's level.
        * [[Gumdrops]].
        * [[Pineapples]].
        * [[Sunflower Seed|Sunflower Seeds]].
        * [[Treats]].
        * [[Royal Jelly]].
        * [[Snowflake]]s (Beesmas Only).
        *[[Oil]] (Rare).
        *[[Magic Bean]] (Rare).
        *[[Enzymes]] (Rare).
        * [[Royal Jelly#Star Jelly|Star Jelly]] (Very Rare).
        * Walking Stick Nymph [[Sticker]] (Extremely Rare)

        ==Trivia==

        *Stick Nymphs can follow the player out of the [[Fields|Field]] and can either go through
        some objects, be obstructed by them, or go into certain locations, such as the [[King Beetle
        Lair|King Beetle's Lair]], which is not always the same as what the player cannot go
        through. This can allow the player to exploit them and deal damage from afar.
        *Stick Nymphs, just like[[Chicks#Mondo Chick|Mondo Chick]] and the [[Festive Nymph]]s, lose
        health every second, Mondo Chick always loses 333 health per second, but Stick Nymphs and
        Festive Nymph's health devours faster when their level is higher, and is possible to be
        defeated naturally unlike Mondo Chick who only has 300 health left when reaching 15 minutes.
        If their health drains to 0 without the players, they still will drop rewards.
        *Stick Nymphs are unable to jump, so if you get on a platform higher than the Stick Nymph
        can reach, they cannot damage you.
        *Stick Nymphs and Festive Nymph are the only mobs that can be summoned by another mob.
        **These are two of 18 mobs that cannot spawn naturally, as in the player cannot simply walk
        in a field and fight it, the other mobs being the five types of [[Ants]], [[Snowbear]],
        Stick Bug, the five [[Robo Bear Challenge]] Mobs, and the four [[Robo Party]] mobs.
        *Stick Nymphs were the first mob to drop tokens upon defeat. Stick Bug doesn't count because
        Stick Bug is impossible to defeat, as knocking Stick Bug down to 0 health will only raise
        its level by 1 point and reset its health to full in the process.
        *Whenever Stick Nymphs spawn in the [[Strawberry Field]], there is a glitch where they can
        go up the ramp.
