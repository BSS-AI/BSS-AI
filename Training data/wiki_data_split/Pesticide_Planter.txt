        The '''Pesticide Planter''' is a reusable [[planter]] added in [[Updates#2021.12.26|12/26/21
        update]]. It grows in about 10 in-game hours and stores around 200,000,000 [[Pollen]]. The
        planter can be purchased from [[Dapper Bear's Shop]].

        |}

        It grows 30% faster (7 Hours) and grants 50% more pollen on harvest in the [[Basic Bee
        Gate|5 Bee Zone]] ([[Strawberry Field|Strawberry]], [[Spider Field|Spider]] and [[Bamboo
        Field|Bamboo]] [[Fields]]). [[Bees]] with [[Mutation]]s are 25% more likely to sip
        [[Nectar]] from this planter, and it grants 30% more Satisfying and Motivating Nectar.
        Harvesting a fully grown Pesticide Planter has a 1/6.5 (15.38%) chance to spawn a
        [[Puffshroom]].
