{"text": "The '''Day/Night Cycle''' is a feature added in the [[Updates#2018.09.10|9/10/2018
        update]]. Before the update, it was always daytime.

        ==Day==
        [[File:RobloxPlayerBeta 2018-09-15 16-34-53.png|thumb|250px|The game during daytime.]]
        Daytime lasts from around 10 to 30 minutes (for an average of 20 minutes). All [[music]]
        plays as normal. This is the default setting for the game.

        ==Night==
        [[File:RobloxPlayerBeta 2018-09-24 18-02-11.png|thumb|250px|The game during nighttime.]]
        When the day ends, the sun goes down quickly, and the sky shifts to black, indicating that
        it is nighttime. During this cycle, all outdoor music is replaced by
        [[Music#mountaincall|mountaincall]]. Entering any area that plays the
        [[Music#vendor|vendor]], [[Music#crawlers|crawlers]], [[Music#gbtune1|gbtune1]],
        [[Music#hibernation1|hibernation1]] music or music playing during an [[Ant Challenge|Ant]],
        [[Robo Bear Challenge|Robo]] or [[Stick Bug Challenge]] will override the night music.
        [[Fireflies]] will also appear, stopping in different [[fields]]. The background of the
        server and walls will turn pitch black. A [[Rogue Vicious Bee]] has a higher chance of
        spawning every night. A moon sprout also has a 60% chance of spawning every night.

        Walking near a firefly will make it fly into the air and leave a 1x1 patch of [[sparkles]].
        Once a player has removed all the fireflies, one of the tokens below will appear in the
        middle of their formation:
        *[[Moon Charm|Moon charms]] (increments of 1, 5, or 10).
        *[[Glitter]] (Very rare).
        *[[Royal Jelly#Star Jelly|Star jelly]] (Extremely rare).
        *[[Night Bell|Night bell]] (Extremely rare).
        *[[Star Treat|Star treat]] (Exceptionally rare).

        Fireflies can only stop in the same field three times. After removing all the fireflies in a
        field three times, they will fly to a different field. Fireflies will only stop in the
        [[Rose Field]], [[Cactus Field]], [[Pineapple Patch]], [[Spider Field]], [[Strawberry
        Field]] and [[Bamboo Field]].

        Night lasts 2 minutes 35 seconds, which is the duration of the night theme (mountaincall).
        Ever since the [[Updates#2018.11.25|11/25/2018]] and [[Updates#2018.12.19|12/19/2018]]
        updates, moon-shaped platforms leading to the [[Diamond Mask]] and the platforms leading to
        [[Bubble Bee Man]] and the [[Memory Match|Night Memory Match]] are solid yellow only during
        nighttime if the player has a [[Moon Amulet]] (if the player owns the Diamond Mask, the
        platforms will be blue). When [[sprout]]s are planted at night, there is a 60% chance for it
        to be a moon sprout, with the odds of all other sprout types being altered (legendary and
        supreme sprouts become more common while the rest are rarer).

        ==Trivia==
        *If the Stick Bug Challenge ends at night, the normal music will play instead of the night
        music. If the time cycles from night to day or day to night while a [[honeystorm]] or
        [[Mythic Meteor Shower|mythic meteor shower]] is active, the fog will disappear, but the
        honeystorm or mythic meteor shower will still be active.
        *In some occasions, the nighttime OST doesn't have to end for it to be daytime, it just
        simply fades.
        *A [[Night Bell]] and [[Spirit Bear]] can manually summon nighttime.
        **[[Onett (Developer)|Onett]] can also summon nighttime, but does it globally and with a
        message (ex: \"🌙 One of [[Mother Bear]]'s cubs has summoned nighttime... 🌙\").
        ***A full gallery of these messages can be found in the [[Night Bell]] page.
