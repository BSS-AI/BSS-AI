        '''Panda Bear''' is a [[Quest Givers|Quest Giver]], and one of the eight permanent Quest
        [[Bears]] that can be accessed in the game, the others being [[Black Bear]], [[Brown Bear]],
        [[Mother Bear]], [[Science Bear]], [[Dapper Bear]], [[Polar Bear]], and [[Spirit Bear]].

        He is the fourth quest bear that the player can reach. His [[Quests]] focus on defeating
        [[Mobs]]. He is located on a small hill between the [[Blue Flower Field]] and the [[Bamboo
        Field]], right next to the [[Gumdrop Shop]]. The [[Global Top Battlers Leaderboard]] is also
        next to him. Panda Bear was a quest giver for [[Egg Hunt 2019]] and gave the
        [[Ornaments|Boxing Glove ornament]] during Beesmas (2019, 2020, 2021 and 2022).

        Panda Bear can be reached through many methods. One option is to climb the ladder on the
        side of the platform, closest to the [[Blue HQ]]. If the player has enough [[System
        Page#Jump Power|Jump Power]], they can jump onto the platform without climbing the ladder.
        Another method is to jump on the Gumdrop Shop and then up to Panda Bear. They can also use
        the [[Slingshot]]; the Slingshot sometimes undershoots and will cause the player to land
        next to Panda Bear.

        == Trivia==
        *The [[Badges#Battle Badge|Battle Badge]] set is represented by Panda Bear.
        *Panda Bear is the only bear whose quests consist solely of defeating mobs. This may be a
        reference to the Kung Fu Panda. However, his 2019, 2020 and 2021 Beesmas quest does include
        the collection of [[Pollen]].
        *Panda Bear is the second quest giver that requires the player to defeat [[Tunnel Bear]] in
        his quests; the first one being [[Onett]], the third one being Science Bear, and the fourth
        being [[Bubble Bee Man]].
        *Excluding limited-time quests, he and [[Mother Bear]] are the only bears who don't require
        collecting pollen from [[Fields]] for their quests.
        *Like many of the other quest bears, Panda Bear is aware he is in a Roblox game because, in
        the conclusion, he says \"maybe after the next update...\"
        *Panda Bear gives a total of 120 [[Ticket]]s when completing all of his quests.
        *In the quest “Spider Slayer 2”, he says the player needs to defeat 10 [[Spider]]s, but the
        quest only requires 5 to complete it.
        *If the player gave a [[Present]] to Panda Bear during [[:Category:Beesmas|Beesmas 2018]],
        he gave 5,000 [[honey]], 1 [[Royal Jelly#Specific Bee Jelly|Rage Bee jelly]], 15
        [[stinger]]s, 10 tickets, and a x4 Bamboo [[Field Boost]].
        *The quest “The REAL Final Showdown” used to require 10 werewolves defeated, but this was
        nerfed.
        *During his conclusion dialogue, he says the word \"BOPMASTER\", which is the name of a
        [[Codes|Code]] of the same name (valid). He also had a code named after him called
        \"PandaPower\" (expired).
        * In his Egg Hunt quest conclusion dialogue, Panda Bear says, \"Here's a [[Marshmallow
        Bee|marshmallow bee]]\", indicating he was only giving one. Instead, he rewarded two.
        * Panda Bear uses the [https://www.roblox.com/catalog/658839027/Ninja-Animation-Package
        Ninja Animation Package].
        *Panda Bear can use the Roblox animation /e point.
        * Panda Bear says that [[Egg#Diamond Egg|Diamond Eggs]] always hatch into
        [[Bees/Legendary|Legendary bees]]. This is because before the
        [[Updates#2019.12.23|12/23/2019 Update]], Diamond Eggs could not hatch into
        [[Bees/Mythic|Mythic bees]].
        *In the quest 'Outrageous Onslaught', Panda Bear once said that [[Ability Tokens#Rage|Rage]]
        could stack up to 5 times, which was inaccurate since the latter can only stack up to 3
        times since an Update lowered the cap.
        **His dialogue later got updated and he now says that it can stack up to 3 times.
        **Which is also inaccurate as an update raised the cap to 4.
        *He says that the Diamond Ant Amulet is the best Ant Amulet in the quest 'Winged Wack
        Attack'. However, the current highest Ant Amulet tier is the Supreme Ant Amulet.
        *Panda Bear is one of the three quest givers who rewards a Star Treat on one of his quests.
        (The others being Mother Bear and Onett.)
        *In total, he gives 131,730,750 honey.
        *In total, he requires you to defeat 2,750 Ants, 440 Flying Ants, 115 Fire Ants, 715 Army
        Ants, 160 Giant Ants, 345 Rhino Beetles, 344 Ladybugs, 140 Mantises, 95 Scorpions, 41
        Spiders, 20 Werewolves, 7 King Beetles, and 1 Tunnel Bear.
        **This is a total of 5173 mobs to defeat.
        *If you were to defeat every mob, you would have to wait a total of 1412100 seconds, which
        is 16 days, 8 hours and 15 minutes for the mobs to respawn.
        **However, if you have the Gifted Vicious Bee, this would take 1200285 seconds, which is 13
        days, 21 hours, 24 minutes, and 45 seconds.
        **With the lowest possible Monster Respawn Time of -40%, this would take 847260 seconds,
        which is 9 days, 19 hours and 21 minutes.
        ***This is achievable with Gifted Vicious Bee, a Diamond Stick Bug amulet giving -10%, and 3
        [[Icicles]] [[Beequip]]s giving -5% each for a total of -15%.
        *Panda Bear's last Ant Annihilation quest, 'Ultimate Ant Annihilation 5', contains a typo.
        Instead of \"Get a Score of 100 in the Ant Challenge,\" it says \"Get a Score of 100 in the
        AntChallenge Challenge.\"
        *In his Beesmas quest dialogue, Panda is revealed to dislike [[Robo Bear]].
        *The name of the quest \"Royal Rumble\" is a direct reference to the wrestling event of the
        same name.
