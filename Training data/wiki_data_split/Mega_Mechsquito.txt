        A '''Mega Mechsquito''' is a [[mob]] that appears during [[Robo Bear Challenge|Robo Bear's
        Challenge]].

        This is the fourth mob type a player will find while doing Robo Bear's Challenge, first
        appearing at Round 15.

        Mega Mechsquitos, like their smaller counterparts, typically hover away from the player,
        shooting at them with small projectiles that inflict the [[Buffs &
        Debuffs#Debuffs|Mechsquito Toxin]] debuff, lowering player Movespeed and pollen. However,
        Mega Mechsquitos have a longer Cooldown between attacks, but each attack consists of a
        barrage of 6 shots, each inflicting a stack of Mechsquito Toxin. Unlike Mechsquitos, Mega
        Mechsquitos have antennae, two extra body parts, and a black cog.

        A Mega Mechsquito's level can range from 13-23? depending on what round of Robo Bear's
        Challenge the player is on.

        On Round 15 of the Robo Bear Challenge, the player has to defeat 10 Mega Mechsquitos and
        collect 2,000,000,000 of a certain color of pollen to complete it.

        ==Strategies==
        * Mega Mechsquitos aim for where the player is, not where the player will be. Because of
        this, moving around in circles will often result in Mega Mechsquitos missing their attack.
        *Stand near the Mega Mechsquito, this will allow your bees to lock on to the Mechsquito and
        quickly deal damage.
        *[[Vicious Bee]] and [[Windy Bee]] provide excellent crowd control, especially since Mega
        Mechsquitos often come in pairs or packs. [[Digital Bee]] can also stun many Mega
        Mechsquitos at once and allow them to take extra damage via [[Ability Tokens#Mind Hack|Mind
        Hack]].
