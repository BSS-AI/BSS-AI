        The '''Broken Drive''' is an inventory [[Items|item]] that was introduced in the
        [[Updates|5/7/23 update]]. They can be used to activate [[Science Bear|Science Bear's]]
        quest to repair [[Robo Bear]]. After completing Science Bear's quest the Broken Drive will
        be replaced with a [[Drives#White Drive|White Drive]].

        A Broken Drive can be found behind the [[Robo Pass Dispenser]]s in the [[Bear Gate|30 Bee
        Zone]]. Unlike the other [[drives]] which can be obtained repeatedly, the Broken Drive can
        only be obtained once.

        == Trivia ==

        * The Broken Drive is the only type of drive that can't be used.
        * The Broken Drive is the only type of drive that can be obtained once, as the others can be
        repeatedly earned through the [[Robo Bear Challenge]] and bought with [[cog]]s.
        * Players do not need to fix the Broken Drive if they have previously repaired Robo Bear
        during Beesmas 2022.
