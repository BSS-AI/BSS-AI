        The '''All-Time Top Red Collectors Leaderboard''' is one of 62 [[leaderboards]] in the game.
        This leaderboard shows how much [[:Category:Red|red]] [[pollen]] players have collected over
        time. It shows 10 leaderboard positions at a time. The player can scroll down the
        leaderboard to see more leaderboard positions, all the way to 100th place. Only legitimate
        players appear on the leaderboards for lengths of time (hackers/exploiters will be removed).
        If the player steps on the white pad in front of it, the message <span
        style=\"background-color:#0090BF; color:white;\">\"You've collected \"x\" Red
        Pollen\"</span> will appear, \"x\" being the total amount of red pollen you have
        collected in total.

        ==Location==
        It is located inside the [[Red HQ]], between the ladder and the [[Dark Scythe]].
