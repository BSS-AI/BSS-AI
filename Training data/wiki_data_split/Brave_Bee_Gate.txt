        }}The '''Brave Bee Gate''', also called the '''10 Bee Gate''', is a [[:Category:Gates|gate]]
        that requires 10 [[bees]] to pass.

        It gets its name from the large [[Brave Bee]] on top of it.

        == Trivia ==
        * The player can reach the top of the [[Blue HQ]] if the gate is unlocked, by jumping off
        The ledge near [[Science Bear]]. This gives a shortcut to [[Brown Bear]], the
        [[Honeystorm|Honeystorm Summoner]], the [[Wealth Clock]], and [[Commando Chick's Hideout]].
        This could also grant access to [[Gifted Bucko Bee|Bucko Bee's]] [[Snow Machine]] during the
        Beesmas 2020, 2021 or 2022 event.
        * Formerly, the [[Slingshot]] could bypass the gate even if the player didn't have 10 bees,
        since it launched them past it.
        ** This was fixed in the [[Updates#2019.09.28|9/28/2019 update]], as the player gets
        teleported back to spawn if they don't meet the requirements to enter an area.
        * This gate doesn't have any fields in which [[Rogue Vicious Bee]] can spawn.
