        '''The Planter Of Plenty''' is a reusable planter added in the 12/26/21 update.

        {| class=\"article-table\"
        !Crafting Ingredients
        |-
        |{{Cost|Honey|{{Hover|100T|100,000,000,000,000}}}}<br>{{Cost|Magic
        Bean|500}}<br>{{Cost|Super Smoothie|100}}<br>{{Cost|Swirled
        Wax|100}}<br>{{Cost|Caustic Wax|100}}<br>{{Cost|Turpentine|25}}
        |}

        It grows 50% faster and grants 50% more pollen on harvest in the [[Pepper Patch]], [[Stump
        Field]], [[Coconut Field]], and the [[Mountain Top Field]]. [[Gifted Bee]]s are 50% more
        likely to sip [[nectar]] from this planter, and grants +50% of all Nectar Types. Harvesting
        a fully grown Planter Of Plenty has a 1/4 (25%) chance to spawn a [[Puffshroom]].
