        The '''Sticker Planter''' is a single-use [[planter]] added in the
        [[Updates#2024.01.12|01/12/2024 update]]. It grows in about 3 hours of playtime and grants
        x2 of every [[nectar]]. It is guaranteed to spawn a rare+ [[Puffshroom]]. Only 1 Sticker
        Planter can be active at a time. It has a cap of 25.

        == Ways To Obtain ==
        * As a drop from [[Sprouts|Sticker Sprouts]].
        * By purchasing it in the [[Robux Shop]] for 100 Robux. however, it requires having 20 bees
        to gain access to purchasing it and has a limit of 10 per player.
        * Completing [[Sticker-Seeker Quest Machine|Sticker-Seeker]] quests.
        ** Completing a Rank that has 3 digits with all numbers repeated will grant (Quests
        Completed)/111) sticker planters:
        *** Completing 111 Quests Guarantees a Sticker Planter.
        *** Completing 222 Quests Guarantees 2 Sticker Planters.
        *** Completing 333 Quests Guarantees 3 Sticker Planters.
        *** Completing 444 Quests Guarantees 4 Sticker Planters.
        *** Completing 555 Quests Guarantees 5 Sticker Planters.
        *** Completing 666 Quests Guarantees 6 Sticker Planters.
        *** Completing 777 Quests Guarantees 7 Sticker Planters.
        *** Completing 888 Quests Guarantees 8 Sticker Planters.
        *** Completing 999 Quests Guarantees 9 Sticker Planters.

        == Trivia ==
        * The Sticker Planter continuously alternates between different colors, which can be tuned
        down in the [[Settings]].
        * Out of the four single-use planters, the Sticker Planter is the only one of them to have a
        cap of 25.
        * This, the [[Ticket Planter]] and the [[Paper Planter]] share the same base models.
        ** They also share the same outline for their decals.
        * It is tied with the Ticket Planter for the second-best Nectar multiplier in the game,
        behind the [[Festive Planter]] at x3.
        * It is one of four single-use planters in the game, the others being the Festive Planter,
        the Ticket Planter and the Paper Planter.
        * This and the Ticket Planter are the only planters that will always spawn a [[Puffshroom]]
        at full growth.
        ** Additionally, this is the only planter to always spawn at least a Rare Puffshroom.
