        The '''Red Field Booster''' is a [[:Category:Machines|machine]] that provides a [[Field
        Boost]] to a random [[:Category:Red|red]] [[field]] for 15 minutes. The machine is located
        in the [[Red HQ]]. In order to use it, the player needs to have discovered 5 different red
        [[Bees|bee]] types. It has a cooldown of 45 minutes.

        The following audio plays when the Red Field Booster has been
        activated:[[File:Fieldbooster.ogg]]

        == Boosts ==
        *[[Mushroom Field]]: +300% [[Pollen]]
        *[[Strawberry Field]]: +100% Pollen
        *[[Rose Field]]: +100% Pollen
        *[[Pepper Patch]]: +100% Pollen (only if player has 35+ Bees)

        == Trivia ==
        * This is one of the three field boosters in the game, along with the [[Field Booster]] and
        the [[Blue Field Booster]].
        ** The Red Field Booster has the same design as the Field Booster and the Blue Field
        Booster, the only difference being the colors.
        * The Red Field Booster, unlike the Blue Field Booster, is located near the entrance of the
        [[Red HQ]], while the Blue Field Booster is located on the second floor in the [[Blue HQ]].
        * In an update, Strawberry Field boost was nerfed from 200% to 100%.
