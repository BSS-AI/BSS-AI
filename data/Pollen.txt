        '''Pollen''' is one of the main features in [[Bee Swarm Simulator]]. Pollen is collected in
        order to make [[honey]]. It can be obtained by using [[tools]] (via tapping/clicking) on
        [[flowers]], from [[bees]] collecting pollen and/or activating their [[Ability
        Tokens|abilities]], from some [[Passive Abilities|passive abilities]], from [[Mythic Meteor
        Shower|meteors]], and from [[coconut]]s. Flowers can have different sizes and different
        colors. Some [[quests]] require pollen to be collected from a specific flower color or
        field. There are many sources of pollen multipliers, including [[amulet]]s, [[bags]],
        [[masks]], [[badges]], [[Gifted Bee|hive bonuses]], abilities, [[Field Boost|field boosts]],
        [[Field Wind|field winds]], and certain passive abilities.

        Pollen is mainly used to convert it into [[honey]] at the player's [[hive]], through
        [[Instant Converter]]s, by using a [[Micro-Converter]], [[Honeysuckle]], etc. This is one of
        the most common ways to get honey. Pollen is also used as a counter to track how much a
        player has farmed from a [[field]] in [[badges]].

        == Pollen Amount Per Flower ==
        In order to get more pollen per collection, collect the biggest flowers on the field. The
        bigger the flower, the larger the pollen multiplier. There are 5 different flower sizes:
        single, double, triple, large, and star.

        Collection amount and efficiency can be increased by using better tools (either collecting
        in a large area or multiplying pollen collection. For example, the [[Scythe]] and the
        [[Bubble Wand]] each collect double red and blue pollen, respectively). Collecting [[Ability
        Tokens#Bear Morph|Bear Morph]], [[Ability Tokens#Inspire|Inspire]], [[Ability Tokens#Beesmas
        Cheer|Beesmas Cheer]], [[Ability Tokens#Baby Love|Baby Love]] ability tokens, [[Bear Bee]]
        and [[Basic Bee]] [[Gifted Bee|gifted]] bonuses, badges, using game [[mechanics]], expanding
        the hive, different [[amulet]]s, using [[items]], and different [[Passive Abilities|passive
        abilities]] can all lead to better pollen collection.

        When a flower is [[Pollination|pollinated]], it upgrades any kind of flower, with star
        flowers being the exception. Flowers can be pollinated by [[Fuzzy Bee]], The [[Passive
        Abilities|Diamond Drain]] Passive, bees with the [[Poinsettia]] or [[Lei]] beequip, and
        [[Passive Abilities#Guiding Star|Guiding Star]].

        == Trivia ==
        * Single, double, and triple flowers are the only types of flowers that can naturally spawn
        in fields. Large and star flowers can only be spawned through pollination.
        * Before the [[Updates#2020.04.06|Egg Hunt 2020 update]], only the single, double, and large
        flowers were in the game. When the Egg Hunt 2020 update was released, large flowers became
        triple flowers, and the flower images were polished to look more attractive and simplistic.
        * In real life, nectar is used by bees to be turned into honey and not pollen.
