        * Looker Bee was the first [[Bees|bee]] to have a [[Royal Jelly#Royal Jelly Variant|royal

        'ThoseEyes' (expired).
        * The [[Looker Guard]], which can be bought in the [[Pro Shop]], has a special design, name,
        and stats dedicated to Looker Bee.
        *Looker Bee used to have the same gifted hive bonus as [[Tabby Bee]] before the nerf on
        12/26/21 update
        * Looker Bee was one of the first bees that were added to the game in an [[Updates|update]],
        along with [[Commander Bee]], [[Music Bee]], and [[Photon Bee]].
        * Looker Bee and [[Stubborn Bee]] are the only Rare bees that were added after the game's
        launch.
        * Looker Bee's skin is the same as Commander Bee's skin.
        * Looker Bee is the only bee that dislikes Sunflower Field.
        *The reason Looker Bee likes the Mountain Top Field might be because high places have very
        good views from above. And the reason Looker Bee likes Clover Field could be based on the
        effort to look for a four-leafed clover in a field of clovers.
        *The reason Looker Bee dislikes the sunflower field may be because of the blinding sunlight
        from the sun that would be present in a field of sunflowers.
