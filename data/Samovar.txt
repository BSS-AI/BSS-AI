        The '''Samovar''' is a [[:Category:Machines|machine]] that was added in [[Beesmas]] 2021 and
        has returned in Beesmas 2022 and 2023. This machine can be unlocked by completing [[Dapper
        Bear]]'s Beesmas quest, and when activated, it rewards a random [[Nectar]] type and
        [[items]] near the cup. The Samovar has a cooldown of 6 hours.

        If a player stands on the activation platform with Samovar without completing Dapper Bear's
        Beesmas 2021/2022 quest, the following message will appear: \"''This strange thing is looking
        a little rusty...''\"

        == Drops ==
        <sup>''It is important to note that the Samovar lists how many times it was used
        '''previously''', not how many uses it is on currently.<br />For example: If it says
        it has been used 24 times, then you are on the 25th use.''</sup>

        Every time the Samovar is used, the amount of Nectar received will increase by 5 minutes,
        capping at 4 hours. Honeysuckles increases by 1 each use, stopping at 30 Honeysuckles.

        The Samovar has a fixed cycle of rewards: Oil -> Enzymes -> Gumdrops -> Glitter
        -> Ticket Planter. On the 25th use, the Ticket Planter is replaced with a Turpentine as a
        1 time reward (there aren't any further milestones at 50, 100, etc.). It will continue to
        cycle through the first 5 rewards afterward.

        Guaranteed:
        * [[Honeysuckle]] (Starts from 3, increases by 1 each use and caps at 30).
        * Random [[Nectar]] buff (Starts from 1 Hour, increases by 5 minutes each use and caps at 4
        hours (4 hours and 12 minutes if you have the [[Ornaments|Potpourri]] ornament)).

        Bonus drops:
        * 1x [[Oil]].
        * 1x [[Enzymes]].
        * 25x [[Gumdrops]].
        * 1x [[Glitter]].
        * 1x [[Ticket Planter]].
        * 1x [[Turpentine]] (25th use only).

        == Trivia ==
        * This and the [[Robo Bear's Party Cake|Party Cake]] are currently the only Beesmas
        decorations not added in the Beesmas 2020 event.
        * It would take 36 uses to get the max amount of nectar from this machine.
        ** It would take 27 uses to get the max amount of Honeysuckles.
        * This is the only unlimited source of [[Ticket Planter]]s.
        * The nectar collected from the Samovar will contribute to quests requiring a specific type
        of nectar.
        *The samovar would be used 284 times if you used it every 6 hours from the beginning of
        beesmas to the end of beesmas.
