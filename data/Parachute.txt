        The '''Parachute''' is a [[:Category:Gliding Tools|gliding tool]] that can be purchased at
        the [[Pro Shop]].

        {| class=\"article-table\"
        !Crafting Ingredients
        |-
        |{{Cost|Honey|500,000}}
        |}

        It allows the player to glide through the air. Its description reads, \"A parachute you can
        use to glide down the mountain and reach new places! Press jump while in the air to open.\"

        To activate it, press jump. When about to fall down, press jump again; if the player just
        jumped into the air, jump once more. If the player got airborne through some other method (a
        cannon, for example), then double-jump. To disengage, just jump. The gliding feature is
        hardcoded to the player, and they will still glide even during a lag spike (The parachute
        doesn't appear immediately).

        Though it is slower than walking, the Parachute enables the player to glide to places on the
        map that would otherwise be inaccessible through walking and jumping normally. It is vital
        to collecting many of the [[Royal Jelly|royal jelly]] and [[ticket]] tokens around the map
        and to perform several shortcuts. The Parachute can also be used to dodge [[mobs]] since
        most of them can't \"detect\" the player while they're in the air. To get the most out of the
        parachute, use the cannons to have access to a wider selection of locations to travel.

        The Parachute's gliding speed is not affected by [[Ability Tokens#Haste|haste]], [[Buffs
        & Debuffs#From Areas|haste+]], [[Ability Tokens#Bear Morph|bear morph]], or other speed
        boosts.
