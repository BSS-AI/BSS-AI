        A {{Color|Bitterberry}} is a type of [[Treats|Treat]] that, when fed to a [[Bees|bee]],
        raises its [[bond]] by 100 and has a 1/1000 to make it [[Mutation|mutated]]. The chance is
        boosted to 1/100 if the bee is [[Radiation|radioactive]]. These are not the favorite treat
        of any bee.

        == Ways to Obtain ==
        * Redeeming certain valid [[codes]]:
        ** Cubly (rewards 10 bitterberries + other stuff).
        ** BeesBuzz123 (rewards 5 bitterberries + other stuff).
        ** 15MMembers (rewards 15 bitterberries + other stuff).
        ** 1MLikes (Gives 1 bitterberry + other stuff).
        * As a stack reward for adding certain [[stickers]] to the [[Sticker Stack]]:
        ** {{Cost|Squashed Head Bear Sticker}} rewards 25 bitterberries.

        === Drops ===
        * Farming in the [[Mushroom Field]], [[Spider Field]], [[Pineapple Patch]], [[Cactus
        Field]], [[Rose Field]], [[Mountain Top Field]], and [[Pepper Patch]].
        ** Harvesting [[Leaves]] in these fields may also yield bitterberries.
        * As a guaranteed drop from [[Commando Chick]], [[Mondo Chick]], and the [[Coconut Crab]].
        * As a drop from [[Ladybug|ladybugs]], [[Spider|spiders]], [[Scorpion|scorpions]],
        [[Aphid#Rage Aphid|rage aphids]], [[Tunnel Bear]], and [[King Beetle]].
        * As a gift from a [[Cub Buddy|cub buddy]].
        * As a common drop from [[Mythic Meteor Shower|Mythic Meteors]].
        * As a drop from a Debug [[Sprout|sprout]].
        * As an uncommon drop from [[Puffshroom]]s.
        * As a drop from the {{Cost|Pesticide Planter|||0}} and {{Cost|The Planter Of Plenty|||0}}.
        ** The {{Color|Pesticide Planter}} has a chance of dropping a bonus 100 bitterberries when
        harvested at full growth in the Spider Field, Mountain Top Field, or Pepper Patch.

        === Quests ===
        * Black Bear rewards bitterberry on the following quest:
        ** Sweet and Sour gives 25 bitterberries.
        ** Weed Wacker 3 gives 25 bitterberries.
        * Brown Bear rewards bitterberry on the completion of these quests:
        ** Brown Bear's 20th quest rewards 25 bitterberries.
        ** Brown Bear's 40th quest rewards 50 bitterberries.
        ** Brown Bear's 135th quest rewards 50 bitterberries.
        ** Brown Bear's 180th quest rewards 50 bitterberries.
        ** Brown Bear's 210th quest rewards 50 bitterberries.
        * Every 20th Brown Bear quest rewards a varying amount of bitterberries.
