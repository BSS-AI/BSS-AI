        The '''Bear Gate''', also known as the '''30 Bee Gate''', is a [[:Category:Gates|gate]]
        added in the [[Updates|2018/7/11 update]] that requires 30 [[bees]] to pass through. It is
        located past the [[Mountain Top Field]], between the [[Ticket Shop]] and the Mountain Top
        [[Instant Converter]].

        It is given its name because of the orange bear head silhouette around it.

        Underneath the platform in the gate, there is a [[Egg#Diamond Egg|Diamond Egg]] token and
        [[Shadow Bear]] behind it. To reach the egg token, equip either the [[Parachute]] or the
        [[Glider]]. The player should make sure their [[Bags|bag]] is empty so they don't lose any
        [[Pollen]] or by using a [[Whirligig]] after getting the diamond egg token to escape it,
        although this is very risky if players are not fast enough.

        ==Trivia==

        * The Bear Gate is the largest gate in the game in terms of size.
        * The Bear Gate requires the second largest number of bees to pass through, only behind the
        Windy Bee Gate.
        * During [[:Category:Beesmas|Beesmas]], the Bear Gate has a gigantic star on top of it.
        * This is the only gate that does not have a [[Fields|field]] behind it. It is also the only
        gate without the requirement pad and the egg sign, and the only gate without a blue arch
        around it.
        * This gate, along with the [[Windy Bee Gate]] and the [[Ant Gate]], are the only gates in
        the game that didn't exist at launch. These three gates, along with the [[Brave Bee Gate]],
        also don’t lead to any other gate in the game.
        * This gate and the Windy Bee Gate are the only gates where buying [[Hive Slot|hive slots]]
        is a requirement to pass through them.
        * This gate, along with the Ant Gate, are only gates that do not have a bee model on top of
        it.
        * The area beyond the gate is indoors, not outdoors. This means that even though there
        appears to be an opening to the sky, it is blocked by an invisible barrier because
        otherwise, it would be accessible through the [[Red Cannon]].
        * The player used to be able to get into the Bear Gate's crown, but this has been patched.
        * There used to be a glitch where if the player respawned and ran to the red cannon, they
        could use the Cannon to enter the gate without dying if they were quick enough using the
        [https://developer.roblox.com/en-us/api-reference/class/ForceField forcefield] applied on
        respawn. This glitch has since been patched.
        * The [[Boost Market]] used to be inside the Bear Gate. It got replaced by the [[Nectar
        Pot]] on the [[Updates|26/12/2021 update]].
