        == Trivia ==
        * The light colors around Diamond Bee make it nearly translucent.
        * Diamond Bee has the highest base convert rate out of all bees, only beaten by [[Tabby
        Bee]] when its [[Tabby Love]] stack is at 626 or higher.
        * Diamond Bee and [[Honey Bee]] are the only [[Bees]] in the game which can generate Honey
        Gift or Honey Gift+ tokens.
        * This, [[Bubble Bee]], [[Fire Bee]], Honey Bee, [[Demon Bee]], and [[Gummy Bee]] are the
        only bees to have masks based on them, specifically the [[Diamond Mask]] for Diamond Bee.
        * Diamond Bee is the blue counterpart of [[Demon Bee]].
        * Diamond Bee and Gummy Bee are the only bees to have a sparkle effect.
        * Diamond Bee has a unique conversion link color (cyan/light blue). It is the only bee to
        have a conversion link that is not yellow.
        * Diamond Bee is one of only two bees to have the same name as an [[egg]] they can hatch
        from. The other being [[Basic Bee]]. Those being the [[Egg|Diamond Egg]] and [[Egg|Basic
        Egg]] respectively.
