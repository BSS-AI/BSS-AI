        The '''Beesmas Feast''' was a [[:Category:Beesmas|Beesmas]] exclusive machine. It was
        introduced in [[Updates#12.25.2020|Beesmas 2020]], returned on Beesmas 2021, and 2022. It
        could be unlocked by completing [[Polar Bear|Polar Bear's]] Beesmas quest. It was positioned
        right behind Polar Bear. It could be used every 90 minutes and granted the player [[bond]]
        for all of their [[bees]] and a variety of [[treats]]. The amount of bond and rewards were
        affected by the amount of [[Buffs & Debuffs#From NPCs|Polar Power]] the player currently
        had.

        If a player walked onto the platform without completing his quest, it would have displayed
        the message, <span style=\"background-color:#a61e19;color:white\">“An empty plate...
        where’s the Beesmas Feast, Polar Bear?”</span>

        ==Granted Items ==
        Guaranteed
        *[[Bond]] (guaranteed). The bond per bee is slightly more than (Polar Bear Quests completed)
        ^ 3/2.
        *[[Treat|Treats]] (guaranteed). There are two tokens that sum to a total slightly less than
        (Polar Bear Quests completed) ^ 3/2 treats.
        *[[Bitterberry|Bitterberries]].

        One of the following:
        *[[Strawberry|Strawberries]].
        *[[Blueberry|Blueberries]].
        *[[Pineapple|Pineapples]].
        *[[Sunflower Seed|Sunflower seeds]].
