        '''Jelly Beans''' are an [[:Category:Inventory|inventory item]] added in the
        [[Updates#2019.04.17|Egg Hunt 2019 update]]. When used, it spreads jelly beans of varying
        colors in the direction the player is facing, with each color representing a different
        [[Buffs & Debuffs|buff]]. They will turn into jelly bean tokens when they land on
        [[flowers]], making them collectable. Each buff lasts for a minute. Each jelly bean item
        spawns 11-13 jelly bean tokens.

        If multiple players are around, a different player picking up the tokens will give both the
        owner and the collector the token's buffs, as well as the Jelly Bean Sharing Bonus to the
        owner. Therefore, it is best to share jelly beans.

        The player can only hold a maximum of 100 at a time in their inventory. The player will need
        to use at least one in order to get any more unless they are obtained from [[quests]],
        purchased from any [[Robux Shop|Robux pack]], or from the [[Ornament Presents|Colorful
        Present]] (now unobtainable).

        '''''Note:''' Jelly Beans can be activated anywhere, though it is recommended to be in a
        field because if the beans do not land on a field, no tokens will be spawned.''

        ==Trivia ==
        *[[Bean Bug]]s are passive mobs that spawn in random fields. They scatter jelly bean tokens
        around their current field. They are the second and only other way of getting jelly bean
        tokens.
        *When picking up a jelly bean item token (the consumable), it will say \"You found a Jelly
        Beans!\" instead of \"You found Jelly Beans!\" or \"You found a Jelly Bean!\". This is an error.
        This was fixed at an unknown date.
        *The Merigold Jelly Bean is misspelled throughout the game. The correct spelling should be
        'Marigold'.
        *The hearts in the Jelly Bean Sharing Bonus buff are similar to the heart of the [[Ability
        Tokens#Baby Love|Baby Love ability token]], but they are not blushing.
        *Rarer jelly beans emit sparkles when being thrown into a field, and the token will also
        emit a sparkle effect.
        *Prior to the [[Updates#2019.09.28|9/28/2019 update]], Jelly Beans did not have a maximum
        capacity and players could obtain them in an unlimited amount.
        *If the player donates any number of Jelly Beans for the first time to the [[Wind Shrine]],
        it gives back 7 Jelly Beans.
        **After donating, it says \"- ( Number ) Jellybeanss\". It should be \"Jelly Beans\" with one S.
        **When claiming the tokens, it says \"+ ( Number ) Jelly Beans ( from >>☻︎ ). This is a
        clue from the [[Ready Player Two|Ready Player Two Event]].
        *If the player attempts to throw Jelly Beans in the [[Ant Field]] from outside, the beans
        will be thrown, but no tokens will spawn even if they hit flowers in the Ant Field.
        *Like [[Field Wind]]s, further Jelly Bean stacks do not add up on each other. Instead, they
        gain their own stacks of percentage.
        *The Spoiled Jelly Bean is the only jelly bean to grant a debuff.
