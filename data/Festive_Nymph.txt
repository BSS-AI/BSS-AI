        The '''Festive Nymph''' is a variant of [[Stick Nymph]] that can be summoned by [[Stick
        Bug]] during the [[Stick Bug Challenge]]. Festive Nymphs are only available after finishing
        Stick Bug's Beesmas 2020, 2021 and 2022 [[Quests|quest]] - Stick Bug's Nymph Hats. Other
        players could attack and collect rewards from Festive Nymphs. (The player who started the
        challenge must complete Stick Bug's quest first, otherwise no Festive Nymphs will appear.)
        Festive Nymphs followed the player closest to them and deal damage if they have touched a
        player, like their regular counterparts. Despite their low stats, Festive Nymphs, along with
        the Stick Nymphs, would usually be summoned in large groups, often outnumbering players.

        == Trivia ==
        * Like Stick Nymphs, Festive Nymphs can follow players out of the [[Fields|field]] they were
        in and can either be gone through or be obstructed by some objects. They can also go into
        certain locations such as the [[King Beetle's Lair]] and get into spots where they couldn't
        reach the player. This allowed the player to exploit them and deal damage from afar.
        * Just like [[Chicks#Mondo Chick|Mondo Chick]] and the stick nymphs, they lose health every
        second; Mondo Chick always loses 333 health per second, but Festive Nymph and Stick Nymph's
        health depletes faster when their level is higher. It is possible for them to be defeated
        naturally unlike Mondo Chick who would have 300 health left after 15 minutes. They would
        drop rewards regardless if they have been attacked or not.
        * Onett has also summoned the Stick Bug Challenge with Festive Nymphs.
        * Festive Nymphs are unable to jump, so if the player got higher than the Festive Nymph's
        hitbox, they would not be able to damage them.
        * Festive Nymphs and Stick Nymphs are the only [[mobs]] that can be summoned by another mob.
        ** These are two of 18 mobs that cannot spawn naturally, as in the player cannot simply walk
        in a field and fight it, the other mobs being the five types of [[Ants]], [[Snowbear]],
        Stick Bug, the five [[Robo Bear Challenge]] Mobs, and the four [[Robo Party]] mobs.
