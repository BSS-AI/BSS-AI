        ==Critical Chance ==
        '''Critical Chance''' is the chance of a hit being critical hit. The player can see how much
        their Critical Chance is by looking for the Critical Chance stat in the [[System Page]].
        Critical Chance can be improved by a variety of ways such as [[Ability Tokens|ability
        tokens]], [[:Category:Guards|guards]], and [[:Category:Inventory|inventory items]].

        ==== Tokens ====
        * [[Ability Tokens#Focus|Focus]] gives +3% Critical Chance per token and caps at +30%
        Critical Chance from 10 tokens.
        * [[Ability Tokens#Target Practice|Precise Mark]] from the [[Ability Tokens#Target
        Practice|Target Practice]] ability gives +7% Critical Chance per token and caps at +21%
        Critical Chance from three tokens.

        ==== Guards ====
        * The [[Looker Guard]] gives +1% Critical Chance.
        * The [[Elite Red Guard]] gives +5% Critical Chance.
        * The [[Riley Guard]] gives +8% Critical Chance.
        * The [[Crimson Guard]] gives +10% Critical Chance.

        ==== [[Amulet]]s ====
        * [[Star Amulet]]s:
        ** The Bronze, Silver, Gold, and Diamond Star Amulet can give from +1% Critical Chance up to
        +5% Critical Chance.
        ** The Supreme Star Amulet can give from +1% Critical Chance up to +7% Critical Chance.
        * [[Ant Amulet]]s:
        ** The Bronze, Silver, Gold, Diamond, and Supreme Ant Amulet can give up to +3% Critical
        Chance.

        ==== [[Gifted Bee#List of Hive Bonuses|Gifted Hive Bonuses]] ====
        * Gifted [[Commander Bee]] gives +3% Critical Chance.

        ==== [[Beequip]]s ====
        * Individual Bee:
        ** A [[Bottle Cap]] gives from +1% Critical Chance to +2% Critical Chance. Using [[waxes]]
        can increase the amount up to +12% Critical Chance.
        ** A [[Charm Bracelet]] gives from +1% Critical Chance to +14% Critical Chance.
        ** A [[Bang Snap]] gives from +1% Critical Chance to +3% Critical Chance. Using waxes can
        increase the amount up to +23% Critical Chance.
        ** [[Pink Shades]] can give from +22% Critical Chance to +26% Critical Chance. Using waxes
        can increase the amount up to +50% Critical Chance.
        ** A [[Beesmas Top]] gives from +5% Critical Chance to +10% Critical Chance. Using waxes can
        increase the amount up to +20% Critical Chance.
        ** A [[Beesmas Tree Hat]] gives from +2% Critical Chance to +5% Critical Chance. Using waxes
        can increase the amount up to +10% Critical Chance.
        ** A [[Snow Tiara]] gives +1% Critical Chance.
        ** A [[Toy Drum]] gives +1% Critical Chance.

        ==== [[Buffs & Debuffs|Buffs]] ====
        * Using a [[Tropical Drink]] gives +5% Critical Chance.
        * Using a [[Super Smoothie]] gives +6% Critical Chance. The buff can also be given from
        certain codes.
        * Collecting [[Jelly Beans|green jelly beans]] gives +3% Critical Chance per jelly bean and
        caps and +5% Critical Chance from 3 jelly beans.

        ==== [[Robo Bear Challenge]] ====
        There are several upgrades that can be bought with [[cog]]s during the Robo Bear Challenge
        which can upgrade Critical Chance.

        * Rare Upgrades:
        ** Commit: This upgrade starts at +5% Critical Chance and maxes at 2 upgrades with +10%
        Critical Chance.
        ** Virus: This upgrade starts at +1% Critical Chance and maxes at 3 upgrades with +3%
        Critical Chance.
        * Epic Upgrades:
        ** RGB: This upgrade gives 1% Critical Chance.
        * Legendary Upgrades:
        ** Pseudo-RNG: This upgrade gives 3% Critical Chance.

        ==== Debuffs ====
        There are certain upgrades that can be bought with cogs during the Robo Bear Challenge which
        can downgrade Critical Chance.

        * Rare Upgrades:
        ** SSD: Blue: This upgrade starts at -3% Critical Chance and maxes at 3 upgrades with -6%
        Critical Chance.
        * Epic Upgrades:
        ** FOV: This upgrade gives -2% Critical Chance.
        ** Normalize: This upgrade gives x0 Critical Chance.

        == Critical Power ==
        '''Critical Power''' is how strong a critical hit can be. The player can see how much their
        Critical Power is by looking for the Critical Power stat in the System Page. Critical Power
        can be improved by a variety of ways such as ability tokens, guards, and beequips.

        ==== Tokens ====
        * [[Ability Tokens#Melody|Melody]] gives +100% Critical Power.

        ==== Guards ====
        * The [[Looker Guard]] gives +25% Critical Power.
        * The [[Looker Guard|Elite Blue Guard]] gives +50% Critical Power.
        * The [[Bucko Guard]] gives +75% Critical Power.
        * The [[Cobalt Guard]] gives +100% Critical Power.

        ==== Amulets ====
        * Ant Amulets:
        ** The Bronze, Silver, Gold, Diamond, and Supreme Ant Amulet can give up to +50% Critical
        Power.

        ==== Gifted Hive Bonuses ====
        * Gifted [[Looker Bee]] gives +25% Critical Power.
        * Gifted [[Tabby Bee]] gives +50% Critical Power.

        ==== Beequips ====
        *Individual Bee:
        **A Thumbtack gives from +5% Critical Power to +10% Critical Power. Using waxes can increase
        the amount up to +40% Critical Power.
        **A Camo Bandana gives from +1% Critical Power to +6% Critical Power.
        **A Bottle Cap can give from +5% Critical Power to +10% Critical Power. Using waxes can
        increase the amount up to +60% Critical Power.
        **A Kazoo gives from +2% Critical Power to +5% Critical Power. Using waxes can increase the
        amount up to +65% Critical Power.
        **A Whistle gives from +15% Critical Power to +25% Critical Power. Using waxes can increase
        the amount up to +85% Critical Power.
        **Pink Shades gives from +29% Critical Power to +49% Critical Power.
        *Hive Bonus:
        **A Bottle Cap can give from +2% Critical Power to +15% Critical Power from waxing.
        **A Kazoo can give from +2% Critical Power to +5% Critical Power. Using waxes can increase

        **A Beesmas Top can give from +2% Critical Power to +5% Critical Power. Using waxes can
        increase the amount up to +15% Critical Power.

        ==== Robo Bear Challenge ====
        There are several upgrades that can be bought with [[cog]]s during the Robo Bear Challenge
        which can upgrade Critical Power.
        *Rare Upgrades:
        **Nullify: This upgrade starts at x1.5 Critical Power and maxes at 2 upgrades with x2
        Critical Power.
        *Epic Upgrades:
        **Demarcate: This upgrade makes Mark Tokens give x1.03 Critical Power for 15 seconds which
        can stack up to 10 times at x1.3 Critical Power.

        ==== [[Badges]] ====
        *The [[Badges#Battle Badge|Battle Badge]] can give up to +100% Critical Power depending on
        the badge rank.
        **The Cadet Battle Badge gives +10% Critical Power.
        **The Hotshot Battle Badge gives +20% Critical Power.
        **The Ace Battle Badge gives +40% Critical Power.
        **The Master Battle Badge gives +70% Critical Power.
        **The Grandmaster Battle Badge gives +100% Critical Power.

        ==== Debuffs ====
        There are certain upgrades that can be bought with cogs during the Robo Bear Challenge which
        can downgrade Critical Power.

        * Common Upgrades:
        ** Defragment: This upgrade starts at x0.9 Critical Power and maxes at 10 upgrades with x0.5
        Critical Power.
        * Epic Upgrades:
        ** Client-Side: This upgrade gives x0.75 Critical Power.

        ==Super-Crit==
        When performing a critical hit, there is a chance to perform a '''Super-Crit''' (purple
        version of a critical hit) which doubles the pollen collection while also instantly
        converting the pollen collected, while for the attack damage is increased by 33% of the
        Super-Crit's pollen. The frequency for a Super-Crit is increased with higher Super-Crit
        Chance, and their strength is increased by greater Super-Crit Power.

        === Super-Crit Chance ===
        '''Super-Crit Chance''' is how often a critical hit will be a Super-Crit. The player can see
        how much their Super-Crit Chance is by looking for the Super-Crit Chance stat in the System
        Page. Super-Crit Chance can be improved by several ways.

        ==== Tokens ====
        * The [[Buffs & Debuffs#Precision|Precision]] buff granted from the [[Ability
        Tokens#Target Practice|Target Practice]] ability gives +2% per token and caps at +20% from
        ten tokens.
        * The [[Buffs & Debuffs#Precise Mark|Precise Mark]] buff from the Target Practice
        ability gives +7% Super-Crit Chance per token and caps at +21% Super-Crit Chance from three
        tokens.
        * The [[Buffs & Debuffs#Tabby Blessing+|Tabby Blessing+]] buff, granted from collecting
        [[Ability Tokens#Tabby Love|Tabby Love]] from a [[Gifted Bee|Gifted]] [[Tabby Bee]] at the
        cap for the buff, grants +1% Super-Crit Chance.

        ==== Gifted Hive Bonuses ====

        * Gifted [[Precise Bee]] gives +3% Super-Crit Chance.

        ==== Buffs ====

        * Using a [[Super Smoothie]] gives +1% Super-Crit Chance. The buff can also be given from
        certain codes.

        ==== Beequips ====
        * Individual Bee:
        ** Pink Shades give from +1% Super-Crit Chance to +15% Super-Crit Chance. Using waxes can
        increase the amount up to +24% Super-Crit Chance.
        * Hive Bonus:
        ** Pink Shades can give from +1% Super-Crit Chance to +2% Super-Crit Chance.

        ==== Robo Bear Challenge ====
        * Legendary Upgrades:
        ** Bruteforce: This upgrade starts gives +6% Super-Crit Chance.

        === Super-Crit Power ===
        '''Super-Crit Power''' is how strong a Super-Crit will be. The player can see how much their
        Super-Crit Power is by looking for the Super-Crit Power stat in the System Page. Super-Crit
        Power can be improved by several ways.

        ==== Amulets ====

        * [[Cog Amulet]]s:
        ** The Diamond Cog Amulet can give from +10% Super-Crit Power up to +12% Super-Crit Power.
        ** The Supreme Cog Amulet can give from +15% Super-Crit Power up to +25% Super-Crit Power.
        ==== Buffs ====

        * Dark Heat made from dark flames using the [[Dark Scythe]] gives x1.05 Super-Crit Power
        with each additional dark flame granting x0.05 Super-Crit Power.

        ==== Beequips ====
        * Individual Bee:
        ** A Camo Bandana can give up to +14% Super-Crit Power.
        * Hive Bonus:
        ** A Whistle can give from +1% Super-Crit Power to +20% Super-Crit Power from waxing.
        ** Pink Shades from +1% Super-Crit Power to +5% Super-Crit Power. Using waxes can increase
        the amount up to +9% Super-Crit Power.

        ==== Robo Bear Challenge ====
        * Legendary Upgrades:
        ** Pseudo-RNG: This upgrade gives x1.25 Super-Crit Power.
