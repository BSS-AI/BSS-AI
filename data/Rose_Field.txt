        This field can spawn [[honey]], [[Strawberry|strawberry]], [[Ability Tokens#Boost|red
        boost]], [[Red Extract|red extract]], [[Bitterberry|bitterberry]], and [[Magic Bean|magic
        bean]] tokens when collecting [[pollen]].

        [[Bees]] that like the Rose Field are [[Rad Bee]], [[Rascal Bee]], [[Rage Bee]], [[Riley
        Bee]], [[Precise Bee]], [[Crimson Bee]], and [[Vicious Bee]]. Bees that dislike the Rose
        Field are [[Puppy Bee]], [[Stubborn Bee]], [[Bucko Bee]], [[Demo Bee]], [[Baby Bee]], and
        [[Diamond Bee]].

        This field gives Motivating [[Nectar]] when a planter is placed here.

        A level 5-7 [[Rogue Vicious Bee]] can spawn here.

        == Obtaining Boosts ==
        [[File:Fis_rose.png|thumb|75px|The Rose Field Boost icon.]]
        * Using the [[Red Field Booster]] may give a Rose [[Field Boost]]. When it does, it will
        give +100% Rose Field pollen for 15 minutes.
        * Using [[glitter]] will add 100% Rose Field pollen onto the current boost for 15 minutes if
        the boost isn't already maxed out.
        * Using [[Field Dice|field dice]], [[Smooth Dice|smooth dice]], or [[Loaded Dice|loaded
        dice]] may add +100% - 300% Rose Field pollen for 15 minutes based on the type used and
        based on its current stack before getting another one.
        * Rose [[Field Wind]]s can be obtained through the [[Wind Shrine]] or [[codes]]. They have a
        base of +20% instant Rose Field conversion, and give +5% instant Rose Field conversion and
        +15% Rose Field pollen for each stack. Rose Field Winds can stack up to a total of 13 times.
        * Redeeming certain valid codes:
        ** Discord100k (Gives Rose Field Boost x3 + other stuff).
        ** CarmenSanDiego (Activates Rose Field Code Buff + other stuff).
        ** 1MLikes (Gives a x1 Field Boost for all Fields + other stuff).
        ** PlushFriday (Gives Rose Field Code Buff + other stuff).

        == Trivia ==
        * This is one of six fields that [[fireflies]] travel to, with the others being [[Bamboo
        Field]], [[Cactus Field]], [[Pineapple Patch]], [[Spider Field]], and [[Strawberry Field]].
        * Vicious Bee likes this field most likely because roses commonly have thorns on their
        stems.
        ** It is also the only blue bee to like a field of the opposite color. The only other bee to
        share this trait is [[Festive Bee]] and [[Pine Tree Forest]].
        * This field was one of the fields which has a small section outside of the map.
        * There is a [[Royal Jelly|royal jelly]] token next to this field in a small ledge, which
        can be accessed by walking a small path along the [[Badge Bearer's Guild]] wall.
        * This is the only field past the Honey Bee Gate to not be defended by the [[Werewolf]].
        * After the [[Updates#2019.09.28|9/28/2019 update]], treat tokens could naturally spawn in
        this field.
        *This is the only field to be at a lower elevation than the gate leading to it.
        *If a Rogue Vicious Bee spawns in this field, it can attack players inside the Red HQ with
        its spikes.
