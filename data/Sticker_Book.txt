        ==Sticker Index==
        The Sticker Index shows how many stickers you have added to the [[Sticker Stack]] and in
        your Sticker Book (denoted by a ✅) on the sticker icon. When clicking on a sticker, it says
        how many of that sticker you have, if you put it in the Sticker Stack or not, and the ways
        of obtaining the sticker. The index is separated into several categories: Cub Skins, Hive
        Skins, and Hive Stickers. There are 9 Cub Skins, 6 Hive Skins, and 254 Hive Stickers to
        obtain making a total of 269 stickers in the index currently.

        ==Sticker Inbox==
        The Sticker Inbox works similarly to the [[Beequip]] Inbox, showing how many slots of the
        inbox are used and how long until a specific sticker is deleted. When the sticker book is
        full, any stickers that you gain go into the Sticker Inbox. The capacity of the Sticker
        Inbox is 200 and stickers can stay in the inbox for up to 48 hours before being
        automatically discarded (meaning you lose the sticker). By clicking on a sticker on a
        sticker, you can claim it (only if your sticker book is not full) or delete it permanently.
