        The '''Sticker-Seeker''' is a [[Tools|tool]] that was released on the 1/12/2024 update and
        can be crafted in the [[Hub Field Shop]].
        {| class=\"article-table\"
        !Crafting Ingredients
        |-
        |{{Cost|Honey|7,000,000}}<br>{{Cost|Glue|1}}<br>{{Cost|Oil|1}}<br>{{Cost|Soft
        Wax|5}}<br>{{Cost|Neonberry|5}}<br>{{Cost|Micro-Converter|10}}
        |}

        The '''Sticker-Seeker''' collects 10 pollen from 13 patches in 1 second. It has a chance to
        find [[Sticker]]s in fields and unlocks the Sticker-Seeker quest. When you equip the
        Sticker-Seeker, you have the ability to find [[Sticker#Seeker Stickers|Seeker-Stickers]].
        Once you click on a Seeker-Sticker, there will be a message on the bottom-right hand corner
        saying, \"🔎You found a Seeker-Sticker! ({amount} to go)\"

        The Sticker-Seeker has a chance to dig up a sticker in the field you are in. Once you dig it
        up, like the Seeker-Sticker, it will say, \"Oh! Your '''Sticker-Seeker''' has dug up
        something weird.\" The sticker appears as a rainbow token on a random flower on the field
        you're in.

        ==Trivia==
        *Not counting the Seeker-Stickers that appear when equipping this tool, the Sticker-Seeker
        is the only tool in the game with no associated tool sticker. This is ironic considering
        that the secondary function of this tool revolves around locating stickers.
        *This tool bears a striking resemblance to a [[Wikipedia:Magnifying glass|magnifying
        glass]].
        *If the ingredients for the item were to be crafted, a total of 7 Million Honey, 50
        Gumdrops, 300 Sunflower Seeds, 25 Honeysuckles, 250 Pineapples, 170 Royal Jellies, 5
        Neonberries and 10 Micro-Converters are needed.
