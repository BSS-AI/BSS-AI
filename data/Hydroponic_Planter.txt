        The '''Hydroponic Planter''' is a reusable [[Planter]]. It grows in 12 in-game hours and
        stores around 10 billion pollen, as well as granting bonus [[Blue Extract]]s and [[Oil]]s.
        It is purchasable in the [[Blue HQ]]. It requires the player to complete 150 [[Gifted Bucko
        Bee]] quests for it to be unlocked.

        When planted in a field, it grows 50% faster in fields with blue flowers. Blue bees are 50%
        more likely to sip [[Nectar]] while red bees are 50% less likely to sip. This planter grants
        40% more Comforting and Refreshing Nectar. Harvesting a fully-grown Hydroponic Planter has a
        1/6 (16.66%) chance of spawning a [[Puffshroom]].

        The Hydroponic Planter drops extra Blue Extracts in the [[Blue Flower Field]], the [[Bamboo
        Field]], the [[Pine Tree Forest]], and the [[Stump Field]].

        * This and the [[Heat-Treated Planter]] had models introduced in Beesmas 2021, but they
        weren't added as an obtainable item until Beesmas 2022.
        *This is the blue counterpart of the [[Heat-Treated Planter]], and its white counterpart is
        the [[Petal Planter]].
        *This is one of the five reusable planters to reside outside of [[Dapper Bear's Shop]], the
        others being the [[Red Clay Planter]], [[Blue Clay Planter]], [[Petal Planter]], and
        [[Heat-Treated Planter]].
        **This and the [[Blue Clay Planter]] are the only planters to be sold from the Blue HQ.
        *If the ingredients for it were to be crafted, it would cost 750B (750,000,000,000) Honey,
