        This field can produce [[honey]], [[Ability Tokens#Boost|red boost]], blue boost,
        [[ticket]], [[glue]], [[glitter]], [[Magic Bean|magic bean]], and [[Treat|treat]] tokens
        when collecting [[pollen]].

        Bees that like the Dandelion Field are [[Baby Bee]], [[Bomber Bee]], [[Demo Bee]],
        [[Exhausted Bee]], [[Music Bee]], [[Stubborn Bee]], [[Windy Bee]], [[Fuzzy Bee]], and
        [[Digital Bee]]. Bees that dislike the Dandelion Field are [[Commander Bee]], [[Brave Bee]],
        and [[Vicious Bee]].

        This field gives Comforting [[Nectar]] when a planter is placed here.

        A level 1-5 [[Wild Windy Bee]] can spawn here.

        == Obtaining Boosts ==
        [[File:Fis_dandelion.png|thumb|75px|The Dandelion Field boost icon.]]

        *Using the [[Field Booster]] may give a Dandelion [[Field Boost]]. When it does, it will
        give +300% Dandelion Field pollen for 15 minutes.
        * Using [[glitter]] in the field will add 100% Dandelion Field pollen onto the current boost
        if it isn't already maxed out.
        * Using [[Field Dice|field dice]], [[Smooth Dice|smooth dice]], or [[Loaded Dice|loaded
        dice]] may add +100%-300% Dandelion Field pollen for 15 minutes based on the dice used if
        the current boost isn't already maxed out.
        * Dandelion [[Field Wind]]s can be obtained through the [[Wind Shrine]] or [[codes]]. They
        have a base of 20% instant Dandelion Field conversion and give +5% instant Dandelion Field
        conversion and +25% Dandelion Field pollen for each stack. Dandelion Field Winds can stack
        up to a total of 13 times.
        *Redeeming certain valid codes:
        **Sure (Gives Dandelion Field Boost x3 + other stuff).
        **Wink (Gives Dandelion Field Boost x7 + other stuff). '''Note that because of the field
        boost nerf, the boost will only be x4.'''
        **Jumpstart (Activates Dandelion Field Code Buff + other stuff).
        **PlushFriday (Gives Dandelion Field Code Buff + other stuff).
        *It can be boosted by the [[Shell Amulet]]. If it has a Dandelion Field Boost, it can boost
        the field by +5% to +30% depending on the amulet rarity.

        ===The methods below are no longer possible.===

        *Giving [[present]]s to certain [[Quest Givers|NPCs]]:

        ==Trivia==

        *Before the [[Updates#2019.09.28|9/28/2019 update]], the Dandelion Field was the only field
        that was mob-proof, meaning no mobs could spawn/attack there.
        *There is a [[Royal Jelly|royal jelly]] token on top of the tallest dandelion which can be
        obtained by using the [[Parachute]]/[[Glider]] with cannons or from a high place.
        **This is also the only dandelion in the field that has collision at its top.
        *The [[Bubble Wand]] model on the [[Blue HQ]] can grow a face and blow [[sparkles]] onto
        this field, along with the [[Mushroom Field]], [[Clover Field]], and [[Blue Flower Field]].
        *The [[All-Time Top Honeymakers|All-Time Top Honeymakers Leaderboard]] is adjacent to the
        Dandelion Field.
        *There used to be a [[Glitches|glitch]] where [[Stick Bug]] could fall from the tallest
        mushroom and land in the Dandelion Field. This has now been patched.
        *During the Ready Player Two event, the field can be seen glitching after using [[the
        Computer]].
        *[[Onett]] threatens to remove every field but the Dandelion Field if the [[bees]] and
        [[bears]] don't stop complaining in his Star Journey 2 quest dialogue. He then follows this
        up stating he wouldn't really do this though.
