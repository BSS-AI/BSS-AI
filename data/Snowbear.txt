        }}The '''Snowbear''' is a [[:Category:Mini Bosses|Mini-Boss]] that can be summoned every 90
        minutes (1 hour and 30 minutes) at the [[Snowbear Summoner]] after completing [[Panda
        Bear]]'s Beesmas quest. When summoned, it spawns in the [[Spider Field]], and players have
        60 seconds (1 minute) to defeat it. If 60 seconds has passed without enough damage done to
        it, it will despawn (melt). All players' bees can attack another player's Snowbear, but the
        player that summoned it would receive more rewards.
        == Attacks ==
        Snowbear has two attacks:
        * Contact &ndash; If a player makes contact with Snowbear, it deals a base damage of 36.
        Snowbear does not track players; instead he moves randomly through the field.
        * Snowball Hurl &ndash; Snowbear hurls a snowball at a player who is in, or around, the
        [[Spider Field]]. This snowball deals 60 base damage and applies the [[Buffs &
        Debuffs#Debuffs|\"Frozen\" Debuff]] to the player who gets hit by the snowball for
        approximately 2.5 seconds, applying ×0 [[System Page#Movespeed|player movespeed]] and
        [[System Page#Jump Power|Jump Power]], as well as ×0.2 [[Stats#Speed|Bee Movespeed]].
        [[File:Frozen.png|thumb|90px|The Frozen effect icon. (Ice Cube's Asset)]]

        Every time Snowbear loses a quarter of its health, it drops {{Cost|Snowflake}} around the
        Snowbear's current position, which only the player that summoned it can pick up (they can
        also be picked up with Token Links). Snowflakes can also spawn in the field randomly,
        instances of which aren't unique to each player.

        ==Strategies==
        Every time the player defeats the Snowbear, its health increases (e.g. 20,000 health at
        level 5), thus making it stronger and harder to defeat in a short amount of time. High-level
        bees that can do a good amount of damage are recommended.

        Its reload time for using a snowball is short, so if it catches the player in ice, it may be
        able to keep them frozen by using the snowball attack on them repeatedly. The snowball will
        still do damage while the player is frozen, and the player will die if they get hit multiple
        times. If they have a [[glider]] or [[parachute]], they can use it to dodge the snowballs.
        If they get frozen, immediately after thawing, they can jump away and use the glider to
        prevent being frozen a second time. It is also a good idea to try and stay behind the
        Snowbear, as it takes time for it to turn around.

        It is also recommended to have another player have their bees attack a high-leveled
        Snowbear.

        ==Tips==
        *Use {{Cost|Oil}} to increase the [[System Page#Bee Movespeed|Movement Speed]] of [[Bees]],
        and {{Cost|Tropical Drink}} to increase [[Critical Hits|Critical Chance]]. {{Cost|Super
        Smoothie}} are also an option, as they increase both stats. However, they are expensive, so
        it is not recommended to use them unless the Snowbear is very high-level.
        *Certain items like [[Demon Mask]] and [[Fire Mask]]'s passive abilities, [[Windy Bee]]'s
        tornado ability, [[Frog]]s and [[Spicy Bee]]'s [[Ability Tokens#Inferno|Inferno]] ability
        ignore enemy levels meaning they will always deal damage regardless of the Snowbear's level.
        * Have multiple bees with high attack such as [[Lion Bee]].
        * Use {{Cost|Stinger}} to multiply the bee's attack by ×1.5 for 30 seconds.
        ** If you have the [[Passive Abilities#Star Saw|Star Saw]] passive, you can use it to deal
        more damage.
        * Bees that can generate battle ability tokens are a must-have.
        ** It is recommended to have multiple [[Rage Bee]]s or Spicy Bees, in order to get more
        [[Ability Tokens#Rage|Rage Tokens]].
        ** Vicious Bee is important, as it can deal massive damage with [[Ability
        Tokens#Impale|Impale]].
        ** Digital Bee is useful, as it can stun and make it take more damage with its [[Ability
        Tokens|Mind Hack]] ability.
        ** Bees that can generate [[Ability Tokens#Focus|Focus]] tokens, such as [[Looker Bee]]s,
        [[Commander Bee]]s, [[Music Bee]]s, and [[Puppy Bee]] (with [[Reindeer Antlers]]) can help
        greatly.
        ** Music Bee can generate [[Ability Tokens#Melody|Melody]], which increases critical power.
        Melody can also be generated by bees that have the {{Cost|Toy Horn|||0}} or the
        {{Cost|Kazoo|||0}} equipped.
        ** [[Precise Bee]]s generate [[Ability Tokens#Target Practice|Target Practice]] tokens,
        which grants [[Buffs & Debuffs#From Ability Tokens|Precision]], increasing [[System
        Page#Super-Crit Chance|Super-Crit Chance]].
        * High-leveled bees are useful for landing hits on high-leveled Snowbears.
        ** Precise Bees can be very useful against high-leveled Snowbears, as their attacks never
        miss if the Snowbear is one level higher (two if Gifted).
        * The Snowball attack will land sooner than the actual snowball, so to prevent being frozen
        by seemingly nothing, try to keep moving while remaining in range of the Snowbear.
        ** In addition, if a snowball comes your way, do not run away in the direction of the
        snowball, as you will likely be frozen. Instead, run towards the left or the right of it.
        * [[Gifted Bee]]s have 50% more attack than normal bees, making them useful for fighting
        Snowbears.
        * Having other players in the server help the player is also a viable strategy.
        * Use Gifted Bees that increase bee attack, critical chance, critical power, super-crit
        chance and bee movespeed, such as Gifted Looker Bee, Gifted [[Brave Bee]], Gifted Rage Bee,
        Gifted Commander Bee, Gifted [[Ninja Bee]], Gifted Lion Bee, Gifted [[Tabby Bee]] and Gifted
        [[Precise Bee]].
        * Use {{Cost|Jelly Beans}} to get the green Jelly Bean token, which boosts Critical Chance.
        * Equip accessories that can boost the attack of the player's bees, primarily guards and
        masks, such as [[Fire Mask]] and [[Demon Mask]].
        * Use [[Onett's Lid Art]], [[Honeyday Candles]], [[Gummy Beacon]], and the [[Honey Bee
        (NPC)|Honey Bee]]'s [[Honey Wreath]] as three of the Beesmas decorations summon 3 extra bees
        and the Honey Wreath will summon 1 extra bee for a certain amount of time.
        * With the [[Coconut Canister]], a player can intentionally activate the [[Passive
        Abilities#Emergency Coconut Shield|Emergency Coconut Shield]] to gain a ×1.25 bee attack
        boost for 10 seconds while also being immune to damage. Do note that having the Emergency
        Coconut Shield active does not make a player immune to freezing.
        * Try to stack battle buffs such as focus and precision to their maximum before spawning the
        Snowbear.

        ==Trivia==
        *Snowbear uses the Cartoony Animation Pack.
        *The icon for the Frozen debuff is the asset for [[w:c:battlefordreamisland:Ice Cube|Ice
        Cube]] from the animated show [[w:c:battlefordreamisland:Battle for Dream Island
        (series)|Battle for Dream Island]].
        *Snowbear and [[Stick Bug]] are the only hostile mobs that can be directly summoned by
        players. They are also the only mobs whose cooldowns are not affected by [[Vicious
        Bee|Gifted Vicious Bee's]] Gifted Hive Bonus.
        *Snowbear is the fourth mob to be removed from the game, after the [[Chicks|Chick]], Spotted
        Chick, and Hostage Chick.
        **It's also the only mob to return after being removed.
        *Snowbear's leveling up mechanic work similarly to Stick Bug and [[Wild Windy Bee]].
        **It's also the only mob that has a changing size, which also increases by leveling. This
        can make it the largest mob in the game.
        *Snowbear is the first and only mob able to freeze a player.
        *Snowbear and [[Tunnel Bear]] are the only hostile mobs that are bears.
        *Similar to [[Tunnel Bear]] and [[Shadow Bear]], Snowbear has no face and uses one texture.
        *This is the fourth mob to not drop [[Ability Tokens#Honey Gift|Honey Tokens,]] with the
        first three being the [[Coconut Crab]], [[ants]] and the [[Cave Monster|cave monsters]].
        *This, the [[Naughty List]] (plus the quest related with it), [[Festive Nymph]]s, and
        [[snowflake]]s were the only Beesmas 2020 content that was extended.
        *Although most stuff in the game refers to this mob as '''Snowbear''', the message the
        player gets when they fail to defeat Snowbear says \"'''Snow Bear'''\".
        *This, [[Tunnel Bear]] and [[Shadow Bear]] are the only faceless bears.
        *There was a glitch which causes the Snowbear to go idle, and it does not move around or
        attack the player. The cause of this glitch is unknown.
        * Snowbear has its own [[Cub Buddy]] skin, Snow Cub which is the final reward for [[Bee
        Bear]]'s 2021 [[Beesmas]] quest.
        **However, unlike its counterpart, the Snow Cub skin has a face and a carrot for a nose, and
        uses the Ninja Animation Pack.
        * There is a glitch which causes Snowbear to spawn inside the mushroom field. The cause of
        this glitch is unknown.
        * Snowbear's top hat is a retextured version of the
        '''[https://www.roblox.com/catalog/80293983/Psychedelic-Top-Hat Psychedelic Top Hat]'''.
