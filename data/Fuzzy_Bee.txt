        * '''[[Ability Tokens#Fuzz Bombs|[Fuzz Bombs]]]''' Spawns 2 (+1 every 5 bee lvls) fuzz bombs
        that wander the [[Fields|Field]] for 8 seconds. Catching them collects pollen and
        [[Pollination|Pollinates]] nearby [[Flowers]]. The amount of pollen collected scales with
        buzz bomb multipliers and bee Level.
        * '''[[Ability Tokens#Bomb|[Buzz Bomb+]]]''' Collects 7 pollen from 29 surrounding flowers

        * '''[[Ability Tokens#Pollen Haze|[🌟Gifted Ability: Pollen Haze]]]''' Summons a yellow haze

        flower growth rate. Bomb Tokens within the haze are automatically collected and pollinate
        flowers. If there has been a Pollen Haze over the field within the last 3 minutes, this
        summons fuzz bombs instead.
        * '''[[Passive Abilities#Fuzzy Coat|[Passive: Fuzzy Coat]]]''' This bee may pollinate up to
        5 nearby flowers (9 if gifted) every time it gathers. Pollinated flowers are temporarily
        upgraded to grant and store more pollen and grow faster. Lower tier flowers have a higher
        chance of being pollinated.

        == Trivia ==
        * This bee, [[Buoyant Bee]], and [[Precise Bee]] are first Mythic bees to be added since the
        release of the original Mythic bees.
        ** It's also the only Mythic Bee to be introduced on its own.
        * This bee is currently the only bee that can pollinate flowers without the help of the
        [[Poinsettia]].
        * This bee may like the Dandelion Field because [[Wikipedia:Taraxacum|Dandelions]] in real
        life are soft and fuzzy.
        * This bee, [[Shy Bee]], [[Ninja Bee]], and [[Windy Bee]] are the only bees with one skin
        color.
        * This bee, [[Spicy Bee]], [[Tadpole Bee]], [[Buoyant Bee]] and [[Digital Bee]] all have
        four different [[Ability Tokens|Abilities]], the most for any [[Bees|Bee]] in the game.
        * The [[Egg#Specific Bee Eggs|Fuzzy Bee Egg]] was the fifth specific bee egg that can/could
        have been bought for [[Robux Shop|Robux]], after [[Bear Bee]], [[Festive Bee]], Windy Bee,
        and [[Puppy Bee]] and before Precise Bee and Buoyant Bee.
        ** Fuzzy Bee was the first non-Event bee that can be purchased with Robux.
        * Fuzz bombs can be popped by using petal Shurikens from the [[Petal Wand]] and [[Passive
        Abilities#Petal Storm|Petal Storm]], Windy Bee's [[Ability Tokens#Tornado|tornado]], and
        [[Puppy Bee|Puppy Bee's]] [[Ability Tokens#Fetch|fetch ball]].
        ** Any player can pop fuzz bombs, even if they were summoned by another player's Fuzzy Bee.
        * Fuzzy Bee is the only [[hive]] bee that was introduced in 2020 (not counting [[Digital
        Bee]], as it wasn't usable until 2022).
        *Fuzzy Bee's Fuzz Bombs can pop bubbles when the fuzz bombs touch them.
        *Fuzzy Bee is the only Mythic Bee to be added outside of [[Beesmas]].
