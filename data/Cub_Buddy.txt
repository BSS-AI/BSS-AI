        A '''Cub Buddy''' is a small, baby version of a [[Bears|bear]] that follows the player
        around, collects tokens, and creates gifts. Whenever the player's cub buddy gets too far
        away, it will teleport back to them.

        Cub buddies have settings that can be accessed and viewed through the [[Cub Console]], which
        is located on the [[System Page]].

        When the player's cub buddy produces [[items]], it dances, emits heart particles, and an
        in-game message will pop up, saying: \"[🐻(Cub Buddy Name) made some gifts!]\".

        == Ways to Obtain ==
        <sub>Note: This list also includes the cub buddy skins.</sub>
        * Redeeming a {{Cost|Cub Buddy Voucher|||0}}
        * Purchasable in the [[Robux Shop]] for {{Cost|Robux|600}} (gives a Cub Buddy Voucher).
        * Obtainable by completing 300 [[Brown Bear]] quests (gives the Brown Bear Cub skin).
        * Obtainable by collecting {{Cost|Cog|250,000}}, beating Round 25 in [[Robo Bear
        Challenge]], or placing Top 10 on the [[Daily Top Robo Bear Challenge Scores]] (gives the
        Robo Cub skin).
        * Obtainable by placing Top 25 on the Daily Stick Bug score leaderboard or as a drop from
        [[Stick Bug]] (Unbelievably Rare) (gives the Stick Cub skin).
        **For the end of challenge reward, the odds vary from around 1 in 25,000 (10 million score)
        to around 1 in 670 (over 1,000,000,000 score)
        <ref>https://discord.com/channels/427553293862961153/676148494276362260/1194516653724139581</ref>
        *Obtainable by sticking all 12 Star Signs to the [[Sticker Stack]], rolling it from the
        [[Sticker Printer]], or from purchasing a [[Star Amulet|Diamond Star Amulet]] (1 in
        1,000,000 chance) (gives the Star Cub skin).

        ==Possible Gift Rewards==
        Cub buddies will produce gifts in a ring of 5 tokens, which usually contain common items
        like {{Cost|Treat}}, but can also produce rarer items like tickets or {{Cost|Royal Jelly}}.
        When a cub buddy starts to dance and heart particles appear above its head, that means it's
        about to produce some gifts. These items can be collected not only by the player, but also
        by [[Ability Tokens#Token Link|token links]], [[Vector Bee|Vector Bee's]] [[Ability
        Tokens#Triangulate|triangulate ability]], [[Windy Bee|Windy Bee’s]] [[Ability
        Tokens#Tornado|tornado ability]] and be collected by a [[Frog]]'s Tongue. Cub buddies
        produce gifts every 1–5 minutes. Similar to [[sprout]]s and [[leaves]], the rewards granted
        by cub buddies typically depend on the [[Fields|field]] the player stands in (e.g. getting 3
        [[Sunflower Seed|sunflower seeds]] while standing in the [[Sunflower Field]]).

        ==Trivia==
        *While Bee Bear said that the cub buddies are dolls animated by \"Beesmas magic,\" [[Polar
        Bear]] said, \"He says the [Cub Buddies] are animated by the magic of Beesmas... But in
        reality, Bee Bear had to build and program them himself.” Also, [[Science Bear]] said,
        \"They're just robots, you know. But they're so lifelike, it's uncanny!\"
        *If the player bought the Cub Buddy Launch Pack, and then completed Bee Bear's 10th quest,
        the player was rewarded with 250 tickets since the player can only have one of each cub
        buddy skin.
        **If the process is reversed, the player will instead be rewarded with 250 tickets in
        addition to the 250 tickets that they would normally get for a total of 500 tickets and the
        Noob Cub skin for purchasing the pack.
        *When the player gets their first cub buddy, it will automatically be named \"Buddy\".
        *A cub buddy uses the wave emote when idle, the point emote when collecting a token, the
        dance emote before it produces gifts, and the cheer emote when it makes the gifts.
        *Most cub buddies use the [https://www.roblox.com/bundles/56/Cartoony-Animation-Package
        Cartoony Animation Package]. The Brown Cub, Snow Cub, Robo Cub, and Peppermint Robo Cub are
        the only cubs to use a different one, being the
        [https://www.roblox.com/bundles/68/Knight-Animation-Package Knight Animation Package], the
        [https://www.roblox.com/bundles/75/Ninja-Animation-Package Ninja Animation Package] and
        [https://www.roblox.com/bundles/82/Robot-Animation-Pack Robot Animation Package],
        respectively.
        **Coincidentally, the Cartoony Animation Package is the one used by [[Black Bear]], [[Noob
        Bear]], and [[Bee Bear]].
        *There used to be a [[Glitches|glitch]] that would make cub buddies \"wear\" an item if they
        teleported on top of it, such as the [[Demon Mask]], [[Elite Blue Guard]], and [[Riley
        Guard]]. This could also happen if the owner died/reset on top of the cub buddy. This was
        because the cub buddy is a Humanoid Model. This [[Glitches|Glitch]] was patched in an
        unknown update.
        *There is a glitch that causes cub buddies to stay in the game after their owners leave. The
        cub buddy can still collect any public tokens when it is left in the game, but it doesn't
        give the rewards to their owner.
        *Cub buddies used to be the most expensive item in the [[Ticket Tent]], costing 2,000
        tickets.
        * The max amount of characters in a cub buddy name is 16. The name can also be filtered by
        Roblox.
        *Although a cub buddy can collect any token, either the player's or public, it cannot
        collect its own gifts it made unless it picks up a Token Link.
        *All of the cub skins except for the Gingerbread Bear, Doodle and the Peppermint Robo cub
        skin are based on their original NPC/mob counterpart.
        **However, the Gingerbread Bear skin looks like a bigger and more elaborate version of a
        [[Gingerbread Bear]], and the Peppermint Robo cub is a reskin of the normal Robo cub.
        *Bee Bear has stated that a Snow Cub’s favorite treat is [[Egg|Basic Eggs]].
        *Cub buddies are affected by the player's [[Ability Tokens#Haste|Haste]] stacks.
        * The Robo Cub skin is the first of 3 skins to have 2 ways to be obtained, the other two
        being the Stick Cub and Star Cub, added in the same update.
        ** It is also the first skin to have 3 ways of obtainment.
        *The Peppermint Robo Cub next to Bee Bear is the only NPC that does an emote originating
        from the Roblox catalog. The emote it does is the
        [https://www.roblox.com/catalog/3576721660/Robot Robot].
