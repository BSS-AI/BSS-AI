        '''Beesmas Lights''' occasionally appeared in fields as the player collected pollen as the
        reward of [[Science Bear|Science Bear's]] Beesmas 2020, 2021, or 2022 quest. They fell from
        the sky like a similar way of the [[Passive Abilities#Star Shower|Star Shower passive]],
        taking 2 seconds to fall. They were used to convert a certain amount of [[pollen]] to
        [[honey]]. Whenever a Beesmas Light was caught, it granted the [[Ability
        Tokens#Inspire|inspire]] buff and converts based on the player's [[System Page#Convert
        Total|convert total]]. If it wasn't caught, it collected pollen. Beesmas Lights spawned
        after collecting 25 Ability Tokens (with a 30 second cooldown), dropping 5 lights into the
        field. Beesmas Lights could spawn in any field, including the [[Ant Field]].

        == Trivia ==
        * Players are required to catch a certain amount of Beesmas Lights for [[Bee Bear]] and
        [[Onett]]'s quests.
        * The Beesmas Lights has the same function as the [[Passive Abilities#Star Shower|Star
        Shower passive]] with the difference of the fact that the star shower is more focused in a
        small area than Beesmas Lights.
        ** This means that if the passive is disregarded, the Beesmas Lights are the only way to get
        the inspire buff without [[Gifted Bee|gifted bees]] or redeeming certain [[codes]].
