        '''Exhausted Bee''' is a [[:Category:Colorless|Colorless]] [[Bees/Epic|Epic bee]].

        Exhausted Bee's favorite type of treat are [[Pineapple|Pineapples]].

        Exhausted Bee likes the [[Stump Field]], the [[Sunflower Field]], and the [[Dandelion
        Field]]. It dislikes the [[Cactus Field]].

        == Stats ==
        * Collects 10 [[Pollen]] in 4.6 seconds.
        * Makes 240 [[Honey]] in 4 seconds.
        * -15% [[Stats#Speed|Move Speed]] and gather speed. +160 [[System Page#Convert
        Amount|Convert Amount]]. Doesn't require [[Energy|Sleep]].
        * 🌟 [[Gifted Bee|Gifted Hive Bonus]]: +20% White Field Capacity.

        === Abilities ===
        * '''[[Ability Tokens#Bomb|[Buzz Bomb]]]''' Collects 7 pollen from 13 surrounding
        [[Flowers]] (+10% pollen per Level). Combo with other bombs to increase power.
        * '''[[Ability Tokens#Token Link|[Token Link]]]''' Collects all other ability tokens,
        granting 25 Honey (+10 per Level) per token collected.

        == Trivia ==
        * Exhausted Bee is the third [[Bees|Bee]] to have a [[Royal Jelly#Royal Jelly Variants|Royal
        Jelly]] dedicated to it.
        * This bee was the first bee to be implemented with unlimited energy, a feature only shared
        with [[Photon Bee]].
        ** This makes the Exhausted Bee the only non-[[Bees/Event|Event bee]] that has unlimited
        energy.
        ** If a player dies, any Exhausted Bees in their hive will go to sleep no matter what.
        * [[Onett (Developer)|Onett]] often calls himself an Exhausted Bee because he dedicates a
        lot of time to [[Bee Swarm Simulator]].
        ** The [[Codes|Code]], \"OnettJelly\" (expired) is a reference to Onett calling himself an
        Exhausted Bee.
        * Although its description implies that it never sleeps, Exhausted Bee can actually go to
        sleep because its energy is set to 280,000,000, which is not an unlimited amount. This is
        because infinity cannot be coded as a number.
