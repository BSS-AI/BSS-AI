        This shop sells four [[bags]], five [[tools]], four [[:Category:Guards|guards]], a
        [[:Category:Hats|hat]], a [[:Category:Belts|belt]], a pair of [[:Category:Boots|boots]],
        [[Paper Planter|paper planters]], and the [[Parachute]].

        As with the [[Noob Shop]], the game does not explicitly name the shop. However, the bear who
        runs the shop is named \"[[Pro Bear]]\". Given that, the community has named the shop as such.

        == Trivia ==
        * The [[Slingshot]] will sometimes undershoot the player near [[Panda Bear]] or in the
        middle of the [[Bamboo Field]]. If the Slingshot does not undershoot, it will regularly
        shoot the player in front of the shop's door.
        * In some cases, the Slingshot will also overshoot the player on top of the Pro Shop at the
        Mythic Meteor Shower Summoner.
        * There are two [[Royal Jelly|royal jelly]] tokens, one of them on top of the shop at the
        end of a dark passage and one within inside the shop, the latter being hidden in the
        [[Mazes#Pro Shop Maze|maze]] behind Pro Bear. The player can obtain it by going all the way
        to the end, turning right, going to the end, turning right, and going to the first corridor
        on the right and follow through it to find the royal jelly.
        * Since the [[Updates#2018.07.11|7/11/2018 update]], there is a [[sunflower seed]] token
        inside the maze that gives 10 sunflower seeds. The player can obtain it by going right two
        times, just like the royal jelly, but on the second right, go to the end of the hall and
        follow through the last corridor to the right.
        * There is a giant pineapple near the Pro Shop, where there are 3 [[ticket]]s on top that
        can be collected by using the [[Red Cannon]] and the [[Parachute]] or [[Glider]] to glide
        down onto the top.
        * If the player stands on the piece of blue wall behind the backpack models and jumps high
        enough, they can go into the space between the roof and the ceiling.
        * [[Chicks#Spotted Chick|Spotted chicks]] were able to spawn here before they were removed.
        ** Spotted chicks were also able to spawn in the space between the roof and the ceiling.
        When they spawned there, the feet of the chick would be visible.
        * With 18 items, the Pro Shop has the most purchasable items out of all of the shops.
        * Out of the three shops that have a shop bear, this is the only one that sells at least 1
        type of guard.
