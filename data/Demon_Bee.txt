        '''Demon Bee''' is a [[:Category:Red|Red]] [[Bees/Legendary|Legendary bee]].

        Demon Bee's favorite type of treat is [[Pineapple|Pineapples]].

        Demon Bee likes the [[Mushroom Field]] and the [[Spider Field]]. It dislikes the [[Mountain
        Top Field]].

        == Stats ==
        * Collects 35 [[Pollen]] in 4 seconds.
        * Makes 60 [[Honey]] in 4 seconds.
        * -25% [[Stats#Speed|Movespeed]], +25 [[Stats#Gather Amount|Gather amount]], -20 convert
        amount, +10% [[Instant Conversion]] (+20% if Gifted), +6 [[Stats#Attack|Attack]].
        * 🌟[[Gifted Bee|Gifted Hive Bonus]]: +20% [[System Page#Instant Bomb Conversion|Instant
        Bomb Conversion]].

        === Abilities ===
        * '''[[Ability Tokens#Bomb|[Red Bomb]]]''' Collects 10 pollen from 13 surrounding red
        [[flowers]] (+10% pollen per lvl). Combo with other bombs to increase power.
        * '''[Red Bomb+]''' Collects 10 pollen from 29 surrounding red flowers (+10% pollen per
        lvl). Combo with other bombs to increase power.
        * '''[[Passive Abilities#Gathering Flames|[Passive:]] [[Flame#Gathering
        Flames|<nowiki>Gathering Flames+]</nowiki>]]''' 65% chance (85% if Gifted) to
        spawn a [[Flame]] when gathering. Flames last 4s. Each second they collect 6 red/3
        [[:Category:Colorless|White]]/1 [[:Category:Blue|Blue]] pollen (+2% per red bee in your
        [[Hive]], 4% if gifted) from 9 flowers, and deal (10 + 25% [[Bee Attack|base Bee Attack]])
        damage to nearby enemies. They also grant Flame Heat if you stand near them, which increases
        [[Pollen|Red Pollen]] and [[Bee Attack]].

        == Trivia ==
        * Demon Bee is one of the slowest [[Bees]] in the game, but is also one of the most powerful
        in terms of attack and gather amount.
        * Demon Bee is the only bee in the game to have 2 bomb abilities (Red Bomb and Red Bomb+).
        * Demon Bee, [[Riley Bee]], and [[Rascal Bee]] have the same skin colors.
        * Both Demon Bee and [[Fire Bee]] have the same flame effect.
        * The [[Demon Mask]], which can be purchasable in the [[Obstacle Courses#Lava Obby|Lava
        Obby]], has a special design, name, and stats dedicated to Demon Bee.
        * [[Onett (Developer)|Onett]] has said on Discord that during early stages, it was supposed
        to summon Fire Bees. This role has now been taken over by [[Spicy Bee]].
        * Before the [[Updates#2020.04.06|4/6/2020 Update]], Demon Bee had the highest base gather
        amount in the game with the exception of a [[Tabby Bee]] with a 500+ stack of [[Tabby
        Love]]. Since then, [[Fuzzy Bee]] has taken that position.
        * Demon Bee is the red counterpart of [[Diamond Bee]].
        *One reason Demon Bee dislikes the Mountain Top Field might be because it is closer to
        heaven (the sky) than all other Fields.
        *This bee can be summoned by the [[Honeyday Candles]].
        *This Bee, Fire Bee, [[Precise Bee]], [[Festive Bee]] and [[Crimson Bee]] are the only red
        bees that do not like Strawberries.
