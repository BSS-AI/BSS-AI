{"text": "'''King Beetle's Lair''' is the location where [[King Beetle]] spawns. It can be accessed
        from the light green door between [[Blue HQ]] and under the [[Clover Field]] and next to the
        far end of the [[Blue Flower Field]]. Inside the lair, the exit can be accessed by climbing
        a ladder leaning adjacent to the entrance. The interior of the lair is completely empty,
        except for the ladder for leaving. Both doors are one-way. The player will be pushed through
        either door if they stand too close, even if they aren’t moving. The entrance to the lair
        cycles in color, so it stands out from the rest of the environment.

        The King Beetle spawns on the back wall adjacent to the Noob Shop and [[Obstacle
        Courses#Lava Obby|Lava Obby]].

        Near the back right corner of the lair, there is some text on the floor, \"Song Name\". It
        refers to the song that plays inside the lair and is a hint to the [[Codes|code]],
        \"Crawlers\".

        == Music ==
        When in the lair, the following audio plays:[[File:Crawlers.ogg]]

        This [[Music|audio]] is known as \"Crawlers\" and also plays inside the [[Commando Chick's
        Hideout]] and the [[White Tunnel]].

        == Trivia ==
        * In early game versions, if players went too close to the side of the lair that is closest
        to the Noob Shop, they could die due to a lava block clipping into the wall from the lava
        obby. This was later patched.
        * The bottom of the pole of the [[Honeystorm|Honeystorm Summoner]] sign pokes into the top
        of the lair.
        * If there is a [[sprout]] in the Clover Field, the bottom of its light column and the
        remaining pollen number can be seen in the lair.
        **This is similar to when [[Rogue Vicious Bee]]’s spikes and [[clouds]] spawn in the Clover
        Field.
        * There is a [[Glitches|glitch]] where the King Beetle can fail to lunge and get stuck when
        in the 'Song Name' corner.
        * During the [[Updates#2018.12.25|Beesmas 2018 Event]], the red roof of the lair could be
        seen on the sides of the flower blocks in the Clover Field.
        ** However, the red roof's glow near the Noob Shop wall is still visible even when it was
        not snowy.
        * Any [[Tools|tool]] can collect [[pollen]] from the Clover Field inside the lair if the
        player continuously jumps and has enough [[System Page#Jump Power|Jump Power]]. However, the
        [[Spark Staff]] doesn't require too much Jump Power.
        * If the player has enough Jump Power and jumps high enough, the [[Rhino Beetle|rhino
        beetle]] and [[ladybug]] in the Clover Field can be triggered. The player's bees may also
        think that they are in the Clover Field and start collecting pollen.
        * The entrance to the lair is lighter than the color of the surrounding ground and slightly
        changes in. During Beesmas, it is much more noticeable as the walls surrounding the entrance
        are white.
        * The cycling colors of the entrance matches the cycling colors and pattern of any
        supreme-tier amulet.
