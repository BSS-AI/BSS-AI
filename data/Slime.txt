        The '''Slime''' is a mob exclusive in the [[Retro Swarm Challenge]]. Its level varies on the
        round of the challenge.

        == Behavior ==
        The '''Slime''' moves constantly towards the hive, depleting flowers under it. Stuns also
        cannot stop the mob from moving forward, with only the trowel's walls being able to halt the
        movement of the mob. Slimes increase in size as they increase in level.

        ==Drops==

        *Bricks

        ==Tips==
        *Slimes along with Zombies are mainly in hordes. It is advised to use crowd-controlling bees
        like [[Digital Bee]] and [[Vicious Bee]], as Mind Hack and Impale can help out massively,
        especially in the later rounds where Slimes and Zombies start increasing in level and health
        drastically.
        *Remember that Slimes can damage you simply by coming into contact with you. If you are not
        careful and become too reckless, you can die very quickly to hordes.
        *Try upgrading from the normal sword to the Firebrand as soon as possible. There is a
        MASSIVE damage boost that comes with this, and you'll be able to kill low to mid level
        Slimes with ease before they even get a chance to sufficiently damage you. The Illumina is
        an even bigger help, but it may start becoming irrelevant by the time you reach round 20 or
        21.
        **Swords as a whole start becoming futile by round 20/21 as the Slimes increase in health
        and spawn in larger numbers. By this point, it is advised that you buy bees that are
        preferably at the level that the Slimes are, which is most likely too much to handle for
        early and midgame players.
        *You can use the Trowel to temporarily block Slimes from approaching the hives. This can
        prove to be extremely useful if you or your team are unable to clear out a horde of Slimes.
        *You can use the [[Passive Abilities#Star Amulet Passives|Star Saw]] to shred hordes of
        Slimes.

        ==Trivia==
        *The slime is based on the ROBLOX game Defend the Statue.
