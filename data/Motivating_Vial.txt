        The '''Motivating Vial''' is a consumable [[Nectar Vial|nectar vial]] [[Items|item]] that
        was added in the [[Updates#2022.12.26|12/26/2022 update]]. Once used, it grants the player 4
        hours of the [[Nectar|Motivating Nectar]] [[Buffs & Debuffs|buff]] instantly. It can
        also be used to craft other [[:Category:Accessories|accessories]].

        == Ways to Obtain ==
        * Completing [[Dapper Bear|Dapper Bear's]] 8th quest.
        * Using the [[Nectar Condenser]] to condense Motivating Nectar.
        * Adding the Motivating Nectar Icon to the [[Sticker Stack]] grants 1 Motivating Vial.

        === The methods below are no longer possible. ===
        * Purchasing the Hefty Honeyday Bundle from [[Bee Bear's Catalog]] for 25,000 [[Snowflake]]s
        and 50 [[Gingerbread Bear]]s granted 5 Motivating Vials and other items.
        * Opening the Mythic Gift Box granted 2 Motivating Vials and other items.
        * Purchasing the Nighttime Nectar Pack in the [[Robux Shop]] gave 1 of each Nectar Vial and
        other items.
        * Completing [[Bee Bear|Bee Bear's]] [[Beesmas|Beesmas 2022]] 17th quest gave 1 Motivating
        Vial + other items.
