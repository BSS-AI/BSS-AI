        {{Color|Box-O-Frogs}} is an [[:Category:Inventory|inventory item]] that was added in the
        [[Updates#2019.12.25|Beesmas 2019 update]]. When used, three regular/gifted [[frog]]s will
        be summoned in any [[Fields|field]] the player is currently standing in, which last for
        three minutes, it used to collect [[Ability Tokens|tokens]], produce [[Passive
        Abilities#Gathering Bubbles|bubbles]] around the field, and attack hostile [[mobs]].

        It can also be used outside of a field, but the frogs summoned will aimlessly hop, which
        then won’t do anything unless one of the frogs is in the field.

        ==Ways to Obtain==
        *Completing 111 [[Brown Bear]] quests rewards 5 Box-O-Frogs.
        *Completing 333 [[Brown Bear]] quests rewards 3 Box-O-Frogs.
        *Completing [[Black Bear]]’s \"Bubble Trouble\" quest rewards 1 Box-O-Frogs and other items.
        *Completing [[Black Bear]]'s \"Boss Battles\" quest rewards 3 Box-O-Frogs and other items.

        ==Trivia==
        *Box-O-Frogs and [[Night Bell]]s are the only two consumable items that summon mobs.
        * Box-O-Frogs are one of the two ways to summon a frog (The other being [[Tadpole Bee]]'s
        [[Ability Tokens#Summon Frog|Summon Frog ability]]).
        *Box-O-Frogs is one of the few non-farmable items, making them more of a collectible than
        actual useful item.
        *Frogs summoned in the [[Gummy Bear's Lair]] fall through the floor.
