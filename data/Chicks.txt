{"text": "[[File:Normal Chicken.png|thumb|A Level 4 Chick, which is now removed.]]
        '''Chicks''' are [[Mobs]] that were added in the [[Updates#2020.04.06|Egg Hunt 2020
        Update]]. They could spawn in a variety of places depending on their type, and have a
        special ability to hide inside their eggshells, increasing their defense and the chance for
        a bee's attacks to be blocked.

        There are five different types of chicks: Chicks, Hostage Chicks, Spotted Chicks, Commando
        Chicks, and Mondo Chicks. Chicks, Spotted Chicks and Hostage Chicks cannot deal damage to
        players while the Commando Chick and Mondo Chick can. When a chick loses all of its health
        points, it gets captured, and the game will notify the player in the chat. Chicks, Hostage
        Chicks, and Spotted Chicks were removed from the game at the end of Egg Hunt 2020.

        Normal '''Chicks''' were passive mobs that could have appeared in the [[Sunflower Field]]
        and would spawn randomly when players were collecting [[Pollen]]. The chicks could've
        spawned with a level range of 1-15. They could only spawn if the player had [[Black Bear#Egg
        Hunt Quest 2020|Black Bear's Sunflower Corral Quest]] active.

        The '''Hostage Chicks''' were passive mobs that had a chance of spawning after defeating a
        hostile mob or a [[:Category:Bosses|Boss]] (Defeating [[Ants]] or other chicks would not
        spawn a hostage chick). They could spawn with a level range of 1-15 and would always despawn
        30 seconds after spawning. Mobs that are more difficult to defeat, such as [[Tunnel Bear]],
        were more likely to cause a hostage chick to spawn. Hostage chicks that spawn from more
        difficult mobs will have higher health, level, and better rewards than ones that spawn from
        easier mobs.

        The '''Spotted Chicks''' were passive mobs that could appear inside of the [[Noob Shop]],
        [[Pro Shop]], [[Ticket Tent]], and the [[Mazes#Blue Maze|Blue Maze]]. Spotted chicks could
        spawn with a level range of 3-9.

        Capturing a spotted chick would cause another spotted chick to spawn in a different
        location. If a spotted chick was captured in the Noob Shop, another spotted chick would
        spawn in the Ticket Tent, and vice versa. Similarly, if a spotted chick was captured in the
        Pro Shop, another spotted chick will spawn in the Blue Maze, and vice versa.

        * [[Honey]].
        * [[Treat|Treats]].
        * [[Strawberry|Strawberries]].

        == Commando Chick ==
        {{Main|Commando Chick}}

        The '''Commando Chick''' is a [[:Category:Mini Bosses|mini-boss]] that spawns in [[Commando
        Chick's Hideout|its hideout]]. Its hideout is located to the left of [[Brown Bear]] and the
        [[Wealth Clock]]. To cut the vines, the [[Clippers]], [[Scissors]], [[Scythe]], or [[Dark
        Scythe]] needs to be equipped. Once the vines are cut, they will never grow back.

        The Commando Chick has a respawn time of 30:00 (25:30 with [[Gifted Bee|Gifted]] [[Vicious
        Bee]]). When Commando Chick hides in its egg, it throws grenades (1 if it has more than 2/3
        of its health, 2 if it has less than 2/3 of its health, 3 if it has less than 1/3 of its
        health, 4 if less than 1/100). The egg cracks after a few seconds.

        The Commando Chick deals 20 damage when colliding with the player and 50 damage if the
        player is near a grenade when it explodes. Starting at 150 health and level 3, the chick
        increases in health and level with each successive capture, reaching level 6 by the fifth
        capture. The chick will grow another level after a progressively higher number of captures
        per level up.

        The '''Mondo Chick''' is a level 8 [[:Category:Bosses|boss]] mob located in the [[Mountain
        Top Field]]. It respawns every hour at the full hour (1:00, 2:00, 3:00, etc; not in time
        zones that are a fractional number of hours away from UTC), and its respawn timer can be
        found on the side of the [[Mountain Top Shop]]. The respawn time is unaffected by the
        Monster Respawn Time stat. Its rewards are shareable between players.

        The Mondo Chick will always spawn at level 8 with 300,000 health. It automatically takes 333
        damage each second, causing its health to reach 300 after 15 minutes. If it isn't defeated
        within 15 minutes of spawning, it will despawn in a similar fashion of the Commando Chick.
        After despawning, a server-wide message will say \"🐣Mondo Chick got away!🐣\" Like the
        [[Coconut Crab]], the faster the Mondo Chick is defeated, the better the rewards will be.
        Due to its large hitbox and damage, it is recommended to put distance between the player and
        the Mondo Chick.

        Sometimes, the Mondo Chick will hide in its egg, allowing it to block some attacks. However,
        when it is hiding, it stops moving until it comes out again. The egg can be cracked early by
        dealing 7500 damage to it.

        When it runs out of time, it will jump out of the field towards the [[Lion Bee Gate]]
        entrance.

        == Trivia ==
        * According to [[Sun Bear]], the chicks are invasive to the Mountain. Their eggs are also
        considered exotic.

        * According to [[Mother Bear]], the Spotted Chicks dislike the sun.

        * According to [[Black Bear]], Bees don't hurt chicks when attacking. They work together to
        calm them down and capture them as they are \"frightened\".

        * Although other player's Commando Chicks can't damage the player directly, their grenades
        can. A player's bees can also damage other players' Commando Chicks.

        * Interestingly, the Commando Chick is considered a mini-boss although every player has
        their “own” Commando Chick.

        * The Mondo Chick's noise can be heard throughout the entire map.
        ** This is also the case with the [[Cave Monster]]s.

        * The Mondo Chick is the only mob that has a fixed spawn point in the Mountain Top Field.

        * The Spotted Chick is the only mob in the game that spawned in [[:Category:Shops|shops]].

        * During the soft launch of the Egg Hunt 2020 update, the Mondo Chick only had 250,000
        health. It was later changed to 300,000 the following day.

        * Because Rogue Vicious Bee's spikes can deal damage to mobs, it is possible for one to
        spawn in the Mountain Top Field and assist in killing the Mondo Chick.

        * If the player already has the Mondo Chick Blessing buff and defeats another Mondo Chick,
        the new blessing will override the old buff, resetting its timer and possibly degrading or
        upgrading the buff depending on the time took to defeat the Mondo Chick.

        * Mondo Chick, Stick Nymphs and Festive Nymphs are the only mobs in the game that take
        damage without any things attacking it.

        * Onett has said on discord that he dislikes Mondo Chick \"on a personal level\".

        * Based on one of Onett's discord messages, the Hostage Chicks may have been cooked for the
        2020 [[Beesmas Feast]].

        * If a bee lands a critical hit on a chick that is hiding inside its egg, it will ignore the
        damage reduction and do the normal amount of critical damage.

        * If the Mondo Chick falls out of the Mountain Top field and into another field, when
        defeated, the rewards will spawn in that field it was defeated in. However if it dies
        outside of a field, their drops will form in a ring.

        * As of the Beesmas 2020 update, the Mondo Chick no longer drops [[Marshmallow Bee]]s.

        * There was a glitch where Commando Chick would stay in its egg and would not go out of its
        egg, even when it is moving. This can also make some player's bees keep on missing their
        shots. The easiest way to fix this glitch was to exit Commando Chick's Hideout and re-enter
        it.

        * Chicks, [[Ants]], [[Aphid]]s, [[Stick Nymph]]s, [[Mechsquito]]s, and [[Cogmower]]s are the
        only mobs that come in different varieties.

        * Commando Chick is the only chick with red eyes; all other chicks have black eyes.
