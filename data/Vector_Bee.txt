        '''Vector Bee''' is a [[:Category:Colorless|Colorless]] [[Bees/Mythic|Mythic bee]].

        Vector Bee's favorite type of Treat is [[Sunflower Seed|Sunflower Seeds]].

        Vector Bee likes the [[Spider Field]] and the [[Coconut Field]]. It dislikes the [[Pineapple
        Patch]].

        == Stats ==
        * Collects 18 [[Pollen]] in 4 seconds.
        * Makes 144 [[Honey]] in 2.72 seconds.
        * +4 [[Stats#Attack|Attack]], +8 [[Stats#Gather Amount|Gather Amount]], +16%
        [[Stats#Speed|Movespeed]], +32% Convert Speed, +64 [[System Page#Convert Amount|Convert
        Amount]], +128% [[Energy]].
        * 🌟 [[Gifted Bee|Gifted Hive Bonus]]: +15% Mark Duration.

        === Abilities ===
        * '''[[Ability Tokens#Mark|[Pollen Mark+]]]''' Marks a large random area on the
        [[Fields|Field]] for 8 seconds (+0.25s per Level) that increases all pollen by 50% while
        that player stands in it. Stacks up to 3 times.
        * '''[[Ability Tokens#Triangulate|[Triangulate]]]''' Draws a triangle between the player,
        the bee, and the token. After 3s, it collects [[Ability Tokens|Ability tokens]] and 10
        pollen (+2 per Level) from all [[Flowers]] inside. All Marks within the triangle increase
        the pollen by 50%.
        **If the triangle contains a Pollen Mark, it gains x2 White pollen.
        **If the triangle contains a Honey Mark or Festive Mark, it gains 50% instant conversion.
        **If the triangle contains a Precise Mark, it always deals critical hits.
        * '''[[Ability Tokens#Mark Surge|[🌟Gifted Ability: Mark Surge]]]''' Causes all of the
        player's active Marks to collect 7 Pollen (+10% per Level) from all flowers in their radius.
        Extends the duration of the Marks by 1s (+0.1s per Level). Each mark can perform up to 5
        surges.
        **When a Honey Mark or Festive Mark surges, the Pollen is Instantly Converted.
        **When a Precise Mark surges, it always does Critical Hits.

        == Trivia ==
        * Vector Bee's stats follow a [[Wikipedia:Geometric Progression|Geometric Progression]]
        pattern, where each number is double the amount of the previous number.
        * Vector Bee is the only Mythic bee to not share any of its abilities with other bees. It is
        also the only Mythic Bee to not have a [[Passive Abilities|Passive Ability]].
        * Vector Bee is the only [[Bees|Bee]] to have a different skin pattern on each of its sides.
        * Vector Bee's design is heavily inspired by mathematics.
        ** Its relation to trigonometry is implied in its description and the triangular motifs on
        its head.
        ** Vectors reference Euclidean vectors, objects with magnitude and direction. A triangle can
        be composed of three vectors.
        ** Triangulation is a method to determine the location of a point through forming triangles
        from known points.
        * Vector Bee, [[Spicy Bee]], [[Precise Bee]] and [[Buoyant Bee]] emit light when they are
        gifted.
        * Vector Bee shares similar color schemes with [[Brave Bee]].
        * Triangulate is [[Onett (Developer)|Onett]]'s favorite ability, due to the fact that he
        thinks it's satisfying.
