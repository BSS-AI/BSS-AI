        The '''Red Balloon''' is a [[:Category:Consumables|consumable]] [[Items#Misc. Items|item]]
        that was added in the [[Updates|12/26/2021 update]]. Once used, it creates a red [[balloon]]
        over the [[field]] the player is in. The balloon floats over the field, storing [[pollen]]
        collected underneath it. The pollen collected increases by 10%, with red pollen increasing
        by 20%. It holds up to 5x the player's capacity, and floats over the field for 3 minutes
        before floating to the [[Balloon#Hive Balloon|hive balloon]].

        Red balloons have a cap of 25 in the player's [[:Category:Inventory|inventory]], and 1 per
        player in the field.

        == Ways to Obtain ==

        * As a exceptionally rare drop from a [[scorpion]].
        * As a rare reward from the [[Sticker-Seeker Quest Machine]].
        * As an item granted by the following codes:
        ** \"5MFavorites\"
        ** \"DarzethDoodads\"
        * Completing [[Dapper Bear]]'s 6th quest gives 1 Red balloon and other items.
