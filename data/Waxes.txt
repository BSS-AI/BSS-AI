{"text": "'''Waxes''' are [[:Category:Inventory|Inventory Items]] that were added in the
        [https://bee-swarm-simulator.fandom.com/wiki/Updates#2021.12.26 12/26/2021] Update. They are
        used by players to modify the stats and abilities of [[Beequip]]s. There are currently 4
        different Waxes, and each Wax can affect a Beequip differently. The player can only use up
        to 5 Waxes on a Beequip, so if the Player would like to use more Waxes on a Beequip, they
        must use a [[Turpentine]] on the Beequip to remove the waxes. Doing this will change the
        stats back to the original ones the Beequip had. Waxes can also be used to craft
        [[Planter]]s, [[Tools]], and better waxes in the [[Blender]].

        ==Wax Types==
        There are currently four wax types listed below:<tabview>
        Soft Wax|Soft
        Hard Wax|Hard
        Caustic Wax|Caustic
        Swirled Wax|Swirled
        </tabview>

        == Use in Quests ==

        * [[Dapper Bear]] requires the player to collect or use waxes in the following quests:
        ** A Good Infection (#3) requires the player to collect 10 Soft Waxes.
        ** All About Appearances (#4) requires the player to use 5 Soft Waxes and 1 Hard Wax.
        ** The Long Haul (#10) requires the player to use 10 Hard Waxes.
        ** Ongoing Maintanence (#13) requires the player to use 50 Soft Waxes.
        * [[Science Bear]] requires to player to obtain waxes in the following quests:
        ** Repairing The White Drive requires the player to obtain 12 Soft Waxes.
        ** Corrupting The Glitched Drive requires the player to obtain 12 Hard Waxes.
        * [[Bee Bear]] requires to player to collect or use waxes in the following quests:
        ** Finishing The Festive Planter (#10) requires the player to collect 10 Soft Waxes.
        ** Upgrading The Honeydays (#13) requires the player to use 5 Soft Waxes.
        ** Cogs For The Cub Buddy (#15) requires the player to use 5 Hard Waxes.
        ** Peppermint Preparation (2/5) (#17) requires the player to collect 30 Soft Waxes.
        ** Peppermint Preparation (3/5) (#18) requires the player to use 1 Swirled Wax.
        ** Peppermint Preparation (4/5) (#19) requires the player to collect 30 Hard Waxes and 5
        Caustic Waxes.
        * [[Bubble Bee Man]] requires to player to collect, use, and destroy Beequips with waxes in
        the following quests:
        ** B.B.M's Naughty List (Beesmas 2021) requires the player to collect 555 Soft Wax tokens
        and use 9 Caustic Waxes.
        ** BBM's Naughty List (Beesmas 2022) requires the player to destroy 31 Beequips with Caustic
        Waxes.
