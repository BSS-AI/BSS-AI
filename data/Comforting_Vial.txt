        The '''Comforting Vial''' is a consumable [[Nectar Vial|nectar vial]] [[Items|item]] that
        was added in the [[Updates#2022.12.26|12/26/2022 update]]. Once used, it grants the player 4
        hours of the [[Nectar|Comforting Nectar]] [[Buffs & Debuffs|buff]] instantly. It can
        also be used to craft other [[:Category:Accessories|accessories]] and [[tools]].

        == Ways to Obtain ==
        * Completing [[Dapper Bear|Dapper Bear's]] 6th, 10th, and 13th quests each grant 1
        Comforting Vial and other items.
        * Completing [[Dapper Bear#Blue Drive Quest|Dapper Bear's Blue Drive quest]] grants 1
        Comforting Vial and other items.
        * Using the [[Nectar Condenser]] to condense Comforting Nectar.
        * Adding the Comforting Nectar Icon to the [[Sticker Stack]] rewards 1 Comforting Vial.

        === The methods below are no longer possible. ===
        * Giving a [[Present]] to [[Gifted Bucko Bee]] granted 1 Comforting Vial and other items.
        * Buying the Hefty Honeyday Bundle from [[Bee Bear's Catalog]].
        * Purchasing the Nighttime Nectar Pack in the [[Robux Shop]] granted 1 of each Nectar Vial.
        * Opening Certain Gift Boxes:
        ** The [[Gift Boxes#2022|Soothing Gift Box]] contained 2 Comforting Vials + other stuff.
        * Completing certain quests:
        ** Completing [[Gifted Bucko Bee#Beesmas 2022 Quest - Bucko Bee's Snow Machine|Bucko Bee's
        Snow Machine]] in Beesmas 2022 gave 1 Comforting Vial and other items.
        **Completing Peppermint Preparation 2 ([[Bee Bear]]) gave 1 Comforting Vial and other items.
        **Completing [[Bubble Bee Man#2022 Beesmas Quest - Naughty List|Bubble Bee Man's Naughty
        List]] in Beesmas 2022 gave 8 Comforting Vials and other items.
