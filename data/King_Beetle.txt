        The '''King Beetle''' is a level 7 [[:Category:Bosses|Boss Mob]] that resides in his [[King
        Beetle's Lair|Lair]]. The entrance to his lair is on the wall between the [[Blue Flower
        Field]] and the [[Clover Field]], above the blue flower closest to the [[Blue HQ]]. He has
        2,500 health and deals 40 damage to the player per hit if the player has no [[System
        Page#Defense|Defense]].

        He takes 1 day (24 hours) to respawn after being defeated (20 hours and 24 minutes with a
        [[Gifted Bee|Gifted]] [[Vicious Bee]]). The rewards for defeating him vary, but always
        include 150 [[Battle Points]], as well as [[Honey]], [[Royal Jelly|Royal jelly]],
        [[Ticket]]s, and [[Bond]].

        There is a one-in-seven (approximately 14%) chance that he will drop a [[King Beetle
        Amulet]] when defeated. Having more [[Loot Luck]] increases the probabilities of other drops
        but doesn’t improve the probability of a King Beetle Amulet dropping. After the
        [[Updates#2019.02.01|2/1/2019 Update]], King Beetle drops honey tokens and rewards in a
        token circle around the center of the lair unlike all other mobs who drop the circle around
        the place of their death (except for [[Stump Snail]], [[Tunnel Bear]], and [[Rogue Vicious
        Bee]]) unless the player receives a King Beetle Amulet, in which it goes directly into the
        player's inventory.

        == Strategies==
        Defeating a King Beetle is challenging and he might kill players several times before they
        succeed (It's recommended that players don't fight King Beetle with a full bag). Here are
        some recommended strategies for defeating him:
        *'''The Stop and Run Method.''' This method counts on the fact that King Beetle has a
        predictable pattern of behavior. He moves toward the player, stops to \"aim,\" and then
        lunges. If the player stays still long enough for him to aim (about 3 seconds), but then
        quickly moves away, King Beetle will lunge at where the player used to be, not where the
        player has moved to. Thus, this method entails running along the edge of the lair, stopping,
        looking at King Beetle and waiting for him to lock onto the player, and then quickly jumping
        and running to a different point along the edge of the lair. Rinse and repeat until he is
        defeated.

        *'''Circling King Beetle.''' This method allows the player to defeat King Beetle faster. If
        the player keeps circling around their King Beetle while keeping a good distance from him,
        their bees can get very close to King Beetle. Though the player might get hit the first few
        times, the beetle will most likely be defeated.

        *'''Running along the perimeter of the lair.''' This method takes a long time, but it works.
        Run around the lair and occasionally look at his health. It's very helpful if players have
        Rage Bees, so they can run around and grab the tokens. However, if the player is too far
        away from him, their bees will come back to them rather than attacking the beetle. Building
        up maximum [[Ability Tokens#Haste|Haste]] can help, although it may run out before King
        Beetle gets defeated. Using the [[Parachute]] or [[Glider]] can also help since gliding
        through the air can be faster than running.

        *'''Have strong bees.''' If the player is certain that their bees will kill him before they
        die, they can just wait for their bees to defeat him while the player stands in front of
        King Beetle, doing nothing during the process.

        *'''The spike attacks from Rogue Vicious Bee.''' If a Rogue Vicious Bee is active in the
        Clover Field, players can trick it into damaging King Beetle.

        * '''Abusing a glitch method.''' Due to a bug, if the player gets the King Beetle stuck on
        the clue 'Song Name' by standing in the top right corner, King Beetle cannot attack them.
        This may take several attempts to work.

        == Look Out For... ==
        With all methods, there are some things to watch out for.

        *If the player gets too far from King Beetle, their bees will follow them instead of
        attacking him. But if the player gets too close, it's very easy to accidentally touch his
        aura which will damage the player.

        *If more than one player is trying to fight King Beetle at the same time, his pattern of
        behavior can become less predictable. It can also be difficult to tell which King Beetle is
        targeting them. For best results, wait for the other player's King Beetle to be defeated.

        *If the player's bees' levels are lower than 7, their attempts to attack will most likely
        miss. This will make defeating it take much longer than if they were level 7 or above.

        * When dodging King Beetle, keep in mind he does not follow directly behind the player but
        can sidestep to get in their way.
        * If you die, the King Beetle's health will reset, unlike [[Commando Chick]] and [[Stump
        Snail]].

        ==Tips==
        Some things can help the player defeat King Beetle, regardless of which strategy the player
        chooses.

        *Having more bees and having bees with high attack power such as [[Lion Bee]], [[Vicious
        Bee]], [[Crimson Bee]], [[Cobalt Bee]], [[Riley Bee]], [[Bucko Bee]], [[Rage Bee]], [[Brave
        Bee]], and [[Precise Bee]].

        *Having high-level bees so attacks don't miss (Recommended level 5-7 with at least one Rage
        Bee and 20 [[bees]]).

        *Wearing an [[:Category:Accessories|Accessory]] that can boost [[System Page#Bee Attack|Bee
        Attack]], [[System Page#Movespeed|Movespeed]], [[System Page#Jump Power|Jump Power]], or
        Defense, such as [[:Category:Guards|guards]].

        *Collecting [[Ability Tokens#Rage|Rage]], [[Ability Tokens#Focus|Focus]] and [[Ability
        Tokens#Melody|Melody]] tokens can be helpful, especially if the player has fewer bees.
        Collecting [[Ability Tokens#Haste|haste]] and [[Ability Tokens#Bear Morph|bear morph]]
        tokens can also help dodge King Beetle's attack.

        *While it is possible to defeat King Beetle using the arrow keys to move, WASD is the
        suggested method, because it allows the player to keep looking at King Beetle while they are
        moving.

        *The Parachute or Glider can be used to quickly get out of King Beetle's attack path.

        *Having at least 20-25 bees with all of them at least level 7 is usually enough to defeat
        King Beetle using the above strategies.

        *Using a [[Stinger]] can be useful with low level bees.

        ==Trivia==
        *King Beetle used to guarantee an amulet upon death
        *In the back right corner of the lair, there is something written on the floor. This is a
        hint for a [[Codes|code]].
        **<sub>(The clue is \"Song Name\", which refers to the music that plays in the lair. If
        the player checks [https://www.roblox.com/library/1598349092/crawlers Onett's inventory],
        the user will see that it's called Crawlers.)</sub>

        *King Beetle was the first boss to be added to the game.

        *King Beetle has the fifth-largest amount of health in the game for any mob, with [[Tunnel
        Bear]] being the fourth, [[Coconut Crab]] the third, [[Chicks#Mondo Chick|Mondo Chick]] the
        second, and [[Stump Snail]] the first. On certain occasions, Rogue Vicious Bee, [[Wild Windy
        Bee]], [[aphid]]s, and [[Stick Bug]] will have more health depending on their level. In this
        case, King Beetle can be considered the weakest boss in terms of health.

        *King Beetle and Stump Snail are the only mobs that can drop an item that can be equipped on
        the player, the King Beetle Amulet and the [[Shell Amulet]] respectively.

        *There was a myth that clicking on King Beetle has an effect. However, this was disproven as
        [[Onett (Developer)|Onett]] has confirmed that King Beetle does not even have a
        [http://robloxdev.com/api-reference/class/ClickDetector click detector].

        *Before the [[Updates#2018.07.11|7/11/2018 Update]], King Beetle took two days to respawn
        after being defeated, granted more battle points and despawned after about 5 minutes.

        *King Beetle is a hybrid of a [[Rhino Beetle]] and a [[Ladybug]], as stated by Onett on
        Discord. [[File:BSS lore.PNG|thumb|220px|Onett stating that King Beetle is a hybrid on the
        Bee Swarm Simulator Discord Server.]]

        *Onett stated that the King Beetle emits toxins that makes the player hallucinate, which is
        why both he and his lair are strangely colored.

        *An active King Beetle's health bar can be seen through the Clover Field and the Blue Flower
        Field.

        *King Beetle is the only boss that doesn’t have a time limit to kill it.
