        {{Color|Oil}} is an [[:Category:Inventory|inventory item]] that was added in the
        [[Updates|11/25/2018 update]]. When used, it grants x1.2 [[Stats#Speed|Bee]] and [[System
        Page#Movespeed|Player Movespeed]] for 10 minutes when activated. Using another will reset
        the timer, but it does not stack. It can also be used to craft other
        [[:Category:Accessories|accessories]] or [[items]]. Using a [[Super Smoothie|super
        smoothie]] will override the buff.

        == Ways to Obtain ==
        * [[Crafting]] it via [[Blender]] for:
        ** {{Cost|Sunflower Seed|50}}
        ** {{Cost|Royal Jelly|10}}.
        * Matching a pair of oil in [[Memory Match]].
        * As a reward for donating to the [[Wind Shrine]].
        * As a common drop from [[Robo Bear Challenge]].
        * As a Stack Reward for adding certain [[Stickers]] to the [[Sticker Stack]]:
        ** Flying Photon Bee rewards 1 oil.
        ** Drooping Stubborn Bee rewards 1 oil.
        ** Fuzz Bomb rewards 1 oil.
        ** Yellow Coffee Mug rewards 1 oil.
        ** White Flag rewards 1 oil.
        ** Standing Beekeeper rewards 1 oil.
        ** Honey Dipper rewards 1 oil.
        * Redeeming certain valid [[codes]]:
        ** Using the code \"SecretProfileCode\" (Activates the buff and other stuff).
        * As a rare gift from a [[Cub Buddy]].

        === Drops ===
        * As a drop from [[Wild Windy Bee]].
        * As a rare drop from a [[Mantis]], [[Werewolf]], [[Aphid#Armored Aphid|Armored Aphid]],
        [[King Beetle]], [[Tunnel Bear]], [[Stump Snail]], and the [[Coconut Crab]].
        * Farming in the [[Sunflower Field]] may rarely yield oil.
        ** Harvesting [[leaves]] in this field may also yield oil.
        * A rare drop from [[sprout]]s (the odds increase depending on the rarity of the sprout and
        if the sprout is in the Sunflower Field).
        * As a drop from a [[Ability Tokens#Festive Gift|Festive Gift]] (if [[Festive Bee]] is
        [[Gifted Bee|gifted]]) or [[Ability Tokens#Festive Mark|Festive Mark]].
        * As a rare gift from a [[Cub Buddy|cub buddy]].
        * As a rare drop from harvesting [[planter]]s. More likely to be dropped if harvested from
        the Sunflower Field.
        ** Fully grown [[Planter]]s in Sunflower Field are almost guaranteed to drop a oil when
        harvested (it can drop tickets instead)

        === Quests ===
        * [[Black Bear|Black Bear's]] repeatable quests have a chance of yielding oil after
        completion.
        * Completing the 35th, 105th, 160th, 185th, 255th, and 285th [[Brown Bear]] quests will
        reward 1 oil each.
        * [[Science Bear]] gives oil on the following quests:
        ** Subsidized Agriculture gives 1 oil and other items.
        ** Meditating On Phenomenon gives 1 oil and other items.
        * [[Polar Bear]] occasionally gives oil after the completion of one of his quests.
