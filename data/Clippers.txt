        ==Trivia==
        *The Clippers is the smaller and weaker version of the [[Scissors]] with the color of the
        handles changed to yellow.
        *The Clippers, [[Electro-Magnet]], [[Golden Rake]], [[Spark Staff]], [[Porcelain Dipper]],
        [[Petal Wand]], [[Dark Scythe]], [[Tide Popper]] and [[Gummyballer]] are the only tools
        added in an update.
        *These are one of the three [[items]] in the game that can cut the vines in front of the
        parkour leading to [[Commando Chick's Hideout]], the others being the Scissors and the
        [[Scythe]].
        *These and the Scissors are the only tools that target one flower.
