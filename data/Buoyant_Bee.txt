        '''Buoyant Bee''' is a [[:Category:Blue|Blue]] [[Bees/Mythic|Mythic bee]].

        Buoyant Bee’s favorite type of treat is [[Blueberry|Blueberries]].

        Buoyant Bee likes the [[Blue Flower Field]], [[Bamboo Field]], [[Mountain Top Field]], and
        [[Coconut Field]]. It does not dislike any Field.

        == Stats ==

        * Collects 15 [[Pollen]] in 5 seconds.
        * Makes 150 [[Honey]] in 3 seconds.
        * +3 [[Stats#Attack|Attack]], -25% Gather Speed, +5 [[Stats#Gather Amount|Gather Amount]],
        +25% Convert Speed, +80 [[System Page#Convert Amount|Convert Amount]], +200% [[Energy]].
        *🌟[[Gifted Bee|Gifted Hive Bonus]]: x1.2 [[Capacity]].

        === Abilities ===

        * '''[[Ability Tokens#Bomb|[Blue Bomb]]]''' Collects 10 pollen from 13 surrounding Blue
        [[Flowers]] (+10% pollen per Level). Combo with other bombs to increase power.
        * '''[[Ability Tokens#Inflate%20Balloons|[Inflate Balloons]]]''' Increases the pollen in
        [[Balloon]]s on your field by 5% (+0.01% per Level), then spawns a Blue Balloon for 20s (+2s
        per level) with capacity that scales with bee level and your capacity.
        ** Pollen collected from flowers near (the shaded ones) the balloon is increased by 10% (20%
        if blue) and added to the balloon instead of your [[Items#Bags|Container]]. Upon filling up
        or timing out, the balloon floats back to your [[Hive]]. Converting from a balloon at your
        hive grants bonus Capacity and [[System Page#Honey At Hive|Honey at Hive]] for 1 hour.
        ** Standing near a balloon grants a small bonus to Pollen, Blue Pollen, and Honey from
        Tokens which stacks up to 10 times.
        ** If Gifted, this has a 5% (+0.5% per level, doubled when the field is boosted) chance to
        summon a Gold Balloon instead, which has x2 Capacity and an additional x1.25 Pollen instead.
        * '''[[Ability Tokens#Surprise%20Party|[🌟Gifted Ability: Surprise Party]]]''' Summons a
        Gold Balloon (+1 every 5 levels) for all players, then causes all Balloons to spawn random
        ability tokens for a few seconds. Gold Balloons gain bonus pollen (increases with bee level)
        and transform nearby [[Passive Abilities#Gathering Bubbles|Bubbles]] into Gold Bubbles,
        which collect 50% more pollen and have a chance to spawn [[Ability Tokens#Honey Gift|Honey
        Tokens]]. If you have triggered a Surprise Party within the last 8 minutes, performs
        \"Inflate Balloons\" instead (always spawns a Gold Balloon). When a Surprise Party is
        activated, a server wide message will say \"🎉Surprise Balloons from [Player's Display
        Name].\"
        * '''[[Passive Abilities#Balloon%20Enthusiast|[Passive: Balloon Enthusiast]]]''' When
        converting from Balloons, this bee has x3 convert amount. Additionally, this bee's base
        attack scales up to x3 with [[Buffs & Debuffs#From Areas|Balloon Blessing]].

        == Trivia ==
        * This bee, [[Digital Bee]], [[Windy Bee]], and [[Festive Bee]] are the only bees that can
        grant boosts to all the players on the server.
        * This bee, [[Commander Bee]] and [[Frosty Bee]] are the only 3 [[bees]] in the game that
        wear hats.
        ** As such, the Buoyant Bee is the only hat-wearing bee that is also a Mythic.
        * This is the only bee to not dislike any field.
        **[[Onett (Developer)|Onett]] stated on Discord that the reason why Buoyant Bee has no
        disliked fields is that it's supposed to be a \"happy\" bee.
        * The Buoyant Bee and the [[Lion Bee]] have the most amount of finite [[Energy]].
        * The Buoyant Bee, [[Precise Bee]], and [[Fuzzy Bee]] are the only [[Bees/Mythic|Mythic
        bees]] to be added since the release of the original three [[Bees/Mythic|Mythic bees]].
        * The Buoyant Bee and the [[Precise Bee]] are the only two bees to have their own Robux pack
        in the same update.
        * The Buoyant Bee and [[Windy Bee]] are the only bees to be able to produce a White Boost
        token. Buoyant Bee, however, only produces it through Surprise Parties, meaning it has to be
        gifted in order to produce white boost tokens.
        * If you have a lot of Balloon Blessing, the bee will have the same attack as [[Lion Bee]]
        because of the Balloon Enthusiast passive.
        * If the player is not in a field when another player in the same server activates a
        Surprise Party, the subtitle for it will appear on the player's screen but won't summon any
        Golden Balloons.
        * Gifted Buoyant Bee's hat glows.
        * This bee’s gifted form is similar to [[Bumble Bee|Bumble Bee's]] gifted appearance.
        ** Coincidentally, both Buoyant Bee and Bumble Bee are the only bees to have gifted
        abilities that multiply a player’s base capacity.
