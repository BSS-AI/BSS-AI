        The {{Color|Red Clay Planter}} is a reusable [[planter]] added in the 12/26/21 update. It
        grows in 6 hours and stores 3 million [[Pollen]]. It grants bonus [[Red Extract]]s and
        [[Waxes]]. It can be purchased in the [[Red HQ]].

        The planter grows 25% faster in fields with red flowers (4.5 hours ) and it gives 50% bonus
        red pollen from harvests. Red bees are 25% more likely to sip [[Nectar]] from it, but Blue
        bees avoid sipping nectar from it. This planter grants 20% more Invigorating and Satisfying
        Nectar. Harvesting a fully grown Red Clay Planter has a 1/8 (12.5%) chance to spawn a
        [[Puffshroom]].
