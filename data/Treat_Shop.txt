        The '''Treat Shop''' is a [[:Category:Shops|shop]] located to the left of [[Mother Bear]].
        It sells [[Treat|treats]] at a base cost of 100 [[honey]]. The treat price increases by 100
        honey for every treat bought after the first, capping at 10,000 honey for one treat.

        A player can buy treats in increments of 1, 10, 100, 1000, 10,000, 100,000, 1,000,000,
        10,000,000, 100,000,000, and 1,000,000,000.
