{"text": "{{Consumables
        |image = Glue.png
        |description = Grants x1.25 Bee Gather Pollen and Tools for 10 minutes.
        |cooldown = 1 second
        }}'''Glue''' is a craftable [[:Category:Inventory|inventory item]] added in the
        [[Updates#2018.11.25|11/25/2018 update]]. Its purpose is as a creating material for
        [[:Category:Accessories|accessories]], or as a 10 minute boost that grants x1.25 [[System
        Page#Bee Gather Pollen|Bee Gather Pollen]] and [[System Page#Pollen From Tools|Tools]] when
        used directly. The [[Buffs & Debuffs|buff]] cannot stack and will only reset its timer
        when another glue is used when one is currently active. Using a [[Purple Potion|Purple
        potion]] or [[Super Smoothie|Super smoothie]] will override the buff.

        == Ways to Obtain ==
        * Crafting it in the [[Blender]] for 50 [[Gumdrops]] and 10 [[Royal Jelly|Royal Jellies]].
        * Redeeming certain valid [[codes]]:
        ** Using the code \"SecretProfileCode\" (Activates the buff and other stuff).
        *[[Black Bear]] gives glue on the following quests:
        ** All-Whitey Then gives 1 glue and other items.
        ** Black Bear's repeatable quests occasionally reward glue.
        *[[Brown Bear]] will give glue on the completion of these quests:
        ** Brown Bear's 140th quest rewards 1 glue.
        ** Brown Bear's 235th quest rewards 1 glue.
        **Brown Bear's 310th quest rewards 3 glues.
        **Brown Bear's 395th quest rewards 5 glues.
        *[[Science Bear]] gives glue on the following quests:
        **Chemical Analysis rewards 1 glue and other items.
        **Epistemological Endeavor gives 4 glues and other items.
        *[[Polar Bear]] occasionally gives glue after the completion of his quests.
        * As a rare drop from [[mobs]] such as the [[Spider]], [[Werewolf]], [[Puffshroom]], [[King
        Beetle]], and [[Coconut Crab]], or guaranteed from the [[Stump Snail]].
        * As a drop from [[Robo Bear Challenge]] (The chances are affected by the round the
        challenge end at)
        *A drop from [[Aphid#Armored Aphids|Armored aphids]].
        *Small chance of dropping from the [[Dandelion Field]], [[Pumpkin Patch]], and the [[Stump
        Field]] in place of a honey token.
        *A rare drop from [[leaves]] that appear in the [[Pineapple Patch]], [[Dandelion Field]],
        [[Pumpkin Patch]], [[Stump Field]], or [[Spider Field]].
        *A rare drop when collecting [[pollen]] in the [[Pineapple Patch]], [[Spider Field]], or the
        [[Pumpkin Patch]].
        *Rarely, a glue token can appear from collecting [[goo]] from [[flowers]].
        *You can claim 2-5 Glues from the [[Glue Dispenser]] depending on your Goo [[Badges|Badge]].
        Hotshot rewards 2, Ace rewards 3, Master rewards 4, and Grandmaster rewards 5 Glues. The
        Glue Dispenser is located inside [[Gummy Bear's Lair]].
        * As a drop from [[Sprout|Gummy Sprouts]].
        *As a rare drop from [[Ability Tokens#Festive Gift|Festive Gifts]] (if [[Festive Bee]] is
        [[Gifted Bee|gifted]]).
        *As a rare drop from [[Ability Tokens#Festive Mark|Festive Mark]].
        *In a crevice between the [[Pineapple Patch]] and the [[Stump Field]].
        *As a badge reward by donating to the [[Wind Shrine]].
        *Matching a pair of glue in [[Memory Match]].
        *Capturing [[Commando Chick]] the 5th time drops 1 Glue.
        *As a rare drop from the [[Candy Planter]], [[Blue Clay Planter]], [[Pesticide Planter]] and
        [[Festive Planter]].
        ** Planting the Candy Planter in the [[Stump Field]] 3 times in a row will guarantee at
        least 10 Glues (Has a cooldown, can give 20 glues)
        *As a Stack Reward for adding certain [[Stickers]] to the [[Sticker Stack]]:
        **Right Facing Stump Snail rewards 1 glue.
        **Yellow Sticker Hand rewards 1 glue.
        **Glowering Gummy Bear Rewards 1 glue.
        **
        ===The methods below are no longer possible. ===
        * Buying the Buoyant Pack for 800 Robux gave 25 glue.
        *Purchasing the Loaded Bundle gave 15 glues in [[Bee Bear's Catalog]] during Beesmas 2021.
        *Purchasing the Booster Bundle gave 1 glue in Bee Bear's Catalog during Beesmas 2020.
        * Giving a [[present]] to certain NPCs
        **Science Bear gave 3 glue, activated the buff, and gave other stuff.
        **[[Onett]] activated the buff and gave other stuff.
        *Opening the [[Ornament Presents|Gooey Present]] gave 10 glues.
        *Opening the [[Gift Boxes|Glowing Gift Box]] gave 10 glues and other items.
        *Opening the [[Gift Boxes|Gummy Gift Box]] gave 10 glues and other items

        *Before its nerf, Science Bear's Epistemological Endeavor quest gave 5 glue instead of 4.
        * [[Bee Bear]] gave glue on the following quests:
        **By Golly, Get Jolly gave 1 glue.
        **Blue Beesmas gave 1 glue.
        **Beequips and Berries Make Me So Merry gave 1 glue.
        **All I want For Beesmas Is Goo gave 3 glues.
        **Snow Cub Reformation (4/5) gave 3 glues.
        *Redeeming certain expired codes:
        **Using the code \"2YearsAfterParty\" (Activated the buff and other stuff).
        **Using the code \"Discord50k\" (Activated the buff and other stuff).
        **Using the code \"Willgoold\" (Gave 1 glue and activated the buff and other stuff).
        **Using the code \"GummySausage\" (Activated the buff and other stuff).
        **Using the code \"HappyNewYear\" (Activated the buff and other stuff).
        *Purchasing the Nighttime Nectar Pack for 400 Robux gives 20 Glues + other items.
        *Purchasing the Kazoo Bundle for 25 [[Gingerbread Bear]]<nowiki/>s gives 20 Glues +
        other items.
        *As a common drop from [[Robo Bear Challenge]]
        *As a reward from the [[Robo Bear's Party Cake]]
        *Giving a present to Brown Bear gives 2 glue and other items
        *[[Onett's Lid Art]] has a chance of dropping glue and other stuff.
        *Opening the [[Ornament Presents|Gooey Gift Box]] gives 10 glues and other items.
        *The candy planter is a good way to get Glue by either giving glue or gumdrops that can be
        used to craft glue
