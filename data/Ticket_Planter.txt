        A '''Ticket Planter''' is a single-use [[planter]] that always drops [[ticket]]s when
        harvested. It's also guaranteed to spawn a [[puffshroom]] at 100% growth.

        Fully grown Ticket Planters gives 10+ tickets upon harvesting''.''

        == Ways to obtain ==
        * [[Dapper Bear]] gives 3 Ticket Planters if the player speaks to him for the first time.
        * If the player completes Dapper Bear’s quests and they already got Beequip slots from Bee
        Bear in previous years, they get a Ticket Planter for each Beequip slot.

        == Trivia ==

        * This is one of the two planters that is guaranteed to spawn a [[puffshroom]]. The other is
        the [[Sticker Planter]].
        * This, the [[Sticker Planter]] and the [[Paper Planter]] share the same base model.
        ** They also share the same outline for their decals.
        * It is the only planter that mentions an in-game item in its name.
        * It gives the second-best nectar multiplier in the game, 2x, behind the [[Festive Planter]]
        with 3x.
        * If a player never uses the [[Samovar]], the current max ticket planters they can get is
        25.
        * It is one of 4 planters that aren't reusable. Others being the [[Paper Planter]], the
        [[Festive Planter]] and the [[Sticker Planter]].
