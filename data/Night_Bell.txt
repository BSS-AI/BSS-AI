        A '''Night Bell''' is an [[:Category:Inventory|inventory item]] that was added in the
        12/25/2018 update. When used, it summons [[Day/Night Cycle|nighttime]], along with a
        [[Sprout|Moon Sprout]] and a [[Rogue Vicious Bee]].

        Whenever a player activates a night bell, the sound of a bell chiming will play and a
        server-wide message will appear, saying \"<span
        style=\"background:silver;color:white\">🌙 {Username} has summoned nighttime...
        🌙</span>\".
        [[File:RobloxScreenShot20190206 162036650.png|thumb|500px|The server-wide message when a
        player in the server uses a night bell.]]

        Upon using a night bell, the server will receive the public announcement above, along with
        the following sound file below.<br/>[[File:Bells.ogg]]

        ==Ways of obtaining==
        * As a rare drop from [[aphid]]s, [[Werewolf|werewolves]], [[King Beetle]], [[Wild Windy
        Bee]]s, [[fireflies]], [[Coconut Crab]] and [[Tunnel Bear]].
        * As a rare reward from [[Memory Match#Night Memory Match|Night Memory Match]].
        * Buying them from the [[Robux Shop]] for 80 robux each.
        * As a Playtime Master [[Badges|badge]] reward from donating [[enzymes]] to the [[Wind
        Shrine]].
        * Completing [[Spirit Bear]]'s \"Do You Bee-lieve In Magic?\" quest rewards 1 night bell.
        * As a rare drop from a [[Festive Planter]].

        ===The methods below are no longer possible.===
        * As a rare drop from [[Stockings]].
        * Completing [[Bee Bear]]'s \"Bee Cub Workshop 5\" quest rewarded 1 night bell.
        * Talking to [[Bubble Bee Man]] after completing his quest, \"B.B.M. Mission\" during Beesmas
        2018 granted 1 night bell.
        * Opening the Brass [[Gift Boxes|Gift Box]] during Beesmas 2020 gave 4 night bells and other
        items.
        * Buying the Night Bundle in [[Bee Bear’s Catalog]] during Beesmas 2020 gave 1 Night Bell
        and other items.
        * As a rare reward from [[Winter Memory Match]].
        * As a rare drop from [[Festive Nymph]]s.
        * Purchasing the Night Bundle in [[Bee Bear's Catalog]] for 1,000 [[snowflake]]s gave 1
        night bell and other items.
        * By purchasing certain packs in the Robux Shop:
        ** Purchasing the Happy Honeyday Pack for 900 robux gave 10 night bells and other items.
        ** Purchasing the Stocking Stuffer Pack for 450 robux gave 5 night bells and other items.
        ** Purchasing the Silent Night Special for 800 robux gave 5 night bells and other items.
        ** Purchasing the Cyber Monday Pack for 800 robux gave 5 night bells and other items.
        ** Purchasing the Nighttime Nectar Pack for 400 robux gave 5 night bells and other items.
        ** Purchasing the Cyber Monday Special for 800 robux gave 5 night bells and other items.

        ==Trivia==
        *If a player attempts to use a Night Bell when it is already night, they will get a message
        that says “You can only use Night Bells during the day.”
        *If you summon night-time right after night-time ends a new set of fireflies will be spawned
        when you can see the other fireflies fly away.
        **The sky will also flash if you summon night-time even quicker (when the sky is changing
        from night to day).
        *Night Bells and [[Box-O-Frogs]] are the only two consumable items that summon mobs.
        **The Night Bell summons two types of mobs, Fireflies and Rogue Vicious Bee.
