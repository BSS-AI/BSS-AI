        Nectar Vials are a type of inventory [[Items|item]] that can be used to increase a player's
        current [[Nectar]] by 4 hours when used. Using any '''Nectar Vial''' grants 4 hours of their
        respective nectar type. You can hold a maximum of 10 Nectar Vials of each type at a time.
        However, this limit can be bypassed by obtaining them through [[Quests]] or Robux Packs.

        == Ways to Obtain ==
        * Using the [[Nectar Condenser]] to convert 12 hours of nectar into a Nectar Vial which
        stores 4 hours of nectar.
        * Adding the Satisfying, Refreshing, Motivating, Invigorating, and Comforting Nectar Icon
        [[sticker]]s to the [[Sticker Stack]] will reward the player with their respective nectar
        vial.
        * As a reward from certain quests.
        ** Completing several [[Dapper Bear]] quests reward the player with at least one of every
        nectar vial.
        *** [[Satisfying Vial]]s:
        **** Means of Mutation (5) rewards 1 Satisfying Vial and other items.
        **** The Trick To Tickets (11) rewards 1 Satisfying Vial and other items.
        **** More Than Myth (15) rewards 1 Satisfying Vial and other items.
        *** [[Comforting Vial]]s:
        **** Creative Comforts (6) rewards 1 Comforting Vial and other items.
        **** The Long Haul (10) rewards 1 Comforting Vial and other items.
        **** Ongoing Maintenance (13) rewards 1 Comforting Vial and other items.
        **** ''Barter For The Blue Drive'' rewards 1 Comforting Vial and other items.
        *** [[Refreshing Vial]]s:

        == Variants ==
        There is a total of five variants of Nectar Vial that immediately grant 4 hours of a type of
        Nectar buff to the player. The type of Nectar that the player is granted depends on the type
        of Nectar Vial that they choose to use.

        === Comforting Vial ===
        Comforting Vials instantly give the player 4 hours of the Comforting Nectar [[Buffs &
        Debuffs|buff]].

        === Invigorating Vial ===
        Invigorating Vials instantly give the player 4 hours of the Invigorating Nectar buff.

        === Motivating Vial ===
        Motivating Vials instantly give the player 4 hours of the Motivating Nectar buff.

        === Refreshing Vial ===
        Refreshing Vials instantly give the player 4 hours of the Refreshing Nectar buff.

        === Satisfying Vial ===
        Satisfying Vials instantly give the player 4 hours of the Satisfying Nectar buff.

        == Trivia ==

        * While most of the Nectar Vials have a letter on them which represents the first letter of
        their name (C for Comforting, I for Invigorating, M for Motivating, S for Satisfying), the
        Refreshing Vial doesn't have an R on it to represent the first letter of its name.
        * There is only a limited amount of Nectar Vials that can be obtained by the player without
        the use of the [[Nectar Condenser]].
        ** For Comforting Vials, the total amount that can be obtained is 23.
        ** For Invigorating Vials, the total amount that can be obtained is 13.
        ** For Motivating Vials, the total amount that can be obtained is 10.
        ** For Refreshing Vials, the total amount that can be obtained is 11.
        ** For Satisfying Vials, the total amount that can be obtained is 16.
