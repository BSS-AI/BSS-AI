        The '''Magnet''' is a [[:Category:Tools|tool]] that can be purchased at the [[Noob Shop]].

        {| class=\"article-table\"
        !Crafting Ingredients
        |-
        |{{Cost|Honey|5,500}}
        |}

        It collects 2 [[pollen]] from 9 surrounding patches every 0.8 seconds. It has a base pollen
        collection rate of 22.5 pollen per second on average, making it the second best tool in the
        Noob Shop.

        ==Appearance==
        The magnet is consisted of a magnet colored red with white tips. The pole is a shade of
        grey.

        ==Trivia==
        *According to LunaTalks, an [[BSS Staff|administrator]] on the official Bee Swarm Simulator
        Discord, the Magnet was the first tool [[Onett (Developer)|Onett]] ever made when developing
        the game.
        *The Magnet is a weaker version of the [[Electro-Magnet]], a tool that can be purchased in
        the [[Pro Shop]] for 300,000 honey.
        **In addition, the Electro-Magnet bears a different appearance to the Magnet; instead of the
        body color being red, it is yellow. In addition, it has four poles instead of two, and there
        is electricity in between the poles, unlike the Magnet.
        **The two are the only tools to collect pollen in a square pattern. They also have the same
        exact collecting pattern.
        *Interestingly, the Magnet's associated sticker portrays a different gray magnet with
        colored ends.
        **This is the only tool sticker in the entire game that is completely different from the
        original tool's appearance.
        *The Magnet’s design is most likely based upon a real-life horseshoe magnet.
