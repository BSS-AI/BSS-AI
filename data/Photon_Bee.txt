        == Trivia ==
        *It was the first Event bee to be added in the Ticket Tent and the second Event bee added to
        the game, with [[Bear Bee]] being the first.
        **It was also the first Event bee that is purchased using tickets.
        *This bee and the [[Ninja Bee]] are tied as the fastest [[Bees]] in the game, with a speed
        of 21.
        *Photon and [[Exhausted Bee]] are the only bees that don't require sleep and have infinite
        energy. However, they still need to rest at the hive when you die.
        *This bee, [[Festive Bee]] and [[Vicious Bee]] are the only Event bees that have [[Ability
        Tokens]] generated by non-Event bees.
        *This bee, Ninja Bee, [[Cobalt Bee]], [[Tadpole Bee]], [[Windy Bee]], [[Crimson Bee]], and
        [[Precise Bee]] are the only bees that create a trail behind them (not counting particle
        trails).
        *This is first event bee with a trail.
        **[[Gummy Bee]] also creates a trail, but it can only be seen while the [[Gummy Mask]]
        ability is active.
        *This bee, Bear Bee, Festive Bee, Gummy Bee, and [[Tabby Bee]] are the only bees to have a
        [[Gifted Bee#List of Hive Bonuses|Gifted Bonus]] that affects their signature ability.
        **If Photon Bee is gifted, its Beamstorm is white instead of yellow and instantly converts
        its collected pollen.
        *The sun model on Photon Bee's rear has 20 spikes.
        *Because Photon Bee has infinite energy, there is a [[Glitches|Glitch]] if a [[Royal
        Jelly|Royal jelly]] is used on a Photon Bee, the transformed bee will have a ridiculous
        amount of energy.
        **This glitch is also shared when transforming an Exhausted Bee, since that has endless
        energy as well.
        *All versions of the Ticket Tent have had this bee on top, except for the initial release.
        *Although Photon Bee's energy is infinite, it is still possible to get an energy
        [[Mutation]] on Photon Bee. However, the mutation will have no effect, similar to an attack
        mutation on a Baby Bee.
