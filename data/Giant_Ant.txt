        This is one of the last Ant Challenge [[mobs]] that the player will encounter, appearing
        every fifth wave.

        Giant Ants are enormous versions of the normal ant that relentlessly pursue the player. They
        have a relatively slow movespeed. However, they have large amounts of health, more than any
        other type of ant, and high attack power, also more than any other type of ant, to
        compensate for their slow speed.

        A Giant Ant's level can increase depending on what wave of the Ant Challenge the player is
        on. They can range from level 1 up to level 16.

        Giant Ants are one of the most threatening of the five ant types, as they are have high
        amounts of health and chase the player nonstop.

        == Strategies ==

        * Due to the Giant Ant's high health, they can 'tank' hits from [[bees]] and allow the
        smaller, faster ants to attack the player with a much lower chance of being defeated. Their
        size makes them much harder to avoid. The player should bring bees with high attack such as
        [[Lion Bee]] to deal enough damage to defeat Giant Ants.
        ** The player can also use a [[Stinger]] to help deal extra damage against the Giant Ant.
        * They always appear every fifth wave on a side of the map so the player can prepare for
        their arrival
        * [[Vicious Bee|Vicious Bee's]]
        [https://bee-swarm-simulator.fandom.com/wiki/Ability_Tokens?so=search#Impale Impale] can
        deal high amounts of damage to single targets which can help take away tons of health from
        Giant Ants. [[Windy Bee|Windy Bee's]]
        [https://bee-swarm-simulator.fandom.com/wiki/Ability_Tokens?so=search#Tornado Tornado] can
        help chip away a Giant Ant's health and also take out other ants that are hiding behind the
        Giant Ant. [[Digital Bee]] can also stun Giant Ants and allow them to take extra damage via
        [[Ability Tokens#Mind Hack|Mind Hack]].

        == Trivia ==

        * Giant Ants are the only ant to follow a spawn pattern where they appear every five waves
        (5th, 10th, 15th, etc.).
        ** Every 40th wave has two Giant Ants spawn instead of one.
        * They are the only ant to have health in the thousands.
