        The '''Blueberry Dispenser''' is a [[dispenser]] located on the first floor of the [[Blue
        HQ]], next to the [[Blue Teleporter]]. When used, it grants the player [[honey]], [[Ability
        Tokens#Haste|haste]] x5, [[Ability Tokens#Boost|blue boost]] x10, and 1
        [[Blueberry|blueberry]] for each [[:Category:Blue|blue]] [[bees|bee]] in the player's
        [[hive]].

        It has a cooldown of 4 hours.

        If the player does not have any blue bees in their hive, it will not grant any blueberries.
        The player must be in the [[Bee Swarm Simulator Club]] in order to use this.

        == Trivia ==
        * Before the [[Updates#2018.11.25|11/25/2018 update]], the Blueberry Dispenser was in the
        [[Gifted Bucko Bee]]'s spot. However it was moved after said update to make room for the
        NPC.
        * The only time this dispenser appears in a quest is in [[Science Bear|Science Bear's]]
        ''\"The Power of Information\"''.
        * This dispenser gives the same honey per bee as the [[Strawberry Dispenser]].
        * This is one of three dispensers that give [[treats]] in the game with the other two being
        the [[Treat Dispenser]] and the Strawberry Dispenser.
        * This is one of four dispensers requiring the player to be part of the Bee Swarm Simulator
        Club with the others being the [[Honey Dispenser]], the Treat Dispenser, and the Strawberry
        Dispenser.
