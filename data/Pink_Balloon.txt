        The '''Pink Balloon''' is a [[:Category:Consumables|consumable]] [[Items#Misc. Items|item]]
        that was added in the [[Updates|12/26/2021 update]]. Once used, it creates a pink
        [[balloon]] over the [[Fields|field]] the player is in. The balloon floats over the field,
        storing [[pollen]] collected underneath it. The pollen collected below is doubled. It holds
        up to double the player's capacity. It floats over the field for 3 minutes before floating
        to the [[hive]] balloon.

        Pink balloons have a cap of 25 in the player's [[:Category:Inventory|inventory]], and 1 per
        player in the field.

        == Ways to Obtain ==
        * As a drop from certain [[mobs]].
        * Using the code \"ThnxCyasToyBox\" (Gives 1 Pink Balloon and other stuff).
        * As a Reward From Retro Swarm Challenge.
