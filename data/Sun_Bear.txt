        '''Sun Bear''' was a Traveling Bear who appeared during Egg Hunts. He was one of the five
        temporary Quest Givers, the others being [[Bee Bear]], [[Gummy Bear]], [[Stick Bug]], and
        [[Bubble Bee Man]].

        He made four appearances. His first visit was from April 27, 2018, to May 13, 2018. He gave
        12 quests with rewards including the [[Belt Bag]] and a final reward of a [[Mondo Belt
        Bag]]. His second visit was from September 10, 2018, to October 8, 2018. He gave 14 quests
        with rewards including the [[Basic Boots]], the [[Hiking Boots]], and the [[Beekeeper's
        Boots]]. His third visit was from September 28, 2019, to October 28, 2019. He gave 10 quests
        themed around [[Bean Bugs|bean bugs]], with a final reward of 250 [[ticket]]s. His fourth
        visit was from April 6, 2020 to May 21, 2020, for the [[Egg Hunt 2020|Roblox Egg Hunt
        2020]].

        == Trivia ==
        *He was the only traveling b<nowiki/>ear to not introduce his own event bee.
        *In the quest ''''Focus Feature'''<nowiki/>', Sun Bear said to defeat a [[Werewolf]]
        instead of 3 [[Spider]]s.
        *The [[Badges#Ability Badge|Ability Badge]] is represented by him in the game's description.
        *After his spring visit, the belt bags can be bought in [[Pro Shop]] ([[Belt Bag]]) and the
        [[Mountain Top Shop]] ([[Mondo Belt Bag]]).
        *During his spring visit, there was a [[Ticket]] behind Sun Bear’s trailer. When Sun Bear
        went away, the ticket stayed there for a little time, but now it's gone.
        *Sun Bear uses the [https://web.roblox.com/catalog/734338331/Knight-Animation-Package Knight
        Animation Package].
        *Sun Bear says that only [[Rage Bee|Rage]], [[Exhausted Bee|Exhausted]], and [[Music
        Bee|Music]] Bees spawn [[Ability Tokens#Token Link|Token Link]], but [[Shocked Bee]] does as
        well. This was fixed a few days later.
        *In his second visit ([[Updates#2018.09.10|9/10/2018 Update]]) and his third visit
        ([[Updates#2019.09.28|9/28/2019 Update]]), his trailer had a
        [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Flag_of_Phoenix Flag of Phoenix] bumper sticker on the back.
        *During his first visit, Sun Bear was shown to be wearing the [[Mondo Belt Bag]], which he
        gave at the very end of his quests. During his second visit, he was shown to be wearing both
        the Mondo Belt bag and [[Beekeeper's Boots]], which he gave at the end of his quests.
        *Sun Bear gives 48 tickets throughout all his quests with the ticket token that was behind
        the trailer.
        *During Sun Bear's Scavenger Hunt, he says 3 more quests are needed to be completed to get
        the [[Beekeeper's Boots]], when it is actually 4 quests. However, [[Onett
        (Developer)|Onett]] could have done this on purpose, because Sun Bear was completely
        surprised by [[Rogue Vicious Bee|Vicious Bee]] in his dialogue, and gives you a quest around
        this.
        *He was the first traveling bear added to the game, with [[Gummy Bear]] being the second and
        [[Bee Bear]] being the third.
        *The item prices of his rewards after his visit are: [[Basic Boots]] are 4,400, [[Hiking
        Boots]] are 2,200,000, Beekeeper's Boots are 15,000,000, Belt Bag is 440,000, and Mondo Belt
        Bag is 12,400,000.
        *He was the only traveling bear who never gave an egg on his last quest.
        *Him and [[Bubble Bee Man]] were the only [[:Category:NPC|NPCs]] to give an
        [[:Category:Accessories|Accessory]].
        *It was hinted that Sun Bear is from Arizona, this is from the [[Codes|Code]]
        \"'''Arizona'''\", and the City of Arizona flag that was on his RV the second and the third
        visit.
        *During the quests in his 3rd visit, it is revealed that the reason he had [[Bean Bug]]s was
        that he was taking them to Orlando for an unknown reason.
        *In the quest 'Total Focus', Sun Bear said to collect 200 [[Ability Tokens#Focus|Focus
        Tokens]] before you received the quest, but he didn't say to collect 200 Focus Tokens after
        you received the quest.
        *For the 'Total Focus' quest, after you finish the dialogue with Sun Bear, a [[Memory
        Match]] game will start automatically for free.
        *He stated only [[Basic Bee]] doesn't generate tokens, but [[Brave Bee]] doesn't as well,
        even though both of them can generate [[Ability Tokens#Inspire|Inspire tokens]] when they
        are [[Gifted Bee|Gifted]].
        *To complete every quest on his 3rd visit, you must collect 3835 tokens from bean bugs.
        *It was possible for his trapped bean bug to jump out of its cage.
        **It was also possible for the player to go in and out of the bean bug's cage.
        *Onett has stated that Sun Bear isn't welcome on the mountain anymore for accidently
        releasing Bean Bugs and the Chicks.
