        The '''Honey Mask''' is a [[:Category:Hats|Hat Accessory]] that can be purchased at the Ace
        Shop in the [[Badge Bearer's Guild]].

        A player must have at least one [[Badges|Ace badge]] to enter the interior store. The mask
        will be there along with three other accessories: the [[Cobalt Guard]], [[Crimson Guard]],
        and the [[Honeycomb Belt]].

        The following audio plays, when Coin Scatter is activated:
        [[File:Coinscatter.ogg]]

        *[[Passive Abilities#Coin Scatter|+Passive: Coin Scatter]]:
        **The passive is activated every 20 [[Ability Tokens#Mark|Mark Tokens]]. When it does, 300%
        of the player's total [[System Page#Convert Total|Convert Total]] will be converted and
        scattered into [[Honey]] tokens (visible only to the player, Honey Tokens last one minute).
        (Cooldown: 2 Minutes)

        ==Trivia==
        *The Honey Mask, the [[Bubble Mask]], [[Fire Mask]], [[Bucko Guard]] and [[Riley Guard]] all
        represent [[Bees/Epic|epic bees]].
        *The Honey Mask and the [[Diamond Mask]] are the only [[items]] that require [[egg]]s to be
        [[Crafting|crafted]].
        *After the [[Updates#2019.04.05|4/5/2019 update]], the [[System Page#Instant
        Conversion|Instant Conversion]] buff was removed from the mask, along with all of the other
        masks.
        *After the [[Updates#2019.09.28|9/28/2019 update]], the Coin Scatter [[Passive
        Abilities|passive ability]] was added to the mask.
        **If the player owns the Honey Mask, its passive is inherited by the [[Gummy Mask]].
        *If the ingredients to craft the Honey Mask item were to be crafted, the Honey Mask mask
        costs 100,000,000 [[Honey]], 9,999 Treats, 2,500 [[Sunflower Seed|Sunflower Seeds]], 1,250
        [[Pineapple|Pineapples]], 750 [[Royal Jelly|Royal Jellies]], and 5 [[Egg#Gold Egg|Gold
        Eggs]]. It also takes approximately 375 minutes (6 hours and 15 minutes) to craft all of the
        required items.
        * The Honey & Gummy Masks are the only masks in the game that require a specific
        [[Badges|Badge]] to access and purchase.
        *During Beesmas 2020 and Beesmas 2021, in order to give [[Honey Bee (NPC)|Honey Bee]] a
        [[Present]], the player had to equip the Honey Mask before doing so.
        *Token links can't collect honey tokens from Coin Scatter. However, [[Vector Bee|Vector
        Bee's]] Triangulate can.
