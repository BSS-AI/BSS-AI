        The '''Scooper''' is a [[:Category:Tools|tool]] that new players automatically receive at
        the beginning of the game, along with the [[Pouch]].

        It collects 2 [[pollen]] from 2 patches in front of it with a speed of 0.8 seconds, which
        equals a base pollen collection rate of 5 pollen per second.

        It can be equipped in the [[Noob Shop]].

        == Appearance ==
        The scooper resembles a shovel with a grey blade and handle and a brown pole made of
        presumably wood.

        == Trivia ==
        * This tool is currently the least effective tool at collecting pollen, because its base
        statistics are lower than any of every other tool in the game.
        * The [[Super-Scooper]] is the upgraded version of this. It can be purchased in the [[Pro
        Shop]].
        * This is the only tool in the game that doesn’t cost any [[honey]].
        * This is the third slowest tool in the game, surpassed by the [[Honey Dipper]] and
        [[Pulsar]]/[[Gummyballer]].
        * This, the [[Pouch]], and a [[Egg|Basic Egg]] are the only items that the player starts off
        with when joining the game for the very first time.
