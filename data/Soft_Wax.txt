        {{Color|Soft Wax}} is an [[:Category:Inventory|inventory item]] and a type of [[Waxes|wax]]
        added in the [[Updates#2021.12.26|12/26/2021 update]] that can be used on a [[Beequip]] to
        boost its stats. Upon usage, it has a 100% chance to slightly improve the Beequip's stats.

        It also can be used for crafting items and tools, such as [[Planter]]s, and other types of
        wax, such as, {{Cost|Hard Wax|||0}}, {{Cost|Swirled Wax|||0}}, and {{Cost|Caustic Wax|||0}}.
        These can all be made in the [[Blender]].

        When giving soft wax to a bee's beequip, the message says “The Wax has Improved the
        Beequip!”.

        == Ways to Obtain ==
        * [[Crafting]] via [[Blender]] for:
        ** {{Cost|Honeysuckle|5}}
        ** {{Cost|Oil|1}}
        ** {{Cost|Enzymes|1}}
        ** {{Cost|Royal Jelly|10}}
        * As a stack reward for adding certain [[Stickers]] to the [[Sticker Stack]]:
        ** Window rewards 3 soft waxes.
        ** TNT rewards 3 soft waxes.
        ** Jack-O-Lantern rewards 3 soft waxes.
        ** Barcode rewards 3 soft waxes.

        ** Morel Mushroom rewards 3 soft waxes.
        ** Chanterelle Mushroom rewards 3 soft waxes.
        ** Porcelain Dipper rewards 3 soft waxes.
