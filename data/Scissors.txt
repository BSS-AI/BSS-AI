        The Scissors collect all [[pollen]] from the patch in front of you in 0.5 seconds. It has a
        maximum base pollen collection rate of 120 pollen per second.

        ==Trivia==
        *The Scissors are an enhanced version of the [[Clippers]]. The main difference in appearance
        is that the Clippers are smaller and have yellow handles while the Scissors have red and
        blue handles.
        *This is one of the four [[items]] in the game that can cut the vines in front of the
        parkour leading to [[Commando Chick's Hideout]], the others being the Clippers, the
        [[Scythe]], and the [[Dark Scythe]].
        *This and the [[Spark Staff]] are the only tools that collect all the pollen from the
        flowers that they hit.
        *This and the Clippers are the only tools that collect pollen from a single flower.
