        The '''Spider''' is a level 4 [[Mobs|Mob]] that can only be found in the [[Spider Field]].

        It takes 30 minutes for the spider to respawn after being defeated (25 minutes 30 seconds
        with a [[Gifted Bee|Gifted]] [[Vicious Bee]]). Defeating it gives 10 [[Battle Points]] and
        1000 [[Honey]] spread across as 5 honey tokens. Various other rewards are also possible with
        their probability increasing with [[Loot Luck]].

        == Strategies ==
        * One way to keep idle while defeating the spider is to stay on the first block in the
        [[Obstacle Courses#Bamboo Field Obby|Bamboo Obby]] - the spider will try to lunge towards
        the player, but it will be stopped by the block. The player can also stand near the edge of
        the [[Vicious Bee Egg Claim]].

        * The [[Glitches#Frozen Field Defenders|Frozen Field Defenders Glitch]] can be used to kill
        the spider easily, just like any other normal mob.

        * Another method is to charge at the spider, then jump and glide over the spider, though
        this will require the [[Parachute]] or [[Glider]].

        == Trivia ==
        * This, the [[Mantis]] and the [[Werewolf]] are the only mobs that can attack players
        outside of their designated fields.

        * There is a [[Ticket]] token located within its residence (which is the Spider Cave).

        * The Spider gives more honey and gives better rewards than Mantises and [[Scorpion]]s
        despite them being in higher-tier fields.

        * The Spider is tied with the Werewolf as the regular mobs with the least amount of spawn
        locations, as they both only have one.

        * Unlike real-life spiders, who have eight legs and six/eight eyes, the spider only has six
        legs and four eyes. It also appears to have a head and thorax, despite spiders and other
        arachnids having a cephalothorax. This is joked about in the Forward Facing Spider
        [[Stickers|sticker]]'s description, which reads: ''\"Not enough legs to technically be a
        spider.\"''

        * Two creatures similar to the spider in appearance called [[Cave Monster]]s are found
        inside the [[Werewolf's Cave]]. However, they have vastly different stats.
