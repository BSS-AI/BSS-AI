        == Trivia ==
        * They could have been obtained from [[Sun Bear]] by completing his last quest before he
        left on 10/8/2018.
        *To craft these boots using the [[Blender]], a total of 15,000,000 honey, 110 [[Royal
        Jelly|royal jellies]], 150 [[Strawberry|strawberries]], 150 [[Blueberry|blueberries]], and
        250 [[Sunflower Seed|sunflower seeds]] must be collected. Also, keep in mind that crafting
        the materials requires a total of 55 minutes of playtime without speeding it up using
        [[ticket]]s.
        * [[Onett]] wears a pair of Beekeeper's Boots. He doesn't wear the [[Gummy Boots]] or the
        [[Coconut Clogs]] because they were added later in the [[Updates#2018.11.25|11/25/2018
        update]] and [[Updates#2019.09.28|9/28/2019 updates]] respectively.
        ** Onett didn't wear any boots prior to the [[Updates#2018.09.10|9/10/2018 update]], as
        boots weren't added then. Shortly after the 9/10/2018 update, this was changed to Onett's
        current gear today.
        * The version worn by Sun Bear is much bigger than the one the player wears to fit his
        model.
        * Prior to the 11/25/2018 [[Updates|update]], this item, [[Basic Boots]] and [[Hiking
        Boots]] didn't require any items as there was no [[crafting]] then.
        * After Sun Bear left, the Beekeeper's Boots were transferred over to the Mountain Top Shop
        for 33,000,000 (33 million) honey.
        ** The original price was changed to the current price (15,000,000 honey) in the 11/25/2018
        update because of the new crafting materials needed to make it.
        * After the nerf of hats, these replaced the removed bonuses of the [[Beekeeper's Mask]],
        including the Movespeed bonus, which was 6, the Jump Power boost, and the Bee Gather Pollen
        boost, which was 25%. Now, both the mask and the boots are 30%, meaning that the boost for
        Bee Gather Pollen is larger when wearing both the Beekeeper's Mask and Beekeeper's Boots.
        * These were the highest-tiered boots in the game until the 11/25/2018 update when the Gummy
        Boots were introduced to the game.
        ** In the 9/28/2019 update, Coconut Clogs were added, also surpassing the Beekeeper's Boots.
        As a result, the Beekeeper's Boots are currently the third highest-tiered boots in the game,
        behind the Gummy Boots and Coconut Clogs.
