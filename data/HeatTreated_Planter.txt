        The '''Heat-Treated Planter''' is a reusable [[Planter]]. It grows in 12 in-game hours and
        stores around 10 billion pollen. It grants bonus [[Red Extract]]s and [[Enzymes]]. It is
        purchasable in the [[Red HQ]]. The player also needs to complete 150 [[Gifted Riley Bee]]
        quests for it to be unlocked.

        It grows 50% faster in fields with red flowers. Red bees are 50% more likely to sip
        [[Nectar]] from it, but blue bees are 50% less likely to sip from it. This planter grants
        40% more Invigorating and Motivating Nectar. Harvesting a fully-grown Heat-Treated Planter
        has a 1/6 (16.66%) chance to spawn a [[puffshroom]].


        The '''Heat-Treated Planter''' drops extra Red Extracts in the [[Mushroom Field]],
        [[Strawberry Field]], [[Rose Field]], and the [[Pepper Patch]].

        ==Trivia ==
        *This and the [[Hydroponic Planter]] had introduced in Beesmas 2021, but they weren't added
        as an obtainable item until Beesmas 2022.
        **This and the Hydroponic Planter were the newest planters to be added.
        *This planter is the red counterpart of the Hydroponic Planter, and its white counterpart is
        the Petal Planter.
        *This is one of the five reusable planters to reside outside of [[Dapper Bear's Shop]], the
        others being the [[Red Clay Planter]], [[Blue Clay Planter]], [[Petal Planter]], and
        Hydroponic Planter.
        ** This and the Red Clay Planter are the only planters to be sold from the Red HQ.
