        The '''Egg Hunt 2020''' was a Roblox event that spanned across multiple games. In each game,
        players had to complete a certain task/quest in order to obtain one of the eggs, which they
        could then wear on their avatar.

        == The Story ==
        [[Sun Bear]] stated that he wanted to work with the bears for the Egg Hunt. He said that he
        would hide exotic eggs on the map (presumably in the form of a scavenger/egg hunt). The eggs
        hatched, however, and Sun Bear needed the players to capture the [[chicks]] and bring them
        to [[Black Bear]], [[Brown Bear]], and [[Mother Bear]].

        == How to Obtain the Egg ==
        To get the egg from [[Bee Swarm Simulator]], the player needed to talk to [[Sun Bear]] near
        the [[Ant Gate]] to receive the [[Quests|quest]]. Then, the player needed to help three
        bears (Black, Brown, and Mother Bears) with their Egg Hunt 2020 quests. Their quests
        revolved around capturing chicks, along with a few other side objectives.

        Once the player had helped the bears, they had to return to Sun Bear for a few special
        rewards along with the [[Swarming Egg of the Hive]], which players can wear on their Roblox
        avatar as a hat.
