        == Trivia ==
        * In the early versions of the game, Ninja Bee had an ability called \"[[Buffs &
        Debuffs#From Areas|Haste+]],\" which doubled the player's Movespeed for 20 seconds. This
        ability has now been stripped from the bee and is currently only obtainable through the
        [[Star Hall|Star Hall's]] [[Royal Jelly Dispenser]] and [[Codes]].
        ** Ninja Bee, [[Hasty Bee]], [[Shocked Bee]], and Photon Bee are the only bees that grant
        haste without any certain [[Beequip]]s.
        * Ninja Bee is the only colored bee to grant haste, with the exception of those with the
        [[Toy Drum]] Beequip.
        * This bee, [[Shy Bee]], [[Fuzzy Bee]], and [[Windy Bee]] are currently the only bees that
        have a solitary colored skin.
        * Ninja Bee is the fastest bee in the game, tied with [[Photon Bee]].
        ** In Ninja Bee's description, it is supposedly the \"swiftest bee\". However, Photon Bee is
        just as fast.
        * Before the [[Updates#2018.11.25|11/25/2018 Update]], Ninja Bee's Gifted Hive Bonus was
        +20% Player Movespeed instead of +5% Bee Movespeed.
        * Ninja Bee, [[Cobalt Bee]], and [[Vicious Bee]] are the only blue bees that do not like
        [[Blueberry|Blueberries]].
        ** It is the only non-[[Bees/Event|Event]] blue bee to not like blueberries.
        ** This and [[Precise Bee]] are also the only non-[[:Category:Colorless|Colorless]] bee to
        like [[Sunflower Seed|Sunflower Seeds]].
        * This bee, Photon Bee, Cobalt Bee, [[Crimson Bee]], [[Tadpole Bee]], and Windy Bee are the
        only bees that leave a trail.
        * Ninja Bee is the blue counterpart of Shy Bee.
        * This bee is the placeholder image for any bee in a hive slot that hasn't loaded yet.
        * Ninja Bee's favourite treat was originally [[Pineapple|Pineapples]].
