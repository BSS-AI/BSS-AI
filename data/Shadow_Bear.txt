        '''Shadow Bear''' (also called '''Dancing Bear''' or '''30 Bee Bear''') is located inside
        the [[Bear Gate|30 Bee Area]], under the platform where [[Onett]] is. It is floating just
        above the floor, similar to [[Gummy Bear]]. There is a [[Egg#Diamond Egg|diamond egg]] token
        in front of it. Unfortunately, the floor around it is made of a shadow substance that kills
        you instantly, no matter how much [[System Page#Defense|defense]] you have.

        To reach the egg token, equip either the [[Parachute]] or the [[Glider]] and make sure your
        [[Bags|bag]] is empty so you don't lose any [[pollen]].
        * If you're using the Parachute, go up to where the ramp meets the platform. Jump to the
        side and immediately deploy the parachute. Make a beeline to the front of Shadow Bear where
        you can just make out the sparkle of the token.
        * If you're using the Glider, jump off the ledge where the ticket token is located, and then
        glide straight to the diamond [[egg]] token.
        * Whichever way, you will die eventually, but that's okay, as you get to keep the diamond
        egg in your inventory.

        It may be easier to use the Parachute as it is slower than the Glider, so you can focus more
        on the location of the diamond egg.

        == Trivia ==
        * If the player is in a laggy server, it is possible for them to walk on the floor and grab
        the diamond egg.
        *Before the 2021 Beesmas Event, you can rapidly jump on the floor and grab the Diamond Egg
        without dying. Currently, this method is patched.
        * Shadow Bear is the largest of all the [[:Category:Bears|bears]]. It looks just like
        [[Tunnel Bear]], except for its greater size and it does not chase you.
        * It is the only bear who has no function - it doesn't give quests, it doesn't run a
        [[:Category:Shops|shop]], and it doesn't protect an area. [[Noob Bear]], [[Pro Bear]], and
        [[Top Bear]] have no function, but they run shops.
        * Shadow Bear, [[Spirit Bear]], all [[Cub Buddy|Cub Buddies]], and [[Stick Bug]] are the
        only NPCs who use a dance emote (/e dance, /e dance2, and /e dance3 are types of Roblox
        dances, Shadow Bear is using /e dance3). Other bears can do /e wave, which is also an emote.
        * Shadow Bear, [[Tunnel Bear]], and [[Snowbear]] are the only faceless bears.
        * Shadow Bear could possibly be the Giant Bear that [[Black Bear]] alludes to in his \"Solo
        On The Stump\" quest.
