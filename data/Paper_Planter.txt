        The '''Paper Planter''' is a single-use [[planter]] added in the 12/26/2021 update. It can
        be bought in the [[Pro Shop]]. It was also obtainable from [[Bee Bear]]'s 2021 [[Quests]].

        {| class=\"article-table\"
        !Crafting Ingredients
        |-
        |{{Cost|Honey|25,000}}<br>{{Cost|Sunflower Seed|1}}
        |}

        When used, it plants a paper planter in the [[Fields|field]] you planted it in. Different
        fields have different [[Nectar]] types. This planter takes 1 hour of playtime to fully grow.
        Harvesting a fully grown Paper Planter has a 1/30 chance to spawn a [[Puffshroom]]. It drops
        9 items.

        A player can only have 1 Paper Planter active. Attempting to plant a Paper Planter while
        there is already one active will prompt a message that says, \"Paper Planter is already in
        the [The field it is in]\".

        == Trivia ==
        * This is the cheapest and fastest-growing planter in the game.
        * It is one of the many planters to not be sold in [[Dapper Bear's Shop]], the others being
        the [[Red Clay Planter]], [[Blue Clay Planter]], [[Festive Planter]], [[Ticket Planter]],
        [[Sticker Planter]], [[Petal Planter]], [[Hydroponic Planter]] and [[Heat-Treated Planter]].
        * This is the cheapest item in the [[Pro Shop]] in terms of honey, only costing 25,000
        honey.
        * This is the only item in the Pro Shop that is cheaper than an item in the [[Noob Shop]],
        being the [[Helmet]].
        * This is the only one-use planter that has an infinite supply.
        * Attempting to purchase this in the Pro Shop while already having the maximum amount (100)
        or above will consume honey and sunflower seeds but will not give a Paper Planter in return.
        ** As of the [[Updates#2021.12.26|12/26/22 update]], this is no longer true as attempting to
        purchase a Paper Planter while having the maximum amount, prevents you from doing so with
        the caption \"Capacity Exceeded\".
        * This is the only planter in the game that stores more pollen than the honey it costs.
        * t is one of the four planters that aren't reusable. Others being the [[Sticker Planter]],
        [[Ticket Planter]] and the [[Festive Planter]].
