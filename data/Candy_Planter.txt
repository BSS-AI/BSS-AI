        The '''Candy Planter''' is a reusable [[planter]] added in 12/26/21 update. It can be
        purchased form [[Dapper Bear's Shop]].

        {| class=\"article-table\"
        !Crafting Ingredients
        |-
        |{{Cost|Honey|5,000,000}}<br>{{Cost|Magic
        Bean|5}}<br>{{Cost|Gumdrops|30}}<br>{{Cost|Jelly Beans|3}}<br>{{Cost|Soft
        Wax|5}}
        |}

        It fully grows in about 4 in-game hours, but it can be boosted by using [[Glitter]] and by
        the player's [[bees]] taking a sip from it. It grows 25% faster in the [[Strawberry Field]],
        [[Pineapple Patch]], and [[Coconut Field]], and it gives 10% bonus white [[pollen]] from
        harvests and grants 20% more [[Nectar|Motivating Nectar]]. It drops 14 [[items]]. Harvesting
        a fully grown Candy Planter has a 1/10 chance to spawn a [[Puffshroom]].
