        }}A '''Present''' is a Beesmas-exclusive [[:Category:Inventory|Inventory Item]] that appears
        in every [[Beesmas]] event since 2020 following its debut in 2018 and hiatus in 2019.
        Presents can be given to most NPCs in the game in exchange for various prizes from the NPC.
        Each NPC can only receive one gift.

        To give a present, the player needs to talk to an NPC of their choice. When interacted with,
        special dialogue will appear, allowing the player to give the present to the chosen NPC. If
        the player does not have a present in their inventory or have already recently given said
        NPC a present, normal dialogue will display. Some NPCs, such as [[Gummy Bear]] and [[Bubble
        Bee Man]], have other requirements for the player before it allows itself to receive a gift,
        such as the completion of a quest.

        == Beesmas 2022 ==
        {{RemovedContent}}
        Presents returned once again in Beesmas 2022. They still have their main function of adding
        [[Ornaments]] to the [[Beesmas Tree]] when given to NPCs.

        === Ways to obtain ===
        * [[Bee Bear]]'s 2nd, 4th, 6th, 8th, 10th, 12th, 14th, 16th, 17th, 18th, 19th and 20th
        quests.
        * Buying one from [[Bee Bear's Catalog]].
        * Receiving one (from Onett) upon claiming a hive first time after New Year's Day 2023.
        *Obtaining one from the [[Wind Shrine#Galentine Shrine|Galentine's Shrine]].
        *A drop from the [[Robo Party]] (Rank 25).

        ==Beesmas 2021==
        {{RemovedContent}}
        Presents returned in Beesmas 2021. They function the same as the years before that they have
        appeared in, but can now be given to the new NPC, [[Dapper Bear]]. Presents must be given to
        obtain [[ornaments]] from NPCs, and they will be needed to be delivered to do quests from
        [[Bee Bear]].

        ===Ways to obtain===
        * Obtained by completing Quests from [[Bee Bear]].
        *Buying one from [[Bee Bear’s Catalog]].
        *Receiving one (from Onett) upon claiming a hive first time after New Year's Day 2022.
        *Obtaining one from the Galentine's Shrine.

        Wind Shrine|Galentine's Shrine]] (Requires \"[[Spirit Bear|Spirit Bear's]] Galentine's
        Shrine\" Quest to be complete).

        ==Beesmas 2018==
        {{RemovedContent}}

        In Beesmas 2018, presents were awarded by [[Bee Bear]], [[Onett]], and the [[Codes|code]]
        \"ByeBeeBear\" (expired). After the [[Updates#2019.04.05|4/5/2019 update]], players could not
        obtain or use presents. The player could not give a present to Bee Bear, [[Bubble Bee Man]],
        or [[Gummy Bear]].

        other side of [[Bubble Bee Man]].

        == Trivia ==
        *During Beesmas 2021, players who had given a present to [[Honey Bee (NPC)|Honey Bee]]
        before 01/06/22 would be refunded with their present due to a bug with the Bag of Honey
        ornament's boost.
        *Stick Bug, Gifted Riley Bee, and Gifted Bucko Bee require a [[Translator]] before giving
        them a present.
        * Onett could not be given a present immediately as he required the completion of the Star
        Journey questline to claim the reward.
        **Gummy Bear was the only other NPC who required the player to complete his quest before
        giving him a present.
        *On Christmas, Onett gave players a free present along with other prizes. The present was
        able to be obtained any day until the x2 event ended. To receive the gift, the player had to
        claim a hive during the x2 event.
        *There were 11 presents in Beesmas 2018: 6 from Bee Bear, 3 from locations, 1 from Onett
        from the Beesmas x2 event, and 1 from the code \"ByeBeeBear\" (Expired). However, only 10
        presents could have been used.
        *Stick Bug was the only NPC who didn't give any field boosts when given a present.
        *When the Beesmas 2018 event ended, the present token in the 30 Bee Zone was replaced by an
        [[enzymes]] token.
