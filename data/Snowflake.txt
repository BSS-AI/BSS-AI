        A '''Snowflake''' was a limited-time [[:Category:Inventory|inventory item]] added in the
        [[Updates#2020.12.25|12/25/2020 update]]. It made a return in the [[Updates|12/26/2021]] and
        Beesmas 2022 updates.

        ==Beesmas 2021==
        When used, it granted 9 seconds of the [[Buffs & Debuffs#From Items|Cool Breeze]] buff,
        with the stats at the minimum of:

        *+10% [[System Page#Convert Rate|Convert Rate]] At [[Hive]].
        *+5% [[Pollen]] From [[Tools]].
        *+5% Pollen From [[Bees]].
        *+1% [[Honey Per Pollen]].
        *+1% [[Loot Luck]].
        *x1.25 [[System Page#Capacity Multiplier|Capacity]].

        The [[Buffs & Debuffs|buff]] could be stacked up to 100% with the time span of 15
        minutes, which gave:
        *+25% Convert Rate At Hive.
        *+25% Pollen From Tools.
        *+25% Bee Gather Pollen.
        *+5% Honey Per Pollen.
        *+10% Loot Luck.
        *x1.25 Capacity (the capacity always remains the same).

        Along with [[Gingerbread Bear|gingerbread bears]], it could also be used to buy various
        items from [[Bee Bear’s Catalog]].

        All snowflakes from Beesmas 2021 had melted and became unusable.

        ==Beesmas 2020==
        During [[Beesmas]] 2020, a snowflake would grant different boosts. When used, it granted 10
        seconds of the [[Buffs & Debuffs#From Items|Cool Breeze]] buff, with the stats at the
        minimum of:

        *x1.25 Capacity.
        *+5% Convert Rate At Hive.
        *+1% Pollen From Tools.
        *+1% White Pollen.
        *+1% Blue Pollen.
        *+1% Loot Luck.

        The buff could be stacked up to 100% with the time span of 15 minutes, which gave:

        *x1.25 Capacity (the capacity always remained the same).
        *+50% Convert Rate At Hive.
        *+25% Pollen From Tools.
        *+25% White Pollen.
        *+25% Blue Pollen.
        *+10% Loot Luck.

        All snowflakes from Beesmas 2020 had melted and became unusable.

        ==Ways to Obtain==
        *A bee wearing the [[Snowglobe]] beequip can spawn a [[Ability Tokens#Snowglobe
        Shake|Snowglobe Shake]] ability token which gives the snowflake buff for 20 sec.
        *As a drop from a [[Sprout|festive sprout]].
        **Snowflakes received from festive sprouts outside Beesmas event will not appear in player's
        inventory.

        ===The methods below are no longer possible.===
        *Opening certain [[Gift Boxes]] across the map:
        ** The Gilded Gift Box gives 50 snowflakes.
        **The Kitsch Gift Box gives 10 snowflakes.
        **The Midnight Gift Box gives 50 snowflakes.
        **The Festive Gift Box gives 50 snowflakes.
        *As a reward from Beesmas [[quests]].
        *Opening the Sky Blue Gift Box and Peppermint Gift Box. The former gift gave 20 snowflakes
        while the latter gave 50 snowflakes.
        *Purchasing the [[Robux Shop|Snowey Starter Pack]] for 400 Robux.
        * Purchasing the Reindeer Puppy Pack for 800 Robux after receiving the [[Reindeer Antlers]]
        from Bee Bear's Catalog. (Gave a refund of 7,500 Snowflakes).
        *Catching fallen snowflakes on [[field]]s.
        **During [[snowstorm]]s, snowflakes fall much more often.
        **Players can use the [[Snow Machine]] to summon snowstorms after completing [[Gifted Bucko
        Bee|Gifted Bucko Bee’s]] Beesmas quest.
        * As a drop from the following [[mobs]]:
        **[[Rhino Beetle]].
        **[[Mantis]].
        **[[Spider]].
        **[[Stick Nymph]].
        **[[Aphid#Diamond Aphid|Diamond aphid]].
        **[[Wild Windy Bee]].
        **[[Festive Nymph]].
        **[[Mondo Chick]].
        *Reducing [[Snowbear]]'s health by quarters drops snowflakes that everyone could collect and
        snowflakes for the player that summoned it.
        *Certain presents contain Snowflakes.
        *Purchasing the Nighttime Nectar Pack in the [[Robux Shop]], granting 500 snowflakes and
        other items.

        ==Trivia==
        *Snowflakes are one of four [[:Category:Consumables|consumables]] that can be used to
        increase capacity, with the first being the [[Marshmallow Bee|marshmallow bee]], the second
        being the [[Purple Potion|purple potion]], and the third being the [[Super Smoothie|super
        smoothie]].
        *Before March 1, 2021, the snowflake description claimed that it would melt after Beesmas.
        **After Beesmas 2020 ended, the additional message was changed to \"Melts this week - use
        them up fast!\".
        * Snowflake's buff, unlike many other item buffs, does not contain its own name. Instead its
        buff is called [[Buffs & Debuffs#From Items|Cool Breeze]].
        **The buff also applied to all players in that server. Thus, anyone can contribute to
        stacking the buff.
        *From using the snowflakes alone, it required at least 129 snowflakes to attain 100% Cool
        Breeze buff. This is because the buff decreased while the snowflakes are being used.
        However, multiple players using snowflakes could have sped up the process, decreasing the
        minimum needed.
        *Attempting to use a snowflake after Beesmas will prompt the message, \"The Snowflake melts
        immediately...\"
        **Thus, snowflakes are useless outside of Beesmas.
        *The only way to see snowflakes after Beesmas was to keep them in the hotbar at the bottom
        of the screen. If removed from the hotbar, player can no longer see their current number of
        snowflakes, as they are hidden from inventory, but player still has them, until onett wipes
        everyone's snowflakes on next beesmas. Keeping snowflakes in hotbar wont save them from
        being wiped, but one can keep them in hotbar forever.
        *After Beesmas is over, snowflakes will still drop from Festive Sprouts. However, they will
        not appear in your inventory even if you grab them.
