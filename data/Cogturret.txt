        A '''Cogturret''' is a [[Mob]] that appears during [[Robo Bear Challenge|Robo Bear's
        Challenge]].

        This is the third type of Mob the Player will encounter while doing the challenge, first
        appearing at round 11.

        Cogturrets move along the edge of the field, aligning themselves with the player before
        shooting a large red cog. All [[Flowers]] run over by the cog will be instantly depleted of
        their pollen and any goo covered flowers will have the goo removed. Afterwards, it will sink
        into the ground, rendering it invulnerable as it cannot be targeted or damaged.

        A Cogturret's level can range from 11-22 depending on the round of Robo Bear's Challenge.

        ==Strategies ==
        *Cogturrets aim for where the player is, not where the player will be. Because of this,
        moving to the side after they fire a cog will often result in Cogturrets missing their
        attack.
        *Cogturrets will always shoot straight at where they are facing so you can avoid their
        attacks simply by not being in front of them.
        *Stand near the Cogturrets, this will allow your bees to lock on to the Cogturrets and
        quickly deal damage.
        *[[Vicious Bee]] and [[Windy Bee]] provide excellent crowd control, especially since
        Cogturrets often come in pairs or packs. [[Digital Bee]] can also stun many Cogturrets at
        once and allow them to take extra damage via [[Ability Tokens#Mind Hack|Mind Hack]].
