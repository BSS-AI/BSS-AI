        The '''Free Ant Pass Dispenser''' is a [[dispenser]] located past the [[Ant Gate]] and to
        the left of the [[Ant Pass Dispenser]]. Players can use the dispenser to get a free [[Ant
        Pass|ant pass]] every 2 hours, which can be used to participate in the [[Ant Challenge]].
        Players cannot use the dispenser if they already have 10 or more ant passes in their
        inventory.

        == Trivia ==
        * This and the [[Free Robo Pass Dispenser]] are the only free dispensers in the game that do
        not grant any [[Buffs & Debuffs|buffs]] when used.
        ** This, the [[Ant Pass Dispenser]], the [[Free Robo Pass Dispenser]], the [[Robo Pass
        Dispenser]], the [[Coconut Dispenser]], and the [[Glue Dispenser]] are the only dispensers
        that do not grant [[Ability Tokens#Haste|Haste]] when used.
        * This, the [[Ant Pass Dispenser]], the [[Free Robo Pass Dispenser]], and the [[Robo Pass
        Dispenser]] are the only dispensers that don't have any visible faucets. Instead, they have
        a small compartment, similar to a vending machine or an ATM.
