{"text": "''<sup>Not to be confused with the flame trail produced by [[Fire
        Ant]]s.</sup>''

        '''Flames''' are objects that can be produced by certain [[tools]], [[Ability Tokens|ability
        tokens]], or [[Passive Abilities|passive abilities]]. Flames have a percentage chance to be
        spawned by certain bees when gathering, lasting for 4 seconds. Each second, the flames
        collect 4 red, 2 white, or 1 blue [[pollen]] from 9 surrounding flowers (+2% per red bee and
        +4% per gifted red bee if spawned by the player, +10% per bee level if spawned by a bee),
        deals 10 + (25% of [[Bee Attack|base Bee Attack]]) damage per second to any mob (15 if
        spawned by the player), and grants the player [[Buffs & Debuffs#From Passives|flame
        heat]].

        Flames can be generated by the [[Scythe]], [[Fire Bee]], [[Demon Bee]], [[Spicy Bee|Spicy
        Bee's]] [[Ability Tokens#Inferno|Inferno]], bees with the [[Bead Lizard]] or [[Electric
        Candle]] beequip, [[Fire Mask]]'s Ignite passive, and [[Demon Mask]]'s X-Flame passive.
        Flames are used to help grow Scorching Stars.

        == Flame Heat ==
        '''Flame Heat''' grants extra Red Pollen and Bee Attack starting at x1.2 [[System Page#Red
        Pollen|Red Pollen]] and +5% [[Bee Attack]] Multiplier. The more flames around the player,
        the faster the Flame Heat grows up to a maximum of 20 seconds at x2 Red Pollen and +25% Bee
        Attack Multiplier. Flame Heat boosts [[Spicy Bee|Spicy Bee's]] Movespeed and gather speed
        and can also be consumed by [[Precise Bee]] to convert Pollen into Honey.

        == Gathering Flames ==
        The '''Gathering Flames''' passive gives a chance for a bee to spawn a flame while gathering
        pollen from a field. Gathering Flames+ gives a higher chance for a bee to spawn a flame.
        There is a 35% chance (50% if gifted) for Fire Bees' Gathering Flames passive to spawn a
        flame while gathering, and a 65% (85% if gifted) chance for Demon Bees' Gathering Flames+ to
        spawn a flame while gathering.

        The Gathering Flames passive chance can be increased with the Bead Lizard or Electric Candle
        beequip for individual bees.

        * A Bead Lizard gives from +9% Gathering Flames chance to +14% Gathering Flames chance.
        * An Electric Candle gives from +1% Gathering Flames chance to +5% Gathering Flames chance.
        Using waxes can increase the amount up to +25% Gathering Flames chance.

        == Flame Pollen ==
        '''Flame Pollen''' is the amount of pollen that flames can collect. The player can check how
        much flame pollen they have in the system page.
        [[File:FlamePollenStat1.png|thumb|The Flame Pollen stat in the System Page.]]
        Flame Pollen can be increased in a few ways:

        * The Demon Mask gives a x1.25 Flame Pollen multiplier.
        * Gifted Fire Bee gives a x1.25 Flame Pollen multiplier.
        * [[Beequip]]s:
        ** An Electric Candle gives from +5% Flame Pollen to +10% Flame Pollen for an individual
        bee. Using [[waxes]] can increase the amount up to 50% Flame Pollen.
        ** An Electric Candle can give +1% Flame Pollen as a hive bonus. Using waxes can increase
        the amount up to 11% Flame Pollen.
        * [[Cog Amulet]]s:
        ** The Silver Cog Amulet can give up to +5% Flame Pollen.
        ** The Gold Cog Amulet can give up to +8% Flame Pollen.
        ** The Diamond Cog Amulet can give from +12% Flame Pollen up to +14% Flame Pollen.
        ** The Supreme Cog Amulet can give from +15% Flame Pollen up to +20% Flame Pollen.
        * [[Robo Bear Challenge]]:
        ** Epic Upgrades:
        *** Furnace: This upgrade gives x1.5 Flame Pollen.

        == Improving Flames ==
        Aside from increasing the amount of pollen that flames can collect, the player can also
        improve flames a few other ways.

        === Flame Duration ===
        '''Flame Duration''' is how long flames will last. Flame Duration can be increased by Spicy
        Bee's gifted bonus (25% more time) and [[Gifted Bee|Gifted]] Spicy Bee's [[Ability
        Tokens#Flame Fuel|Flame Fuel]] ability (50% more time). In addition, Flame Fuel causes the
        flames to instantly convert 50,000 pollen plus 10% (+5% per Field Boost) of the hive's total
        convert amount.

        === Instant Flame Conversion ===
        [[File:InstantFlameConversionStat1.png|thumb|The Instant Flame Conversion stat in the System
        Page.]]
        '''Instant Flame Conversion''' is how much pollen collected by flames are instantly
        converted. It can be increased by a few ways.

        * The Fire Mask gives +50% Instant Flame Conversion.
        * The Demon Mask gives +50% Instant Flame Conversion.
        * A Scorching Star gives +50% Instant Flame Conversion.

        === Dark Flames ===
        Flames that are hit with [[Dark Scythe]] will be converted into '''dark flames''', a dark
        violet version of normal flames which collects pollen from 13 surrounding flowers (instead
        of 9), deals 50% more damage, and grants dark heat and also normal flame heat. Dark flames
        also grant 33% more growth to Scorching Stars.

        === Other Improvements ===
        Certain upgrades from the Robo Bear Challenge can be used to improve flames.

        * Epic Upgrades:
        ** Furnace: This upgrade gives x1.5 Flame Duration and x1.5 Flame Damage.
        ** Invert: This upgrade makes flames collect x4 pollen from blue flowers.
