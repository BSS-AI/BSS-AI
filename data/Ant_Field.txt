        This field can produce [[honey]] and [[ticket]] tokens while collecting [[pollen]].

        The only [[Bees|bee]] that likes the Ant Field is [[Lion Bee]]. There are no bees that
        dislike the Ant Field.

        [[Planter]]s cannot be planted in this field, although [[Onett (Developer)|Onett]] has
        stated that the Ant Field contains Invigorating [[Nectar]]

        == Obtaining Boosts ==

        * Using an [[Ant Pass]] to start the Ant Challenge will give 100% Instant Ant Field
        Conversion.
        * There is no possible to way to boost the pollen for the Ant Field.
        * The flowers can still be [[Pollination|pollinated]].

        == Trivia ==

        * You can collect pollen from the Ant Field outside the challenge by using the
        [[Sticker-Seeker]] or spawning flames with the [[Scythe]].
        * The Ant Field is the only [[fields|field]] that does not have its own [[badges|badge]].
        * [[Lion Bee]] is the only bee to like the Ant Field; all other bees are neutral to this
        field.
        * The Ant Field is the only field to have exclusively single [[flowers]].
        ** The flowers on this field contain 50 pollen per flower, and instead of going up by 15
        pollen per tier, go up 10 per [[pollination]] tier up to 90, although pollen multiplier
        remains the same as regular flowers.
        * The Ant Field is the only field that can't be boosted with [[glitter]], the only field
        where a [[Magic Bean|magic bean]] cannot be planted, the only field where [[clouds]] cannot
        be summoned, the only field that had no Market Boost from the [[Boost Market]], the only
        field that can't get [[Field Wind|winds]] from the [[Wind Shrine]], and the only field where
        [[planter]]s cannot be planted.
        * The Ant Field is the smallest field in the game with 384 flowers, smaller than the
        [[Pumpkin Patch]] with 561 flowers and the [[Stump Field]] with 563 flowers.
        * The Ant Field is one of the 5 fields that are exclusively used for a challenge. The others
        are: Mixed Brick Field, White Brick Field, Blue Brick Field and Red Brick Field.
        * Players are able to glitch into the Ant Field without triggering the actual challenge.
        This can only be achieved by having a slow internet connection or [[Ability Tokens#Bear
        Morph|bear morph]] and/or [[Buffs & Debuffs#From Areas|haste+]].
        * Despite being impossible to boost, the Ant Field has an unused [[Field Boost]] icon.
