        '''Mother Bear''' is a [[Quest Givers|quest giver]], and one of the eight permanent quest
        [[bear]]s that can be accessed, the others being [[Black Bear]], [[Brown Bear]], [[Panda
        Bear]], [[Science Bear]], [[Dapper Bear]], [[Polar Bear]], and [[Spirit Bear]]. Her quests
        are focused on [[Bond|leveling]] up the player's [[bees]] and feeding them [[treats]]. She
        is located below the red-and-white-striped awning at the far end of the [[Sunflower Field]],
        next to the [[Strawberry Field]] and [[Mushroom Field]].

        Each time she introduces a new treat to feed to the player's bees, she tells them which bees
        love the treat. Mother Bear gives a [[Egg#Star Egg|star egg]] near the end of questline and
        a [[Star Treat|star treat]] at the end.

        ==Trivia==
        *She, Brown Bear, and [[Onett]] are the only quest givers to give out [[Ticket]]s in all
        non-event Quests.
        *She is the first female bear added to the game.
        **She and Spirit Bear are the only female bears in the game.
        *She, Panda Bear, Brown Bear, and Onett are the only quest givers to give a star treat.
        *She is the first permanent bear released in an [[Updates|Update]], before Spirit Bear.
        *Mother Bear gives 65 Tickets in total.
        **She also gives a total of 19,438,250 [[honey]] throughout her quests.
        *Like Dapper Bear, Mother Bear uses the
        [https://web.roblox.com/catalog/619520125/Stylish-Animation-Pack Stylish Animation Pack].
        *Mother Bear, when giving the player the \"Search for Sunflower Seeds\" quest, tells the
        player to get 3 bees to level 6 even though the quest requires 6 level 6 bees.
        **Also, when receiving the \"Search for Sunflower Seeds\" quest, she mentions that the
        [[Werewolf]] is level 6 when it is actually level 7. This is because the werewolf was level
        6 when Mother Bear was introduced to the game but was Leveled up to level 7 later on.
        **Both of these have been fixed.
        *Mother Bear mentions in the \"Mother Bear's Midterm\" quest that the [[Royal Jelly
        Dispenser]] gives one [[royal jelly|Royal Jelly]] per gifted bee type when it actually gives
        one Royal Jelly per Gifted Bee in the Player's Hive. This is because it gave one royal jelly
        per gifted bee type when Mother Bear was added but was changed to one royal jelly per gifted
        bee later on.
        *She is the first bear to give quests about leveling bees, using royal jelly, and feeding
        treats to bees.
        *Excluding her and Panda Bear's Ornament Quest, she, [[Gummy Bear]], Panda Bear, and [[Honey
        Bee (NPC)]] are the only quest givers so far to not give quests that include collecting
        [[Pollen]].
        *Mother Bear has the fourth-fewest number of quests (18 quests), the third being Onett (7
        quests), the second is [[Bubble Bee Man]] (4 quests), and the fewest is [[Stick Bug]] (3
        quests).
        **This makes her the bear with the fewest number of [[Quests]].
        *She is self-aware that she is in a [[Bee Swarm Simulator|Roblox game]], along with Panda
        Bear, [[Bee Bear]], Black Bear, Science Bear, and Spirit Bear, stating in “Mother Bear's
        Midterm\", that the quest-giving Onett is the developer of the game.
        *She, Black Bear, and Brown Bear were \"nice\" during the 2018 Beesmas Event, as stated by Bee
        Bear.
        *During the [[Ready Player Two]] event, Mother Bear's conclusion dialogue is changed, as she
        got <nowiki>''</nowiki>the strangest text<nowiki>''</nowiki>. The
        text is a riddle for one of the codes that give the 7-Pronged-Cog, used to craft the Strange
        Goggles and do the puzzles.
        *She is the only quest bear who (outside of event quests) never asks the player to collect
        pollen OR defeat mobs. Honey Bee and [[Egg Hunt Info]] are the only other quest-givers who
        do not require these.
        *Like [[Dapper Bear]] and [[Gummy Bee]], Mother Bear's face decal emits light. This is most
        noticeable if the player looks at the glass behind Mother Bear at night or if they transform
        into Mother Bear using a [[Bear Bee|Gifted Bear Bee's]] [[Ability Tokens#Bear Morph|Bear
        Morph]] or a code and walk into a dark place such as the [[Werewolf's Cave]].
        *She is the only quest bear in the [[Starter Zone]] to not have any extensions/changes to
        their permanent quests.
