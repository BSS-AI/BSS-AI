        This is the last Ant Challenge [[Mobs|mob]] that the player will encounter. Fire Ants
        typically spawn in the later waves, such as after the first [[Giant Ant]].

        Fire Ants are colored red and act the same as [[Regular Ant]]s, but they leave a damaging
        flame trail behind them as they move. The flames will linger even after the Fire Ant that
        left it is defeated. However, the fire disappears after a short amount of time.

        A Fire Ant's level can increase depending on what wave of the Ant Challenge the player is
        on. They can range from level 1 up to level 16.

        Fire Ants are one of the most threatening of the five ant types, as their flame trails can
        stack up on the [[Fields|field]].

        == Strategies ==

        * Fire Ants move in predetermined straight lines and are quite slow. This enables to player
        to easily dodge any coming their way.
        * Standing near the Fire Ant will allow your [[bees]] to quickly target the Fire Ant to
        quickly deal damage and defeat it.
        * Focus on taking out Fire Ants first as they can make the field very hard to navigate
        without taking damage from their flame trails. This is especially true if there is a large
        amount of them on the field.
        * Fire Ants will come out in groups and they could overwhelm the player if they are
        collecting pollen quickly to advance to the next waves. [[Vicious Bee]] and [[Windy Bee]]
        provide excellent crowd control with their
        [https://bee-swarm-simulator.fandom.com/wiki/Ability_Tokens?so=search#Impale Impale] and
        [https://bee-swarm-simulator.fandom.com/wiki/Ability_Tokens?so=search#Tornado Tornado]
        abilities which could greatly help counter the large amounts of Fire Ants landing on the
        field. [[Digital Bee]] can also stun many Fire Ants at once and allow them to take extra
        damage via [[Ability Tokens#Mind Hack|Mind Hack]].

        == Trivia ==

        * This is the only ant to have a second method of attack.
        * They have the same health per level as Regular Ants.
        * Noted by [[Panda Bear]], the fire from a Fire Ant is not affected by [[System
        Page#Defense|Defense]], but this seems to be untrue. However, having 100% defense (through
        [[Passive Abilities#Emergency Coconut Shield|Emergency Coconut Shield]]) will nullify the
        damage.
