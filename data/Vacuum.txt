        The '''Vacuum''' is a [[:Category:Tools|tool]] that can be purchased in the [[Noob Shop]].

        {| class=\"article-table\"
        !Crafting Ingredients
        |-
        |{{Cost|Honey|14,000}}
        |}

        It collects 2 [[pollen]] from 13 surrounding [[Flowers|flower]] patches every 0.8 seconds
        and has a base pollen collection rate of 32.5 pollen per second.

        ==Appearance==
        The vacuum cleaner is easily the most complex of the tools in the [[Noob Shop|noob shop]].
        It looks like the standard vacuum, a brownish bag with a grey handle connecting to the
        suction colored grey, black, and 2 yellow squares at the front, resembling lights.
