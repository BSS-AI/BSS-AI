{"text": "'''Mazes''' are secret areas in the game that contain [[items]] inside of them. To obtain
        these items, the player needs to navigate through the mazes and find them. There are
        currently two mazes that exist in the game, each located in their own respective location.

        == Blue Maze==
        The '''Blue Maze''' is located next to the [[Ticket Tent]] (or behind the Beige Gift Box in
        Beesmas 2021) . It was added in the [[Updates#2019.04.17|Egg Hunt 2019 Update]]. This
        requires no [[Bees|bee]] requisites to enter since it's located in the [[Starter Zone]].
        Inside the maze, the player can obtain 5 [[ticket]]s by taking a left turn first and then a
        right turn, and a [[Jelly Beans|jelly bean]] token by taking the path on the right. There
        used to be a [[Plastic Egg|plastic egg]] where the tickets were in the 2019 Egg Hunt. The
        player can also find a [[Royal Jelly#Star Jelly|star jelly]] token on the right, but it is
        blocked by a glass wall. It can be obtained by going through a passage behind the [[Petal
        Shop]].

        [[Chicks#Spotted Chick|Spotted Chicks]] could spawn in this maze before they were removed.

        == Pro Shop Maze ==
        The '''Pro Shop Maze''' is located behind [[Pro Bear]] in the [[Pro Shop]]. It requires at
        least 10 bees to pass through the [[Brave Bee Gate]], where the Pro Shop is located.

        The player can obtain a [[Royal Jelly|royal jelly]] by going forward to the end and turning
        right two times, then going to the first opening available. Follow through it to find the
        royal jelly token.

        There is also a [[Sunflower Seed|sunflower seed]] token inside the maze that awards 10
        sunflower seeds. The player can obtain it by taking same path as the royal jelly, but
        instead of turning on the last right, go to the end of the hall and follow through the last
        corridor.

        If the player gets close enough, they can grab both tokens through the walls, making
        collecting them easier.
