        A {{Color|Moon Charm}}, when fed to a [[Bees|bee]], raises its [[bond]] by 250. Moon Charms
        are also used in generating [[Moon Amulet|Moon Amulets]] and they are used in crating of
        [[glitter]] and the [[Helmet]]. If fed to a [[Radiation|radioactive]] bee, it has a 1/2,500
        (0.04%) chance to give that bee a [[Mutation]].

        This is one of the 8 tokens that can glow, the others being [[Ability Tokens#Festive
        Gift|Festive Gift]], {{Cost|Festive Blessing Token}}, {{Cost|Beesmas Cheer Token}},
        [[Ability Tokens#Map Corruption|Map Corruption]], as well as {{Cost|Glitter}}, {{Cost|Night
        Bell}}, {{Cost|Star Jelly}}, and {{Cost|Star Treat}} tokens (as exceptionally rare drops
        from [[fireflies]]).

        Moon charm tokens last for 10 seconds before disappearing.

        ==Ways to Obtain==
        * [[Crafting]] via [[Blender]] for:
        ** {{Cost|Pineapple|5}}
        ** {{Cost|Gumdrops|5}}
        ** {{Cost|Royal Jelly|1}}
        * As a reward from [[Memory Match]]es (Every type except Extreme Memory Match and the Winter
        Memory Match).
        * As a reward from the [[Wind Shrine]].
        * Redeeming certain valid [[codes]]:
        ** 1MLikes (Gives 1 moon charm + other stuff).
        * As a Stack Reward for adding certain [[stickers]] to the [[Sticker Stack]]:
        ** {{Cost|Waxing Crescent Moon Sticker|||0}} rewards 10 moon charms.
        ** {{Cost|Petal Wand Sticker|||0}} rewards 10 moon charms.

        ===Drops===
        * As a drop from certain [[Mobs]]:
        ** [[King Beetle]] and [[Tunnel Bear]] always drop moon charms upon defeat.
        ** [[Fireflies]] (Increments of 1, 5 and 10.)
        ** As a rare drop from [[Mantis]] and [[Stump Snail]].
        *As a common drop from [[Mythic Meteor Shower|Mythic Meteors]].
        *As a rare drop from harvesting [[sparkles]].
        *A guaranteed drop from a [[Sprout|Moon Sprout]].
        *Harvesting [[planter]]s in the [[Pineapple Patch]].
