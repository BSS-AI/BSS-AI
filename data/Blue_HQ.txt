        }}The '''Blue HQ''' is a two-story base with many features. The entrance can be found at the
        far end of the [[Blue Flower Field]], located in the [[Starter Zone]]. It has a [[Bucko
        Bee]] model above the entrance. To enter, the player must have discovered 4 different blue
        bee types. The Blue HQ is the counterpart to the [[Red HQ]].

        On the roof, there is an enlarged [[Bubble Wand]]. This wand occasionally grows a face and
        blows [[sparkles]] to nearby fields. This includes the [[Clover Field]], [[Mushroom Field]],
        Blue Flower Field, and the [[Dandelion Field]].

        == Interior ==
        On the first floor, there are four different objects:
        * A [[Blue Teleporter]] that will teleport the player near the [[Ticket Tent]] and [[Red
        Cannon]],
        * A set of blue [[items]] for sale, (See the 'Store' section below.)
        * The [[Blueberry Dispenser]], which gives a varying amount [[Blueberry|blueberries]]
        depending on the number of * blue bees the player has in their [[hive]] every 4 hours,
        * The [[All-Time Top Blue Collectors]] [[Leaderboards|leaderboard]], which shows the top 100
        players who have collected the most blue pollen.

        On the second floor, there are more features:
        * A [[Royal Jelly|royal jelly]] token located near the window at the front of the base,
        * A [[Blue Field Booster]] that gives a 15-minute duration [[Field Boost|field boost]] for
        either the [[Pine Tree Forest]], [[Bamboo Field]], or the Blue Flower Field,
        * [[Gifted Bucko Bee]], who will require a [[translator]] for it to hand out [[quests]],
        * The [[Top Bucko Bee Helpers]] leaderboard, which is located to the left of Gifted Bucko
        Bee and shows the top 100 players who have completed the most Bucko Bee quests,
        * The [[Daily Top Blue Collectors]] leaderboard, which is located to the right of Gifted
        Bucko Bee and shows the top 100 players who have collected the most blue pollen in a day.

        == Store ==
        There's a store in the Blue HQ that sells nine items. To open the store, press E when near
        the store's items and ''not'' on the Blue Teleporter, Blueberry Dispenser, the Leaderboards,
        or the second floor.

        == Trivia ==
        *The Tide Popper is currently the most expensive item able to be bought in the Blue HQ.
        *If the player goes to the corner of the ramp inside the Blue HQ closest to the [[Clover
        Field]], they will be able to hear the Crawlers [[music]].
        *Unlike the [[Riley Bee]] model on the [[Red HQ]], the wings of the Bucko Bee model don't
        have collision.
        *On 01/01/22, there was a [[Glitches|glitch]] where if the player attempted to view the Tide
        Popper, the game would freeze and they won't be able to exit the shop, except for rejoining
        the game.
        *The Blue HQ is also known as the \"Blue Clubhouse.\" If the player does not have 4 blue bee
        types discovered, the top of their screen will say, \"Discover 4 Blue Bee types to enter the
        '''Blue Clubhouse'''.\"
