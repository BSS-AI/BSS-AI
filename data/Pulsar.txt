        The '''Pulsar''' is a [[:Category:Tools|tool]] that can be purchased in the [[Pro Shop]].

        {| class=\"article-table\"
        !Crafting Ingredients
        |-
        |{{Cost|Honey|125,000}}
        |}

        It collects 2 [[pollen]] from 29 surrounding patches in 1 second, which comes out to a base
        pollen collection rate of 58 pollen per second.

        It has what looks to be a black orb with green smoke emitting from it and is surrounded by
        three black rings. Two of these rings have a small black orb connected to them, with one
        above and one to the right of the middle orb.

        *The Pulsar and [[Gummyballer]] are tied for the slowest tool in the game (in terms of how
