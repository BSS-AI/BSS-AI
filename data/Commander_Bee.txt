        == Trivia ==
        * Commander Bee's skin is the same as [[Looker Bee|Looker Bee's]].
        * This bee, [[Buoyant Bee]] and [[Frosty Bee]] are the only 3 [[bees]] in the game that wear
        hats.
        ** This is the only Colorless bee that wears a hat.
        * This bee is one of the 4 bees that have the focus ability, the other 3 are Looker Bee,
        [[Music Bee]], and [[Puppy Bee]] with [[Reindeer Antlers]].
        * Commander Bee is the only epic bee added in an [[Updates|update]].
        * A [[Egg#Specific Bee Egg|Commander Bee Egg]] can be obtained by capturing [[Commando
        Chick]] 200 times.
        * As the name and model suggest, this bee is likely inspired by the eponymous video game
        protagonist known as Commander Keen.
