        The '''Gumdrop Shop''' is a [[:Category:Shops|shop]] introduced in the
        [[Updates#2018.05.26|5/26/2018 update]], located between the [[Spider Field]] and the
        [[Bamboo Field]]. It can be used to purchase [[gumdrops]] with [[ticket]]s, in increments of
        3, 30, and 300.

        It is decorated with a teal gumdrop on the top, a picture of the gumdrops item on the sides
        with a pink background, a teal base, and gray rims.

        The description of the shop is: \"Use while standing in a [[field]] to cover flowers in
        [[goo]]. Goo grants bonus [[honey]].\"
