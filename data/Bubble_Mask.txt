        ==Trivia==
        *This, the [[Fire Mask]], [[Honey Mask]], [[Riley Guard]] and [[Bucko Guard]] all represent
        [[Bees/Epic|epic bees]].
        *The bubble mask represents the [[Bubble Bee]], having an ability similar to it.
        *Before the [[Updates#2019.04.05|4/5/2019 Update]], the Bubble Mask's stats were:
        **+25% [[System Page#Bee Gather Pollen|Bee Gather Pollen]]
        ** +25% [[System Page#Instant Conversion|Instant Conversion]]
        **+30% Damage Reduction
        **+11% Bee Ability Rate
        **+115% Luck ([[System Page#Loot Luck|Loot Luck]])
        **+25% [[System Page#Blue Bomb Pollen|Blue Bomb Pollen]]
        **x1.15 [[System Page#Blue Pollen|Blue Pollen]].
        *Before the [[Updates#2019.09.28|9/28/2019 Update]], the Bubble Mask didn't have any
        [[Passive Abilities|Passive Ability]]. Other stats were also changed.
        **If the player owns the Bubble Mask, its passive is inherited by the [[Diamond Mask]].
        *If a player were to [[Crafting|Craft]] the ingredients to craft the item, it would take 100
        million [[Honey]], 7,500 [[Pineapple|Pineapples]], 8,625 [[Blueberry|Blueberries]], 5,625
        [[Strawberry|Strawberries]], 1,250 [[Sunflower Seed|Sunflower Seeds]], 15 [[Magic Bean]]s,
        and 1,125 [[Royal Jelly|Royal Jellies]].
        *The Bubbles from its Bubble Bombs passive have a unique sound effect when popped.
