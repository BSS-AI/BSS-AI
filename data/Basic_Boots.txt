        ==Trivia==
        *These could've been obtained from [[Sun Bear]] by completing his first 5 [[quests]] before
        he left on 10/8/2018.
        *These are the only boots that don't grant [[System Page#Jump Power|Jump Power]].
        *Before the [[Updates#2018.11.25|11/25/2018 update]], this item, the [[Hiking Boots]], and
        the [[Beekeeper's Boots]] didn't require any [[crafting]] materials.
        *Unlike the [[Gummy Boots]] and [[Coconut Clogs]], these, along with the Hiking Boots and
        Beekeeper's Boots, were obtainable from quests.
        *These boots are used to obtain the [[Sticker#Sticker Index|Yellow Wiggly Walking Person
        Sticker]], by walking over a certain flower in the Starter, 5, and 10 bee zones (Flower is
        different for every player).
