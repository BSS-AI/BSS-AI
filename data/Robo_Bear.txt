        '''Robo Bear''' is an [[:Category:NPC|NPC]] located in the 15 Bee Zone, found between the
        [[Badge Bearer's Guild]] and [[Red HQ]], and behind the [[Rose Field]]. In the 2022 Beesmas
        update, players can interact with them to start the [[Robo Bear Challenge]] after they
        finish their first quest, \"Repairing Robo Bear.” To completeCorrupting The Glitched Drive”
        quest.

        During Beesmas 2022, players were able to complete the quest by purchasing a white drive in
        [[Bee Bear's Catalog]], completing Science Bear's Beesmas 2022 quest for a red drive,
        completing Dapper Bear's Beesmas 2022 quest for a blue drive, and completing [[Bee Bear|Bee
        Bear's]] “Decorating For Drives” quest for a glitched drive.
