        }}The '''Mondo Chick''' is a level 8 [[:Category:Bosses|boss]] [[Chicks|chick]] located in
        the [[Mountain Top Field]]. It respawns every hour at the full hour (1:00, 2:00, 3:00, etc;
        not in time zones that are a fractional number of hours away from UTC), and it's respawn
        timer can be found on the side of the [[Mountain Top Shop]]. The respawn time is unaffected
        by the Monster Respawn Time stat. Its rewards can be shared between players.

        The Mondo Chick will always spawn at level 8 with 300,000 health. It takes 333 damage
        automatically each second, causing its health to reach 300 after 15 minutes. If it isn't
        defeated within 15 minutes of spawning, it will despawn in a similar fashion of the
        [[Commando Chick]]. Like the [[Coconut Crab]], player(s) will be rewarded depending how fast
        the Mondo Chick is defeated. Due to its large range and damage, it’s recommended to use
        distance between the player and the Mondo Chick.

        When nighttime appears, a [[Rogue Vicious Bee]] will be spawned by any chance in the
        Mountain Top Field and it can attack the Mondo Chick, causing its health to drop rapidly.

        The Mondo Chick will hide in its egg after several seconds, causing it to stop moving and
        block some attacks. The egg can be cracked by dealing 7,500 damage to it or by waiting 23
        seconds via automatic damage.

        When Mondo Chick spawns, a server-wide message displays: \"<span
        style=\"background-color:#fffdb5;color:black\">🐣 Mondo Chick has spawned!
        🐣</span>\", and when it is captured, players that damaged it gets three messages
        displaying:

        ==Trivia==
        *The Mondo Chick's noises can be heard throughout the entire map.
        **This is also the case with the [[Cave Monster]]s.
        *The Mondo Chick is the only mob that has a fixed spawn point in the Mountain Top Field.
        *During the soft launch of the [[Egg Hunt 2020]] update, the Mondo Chick only had 250,000
        health. It was later changed to 300,000 the following day.
        *If the player already has the Mondo Chick Blessing buff and defeats another Mondo Chick,
        the new blessing will override the old buff, resetting its timer and possibly degrading or
        upgrading the buff depending on the time took to defeat the Mondo Chick.
        *Mondo Chick, [[Stick Nymph]]s and Festive Nymphs are the only mobs in the game that take
        damage without any things attacking it.
        *Mondo Chick, Festive Nymphs and [[Robo Party|Party Robots]] are the three mobs in the game
        that can drop a “blessing” buff.
        ** Out of these three, Mondo Chick is the only one to guarantee a blessing buff after
        defeating it.
        **Mondo Chick does not drop the blessing buff in token form, instead it goes directly to the
        player’s active buffs.
        *Onett has said on discord that he dislikes Mondo Chick \"on a personal level.\"
        *If the Mondo Chick falls out of the Mountain Top field and into another field, when
        defeated, the rewards will spawn in that field it was defeated in. However if it dies
        outside of a field, their drops will form in a ring.
        * As of the Beesmas 2020 update, the Mondo Chick no longer drops [[Marshmallow Bee]]s.
        *There is a glitch where the Mondo Chick would fall into other areas outside of the
        [[Mountain Top Field]], such as into the [[Bamboo Field]] or in front of the [[Pro Shop]].
        The fallen Mondo Chick will not be able to move unless it falls into the [[Pineapple
        Patch]]. This is a rare occasion. On an even rarer occurrence, the Mondo Chick will get
        stuck on the tall rocks adjacent to the Lion Bee Gate.
