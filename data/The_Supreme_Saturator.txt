        purchase in the [[Badge Bearer's Guild]]. To unlock it, the player must obtain 10 different
        [[Badges|Grandmaster badges]]. Once the player unlocks it, they can purchase it for
        55,555,555,555 (~55.5 billion) [[honey]]. Its description reads, “The ultimate sprinkler.
        Nobody knows how it works or where it came from.\"

        The Supreme Saturator is a single sprinkler. Its radius extends to 16 [[flowers|flower]]
        tiles, its rate is 1 second, and its power is 10. This adds up to 8042 [[pollen]] per
        second, outperforming four [[Diamond Drenchers]] (1809 [[pollen]] per second) by over 340%
        (4.44x as effective).
