        <sup>''Not to be confused with the [[Royal Jelly Dispenser]].''</sup>

        The '''Royal Jelly Shop''' is a [[:Category:Shops|shop]] located on a rocky hill between the
        [[Rose Field]] and the [[Pine Tree Forest]], to the right of and just below the [[Blue
        Cannon]]. Players can purchase [[Royal Jelly|royal jelly]] in exchange for [[honey]].

        The first royal jelly the player buys will cost 250,000 honey. Each subsequent royal jelly
        will cost an additional 10,000 honey, eventually reaching a maximum of 1,000,000 honey per
        royal jelly.

        The formula for the price of one royal jelly is

        <math>P(n) = \begin{cases}250{,}000+10{,}000n & 0 \leq n < 75\\1{,}000{,}000
        & n \geq 75\end{cases}</math>
        Where

        <math>n</math>
        is the amount of royal jelly already purchased.

        Royal jelly can be purchased in increments of 1, 10, 100, 1,000, 10,000, or 100,000.
