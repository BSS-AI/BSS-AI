{"text": "The [[Stick Bug Challenge]] is a raid boss fight feature that lasts 10 minutes. It is
        primarily activated when a player talks to [[Stick Bug]] and starts the challenge but can
        also initiate when a player begins [[Spirit Bear]]'s \"Dancing With Stick Bug\"
        [[Quests|quest]], when [[Onett (Developer)|Onett]] chooses to activate it globally, and
        during [[:Category:Beesmas|Beesmas]] when completing Stick Bug's Beesmas quest. Starting the
        challenge by yourself requires having given a [[translator]] to Stick Bug, and either costs
        50 [[ticket]]s or can be started for free once every 36 hours/1.5 days. The option to pay 50
        tickets will be unavailable if the challenge can be started for free. The challenge takes
        place across the entire server and can be participated in by anyone. Giving a [[present]] to
        Stick Bug or starting the Egg Hunt quest will also start the challenge (if a translator has
        been given to Stick Bug).
        [[File:BB610AB5-580B-434C-841D-40796C058263.jpeg|thumb|200px|This message pops up when Onett
        is about to start the Stick Bug Challenge.]]

        When the challenge starts, a message in the chat appears, saying: \"<span
        style=\"color:lightgrey;background:#8B4513;\">⚠ {Username} has started the Stick Bug
        Challenge! ⚠</span>\" and Stick Bug spawns in the [[Sunflower Field]]. If Onett summons
        Stick Bug, it would say: \"<span style=\"color:lightgrey;background:#8B4513;\">⚠ Onett
        has started the Stick Bug Challenge! ⚠</span>\"

        During the challenge, Stick Bug moves around to different fields and fights players using
        various attacks. After players deal enough damage to Stick Bug, it will go to a different
        field, leaving a circle of rewards behind. When Stick Bug moves to different fields, it
        gains more health, becomes faster, gets better attacks (e.g. more [[Stick Nymph]]s will
        spawn) and gains a level. During the challenge, a brown window on the side of the screen
        shows how long the Stick Bug Challenge will be active, what level and which field Stick Bug
        is in, and how many points the player has obtained so far.

        Stick Bug will only appear in the Sunflower Field, [[Clover Field]], [[Strawberry Field]],
        [[Bamboo Field]], [[Pineapple Patch]], [[Cactus Field]], [[Pumpkin Patch]] or [[Mountain Top
        Field]]. Stick Bug always starts in the Sunflower Field. When Stick Bug moves to a different
        field, a message in the chat will appear, saying: \"<span
        style=\"color:lightgrey;background:#8B4513;\">⚠ Stick Bug's headed to the {Field Name}!
        ⚠</span>\"

        ==Attacks==
        Stick Bug will attack in many different ways.

        ===Normal Behavior===
        Stick Bug will run towards players regularly and before doing an attack. Getting hit by him
        will deal damage to the player.

        ===Flailing Sticks===
        A large red warning circle appears where Stick Bug will flail sticks and deal damage. The
        red circle is similar to [[Rogue Vicious Bee]]'s red circles, as it appears before the
        actual attack. The circle is about 6 [[flowers]] wide in radius. Flailing sticks only deal
        damage on the first tick of the attack. Stick Bug also deploys [[Stick Nymph]]s when it uses
        this attack. When using the flailing sticks, Stick Bug will either use /e dance, /e dance2
        or stand still and use /e point when attacking. The number of Stick Nymphs deployed depends
        on which emote was used.

        ===Stick Nymphs===
        {{Main|Stick Nymph}}
        A large red warning circle appears around Stick Bug, and Stick Bug deploys few or many Stick
        Nymphs in this red circle. They are small, slow [[mobs]] that have little health. When
        defeated, they drop various tokens. Stick Nymphs get stronger and move faster as Stick Bug's
        level increases. [[Festive Nymph]]s can spawn if the player that started the challenge has
        completed Stick Bug's Beesmas 2020/2021 quest and it is still Beesmas. When deploying Stick
        Nymphs, Stick Bug will use /e dance2 and conclude the deployment by using /e wave, this
        sequence also spawns much more Stick Nymphs than /e dance.

        ===Spinners===
        A small red warning circle appears on the field before Stick Bug deploys multiple twig
        spinners that spin around a point. They rotate relatively fast and do a considerable amount
        of damage that increases with Stick Bug level. The amount of spinners deployed by Stick Bug
        increases depending on Stick Bug's level. When Stick Bug is about to deploy the spinners, he
        will use /e dance 2 for a few seconds and use /e point on deployment. Sometimes, two
        spinners can be in the same location, but the spinners that were first deployed should
        disappear a little while after.

        ===Hiding===
        Stick Bug will stand still before using /e point and trapping players in twigs. Shortly
        after, Stick Bug drops down into the flowers and becomes 2D. While flat, Stick Bug is immune
        to damage and will move about the field while also gradually healing. To release it back
        into its regular form, players must collect [[pollen]] from the dark flowers. There is a
        counter above it that shows how much pollen the players need to collect from the dark brown
        flowers.

        ===Splinter Trap===
        [[File:Splinter Trap.png|thumb|155px|The Splinter Trap effect on the player.]]
        Stick Bug points at a player and then traps players in twigs, giving them the [[Buffs &
        Debuffs#Debuffs|Splinter Trap]] debuff. This deals 3 damage per second and decrease [[System
        Page#Movespeed|player movespeed]] and [[System Page#Jump Power|jump power]] by 25%. The
        damage is not reduced by defense at all. The player must collect pollen to remove them. This
        attack occurs before Stick Bug hides in flowers or before Stick Bug moves to a different
        field, but starting at level 12, the splinter trap can happen at any time during the course
        of the battle. When a player is trapped in twigs, they will receive a message in the chat,
        saying: \"<span style=\"color:red;background:black;\">You've been trapped in twigs!
        Collect pollen to remove them.</span>\"

        Note that the splinter trap icon is the same as the [[Ability Tokens#Haste|Haste+]] icon.
        ===Defense Totem===
        When Stick Bug reaches level 6 and above, Stick Bug can build a defense totem in a nearby
        field, and players will receive a message in chat telling them this and the field the totem
        is located in. When preparing to build a defense totem, Stick Bug will use /e dance 2 and /e
        point when finished. Stick Bug only builds totems in the [[Stump Field]], [[Dandelion
        Field]], [[Mushroom Field]], [[Blue Flower Field]], [[Spider Field]], [[Pine Tree Forest]],
        or [[Rose Field]]. The totem gives Stick Bug 25% more defense. There can be only 3 totems at
        a time, which gives a total of 75% defense to Stick Bug. The totem can be removed by
        collecting pollen in its field; the higher the Stick Bug level, the more pollen players will
        need to collect. Removing the totem does not add to the player's score, but it will drop
        various reward tokens in a circle around it, such as treats and honey. When a totem is
        summoned, a message in chat will say: \"<span
        style=\"color:lightgrey;background:#8B4513;\">⚠ Stick Bug has built a Defense Totem in the
        {Field Name}! ⚠</span>\"

        ==Tips==
        In this section, there will be some tips to progress further in the Stick Bug Challenge.
        *Make sure there are no mobs to distract the players' bees, like the [[Chicks#Mondo
        Chick|Mondo Chick]], before starting the challenge.
        *Level up your bees. Bees with a lower level than Stick Bug would most likely miss its
        attacks.
        *Try to get as many strong players on the server as possible. A way of communicating with
        other players, like voice chat, can be used. The more players that are doing the challenge,
        the faster Stick Bug will level up.
        *Certain equipment can be very useful in the challenge.
        **Many gears boost bee attack in some way.
        **The [[Fire Mask|Fire]] and [[Demon Mask]]s boost multiple helpful stats, like bee attack
        and defense, as well as spawning many flames with their passives.
        **The [[Gummy Mask]]'s Gummy Morph passive greatly boosts [[Gummy Bee]]'s stats, including
        attack.
        **The [[Dark Scythe]] can create Dark Flames, which boost Super-Crit Power. The flames
        themselves also deal more damage.
        **The [[Coconut Canister]] has two useful passives, being Inspire Coconuts and Emergency
        Coconut Shield.
        ***When activating the Emergency Coconut Shield, it grants 100% defense and x1.25 bee attack
        for a few seconds. Try to make the most of these boosts, like using the invincibility to
        escape or using the increased attack to do lots of damage.
        ****It should be activated later on in the challenge. Activating it early would be a waste.
        Before the Stick Bug Challenge, the shield should be intentionally activated so that there
        is no risk of it activating early.
        **The [[Glider]] and boots can help with navigating the map or escaping tough situations.
        ***The [[Coconut Clogs]]/[[Gummy Boots]]' Coconut Haste Surge can prove useful in a pinch.
        *There are many bees that are recommended for the Stick Bug Challenge.
        **Some bees, like [[Lion Bee]], [[Spicy Bee]] and [[Vicious Bee]], have high attack stats or
        abilities that do lots of damage.
        **Bees with helpful gifted hive bonuses like [[Looker Bee]], [[Rage Bee]] or [[Precise Bee]]
        will increase damage output.
        **Certain abilities from bees can grant useful buffs.
        ***Precise Bee's Target Practice can boost Super-Crit Chance.
        ***Multiple bees have Focus, which boosts Critical Chance.
        ***Music Bee's Melody boosts Critical Power.
        ***Rage Bee and Spicy Bee have Rage, which boosts attack.
        ***Haste-producing bees are also helpful in traversing the map and dodging attacks.
        **All [[Gifted Bee|gifted bees]] have x1.5 attack over their non-gifted counterparts.
        **Certain mutations can grant attack or ability rate bonuses.
        **Certain beequips can grant useful bonuses or abilities.
        *Many items can be used to further enhance helpful stats.
        **Using [[stinger]]s will boost bee attack by 1.5x for 30 seconds.
        ***Using stingers activates Star Saw, which can deal lots of damage to both Stick Bug and
        the Stick Nymphs.
        **The player can use [[Oil]] to increase movespeed, and [[Tropical Drink]]s to increase the
        chance for critical hits, each for 10 minutes.
        ***[[Super Smoothie]]s can also be used for both improved buffs and an additional +1%
        Super-Crit Chance.
        **[[Coconut]]s will deal lots of damage when landing on Stick Bug or any Stick Nymphs.
        ***Besides using the coconut item, the two Coconut Canister passives can also summon
        coconuts.
        *Try to save [[Nectar|Invigorating Nectar]] for an additional 1% to 10% bee attack. The bee
        attack depends on how much time the nectar has left.
        *When Stick Bug builds a defense totem on a nearby field, send the teammate(s) who can deal
        with it the fastest. For example, if a totem is built in the Blue Flower Field, the player
        with the best blue pollen collection should be sent to take the totem down.
        **If the defense totem is in the Stump Field, send somebody who has taken down the [[Stump
        Snail]] recently instead of somebody with a Stump Snail still in the field.
        *One teammate can try to lure the Stick Nymphs away from the other players so that their
        bees will not be distracted by them.
        *Place [[sprinklers]] in the field in which Stick Bug is currently in. Since flowers
        (especially in low-level fields) tend to \"drain\" quickly, this puts players at risk of dying
        from the Splinter Trap or losing time when Stick Bug is hiding in flower patches.
        **[[Clouds]] can also help with flower regeneration.
        *Lag may be an issue for players, so making optimizations, like reducing the graphic
        settings, could help in the challenge.
        *Stand at higher areas like the ramp at the [[Pumpkin Patch]]. Stick Nymphs will not be able
        to reach these places, and most of Stick Bug's attacks will not hit.

        ==Points and Health per Level==
        Points earned are equal to the amount of damage dealt to Stick Bug multiplied by its level
        squared (for example, 2,000 damage against a level 6 Stick Bug would be worth 2,000 * 6 * 6
        = 72,000 points). Sometimes the player may gain a few extra points.

        *There is a glitch that when Stick Bug runs into the walls of the ramp next to the Bamboo
        Field, he will get stuck and not be able to move, then teleport back into the field after a
        few seconds. Players can use this to their advantage and deal extra damage to Stick Bug
        without risk of dying.
        *There is another, similar glitch where Stick Bug will hit the boundary right up against the
        [[Clover Field]] and fall over for a couple seconds.
        *If the challenge ends during the [[Day/Night Cycle|night]], normal [[music]] will start to
        play instead of the night music.
        *The splinter trap icon is the same as the Haste+ icon.
        *When beginning the quest \"Dancing With Stick Bug\" a Stick Bug Challenge will begin saying
        \"<span style=\"color:lightgrey;background:#8B4513;\">⚠ {Username} has started the Stick
        Bug Challenge! ⚠</span>\", opposed to saying that Spirit Bear began the challenge.
        **Even when the player beginning the quest \"Dancing With Stick Bug\" has completed Stick
        Bug's Beesmas Quest, Festive Nymphs will not spawn.
        *The second in-game message that played before the first Stick Bug Challenge was \"Stick Bug
        will spawn in the Sunflower Field. Work together to defeat him and level him up. The higher
        the level, the better the rewards.\"
        *If players join a server that has already started the challenge, the stat file will say
        Stick Bug is level 1, but when Stick Bug is defeated again, the level in the stat file will
        return to normal.
        *Giving [[Stick Bug]] a present will start a Stick Bug Challenge.
        *Completing Stick Bug's Beesmas 2020/2021 quest will also start a Stick Bug Challenge.
        *There is a [[Glitches#Invisible Splinter Trap|glitch]] where if the player dies while
        having the splinter trap activated, it will still keep the effect and damage the player
        until they die again.
        **The pollen counter is still there though and you can remove them by collecting pollen, it
        is just invisible.
        *When Stick Bug jumps to another field, it maintains its hitbox and can still damage
        players.
