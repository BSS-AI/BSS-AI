        ==Overview==
        ''Main Article: [[Retro Swarm Challenge]]''

        The Retro Swarm Challenge is a round-based minigame where the main objective is to defend
        the hives from [[Slime|Slimes]] and [[Zombie|Zombies]]. In order to be successful, the
        player must save up Bricks to buy bees and weapons. The challenge subplace hosts 4 exclusive
        fields (Red, White, Blue and Mixed Brick Fields).
