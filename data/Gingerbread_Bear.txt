        A {{Color|Gingerbread Bear}} is a unique type of [[Treats|Treat]] that was added in the
        [[Updates#2020.12.25|12/25/2020 update]]. It is obtainable by checking [[Mother Bear|Mother
        Bear's]] [[Gingerbread House]], defeating certain [[mobs]] and bosses, opening [[Gift
        Boxes]], completing [[Bee Bear|Bee Bear's]] quests, giving [[present]]s to certain [[Quest
        Givers|NPCs]] around the map, completing NPCs Beesmas quests, using [[Onett's Lid Art]], and
        buying certain [[Robux Shop|Robux Packs]].

        ==Overview==
        Gingerbread bears have 2 functions. The player could save them up and spend it on [[Bee
        Bear's Catalog]] during the Beesmas 2020, Beesmas 2021, and Beesmas 2022 events, or feed
        them to [[bees]]. In addition to granting [[bond]], it has a 1% chance to make them [[Gifted
        Bee|gifted]]. They can also be saved up until the next Beesmas event, where they turn into
        [[Aged Gingerbread Bear]]s.

        The formula of turning a bee gifted:

        <math>(1 - 0.99^x) * 100%</math>(x is the number of the gingerbread bears fed to
        a bee)

        == Trivia ==
        * Gingerbread bears, along with {{Cost|Snowflake}}, are the only currencies that can be used
        in Bee Bear's Catalog.
        ** However, unlike {{Cost|Snowflake}}, gingerbread bears are not removed from the player's
        inventory when Beesmas ends and are still obtainable and usable.
        ** When the Beesmas 2021 event began, all of the gingerbread bears the players had from last
        Beesmas turned into \"[[Aged Gingerbread Bear]]s\", which instead give 500 bond (up from the
        previous 250 bond) and have a 1.1% chance to turn a bee gifted (up from 1%). However, these
        cannot be used as currencies in Bee Bear's Catalog.
        ** When the Beesmas 2022 event begun, all of the gingerbread bears players had from 2021
        also turned into Aged gingerbread bears.
        * This, along with the {{Cost|Star Treat}} and Aged Gingerbread Bears, are the only items in
        the game that can turn [[Bees/Event|Event bees]] gifted.
        * This and the {{Cost|Festive Bean}} are the only event items that are infinitely renewable
        not during an event.
