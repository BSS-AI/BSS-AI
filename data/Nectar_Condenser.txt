        ==Usage==
        The Nectar Condenser allows you to condense 12 hours of any nectar to receive a [[Nectar
        Vial|vial]] that holds 4 hours of nectar. This vial can be used at any time and grants 4
        hours of that nectar type. Unlike the [[Nectar Pot]], there is no extra cost for this
        action.

        When condensing nectar. It has a 1/25 chance to drop a [[Sticker]] corresponding to the
        nectar condensed, the place it will drop depends on the nectar that was condensed:
        *Refreshing in [[Coconut Field]].
        *Satisfying in [[Sunflower Field]].
        *Motivating in [[Rose Field]].
        *Comforting in [[Dandelion Field]].
        *Invigorating in [[Pepper Patch]].

        *There is currently a bug with its hitbox. You can be a bit off to the side on the Nectar
