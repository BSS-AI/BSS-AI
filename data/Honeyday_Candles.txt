        The '''Honeyday Candles''' is a machine that sits right outside and next to the [[Red HQ]]'s
        interior ladder. It was introduced in the [[Updates|Beesmas 2020 event]] and returned in
        Beesmas 2021 and Beesmas 2022. The player can use it after completing [[Gifted Riley
        Bee|Gifted Riley Bee's]] Beesmas quest. It has a cooldown of 4 hours. When activated, 3
        random level 10 red [[bees]] are summoned for 30 minutes, and has a chance to drop either 3
        [[Soft Wax]]es, 3 [[Hard Wax]]es, 3 [[Swirled Wax]]es or 3 [[Caustic Wax]]es.

        During the Beesmas 2020 event, the Honeyday Candles used to only & always drop 1 Swirled
        Wax, and it was one of the 2 only ways to get the Swirled Wax back then. When it was
        introduced back in the Beesmas 2021 event, it could also have a chance of dropping other
        [[Waxes]].

        The Honeyday Candles have an 81.3% chance to give 3 Soft Waxes, 16.3% chance to give 3 Hard
        Waxes, 1.63% chance to give 3 Swirled Waxes, and a 0.81% chance to give 3 Caustic Waxes.

        When a player stands on top of the pad before completing the quest, a red text box will
        appear on the top of the screen saying: \"''The red bees are having trouble lighting the
        candles...''\"

        Bees summoned by the Honeyday Candles can collect [[pollen]] and attack [[mobs]] for the
        player. However, they cannot convert for them at the [[hive]], though they are still able to
        convert through conversion links from [[Ability Tokens#Mark|Honey Marks]].

        The candles can summon the following bees:

        *[[Fire Bee]].
        *[[Demon Bee]].
        *[[Spicy Bee]].

        ==Trivia==

        [[Onett's Lid Art]], [[Gummy Beacon]], and the [[Honey Wreath]] (after completing [[Honey
        Bee (NPC)|Honey Bee's]] Beesmas quest).
        * There is a glitch when the player leaves, the bees generated would stay in place until
        they despawned.
        **Even when the player joins the same server, the bees will not despawn.
        *During Beesmas 2020, using these candles were one of the two only ways of getting Swirled
        Wax, the other way is by giving Gifted Riley Bee a [[Present]] during that same event.
        **In Beesmas 2021 and 2022, the Honeyday Candles no longer guarantee a Swirled Wax.
        *This is the only machine that can summon bees who produce [[Passive Abilities#Gathering
        Flames|flames]].
        *A [[Hard Wax]] token used to be found hidden behind the candles.
