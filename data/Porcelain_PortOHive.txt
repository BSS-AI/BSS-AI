        == Trivia ==
        * Out of the four variants of [[Port-O-Hive]], the Porcelain Port-O-Hive was the second
        Port-O-Hive added into the game, after the regular Port-O-Hive.
        * Before the [[Updates#2019.04.05|4/5/2019 update]], its base capacity was 500,000 pollen.
        After the update, it was increased to 1,000,000 pollen before being reverted back to 500,000
        after the [[Updates#2019.05.13|5/13/2019 Update]]. Changed to 600,000 Pollen after the
        5/23/2024 Update.
        * Before the 4/5/2019 update, its description was read as, \"The rarest and most precious
        Port-O-Hive in the world. Can store up to 500,000 pollen.\"
        * This container is worn by [[Onett]] in the [[Bear Gate]], along with the [[Porcelain
        Dipper]], [[Beekeeper's Boots]], [[Mondo Belt Bag]], [[Beekeeper's Mask]], and the [[Riley
        Guard|Riley]] and [[Bucko Guard]]s.
