        The '''Glider''' is a [[:Category:Gliding Tools|gliding tool]] that can be purchased at the
        [[Mountain Top Shop]].

        {| class=\"article-table\"
        !Crafting Ingredients
        |-
        |{{Cost|Honey|5,000,000}}
        |}

        It allows the player to glide much quicker and change direction faster than the
        [[Parachute]].

        To activate it, jump, and jump again shortly after while in mid-air. If the player goes
        airborne through some other method, (a cannon, for example), double-jump. To disengage it,
        jump once more or land on the ground. The gliding feature is hardcoded to the player and
        they will still glide even during a lag spike, although the Glider doesn't immediately
        appear.

        The primary use of the Glider is to glide to places on the map that would otherwise be
        inaccessible. It is vital to collecting many of the [[Royal Jelly|royal jelly]] and
        [[ticket]] tokens around the map and to perform several shortcuts. The Glider, along with
        the Parachute, are the only ways to obtain the [[Egg|Diamond Egg]] token below the platform
        of the [[Bear Gate|30 Bee Zone]]. It is also used to reach the [[Star Hall]] located on top
        of the [[Noob Shop]]. The Glider can also be used to dodge [[mobs]], since most of them do
        not have a high detection range in the air. Combined with the cannons (especially with the
        [[Red Cannon]]), it is essentially the most efficient way to travel around the map.

        The Glider's gliding speed is not affected by speed boosts, such as Haste, Haste+, Bear
        Morph, and other boosts that enhance the player’s movement speed. The Glider is the upgraded
        version of [[Parachute]] having a more expensive cost.

        ==Trivia==
        *The Glider and the Parachute are the only gliding tools in the game.
        *With the Glider, it is possible to glide outside of the map.
        **It isn't the only way, however. You can exit out of the map via a [[Wild Windy Bee]]
        blowing at you with a gust of wind.
        *If the player enables shift-lock, they can slightly strafe to one side while using the
        gliding tools.
        *The Glider is located on top of the Mountain Top Shop as a decoration, similar to how a
        Parachute model is located on top of the [[Pro Shop]].
        *The Glider is the second cheapest item in the Mountain Top Shop, after the first [[Hive
        Slot|hive slot]].
        **It was the third cheapest item in the shop before the [[Eviction]] was removed.
