        The '''Glue Dispenser '''is a [[dispenser]] located in [[Gummy Bear's Lair]], behind the
        location where the player teleports to. It can be used every 22 hours and dispenses [[glue]]
        (number given depends on what [[Badges#Goo Badges|goo badge]] the player has) along with the
        [[Buffs & Debuffs#From Areas|Unlimited Gumdrops buff]] for 3 minutes. The type of goo
        badge doesn't affect the duration of the buff.

        == Trivia ==
        * Using this is one of the two only ways to get the Unlimited Gumdrops Buff, the other being
        certain [[codes]].
        * It is the only dispenser that requires the player to have a certain badge to access, as
        the Gummy Bear's Lair requires the player to have [[Badges#Goo Badge|Goo Hotshot]] or above
        to enter.
        * This, the [[Free Ant Pass Dispenser]], [[Ant Pass Dispenser]], [[Free Robo Pass
        Dispenser]], [[Robo Pass Dispenser]], and the [[Coconut Dispenser]] are the only dispensers
        to not grant [[Ability Tokens#Haste|haste]].
        * This is the only dispenser that can give a [[Passive Abilities|Passive Ability]].
        * This, the Free Robo Pass Dispenser, and the [[Royal Jelly Dispenser|Free Royal Jelly
        Dispenser]] are the only dispensers with a 22 hour cooldown, the longest in the game.
        * This and the Coconut Dispenser are the only dispensers to give more items based on the
        rank of a badge.
