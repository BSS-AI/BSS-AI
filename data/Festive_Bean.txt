        '''Festive Bean''' is a special variant of a [[Magic Bean|magic bean]] that always plants a
        [[Sprout|festive sprout]] in a [[Fields|field]]. When harvested, it grants [[ticket]]s,
        [[Crafting|crafting materials]], and unique tokens that are commonly found in [[Ability
        Tokens#Festive Gift|festive gifts]] in abundant quantities. There is a 120 minute (2 hour)
        cooldown between planting festive sprouts. Since the end of Beesmas 2019, only the player
        who planted can receive the [[Ability Tokens#Festive Blessing|Festive Blessing]] and
        [[Ability Tokens#Beesmas Cheer|Beesmas cheer]] buffs to avoid overuse. When a player plants
        a festive sprout, a message says <span style=\"background-color:red; color:white;\">\"🌱
        {Username} has planted a Festive Sprout 🌱.\"</span>.

        When a festive sprout is planted, the following audio plays:[[File:Dth1.ogg]]

        ==Trivia==

        * At the end of Beesmas 2019, tokens from festive sprouts became unshareable. However, they
        are now shareable again, with the \"Festive Blessing\" and \"Beesmas Cheer\" buffs being the
        only private rewards.
        **If summoned by [[Onett (Developer)|Onett]] (or one of his many aliases), a Festive
        Sprout's \"Festive Blessing\", and \"Beesmas Cheer\" can be shared between all the players. They
        cannot be grabbed by abilities that collect personal tokens (e.g. [[Ability Tokens#Token
        Link|Token Link]] or [[Ability Tokens#Triangulate|Triangulate]]) as the tokens do not belong
        to any player.
        *This bean is the only variant of [[Magic Bean]]s.
        *This bean is also the only bean to reward one type of sprout.
        *This and the [[Marshmallow Bee]] are the only event items that can be donated to the [[Wind
        Shrine]].
        *This bean is also the only way for a Festive Sprout to be planted, with the exception of
        Onett planting them.
        *Festive bean is the item with the longest cooldown in the game, being 2 hours.
        *This and the [[Gingerbread Bear]] are the only event items that are infinitely renewable
        not during an event.
        * Festive beans are the only way to get [[snowflake]]s outside of Beesmas.
        **They will only be added to the inventory if [[snowflake]]s are in the hotbar.
