{"text": "'''Bags''' are [[:Category:Accessories|accessories]] that can be bought in the various
        [[:Category:Shops|shops]] around the map. The main purpose of bags is to store [[pollen]]
        gathered by the player until it is full, to which the player will need to go to their
        [[hive]] and convert it into [[honey]], use a Coconut, use an [[Instant Converter]], use a
        [[Micro-Converter]], use a [[Honeysuckle]] (only converts some) or admire [[Black Bear|Black
        Bear's]] [[Honey Wreath]] (only during Beesmas). When the player's bag is full, the
        following message is displayed: \"<span style=\"background-color:red;
        color:white;\">Pollen container full. Go to your hive to make honey.</span>\" They
        may use an [[Instant Converter]] to instantly get all their honey (costs 1 ticket) or use a
        micro-converter. Certain bags are intended to give the player additional boosts other than
        capacity, such as [[System Page#Instant Conversion|Instant Conversion]], [[System
        Page#Convert Rate|Convert Rate]], [[Bee Attack]] and even [[Passive Abilities|Passives]]
        (currently only from the [[Coconut Canister]]). There are other accessories (such as
        [[:Category:Belts|belt bags]]) that increase capacity. The Pouch bag is given automatically
        to new players. There are currently 12 bags in the game.
