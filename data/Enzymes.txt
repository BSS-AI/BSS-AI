        {{Color|Enzymes}} are an [[:Category:Inventory|inventory item]] that was added in the
        [[Updates|11/25/2018 update]]. When used, it grants +10% [[System Page#Instant
        Conversion|Instant Conversion]] and x1.25 [[System Page#Convert Rate|Convert Rate]] for 10
        minutes when activated. Using another '''enzymes''' will not stack them, but will reset the
        timer. Enzymes can also be used for crafting other [[:Category:Accessories|accessories]] or
        items. Using a [[Super Smoothie|super smoothie]] will override the buff.

        == Ways to Obtain ==
        * [[Crafting]] it using the [[Blender]] for {{Cost|Pineapple|50}} and {{Cost|Royal
        Jelly|10}}.
        * Redeeming certain valid [[codes]]:
        ** Using the code \"SecretProfileCode\" (Activates the buff and other stuff).
        * As a rare drop from [[leaves]] in the [[Pineapple Patch]] and the [[Spider Field]].
        * [[Black Bear]] gives enzymes on the following quests:
        ** The 15 Bee Zone gives 1 enzymes and other items.
        ** Black Bear will occasionally give enzymes following the completion of his repeatable
        quests.
        * [[Brown Bear]]'s 45th, 115th, 130th, 165th, and 260th quests give 1 enzymes, while his
        230th quest gives 3.
        * [[Science Bear]] gives enzymes on the following quests:
        ** Breaking Down Badges gives 1 enzymes and other items.
        ** Meditating On Phenomenon gives 1 enzymes and other items.
        ** The Power Of Information gives 3 enzymes and other items.
        ** Epistemological Endeavor gives 4 enzymes and other items.
        * [[Polar Bear]] has a chance to give enzymes after completing a quest.
        * [[Spirit Bear]] gives enzymes on the following quests:
        ** Rhubarb That Could Have Been gives 5 enzymes and other items.
        ** Dancing With Stick Bug gives 25 enzymes and other items.
        * As a rare drop from a [[Scorpion]], [[Spider]], [[Werewolf]], [[Aphid#Armored
        Aphid|Armored Aphid]], [[King Beetle]], [[Stump Snail]], or [[Tunnel Bear]].
        * As a rare drop from [[sprout]]s (the odds increase depending on the rarity of the sprout
        and if the sprout is in the Pineapple Patch).
        * Completing the [[Stick Bug Challenge]] may reward enzymes.
        * As a drop from [[Stick Bug]], [[Stick Nymph|stick nymphs]], or [[Stick Bug
        Challenge#Defense Totem|defense totems]].
        * A rare drop from a [[Ability Tokens#Festive Gift|Festive Gift]] (if [[Festive Bee]] is
        [[Gifted Bee|gifted]]).
        * As a rare [[Cub Buddy]] gift.
        * As a drop from [[planter]]s.
        ** Planters in Pineapple Patch makes them drop enzymes.
        ** [[Heat-Treated Planter]] drops enzymes as a bonus drop and planting it in Pineapple Patch
        will make it drop 100 enzymes.
        * As a potential match from all [[Memory Match]]es.
        * As a Stack Reward for adding certain [[stickers]] to the [[Sticker Stack]]:
        ** Honey Bee Bear rewards 1 enzymes.
        ** Panicked Science Bear rewards 1 enzymes.
        ** Forward Facing Spider rewards 1 enzymes.
        ** Standing Caterpillar rewards 1 enzymes.
        ** Yellow Hi rewards 1 enzymes.
        ** Taunting Doodle Person rewards 1 enzymes.
        ** Orange Step Array rewards 1 enzymes.
        ** Orange Green Tri Deco rewards 1 enzymes.

        === Token Locations ===
        * There is an enzymes token in the bottom left corner of [[Gummy Bear's Lair]].
        * Right beside [[Memory Match#Night Memory Match|Night Memory Match]] inside the [[Bear
        Gate]].
        * There is an enzymes token in the tunnel that can be accessed through the hole above the
        [[Coconut Dispenser]].

        ==Trivia==
        * Enzymes used to look like pills. However, Roblox removed them because they appeared to
        have violated the Roblox Terms Of Service. They were later re-created, published under the
        name \"Pineapple Candies\" and with the appearance they have currently.
        * If multiple Enzymes are used in a [[Blender|Blender's]] crafting recipe, the chat log
        displaying the lost materials would display \"Enzymess\" adding an extra 's' instead of the
        proper plural. This small bug applies to [[Gumdrops]] and various other items.
