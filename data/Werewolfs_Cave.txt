        The '''Werewolf's Cave''', located beyond the [[Honey Bee Gate]], sits between the [[Cactus
        Field]] and the [[Pumpkin Patch]]. It is home to the [[werewolf]] and two spider-like
        creatures called [[Cave Monster]]s inside of it that will try to kill you. There is a
        [[Egg#Gold Egg|gold egg]] token at the end of the cave. 

        To get the gold egg easily, prepare items or gear that give haste, such as
        [[:Category:Boots|boots]], [[:Category:Guards|guards]], [[oil]], [[Ability Tokens#Bear
        Morph|Bear Morph]], and stacks of [[Ability Tokens#Haste|Haste]] and/or Haste+. Afterwards,
        make a run for it, jumping the whole way to avoid getting killed. Extra boosts, however, are
        not required nor needed. If you are lucky, you may be able to get the golden egg through
        jumping. Sometimes, there is a [[Glitches|glitch]] where the cave monsters don't spawn or
        they are lagging, and in that case, it is easy to get the gold egg without preparation.
        Getting Haste+ by using the [[Royal Jelly Dispenser]] in the [[Star Hall]] or by using
        [[codes]] can help, and another option is to defeat the cave monsters in the cave, and then
        get the gold egg, though that solution can be hard. If you have [[pollen]] in your
        [[Bags|bag]] and wish to keep it, you can exit the cave using a [[Whirligig]] to exit the
        Werewolf's Cave safely. Otherwise, if the Cave Monsters haven't spawned in or have been
        defeated, you will have to die or reset in order to exit the cave.
        [[File:MTsprout-WWcave.png|thumb|200px|A sprout in the Mountain Top Field, as seen from the
        cave.]]
        If there is a [[sprout]] in the [[Mountain Top Field]], its light beam is visible inside of
        the Werewolf's Cave. If there's a cloud on the Mountain Top Field, the rain might be
        visible. Honeystorms, snow storms, and mythic meteor showers are also visible while inside
        the cave.
