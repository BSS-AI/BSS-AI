        '''Onett's Lid Art''', or '''Yard Art''', is a 2D art-piece that would be located in the
        [[Bear Gate]] next to [[Onett]]. It could be used every 8 hours after completing Onett's
        Yard Art Quest. If a player attempts to use the Lid Art before completing his
        [[Quests|quest,]] it displays the message: ''\"This art is missing something... It's not very
        impactful.\"''
        ==Appearance==
        The Lid Art consists of (left to right) [[Spirit Bear]] on [[King Beetle]], [[Black Bear]],
        [[Mother Bear]], [[Bumble Bee]] and [[Stick Bug]], and a [[Basic Bee]] fence gate.

        The names confirmed by Onett so far are: Queen Spirit Bear (Spirit Bear), King Stick Bug
        (Stick Bug), and King Bumble Bee (Bumble Bee).[[File:LidArtIncomplete.png.png|thumb|Onett's
        Lid Art before the completion of Onett's Beesmas 2020, 2021 and 2022 quest]]
        After completing Onett's Yard Art Quest, [[Baby Bee]], wearing a Santa hat, appears in
        between Black Bear and Mother Bear in the cradle.

        ==Trivia==
        *Originally, the message that would appear if the player tried to use Onett's Lid Art before
        they completed [[Onett]]'s quest was 'This art isn't as impactful without Baby Bee in the
        middle...'
        *This, the [[Gummy Beacon]], and the [[Naughty List]] are tied for the longest cooldown of
        Beesmas decorations of 8 hours.
        *This and the Gummy Beacon were the only ways to have multiple [[Bees/Event|Event bees]] of
        the same kind, although they are temporary.
        *This art is a reference to the birth of Jesus, which is celebrated by people of Christian
        faith during Christmas.
        *There was a [[Glitches|glitch]] where once the player's extra bees despawn, they end up
        stuck in mid-air anywhere on the map.
        *The summoned bees can level up, even if they are not in the player's [[hive]]. This could
        be achieved by [[Ability Tokens#Puppy Love|Puppy Love]] or [[Beesmas Feast]].
        **Players cannot make the temporary bees level up by feeding them treats. However, the
        summoned bees can level up by other means such as collecting pollen.
        *This is one of the 4 machines that grant the player temporary bees, the others being the
        [[Honeyday Candles]], the [[Gummy Beacon]] and the [[Honey Wreath]] (after completing
        [[Honey Bee (NPC)|Honey Bee]]'s quest).
        *Sometimes when players look at it from a certain view, some parts of it don't show up.
        *Having a Cobalt Bee from the lid art with Crimson Bee in your hive also activates both a
        red and blue pulse.
        *[[Bees]] given by Onett's Lid Art are able to attack the mobs the player's actual bees are
        attacking. However, they cannot convert at the [[hive]].
        *Bees summoned by the lid art in 2020 may also have been gifted. It is unknown whether they
        granted a hive bonus or not.
        *This is the only way to summon a Guiding Star without a [[Star Amulet#Supreme Star
        Amulet|supreme]] or [[Star Amulet#Diamond Star Amulet|diamond]] star amulet with the
        respective passive.
