        This field can spawn [[honey]], [[Blueberry|blueberry]], [[ticket]], [[Ability
        Tokens#Boost|blue boost]], [[Magic Bean|magic bean]], and [[Blue Extract|blue extract]]
        tokens when collecting [[pollen]].

        [[Bees]] that like the Bamboo Field are [[Cool Bee]], [[Bucko Bee]], [[Buoyant Bee]],
        [[Carpenter Bee]], and [[Ninja Bee]]. Bees that dislike the Bamboo Field are [[Riley Bee]],
        [[Precise Bee]], & [[Windy Bee]].

        This field gives Comforting [[Nectar]] when a planter is placed here.

        == Obtaining Boosts ==
        [[File:Fis bamboo.png|thumb|75px|The Bamboo Field Boost icon.]]

        *Using the [[Blue Field Booster]] may give a Bamboo [[Field Boost]]. When it does, it will
        give +100% Bamboo Field pollen for 15 minutes.
        * Using [[Glitter]] will add 100% Bamboo Field pollen onto the current boost for 15 minutes
        if the boost isn't already maxed out.
        * Using [[Field Dice|Field dice]], [[Smooth Dice]], or [[Loaded Dice]] may add +100% to 300%
        of Bamboo Field pollen for 15 minutes based on what type of dice is used, and also based on
        the current stack before the boost is added.
        *Bamboo [[Field Wind]]s can be obtained through the [[Wind Shrine]] or [[codes]]. They have
        a base of +20% instant Bamboo Field conversion, and give +5% instant Bamboo Field conversion
        and +15% Bamboo Field pollen for each stack. Bamboo Field Winds can stack up to a total of
        13 times.
        *Redeeming certain valid codes:
        **Teespring (Gives Bamboo Field Boost x3 + Bamboo Field Winds x3 + other stuff).
        **500mil (Gives Bamboo Field Boost x3 + other stuff).
        **1MLikes (Gives Bamboo Field Boost x1 + other stuff).
        **WeekExtension (Gives Bamboo Field Boost x3 + Bamboo Field Winds x10 + other stuff).
        *It can be boosted by the [[King Beetle Amulet]]. If it has a Bamboo Field Boost, it can
        boost the field by +1% to +100%.

        ===The methods below are no longer possible.===

        *Giving a present to certain [[:Category:NPC|NPC]]<nowiki/>s (2018):
        **[[Panda Bear]] gave Bamboo Field Boost x4 and other [[items]].
        **[[Gifted Bucko Bee]] gave Bamboo Field Boost x3 and other boosts and items.
        **[[Onett]] gave Bamboo Field Boost x1 and other boosts and items.
        **Giving a present to Panda Bear during Beesmas 2020 gave Bamboo Boost x3 and other items.
        **Panda Bear gave Bamboo Field Boost x3 and other [[items]] during Beesmas 2021.
        * Redeeming certain expired codes:
        **PandaPower (Gave Bamboo Field Boost x1 + other stuff).
        ** Friday (Gave Bamboo Field Boost x5 + other stuff).
        **RobziRobot (Gave Bamboo Field Boost x4 + other stuff).
        ** 1mFavorites (Gave Bamboo Field Boost x1 + other stuff).
        **ExoExtract (Gave Bamboo Field Boost x3 + other stuff).
        **August (Gave Bamboo Field Boost x3 + other stuff).
        **FestiveFrogs (Gave Bamboo Field Boost x3 and Bamboo Field Winds x3 + other stuff).
        **FestiveFinale (Gave Bamboo Field Boost x3 and Bamboo Field Winds x2 + other stuff).
        **BlackFriday (Activated Bamboo Field Code Buff + other stuff).
        *Purchasing a Bamboo Field [[Buffs & Debuffs#From Areas|Market Boost]] from the [[Boost
        Market]] will give x1.5 Bamboo [[Field Capacity]], x1.25 Bamboo Field Pollen, x1.1 [[System
        Page#Blue Pollen|Blue Pollen]], and x1.25 [[System Page#Hive Convert Rate|Convert Rate At
        Hive]].


        == Trivia==

        * This is one of six fields that [[fireflies]] can travel to, with the others being [[Cactus
        Field]], [[Pineapple Patch]], [[Rose Field]], Spider Field, and Strawberry Field.
        * The player can find 2 [[Royal Jelly|royal jellies]] near the Bamboo Field, one in a nook
        under the ramp to the [[Brave Bee Gate]], and the other at the end of an [[Obstacle
        Courses#Bamboo Obby|obstacle course]], starting in between this field and the Spider Field.
        * The bamboo stems have claw marks on them, presumably from Panda Bear.
        * If a player stands on the corner of the Bamboo Field closest to the Spider Field while a
        [[Snowbear]] is summoned, the Snowbear can throw snowballs and damage the player.
        *The three bees that dislike the Bamboo Field also have another disliked field (Riley Bee
        and Precise Bee; Pine Tree Forest, Windy Bee; Strawberry Field)
