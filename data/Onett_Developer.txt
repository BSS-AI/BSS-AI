        '''Onett''' is the owner and programmer of [[Bee Swarm Simulator]]. Bee Swarm Simulator is
        his first and main game on Roblox. He owns the
        [https://www.roblox.com/My/Groups.aspx?gid=3982592 Bee Swarm Simulator Club],
        [https://www.roblox.com/My/groups.aspx?gid=5211428 Onett's Testing Group], and is in charge
        of different [[codes]].

        [https://www.roblox.com/users/1912490/profile Onett] also runs alternate accounts to handle
        messages about errors experienced while playing Bee Swarm Simulator.
        [https://www.roblox.com/users/556387844/profile BeeSwarmBugReport] handles bug reports and
        [https://www.roblox.com/users/556383852/profile BeeSwarmSupport] handles the purchase and
        saves file reports. Onett also has a secret alternate account named
        [https://www.roblox.com/users/1953773238/profile/ ltpb2020], which he mainly used to upload
        decals for the [[Ready Player Two]] event. Another account was found by the name
        [https://www.roblox.com/users/5098329817/profile HiddenBeePotential] but seems to only be
        used for testing as of now.

        [https://twitter.com/OnettDev/ Onett's Twitter account] can be found by searching @OnettDev.
        Onett also has an [https://www.instagram.com/beeswarmsimulator/ Instagram account], a
        [https://www.youtube.com/@PiercenHarbut YouTube account] and a
        [https://www.reddit.com/u/BeeSwarmDev/s/iutuKGHL2H Reddit account]

        Onett lives in the CST timezone, as confirmed on Discord.

        == Trivia ==
        *Onett has a cat in real life because he says that [[Tabby Bee]] looks like his cat in
        [[Onett|his NPC's]] quest dialogue. The cat he owns is named Sam (revealed in Discord).
        *Onett’s real name is Piercen Harbut.
        *Onett has a [https://www.youtube.com/@PiercenHarbut YouTube] account, a
        [https://twitter.com/OnettDev Twitter] account, an
        [https://www.instagram.com/beeswarmsimulator/ Instagram] account, a
        [https://www.twitch.tv/OnettBSS Twitch] account, and Discord account (onett1).
        *Onett's favorite bees are [[Basic Bee]], [[Rascal Bee]], [[Brave Bee]], and Tabby Bee.
        **Onett claimed he likes Basic Bee because it knows it is not as talented as other bees, but
        it does not care and smiles non-stop and always exudes pride. He said he likes Rascal Bee
        because of its laughing face.
        *Onett's favorite ability is Triangulate, due to the fact that he thinks it's satisfying.
        *Onett has a sister (confirmed in Discord).
        *His username could be a reference to a city in the Nintendo game EarthBound.
        *Onett owns a special [[Tools|tool]] called Onett's Honey Hammer.
        *Onett's favorite [[Mobs|mob]] is the [[Rhino Beetle]], as confirmed on Discord.
        *Onett's favorite in-game music is bpatrol. You can find the audio
        [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GTCc9u7d-eM here].
        *Onett has broken his hands once.
        *Occasionally, Onett would summon mini events. He would then go by various aliases in place
        of the username who summons the events, such as \"[Alias] has summoned (a) [mini event]\".
        *Onett's birthday is on 12/24/89. He is 35 years old this year, or 34 currently, since his
        birthday this year has yet to come.
