        '''Gifted Riley Bee''' is a [[Quest Givers|Quest Giver]] and one of three permanent
        [[:Category:Quest Bees|Quest Bees]], the others being [[Gifted Bucko Bee]] and [[Honey Bee
        (NPC)|Honey Bee]]. It resides on the roof of the [[Red HQ]], next to the [[Top Riley Bee
        Helpers]] [[leaderboards|leaderboard]]. The player cannot speak or receive/complete its
        [[Quests]] without a [[Translator]], which is given by [[Science Bear]] three times. The
        player must complete 150 quests from Gifted Riley Bee to unlock crafting the [[Heat-Treated
        Planter]], and 250 to unlock crafting the [[Dark Scythe]].

        == Quests ==
        Gifted Riley Bee is an infinite quest giver. Its quests scale up in difficulty the more the
        player completes them, with the rewards also getting better the more difficult the quests
        get. Each quest always rewards one [[Red Extract]] and [[Honey]], depending on the current
        difficulty of the current quest. Every fifth completed Quest has a bonus reward.

        For the requirements below, ''X'' represents the number of quests received from Gifted Riley
        Bee, or the number of quests completed plus one, ''Y'' represents how many times 10 quests
        have been completed, ''Z'' represents how many times 25 quests have been completed, and
        ''A'' represents how many times 50 quests have been completed. Requirements not represented
        this way are static or may progress in a way not yet determined.

        In addition to red extracts and honey as rewards for completing each quest, certain
        thresholds may reward other items such as treats, strawberries, tickets, stingers, royal
        jellies, magic beans, and eggs.

        For every 25th Quest that is completed, a [[Royal Jelly#Star Jelly|Star Jelly]] is rewarded
        (excluding special milestones).

        == Trivia ==
        * Since the quests are all chosen at random, including the first, the formula for the first
        requirements of any quest is \"x multiplied by (number of Gifted Riley Bee quests completed
        +1)\"
        * Gifted Riley Bee is one of the three [[:Category:NPC|NPCs]] that are based on [[Bees]],
        the others being Gifted Bucko Bee and Honey Bee (NPC).
        * Gifted Riley Bee is the first and only quest giver so far to be in the Red HQ.
        * There was a [[Glitches#Wingless Bees|Visual Glitch]] where looking through the glass roof
        over the Red HQ and looked at Gifted Riley Bee, Gifted Riley Bee would have appeared
        wingless. However, in the [[Updates#2019.09.28|9/28/2019 Update]], the glass roof was
        removed.
        * While Gifted Riley Bee says to defeat 50 scorpions in the Honeyday Candles dialogue, the
        requirement was replaced with collecting 500 red bomb tokens.
        * Gifted Riley Bee says that the 2021 Honeyday Candles give a Swirled Wax for each use, but
        the candles can actually give all types of waxes and at a varying amount.
        * The second half of the dialogue of Gifted Riley Bee's Beesmas 2021 present dialogue is
        repeated from Beesmas 2020's as Gifted Riley Bee mentions setting up the bPad.
