        The {{Color|Honeysuckle}} is a consumable inventory item that was introduced in the
        [[Updates#2021.12.26|12/26/2021 update]]. Unlike other consumable items with an auto
        setting, '''Honeysuckle''' is automatically used once a player's pollen container is full,
        and is also the only item with an auto setting that cannot be put in the hotbar. When a
        player's pollen container is full, the Honeysuckle causes the player's [[bees]] to instantly
        convert some [[pollen]] in their [[Bags|bag]] into [[honey]]. It has a cooldown of 30
        seconds before it can activate again, and can be toggled on or off by clicking the item in
        the inventory.

        Unlike most consumables, this cannot be dragged to the hotbar. There is, however, an On/Off
        switch in the honeysuckle's inventory section. Turning it ON causes it to work automatically
        when the bag becomes full, and turning it OFF disables using honeysuckles.

        * As a reward from the [[Robo Bear Challenge]].
        * As a reward from the [[Memory Match#Mega Memory Match|Mega Memory Match]], [[Memory
        Match#Night Memory Match|Night Memory Match]] or [[Memory Match#Extreme Memory Match|Extreme
        Memory Match]].
        * As a Stack Reward for adding certain [[stickers]] to the [[Sticker Stack]]:
        ** The Small Tickseed rewards 10 honeysuckles.
        ** The Small White Daisy rewards 10 honeysuckles.
        ** The Small Pink Tulip rewards 10 honeysuckles.
        ** The Small Dandelion rewards 10 honeysuckles.

        == How to Use ==
        Three things are needed for a honeysuckle to activate:
        # There is an available honeysuckle in inventory, i.e. it is turned on and not on cooldown.
        # The player's bag is full.
        # More pollen is collected.
