        ''<small>Not to be confused with the [[Hub Field]].</small>''

        The '''Hive Hub''' is a unique location added in the [[Updates|1/12/2024]] update. It is
        inside the Hive Hub Portal, which is beyond the [[Ant Gate]]. It has several features,
        including a total of 36 hives for players to claim, the [[Public Sticker Board]], the
        [[Sticker-Seeker]], the [[Hub Field]], a [[Beequip]] Storage chest, and [[Sprout|Sticker
        Sprouts]] that spawns every 3 hours or through [[Magic Bean]]s. The Hive Hub's purpose is to
        seek out opportunities to trade with other players and to craft the [[Sticker-Seeker]], a
        tool that specializes in collecting stickers and unlocks the [[Sticker-Seeker Quest
        Machine|Sticker-Seeker's quests]]. The Hive Hub also has a field, aptly named the Hub Field,
        which instantly converts all pollen collected into honey, as well as [[flowers]] regrowing
        instantly, so there is no need for Sprinklers. [[Bees]] do not spawn to reduce lag.

        == Tips ==
        * Sharing [[Jelly Beans]] is possible here. This makes it easy to share Jelly Beans for
        quests, the sharing buff, or other purposes.
        * Be aware that the Hive Hub isn't very well optimized (Bee Swarm Simulator wasn't made to
        handle this much data at once), so its best to have a decent device and set your graphics to
        1 in order to avoid crashing and major framedrops. Still, expect your frames per second to
        drop to at least 50.

        == Trivia ==
        * The following items are not usable in the Hive Hub:
        **[[Sprinklers]].
        **[[Snowflake]].
        **[[Stinger]] (This means that [[Passive Abilities#Star Saw|Star Saw]] cannot be activated
        here).
        **[[Cloud Vial]].
        **[[Field Dice]], [[Smooth Dice]], and [[Loaded Dice]].
        **[[Whirligig]]s.
        **[[Night Bell]]s.
        **Every type of [[Balloon]].
        **Every type of [[Planter]].
        *The Hive Hub is the only field in which your Bees don't spawn.
