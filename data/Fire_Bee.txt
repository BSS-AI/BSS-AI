        '''Fire Bee''' is a [[:Category:Red|Red]] [[Bees/Epic|Epic bee]].

        Fire Bee's favorite type of treat are [[Treat#Pineapple|Pineapples]].

        Fire Bee likes the [[Mushroom Field]], and [[Strawberry Field]]. It dislikes the [[Pine Tree
        Forest]].

        == Stats ==
        * Collects 10 [[Pollen]] in 4 seconds.
        * Makes 80 [[Honey]] in 4 seconds.
        * -20% [[Stats#Speed|Movespeed]], +25% [[Energy]], +3 [[Stats#Attack|Attack]].
        * 🌟 [[Gifted Bee|Gifted Hive Bonus]]: x1.25 [[Flame#Flame Pollen|Flame Pollen]].

        === Abilities ===
        * '''[[Ability Tokens#Bomb|[Red Bomb+]]]''' Collects 10 pollen from 29 surrounding red
        [[Flowers]] (+10% pollen per Level). Combo with other bombs to increase power.
        * '''[[Passive Abilities#Gathering Flames|[Passive:]] [[Flame#Gathering
        Flames|<nowiki>Gathering Flames]</nowiki>]]''' 35% chance (50% if Gifted) to
        spawn a [[Flame]] when gathering. Flames last 4s. Each second they collect 4 red/2
        [[:Category:Colorless|White]]/1 [[:Category:Blue|Blue]] pollen (+2% per Level) from 9
        flowers, and deal 10 (+25% [[Bee Attack|base attack]]) damage to nearby enemies. They also
        grant Flame Heat if you stand near them, which increases [[System Page#Red Pollen|Red
        Pollen]] and [[Bee Attack]].

        == Trivia ==
        * Fire Bee and [[Demon Bee]] have the same flame effect.
        ** They both are also the only red bees that like pineapples.
        * Fire Bee can be summoned by [[Spicy Bee]] with its [[Ability Tokens|Inferno]] ability,
        making it the only bee summon-able by [[Ability Tokens]].
        ** This bee could also be summoned by using the [[Honeyday Candles]].
        * Fire Bee is the red counterpart of [[Bubble Bee]].
        * The [[Fire Mask]], which can be purchased in the [[Red HQ]], has a special design, name,
        and stats dedicated to Fire Bee.
        * This bee was one of the bees to have a specific jelly in the [[Bee Bear's Catalog]]. The
        jelly was given as part of the Cozy Bundle.
