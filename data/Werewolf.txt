        ==Strategies==
        The main strategy players use is to have something in the way between them and the Werewolf.
        The main things players use are the bigger cactus or the smaller pumpkin. The werewolf will
        bounce off the object when he tries to attack, making killing him easy.

        The run-and-jump method also works: like most Mobs, the Werewolf gets confused while the
        player is in the air and will lunge at the point the player was when they jumped, not where
        they’re at after they jumped.

        The [[Glitches#Frozen Field Defenders|Frozen Field Defenders Glitch]] works well against the
        Werewolf.

        Players can also just go through the Honey Bee Gate, then quickly run behind the Pine Tree
        nearest to the [[Badge Bearer's Guild]]. Then, if he goes back to the cave, just walk beside
        him and then run back behind the tree. The player should keep doing this until they defeat
        him.

        ==Trivia==
        *This and the [[spider]] are the only mobs that attack players beyond the field they spawn
        in.
        *This is the only normal mob to defend three fields (Cactus Field, Pumpkin Patch and Pine
        tree forest).
        *The werewolf uses the [https://www.roblox.com/catalog/132574130/White-Fang \"White Fang
        Package\"] on the Roblox Catalog.
        *This was the first non-bug/arachnid mob added into the game.
        *The player can earn more than one egg after defeating the werewolf; for example, he can
        drop 5 [[Egg|Silver Eggs]] at once.
        *This is the second strongest normal mob in the game, only beaten by the estimated health of
        the [[Cave Monster]]s.
        *Among the normal mobs, the werewolf is the one that spawns the least frequently, at 1 hour
        after every defeat.
        *This is the first mob in which the respawn timer is 1 hour or more, the other 7 being
        [[Stump Snail]], [[Snowbear]] and [[:Category:Bosses|Bosses]].
        *If you run inside the Werewolf's Cave, you would be faced with a tunnel, which, if you
        cross, rewards you with a gold egg. However, the cave is guarded by the previously mentioned
        Cave Monsters that you will have to rush past to reach the egg.
