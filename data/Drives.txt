{"text": "'''Drives''' are inventory items that were introduced in the [[Updates#2022.12.26|12/26/22
        update]]. They can be used to repair [[Robo Bear]], purchase [[Digital Bee]], upgrade
        Digital Bee, and grant boosts during a round of the [[Robo Bear Challenge]]. All Drives
        (except Broken Drives) can be purchased from Robo Bear's Shop for 50 Cogs (100 Cogs for
        Glitched, and 5 of each other drive), and an increasing amount of Honey with each purchase.
        ==List of Drives==

        ===White Drive===
        [[File:WhiteDrive.png|thumb|150x150px]]
        The '''White Drive''', when used, grants the following boosts during a single round of Robo
        Bear's Challenge:

        *x1.25 [[System Page#White Pollen|White Pollen]]
        *x1.25 [[System Page#White Field Capacity|White Field Capacity]]
        *+1 [[Bee Attack#Colorless Bee Attack|Colorless Bee Attack]]

        Ways to Obtain:
        *Purchasing it for 50 [[Cog]]s and 250,000 Honey (Honey price increases each time you buy
        one with an unknown cap) in Robo Bear's Shop.
        *Chance to receive from Robo Bear's Challenge.
        *Completing [[Science Bear]]’s \"Repairing The White Drive\" quest gives 1 White Drive,
        replacing the Broken Drive.
        The methods below are no longer possible.

        * Purchasing it in Bee Bear's Catalog for 222 [[Snowflake]]s.
        * Purchasing the [[Robux Shop|Digital Bee pack]] for 2500 Robux granted 10 White Drives +
        other stuff.
        * As a reward for finishing your own [[Robo Party]].

        ===Red Drive===
        [[File:RedDrive.png|thumb|150x150px]]
        The '''Red Drive''', when used, grants the following boosts during a single round of Robo
        Bear's Challenge:

        *x1.25 [[System Page#Red Pollen|Red Pollen]]
        *x1.25 [[System Page#Red Field Capacity|Red Field Capacity]]
        *+1 [[Bee Attack#Red Bee Attack|Red Bee Attack]]

        Ways to Obtain:
        *Purchasing it for 50 [[Cog]]s and 250,000 [[Honey]] (Honey price increases each time you
        buy one with an unknown cap) in [[Robo Bear's Shop]].
        *Chance to receive from Robo Bear's Challenge.
        *Completing [[Panda Bear]]'s \"Ready For The Red Drive?\" Quest gives the Red Drive (Requires
        you to finish [[Science Bear]]'s White Drive quest first)
        The methods below are no longer possible.

        * Completing [[Science Bear|Science Bear's]] 2022 [[Beesmas]] quest.
        * Purchasing the Digital Bee pack for 2500 Robux granted 10 Red Drives + other stuff.
        * As a reward for finishing your own Robo Party.
        * Opening the Festive Gift Box rewards 5 Red Drives.

        ===Blue Drive===
        [[File:BlueDrive.png|thumb|150x150px]]
        The '''Blue Drive''', when used, grants the following boosts during a single round of Robo
        Bear's Challenge:

        *x1.25 [[System Page#Blue Pollen|Blue Pollen]]
        *x1.25 [[System Page#Blue Field Capacity|Blue Field Capacity]]
        *+1 [[Bee Attack#Blue Bee Attack|Blue Bee Attack]]

        Ways to Obtain:
        *Purchasing it for 50 [[Cog]]s and 250,000 [[Honey]] (Honey price increases each time you
        buy one with an unknown cap) in Robo Bear's Shop.
        *Chance to receive from Robo Bear's Challenge.
        *Completing [[Dapper Bear]]'s \"Barter For The Blue Drive\" Quest grants 1 Blue Drive
        (Requires you to finish [[Science Bear]]'s White Drive quest first)
        The methods below are no longer possible.

        * Completing Dapper Bear's 2022 [[Beesmas]] quest.
        * Purchasing the Digital Bee pack for 2500 Robux granted 10 Blue Drives + other stuff.
        * As a reward for finishing your own Robo Party.

        ===Glitched Drive===
        [[File:GlitchedDrive.png|thumb|150x150px]]
        The '''Glitched Drive''', when used, grants the following boosts during a single round of
        Robo Bear's Challenge:

        *x1.25 [[Pollen]]
        *x1.25 [[Field Capacity]]
        *+1 [[Bee Attack#Regular Bee Attack|Bee Attack]]

        Ways to Obtain:
        *Purchasing it for 100 [[Cog]]s, 5 Red Drives, 5 Blue Drives, 5 White Drives, and 1,000,000
        [[Honey]] (Honey price increases each time you buy one with an unknown cap) in Robo Bear's
        Shop. It requires a Gold Cog Amulet to unlock the ability of purchasing it.
        *Completing 10 or more rounds of Robo Bear's Challenge has a chance to reward a Glitched
        Drive (Chance increases as the player finished the challenge on a higher round).
        *Completing [[Science Bear]]'s \"Corrupting The Glitched Drive\" quest (Requires you to
        complete Science Bear's White Drive quest, Dapper Bear's Blue Drive quest, and [[Panda
        Bear]]'s Red Drive quest).
        The methods below are no longer possible.

        * Completing [[Bee Bear|Bee Bear's]] 5th 2022 [[Beesmas]] quest.
        * Purchasing the Digital Bee pack for 2500 Robux granted 10 glitched drives + other stuff.
        * Purchasing the Mondo Robo-Bundle for 4500 Robux grants 10 glitched drives + other stuff.
        * Purchasing the Glitching Bundle in [[Bee Bear's Catalog]] for 111 [[Gingerbread
        Bear|Gingerbread Bears.]]
        * As a reward for finishing your own Robo Party.
        * Opening the Mondo Gift Box rewards with 10 Glitched Drives.

        === Broken Drive ===
        {{Main|Broken Drive}}
        [[File:Broken Drive.png|thumb|113x113px]]
        The '''Broken Drive''' is a unique drive that can be collected only once, from an area
        behind the [[Robo Pass Dispenser]]s. Once collected, it initiates Science Bear's drive
        questline. After completing Science Bear's quest the Broken Drive will be replaced with a
        White Drive.

        Ways to Obtain:
        *Found behind the Robo Pass Dispensers in the [[Bear Gate|30 Bee Zone]].
