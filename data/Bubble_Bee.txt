        == Trivia ==
        * Bubble Bee is the Blue counterpart of [[Fire Bee]].
        * Bubble Bee emits a Bubble effect, a trait also shared with [[Honey Bee]] and its Gifted
        variant.
        * Bubble Bee and [[Tadpole Bee]] are the only [[bees]] that have the Gathering Bubbles
        Passive Ability.
        * The [[Bubble Mask]], which can be purchased in the [[Blue HQ]], has a special design,
        name, and stats dedicated to Bubble Bee.
        * Despite this bee's name, this bee has nothing to do with [[Bubble Bee Man]].
        ** However, Bubble Bee Man did give out a [[Royal Jelly#Royal Jelly Variants|Bubble Bee
        jelly]] in [[Beesmas]] 2018 and a [[Egg#Specific Bee Eggs|Bubble Bee egg]] in Beesmas 2020
        and Beesmas 2021.
