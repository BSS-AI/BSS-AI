        '''Vicious Bee''' is a [[:Category:Blue|Blue]] [[Bees/Event|Event bee]]. It can be claimed
        for 250 [[Stinger]]s at the [[Vicious Bee Egg Claim]].

        Like all other Event bees, this bee does not have any favorite treat. It can only be gifted
        by feeding it a [[Star Treat|Star Treat]], [[Gingerbread Bear]]s, or [[Aged Gingerbread
        Bear]]s.

        Vicious Bee likes the [[Cactus Field]] and the [[Rose Field]]. It dislikes the [[Dandelion
        Field]].

        ==Stats==
        *Collects 10 [[Pollen]] in 4 seconds.
        *Makes 80 [[Honey]] in 4 seconds.
        *+150% [[Energy]], +25% [[Stats#Speed|Movespeed]], +7 [[Stats#Attack|Attack]].
        *🌟 [[Gifted Bee|Gifted Hive Bonus]]: -15% Mob Respawn Time.

        === Abilities===
        *'''[[Ability Tokens#Impale|[Impale]]]''' Summons spikes (1 per level) to damage enemies
        equal to 5% of their current health (Increased by Vicious Bee's attack. Damage reduced past
        1000). Creates a honey token per enemy hit (worth 50 honey plus 50 per bee level). Multiple
        spikes on the same target deal less damage. Impale can damage [[mobs]] that target other
        players but will not target other players' [[Tunnel Bear]], [[King Beetle]], [[Stump
        Snail]], or [[Coconut Crab]]. If gifted, Impale has +1 level of accuracy.
        *'''[[Ability Tokens#Bomb|[Blue Bomb+]]]''' Collects 10 pollen from 29 surrounding blue
        [[Flowers]] (+10% pollen per Level). Combo with other bombs to increase power.

        ==Trivia==
        *Vicious Bee's base attack was previously 7 before it increased to the current attack (8) in
        the 12/26/21 update.
        *Gifted Vicious Bee's impale ability gets golden-colored spikes, instead of the normal
        colored (gray) ones. The Impale ability does more base damage when gifted due to the +50%
        gifted attack bonus. The attack bonus is applied to the bee itself, but it indirectly
        increases the amount of damage Impale does—though this is not a significant buff due to the
        majority of Impale's attack scaling off of mob health.
        *Vicious Bee is the only [[Bees|Bee]] who can summon an [[Ability Tokens|Ability Token]]
        that directly attacks enemies.
        ** This bee and [[Precise Bee]] are the only bees who have abilities that directly attack
        the enemies, namely Impale and [[Passive Abilities#Sniper|Sniper]] respectively. ([[Digital
        Bee]]'s Mind Hack ability doesn't count since it doesn't directly attack enemies).
        *Vicious Bee is one of three bees to have an official plushie on the
        [https://shop.beeswarm.com/ Official Bee Swarm Simulator Website], the others being Basic
        Bee and [[Tabby Bee]].
        *Vicious Bee is the only blue bee that likes the Rose Field. It may like the Rose Field
        along with the Cactus Field due to the fact that roses and cacti have thorns on them. The
        [[Rogue Vicious Bee|Rogue Version]] can also spawn in both of these fields.
        * Vicious Bee and [[Windy Bee]] are the only bees with a hostile variant.
        *It would cost the player 2500 [[Ticket]]s if they were to buy all 250 stingers from the
        [[Stinger Shop]] to get a Vicious Bee, which makes it the most expensive event bee to get by
        spending tickets.
        *Vicious Bee, [[Ninja Bee]], and Cobalt Bee are the only blue bees that don’t like
        [[Blueberry|Blueberries]].
        *Vicious Bee, [[Bear Bee]], [[Gummy Bee]], Windy Bee, and [[Digital Bee]] are the only Event
        bees that cannot be purchased in the [[Ticket Tent]]. These four bees, along with [[Festive
        Bee]] and Puppy Bee, are also the only Event bees that could have been obtained without
        spending tickets. Gummy Bee was in the Ticket Tent at one point, however.
        *Vicious Bee, Cobalt Bee, Crimson Bee, and [[Photon Bee]] are the only event bees that can't
        have a 1st Edition Flag.
        **[[Onett (Developer)|Onett]] has stated that the reason a [[First Edition Bee|First
        Edition]] Vicious Bee does not exist is because there is no room for a 1st Flag on its back
        with the
        spikes.<ref>[https://discord.com/channels/427553293862961153/427553293862961155/506287555231088658]
        Discord message from Onett</ref>
        *Vicious Bee, the [[Stick Bug Amulet]], and the Spider Field [[Boost Market|Market Boost]]
        are the only sources of the -% Mob Respawn Time stats.
        *According to [[Black Bear]]'s dialogue during his [[Quests|Quest]] \"Black Bear, Why?\",
        Black Bear dislikes this bee.
