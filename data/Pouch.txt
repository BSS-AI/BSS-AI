        The '''Pouch''' is the starter [[:Category:Bags|Bag]] that new Players automatically receive
        at the beginning of the game, along with the [[Scooper]], the starter
        [[:Category:Tools|Tool]]. You can equip it in the [[Noob Shop]].

        It doesn't have a price as it is the first Bag given to the Player for free.

        == Stats ==
        * +200 Capacity.

        == Trivia ==
        * In early versions of the game, the Pouch stored 150 pollen.
        ** It was later buffed to 250 then later nerfed to 200.
        * This and the [[Jar]] are the only bags that cost less Honey than their base capacity.
        * This, the [[Scooper]], and a [[Egg|Basic Egg]] are the only items that the player starts
        off with when joining the game for the very first time.
