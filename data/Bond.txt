        '''Bond''' is a [[Mechanics|mechanic]] that is equivalent to the leveling system found in
        many other games. When [[bees]] have a high enough bond with the player, they will level up,
        gaining the following boosts per level above 1:
        * +10% [[Stats#Production Amount|production amount]];
        * +10% [[Stats#Gather Amount|gather amount]];
        * +5% [[energy]];
        * +3% [[Stats#Speed|movespeed]].

        Bond can be checked at any time by clicking on the bee's [[Hive Slot|hive slot]] and it will
        show the bee's bond and [[stats]].

        A bee will automatically gain bond when collecting [[pollen]] in a [[Fields|field]]. When
        collecting pollen, each bee type has different opinions of each field and either likes, is
        neutral toward, or dislikes any given field. If a bee has a sad [[Emoticons|emoticon]] over
        its head, it means that the bee does not like that field and will gain only minimal bond
        from collecting pollen in that field. If it has a happy emoticon instead, it likes the field
        and will gain extra bond. If it doesn't have an emoticon at all, it's neutral towards that
        field and will gain the usual amount of bond. Bees can be fed [[treats]] to increase the
        bee's bond by 10, 25, 50, 100, 250, 500, or 1000 depending on the type of treat the bee is
        fed. If the player feeds a bee its favorite treat, it will gain double the amount of bond it
        would otherwise get, as well as having a very low chance to become [[Gifted Bee|gifted]] or
        [[Mutation|mutated]]. Bond is also rewarded from defeating [[mobs]] and collecting a
        [[Ability Tokens#Puppy Love|Puppy Love]] token from [[Puppy Bee]].

        A bee's bond does '''not''' reset after a level up; however, the information window only
        shows the bond required for the next level, so it can appear as if the bond went down to
        zero. (This is why when feeding a bee a treat, the server message on the lower right of the
        screen and the chat will have a different number than the bee information window.) A bee's
        rarity does not affect how much bond it needs to level up. Both a [[Bees/Common|Common]] and
        [[Bees/Mythic|Mythic bee]] at level 1 would require 10 bond to level up. Bond does not
        diminish when using [[Royal Jelly|royal jelly]] or replacing it with an [[egg]].

        Mobs also have levels, which affects whether a bee will hit, or miss its attack. If the bee
        has the same or a higher level than the mob, it is guaranteed to hit. If the bee is one
        level lower than the mob, it has a {{Hover|50%|1/2}} chance to hit the mob. If the bee is
        two levels down, it only has a {{Hover|25%|1/4}} chance to hit, and so on.

        The formula to calculate the chance of hitting a mob is: <math>1/[2^(monster level -
        bee level)]</math>.

        The maximum level a bee can achieve is 25. It is generally difficult to reach levels above
        20, but if a bee reaches the maximum level, the bee will still gain bond.

        == Trivia==
        *Prior to the [[Updates#2018.11.25|11/25/2018 update]], the bee information page always
        showed the bee's total bond, not the current bond-to-next-level.
        *Leveling a full hive of 50 bees from level 0 to level 20 would cost
        {{Hover|1.25Qd|1,250,000,000,000,000}} (1.25 quadrillion) honey and costs
        {{Hover|1.041Qd|1,041,666,666,666,650}} (~1.041 quadrillion) honey in treats with gifted
        Puppy Bee.
        *Leveling a full hive of 50 bees from level 0 to level 25 would cost 5.005Sx (~5.005
        sextillion) honey in treats and 4.1708Sx (~4.1708 sextillion) honey in treats with gifted
        Puppy Bee.
        *There was a exploit that allowed bees to reach level 25 instantly.
