{"text": "'''Bubbles''' are objects that can be produced by certain [[tools]], [[Ability
        Tokens|abilities]], or [[Passive Abilities|passive abilities]]. Bubbles have a percentage
        chance to be spawned by certain bees when gathering. Bubbles spawn on the flower that the
        bee gathered and last for 4 seconds. When popped, they collect 3 red, 5 white, and 10 blue
        pollen from 29 surrounding flowers (+15% per gifted blue bee type, +5% per bee level) and
        cause nearby flowers to grow back to full.

        Bubbles can be generated by [[Bubble Bee]], [[Tadpole Bee]], [[frog]]s, the [[Bubble Wand]],
        [[Bubble Mask]]'s Bubble Bombs passive, and the Pop Star passive.

        Bubbles can be popped by other players, petal shurikens, tornadoes, Star Saw, [[Ability
        Tokens#Fuzz Bombs|Fuzz Bombs]], the [[Dark Scythe]]'s trail, and the [[Tide Popper]]'s
        waves.

        Popping bubbles helps to grow Pop Stars and will increase [[Buffs & Debuffs#From
        Passives|Bubble Bloat]]. The bigger the Pop Star grows, the more bubbles it will spawn.

        When a bubble is popped, the following audio plays: [[File:Bubblepop.ogg|thumb]]

        == Gathering Bubbles ==
        The '''Gathering Bubbles''' passive gives a chance for a bee to spawn a bubble while
        gathering pollen from a field. Gathering Bubbles+ gives a higher chance for a bee to spawn a
        bubble.

        There is a 25% chance (50% if gifted) for Bubble Bees' Gathering Bubbles passive to spawn a
        bubble while gathering, and a 65% (85% if gifted) chance for Tadpole Bees' Gathering
        Bubbles+ passive to spawn a bubble while gathering.

        A [[Bead Lizard]] gives from +10% Gathering Bubbles chance to +12% Gathering Bubbles chance
        for an individual bee.

        == Bubble Pollen ==
        [[File:BubblePollenStat1.png|thumb|The Bubble Pollen stat in the System Page.]]
        '''Bubble Pollen''' is the amount of pollen that bubbles can collect. The player can check
        how much bubble pollen they have in the system page.

        Bubble Pollen can be increased by a few ways.

        * The [[Diamond Mask]] gives a x1.5 Bubble Pollen multiplier.
        * The [[Bubble Mask]] gives a 1.25x Bubble Pollen multiplier.
        * [[Bubble Bee|Gifted Bubble Bee]] gives a x1.25 Bubble Pollen multiplier.
        * [[Beequip]]s:
        ** A Bead Lizard can give +1% Bubble Pollen as a hive bonus from waxing.
        ** A [[Bubble Light]] gives from +15% Bubble Pollen to +30% Bubble Pollen for an individual
        bee. Using [[waxes]] can increase the amount up to +60% Bubble Pollen.
        ** A Bubble Light can give +1% Bubble Pollen as a hive bonus. Using waxes can increase the
        amount up to +11% Bubble Pollen.
        * [[Cog Amulet]]s:
        ** The Silver Cog Amulet can give from +5% Bubble Pollen up to +6% Bubble Pollen.
        ** The Gold Cog Amulet can give from +8% Bubble Pollen up to +9% Bubble Pollen.
        ** The Diamond Cog Amulet can give extra Bubble Pollen.
        ** The Supreme Cog Amulet can give from +15% Bubble Pollen up to +20% Bubble Pollen.
        * [[Robo Bear Challenge]]:
        ** Epic Upgrades:
        *** Pop-Up: This upgrade gives x2 Bubble Pollen.
        *** RGB: This upgrade gives x1.25 Bubble Pollen.

        ==Improving Bubbles==
        Aside from increasing the amount of pollen that bubbles can collect, the player can also
        improve bubbles a few other ways.

        === Golden Bubbles ===
        A '''Golden Bubble''' is a special variant of a bubble that can only occur when a
        [[Balloon#Golden Balloon|golden balloon]] floats over a normal bubble or it can be summoned
        by a gifted frog.

        When a golden bubble is popped, it collects +100% more pollen than a normal bubble per field
        boost stack, contributes 50% more time to Pop Star's bubble bloat than a normal bubble, has
        100% [[Instant Conversion]] (unless going into a balloon), and has a 10% chance to spawn a
        honey token.

        === Instant Bubble Conversion ===
        [[File:InstantBubbleConversionStat1.png|thumb|The Instant Bubble Conversion stat in the
        System Page.]]
        '''Instant Bubble Conversion''' is how much pollen collected by the bubbles is instantly
        converted. There are currently no ways to increase Instant Bubble Conversion.

        === Bubble Duration ===
        '''Bubble Duration''' affects how long bubbles last before they disappear. The base duration
        is 4 seconds and can be increased to 5 seconds. There is currently only one way to increase
        Bubble Duration. Note that bubbles spawned from Bubbles Bombs last 8 seconds (10 seconds if
        one has a Gifted Tadpole bee).

        * Gifted Tadpole bee grafts +25% Bubble Duration

        === Other Improvements ===
        Certain upgrades from the Robo Bear Challenge can be used to improve bubbles.

        * Epic Upgrades:
        ** Invert: This upgrade makes bubbles collect x4 pollen from red flowers.
        ** Pop-Up: This upgrade gives x1.5 Bubble Lifespan.
