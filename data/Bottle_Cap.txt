        The '''Bottle Cap''' is a [[Bond|level]] 4 [[beequip]]. It has an equip limit of two. There
        is no color requirement. It can only be equipped to [[Bees/Common|common]] or
        [[Bees/Rare|rare]] [[bees]]. It was released in the [[Updates|04/01/2022 update]]

        ==Possible Stats==

        *[[System Page#Convert Amount|Convert Amount]] (Guaranteed) - +10 to +20. (up to +95 from
        [[Waxes|waxing]])
        *[[Critical Hits|Critical Power]] (1% chance as a base stat, ~30% chance from waxing) - +5%
        to +10%. (up to +60% from waxing)
        *[[Critical Hits|Critical Chance]] (Guaranteed) - +1% to +2%. (up to +12% from waxing)
        *[[Ability Tokens#Bomb|Ability: Buzz Bomb]] (from waxing, rare, caustic only)
        *[Hive Bonus] Critical Power (from waxing, rare) - +2% to +15%.

        ==Ways to Obtain==
        *As a drop from certain [[planter]]s:
        **As a rare drop from [[The Planter Of Plenty]].
        **As a very rare drop from the [[Plastic Planter]].
        **As a very rare drop from the [[Candy Planter]].
        *Buying it from [[Dapper Bear's Shop]].
        *A very rare chance from the [[Ant Challenge]].

        ===These methods are no longer possible.===
        *From the [[Gift Boxes|Vibrant Gift Box]].
