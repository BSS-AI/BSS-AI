        This is the first Ant Challenge [[Mobs|mob]] that the player will encounter, appearing on
        the very first wave.

        Regular Ants move in a fixed straight line and are the most generic ant. They are the most
        common ant and will always appear in the first few waves. They are the easiest ant to deal
        with due to how basic they are compared to the other ants.

        A Regular Ant's level can increase depending on what wave of the Ant Challenge the player is
        on. They can range from level 1 up to level 16.

        They are by far the least threatening of the five ant types, as they are often easily and
        completely avoidable.

        == Strategies ==

        * Regular Ants move in predetermined straight lines and are quite slow. This enables to
        player to easily dodge any coming their way.
        * Standing near the Regular Ant will allow your bees to quickly target the Regular Ant to
        quickly deal damage and defeat it.
        * Regular Ants will come out in groups and they could overwhelm the player if they are
        collecting pollen quickly to advance to the next waves. [[Vicious Bee]] and [[Windy Bee]]
        provide excellent crowd control with their
        [https://bee-swarm-simulator.fandom.com/wiki/Ability_Tokens?so=search#Impale Impale] and
        [https://bee-swarm-simulator.fandom.com/wiki/Ability_Tokens?so=search#Tornado Tornado]
        abilities which could greatly help counter the large amounts of Regular Ants landing on the
        field. [[Digital Bee]] can also stun many Regular Ants at once and allow them to take extra
        damage via [[Ability Tokens#Mind Hack|Mind Hack]].
