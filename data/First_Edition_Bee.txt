        A '''First Edition Bee''' is a designation given to certain [[Bees/Event|Event Bees]] bought
        or earned during their first release. This designation is represented by a white and yellow
        \"1st\" flag appearing on top of the bee, and the flag emits a sparkling effect. The flag is
        purely cosmetic and does not change or affect gameplay at all.

        This designation is currently only available for [[Bear Bee]], [[Tabby Bee]], [[Gummy Bee]],
        [[Puppy Bee]], [[Festive Bee]], [[Digital Bee]], and [[Windy Bee]].

        If the player removes a first edition bee, the flag will NOT disappear once it is added back
        to the [[hive]].

        == First Edition Bees ==
        * Bear Bee: Bought at the first month of BSS launching (all first edition Bear Bees got an
        additional bonus of being automatically [[Gifted Bee|gifted]]).
        * Tabby Bee and Puppy Bee: Purchased during the their respective release sales (half price).
        * Gummy Bee: Obtained after completing all of [[Gummy Bear|Gummy Bear's]] [[quests]] during
        the [[:Category:Gummy Invasion|Gummy Invasion Event]].
        *Festive Bee: Obtained after completing all of [[Bee Bear|Bee Bear's]] quests during the
        [[:Category:Beesmas|Beesmas 2018 Event]] or obtaining it from the Festive Bee Pack for 1,600
        Robux in the [[Robux Shop]].
        * Windy Bee: Obtained as a special prize from the [[Wind Shrine]] or purchased in the Robux
        Shop before October 27, 2019.
        * Digital Bee: Obtained from [[Robo Bear's Shop|Robo Bear's shop]] behind [[Robo Bear]], or
        by purchasing the Digital Bee Pack for 2,500 Robux in the [[Robux Shop]] during
        [[:Category:Beesmas|Beesmas 2022.]]
