        }}'''Plastic Eggs''' were blue-colored eggs that were scattered around the map for [[Brown
        Bear#Egg Hunt Quest 2019|Brown Bear’s 2019 Egg Hunt Quest]]. The player had to find all
        three to finish Brown Bear's Egg Hunt Quest, which awarded a [[Marshmallow Bee|marshmallow
        bee]]. They had no special abilities and were removed when turning in the [[Quests|quest]].
        The locations were replaced by [[ticket]] tokens after the [[Egg Hunt 2019]] ended and the
        [[Updates#2019.05.13|5/13/2019 update]].

        == Locations ==

        * In the [[Mazes#Blue Maze|Blue Maze]], next to the [[Ticket Tent]].
        ** Turn left first, and follow the right wall after that to find the token.
        * [[File:60C05C6F-C42A-4EFB-8944-15A53C04CDF7.webp|thumb|127x127px|A Plastic Egg token.]]In
        the ramp leading up to the [[Basic Bee Gate]], opposite the [[Blue Flower Field]] and behind
        the [[Honey Bee (NPC)|Honey Bee's]] original location.
        * Inside the [[Obstacle Courses#Lava Obby|Lava Parkour]]. There will be a little window
        outside of the lava parkour that shows the token.
        ** When entering, immediately turn right, and climb up the rocks.

        == Trivia ==
        * This is the first and only [[egg]] that can be obtained as an inventory item and yet can't
        hatch into a [[Bees|bee]].
        * In the Egg Hunt 2019 lobby, the portal to Bee Swarm Simulator said, \"Find 5 Plastic Eggs\",
        though the player only needed 3 to obtain a Marshmallow Bee.
        ** Compounding the error, this was also not the only quest needed to complete the Egg Hunt.
        * This and the [[Drives#Broken Drive|Broken Drive]] are the only [[items]] that only serve a
        purpose to complete a quest.
