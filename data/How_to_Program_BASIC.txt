        The '''How to Program BASIC''' is a Roblox cosmetic hat that can be obtained by solving all
        the puzzles from [[The Computer]]. It has been available starting from November 23, 2020
        through the [[Ready Player Two]] event. As it is a Roblox hat, players can wear it on their
        avatar. When the player solves the puzzles, the hat will appear in the center inside the
        computer. However, if they leave the game, it won't appear when they rejoin.
