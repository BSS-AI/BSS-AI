        }}The '''Egg Hunt 2019''' was a Roblox event that spanned across 42 games, including [[Bee
        Swarm Simulator]], all connected to one central hub. In each game, players had to complete a
        certain task/quest in order to obtain an egg, which they could then wear on their avatar. In
        doing so, they could unlock more areas in the Hub World to gain powers, which they could use
        to defeat the final boss. More information can be found
        [https://roblox.fandom.com/wiki/Egg_Hunt_2019%3A_Scrambled_in_Time here].

        To get the egg from Bee Swarm Simulator, players needed to interact with the [[Egg Hunt
        Info]] near the [[Ant Gate]] to receive the quest. Then, three [[Marshmallow Bee|marshmallow
        bees]] are needed to be obtained. When the player had obtained three Marshmallow Bees, they
        would have to return to Egg Hunt Info for a few special rewards along with the [[Flight of
        the Bumble Egg]], which players can wear on their Roblox avatar as a hat.
