        == Trivia ==
        * This is the second boot accessory that could not have been received from [[Sun Bear]],
        mainly because it did not exist back then. It was preceded by the [[Gummy Boots]].
        * It is the first and currently only pair of boots whose name doesn't end with \"Boots\".
        * This is the ninth most expensive item in the game in terms of honey (excluding hive
        slots).
        * This is the second boot accessory to have a [[Passive Abilities|passive ability]]; the
        first one being the Gummy Boots.
        * This and the [[Coconut Canister]] are currently the only items that require a [[mob]]
        ([[Coconut Crab]]) to be defeated to access the item.
        * This is the only item to boost pollen from field.
