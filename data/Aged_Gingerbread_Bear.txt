        An '''Aged Gingerbread Bear''' is a type of [[Treats|Treat]] that was added in the
        [[Updates#2021.12.26|12/26/2021 update]]. When fed to a bee, it raises its [[bond]] by 500
        and has a 1.1% chance of turning the bee gifted. Unused {{Cost|Gingerbread Bear}} would
        transform into aged gingerbread bears after the next [[Beesmas]] has started. These cannot
        be used to purchase items from [[Bee Bear's Catalog]].

        The formula for the chance of turning a bee gifted is as follows: <math>([1 -
        0.989^x]* 100)\%</math>, where x is the number of aged gingerbread bears fed to a bee.
        == Ways to Obtain ==
        * The only way to obtain aged gingerbread bears is by saving {{Cost|Gingerbread Bear}} until
        the start of the next Beesmas.

        {{Cost|Gingerbread Bear}} to their bees, such as [[Bee Bear|Bee Bear's]] \"Snow Cub
        Reformation (2/5)\" quest.
