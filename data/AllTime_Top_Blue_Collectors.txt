        The '''All-Time Top Blue Collectors Leaderboard''' is one of 62 [[leaderboards]] in the
        game. This leaderboard shows how much blue [[pollen]] players have collected over time. It
        shows 10 leaderboard positions at a time. The player can scroll down the leaderboard to see
        more players, all the way to 100th place. Only legitimate players appear on the leaderboards
        for lengths of time (hackers/exploiters will be removed).
        If the player steps on the white pad in front of it, the message <span
        style=\"background-color:#0090BF;color:white\">\"You've collected \"x\" Blue
        Pollen\"</span> will appear, \"x\" being the amount of blue pollen the player has
        collected.

        == Location ==
        It is located on the first floor of the [[Blue HQ]], next to the ramp to the second floor.

        == Achievements ==

        * McProsephYouTube was the first user to reach 10 trillion total blue pollen collected.
        * Lipsisas was the first user to reach 100 trillion total blue pollen collected.
        * ggbondsupreme was the first user to reach 1 quadrillion total blue pollen collected.
