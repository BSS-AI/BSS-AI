        The '''Refreshing Vial''' is a consumable [[Nectar Vial|nectar vial]] [[Items|item]] that
        was added in the [[Updates#2022.12.26|12/26/2022 update]]. Once used, it grants the player 4
        hours of the [[Nectar|Refreshing Nectar]] [[Buffs & Debuffs|buff]] instantly. It can
        also be used to craft other [[:Category:Accessories|accessories]].

        == Ways to obtain ==
        * Completing [[Dapper Bear|Dapper Bear's]] 7th and 14th quests.
        * Using the [[Nectar Condenser]] to condense Refreshing Nectar.
        * Adding the Refreshing Nectar Icon to the [[Sticker Stack]] grants 1 Refreshing Vial.
        ===The methods below are no longer possible.===
        * Purchasing the Refreshing Bundle in [[Bee Bear's Catalog]] for 50 [[Snowflake]]s and 5
        [[Gingerbread Bear]]s granted 1 Refreshing Vial and other items.
        * Purchasing the Hefty Honeyday Bundle in Bee Bear's Catalog for 25,000 Snowflakes and 50
        Gingerbread Bears granted 5 Refreshing Vials and other items.
        * Purchasing the Nighttime Nectar Pack in the [[Robux Shop]] granted 1 of each Nectar Vial.
        * Opening the [[Gift Boxes#2022|Tropical Gift Box]] gave 1 Refreshing Vial and other items.
        * Completing [[Onett#Beesmas Event 2022 - Yard Art on The Lid|Onett's Beesmas 2022 Quest]]
        gave 1 Refreshing Vial and other items.
