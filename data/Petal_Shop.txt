        The '''Petal Shop''' is a [[:Category:Shops|shop]] located past the [[Windy Bee Gate]].
        Inside, the player can craft the [[Petal Wand]], the [[Petal Belt]] and the [[Petal
        Planter]]. The [[All-Time Top White Collectors]] Leaderboard and [[Daily Top White
        Collectors]] Leaderboard can also be found here.

        It is called the Petal Shop because of the giant petals draped across the back wall. In
        addition, each of its items require a [[Spirit Petal|spirit petal]] to
        [[:Category:Crafted|craft]] (except for the Petal Planter).

        == Trivia ==
        * The wall behind the Petal Shop is semi-transparent and can be walked through. Behind it,
        there's a corridor which leads to a [[Royal Jelly#Star Jelly|star jelly]] token that can be
        seen in the [[Mazes#Blue Maze|Blue Maze]], from where the jellybeans token is found. The
        token awards one star jelly.
        ** There used to be the [[Mysterious Figure]] in the place of the Star Jelly.
        * Occasionally, rain from [[clouds]] in the [[Pepper Patch]] may be visible in the Petal
        Shop.
        **The beam of a [[sprout]] can also be seen behind the semi-transparent wall.
