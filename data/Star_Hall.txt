        The '''Star Hall''' is located on the roof of the [[Noob Shop]]. Players can reach it using
        the [[Parachute]] or the [[Glider]] after using a [[Red Cannon|cannon]]. They can also glide
        down from the [[Lion Bee Gate]]. If the player has enough [[System Page#Jump Power|Jump
        Power]], they can glide from the rock that is above [[Brown Bear]] to the edge of the Star
        Hall or jump off the top of the [[Royal Jelly Dispenser]] that is next to the [[Clover
        Field]].

        Outside of the Star Hall, there is a [[Royal Jelly]] token to the right of the gate and a
        Green Cog with a [[Robo Pass]] token to the left of the gate. Inside, it has five types of
        [[Star Amulet]] Generators, an [[Ant Pass]] token (located behind the Supreme Star Amulet
        Generator), and the Royal Jelly Dispenser.
        *'''Royal Jelly Dispenser''': Allows players to claim 1 Royal Jelly per [[Gifted Bee]] in
        their [[hive]] and activates the [[Ability Tokens#Haste|Haste+]] boost. This boost lasts for
        exactly 60 seconds after using the machine. Naturally, players need at least one Gifted Bee
        in order to use the machine. It does not cost anything to use the machine, but there is a
        22-hour cooldown.
        *'''Bronze Star Amulet Generator''': Players must discover 5 Gifted Bee types (not gifted
        bees in hive) to use this machine. It costs 25 million [[honey]] to generate a Bronze Star
        Amulet.
        *'''Silver Star Amulet Generator''': Players must discover 10 Gifted Bee types (not gifted
        bees in hive) to use this machine. It costs 50 million honey to generate a Silver Star
        Amulet.
        *'''Gold Star Amulet Generator''': Players must discover 20 Gifted Bee types (not gifted
        bees in hive) to use this machine. It costs 250 million honey to generate a Gold Star
        Amulet.
        *'''Diamond Star Amulet Generator''': Players must discover 30 Gifted Bee types (not gifted
        bees in hive) to use this machine. It costs 1 billion honey to generate a Diamond Star
        Amulet.
        *'''Supreme Star Amulet Generator''': Players must discover 40 Gifted Bee types (not gifted
        bees in hive) to use this machine. It costs 10 billion honey to generate a Supreme Star
        Amulet and an option to spend 500 billion honey to guaranteed two Supreme Star Amulet
        [[Passive Abilities]].

        == Trivia ==
        [[File:Antpassinstarhall.png|thumb|220x220px|The ant pass at the back of the Star Hall. Now
        behind the supreme star amulet generator.]]
        * The room gets its name from the [[music]] playing inside it:
        \"[https://www.roblox.com/library/2062483297/starhall starhall]\".
        * The cog outside the Star Hall is a hint to the in-game [[Codes|code]], \"Cog\".
        * This room contains 5 of the 6 Amulet Generators in the game, the last one being the [[Moon
        Amulet Generator]].
        * Before the [[Updates#2019.09.28|9/28/2019 update]], the Royal Jelly Dispenser inside the
        Star Hall gave out 1 Royal Jelly per Gifted Bee type, rather than the number of gifted bees
        you have.
        * Before the [[Updates#2020.04.06|4/6/2020 update]], the [[Ant Pass]] token used to be
        behind the Diamond Star Amulet Generator. This is because of how the Diamond Star Amulet
        Generator sat in the location of the Supreme Star Amulet generator before the
        [[Updates#2020.06.06|6/6/2020 update]].
        * The Supreme Star Amulet Generator is currently the only Amulet Generator in the Star Hall
        that has not been released with the Star Hall itself, as the generator was released in the
        6/6/2020 update.
        * In order to use the Supreme Star Amulet Generator, the player must discover at least 2
        Gifted [[Bees/Mythic|Mythic]] bee types, because there are only 38 non-mythic bee types. (39
        including [[Bear Bee]].)
        * The sound pitch when creating a Star Amulet decreases when going up in tiers.
        * An ant pass can be found at the back of the Star Hall before the
        [https://bee-swarm-simulator.fandom.com/wiki/Updates#2020.06.06 6/06/2020] update
