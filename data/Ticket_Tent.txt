        The '''Ticket Tent''' is a [[:Category:Shops|shop]] located behind the [[Red Cannon]] and
        near the [[hive]]s. It sells six types of [[Bees/Event|event]] [[bees]], [[Egg#Silver
        Egg|silver eggs]], [[Egg#Gold Egg|gold eggs]], [[Egg#Diamond Egg|diamond eggs]],
        [[Egg#Mythic Egg|mythic eggs]], and [[Star Treat|star treats]]. They can only be purchased
        with [[ticket]]s, hence its name. There is also a [[Royal Jelly|royal jelly]] token on top
        of the Ticket Tent.

        == Trivia==
        *The only bee in this shop that could be obtained without tickets (for a limited time) was
        [[Festive Bee]].
        **This used to also apply to [[Gummy Bee]], which is now permanently available for 2500
        [[gumdrops]].
        *The Royal Jelly token on top of the Ticket Tent was mentioned by [[Sun Bear]] in his
        dialogue.
        *Buying a [[Treat#Star Treat|star treat]] from here is one of the only ways to obtain star
        treats without Robux.
        *Before the [[Updates#2018.04.05|4/5/2019 update]], if the player had the [[Badges#Goo
        Badge|Goo Hotshot Badge]], they could go up to the Gummy Bee on top of the tent and use
        gumdrops to enter [[Gummy Bear's Lair]]. However, this mechanic was moved to the [[Gummy Bee
        Egg Claim]] when it was added.
        *The wings on the gifted Photon Bee's model are not transparent, while the giant Festive Bee
        and Puppy Bees' wings are.
        **Additionally, the wings of the Festive Bee model aren't solid, while the wings of the
        Puppy Bee and Gifted Photon Bee models are.
        **The Photon Bee is also the only model that is in [[Gifted Bee|gifted]] form.
        *When the Ticket Tent was implemented, the most expensive item was the star treat, costing
        1,000 tickets. However, after the Beesmas 2019 event ended, the most expensive item was the
        Cub Buddy, costing 2,000 tickets. After the 5/23/2024 update, Mythic Eggs now cost the most,
        at 2,500 tickets.
        *Like the [[Noob Shop]] or [[Pro Shop]], [[Chicks#Spotted Chick|Spotted chicks]] could spawn
        here during the [[Egg Hunt 2020]] event.
        *[[Cub Buddy|Cub Buddies]] used to be sold in this shop, but after the 5/23/2024 update,
        this is no longer the case.
