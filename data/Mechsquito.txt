        A '''Mechsquito''' is one of the five [[Mobs]] that spawns during [[Robo Bear Challenge|Robo
        Bear's Challenge]].

        This is the first Robo Bear Challenge mob that the player will encounter, appearing as early
        as round one. Mechsquitos often spawn a few seconds into the round.

        Mechsquitos typically hover away from the player, shooting at them with small projectiles
        that deal area damage, lowering the further away the player is and inflict the [[Buffs &
        Debuffs#Debuffs|Mechsquito Toxin]] debuff, lowering player movespeed and pollen. Touching a
        Mechsquito also deals more damage than an attack, but it does not apply the [[Buffs &
        Debuffs#Debuffs|Mechsquito Toxin]] debuff.

        A Mechsquito's level can increase depending on what round of Robo Bear's Challenge the
        player is on.

        == Strategies ==
        * Mechsquitos aim for where the player is, not where the player will be. Because of this,
        moving around in circles will often result in Mechsquitos missing their attack.
        * Stand near the Mechsquito, this will allow your bees to lock on to the Mechsquito and
        quickly deal damage.
        * [[Vicious Bee]] and [[Windy Bee]] provide excellent crowd control, especially since
        Mechsquitos often come in pairs or packs. [[Digital Bee]] can also stun many Mechsquitos at
        once and allow them to take extra damage via [[Ability Tokens#Mind Hack|Mind Hack]].

        * Mechsquito is a portmanteau of \"Mechanical\" and \"Mosquito\".
        * Mechsquitos have a party variant of them called a Party Mechsquito.
        ** They also have a larger counterpart of them called a Mega Mechsquito which has its own
        party variant called a [[Party Mega Mechsquito]].
        *Mechsquitos are the only mob that is able to appear in every single round of Robo Bear's
        Challenge.
        **It's also the mob with the most levels in Robo Bear's Challenge.
