        * [[Obstacle Courses#Bamboo Field Obby|Bamboo Field Obby]].}}The '''Basic Bee Gate''', also
        called the '''5 Bee Gate''', is the first [[:Category:Gates|gate]] a player is meant to
        unlock. The gate requires 5 [[bees]] or more to pass through.

        It gets its name from the large [[Basic Bee]] that's on top of it.

        The audio known as \"[[Music|bpatrol]]\" plays in the daytime while you are past this gate.


        == Trivia ==

        * This is the only gate to have more than one gate directly leading from it, with those
        being the [[Brave Bee Gate]] and the [[Honey Bee Gate]].
        * This gate requires the least amount of bees to pass through.
        * This and the [[Ant Gate]] are the only gates that lead to a zone where there is nothing
        that can be purchased with [[Honey]].
        * [[Bubble Bee Man]] used to be behind the [[Global Top Battlers]] leaderboard. However, he
        was removed after Beesmas 2018.
        * Before the [[Updates#2020.06.06|6/6/2020 update]], [[Honey Bee (NPC)|Honey Bee]] was
        located next to the ramp.
        * Underneath the ramp, on the side facing the [[Blue HQ]], there is a [[ticket]] token,
        previously a [[Plastic Egg]].
        * Due to a glitch, the [[Easter Eggs#Onett NPC|Onett statue]], located near the hives could
        have been seen near this gate. This glitch is now patched.
        * This gate is the only gate that cannot have a [[Wild Windy Bee]] spawn in any of its
        fields (without the assistance of [[Onett (Developer)|Onett]], during Beesmas or flying
        there from another field) (not counting the [[Ant Gate]] as it has only a challenge field or
        [[Bear Gate]] as the Bear Gate does not have any fields).
        * During Beesmas 2021, the Basic Bee model is seen wearing a festive Santa hat.
        ** This also makes the Basic Bee Gate the only gate that changes the bee's appearance during
        an event.
