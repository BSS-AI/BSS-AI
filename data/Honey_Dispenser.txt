{"text": "[[File:Dispenser.png|thumb|The Honey Dispenser.]] The '''Honey Dispenser''' is a
        [[dispenser]] that grants [[honey]] and x5 [[Ability Tokens#Haste|haste]] every time it's
        used. It has a 1 hour cooldown and requires the player to be in the
        [https://www.roblox.com/groups/3982592/Bee-Swarm-Simulator-Club#!/about Bee Swarm Simulator
        Club] in order to be used by the player. It initially gave 5x haste and 500 honey, but after
        the [[Updates#2018.04.27|4/27/2018 update]], the amount of honey was changed to 100 per
        [[bee]] in the player's [[hive]] and x5 haste. Since then, the amount of honey was changed
        again.

        It is one of the four club dispensers in the game. However, it is the first one to be
        accessed.

        The [[Obstacle Courses#Lava Obby|Lava Obby]] can be found behind it and leads to a [[Royal
        Jelly|royal jelly]] token at the back of the [[Noob Shop]] and also to the [[Demon Mask]]
        shop.

        If the player tries to use the Honey Dispenser without being in the group, they will get a
        prompt saying that they must join the Bee Swarm Group (or club).

        == Trivia ==
        * During the [[:Category:Gummy Invasion|Gummy Invasion]], the dispenser granted an
        additional [[gumdrops|gumdrop]] per use.
        * This, the [[Strawberry Dispenser]], the [[Blueberry Dispenser]], the [[Royal Jelly
        Dispenser]], and the [[Glue Dispenser]] are the only dispensers that are required to be used
        for a [[Quests|quest]] (\"Honey Bee's Honey Wreath?\" from [[Honey Bee (NPC)|Honey Bee]]
        requires the player to use the Honey Dispenser, \"BBM's Naughty List\" from [[Bubble Bee Man]]
        requires the player to use the Royal Jelly Ticket Dispenser, and \"The Power of Information\"
        from [[Science Bear]] requires the player to use the other three).
        * This is one of four dispensers requiring the player to be part of the Bee Swarm Simulator
        Club with the others being the Treat Dispenser, the Strawberry Dispenser, and the Blueberry
        Dispenser.
        * When using this dispenser, there is no notification in the chat log of the honey given.
