        The '''Moon Amulet''' is an [[amulet]] created by spending 100 [[Moon Charm|Moon Charms]] at
        the [[Moon Amulet Generator]]. This amulet gives a few [[Buffs & Debuffs|buffs]] and
        allows the player to walk on the moon-shaped platforms (located at the back of [[Dapper
        Bear’s Shop]], in the [[Obstacle Courses#Diamond Room|Diamond Room]], and at the [[Bear
        Gate|30 Bee Zone Obby]]) at [[Day/Night Cycle|night]].

        Each time the player purchases the amulet, the buffs given tend to improve. The player will
        always receive a [[capacity]] expansion, which starts at +25,000 capacity and progressively
        increases by 25,000. The buff is capped off at 250,000 extra capacity.

        When using the generator, a message will appear in the chat, saying, \"🌙 The moon seems to
        grow brighter... 🌙.\" If the player has maxed out their capacity buff, the message will
        instead say, \"🌙 The moon seems happy! 🌙.\"

        == Trivia ==
        [[File:Moon amulet glitch.png|thumb|230px|The Moon Amulet glitch.]]
        * There's a [[Glitches|glitch]] that when the player upgrades the Moon Amulet for the 7th
        time, instead of getting +175K capacity, they will get +150K capacity again. However, on the
        8th upgrade, it'll go back up to +175K and it continues from there until reaching the max of
        +250K capacity.
        * This and the [[King Beetle Amulet]] are the only amulets that don't have different tiers.
        * Despite the capacity capping at +250K, the quality of the amulet continues to rise for
        another 10 Moon Amulets generated, meaning that the player will need to generate 25 or more
        Moon Amulets in order to get the possibility of generating the best amulet.
        * While the current maximum Honey Per Pollen stat is +8%, it was briefly possible to receive
        +9%, although this is not possible anymore.
