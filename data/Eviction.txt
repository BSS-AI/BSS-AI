        }}An '''Eviction''' was an [[:Category:Inventory|inventory item]] that used to be
        purchasable in the [[Mountain Top Shop]] for 50,000 [[honey]]. When used, it removed a
        [[Bees|bee]] from the [[hive]]. It was the cheapest item available at the Mountain Top Shop
        before being removed.


        Evictions were mainly used to remove bees and replace them with [[egg]]s if the player
        couldn't afford a [[Hive Slot|hive slot]], taking note that eggs could not be placed on hive
        slots with a bee in the game version that had evictions.
        [[File:Screenshot 31.png|thumb|210x210px|The eviction machine/dispenser on the Mountain Top
        Shop.]]
        Evicting a bee would completely reset the hive slot, meaning that the bee's [[bond]], level,
        and [[Gifted Bee|gifted]] status, will be reset, so if a new egg was hatched, the new bee
        would have started at level 1. The previous bee will not have an effect on the bee type or
        gifted status of the new bee.

        '''All evictions that anyone might have had will no longer be in their inventory after the
        removal.'''

        == Way(s) To Obtain ==
        There are currently no methods to obtain the eviction, as it has been removed from the game.
        '''The methods below are no longer possible.'''

        '''Please remember that Onett removed evictions in the 9/28/2019 update, meaning they can no
        longer be obtained or used.'''
        * Purchasing an eviction in the Mountain Top Shop for 50,000 honey.
        * Finishing [[Bubble Bee Man|Bubble Bee Man's]] [[Quests|quest]] 'B.B.M. Mission' rewarded
        800 evictions and other items.

        == Removal Reason ==
        As of the [[Updates#2018.09.28|9/28/2019 update]], the player can now use eggs on existing
        bees, removing the need to evict bees, while also allowing a bee to keep its level. Thus,
        [[Onett (Developer)|Onett]] removed the eviction from both the shop and every player's
        inventory.
