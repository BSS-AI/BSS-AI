        A '''Sprout''' is a plant that spawns in [[fields]]. When players collect [[pollen]] or if
        there is a cloud on the field, it grows taller. Once the pollen counter reaches 0, it sends
        a shockwave effect outwards around it and scatters various tokens throughout the field.
        There will be a number representing the amount of pollen needing to be collected until the
        sprout releases tokens. If no interaction with a sprout takes place for more than 5 minutes,
        it will despawn by going underground and then disappear.

        Depending on where the sprout is planted, the drops spawned can vary in amounts or may drop
        certain items.
        *A sprout in a [[:Category:Red|Red]] field yields 4 times more [[Strawberry|Strawberries]]
        than in any other field. No [[Blueberry|Blueberries]] will spawn.
        *A sprout in the [[Strawberry Field]] will yield twice as many strawberries than sprouts in
        other red fields.
        *A sprout in a [[:Category:Blue|Blue]] field yields 6 times more blueberries than in any
        other field. No strawberries will spawn.
        *A sprout in the [[Sunflower Field]] yields 7 times more [[Sunflower Seed|Sunflower Seeds]]
        than in any other field.
        *A sprout in the [[Pineapple Patch]] yields 7 times more [[Pineapple|Pineapples]] than in
        any other field.
        *A sprout in the [[Windy Bee Gate|35 Bee Zone]] yields 20% more rewards than sprouts in
        other areas. It also requires more pollen to pop.
        *A sprout in the [[Coconut Field]] yields [[coconut]]s and [[Tropical Drink|Tropical
        drinks]] in addition to other treats.
        *Other fields may produce more regular [[Treats]] than any other treats depending on the
        field.

        There are ten different variants of sprouts:
        *Sprout (Green)
        *Rare Sprout (Silver) (Rare)
        *Epic Sprout (Gold) (Very Rare)
        *Legendary Sprout (Teal) (Extremely Rare)
        *Supreme Sprout (Luminescent green) (Exceptionally Rare)
        *Moon Sprout (Only available during nighttime, luminescent blue during both night and day)
        *Gummy Sprout (Translucent pink) (Very Rare)
        *Sticker Sprout (Spawns only every 3 hours in the [[Hub Field]], or a very rare chance when
        planting a magic bean in the Hub Field) (Rainbow)
        *Festive Sprout (Planted by [[Festive Bean]]s or [[Onett (Developer)|Onett]] (White and Red
        stripes)
        *Debug Sprout (Can only be planted by [[Onett]]) (Black)
        The amount of pollen needed for the sprout for it to be harvested varies on the rarity of
        the sprout and the field it's in.

        Sprouts can spawn randomly around the map and can also be planted using [[Magic Bean]]s. It
        can also be summoned by using the [[Special Sprout Summoner]] near the [[Red HQ]], by anyone
        who has discovered all eight [[Bees/Legendary|Legendary Bee]] types, once every sixteen
        hours. When a sprout spawns, it will broadcast a server-wide message, \"🌱A (Rarity) Sprout
        has appeared...🌱\". If a player has planted a sprout, the message would read, \"🌱{Username}
        has planted a (Rarity) Sprout...🌱\". If Sticker Sprouts are about to spawn in [[Hive Hub]],
        the message will reads, \"🌱 Sticker Sprouts are about to spawn in the Hive Hub...🌱\" When
        the Sticker Sprout has spawned in the Hive Hub, the message will reads, \"🌱 A Sticker Sprout
        has spawned in the Hive Hub...🌱\". Sprouts cannot spawn or be planted in the [[Ant Field]].
        As a sprout grows, the following audio is played once the sprout has hit the next respective
        stage:
        [[File:Sproutstages.ogg]]

        The higher tier the sprout is, the more tokens spawn when fully harvested. The possible

        ==Tips==
        *Having [[Ability Tokens#Haste|haste]] producing bees can increase the player's [[System
        Page#Movespeed|movespeed]] up to 2x, helping collect tokens they would have missed
        otherwise.
        **If possible, have [[Bear Bee]] since its [[Ability Tokens#Bear Morph|Bear Morph]]
        [[Ability Tokens|ability]] can significantly increase player movespeed as well.
        **Using [[Oil]] to grant 1.2x player movespeed.
        **If the player owns the [[Coconut Clogs]] or the [[Gummy Boots]], they can have a coconut
        fall on them and grant the player a short burst of [[Passive Abilities#Coconut Haste|Coconut
        Haste Surge]].
        **The Honey, Treat, Strawberry, and Blueberry [[Dispensers]] give 5 stacks of [[Ability
        Tokens#Haste|Haste]].
        *[[Tadpole Bee]] and the [[Box-O-Frogs]] summon [[frog]]s that collect sprout tokens.
        *[[Passive Abilities#Star Saw|Star Saw]] can collect massive amounts of sprout tokens.
        *[[Windy Bee|Windy Bee's]] [[Ability Tokens#Tornado|tornado]] can also collect massive
        amounts of sprout tokens.
        *[[Cub Buddy|Cub Buddies]] can also collect sprout tokens.

        ==Trivia==
        *Gummy Sprouts are the only sprouts that are translucent.
        *Most sprout notifications are displayed in gold, but Gummy Sprout notifications are light
        purple; Supreme, Moon, and Debug Sprouts have gray notifications; and Festive Sprouts have
        red ones.
        *Many of the treat drop statistics are mentioned in Spirit Bear's dialogue.
        *Sprouts were originally called \"Seedlings\" when they were released. Its original message
        was \"🌱A Seedling has sprouted...🌱\"
        *The Supreme Sprout previously required half the [[pollen]] that a Legendary Sprout would
        need. It now requires 50% more pollen than a Legendary Sprout.
        *Since the [[Updates#2019.12.23|12/23/2019 update]], Onett has been able to plant sprouts
        server-wide throughout the game. They can be planted under unique names.
        *Supreme Sprouts were formerly named \"Mythical Sprouts\" before the 12/23/2019 update.
        *If the player turned in [[Stick Bug#Egg Hunt 2019 Quest|Stick Bug's Egg Hunt 2019 Quest]],
        Stick Bug spawned an Epic, Legendary, and a Supreme Sprout.
        *If a Festive Bean is planted by a player, the Festive Blessing and Beesmas Cheer tokens are
        only obtainable by the player that planted it.
        **If the sprout is planted by Onett, anyone can get the tokens.
        *The \"[[Special Sprout Summoner]]\" was formerly called \"Sprout Summoner\", and it would plant
        any type of sprout randomly except for Debug and Festive Sprouts.
        *The Debug Sprout is the only sprout that does not drop honey tokens.
        *If a sprout spawns before a player joins the server, the sprout will be invisible to that
        player, however it can still be popped by the player.
        *On mobile, there is a glitch where the sprouts in the server are invisible and can't be
        popped by the player.
        *Sticker Sprouts are the only sprouts that grow on their own (without clouds). It loses
        about 1M pollen per second, even if no one is collecting in the Hub Field.
        *If the player who spawned the sprout leaves before the sprout has popped the sprout will
        not spawn anything.
