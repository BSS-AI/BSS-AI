        The '''Public Sticker Board''' is located in the [[Hive Hub]], and allows players to add or
        take [[sticker]]s from it. There is a 22 hour cooldown for taking a sticker from the board
        and a 10 minute cooldown for adding a sticker to the board. The [[Sticker-Seeker Quest
        Machine]] might occasionally require players to donate a sticker to the Public Sticker Board
        as well. This is a great way of getting stickers or donating if you have too much (trading
        away stickers can be too hard if you have no one), as most games have a donation board.
