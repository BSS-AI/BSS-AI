        '''Icicles''' are a [[Bond|level]] 10 [[:Category:Beesmas|Beesmas]] [[beequip]]. They have
        an equip limit of three. They have a blue color requirement. They can only be equipped to
        bees that know the [[Ability Tokens#Bomb|Blue Bomb]] ability.
        == Possible Stats ==
        * [[System Page#Attack Multiplier|Attack]] (Guaranteed) - +3% to +5%. (up to +45% from
        [[Waxes|waxing]])
        * [[System Page#Blue Bomb Pollen|Blue Bomb Pollen]] (Guaranteed) - +20% to +30%. (up to
        +110% from waxing)
        * [Hive Bonus] [[Cactus Field]] [[Pollen]] (Guaranteed) - +5% to +10%. (up to +20% from
        waxing)
        * [Hive Bonus] Blue Bomb Pollen (Guaranteed) - +3% to +5%. (up to +15% from waxing)
        * [Hive Bonus] [[Mobs|Monster]] Respawn Time (from waxing, rare) - -1% to -5%.

        == Ways to Obtain ==
        ''There are currently no methods to obtain the Icicles.''

        === These methods are no longer possible. ===
        *Buying the Beequip Bundle in [[Bee Bear’s Catalog]].
        *As a drop from a level 15 [[Snowbear]] during Beesmas 2021.
        *As a reward for completing [[Gummy Bear]]'s Beesmas 2021 quest.
        * Purchasing the Jolly Jumbo Pack in the [[Robux Shop]].
        *As a drop from a level 15 [[Snowbear]] (Beesmas 2022).
        *As a drop from [[Rogue Vicious Bee]] during [[Beesmas]]. The higher level the Rogue Vicious
        Bee is, the higher chance it has of dropping Icicles.
