{"text": "The '''Cog Amulet''' is an amulet that can be obtained from the [[Robo Bear Challenge]].

        In order to obtain these amulets, the player will need to beat certain rounds during the
        Robo Bear Challenge. The more rounds the player beats, the higher the tier of the Cog
        Amulet. The average stats of the amulet also scale higher the more cogs you collect in a
        round.

        == Requirement(s) ==
        The requirements are based on what round was completed during a challenge.

        == Trivia ==

        * The Cog Amulet is the only amulet that unlocks items in a [[:Category:Shops|shop]].
        * The Cog Amulet is the only amulet to boost [[Nectar]].
        ** It is also the only amulet to boost Event Bee Ability Pollen, Mark Ability Pollen,
        [[Flame#Flame Pollen|Flame Pollen]], and [[Bubble#Bubble Pollen|Bubble Pollen]].
