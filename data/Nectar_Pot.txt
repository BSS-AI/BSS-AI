        The '''Nectar Pot''' is a pot in the [[Bear Gate|30 Bee Zone]] that can store any type of
        [[Nectar]] buff that the player can take out to use later, for the cost of 5 [[ticket]]s.
        [[File:Nectar jar menu.png|thumb|The menu that appears when choosing which type of nectar to
        deposit.]]
        There are 5 types of nectar the player can store. These types are Satisfying Nectar,
        Motivating Nectar, Refreshing Nectar, Comforting Nectar, and Invigorating Nectar. The player
        can only store one type of nectar at once, but they can leave it in the pot for as long as
        they wish. To store another type of nectar, the player must claim the stored nectar, and
        then pay an additional 5 tickets to put in the new nectar.
        [[File:Nectar pot partly filled.png|thumb|The Nectar Pot partly filled with Comforting
        Nectar.]]
        When storing nectar, the nectar in the pot's color will change into the color of the nectar
        stored in, and the icon in front of it will change to the nectar's icon. Nectar will be
        visually filled in the pot, and the amount in it increases with the amount nectar stored in
        it. When the player claims the nectar, therefore emptying the pot, it will drain and only a
        little nectar will visually remain, and the icon and color will not change.

        == Tips ==

        * The nectar pot is very useful for holding nectar to prepare for a boost. Store the
        selected nectar beforehand and take the nectar out before boosting.
        * It is best to use this when storing a large quantity of nectar, as storing less and taking
        it out to store more results in higher ticket loss.
        * Store Invigorating and Refreshing Nectar before boosting if you have mainly red bees, and
        Motivating and Comforting Nectar before boosting if you have mainly blue bees. If you have
        mostly colorless bees, you are best off storing Satisfying Nectar. If you have a variety of
        colors of bees, Satisfying Nectar is usually the best to store, however it can depend on the
        field you're in.
        * If you have high nectar and are about to log off, store the nectar in the pot for use next
        time.
        * You can make your bees harvest a planter for a few hours to have the maximum nectar, which
        you can then store in the pot for later usage.
        [[File:Full Nectar Pot.png|thumb|The Nectar Pot fully filled with Comforting Nectar.]]

        == Trivia ==
        *The Nectar Pot is located where the [[Boost Market]] used to be.
        *If the player leaves the game and rejoins while there is no nectar in the Nectar Pot, the
        color and icon changes to the last nectar the player stored. If the player has never stored
        nectar, it will become a random one instead.
