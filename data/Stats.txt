        '''Stats''' (short for statistics) refer to the aspects of each [[bee]] that are the basis
        for how that bee performs. Certain stats increase with higher [[bond|levels]].

        == Rarity ==
        '''Rarity''' refers to how often bees of each category can be expected to be generated from
        [[eggs]] or [[Royal Jelly|royal jelly]]. Bees of greater rarity tend to be of higher stats
        and possessing their own unique ability, but rarity itself does not have any bearing on
        performance. An [[Bees/Event|Event]] tier of bees are aquired through their unique
        obtainment method.

        == Color ==
        '''Color''' affects a bee's ability to gather [[pollen]] from [[flowers]], improving their
        Gather Amount by 50% when collecting from flowers of a matching color.
        [[:Category:Blue|Blue]] and [[:Category:Red|Red]] are the only colors of bees that have this
        bonus. [[:Category:Colorless|Colorless]] bees do not experience any boost to Gather Amount,
        even from white flowers.

        == Bond ==
        '''[[Bond]]''' is a stat to determine if a bee has enough \"experience\" to level up. The
        following methods can affect experience:

        * Treats
        * Collecting Pollen
        * Bond from battle
        * Puppy Love

        == Energy ==
        '''[[Energy]]''' represents the number of times a bee will gather pollen from a flower and
        attack [[mobs]] before returning to the [[hive]] to rest (30 seconds to fully recharge upon
        entering the hive)

        * Spawning [[Ability Tokens]] does not cost a bee any energy
        * The energy of all bees in a swarm can be increased with stacks of ''[[Buffs &
        Debuffs#From NPCs|Polar Power]]'' from [[Polar Bear]]'s quests, or from an energy
        [[mutation]]
        * Energy is increased by 5% for each level above 1
        * Reseting yourself will cause bees to go back to the hive but not recharge their energy
        * An ''energy reset'' can be achieved by reseting again when bees are inside of the hive
        after a prior reset

        The formula for energy is: ''Base Energy * (1 + .05 * (Level - 1)) * Polar Power + Energy
        Mutation''

        == Speed ==
        '''Speed''', short for '''Bee Movespeed''', is how fast a bee moves and performs actions
        (contrary to its name). Speed indrectly affects all basic processes of a bee such as:

        * Gathering
        * Attacking
        * Sipping nectar in a planter
        * Converting at hive.

        Bees move at approximately 1.5 times their move speed when not close to their owner and 0.5
        of their move speed when returning to the hive to rest. The speed of all bees in a swarm can
        be increased by [[Gifted Bee|Gifted]] [[Ninja Bee|Ninja Bee's]] hive bonus, [[Ability
        Tokens#Beesmas Cheer|Beesmas Cheer]], [[Badges#Playtime Badge|Playtime Badge]], [[oil]],
        [[Super Smoothie|super smoothies]], and activating [[Passive Abilities#Coconut Haste|Coconut
        Haste]].

        The formula for Bee Movespeed is: ''Base Bee Movespeed * (1 + .03 * (Level - 1)) * (1 +
        Ninja Bee Hive Bonus + Playtime Badge Bonus)''

        == Attack ==
        '''Attack''' is how much damage the bee will do when fighting mobs. Attack remains constant
        at all levels, but can be boosted through various [[:Category:Accessories|accessories]],
        [[items]], [[amulet]]s, certain [[Gifted Bee#List of Hive Bonuses|gifted hive bonuses]] and
        ability tokens. Gifted bees have 50% more attack.

        The formula for a bee's Attack is: (Base Attack Damage + Attack Buffs) * Attack multiplier
        (* 1.5 If the bee is gifted)

        == Gather Amount ==
        '''Gather Amount''' describes how much pollen a bee will collect from a flower and how
        quickly it does so. The amount collected increases by 10% for each level above 1. Gather
        amount may be increased with; amulets, certain Gifted Bee bonuses, [[Robux
        Shop|Gamepasses,]] or items.

        The formula for Gather Amount is: ''Base Gather Amount * (1 + .10 * (Level - 1)) * Gifted
        Multiplier''

        == Production Amount ==
        '''Production Amount''' describes how much pollen a bee will convert into [[honey]] at the
        hive and how quickly it does so. The amount converted increases by 10% for each level above
        1.

        The formula for Production Amount is: ''(Base Production Amount + Production Amount) * (1 +
        .10 * (Level - 1)) * Conversion Rate * Gifted Multiplier''
        == Mutations ==
        '''[[Mutation]]s''' are a bonus granted through feeding bitterberries to a bee when they are
        under the '''radioactive''' effect. Mutations can grant bonus:

        * Attack
        * Convert Amount
        * Gather Amount
        * Energy
        * Bee Ability Rate

        == Gifted ==
        '''[[Gifted Bee|Gifted]]''' is a bonus granted through feeding a bee's favourite treat, star
        treat, gingerbread bears, aged gingerbread bears. This stat grants:

        * Multiplier to gather amount, convert amount, and bee attack all together by 1.5x
        * Ability to spawn - [<nowiki/>[[Ability Tokens|Inspire]]] tokens
        * A gifted hive bonus (only once for each bee)
        * A gifted ability (if the bee has one)
        * A new appearance
