        The '''Gummy Beacon''' is a [[:Category:Beesmas|Beesmas]] 2020, Beesmas 2021, Beesmas 2022
        Part 2 machine that could have been unlocked after completing [[Gummy Bear|Gummy Bear's]]
        Beesmas [[Quests|quest]]. It was located on top of the [[Noob Shop]], on the corner closest
        to the [[Royal Jelly Dispenser]] next to the [[Clover Field]], in front of the [[Star
        Hall]]. If the player attempted to activate it without completing Gummy Bear's Beesmas
        quest, it would read: ''\"The satellite dish isn't working, but it still emits bad
        vibes...\"''

        The Gummy Beacon would start a [[Gummy Siege]] and summons 3 temporary [[Gummy Bee]]s of a
        random level between 8-20 that last for 5 minutes. It had a cooldown of 8 hours.

        == Trivia ==
        * This is the second and currently the only way of making Gummy Bear appear in the mountain,
        the first being the [[Gummy Cannon]].
        ** This is also the only way that the Gummy Cannon can appear.
        * This is one of the 4 machines that summons temporary bees, the others being the [[Honeyday
        Candles]], [[Onett's Lid Art]], and Honey Bee's [[Honey Wreath]].
        * This is the only way to have more than 1 Gummy Bee, though it is only for a limited amount
        of time.
        * The Gummy Bees given by the Gummy Beacon can help player activate the [[Passive
        Abilities#Gummy Morph|Gummy Morph]] very quickly by spawning tokens that contribute towards
        the passive.
        * This and Onett's Lid Art are the only ways to have multiple [[Bees/Event|Event Bees]].
        However, they only last for a limited amount of time.
        * This, Onett's Lid Art and the [[Naughty List]] are Beesmas machines that have the longest
        cooldowns in the game, at 8 hours.
