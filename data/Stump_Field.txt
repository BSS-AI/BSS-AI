        This field can spawn [[honey]], red [[Ability Tokens#Boost|boost]], blue boost,
        [[Treat#Treat|treat]], [[gumdrops]], [[glue]], [[Magic Bean|magic beans]], [[ticket]]s and
        [[Blue Extract|blue extract]] tokens when collecting [[pollen]].

        [[Bees]] that like the Stump Field are [[Bumble Bee]], [[Exhausted Bee]], [[Tadpole Bee]],
        and [[Gummy Bee]]. Bees that dislike the Stump Field are [[Hasty Bee]], [[Baby Bee]], and
        [[Spicy Bee]].

        This field gives Motivating [[Nectar]] when a planter is placed here.

        == Obtaining Boosts ==
        * Using the [[Blue Field Booster]] may give a Stump [[Field Boost]] (only if the Stump Snail
        is on respawn cooldown). When it does, it will give +100% Stump Field pollen for 15 minutes.
        * Using [[glitter]] will add 100% Stump Field pollen onto the current boost for 15 minutes
        if it isn't already maxed out.
        [[File:Fis stump.png|thumb|75px|The Stump Field Boost icon.]]
        *Using [[Field Dice|field dice]], [[Smooth Dice|smooth dice]], or [[Loaded Dice|loaded
        dice]] may add +100% - +300% Stump Field pollen for 15 minutes if the current boost isn't
        already maxed out and is based on the type of dice used.
        *Stump [[Field Wind]]s can be obtained through the [[Wind Shrine]] or [[codes]]. They have a
        base of +20% instant Stump Field conversion, and give +5% instant Stump Field conversion and
        +10% Stump Field pollen for each stack for 30 minutes. Stump Field Winds can stack up to a
        total of 15 times.
        *Using [[Onett's Lid Art]] can summon a guiding star to this field.
        *Redeeming certain valid codes:
        **500mil (Gives Stump Field Boost x2 + other stuff).
        **10MilMembers (Gives Stump Field Code Buff + other stuff).
        *It can be boosted by the [[Shell Amulet]], which boosts the field by +10% to +65% depending
        on the amulet's rarity. All Shell Amulets are guaranteed to give this buff.
        *Activating the [[Passive Abilities#Guiding Star|Guiding Star]] passive can summon a guiding
        star to this field if it is one of the player's five least collected fields.

        ===The methods below are no longer possible. ===

        *Giving a [[present]] to [[Onett]] gave Stump Field Boost x1 and other boosts and [[items]].
        *Purchasing a Stump Field [[Buffs & Debuffs#From Areas|Market Boost]] from the [[Boost
        Market]] will give x1.5 Stump [[Field Capacity]], x1.25 Stump Field Pollen, x1.1 [[System
        Page#Goo|Goo]], and x1.25 [[System Page#Hive Convert Rate|Convert Rate At Hive]] for 30
        minutes.
        *Redeeming certain expired codes:
        **MoreTime (Gave Stump Field Boost x1 + other stuff).
        ** 2MFavorites (Gave Stump Field Boost x2 + other stuff).
        **Reboooot (Gave Stump Field Boost x1 + other stuff).
        ** Willgoold (Gave Stump Field Boost x1 + other stuff).
        **GravyGoodies (Gave Stump Field Boost x1 + other stuff).
        **RobziRampage (Gave Stump Field Boost x1 + other stuff).
        **FrozenBugReboot (Gives Stump Field Code Buff and Stump Field Boost x4 + other stuff).

        == Trivia ==
        * During the [[Stick Bug Challenge]], if [[Stick Bug]] is at the Pineapple Patch, [[Mountain
        Top Field]] or [[Bamboo Field]], it will be able to create a [[Stick Bug Challenge#Defense
        Totem|Defense Totem]] in this field.
        * Before the [[Updates#2019.09.28|9/28/2019 update]], the Stump Field was originally a more
        balanced colored field. It was updated to act as a blue-oriented counterpart to the [[Pepper
        Patch]].
        * This is the first field the player will encounter that only has triple flowers naturally,
        the second one being the [[Mountain Top Field]]. Third being the [[Coconut Field]] and
        Fourth being [[Pepper Patch]].
        * This is the only field where gumdrops can be obtained from [[leaves]]
        *This and the [[Hub Field]] are the only non-quadrilateral fields in the game.
        ** Before the 1/12/2024 Update, the Stump Field was the only non-quadrilateral field in the
        game.
        * This is the only blue field where [[Blueberry|blueberries]] cannot spawn naturally.
        ** This field is also the only blue field that has red flowers
        * This, the Ant Field and the [[Hub Field]] are the only fields where [[Wild Windy Bee]]
        cannot spawn in nor fly to.
        ** However, [[Onett (Developer)|Onett]] can summon Wild Windy Bees in this field. If they
        are defeated, they will fly in place until their timer is up as there is no flight pattern
        programmed for Stump Field Windy Bees.
        * This, the [[Coconut Field]], the [[Pepper Patch]], the [[Ant Challenge|Ant Field]] and the
        [[Hub Field]] are the only fields that were added after the game's first release.
        * The Stump Field, the [[Coconut Field]], and the [[Spider Field|Spider Field's]] caves have
        the same appearance.
        ** No special rewards can be found in the Stump Snail's Cave, unlike the [[Werewolf's Cave]]
        and the [[Coconut Cave]], making it closer to the Spider Field's cave.
        * There is a [[glue]] token between the large pineapple and the base of the stump.
        * According to [[Black Bear]], there used to be a gigantic tree that blocked the sunlight
        and didn't let the plants and flowers grow. Then one day, a huge bear came and cut the tree
        down. A field then grew on the stump of the tree, which many assumed is what the Stump Field
        is. It is unknown which bear cut the tree down in the first place.
        * This field is one of three fields with no decorations in it, indicating what field it is.
        The other two fields to do this are the [[Ant Field]], the [[Mountain Top Field]] and the
        [[Hub Field]].
        ** This field gets its name from the stump it sits on top of instead.
        * During Beesmas 2019 and 2020, there was an [[Obstacle Courses#Mythic Present Obby|obby]]
        in a hole to the left of the Stump Field. Completing the obby would lead to the [[Gift
        Boxes|Purple Gift Box]].
        ** This has been replaced by [[Dapper Bear's Shop]].
        * This is the only field that can be completely covered by a sprinkler, that being the [[The
        Supreme Saturator|Supreme Saturator]].
        * This [[Field]] is currently the third smallest field in the game, only being behind the
        [[Pumpkin Patch]] and the [[Ant Field]].
        * There is a glitch where you can place a planter on the outer rim of the field outside of
        any flowers.
        ** Harvesting a planter utilizing this glitch will have the loot tokens spawn on the closest
        flowers in the field.
