        '''Bees''' are one of the main features of Bee Swarm Simulator. They follow the player
        around, collect [[Pollen]] from [[Fields]], and defend their beekeeper from [[Mobs]] and
        bosses. Bees are one of the primary sources for collecting pollen, as they do it
        automatically, and most bees produce [[Ability Tokens]]. Upon returning to the [[Hive]],
        they will convert pollen into [[Honey]], the main [[:Category:Currency|Currency]] used in
        [[:Category:Shops|Shops]] to buy [[Items]]. The hive is also where bees sleep when they run
        out of [[Energy]], or where they rest if their beekeeper dies or resets, doing so for about
        15 seconds before returning to work. When a bee runs out of energy, a message in the
        player’s notifications or chat will pop out: ”😴[Bee’s name] is out of energy! It’s going to
        sleep.”

        As bees gather pollen, fight Mobs, and consume [[Treats]] and other items, the Bees'
        [[Bond]] with their Beekeeper grows, increasing their level after reaching a certain
        threshold. Higher-level bees are capable of collecting and converting more pollen into honey
        and having more energy to work longer (except for [[Photon Bee]] and [[Exhausted Bee]], who
        both have unlimited energy), and being able to attack higher-level mobs.

        Bees are hatched from [[Egg]]s, with the probability of hatching a bee of a particular
        rarity or type depending on the rarity of the egg (i.e., a Gold Egg can hatch into an
        [[/Epic|Epic]], [[/Legendary|Legendary]], or [[/Mythic|Mythic]]). The player can change the
        type of an already-hatched bee by applying a [[Royal Jelly]] or another egg. Once the 25
        default [[Hive Slot]]s are filled up with bees, the player can expand their hive by buying
        extra hive slots from the [[Mountain Top Shop]]. While hive slots increase in price
        exponentially, this allows the player to have 25 different hive slots for a maximum of 50
        bees. However, it is now possible to exceed the limit of 50 bees using mechanics that allow
        the player to have temporary bees, such as [[Onett's Lid Art]], [[Honeyday Candles]], and
        [[Gummy Siege]]. It is also possible through [[Spicy Bee|Spicy Bee's]] [[Ability
        Tokens#Inferno|Inferno]].

        The eight types of eggs are basic eggs, silver eggs, gold eggs, diamond eggs, star eggs,
        mythic eggs, specific bee eggs, and event bee [[egg]]s, along with gifted variants of
        silver/gold/diamond/mythic eggs.

        There are currently 46 bee types that can be discovered and collected, but only 45 can be
        obtained without spending robux, as [[Bear Bee]] requires robux to be purchased.

        A [[Gifted Bee|gifted bee]] is a type of bee that has improved stats and gives the entire
        hive or the player a bonus which varies from bee to bee and cannot stack. Some [[Gifted
        Bee|gifted bees]] have their gifted abilities, and most Gifted Event bees have enhanced
        abilities, such as Photon Bee's [[Ability Tokens#Beamstorm|beamstorm]]. There is a 1/8000
        (0.0125%) chance that feeding a [[/Rare|Rare bee]] its favorite treat will make it gifted, a
        1/10000 (0.01%) chance for [[/Epic|Epic bees]], a 1/12000 (0.0083%) chance for both
        [[/Legendary|Legendary bees]] and [[/Common|Common bees]], and a 1/25000 (0.004%) chance for
        [[/Mythic|Mythic bees]]. In addition, there is a 1/250 (0.4%) chance of getting a gifted bee
        by using royal jelly or by hatching it from a [[#Basic Egg|basic egg]]. There is also a
        guaranteed chance of getting a gifted bee from a [[Royal Jelly#Star Jelly|star jelly]],
        feeding them a [[Star Treat|star treat]], or hatching one from a star egg. There is also a
        1% chance of getting a gifted bee from [[Gingerbread Bear]]s, which is available in the
        [[Beesmas]] 2020+ event for a limited time. and a 1.1% chance from [[Aged Gingerbread
        Bear]]s is also available when they have not used the gingerbread bears in previous Beesmas
        events.

        ==Trivia==
        *The [[/Mythic|Mythic]] bee type is the first and only bee rarity type to be added in an
        [[Updates#2019.12.23|update]].
        *[[Buoyant Bee]] is the only bee to '''NOT''' dislike a field.
        *If the player's internet is lagging (slow internet), the bees have a high chance of being
        slower at fighting mobs, making honey, and collecting pollen.
        *If the player moves off the pad in front of their hive while converting honey, the bees
        will stop converting. However, they will not if the player only barely leaves the hive.
        *Moving closer to the hive will cause bees to convert [[honey]] faster as they will not need
        to fly as far to get to the player and back.
        * If the player gets too far from their bees, they will fly to the player, ignoring every
        [[Flowers|flower]] and [[Mobs|mob]] in the way.
        *When bees are sleeping in their [[Hive Slot|hive slot]], they emit faint \"Z\" particles for
        a little while until they wake.
        *If the player jumps with over 20 [[System Page#Jump Power|jump power]] while their bees are
        making honey, they will stop converting.
        *The higher the player's bees' level, the more effective they are at attacking, converting,
        and collecting.
        *The average attack of a bee is approximately 3 (3.011628...).
        *All event bees have only one disliked field (except for [[Windy Bee]], which dislikes two).
        *A bee spins and emits white sparkles before a bee produces a token. However, if a bee gives
        off green sparkles, that means the bee is about to collect critical pollen or do critical
        damage.
        *The [[Marshmallow Bee]] is the only \"item bee.\"
        *All bees of the [[/Legendary|legendary]] rarity or higher have a “special” physical feature
        (e.g., [[Lion Bee|Lion Bee’s]] mane, [[Festive Bee|Festive Bee’s]] “lid,” [[Tadpole
        Bee|Tadpole Bee’s]] size and trail, [[Gummy Bee|Gummy Bee's]] translucent body, etc.). Some
        Epic bees also have at least one feature (e.g., Commander Bee's helmet).
        *Legendary and Mythic are the only rarities of bee that can be used to activate the special
        pads found around the map; Legendary for the [[Honeystorm]] and [[Special Sprout Summoner]],
        and Mythic for the [[Mythic Meteor Shower|Mythic Meteor Shower Summoner]].
        *Not counting Common, the Mythic rarity is the rarity that contains the least amount of
        bees, with only 6.
        **The Epic bees rarity is tied with the Event bees rarity for having the most bees, being
        11.
        *[[Exhausted Bee]] and [[Photon Bee]] still return to the hive and rest when a player
        dies/resets, despite their unlimited energy.
        *The maximum number of bees the player can have is 150. This can be achieved if the player
        has 50 Spicy Bees that all simultaneously produced inferno and 2 Fire Bees. This number can
        be further increased to 250 if the player is in a corrupted field while 50 Spicy Bees that
        all simultaneously produced inferno and each token was duplicated. During Beesmas 2020 and
        Beesmas 2021, the maximum amount of bees the player can have at one time is 266. From the
        previous total of 250, 3 Gummy Bees can be summoned from the [[Gummy Siege]], three bees
        from [[Onett's Lid Art]] (including a level 20 Bumble Bee and a level 1 Baby Bee), a level
        20 Honey Bee from [[Honey Wreath|Honey Bee's Honey Wreath]], and a maximum of 9 bees from
        the [[Honeyday Candles]].
        *All gifted bees have a star on the model.
        *Mythic bees are the only rarity not to have a gate representing them.
        *The chance of getting a mythic bee from a basic egg is estimated to be 1 x 10^-32% (1 in
        100 Nonillion).
        *[[Lion Bee]] is the only bee to like the [[Ant Field]].
        *In order, the color of the hive slots according to rarity are:
        **Bronze (Common).
        **White (Rare).
        **Yellow (Epic).
        **Light Blue (Legendary).
        **Purple (Mythic).
        **Green (Event).
        *The color of a bee's face in their hive slot is dependent on their color with red bees
