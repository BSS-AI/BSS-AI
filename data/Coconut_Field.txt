        The field can spawn [[honey]], [[Treat|treats]], [[coconut]]s, [[Tropical Drink|tropical
        drinks]], [[ticket]] and [[Magic Bean|magic bean]] tokens when collecting [[pollen]].
        [[Bees]] that like the Coconut Field are [[Vector Bee]], [[Buoyant Bee]], [[Digital Bee]],
        and [[Windy Bee]]. There are no bees that dislike the Coconut Field.

        This field gives Refreshing [[Nectar]] when a planter is placed here.

        A level 1-20 [[Wild Windy Bee]] can spawn in this field.

        == Obtaining Boosts ==
        * Using [[glitter]] will add 100% Coconut Field [[pollen]] onto the current boost for 15
        minutes if it isn't already maxed out. [[File:CoconutFieldBoost.png|thumb|75px|The Coconut
        Field boost icon.]]
        * Using [[Field Dice|field dice]], [[Smooth Dice|smooth dice]], or [[Loaded Dice|loaded
        dice]] may add +100% - 300% Coconut Field pollen for 15 minutes based on the type used if it
        isn't already maxed out.
        * Coconut [[Field Wind]]s can be obtained through the [[Wind Shrine]] or [[codes]].
        They have a base of +20% Instant Coconut Field Conversion, and give +5% Instant Coconut
        Field Conversion and +10% Coconut Field pollen for each stack. Coconut Field Winds can stack
        up to a total of 15 times.
        *Redeeming certain valid codes:
        **DarzethDoodads (Coconut Field Code Buff + other stuff).
        **FrozenBugReboot (Coconut Field Code Buff and Coconut Field Boost x4 + other stuff INVALID
        NOW)
        * Using the [[Coconut Dispenser]] will always give Coconut [[Field Boost]] x1 (if the player
        has [[Badges#Coconut Badges|Coconut Badge]] cadet or higher), along with coconuts depending
        on the player's badge.

        === The methods below are no longer possible. ===
        * Giving [[Gummy Bear]] a [[present]] gave Coconut Field Boost x3 + other stuff.
        * Redeeming certain expired codes:
        ** Gumaden10T (Gave Coconut Field Boost x1 and Coconut Field Winds x3 + other stuff).
        ** Purchasing a Coconut Field [[Buffs & Debuffs#From Areas|Market Boost]] from the
        [[Boost Market]] will give x1.5 Coconut [[Field Capacity]], x1.25 Coconut Field Pollen, x2.5
        Pollen From Coconuts, and x1.1 [[System Page#White Pollen|White Pollen]].
        ** Mocito100T (Gives Coconut Field Boost x2 and Coconut Field Capacity x2 + other stuff).

        == Trivia ==
        * This is the only field where [[sprout]]s and [[leaves]] can yield coconuts and tropical
        drinks.
        * It is also the only field to have a regular [[:Category:Bosses|boss]] defending it.
        * This is one of four fields to solely have contain triple flowers without pollination, the
        others being the [[Mountain Top Field]], [[Stump Field]], and the [[Pepper Patch]].
        ** Furthermore, this field is the only triple-flower field dominated by white flowers.
        * Under the coconut tree opposite of the cave and closest to [[Spirit Bear]], there is a
        coconut token that can be collected to give 10 coconuts.
        * This and the Pepper Patch are the only fields where sprouts cannot naturally appear or be
        planted with the [[Special Sprout Summoner]].
        * Like the [[Badges#Pepper Badge|Pepper Badge]], the [[Badges#Coconut Badge|Coconut Badge]]
        requires more pollen collected than the other field badges. For example, the cadet badge for
        the Coconut Field requires 50,000,000 (50 million) pollen, compared to the normal
        requirement of 250,000 (250 thousand) pollen.
        * It is possible to gather pollen from this field without 35 [[bees]] from the ledge facing
        the [[Ticket Tent]].
        ** Players can also collect from the field by going outside the map and finding the back of
        the Ticket Tent. Jumping on the Ticket Tent with enough jump height will reach the field,
        allowing bees to farm.
        * The Coconut Dispenser used to boost this field differently based on the player's Coconut
        Badge, with the Junior badge giving no boost, Cadet giving a +100% pollen boost, Hotshot
        giving a +200% pollen boost, and anything Ace and above giving a +300% pollen boost. After
        the [[Updates#2019.12.23|Beesmas 2019 update]], the Coconut Dispenser will always provide a
        +100% pollen boost, regardless of the player's Coconut Badge.
        * This is the only field to have no bees that dislike it.
