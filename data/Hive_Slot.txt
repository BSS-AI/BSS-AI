        * The maximum amount of hive slots used to be 40, but it is now 50.

        *Originally, Onett planned to add five additional hive slots that cost Robux, but this was
        canceled.

        *In the Test Realm it was possible to buy 75 hive slots for testing purposes, but this was
        removed, and was never added to the main game.
