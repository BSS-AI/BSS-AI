        '''Cogs''' are inventory items added in the [[Updates|12/26/2022 update]]. They are used to
        purchase upgrades, purchase [[drives]], and reroll bees and upgrades in the [[Robo Bear
        Challenge]]. They are removed from the inventory when the [[Robo Bear Challenge]] ends.
        Additionally, you can collect 250,000 Cogs to unlock the [[Cub Buddy|Robo Cub]].

        == Ways to Obtain ==
        *Completing quests in the Robo Bear Challenge.
        **Starting a challenge gives 10 Cogs.
        **The harder the quest, the more Cogs it gives.
        **You may get bonus cogs for completing a quest within the first two minutes.
        **You may also get bonus cogs from certain upgrades you can purchase in the challenge.
        *Defeating a [[Golden Cogmower]] guarantees at least 14 Cogs and scales up to 18.

        == Trivia ==
        * This is the first [[Items|item]] to be added that is not only exclusively rewarded in a
        challenge, but also used as a temporary form of currency.
        *The item slightly resembles the cog on top of the [[Noob Shop]].
