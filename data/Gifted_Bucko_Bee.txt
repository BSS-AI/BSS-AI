        '''Gifted Bucko Bee''' is a [[Quest Givers|Quest Giver]] and one of three permanent
        [[:Category:Quest Bees|Quest Bees]], the others being [[Gifted Riley Bee]] and [[Honey Bee
        (NPC)|Honey Bee]]. Gifted Bucko Bee is located on the second floor of the [[Blue HQ]], in
        between the [[Top Bucko Bee Helpers]] and the [[Daily Top Blue Collectors]]
        [[Leaderboards]]. The player cannot start receiving and completing its [[Quests]] without a
        [[Translator]], which is given by [[Science Bear]] three times. Completing 150 quests from
        Gifted Bucko Bee is required to craft the [[Hydroponic Planter]] and completing 250 quests
        from Gifted Bucko Bee is required to craft the [[Tide Popper]].

        == Quests ==
        Gifted Bucko Bee is an infinite quest giver whose quests scale up in difficulty. However,
        some specific quest requirements may stay the same. Due to this, the reward will vary
        depending on the quest. Rewards will always contain one [[Blue Extract]] and [[Honey]].

        For the requirements below, ''X'' represents the number of quests the player has completed
        for Gifted Bucko Bee, and ''Y'' represents how many times 10 quests have been completed.
        Requirements are not represented this way are static or may progress in a way not yet
        determined.

        ==Trivia==
        *Since the quests are all chosen at random, including the first, the formula for the first
        requirements of any quest is \"x divided by (number of Gifted Bucko Bee quests completed
        +1)\".
        * Gifted Bucko Bee is one of the three [[:Category:NPC|NPCs]] to be based on [[Bees]], the
        other two being [[Gifted Riley Bee]] and [[Honey Bee (NPC)]].
        **This Bee is the only quest bee to reside indoors, though [[Gifted Riley Bee]] resided
        indoors too before an update.
        *In the Bucko Bee's [[Snow Machine]] for Beesmas 2020 dialogue, Gifted Bucko Bee says to
        defeat ten Spiders. However, the [[Quests|Quest]] requires the Player to defeat five
        Spiders.
