{"text": "The [[Daily Top Mushroom Field Collectors]] is one of the 62 [[leaderboards]] in the game
        and is only viewable via the Global Leaderboards menu in the [[System Page]]. This
        leaderboard shows how much [[Pollen]] players have collected in the [[Mushroom Field]] on
        that day. It shows 10 leaderboard positions at a time. The player can scroll down the
        leaderboard to see the rankings of other players, all the way down to the 100th place. It
        resets every day at 12:00 AM MST.

        ==Prizes==

        * For being in the Top 25 when the leaderboard resets, the player will be awarded with the
        '''Mushroom Field Stamp [[Sticker]].'''

        * For being in the Top 100 when the leaderboard resets, the player will be rewarded with 25
        [[Ticket]]s.
