        The '''Spark Staff''' is a [[:Category:Tools|tool]] that was added in the [[Updates|4/5/2019
        update]]. It can be bought in the [[Mountain Top Shop]].

        {| class=\"article-table\"
        !Crafting Ingredients
        |-
        |{{Cost|Honey|60,000,000}}
        |}

        The Spark Staff collects all [[pollen]] from the 3 fullest nearby [[flowers]] in 0.5 seconds
        and increases it by 20%. It has a maximum base pollen collection rate of 207 pollen per
        second. However, the nearby flowers may not be full, so it doesn't always reach its complete
        potential.

        The following audio plays when the Spark Staff collects pollen:[[File:Sparkstaffuse.ogg]]

        ==Trivia==
        *This is the only tool that makes a sound every use. It is also the only tool that can
        collect pollen while in mid-air and the most expensive tool without an ability.
        *This is the only tool in the Mountain Top Shop that neither have an ability nor make a
        specific \"shape\" when collecting pollen.
        *This and the [[Scissors]] are the only tools that collect all pollen from the flowers they
        hit.
        *This tool is based on the [https://www.roblox.com/catalog/27860496/Nova-Wand \"Nova Wand\"],
        which is an official Roblox gear that can be bought for 1000 Robux.
        *This tool is needed for [[Science Bear|Science Bear's]] [[Drives|Drive]] questline.
        **Collecting 16,777,216 pollen with this tool is required for \"Repairing The White Drive.\"
        **Collecting 33,554,432 pollen with this tool is required for \"Corrupting The Glitched
        Drive.\"
        *The oldest version of the tool is known to not have the pollen increase of the current one.
        The previous version of the tool is known to have 15% pollen increase instead of the current
        20%.
