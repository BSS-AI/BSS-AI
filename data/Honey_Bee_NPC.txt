        '''Honey Bee''' is a [[Quest Givers|Quest Giver]] and one of the three permanent
        [[:Category:Quest Bees|Quest Bees]], the others being [[Gifted Riley Bee]] and [[Gifted
        Bucko Bee]]. Honey Bee is located on the rock near the [[Pine Tree Forest]]. Added in the
        [[Updates#2019.02.01|2/1/2019 Update]], its original purpose was to give players something
        to do while the next update was being worked on, but it has now become a Permanent Quest
        Giver.

        It doesn't require a [[Translator]] to give quests, making it different from the other
        quest-giving bees.

        It used to be located next to the ramp leading to the [[Basic Bee Gate]], but it was moved
        behind the biggest tree in the Pine Tree Forest in the [[Updates#2020.06.06|6/6/2020
        Update]].

        == Quests ==
        Honey Bee only gives quests that solely revolve around collecting [[Ability Tokens#Honey
        Gift|Honey Tokens]]. The first Quest starts from collecting 500 honey tokens, then the
        amount of Honey Tokens needed to complete a Quest is increased by 500 per subsequent Quest.
        Completion of each quest will award the player with a [[Buffs & Debuffs#From
        NPCs|Conversion Boost]] (×2 [[System Page#Convert Rate|Convert Rate]]) that lasts for 30
        minutes. Other rewards, such as [[Honey]], [[Treat#Treat|Treats]], [[Magic Bean]]s, [[Royal
        Jelly]], or [[Ticket]]s, can be rewarded. For every 10th Quest that is completed, a [[Royal
        Jelly#Star Jelly|Star Jelly]] is rewarded.

        ==Tips==
        *Fighting [[Stick Bug]] and [[Stick Nymph]]s and/or the [[Mondo Chick]] are very effective
        for completing its quests, as they spawn many honey tokens. Defeating normal [[mobs]] for
        their token rewards can also help with Honey Bee's quests.
        *Defeating many [[Wild Windy Bee]]s is another way of completing this quest faster.
        *Farming [[sprout]]s is a viable way, as they spawn many honey tokens.
        *[[Passive Abilities#Coin Scatter|Coin Scatter]] is also an effective way of collecting
        honey tokens quickly.
        **Coin Scatter can be easily triggered by having multiple [[Stubborn Bee]]s, [[Honey Bee]]s,
        [[Carpenter Bee]]s and [[Vector Bee]]s.
        **Use Coin Scatter on fields where there aren't many decorations blocking them, such as the
        [[Mountain Top Field]].
        *Catching [[coconut]]s and [[Mythic Meteor Shower|meteors]] produces many honey tokens.
        **Summoning a [[honeystorm]] is also an option.
        *Lower level [[fields]] such as the [[Sunflower Field]] spawn more honey tokens than
        higher-level fields such as the Mountain Top Field.
        *Have bees that can spawn [[Ability Tokens#Honey Gift|honey gift]] tokens in your hive in
        order to complete the quests faster.
        *Using the [[Passive Abilities#Gummy Star|Gummy Star]] passive would be effective as it
        spawns many honey tokens.
        *When the quest is finished, claim it '''ONLY''' when you're about to boost due to its 2x
        conversion.

        ==Trivia==
        *This is the third bee-based [[:Category:NPC|NPC]] in the game. It is also first bee-based
        NPC that isn't in its gifted form, the second being the [[Digital Bee (NPC)|Digital Bee
        NPC]].
        *Honey Bee is the only Bee Quest giver that you don't need a translator to talk to.
        *Honey Bee, [[Science Bear]], and [[Polar Bear]] are the only quest givers to give a buff as
        a reward for finishing a quest.
        *Honey Bee, along with [[Gifted Bucko Bee]], [[Gifted Riley Bee]], [[Onett]], and [[Bubble
        Bee Man]] are the only non-bear quest givers.
        *In the [[Updates#2019.04.17|Egg Hunt 2019 update]], there was a [[Plastic Egg|plastic egg]]
        token hidden behind it.
        **It has been replaced by a ticket token granting 3 [[Ticket]]s.
        **Honey Bee was one of the three quest givers that did not give an Egg Hunt quest, the
        others being Onett and [[Spirit Bear]].
        *Honey Bee is the only NPC whose [[Beesmas]] quest affected another NPC's machine (Black
        Bear's Honey Wreath).
        *Honey Bee along with [[Spirit Bear]] are the only quest givers that can summon a
        [[Honeystorm]].
        *Honey Bee is one of 3 NPCs who have moved place, the others being [[Bubble Bee Man]] and
        [[Gummy Bear]].
        *Honey Bee's quests are the only repeatable quests which don't contain the NPCs name
        followed by a colon (:).
        *During Beemas 2021, there was an error with Honey Bee’s ornament that made the Bag of Honey
        ornament and buffs are not visible to the player. This bug has been fixed and all players
        who gave Honey Bee a present before 01/06/2022 were refunded.
        *Honey Bee is one of the 2 NPCs (the other being Science Bear) that require you to join the
        [https://www.roblox.com/groups/3982592/Bee-Swarm-Simulator-Club#!/about Bee Swarm Simulator
        Club] in order to access the Club Dispensers and complete their quests. Its Beesmas quests
        require you to use the Honey Dispenser.
