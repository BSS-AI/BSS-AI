{"text": "[[File:GummyBeeEggClaim.png|thumb|220px|The Gummy Bee Egg Claim.]] The '''Gummy Bee Egg
        Claim''' is a platform that can be found beyond the [[Ant Gate]]. It's located on a ledge
        near the [[Ant Challenge]] and the Player [[Hive]]s. It can be reached by going through the
        gap behind the [[Top Ant Exterminators|Top Ant Exterminators Leaderboard]], going on top of
        the Ant Gate, or by walking on top of the Hives to the egg claim.

        == Claiming the Egg==
        Standing on the white platform prompts players with a message saying, \"Combine 2500
        [[Gumdrops]] into a [[Gummy Bee]]\" if they haven't claimed a [[Egg#Event Bee Eggs|Gummy Bee
        egg]]. If the player already owns one, the text saying, \"You Already Own a Gummy Bee\" is
        displayed. Whenever a player forms a Gummy Bee egg, a slight remix of \"[[Music|gbtune1]]\"
        (the music played around the Gummy Bee Egg Claim and in [[Gummy Bear's Lair]]) is played
        throughout the map and a server-wide announcement is made reading: \"<span
        style=\"background:#F4A90D;color:black\">🎉 {Username} has formed a Gummy Bee
        🎉</span>\".

        == Teleporting to the Lair ==
        Standing on the Gummy Bee model without the Goo Hotshot [[Badges|badge]] prompts the player
        with \"<span style=\"background:#23e8b7;color:black\">Only Goo Hotshots can hear Gummy
        Bee...</span>\", and using gumdrops when this message is shown will not teleport the
        player to Gummy Bear's Lair. If the player has the Goo Hotshot badge, standing on the model
        prompts the player with \"<span style=\"background:#23e8b7;color:black\">The Gummy Bee
        wants Gumdrops</span>\". Using Gumdrops while standing on the model with Goo Hotshot
        teleports the player to Gummy Bear's Lair. A Gummy Bee is not required to enter the lair.

        ==Trivia==
        *During the [[:Category:Beesmas|Beesmas 2019 event]], there was an [[Obstacle
        Courses|obstacle course]] beside this that led to the [[Ornament Presents|Gooey Present]].
        It returned in Beesmas 2020, this time led to the Glowing [[Gift Boxes|Gift Box]] and back
        again in Beesmas 2021, this time, it leading to Gummy Gift Box, and yet again it returned in
        Beesmas 2022, leading to the Gooey Gift Box.
        *This is one of two egg claims in the map, the other being the [[Vicious Bee Egg Claim]] for
        [[Vicious Bee]].
        *At night time, the Gummy Bee model's face glows as well as the teal outline of the pad.
        *An alternative way to reach this is by jumping on top of one of the Ant Pass Dispensers,
        jumping on to the rock by the Ant Gate, walking across the Ant Gate, and jumping onto the
        pad.
        *This is the only bee claim to have a different soundtrack than the area around it playing
        gbtune1.
        *While Gummy Bee was available in the [[Ticket Tent]], the giant Gummy Bee used in the egg
        claim was located on the top of the Ticket Tent to the right of [[Gifted Bee|Gifted]]
        [[Photon Bee]] where the [[Festive Bee]] is now. It would still teleport players to Gummy
        Bear's Lair if a gumdrop was used on top of it.
