{"text": "The '''Stick Bug Amulet''' is an [[amulet]] that can be obtained from the [[Stick Bug
        Challenge]].

        In order to obtain these amulets, the player will need to get [[Stick Bug]] to a certain
        level as well as reach a certain score during the Stick Bug Challenge. Stick Bug Amulets
        will have at least four buffs, with a fifth and sixth possible buff. These buffs can include
        [[Mobs|monster]] respawn time, [[System Page#Red Attack|red]] or [[System Page#Blue
        Attack|blue]] bee attack, [[System Page#Bee Attack|general bee attack]], and [[System
        Page#Bond From Battle|bond from battle]].

        == Requirement(s) ==
        The requirements are based on your score and Stick Bug's level on a challenge.
