        The '''Robo Bear Challenge''' is a 5 minute Challenge that can be started by talking to
        [[Robo Bear]]. Upon entering the Challenge, the Player receives 2 Quests, which can be
        rerolled once in the whole run. Then, the Player should pick 3 Bees from their Hive, which
        can also be rerolled. After the 3 Bees are chosen, all other Bees that isn’t picked in prior
        Rounds or the current Round are deactivated. The Player is then allowed to purchase
        Buffs/upgrades that cost [[Cog]]s. After this, the Player has 5 minutes to complete the
        Quest that the player have chosen. [[Mechsquito]]s, [[Mega Mechsquito]]s, [[Cogmower]]s,
        [[Cogturret]]s, are mainly spawned and [[Golden Cogmower]]s spawns at random fields during
        the Challenge. Each 5 rounds completed; gets to earn a new tier of [[Amulet#Cog Amulet|Cog
        Amulet]].

        == Gameplay ==
        [[File:Robo.jpg|thumb|280x280px|The starting of a Robo Bear challenge displays a quest
        selection screen]]

        === Before Starting ===
        To start the Robo Bear Challenge, the Player must spend a [[Robo Pass]]. Once spent, the
        Player receives free 10 Cogs and a Quest Reroll. Then, the Player has to select one of two
        Quests. After this, the Player will select one of the three selected Bees in the Player's
        Hive (the Player must select 3 Bees in the first Round and 2 Bees in subsequent rounds) and
        can be Rerolled at the expense of Cogs. This expense increases as it is used more. The Bees
        in the selection is shown their Hive Level, their Gifted Status, their equipped [[Beequip]]
        and their Mutation. After choosing the three Bees, the Player can purchase upgrades for
        Cogs, there are three different Upgrades the Player can choose from and can also be rerolled
        for Cogs that also has an expense increasing as it is used more.

        === After Starting ===
        After starting the Round, the Player will receive the Quest, the Robo Bear Challenge Debuff,
        and their selected Bees. The unselected Bees are shut down in the Player's Hive. The Robo
        Bear Challenge Debuff affects Capacity, Pollen from Tools, Pollen from Movement Collection,
        and Pollen from Coconuts. The Player has 5 minutes to finish the Quests. During the
        Challenge, the Player is unable to harvest Planters and use [[:Category:Beesmas|Beesmas]]
        Decorations. Upon completion of the Quest, the Round ends, and the Player will receive Cogs
        based on how many cogs the Quest said it would give, along with bonus Cogs depending on how
        fast the player finished the quest. The Cogs from the Round Speed Bonus is given if the
        Player completes the quest within 3 minutes and 45 seconds and the amount of Cogs given is
        calculated proportionately. If the Player has upgrades that grant bonus Cogs, they will also
        be granted bonus Cogs based on the total amount of Cogs from all Upgrades. After a Round is
        finished or if the challenge ends, all Mobs exclusive in the Challenge will be gone along
        with the Upgrades and the Robo Bear Challenge Debuff. This process will repeat again in
        subsequent Rounds, but the Player can only select two Bees to add on to the Bees they
        already selected.

        * '''Upon reaching Round 5''', there is a guaranteed Quest to that requires the Player to
        defeat 25 Mechsquitos and collect 1,600,000 of a certain color of Pollen. This Round grants
        the Player a [[Cog Amulet#Bronze Cog Amulet|Bronze Cog Amulet]] upon completion.
        * '''Upon reaching Round 10''', there is a guaranteed Quest that requires the Player to
        defeat 10 Cogmowers and collect 60,000,000 of a certain color of Pollen. This Round grants
        the Player a [[Cog Amulet#Silver Cog Amulet|Silver Cog Amulet]] upon completion.
        * '''Upon reaching Round 15''', there is a guaranteed Quest that requires the Player to
        defeat 10 Mega Mechsquitos and collect 2,000,000,000 of a certain color of Pollen. This
        Round grants the Player a [[Cog Amulet#Gold Cog Amulet|Gold Cog Amulet]] upon completion.
        * '''Upon reaching Round 20''', there is a guaranteed Quest that requires the Player to
        defeat 20 Cogmowers and collect 60,000,000,000 of a certain color of Pollen. This Round
        grants the Player a [[Cog Amulet#Diamond Cog Amulet|Diamond Cog Amulet]] upon completion.
        * '''Upon completing Round 25''', the Challenge ends and grants the Player a [[Cog
        Amulet#Supreme Cog Amulet|Supreme Cog Amulet]] and the [[Cub Buddy|Robo Bear Cub Buddy]] (If
        not already owned) upon completion.

        During and between Rounds, Cogs may be spent at [[Robo Bear's Shop]].

        All Cogs in the Player's Inventory are removed once the Challenge ends, which happens when
        either the Player hatches or transforms a Bee, the timer of the challenge Quest runs out, or
        if the Player presses the “Quit” button which appears on the Challenge Quest box.

        == Tips ==

        === Pre-Round Preparations ===
        * If you are aiming for a high score, you should '''use consumables''', such as Super
        Smoothies. You are also able to use field boosts and winds during a round. Even if you're
        not aiming for a high score, doing so can still be useful.
        ** Giving Onett a Present grants the Super Smoothie buff as well as a 3x Mountain Top Field
        buff. It also gives a free Robo Pass.
        * Attempt to '''save your quest rerolls''' or get the Reboot upgrade for an extra quest
        re-roll. Quest re-rolls are important if you get bad quests (i.e. being a red hive and
        getting two quests related to blue pollen, or a convert at hive quest).
        * Don't be afraid to '''save up cogs''' if the options are bad. They can be spent later on
        other re-rolls, upgrades, or drives.
        * '''Use the lock feature when necessary.''' If there is an upgrade that you need but cannot
        afford it, this feature will allow that specific upgrade to stay where it is until you
        select it. There is a lock beside each upgrade.
        * '''Prioritize attack bees''' such as Precise Bees, Spicy Bees, Vicious Bees, and Windy
        Bees. Vicious Bee is an especially useful as it can attack entire swarms of mobs with
        Impale. Windy Bees provide excellent crowd control by damaging multiple mobs at a time with
        Tornado.
        * Digital Bee can freeze enemies and makes them receive more damage.
        * Avoid picking a \"Convert at Hive\" quest, unless you are a Blue Hive or the required amount
        is low.

        === Tips During Rounds ===
        * '''Always keep on your toes!''' Mechsquitoes will apply Mechsquito Toxin on you, which
        reduces Pollen and Player movespeed. This can quickly accumulate in later rounds and
        severely hamper your collection. Mega Mechsquitoes can also do considerable damage to a
        still player.
        * '''Prioritize Cogturrets''' if possible. Cogturrets can make large swathes of the field
        dangerous with their rolling cogs, so try to stay near them so your Bees will attack them
        and take them down before they pile up and make the field impossible to navigate.
        ** In addition, Cogturrets only shoot their rolling cogs forward, to the left, to the right
        or backwards, so tracking where the rolling cogs will go and avoiding those spaces can be
        very helpful in dodging their cogs.
        * If the Cogmower/Cogturret spam is becoming too great, '''use Star Saw (if you have it).'''
        Star Saw circles around you for 30 seconds after activation, and while it's difficult to hit
        Mechsquitoes and Mega Mechsquitoes (owing to their flying hitboxes), Cogmowers and
        Cogturrets can easily be hit with it, allowing you to quickly take down these threats if the
        Bees are preoccupied with Mechsquitoes and Mega Mechsquitoes.
        * '''Finish the round quickly!''' A fast and decisive completion is key to a good run. As
        the round drags on, waves of enemies will pile up on each other, making them harder to
        clear. This in turn leads to a more cluttered field and a higher chance of death, ending in
        a death spiral that's sure to end in defeat (especially in later rounds).
        * If you're a Blue Hive or a mid-game Bomb Hive, make sure to use both Crimson and Cobalt
        Bee as the synchronize upgrade can save you. Also, in general there are a ton of upgrades
        that synergize around bombs and Crimson and Cobalt bee.
        * If you have any boost items active, such as [[Purple Potion]] or [[Super Smoothie]] when
        the round seems unbeatable, it is best to leave the challenge to give their buffs more time
        to be used in later sessions.

        == Rewards ==
        After the challenge ends, a message box displays the rewards. The number of rewards received
        tends to increase with a higher score. The rewards also increase in quality and quantity
        relative to the player's level. A higher level will also give players higher-tier Cog
        Amulets. The player will also only receive four rewards (other than the amulet) in varying
        quantities (except for the drive, which is guaranteed to have only one).

        *When you complete a quest, you might briefly see a green 'Finish' button that's in the
        place of the 'Quit' button. It is unknown what purpose this button would serve, as the round
        automatically finishes once the quest(s) are completed.
        *There is a glitch where after the timer ends, it disappears and the challenge doesn't end.
        In this period, you get infinite time to complete the quest. It is unknown why this glitch
        is caused, but it's most likely caused by lag.

        rounds, it will prompt you with the message: \"⚠ Hatching or transforming a bee will end your
        Robo Bear Challenge. Continue?⚠\". Choosing \"Yes\" will end your Robo Bear Challenge, and \"No\"
        will cancel it.
        *When in the quest picking menu, your bees are frozen in place.
        *If the player ended the session through disconnecting, the player will not obtain the
        amulet of the session until they claim their hive when they rejoined.
        *You can obtain [[Cog]]s outside of Robo Bear's Challenge. To do this, end RBC right after
        you kill a [[Golden Cogmower]], however the cogs won't transfer into the next challenge.
        *When you complete a round your balloon blessing will refresh.
        *TurboTobyTwo was the first player who triumphed the Robo Bear Challenge.
        *While receiving a quest, the players movespeed is set to 0, meaning it's impossible for
        them to move.
        * If a bee isn't picked in the challenge, its gifted hive bonus, or any bonus on it's
        beequip doesn't apply.
        *The final score is calculated with the following formula: Score = (5000 x Round Reached) +
        (1000 x Progress % made in round you lost) + (Total number of earned cogs)

        (Total number of earned cogs)
