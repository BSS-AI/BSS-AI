        consumable items combined for 20 minutes!|cooldown = 20 minutes|}}A '''Super Smoothie''' is
        a [[Crafting|craftable]] [[:Category:Inventory|inventory item]] added in the
        [[Updates#2020.06.06|6/6/2020 update]]. When used, it grants the following for 20 minutes:
        *x1.5 [[System Page#Capacity Multiplier|Capacity]].
        *x1.6 [[System Page#Red Pollen|Red Pollen]].
        *x1.6 [[System Page#Blue Pollen|Blue Pollen]].
        *x1.6 [[System Page#White Pollen|White Pollen]].
        *x1.4 [[System Page#Bee Gather Pollen|Bee Gather Pollen]].
        *x1.4 [[System Page#Pollen From Tools|Pollen From Tools]].
        *x1.5 [[System Page#Convert Rate|Convert Rate]].
        *x1.5 [[System Page#Hive Convert Rate|Convert Rate At Hive]].
        * +20% [[System Page#Instant Conversion|Instant Conversion]].
        *+1% [[System Page#Super-Crit Chance|Super-Crit Chance]].
        *+6% [[System Page#Critical Chance|Critical Chance]].
        *x1.25 [[Stats#Speed|Bee Movespeed]].
        *x1.25 [[System Page#Movespeed|Player Movespeed]].
        {{Cost|Red Extract}}, {{Cost|Blue Extract}}, {{Cost|Glue}}, {{Cost|Oil}}, {{Cost|Tropical
        Drink}}, {{Cost|Purple Potion}}, or {{Cost|Enzymes}} buffs cannot be active simultaneously
        along with Super Smoohie. If any of these buffs are active, when a Super Smoothie is used,
        they will be overridden.

        ** {{Cost|The Planter Of Plenty}} drops 10 Super Smoothies when harvested from the [[Bamboo
        Field]], 36 days cooldown.
        ** {{Cost|Candy Planter}} has a low chance to drop 1-3 Super Smoothies.
        * As a reward from certain [[quests]]:
        ** Dapper Bear's \"More Than Myth\" (#15) gives 1 Super Smoothie and other items.
        ** Science Bear's \"Corrupting The Glitched Drive\" gives 1 Super Smoothie and other items.
        * Redeeming certain valid [[codes]]:
        ** 1MLikes (Activates the buff + other stuff).
        ** WalmartToys (Activates the buff + other stuff).

        ==Trivia==

        * This is one of the 4 [[:Category:Consumables|consumable]] item that increases capacity,
        with the other being {{Cost|Marshmallow Bee}}, {{Cost|Purple Potion}} and {{Cost|Snowflake}}
        * Super smoothies were once misspelled as \"Super Smoothy\".
        * The Super Smoothie is the consumable that gives the most amount of different buffs in the
        game.
        * Super Smoothie's raw ingredients cost (excluding gumdrops and moon charms downgrading):
        ** {{Cost|Neonberry|12}}
        ** {{Cost|Coconut|60}}
        ** {{Cost|Sunflower Seed|600}}
        ** {{Cost|Strawberry|450}}
        ** {{Cost|Pineapple|600}}
        ** {{Cost|Blueberry|450}}
        ** {{Cost|Moon Charm|225}}
        ** {{Cost|Royal Jelly|810}} ({{Cost|Honey|810,000,000}})
        ** {{Cost|Magic Bean|9}} ({{Cost|Ticket|180
        }})
        ** {{Cost|Gumdrops|450}} ({{Cost|Ticket|150}})
        * Super smoothies, [[translator]]s, [[Sprinklers|sprinkler builders]], certain
        [[Egg#Specific Bee Egg|specific bee eggs]] (all except for [[Egg|Honey Bee eggs]]), [[Royal
        Jelly#Specific Bee Jelly Variants|specific bee jellies]], [[Spirit Petal|spirit petals]] (if
        the player has already donated one), [[Gingerbread Bear|gingerbread bears]], [[snowflake]]s,
        [[balloon]]s, [[planter]]s, the [[Beequip#Beequip Cases and Storage|beequip case]] and
        [[7-Pronged Cog|7-pronged cogs]] are the only items that cannot be donated to the [[Wind
        Shrine]].
        ** The Super Smoothie is also the only craftable item that cannot be donated to the [[Wind
        Shrine]].
        * [[Beequip#Beequip Cases and Storage|Bee Bear]] calls this the \"ultimate boost granting
        beverage\" in his 2021 beesmas quest, \"Quality Cousin Time\".
        * The only other mention of a smoothie is by [[Polar Bear]] and his \"Thick Smoothie\" quest.
        * The Super Smoothie used to have +7% [[Critical Hits|Critical Chance]] and no [[Critical
        Hits|Super-Crit Chance]].
