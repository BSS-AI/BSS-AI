        It is given its name from the large [[Honey Bee]] located on top of it.

        *This gate has the highest number of [[fields]] behind it, which is four.
        *There were numerous ways to bypass the gate without 15 bees, but as of the
        [[Updates#2019.09.28|9/28/2019 update]], it will always teleport the player back to spawn.

        *This gate holds the ramp that leads to the [[Lion Bee Gate]], also known as the the 25 Bee
        Gate.
