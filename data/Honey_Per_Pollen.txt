{"text": "'''Honey Per Pollen''' is a [[Mechanics|mechanic]] that increases the amount of [[honey]]
        earned when converting [[pollen]]. For example, if a player has 10 pollen in their
        [[Bags|container]], with a 110% Honey Per Pollen multiplier, they would receive 11 honey if
        converted. 20 to 22, etc. To determine how much honey will be earned when converting pollen,
        multiply bag space ''B'', by Honey Per Pollen percentage.

        Similar to many other boosts, (for example, [[Instant Conversion]]) the player can always
        check their current percentage of Honey Per Pollen on the [[System Page]]. It starts off at
        100% and increases later on when the player gets more and more Honey Per Pollen boosts.

        The player can increase their Honey Per Pollen through the [[Moon Amulet]], [[Gifted
        Bee|Gifted]] [[Gummy Bee]], and through the [[Badges#Goo Badge|Goo Badge]]. They can also
        obtain it temporarily when using the [[Wealth Clock]], granting x1.01 Honey Per Pollen every
        hour (stacks up to x1.05 Honey Per Pollen), lasting until they leave the current server they
        were in and not join another server for 15 minutes. The player could have also gotten a
        temporary Honey Per Pollen boost from the [[Buffs & Debuffs#From Ability Tokens|Beesmas
        Cheer]] buff before it was modified in the [[Updates#2019.04.17|Easter 2019 update]]. The
        player can also gain up to x1.05 Honey Per Pollen from each of the 5 [[Nectar]] types. The
        maximum Honey Per Pollen possible used to be 138%, today it is now 165%.

        Honey Per Pollen, along with other boosts like Instant Conversion, [[goo]], and extra
        capacity, is one of the best ways to get maximum efficiency when producing honey. These
        boosts, when combined with each other, are extremely helpful overall. It can affect the
        player's condition when, for example, grinding or converting your [[Balloon|hive balloon]].
        Keep in mind that there is a difference between Instant Conversion and Honey Per Pollen.
        While both help with honey production, Instant Conversion converts pollen collected in
        [[fields]] into honey instantly. However, Honey Per Pollen affects the amount of honey the
        player makes for every pollen.

        [[Category:Mechanics]]"}
