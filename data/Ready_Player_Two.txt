        the [[How to Program BASIC]] accessory to wear on their avatar.

        Each code awards the player a [[7-Pronged Cog]]. They can be used to craft [[Strange
        Goggles]]. When equipped, the player needs to go to [[The Computer]], where they can now
        interact with it. Upon use, the player will see [[Digital Bee (NPC)|Digital Bee]]. Digital
        Bee acts as an NPC and will begin citing the first puzzle. Upon completing each puzzle, the
        player is rewarded with various items, and can go back to The Computer to receive the next
        puzzle. The puzzle guides are listed below.
