        The '''Red Cannon''' is a cannon available for use once the player has discovered 25
        [[Bees|bee]] types. It is located to the right of the [[hive]]s, next to [[Black Bear]], and
        near the [[Ticket Tent]] and the [[Blue Teleporter]] exit. It has a 5 second cooldown
        period.

        If the player has the [[Parachute]] or [[Glider]], they can use it in combination with the
        Red Cannon to navigate quickly around the map due to its large range. It gives access to
        various places that were once tedious or impossible to access, such as the [[Star Hall]],
        going outside the map, or to collect most [[Royal Jelly|royal jelly]] tokens, like the one
        on top of the [[Mountain Top Shop]] and the one on top of the [[hive]]s.

        If used without any [[Buffs & Debuffs|buffs]] or [[:Category:Gliding Tools|gliding
        tools]], the player will usually land in the middle of the [[Mountain Top Field]], though it
        has been known to undershoot (landing on the ramp up to the [[Lion Bee Gate]], into the
        [[Mushroom Field]], or very rarely, the fence on the border of the [[Sunflower Field]]) or
        overshoot (putting the player outside the boundaries of the map). If the player has the
        [[Scythe]] equipped, the Red Cannon may only manage to shoot them into the [[Spider Field]]
        due to unknown reasons. This can also occur without the Scythe equipped. If this happens,
        simply jumping and pressing E at the same time will allow the cannon to shoot normally. If
        the player uses the Parachute or Glider and activates the cannon, the cannon will not launch
        them into the Mountain Top Field. Instead, they will lose momentum as soon as the cannon
        fires and continue gliding until they land on the ground.

        == Trivia ==
        * The Red Cannon is the only cannon that has a crosshair on it.
        * It is one of the four cannons in the game, the other three cannons are the [[Yellow
        Cannon]], [[Blue Cannon]], and [[Gummy Cannon]].
        ** This is one of the three transportational cannons in the game, which the other two
        cannons are [[Yellow Cannon]] and [[Blue Cannon]].
        * This cannon has the biggest range of all the cannons in the game.
        * It is the transportation method that requires the largest amount of discovered bee types
        to be used.
        * The Red Cannon has a cooldown of 5 seconds. While this is not normally possible to see
        during regular gameplay as the cannon launches the player too far to return in 5 seconds,
        the cooldown can be seen by using the Glider/Parachute glitch to stop all momentum as soon
        as the player exits the cannon.
        ** This cooldown is shared by all the transportational cannons.
        * On game release, the cannon could be used by discovering 20 bee types. In the
        [[Updates#2018.04.27|4/27/2018 update]], it was changed to 22 bee types. In the
        [[Updates#2018.11.25|11/25/2018 update]], it was changed to 25 bee types and has remained
        that way since.
        * It is the only cannon to not launch the player on the [[Starter Zone]], it instead
        launches the player to the Mountain Top Field, as the cannon is already in the Starter Zone.
        * On the game release, the player could launch themself from the Red Cannon to the Mountain
        Top Field even if they didn't have 25 bees in their hive. This has been patched.
