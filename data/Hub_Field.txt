        The '''Hub Field''' is a [[field]] that was added in the [[Updates#2024.01.12|1/12/2024
        update]], and is located in the trading portal beyond the [[Ant Gate]] inside the [[Hive
        Hub]].

        This field contains 697 [[flowers]]. The flowers are white, blue, and red, in single,
        double, and triple sizes.

        When planting a sprout using a magic bean on the Hub Field, there is a rare chance of it
        spawning a [[Sprout|Sticker Sprout]]. A Sticker Sprout is also guaranteed to spawn every 3
        hours, granted there is no sprout occupying the field.

        All pollen collected in this field will [[Instant Conversion|instantly convert]] to
        [[Honey]].

        *Using [[Glitter]] will add 100% Hub Field pollen onto the current boost for 15 minutes if
        the boost isn't already maxed out.

        ==Trivia==
        *This, the [[Ant Field]], and the [[Stump Field]] are the only fields that the player can
        collect pollen from while not standing on the flowers.
        *This and the [[Stump Field]] are the only non-quadrilateral fields in the game.
        *This, the [[Ant Field]], and the [[Stump Field]] are the only fields where [[Wild Windy
        Bee]] cannot spawn in nor fly to.
        *The [[Coconut Field]], the [[Pepper Patch|Pepper Patch, ⁣]] the [[Ant Field]] and the
        [[Stump Field]] are the only fields that were added after the game's first release.
        *This, the [[Mountain Top Field]], [[Ant Field]], [[Coconut Field]], [[Pepper Patch]] and
        the [[Stump Field]] are the only fields that can't be given a field boost by [[Field
        Booster|field boosters]].
        *This field is one of four fields with no decorations in it, indicating what field it is.
        The other three fields to do this are the [[Ant Field]], the [[Mountain Top Field]] and the
        [[Stump Field]].
        **This field gets its name from the place it is located instead, which is the [[Hive Hub]].
        *The Hub Field is the only field in the entire game without a truly unique field boost icon.
        Instead, its icon is reused from its associated [[Sticker#Stickers|field stamp]].
        *This is the only field to be in a sub-place.
        *You cannot use many types of consumable items while on this field.
        **You cannot use [[Sprinklers]], [[Stinger]]s, [[Cloud Vial]]s, [[Balloon]]s,
        [[Snowflake]]s, [[Planter]]s, [[Whirligig]]s, [[Night Bell|Night bells]] or any kind of
        [[Field Dice]] while on this field.
        ***As such, this is the only field in the game where [[Sprinklers]] are completely disabled.
        ***However, you can use [[Glitter]], [[Jelly Beans]], and [[Box-O-Frogs]].
        *[[Planter]]s cannot be placed in this field, so there is most likely no [[nectar]] in this
        field.
        *This is the only field that instantly converts your collected pollen into honey (the Ant
        Field does not instantly convert your honey by itself, and it happens instead because of the
        [[Buffs & Debuffs#From Areas|Ant Challenge Buff]]).
