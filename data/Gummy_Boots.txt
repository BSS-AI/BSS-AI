        == Trivia==
        *In order to craft this item, a total of 100,000,000,000 honey, 168,750
        [[Blueberry|blueberries]], 200,000 [[Pineapple|pineapples]], 168,750
        [[Strawberry|strawberries]], 250 [[Magic Bean|magic beans]], and 16,250 [[Royal Jelly|royal
        jellies]] are needed. Also, keep in mind that crafting all the glue, glitter, red extracts,
        and blue extracts require a total of 6,250 minutes of playtime, which is equivalent to
        104.16 hours or 4.3 days (minus drops from mobs and quest rewards and using tickets to speed
        crafting.) If crafting gumdrops and moon charms are included, in total the crafting would
        take 156,250 minutes, or 2,604.16 hours, or 108.5 days. Added together, it would take
        162,500 minutes, or 2,708.8 hours, or 112.8 days. The gumdrops, strawberries, magic beans,
        and royal jelly amount is for [[crafting]] the items required to craft the Gummy Boots.
        *These are the first boots to have a [[Passive Abilities|passive ability]], as the [[Coconut
        Clogs]] were not in the game when the Gummy Boots were added.
        *These are the first boots that could not be obtained by [[Sun Bear]], followed by the
        Coconut Clogs.
        *This, the [[Gummy Mask]], and [[Shell Amulet]]s are the only accessories that give a goo
        buff.
        * Behind the Gummy Boots figure in the shop in [[Gummy Bear's Lair]], there is a glitter
        token.
        *The player needed this or the Gummy Mask in order to talk to Gummy Bear for his [[Beesmas]]
        quest.
        **The Gummy Boots were necessary if the player wanted to give [[Gummy Bear]] a present.
        *Before the [[Updates#2021.12.26|12/26/2021 update]], these boots were the most expensive
        item in terms of honey, excluding hive slots.
        *Initially, the Goo Trail passive ability covered only one flower, directly underneath the
        player, in goo. This has been buffed to the nearest 5 flowers.
        *During the release of the Gummy Boots, it costed 999 [[glue]], 300 [[glitter]], 300 [[Red
        Extract|red extracts]], and 300 [[Blue Extract|blue extracts]] in order to be crafted.
        However, the price was later nerfed to 500 [[glue]], 250 [[glitter]], 250 [[Red Extract|red
        extracts]], and 250 [[Blue Extract|blue extracts]] on the [[Updates#2021.12.26|12/26/2021
        update]]. Everyone who bought the Gummy Boots before the update were refunded the extra
        items when Beesmas 2021 was released.
        *Unlike other items like the [[Diamond Mask]] and the [[Gummy Mask]], the gummy boots do not
        require another item to 'inherit' the passive from, rather having two passives regardless of
        whether you have bought the [[Coconut Clogs]]. This makes it one of the only two items to
        have this characteristic, the other being the [[Coconut Canister]].
