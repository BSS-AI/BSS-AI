        '''Ticket'''s are a form of [[:Category:Currency|currency]] added in the
        [[Updates#2018.4.27|4/27/2018 update]]. They can be used to purchase certain [[items]].

        == Ways to Obtain==
        [[File:TicketShop-0.png|thumb|The Ticket Shop.]]

        * The player can obtain tickets as a reward for acquiring [[badges]]. Collecting badges
        gives 5 tickets for earning Cadet badges, 10 tickets for earning Hotshot badges, 15 tickets
        for earning Ace badges, 25 tickets for earning Master badges, and 40 tickets for earning
        Grandmaster badges.
        * The player can buy tickets from the [[Ticket Shop]], located outside the [[Mountain Top
        Shop]].
        * Very rarely, [[flowers]] will generate a ticket token instead of a [[honey]] or
        [[Treats|treat]] token when collecting [[pollen]]. The base probability is around 1/1000
        (0.1%), however it does increase with [[Loot Luck]] and [[System Page|Ticket Chance]].
        * Some [[:Category:Bears|bears]] give tickets as rewards for completing certain [[quests]].
        * There is a chance of getting a ticket as a reward for defeating [[mobs]]. The probability
        depends on the type of mob as well as the player’s Loot Luck.
        ** [[Tunnel Bear]], [[Stump Snail]], and [[King Beetle]] are guaranteed to grant tickets
        upon defeat. [[Wild Windy Bee]] will also yield ticket tokens for limited amounts per day.
        * Collecting pollen from [[sparkles]] may yield tickets.
        * There are ticket tokens hidden in various locations (See \"Locations\" below).
        * Redeeming certain valid [[codes]]:
        ** 38217 (Gives 5 tickets).
        ** Bopmaster (Gives 5 tickets).
        ** Cog (Gives 5 tickets).
        ** Crawlers (Gives 5 tickets).
        ** Roof (Gives 5 tickets).
        ** Connoisseur (Gives 5 tickets).
        ** Wax (Gives 5 tickets + other items).
        ** Wink (Gives 5 tickets + other items).
        ** 1MLikes (Gives 1 ticket + other items).
        ** DarzethDoodads (Gives 3 tickets + other items).
        * Buying them from the [[Robux Shop]]. (One ticket for 10 R$, 100 tickets for 400 R$, 510
        tickets for 1,700 R$, and 1,800 tickets for 4,500 R$)
        * Harvesting a normal, rare, epic, legendary, supreme, or festive [[sprout]] often yields
        ticket tokens (the rarer the sprout, the more tickets it will yield; rare sprouts and above
        guarantee tickets).
        * [[Honeystorm]]s are guaranteed to spawn ticket tokens.
        * The [[Wealth Clock]] gives tickets every hour after interacting with it, and increases
        incrementally every hour, stopping at the fifth (1 ticket first time, 2 second time, 3 third
        time, 4 fourth time, and 5 fifth time and onwards).
        * As a rare drop from [[Ability Tokens#Festive Gift|Festive Gifts]].
        * They can spawn as a potential match from Normal, Mega, Night and Extreme [[Memory
        Match]]es.
        * Through a [[Cub Buddy|cub buddy]] gift.
        * The [[Commando Chick]] will always drop 1 ticket upon being captured.
        ** Capturing it on the 25th time gives 100 tickets.
        * As a common drop from a [[Mythic Meteor Shower|mythic meteor]].
        * As a drop from planters.
        ** The amount of tickets it can potentially drop depends on the type of planter.
        ** Fully grown [[Ticket Planter]]s drop at least 10 or more tickets when harvested.
        * Puffshrooms drops tickets (the rarer the Puffshroom, the more it gives).
        * Clicking on any Hidden [[Sticker]] grants 1 ticket each (excluding seeker-stickers).

        ==Uses==
        [[File:TicketTent2021.png|thumb|The Ticket Tent.]]
        *6 tickets are required to use the [[Royal Jelly Dispenser]], located between the [[Clover
        Field]] and [[Dandelion Field]].
        *1, 10 or 100 tickets are required to buy [[gumdrops]] from the [[Gumdrop Shop]] (1 ticket =
        3 gumdrops; 10 tickets = 30 gumdrops; 100 tickets = 300 gumdrops).
        *10, 100 or 1,000 tickets are required to buy [[stinger]]s from the [[Stinger Shop]] (10
        tickets = 1 stinger; 100 tickets = 10 stingers; 1,000 tickets = 100 stingers)
        * 20 or 200 tickets are required to buy [[Magic Bean]]s from the [[Magic Bean Shop]] (20
        tickets = 1 magic bean; 200 tickets = 10 magic beans)
        * 1 ticket is required to use an [[Instant Converter]].
        *10 tickets are required to use the [[Ant Pass Dispenser]].
        *The [[Ticket Tent]] has the following items available:
        **[[Eggs#Silver Egg|Silver Egg]] (50 tickets), [[Eggs#Gold Egg|Gold Egg]] (100 tickets),
        [[Eggs#Diamond Egg|Diamond Egg]] (400 tickets), [[Eggs#Mythic Egg|Mythic Egg]] (2500
        tickets) [[Cobalt Bee|Cobalt]] and [[Crimson Bee]] (250 tickets each), miscellaneous
        [[Bees/Event|Event Bees]] (500 tickets), and [[Star Treat|star treats]] (1000 tickets).
        * Tickets are currently the only way to obtain most [[Bees/Event|Event bees.]]
        *50 tickets are required to start another [[Stick Bug Challenge]] when the player’s free
        challenge is already used.
        *Tickets, along with [[honey]], are used to purchase Market Boosts from the [[Boost
        Market]]. (No longer possible.)
        *Tickets can be used to speed up materials that are currently being made in the [[Blender]].
        *5 Tickets are required to store [[nectar]] in the [[Nectar Pot]].
        *50 Tickets are required to use the Robo Pass Dispenser.
        *Varying amounts of Tickets can be used to increase the maximum amount of [[Beequip]]s one
        can hold in their Beequip Storage, maxing out at 20,000 Tickets for the final upgrade.
        *Tickets, along with honey, are used to purchase Beequips in [[Dapper Bear's Shop]].

        == Trivia ==
        * It bears the face of a [[Basic Bee]].
        * Currently, the most expensive purchase that can be made using Tickets is the final slot
        upgrade for the Beequip Storage, costing 20,000 Tickets to increase storage from a maximum
        of 95 stored Beequips to 100 stored Beequips.
