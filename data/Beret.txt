        A '''Beret''' is a [[Bond|level]] 11 [[beequip]]. It has an equip limit of two. And can only
        be equipped by blue bees.

        == Potential ==
        * Convert Amount (Guaranteed) - +10 - +20. (up to +60 from [[Waxes|waxing]])
        * Convert Amount (Guaranteed) - +5% - +10%. (up to +50% from waxing)
        * Ability [[Pollen]] - +1% - +5%.
        * Ability: Blue [[Ability Tokens#Boost|Boost]] (from waxing, very rare, caustic only)
        * [Hive Bonus] [[Capacity]] (Guaranteed) - +5,000 - +10,000. (up to +20,000 from waxing)
        * [Hive Bonus] Blue [[Capacity]] (from waxing) - +1% - +5%.
        * [Hive Bonus] [[Capacity]] (from waxing, rare, caustic only) - +1% - +5%.

        == Ways to obtain ==
        * Small chance of dropping from [[The Planter Of Plenty]].
        * Small chance of dropping from the [[Blue Clay Planter]] in the [[Dandelion Field]] and
        [[Blue Flower Field]].
        *Can be purchased from [[Dapper Bear's Shop|Dapper Bear's Beequip Shop.]]
