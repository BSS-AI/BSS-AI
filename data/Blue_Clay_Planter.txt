        The '''Blue Clay Planter''' is a reusable planter that was added in the 12/26/21 update. It
        grows in 6 in game hours and stores around 3 million [[Pollen]]. It is purchasable in the
        [[Blue HQ]].

        {| class=\"article-table\"
        !Crafting Ingredients
        |-
        |{{Cost|Honey|10,000,000}}<br>{{Cost|Magic Bean|5}}<br>{{Cost|Blue
        Extract|15}}<br>{{Cost|Soft Wax|20}}
        |}

        It grows 25% faster in fields with Blue flowers, and gives 50% Bonus Blue pollen from
        harvests. Blue bees are 25% more likely to sip [[Nectar]] from this planter, but Red bees
        avoid sipping nectar from it. This planter grants 20% more Refreshing and Comforting Nectar.
        Harvesting a fully grown Blue Clay Planter has a 1/8 (12.5%) chance to spawn a
        [[puffshroom]].

        ==Trivia==

        * Its red counterpart is the [[Red Clay Planter]].
        *This is the only planter that drops Honeysuckles as a bonus item.
        *This is one of the five reusable planters to reside outside of [[Dapper Bear's Shop]], the
        others being the [[Red Clay Planter]], [[Petal Planter]], [[Hydroponic Planter]], and
        [[Heat-Treated Planter]].
        **This and the Hydroponic Planter are the only planters to be sold from the Blue HQ.
