        A '''Cogmower''' is a [[Mob]] that exclusively appears during [[Robo Bear Challenge|Robo
        Bear's Challenge]].

        This is the second mob type that a player can encounter, appearing as early as round 5.
        Cogmowers rise up from the field before slowly moving around the field, draining flowers and
        goo patches they pass over. The higher the level of the Cogmower, the more pollen is
        drained. Cogmowers bounce off of the edges of the field and each other. They also only take
        half as much damage from [[Critical Hits]].

        A Cogmower's level can range from 6-22, depending on what round of Robo Bear's Challenge the
        player is on.

        In round 10, four Cogmowers will spawn on one field which is either the [[Bamboo Field]],
        [[Clover Field]] or [[Pineapple Patch]]. Three Cogmowers will also spawn on two fields which
        can be the [[Sunflower Field]], [[Dandelion Field]], [[Strawberry Field]], [[Cactus Field]],
        [[Pumpkin Patch]], or [[Pine Tree Forest]]. In round 20, 5 Cogmowers will spawn in 4
        different fields. These Cogmowers do not despawn, nor do they follow the player. The player
        must instead seek them out as they're required to be defeated in order to complete rounds 10
        and 20. When a player steps in another field, the Cogmower will sink into the flowers of the
        field it is currently in, and rise in the field the player is in.

        ==Strategies==
        *Cogmowers do not aim for the player, allowing players to easily sidestep them.
        *Stand near the Cogmower, this will allow your bees to lock on to the Cogmower and quickly
        deal damage.
        *[[Vicious Bee]] and [[Windy Bee]] provide excellent crowd control, especially since
        Cogmowers often come in pairs or packs. [[Digital Bee]] can also stun many Cogmowers at
        once, and allow them to take extra damage via [[Ability Tokens#Mind Hack|Mind Hack]].
        *The '''Star Saw''' Passive from the [[Star Amulet|Supreme Star Amulet]] can be useful as it
        circles around you for 30 seconds when activated and can deal damage to multiple Cogmowers
        at once.
        *Frogs, Pollen Haze, and the [[The Supreme Saturator|Supreme Saturator]] provide decent
        field regeneration, which can be used to combat the flowers drained by the cogmowers.

        ==Trivia==
        *Cogmower is a portmanteau of \"Cog\" and \"Lawnmower'.
        *Cogmowers have a rarer, golden counterpart known as a [[Golden Cogmower]].
        **They also have a Beesmas variant called the [[Party Cogmower]].
        *Cogmowers, [[Golden Cogmower]]s, [[Cogturret]]s and [[Wild Windy Bee]] are the only mobs
        that are able to collect layers of flowers.
        * If a Cogmower is stunned due to Digital Bee's Mind Hack, and is hit by an unaffected one,
        it would still be slightly knocked back.
