        The '''Bubble Wand''' is a [[:Category:Tools|tool]] that can be bought in the [[Blue HQ]].

        {| class=\"article-table\"
        !Crafting Ingredients
        |-
        |{{Cost|Honey|3,500,000}}
        |}

        The Bubble Wand collects 6 [[pollen]] from 16 [[Flowers|flower]] patches in 0.8 seconds and
        collects x2 pollen from [[:Category:Blue|blue]] flowers. Every 10th swing it spawns a
        [[bubble]] somewhere on the field. It has an average base pollen collection rate of 160
        pollen per second: 240 from blue flowers and 120 from red and white flowers. It emits bubble
        particles.

        ==Trivia==
        *A replica of the Bubble Wand rests on the roof of the Blue HQ.
        *Its [[:Category:Red|red]] counterpart is the [[Scythe]].
        *The Bubble Wand collects about one more pollen per second than the Scythe, though this is
        hardly noticeable. It is also offset by its collection pattern, sometimes making it harder
        to collect [[goo]], [[sparkles]], or [[leaves]].
        *This is the second cheapest item in the Blue HQ, behind the [[Blue Guard]].
