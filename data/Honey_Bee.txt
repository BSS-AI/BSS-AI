        '''Honey Bee''' is a [[:Category:Colorless|Colorless]] [[Bees/Epic|Epic Bee]].

        Honey Bee's favorite type of treat are [[Pineapple|Pineapples]].

        Honey Bee likes the [[Pumpkin Patch]] and the [[Mountain Top Field]]. It dislikes the
        [[Spider Field]].

        == Stats ==
        * Collects 10 [[Pollen]] in 4 seconds.
        * Makes 360 [[Honey]] in 2 seconds.
        * +280 [[System Page#Convert Amount|Convert Amount]], +50% convert speed.
        * 🌟 [[Gifted Bee|Gifted Hive Bonus]]: x1.5 [[System Page#Honey From Tokens|Honey From
        Tokens]].

        === Abilities ===
        * '''[[Ability Tokens#Honey Gift|[Honey Gift]]]''' Sometimes spawns a honey token. The
        amount of honey is equal to 100x (x being the bee's level).
        * '''[[Ability_Tokens#Mark|[Honey Mark]]]''' Marks a random area on the [[Fields|Field]] for
        7 seconds (+0.2s per Level) that grants 2 Conversion Links and x1.25 [[System Page#Convert
        Rate|Convert Rate]] when you stand in it. Stacks up to 3 times.

        == Trivia ==
        * There is a [[:Category:Gates|Gate]] dedicated to the bee, the [[Honey Bee Gate]] (or the
        15 Bee Zone).
        * The only way to obtain [[Egg#Specific Bee Eggs|Honey Bee Eggs]] is to defeat [[Cave
        Monster]]s.
        * The [[Honey Mask]], which can be purchased in the [[Badges|Ace Badge]] area of the [[Badge
        Bearer's Guild]], resembles Honey Bee's face decal and color scheme.
        * Before the [[Updates#2018.11.25|11/25/2018 Update]], the [[Gifted Bee|Gifted]] Hive Bonus
        of Honey Bee was +250% [[System Page#Convert Rate|Conversion Rate]]. This was later updated
        to x1.5 Honey From Tokens.
        * Honey Bee is one of the two [[Bees]] that generate honey gifts, the other being [[Diamond
        Bee]].
        * Honey Bee was given the honey mark ability after the [[Updates#2019.04.05|4/5/2019
        update]].
        * Both Honey Bee and [[Bubble Bee]] emit the same bubble effect, although the colors of
        these 2 bees are different.
        * The reason Honey Bee dislikes the Spider Field might be because the [[Cave Monster]]s tend
        to have Honey Bee Eggs.
        * Along with [[Bumble Bee]] and [[Carpenter Bee]], Honey Bee are based on bees found in real
        life, though they don't show resemblance to the actual bee.
        * During [[Beesmas]] 2020 and 2021, players were able to receive a Honey Bee Jelly if they
        gave a present to [[Honey Bee (NPC)]].
        ** During Beesmas 2021, [[Royal Jelly#Specific Bee Jelly|Honey Bee Jelly]] was also
        available by purchasing the Honeysuckle Bundle from Bee Bear's Catalog.
