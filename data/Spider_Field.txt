        The '''Spider Field''' is a [[Fields|field]] located behind the [[Basic Bee Gate]] and
        between the [[Strawberry Field]] and [[Bamboo Field]]. The field is protected by the
        [[spider]].

        It is a 26x28 field, containing 728 [[flowers]]. The flowers are all white, in sizes of
        single, double, and triple.

        This field can spawn [[Bitterberry|bitterberry]], [[Treat|treat]], [[honey]], [[ticket]],
        [[enzymes]], and [[Magic Bean|magic bean]] tokens when collecting [[pollen]].

        [[Bees]] that like the Spider Field are [[Brave Bee]], [[Commander Bee]], [[Rage Bee]],
        [[Shocked Bee]], [[Demon Bee]], [[Vector Bee]], and [[Tabby Bee]]. Bees that dislike the
        Spider Field are [[Basic Bee]], [[Honey Bee]], and [[Baby Bee]].

        This field gives Motivating [[Nectar]] when a planter is placed here.

        A level 2-4 [[Rogue Vicious Bee]] can spawn here.

        == Obtaining Boosts ==
        * Using the [[Field Booster]] may give a Spider [[Field Boost]]. When it does, it will give
        +200% Spider Field pollen for 15 minutes. [[File:Fis_spider.png|thumb|75px|The Spider Field
        Boost icon.]]
        * Using [[glitter]] will add 100% Spider Field pollen onto the current boost for 15 minutes
        if it isn't already maxed out.
        * Using [[Field Dice|field dice]], [[Smooth Dice|smooth dice]], or [[Loaded Dice|loaded
        dice]] may add +100% Spider Field pollen for 15 minutes if the current boost isn't already
        maxed out.
        * Spider [[Field Wind]]s can be obtained through the [[Wind Shrine]] or [[codes]]. They have
        a base of +20% instant Spider Field conversion, and give +5% instant Spider Field conversion
        and +20% Spider Field pollen for each stack. Spider Field Winds can stack up to a total of
        13 times.
        * Redeeming certain valid codes:
        ** BANNED (Activates Spider Field Code Buff + other stuff).
        ** 1MLikes (Gives Spider Field Boost x1 + other stuff).
        **PlushFriday (Gives Spider Field Code Buff + other stuff).
        **WeekExtension (Gives Spider Field Boost x3 + Spider Field Winds x10 + other stuff).
        * It can be boosted by the [[Shell Amulet]]. If it has a Spider Field Boost, it can boost
        the field by +5% to +30% depending on the amulet rarity.

        == Trivia ==
        * The Spider Field is the only field where all of the flowers are white. This also makes it
        the only field where all of the flowers are the same color, as there are no fields that are
        all blue or red.
        * There is a [[ticket]] hidden in the middle of the spider cave.
        * This is one of six fields where fireflies can land, with the others being Bamboo Field,
        [[Cactus Field]], [[Pineapple Patch]], [[Rose Field]], and Strawberry Field.
        * There is a [[Bugs/Glitches|glitch]] that occurs at the Spider Field where amulets and cub
        buddies are spotted without an owner. This happens when a player left the server at the
        Spider Field.
        * This field is one of [[Black Bear|Black Bear's]] favorite fields, as mentioned in his
        dialogue.
        * This field, the [[Stump Field]], the [[Hub Field]], and the [[Mountain Top Field]] are the
        only fields that are not named after a plant or a fruit.
        * The Spider Cave, the Stump Field's Cave, and the [[Coconut Cave]] have the exact same cave
        decoration.
        * This field, along with Clover Field, Cactus Field, Rose Field, Mountain Top Field, and
        Pepper Patch are the only fields in which a Rogue Vicious Bee can spawn.
        * This is one of the only two fields named after the mob(s) that reside within the field.
        The other being the [[Ant Field]], with the multiple types of [[Ants]].
