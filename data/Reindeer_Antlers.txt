        The '''Reindeer Antlers''' is a [[Bond|level]] 8 [[Beesmas]] [[beequip]]. It has an equip
        limit of one. There is no color requirement. It can only be equipped to [[Puppy Bee]].

        == Possible Stats ==
        * Convert Amount (Guaranteed) - +10% to +15%. (up to +95% from [[Waxes|waxing]])
        * Ability [[Pollen]] (Guaranteed) - +10% to +15%. (up to +95% from waxing)
        * [[Ability Tokens#Focus|Ability: Focus]] (Guaranteed).
        ** Grants +3% [[System Page#Critical Chance|Critical Chance]] for 20 seconds. Stacks up to
        10 times. [[Critical Hits]] increase pollen and damage by 100%.
        * [[Ability Tokens#Fetch|Ability: Reindeer Fetch]] (Guaranteed).
        ** Enhances Puppy Bee's \"[[Ability Tokens#Fetch|Fetch]]\" ability. Puppy bee’s ball will glow
        bright red, and every 3rd time Puppy bee hits it you'll receive \"[[Buffs & Debuffs#From
        Ability Tokens|Reindeer Guidance]]\", which grants x1.01 Capacity for 15 minutes and stacks
        up to 10 times. Additionally, the ball has a small chance to spawn [[Gingerbread
        Bear|gingerbread bear]]s at high combos (max 1 per day). The chance is increased during
        Beesmas Events.
        * [Hive Bonus] [[Capacity]] (from waxing) - +1% to +20%.
        * [Hive Bonus] [[System Page#Bond From Treats|Bond From Treats]] (from waxing, very rare) -
        +1% to +3%.

        == Trivia ==
        * The Reindeer Antlers and the [[Festive Wreath]] share many similarities:
        ** This and the Festive Wreath are the only permanent beequips in the game.
        *** This is the first permanent beequip added to the game, followed by the Festive Wreath.
        ** The two are the only beequips to have models visible on the bee that they are equipped
        to.
        ** They are the only beequips to have a set potential, with this beequip always having max
        potential and the Festive Wreath always having a potential of 3.
        ** They are the only beequips that cannot be discarded.
        ** [[Caustic Wax]] is unable to be used on these two beequips.
        * A model of a Puppy Bee with Reindeer Antlers can be found outside the map, next to a model
        of a [[Festive Bee]] with the Festive Wreath.
        * This is the one of three beequips to give a unique ability.
        ** The other two are the [[Snowglobe]] and Festive Wreath.
