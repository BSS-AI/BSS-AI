        '''Bucko Bee''' is a [[:Category:Blue|Blue]] [[Bees/Epic|Epic bee]].

        Bucko Bee's favorite type of [[Treats|Treat]] are [[Blueberry|Blueberries]].

        Bucko Bee likes the [[Blue Flower Field]], [[Bamboo Field]], and the [[Pine Tree Forest]].
        It dislikes the [[Strawberry Field]], and [[Rose Field]].

        == Stats ==
        * Collects 17 [[Pollen]] in 4 seconds.
        * Makes 80 [[Honey]] in 3 seconds.
        * +10% [[Stats#Speed|Movespeed]], +50% [[Energy]], +4 [[Stats#Attack|Attack]], +7
        [[Stats#Gather Amount|Gather Amount]], +25% convert speed.
        * 🌟 [[Gifted Bee|Gifted Hive Bonus]]: +20% Blue Field Capacity.

        === Abilities ===
        * '''[[Ability Tokens#Boost|[Blue Boost]]]''' Grants x1.2 pollen from blue [[Flowers]] for
        15s. Stacks up to 10 times.

        == Trivia ==
        *As per the description, this [[Bees|Bee]] is the rival and counterpart of [[Riley Bee]].
        **Both bees are the leaders of their respective colors.
        *Bucko Bee and [[Bumble Bee]] have the same skin colors.
        *[[Black Bear]] refers to the Bucko Bee as “him.” (“I wonder who put him in charge?”) This
        makes him, [[Rad Bee]] and [[Lion Bee]] the only confirmed male bees in the game.
        *There is a giant model of this bee on top of the entrance of the [[Blue HQ]].
        *A [[:Category:Quest Bees|Quest Bee]] represents the gifted version of it in the top floor
        of the Blue HQ.
        *Bucko Bee is the third strongest blue bee, only behind Vicious Bee and Cobalt Bee.
        *The [[Bucko Guard]] is based on Bucko Bee.
        *Bucko Bee, [[Buoyant Bee]], and Bumble Bee are the only Blue bees to like at least three
        fields.
        **Bucko Bee is also the only Blue bee to dislike two fields.
