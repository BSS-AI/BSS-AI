        '''Bee Bear''' is a [[:Category:Traveling Bears|Traveling Bear]] who appears during
        [[:Category:Beesmas|Beesmas]]. He is one of the five temporary [[Quest Givers]], the others
        being [[Sun Bear]], [[Gummy Bear]], [[Stick Bug]] and [[Bubble Bee Man]].

        In 2018, his [[Quests]] were focused on creating [[Present]]s, which could be later given to
        [[:Category:NPC|NPCs]]. At the end of his quests, he gave players a special bee, [[Festive
        Bee]]. Alternatively, if the player already had one by buying the [[Robux Shop|Festive Bee
        Pack]] or from the [[Ticket Tent]], he gave 500 [[Ticket]]s instead. He was located in the
        in front of the [[Ant Gate]] between December 19, 2018 to January 31, 2019.

        His second visit was from December 22, 2019 to February 10, 2020. Bee Bear rewarded the
        player a [[Cub Buddy]] after the 10 quests that a player did, and the Bee Cub Skin after
        another 5 quests. His quests consisted of a variety of different requirements. Some included
        collecting [[Pollen]], [[Ability Tokens|Tokens]], [[Goo]], defeating [[Mobs]], putting
        [[Ornaments]] on the [[Beesmas Tree]], and more. He stood in front of the [[Dandelion
        Field]], next to the [[Ornament Presents|Festive Present]], the first time that a traveling
        bear was not located in front of the Ant Gate.

        His third visit was from December 25, 2020 to March 1, 2021. His quests were used to help
        introduce [[Beequip]]s. His quests also required completing and using Beesmas Decorations
        from multiple NPCs, collecting [[Snowflake]]s, purchasing items from his [[Bee Bear's
        Catalog|Catalog]], and delivering presents. He rewarded players with a Festive Bee ( or 500
        Tickets if [[Festive Bee]] was already owned by the player) when his first 15 quests were
        completed, and rewarded a [[Festive Wreath]] beequip for the Festive Bee when all his quests
        were completed.

        His fourth visit was from December 26, 2021 to April 1, 2022. His [[Quests]] were used to
        help introduce the new [[Planter]]s, rewarded a special type of his own ([[Festive
        Planter]]s), alongside giving players the opportunity to obtain a Cub Buddy (or a [[Star
        Treat|Star Treat]] if already owned) by completing his first 15 quests once again. In the
        second part of Beesmas, he rewards players a [[Cub Buddy#Skins|Snow Cub Buddy Skin]] after
        all of his quests (new ones included) were completed.

        His fifth visit was from December 26, 2022, to May 7, 2023. His quests are used to help
        introduce the [[Robo Bear Challenge]] and [[Drives]]. He rewards players with a Cub Buddy
        (or a Star Treat if Cub Buddy was already owned by the player) when his first 15 quests are
        completed. In the second part of Beesmas, he rewards players a [[Cub Buddy#Skins|Peppermint
        Robo Cub Buddy Skin]] after all of his quests (new ones included) were completed.

        ==Tips ==

        *'''Use [[Field Boost|field boosters]] whenever possible'''. They help with collecting
        pollen, especially for the Festive Bee Workshop Quests and the Bee Cub Workshop Quests.
        These can also be stacked using present rewards (2018 only) or codes. If necessary, using
        [[crafting|crafting material]] boosts can also help.
        *'''Collect honey tokens from [[sprout]]s, [[honeystorm]]s, and the [[Stick Bug
        Challenge]]'''. Players can summon a honey storm using the [[Honeystorm|Honey Storm
        Summoner]] near Brown Bear every 4 hours or use the [[Special Sprout Summoner]] every 16
        hours near the [[Red HQ]]. [[Stick Bug]], [[Stick Nymph]]s, and Stick Bug's Defense Totems
        also drop honey tokens so collect those too. This applies to A Pinch Of Magic and Honeyday
        Harvest. Many of Bee Bear's quests (All I want for Beesmas is Goo, Unsilent Night, and A
        Beesmas Miracle) also require special tokens ([[Gumdrops]]/[[Moon Charm|Moon Charms]]) that
        require active [[Fireflies|firefly]] hunting or sprout planting.
        *'''Use the [[Glue Dispenser]]'''. If the player doesn't have a [[Gummy Bee]] but has a Goo
        Hotshot badge, use a Field Booster and then the Glue Dispenser in [[Gummy Bear's Lair]].
        Using it gives the player the [[Buffs & Debuffs#From Areas|Unlimited Gumdrops buff]]
        that, when coupled with a field booster, would help the player with the goo requirements in
        Festive Bee Workshops 1 and 3, as well as in All I Want for Beesmas is Goo.
        *'''Give [[present]]s to NPCs.''' If an NPC gives a [[Field Boost]] that the player needs
        for a quest, they should give them a present if they haven't yet. In 2018, players could
        only give a present to [[Onett]] after completing all of his Star Journey quests. [[Gifted
        Bucko Bee]], [[Gifted Riley Bee]], and Stick Bug require a [[Translator]] before a present
        can be given to them.
        *'''Use [[Magic Bean]]s to summon Sprouts'''. In some of Bee Bear's quests, it is required
        to collect special treats such as strawberries. Sprouts are the best way to collect special
        treats. Sprout drops are varied towards the [[fields|field]] it is in. For example, a sprout
        found in the [[Strawberry Field]] will drop more strawberry tokens than any other treat.
        Sprouts are especially useful for Festive Bee Workshop 3. Some of Bee Bear's 2019 quests
        also required tokens from sprouts.
        *'''Preserve (don't defeat) any mobs that take a long time to respawn''' such as the
        [[mantis]], [[scorpion]], [[spider]], and [[werewolf]], because players will need these mobs
        for [[Polar Bear]]. Players' time will be wasted if they've recently defeated the werewolf
        and got Teriyaki Jerky from Polar Bear (which requires 1 werewolf to be defeated). When
        collecting pollen, it will be hard not to defeat the mobs but it is easy if players don't
        claim their hive and do some dodging.
        *The player should make sure they have already done crafting the ingredients with the
        [[Blender]] before doing the Festive Bee Workshop 1 quest which requires players to craft
        '''25 ingredients (2 hours 5 minutes)''' and before doing the Festive Bee Workshop 3 quest
        which requires players to craft '''100 ingredients (8 hours 20 minutes)'''. For item saving,
        craft [[gumdrops]], which are the cheapest to craft. If the player doesn't have the item
        needed to craft the items, they should get it from sprouts, Stick Bug, etc. '''Players
        should not end the crafting before the quest is received''' or the time and items they spend
        on the crafting are being wasted.
        **You can also speed up your crafting by spending tickets.
        *'''Server-hop''' for [[Rogue Vicious Bee]]s, [[Wild Windy Bee]]s, and [[Bean Bug|bean
        bugs]]. This is as many of Bee Bear's Cub Buddy quests require tokens from Wild Windy Bee or
        the defeat of Wild Windy Bee.
        *Place [[Planter]]s in specific fields ahead of time if you know you will need to harvest
        hours' worth of nectar in an upcoming quest.

        ==References==

        *The quest title, 'Bee-liz Honey-dad,' refers to 'Feliz Navidad,' ('Merry Christmas' in
        Spanish) a popular Christmas song. Similarly, 'White Beesmas' is an allusion to the classic
        Christmas song (and movie), 'White Christmas,' and 'All I Want For Beesmas Is Goo'
        references the song, 'All I Want For Christmas Is You'. Also, the quest title, \"Walking In A
        Windy Wonderland\" is a reference to the song 'Walking In A Winter Wonderland'. Blue Beesmas
        is a reference to the song \"White Christmas\".
        *Bee Bear is bilingual, or at least knows enough Spanish to rewrite the \"Prospero año y
        felicidad\" (\"prosperous year and happiness\"), a line of 'Feliz Navidad' to \"Osos felices,
        esa es la verdad\" (\"happy bears, that's the truth\").
        *The dialogue in 'All I Want For Beesmas Is Goo' referenced hidden tokens in the Beesmas
        Tree.
        * In Festive Wreath Workshop 8, Bee Bear says \"Have you ever seen Monsters Inc? The Pixar
        movie? Well, you know how the monsters charge up batteries by scaring children? Ho ho ho,
        well what we're doing is basically that, but the opposite! We charge up our festive powers
        through spreading pure joy! ... Festive Bee has just informed me that the monsters end up
        charging their batteries through laughter. So basically the same thing we're doing.
        '''Please don't sue us Pixar!''' Ho ho ho ho!\", which is a direct reference to Monsters.
        Inc.
        *The completion dialogue for the quest Merry Mechsquitos is a reference to the song “Jingle
        Bell Rock” by Bobby Helms.
