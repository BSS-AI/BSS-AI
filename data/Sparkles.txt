{"text": "[[File:Sparkles.png|thumb|A patch of flowers that has sparkles.]]'''Sparkles''' are an
        effect applied to certain patches of [[flowers]] that yields a token containing [[honey]],
        random [[treats]] corresponding to the [[Fields|field]] it is in, [[Moon Charm|moon
        charms]], or rarely [[ticket]]s, [[Royal Jelly|royal jelly]], [[Royal Jelly#Royal Jelly
        Variants|star jelly]], or [[glitter]] when harvested. When enough [[pollen]] is collected
        from a sparkling flower, it will yield a token for any player to collect. If it isn't
        collected within 10 seconds, the token will disappear.

        The largest plants in the [[Sunflower Field]], [[Pineapple Patch]], and [[Pumpkin Patch]]
        will occasionally generate a face and blow bubbles 3 to 5 times on their respective fields,
        creating 13 patches of pollen that sparkle. The [[Blue HQ|Blue HQ's]] Bubble Wand blows
        bubbles on the [[Dandelion Field]], [[Mushroom Field]], [[Blue Flower Field]], and [[Clover
        Field]] in a similar fashion. Individual [[fireflies]] that spawn during the [[Day/Night
        Cycle|night]] also leave behind sparkles on flowers they were on as they are chased off.

        == Trivia ==
        * In the [[Updates#2018.07.11|7/11/2018 update]], the change to the size of the Clover Field
        was not taken into account, so the [[Bubble Wand]] model on the Blue HQ could sometimes
        produce sparkles in the area where there are no flowers.
        * Prior to the [[Updates#2019.09.28|9/28/2019 update]], sparkles covered 4 flowers instead
        of 13.
        * In the [[Updates#2018.09.10|9/10/2018 update]], the chances of sparkles being spat were
        doubled.
        * The Bubble Wand model on the Blue HQ can leave sparkles right under the largest mushroom
        in the Mushroom Field, making it difficult to collect the rewards.
        * Sparkles used to be called '''spit''' by the community, which came from the various faces
        looking like they were spitting upon the field. [[Onett (Developer)|Onett]] later on named
        this effect \"sparkles\" like it is today.
        * [[File:Sparkletificated.png|thumb]]The verb form for sparkles is ‘sparkletificate’.
        * When disappearing, the eyes on a face close before fading away.
        * Spirit Bear mentions the Sparkles in her dialogue before you receive her 8th quest,
        remarking how the Sparkles are \"gross\" in terms of how they were created by the faces.
