        The '''Free Royal Jelly Dispenser''' is a [[dispenser]] that is located in the [[Star
        Hall]]. It requires at least 1 [[Gifted Bee|gifted bee type]] to redeem a [[Royal
        Jelly|royal jelly]] and [[Ability Tokens#Haste|Haste+]]. The number of royal jellies the
        player receives is based on how many [[Gifted Bee|gifted bees]] they currently have in their
        [[hive]]. For example, if the player has 15 gifted bees, but two of them are the same gifted
        bee, they would still get 15 royal jellies.

        This dispenser has a cooldown of 22 hours. Currently, players can get a maximum of 50 royal
        jellies per use with 50 gifted bees.

        == Trivia ==

        * Unlike the Royal Jelly Dispenser which gives a stack of 10x Haste, the Free Royal Jelly
        Dispenser gives Haste+.
        * Using this is one of the two only ways to get the Haste+ [[Buffs & Debuffs|Buff]], the
        other being certain [[codes]].
        * Unlike the Royal Jelly Dispenser which is limited to one Royal Jelly per use, the Free
        Royal Jelly Dispenser can give up to 50 Royal Jellies.
        * Unlike how the [[Free Ant Pass Dispenser]] or the [[Free Robo Pass Dispenser]] are
        directly next to their [[ticket]] counterparts, the Free Royal Jelly Dispenser is not
        directly next to its ticket counterpart, the Royal Jelly Dispenser.
        * This, the Free Robo Pass Dispenser, and the [[Glue Dispenser]] are the only dispensers
        with a 22 hour cooldown, the longest in the game.
