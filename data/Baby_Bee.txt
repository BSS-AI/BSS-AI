        == Trivia ==
        * Baby Bee has the second-lowest base energy (lowest being [[Tadpole Bee]]), second-lowest
        base [[System Page#Convert Rate|Convert Rate]] (lowest being [[Fuzzy Bee]]), and the lowest
        base gather amount in the game. Baby Bee also has the second-slowest base gather speed, 1
        second faster than Tadpole Bee and Fuzzy Bee.
        * Baby Bee and Tadpole Bee tie for the smallest [[Bees]] in the game. They are also the only
        two bee types to have a different size compared to other bees, and the only bees who have
        less than 1 base [[Stats#Attack|Attack]]. Attack mutations won't give Baby Bee attack so
        Baby Bee '''<u>''can't''</u>''' attack.
        ** They are also the only bees who can generate Baby Love tokens. However, Tadpole Bee will
        need to be gifted in order to do so.
        * Baby Bee is the only bee that has 0 base attack. Because of that, it cannot attack any
        [[Mobs]].
        ** Baby Bee used to be able to attack and could do damage if the player had any damage
        boosts. After the [[Updates#2018.11.25|11/25/2018 Update]], this is no longer the case.
        ** According to [[Black Bear]], the reason why they don't attack is because \"They're too
        dumb to notice\" the mobs.
        ** Even though it says, \"0 attack\", attack added to baby bee still counts towards total
        attack, which can buff [[Passive Abilities#Star Saw|Star Saw]].
        * Baby Bee, [[Bumble Bee]], [[Demon Bee]], and [[Exhausted Bee]] are tied as the slowest
        bees in the game.
        * Baby Bee has the most field preferences in the game. It also dislikes the most [[Fields]].
        ** Baby Bee prefers the lower-tier fields (Dandelion, Blue Flower, Sunflower, etc.) and
        dislikes the higher-tier fields (Rose, Cactus, Pine Tree Forest, etc.).
        * Baby Bee is the only bee that isn't red to like the Mushroom Field.
        *Baby Bee and [[Stubborn Bee]] are the only [[:Category:Colorless|Colorless]] bees that like
        strawberries.
        * Baby Bee and [[Rage Bee]] are the only bees with three unique colors on their skins. Baby
        Bee is also the only gifted bee with three unique colors on its skin.
        * There is a small chance that a Baby Bee egg will drop from [[Tunnel Bear]].
        * Before 11/25/2018 update, the Gifted Hive Bonus of Baby Bee was +5% Loot Luck, which was
        then changed to +10% in said update. This was then changed to +20% Loot Luck in the
        [[Updates#2019.04.05|4/5/2019 Update]] and once again to +25% Loot Luck in the Beesmas 2019
        update.
        * Onett paid $2 for the laughing baby sound that Baby Love uses. It can be bought
        [https://audiojungle.net/item/baby-laughing/21354817 Here].
        * This is one of the only 4 bees that has a specific egg obtainable from mob drops, with the
        others being a Rascal Bee egg from ladybugs, a Bumble Bee egg from rhino beetles, or a Honey
        Bee egg from cave monsters.
        * Baby Bee, [[Basic Bee]], and Bumble Bee are the only bees shown on [[Onett's Lid Art]].
