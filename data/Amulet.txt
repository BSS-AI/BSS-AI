{"text": "An '''amulet''' is an item introduced in the [[Updates#2018.07.11|7/11/2018 update]]. They
        grant several [[Buffs & Debuffs|buffs]] to the player and/or their [[bees]], and can be
        obtained by completing special challenges, which are defeating the [[King Beetle]],
        discovering [[Gifted Bee|gifted bee]] types, participating in the [[Ant Challenge]], using
        the [[Moon Amulet Generator]] (requires 100 [[Moon Charm|moon charms]] to activate),
        defeating the [[Stump Snail]], having at least 1 million (1,000,000) score and at least a
        level 5 [[Stick Bug]] in the [[Stick Bug Challenge]], or beating at least 5 levels in the
        [[Robo Bear Challenge]].

        == Scheme Of Table ==
        [[File:Scheme-0.png|thumb|246px|The scheme for the Ant Amulet.]]
        [[File:Amulet disconnection message.png|thumb|The message that appears when a player joins a
        server after disconnecting with an undecided amulet.]]
        Collecting an amulet will bring up a table with the old and new amulets' stats, and the
        option the keep old amulet or replace it. The scheme on the left shows an example table,
        with the player's amulet type as well as other rewards. If the player has obtained the
        amulet from the Ant Challenge or Stick Bug Challenge, their score will be shown above the
        rewards.

        Clicking \"'''Keep Old'''\" will cause the player to keep their current amulet and clicking
        \"'''Replace'''\" will cause the player to receive a confirmation message asking if they want
        to replace it. If the player has agreed to replace their amulet after the confirmation
        message, the player's old amulet will be replaced with the new one. '''This cannot be
        reversed'''. Note that the drops will still be received even if the player didn't replace
        their amulet. If two or more tables are shown, the previous table(s) are closed, and the
        newest table is displayed.

        As of the [[Updates#2020.06.06|6/6/2020 update]], a confirmation message will now pop up if
        the player tries to replace an amulet if they already had that type of amulet.

        As of the [[Updates#2021.12.26|12/26/2021 update]], disconnecting while a recently generated
        amulet is undecided gives the player another chance to choose upon going back in a server.
        [[File:Equipnewamulet.png|thumb|The confirmation message that appears when the player wants
        to replace their amulet.]]

        ==Trivia==
        *Supreme amulets are the only amulets that change color slightly.
