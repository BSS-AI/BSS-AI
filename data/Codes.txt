        Promo '''Codes''' are a feature added in the May 18, 2018 update. It is located at the top
        of the [[System Page]]. Redeeming them gives prizes such as [[honey]], [[Ticket|tickets]],
        [[gumdrops]], [[Royal Jelly|royal jelly]], [[crafting]] materials, [[Wealth Clock|wealth
        clock]], [[Magic Bean|magic beans]], boosts from [[Ability Tokens|ability tokens]], or
        [[Field Boost|field boosts]].

        [[Onett (Developer)|Onett]] posts codes (or hints for codes) in the game itself, on the
        game's [https://www.roblox.com/games/1537690962 Roblox page], on the
        [https://www.roblox.com/My/Groups.aspx?gid=3982592 Bee Swarm Simulator Club] page, on
        [https://twitter.com/OnettDev his Twitter account], and on the game's
        [https://discord.gg/bee official Discord server]. He also sometimes gives out codes to
        YouTubers, and there have been reports of codes posted on his
        [https://www.roblox.com/users/1912490/profile Roblox profile page] and on Roblox
        advertisements.

        ==Notes==
        *Do '''''<u>NOT</u>''''' join the Discord server just to ask for codes! Instead,
        use the search function to look for posts by Onett (Onett#0001).
        *Codes can only be used '''once'''.
        *Some older codes may tell you once you redeem them that you have obtained a >x3 boost,
        though they will still only give you x3.
        * They are ''not'' case-sensitive: \"Bopmaster\", \"BopMaster\", \"BOPMASTER\", and \"bopmaster\"
        will all work equally.
        **Codes allow spaces in between: \"Bees Buzz 123\" and \"BeesBuzz123\" will work equally.
        *Expired codes do not always say \"expired\"; sometimes, they say \"invalid.\"
        *When you try to redeem a valid code that you have already used, you will be told: \"You have
        already redeemed [code].\"
