        The '''White Balloon''' is a [[:Category:Consumables|consumable]] [[Items#Misc. Items|item]]
        added in the [[Updates|12/26/2021 update]]. Once used, it creates a white balloon over the
        [[Fields|field]] the player is in, storing [[pollen]] collected underneath it. The pollen
        collected below it increases by 10%, with white pollen increasing by 20%. It holds up to 10x
        the player's capacity and floats over the field for 5 minutes before floating to the
        [[hive]] balloon.

        White balloons have a cap of 25 in the player's [[:Category:Inventory|inventory]], and 1 per
        player in the field.
        [[File:White Balloom.webp|thumb|A White Balloon floating above a field.]]

        == Ways to Obtain ==
        * As a exceptionally rare drop from a [[spider]].
        * As a reward after finishing a [[Robo Bear Challenge]].
        * Completing certain [[Dapper Bear]] quests:
        ** Wandering Ambition rewards 1 white balloon and other items.
        ** The Long Haul rewards 1 white balloon and other items.
        ** Solitarily Steadfast rewards 3 white balloons and other items.

        <u>'''These methods are no longer possible:'''</u>
        * Completing [[Bee Bear]]'s Beesmas Bouquet and Snow Cub Reformation (4/5).
        * Purchasing the Buoyant Pack in the [[Robux Shop]] gave 3 white balloons and other items.
        * Purchasing the Balloon Bundle in [[Bee Bear’s Catalog]] during Beesmas 2021, granting 3

        * As a reward from certain [[Robo Party|Robo Parties]].
