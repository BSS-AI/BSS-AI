        [[File:Beesmastree.png|thumb|The Beesmas Tree.]]
        The '''Beesmas Tree''' is a feature that appeared during Beesmas 2019, 2020, 2021, and 2022.
        The Beesmas Tree allows the player to view their collected [[ornaments]], who they are from,
        and what boosts they give. The tree is located in front of the [[Ant Gate]], near the [[Noob
        Shop]], and next to the hives.

        ==Beesmas 2022==
        {{RemovedContent}}
        The Beesmas Tree made another return in 2022, functioning the exact same way as during
        Beesmas 2021. Conducting a gift exchange with an NPC would grant players an ornament that
        would grant special boosts on the Beesmas Tree.

        The bottom row holds five ornaments. They are given, from left to right, from [[Black
        Bear]], [[Mother Bear]], [[Brown Bear]], [[Panda Bear]], and [[Science Bear]]. The second
        row of the tree holds another five ornaments. They are, from left to right, from [[Polar
        Bear]], [[Dapper Bear]], [[Onett]], [[Spirit Bear]], and [[Robo Bear]]. The third row of the
        tree holds three ornaments. They are, from left to right, from [[Gifted Riley Bee]],
        [[Gifted Bucko Bee]], and [[Stick Bug]]. The ornaments on the 4th row of the Beesmas Tree
        are currently unknown, though it can be assumed they are Honey Bee and Gummy Bear. The
        ornament on the top of the tree is [[Bubble Bee Man]]'s ornament.

        There is a [[Neonberry|neonberry]] token under the Beesmas Tree and a [[Robo Pass]] token at
        the top of the trunk of the Beesmas Tree.

        == Beesmas 2021 ==
        {{RemovedContent}}
        The Beesmas Tree made another return in 2021, functioning the exact same way as during
        Beesmas 2020. Conducting a gift exchange with an NPC would grant players an ornament that
        would grant special boosts on the Beesmas Tree.

        The bottom row held five ornaments. They were given, from left to right, from [[Black
        Bear]], [[Mother Bear]], [[Brown Bear]], [[Panda Bear]] and [[Science Bear]]. The second row
        of the tree held another four ornaments. They were, from left to right, from [[Polar Bear]],
        [[Dapper Bear]], [[Onett]], and [[Spirit Bear]]. The third row of the tree held three
        ornaments. They were, from left to right, from [[Gifted Riley Bee]], [[Gifted Bucko Bee]],
        and [[Stick Bug]]. An additional three ornaments could also be added to the tree, and they
        were, from left to top, [[Honey Bee (NPC)]], [[Gummy Bear]], and [[Bubble Bee Man]].

        There is a Honeysuckle token at the bottom, a [[Neonberry|Neonberry]] to the left, a
        [[Whirligig]] token, and a [[Smooth Dice]] token above the Beesmas Tree.

        == Beesmas 2020 ==
        {{RemovedContent}}
        The Beesmas Tree returned in 2020, and it functioned the same. To put ornaments on the tree
        the player needed to collect and give [[present]]s to each corresponding
        [[:Category:NPC|NPC]]. In exchange, the player would receive their ornament along with a few
        special boosts.
        [[File:Gingerbread bear under beesmastree.png|thumb|The Gingerbread Bear under the Beesmas
        Tree.]]
        The bottom row held four ornaments. They were given, from left to right, from [[Black
        Bear]], [[Mother Bear]], [[Brown Bear]], and [[Panda Bear]]. The second row of the tree held
        another four ornaments. They were, from left to right, from [[Science Bear]], [[Polar
        Bear]], [[Onett]], and [[Spirit Bear]]. The third row of the tree held three ornaments. They
        were, from left to right, from [[Gifted Riley Bee]], [[Gifted Bucko Bee]], and [[Stick
        Bug]]. An additional three ornaments could also be added to the tree, and they were, from
        left to top, [[Honey Bee (NPC)]], [[Gummy Bear]], and [[Bubble Bee Man]].

        There was a [[Gingerbread Bear]] token at the bottom and a [[Neonberry|Neonberry]] token at
        the top of the trunk of the Beesmas Tree.

        == Beesmas 2019 ==
        {{RemovedContent}}
        To put ornaments on the tree, the player had to complete the [[Quests|quest]] of the
        corresponding [[:Category:NPC|NPC]], then talk to them. The ornament would then
        automatically go onto the tree.

        [[File:BeesmasTree_SomeOrnaments.png|thumb|Some ornaments on the Beesmas Tree.]]
        The bottom row of the tree held four ornaments. They were given, in order from left to
        right, from [[Black Bear]], [[Mother Bear]], [[Brown Bear]], and [[Panda Bear]]. The second
        row of the tree held another four ornaments. They were, from left to right, from [[Science
        Bear]], [[Polar Bear]], [[Onett]], and [[Spirit Bear]]. The third row of the tree held three
        ornaments. They were, from left to right, from [[Gifted Riley Bee]], [[Gifted Bucko Bee]],
        and [[Stick Bug]]. The ornament placed on the highest row of the tree was from [[Bubble Bee
        Man]].

        There was a [[Field Dice|field dice]], 5 [[gumdrops]], and 5 [[Moon Charm|moon charms]]
        located in the Beesmas Tree. On the top of its trunk, there were 10
        [[Bitterberry|bitterberries]]. To access the platform and the 10 bitterberries on it,
        players had to glide down from on top of [[Noob Shop]] or drop down from the Ant Gate.
