        The {{Color|Black Balloon}} is a [[:Category:Consumables|consumable]] [[Items#Misc.
        Items|item]] that was added in the [[Updates|12/26/2021 update]]. Once used, it creates a
        black balloon over the [[Fields|field]] the player is in. The balloon floats over the field,
        storing [[pollen]] collected underneath it. The pollen collected below is increased by 4x.
        It holds up to 25x the player's capacity, and floats over the field for 5 minutes before
        floating to the hive balloon. Only 1 Black Balloon can be summoned in a field per player.
        Black balloons have a cap of 25 in the player's [[:Category:Inventory|inventory]]. Obtaining
        one while already having 25 will not add a Black Balloon to the player's inventory, unless
        obtained from robux packs, [[quests]], or [[codes]].

        == Ways to Obtain ==
        * As an exceptionally rare drop from [[Tunnel Bear]].
        * As an exceptionally rare drop from a [[Spider]].
        * Completing [[Dapper Bear|Dapper Bear's]] 12th quest rewards 1 black balloon and other
        items.
        * Sticking the Black Star Sticker to the Sticker Stacker rewards one Black Balloon.
