        The '''Party Mega Mechsquito''' was one of the mobs exclusive to the [[Robo Party]]. It
        functioned similarly to a regular [[Mega Mechsquito]], but they roam around a field rather
        than spawning at the vicinity of the player. They first spawned on Rank 15 and continued to
        keep spawning in the following ranks. The Party Mega Mechsquitos, similarly to [[Mega
        Mechsquito|Mega Mechsquitos,]] fly away whenever a player is near them. This is the
        explanation to why the player always keeps distance from them.

        == Strategies ==
        Since they are essentially the same as their normal counterparts, Mega Mechsquitos, the same
        methods used for them can be followed.

        * Party Mega Mechsquitos aim for where the player is, not where the player will be. Because
        of this, moving around in circles will often result in Party Mega Mechsquitos missing their
        attack.
        * Stand near the Party Mega Mechsquito, this will allow your bees to lock on to the Party
        Mega Mechsquito and quickly deal damage.
        * [[Vicious Bee]] and [[Windy Bee]] provide excellent crowd control, especially since Party
        Mega Mechsquitos often come in pairs or packs. [[Digital Bee]] can also stun many Party Mega
        Mechsquitos at once and allow them to take extra damage via [[Ability Tokens#Mind Hack|Mind
        Hack]].
        * Unlike their normal counterparts, they stay in fields which can be used for the players
        advantage. Stand right outside the field they are in and your bees will attack it while it
        doesn't aim for you. Note that this can be a bit risky since they can hover around the other
        parts of the field and don't come close enough for your bees to target them.
